《**Old Town Road**》（字面意思：「*老城路*」）是美國說唱歌手[利爾·納斯·X的一首歌](../Page/利尔·纳斯·X.md "wikilink")，於2018年12月3日首次獨立發行。積累一定人氣後，該單曲被一主要唱片公司[哥倫比亞唱片重新發行](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/哥倫比亞唱片 "wikilink")。利爾·納斯·X還錄製了與美國鄉村歌手比利·雷·賽勒斯的重混版，於2019年4月5日發行。[利爾·納斯·X的首張EP](../Page/利尔·纳斯·X.md "wikilink") **（2019）中就同時包括原版和重混版。

這首歌被廣泛稱為「鄉村說唱」——一個發行前一年才開始流行的音樂類型。荷蘭音樂人在2018年製作了伴奏，並將其發上網出售。此伴奏含有取自美國工業搖滾樂隊九吋釘樂團作品《》的班卓琴表演片段。這一段班卓琴被放置在陷阱音樂式的鼓和低音的背景後播放。[利爾·納斯·X以](../Page/利尔·纳斯·X.md "wikilink")30美元的價格購入此伴奏，然後在一天內就錄製好《**Old Town Road**》全曲。當時，他因為輟學一直和姐姐住在一起。他在現實世界中的掙扎影響了這首歌的歌詞。

這首歌最初在社交視頻共享應用TikTok（[抖音短視頻](../Page/抖音短视频.md "wikilink")）上流行起來，最終於2019年3月進入[告示牌榜單](../Page/告示牌榜單.md "wikilink")。這首歌在[告示牌熱門鄉村歌曲榜上排名一度達第](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/告示牌热门乡村歌曲榜 "wikilink")19位；但是，告示牌雜誌隨後以其不符合[鄉村音樂流派為由](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鄉村音樂 "wikilink")，從榜單中撤下本曲，隨後惹起爭議。\[1\]儘管最終本曲未能重新列入鄉村音樂榜單，其原版和重混版均最終在告示牌旗艦榜單「[Hot 100](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hot_100 "wikilink")」上均達到第一，並且。\[2\] \[3\]在國際上，《**Old Town Road**》至少一個版本在[澳大利亞](../Page/澳大利亚.md "wikilink")，[加拿大](../Page/加拿大.md "wikilink")，[法國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法國 "wikilink")，[德國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/德國 "wikilink")，[愛爾蘭](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/愛爾蘭 "wikilink")，[荷蘭](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/荷蘭 "wikilink")，[新西蘭](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/新西蘭 "wikilink")，[挪威](../Page/挪威.md "wikilink")，[瑞士和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/瑞士 "wikilink")[英國的單曲榜上名列第一](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/英國 "wikilink")，也在其他許多國家列入前十。

## 背景

[左](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Lil_Nas_X_\(cropped\).jpg "fig:左")

「鄉村說唱」這一流派（以及其子流派「鄉村trap」）是在2017年[揚·薩格實驗性質的混音帶](../Page/扬·萨格.md "wikilink")《》出現後才逐漸融入主流的。\[4\]利爾·納斯·X也將揚·薩格稱為這一流派的先驅。\[5\]2018年5月，Lil Tracy和Lil Uzi Vert用鄉村說唱單曲《Like a Farmer》重混版再度點燃了鄉村說唱的潮流。\[6\]\[7\]

2018年，Lil Nas X輟學去從事音樂事業，但遭到父母反對。他和姐姐住在一起，盡力在網上宣傳自己的音樂，每晚僅睡三個小時。2018年10月，他找到了《Old Town Road》的節拍伴奏。與之同時，他的姐姐告訴他必須盡快搬走；他於是開始寫作本曲。\[8\]Lil Nas X覺得自己「已經無路可走」，並稱他姐姐和父母覺得他無可理喻；他便由此得到本曲主旋律歌詞的靈感（「我誰也不聽」）。在開始寫作的同月，Lil Nas X決定改變歌詞的意義，讓「老城路」變成成功的象徵\[9\]。Lil Nas X於2018年12月2日在\[10\]在亞特蘭大的CinCoYo錄音棚錄製了這首歌，\[11\]次日便正式發行。\[12\]

荷蘭音樂人在《Old Town Road》發行一年多前就已製作出其伴奏節拍，並放上網絡銷售。他對[九寸釘樂團](../Page/九寸釘樂團.md "wikilink")的歌曲《34 Ghosts IV》進行了採樣，而且完全保留原曲的樣本順序，當作對自己的一點挑戰。他承認自己「完全沒想到能將其變成鄉村音樂」。在《34 Ghosts IV》以前，他從未聽過這個樂團。\[13\]Lil Nas X以30美元的價格從YoungKio買下了這段節拍，\[14\]但當時並未透露自己的身份，因此YoungKio一直到2018年12月都不知道買主是誰。\[15\]

## *Billboard* genre classification controversy

[右](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:BillboardLogo2013.svg "fig:右") chart after it debuted at number 19.\[16\]\[17\]\]\] "Old Town Road" achieved a rare feat in *[告示牌 (雜誌)](../Page/告示牌_\(雜誌\).md "wikilink")* history when it simultaneously charted on the *Billboard* Hot 100, [公告牌热门乡村歌曲榜](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/公告牌热门乡村歌曲榜 "wikilink") and  charts in March 2019. However, *Billboard* quietly removed "Old Town Road" from its Hot Country chart for "not \[embracing\] enough elements of today's country music".\[18\] Had it not been disqualified, "Old Town Road" would have been the Hot Country Songs , as of the chart dated April 6, 2019.\[19\] Lil Nas X stated he was "extremely disappointed" by the decision.\[20\] In an interview, *[時代雜誌](../Page/時代雜誌.md "wikilink")* reporter Andrew R. Chow brought up *Billboard*{{'}}s decision to remove "Old Town Road" from the Country chart but keep it on the R\&B/Hip-Hop chart, asking Lil Nas X if he considers "Old Town Road" a country song. Lil Nas X replied, "The song is country trap. It's not one, it's not the other. It's both. It should be on both \[charts\]."\[21\]

The exclusion of "Old Town Road" brought criticism of the evaluation of the work of non-white artists in the country genre, with *[滾石 (雜誌)](../Page/滾石_\(雜誌\).md "wikilink")* writer Elias Leight referring to [碧昂絲](../Page/碧昂絲.md "wikilink"), another black artist, whose song "" failed to be considered a country song by  in 2016.\[22\] Leight also pointed out other difficulties faced by black artists making cross-genre music, noting that rapper [朱斯·沃尔德](../Page/朱斯·沃尔德.md "wikilink")'s ** (2019) would "probably be the most commercially successful rock album of 2019", but would never appear on rock charts or playlists, another genre like country predominated by white artists.\[23\] In music journalist [羅伯特·克里斯特高](../Page/羅伯特·克里斯特高.md "wikilink")'s opinion, "Taking 'Old Town Road' off the country chart strikes me as racist pure and simple, because  remains racist regardless of the s and [凱恩·布朗s](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/凱恩·布朗 "wikilink") it makes room for."\[24\] In light of the criticism, *Billboard* later stated that the decision to remove "Old Town Road" from the Hot Country Songs chart had nothing to do with Lil Nas X's race.\[25\]\[26\] When asked if he believed the decision by *Billboard* had any racial undertones, Lil Nas X replied, "I believe whenever you're trying something new, it's always going to get some kind of bad reception."\[27\]

Despite being removed from the main Country Songs chart, "Old Town Road" managed to chart on *Billboard*'s  chart, debuting at number 53 and peaking at number 50.\[28\]\[29\] In response,  CEO Randy Goodman told *Billboard* that his team started testing the song in some country radio markets, adding "it would be negligent not to look at it".\[30\]

## Commercial performance

[右](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Billy_Ray_Cyrus_VMA_2015.png "fig:右") (pictured), "Old Town Road" charted at number one on the *Billboard* Hot 100 for a record-setting nineteen weeks\]\] In the United States, the original version of "Old Town Road" ascended to number one on the *Billboard* Hot 100 in the week ending April 13, 2019;\[31\] at one minute and fifty-three seconds in length, it became the shortest number-one single since "" by  in 1965 and the fifth-shortest in the history of the chart.\[32\] The following week, it and the newly-released remix featuring Billy Ray Cyrus received a combined 143 million streams, surpassing [德雷克 (歌手)](../Page/德雷克_\(歌手\).md "wikilink")'s "", which in 2018 had amassed 116.2 million in the same period, as the song with the highest single-week streaming figure in the country.\[33\] "Old Town Road" continued to exceed 100 million streams in each of the eight following weeks, and as of June 2019 is responsible for the three highest weekly U.S. streaming totals in the history of digital music, as well as nine of the eleven highest overall.\[34\]\[35\] The song was certified triple Platinum by the RIAA on May 10, 2019, indicating combined sales and streams of three million units in the United States.\[36\] The song has sold 1,361,000 copies in the United States as of September 2019.\[37\]

"Old Town Road" topped the Hot 100 for nineteen consecutive weeks from April to August 2019. Its overall run at number one is the longest in chart history, surpassing the previous record of sixteen weeks achieved by both "[甜蜜一日](../Page/甜蜜一日.md "wikilink")", by [瑪麗亞·凱莉](../Page/瑪麗亞·凱莉.md "wikilink") and [大人小孩雙拍檔](../Page/大人小孩雙拍檔.md "wikilink") (1995–96), and "[慢慢來 (路易斯·方辛歌曲)](../Page/慢慢來_\(路易斯·方辛歌曲\).md "wikilink")", by [路易斯·馮西](../Page/路易斯·馮西.md "wikilink") and [洋基老爹](../Page/洋基老爹.md "wikilink") featuring [贾斯汀·比伯](../Page/贾斯汀·比伯.md "wikilink") (2017).\[38\] In accordance with its policies, *Billboard* assigns a single position to "Old Town Road" on the Hot 100 by combining the performance figures of every released version of the song determined to be sufficiently similar to the original; as a result, the entirety of its chart run is credited solely to the remix featuring Cyrus.\[39\]\[40\] "Old Town Road" was also the first-ever number-one song on the  chart.\[41\]

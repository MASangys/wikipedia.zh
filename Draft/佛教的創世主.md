[佛教](../Page/佛教.md "wikilink")思想中有關[創世主的學說存在分歧](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/創造神 "wikilink")。

於[小乘佛教](../Page/小乘佛教.md "wikilink")而言，其否定創世主論。佛教在其[輪迴學說中開示了其對於神](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/輪迴 "wikilink")、天堂和[結生](../Page/結生.md "wikilink")的觀念，但當中沒有一個神是其所謂的創世主；佛教也認為把[梵天等平凡的神視為創世主是一種謬誤](../Page/梵天_\(佛教\).md "wikilink")。由於佛教的本體論建基於主張「諸法因緣生，諸法因緣滅」的[緣起](../Page/緣起.md "wikilink")學說，故佛教否定無原因的世間見的存在。

然而，[大乘佛教](../Page/大乘佛教.md "wikilink")相信the doctrine of [Sunyata](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sunyata "wikilink") (emptiness) or Tathata (thatness) out of which all things emerge. Furthermore, this ultimate reality is considered to be the [Tathagatagarbha](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tathagatagarbha "wikilink") (Womb of all Buddhas) and the [Adi Buddha](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adi_Buddha "wikilink") (Primordial Buddha) who is worshipped differently in various Mahayana traditions as [Vairocana](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vairocana "wikilink") or [Amitabha](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amitabha "wikilink") and in the [Vajrayana](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vajrayana "wikilink") traditions as [Samantabhadra](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samantabhadra "wikilink") or [Vajradhara](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vajradhara "wikilink"). Thus this concept of an ultimate reality which is the source of all things approximates the idea of a creator God.

## 早期佛經

根據[佛教研究者](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/佛教研究 "wikilink")所述，早期佛教[尼柯耶](../Page/尼柯耶.md "wikilink")文學主要由認識論觀點與道德觀點看待創世主是否存在的問題。在這些文獻中，[佛陀被描繪成一個不否認創世主存在](../Page/释迦牟尼.md "wikilink")，也未能夠證明創世主不存在的無神論者，但他的重點在於其他教師聲稱他們的教導帶來了最大的利益。

海耶斯也引用《[中部](../Page/中部_\(佛經\).md "wikilink")·天臂經》（）指，雖然讀者可以從中得出結論，認為過去生活中的行為、命運、出生，又或是生活中使我們悲傷的事，諸如此類是業力而非創世主所為，佛教並沒有作出系統性論證以試圖否定創世主的存在。

The Pali Canon however also gives a different view. In the Nibbana Sutta of the Udana Nikaya, the Buddha says, "There is, monks, an unborn, unbecome, unmade, unfabricated. If there were not that unborn, unbecome, unmade, unfabricated, there would not be the case that escape from the born, become, made, fabricated would be discerned. But precisely because there is an unborn, unbecome unmade, unfabricated escape from the born, become, made, fabricated is discerned." Buddhism thus acknowledges an Ultimate that transcends the material world. This in [Theravada](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theravada "wikilink") is called Nibbana and in Mahayana it is called [Sunyata](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sunyata "wikilink") and worshipped as [Adi Buddha](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adi_Buddha "wikilink").\[1\]

### 梵天作為偽創世主

根據彼得·哈維（）所述，佛教認為宇宙沒有終極的開端，因此並不需要創世主。在佛教的早期文本中，最接近創世主的術語是「大[梵天](../Page/梵天.md "wikilink")」（），例如《[长部](../Page/長部_\(佛經\).md "wikilink")·[梵網經](../Page/梵網經_\(上座部\).md "wikilink")》（）所述。然而，那些梵天雖然善良和富有同情心，但他們之中沒有一個是創世主。

In the [Pali canon](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pali_canon "wikilink"), Buddhism includes the concept of reborn gods. According to this theory, periodically the physical world system ends and beings of that world system are reborn as [gods](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deva_\(Buddhism\) "wikilink") in lower heavens. This too ends, according to Buddhist cosmology, and god [Mahabrahma](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brahmā_\(Buddhism\) "wikilink") is then born, who is alone. He longs for the presence of others, and the others gods are reborn as his ministers and companions. Mahabrahma, states the Buddhist Canon, forgets his past lives, and falsely believes himself to be the Creator, Maker, All-seeing, the Lord. This belief, state the Buddhist texts, is then shared by other gods. Eventually, however one of the gods die and is reborn as human with the power to remember his previous life. He teaches what he remembers from his previous life in lower heaven, that Mahabrahma is the Creator. It is this that leads to the human belief in Creator, according to the Pali Canon.

According to Harvey, "\[a\]fter a long period, the [three lowest form heavens](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buddhist_cosmology_of_the_Theravada_school#Brahm.C4.81_Planes "wikilink") appear, and a [Streaming Radiance](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buddhist_cosmology_of_the_Theravada_school#.C4.80bh.C4.81svara_Planes "wikilink") god dies and is reborn there as a Great Brahmā." Then "other Streaming Radiance gods die and happen to be reborn, due to their karma, as his ministers and retinue." The retinue erroneously believes Mahabrahma created them. When one of these ministers "eventually dies and is reborn as a human, he develops the power to remember his previous life, and consequently teaches that Great Brahmā is the eternal creator of all beings."

## 中世紀佛學家

### 世亲

公元5世紀的印度佛教論師[世亲](../Page/世亲.md "wikilink")著論書《[阿毗达磨俱舍论](../Page/阿毗达磨俱舍论.md "wikilink")》，而在其中，他認為創世主的獨特身份與他創造的世界是不相容的。

### 其他

公元七世紀印度佛學家[法稱緊隨世親之後](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法稱 "wikilink")，在其所著的《[釋量論](../Page/釋量論.md "wikilink")》中提出了一些反對創世主存在的論據。後來，[寂護](../Page/寂護.md "wikilink")與[蓮花戒](../Page/蓮花戒.md "wikilink")等大乘佛學家也延續了這一學説。

公元十一世紀印度佛學家在[超戒寺](../Page/超戒寺.md "wikilink")（今[比哈尔邦](../Page/比哈尔邦.md "wikilink")[巴加爾布爾](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴加爾布爾 "wikilink")）批評了稱為“[自在](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/自在 "wikilink")”的類似神的行為的論點，that emerged in the [Navya-Nyaya sub-school of Hinduism](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nyaya#Nyaya_on_God_and_salvation "wikilink"), in his “Refutation of Arguments Establishing Īśvara” (Īśvara-sādhana-dūṣaṇa). These arguments are similar to those used by other sub-schools of Hinduism and Jainism that questioned the Navya-Nyaya theory of dualistic creator.

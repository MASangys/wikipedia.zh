**王妍**（）是一名出生于[中华人民共和国](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/中华人民共和国 "wikilink")[辽宁省](../Page/辽宁省.md "wikilink")[本溪市](../Page/本溪市.md "wikilink")的[竞走](../Page/竞走.md "wikilink")运动员, who won the bronze medal over 10 km at the 于[亚特兰大](../Page/亚特兰大.md "wikilink")[1996年夏季奥林匹克运动会](../Page/1996年夏季奥林匹克运动会.md "wikilink") in [Atlanta](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atlanta "wikilink"). She also won the 1993 [競走世界盃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/競走世界盃 "wikilink"), as well as a silver medal at the  and a bronze at the 2001 [東亞運動會](../Page/東亞運動會.md "wikilink").

She was the world record holder for the 20 km walk from 2001 to 2005. She held the [亞洲田徑紀錄](../Page/亞洲田徑紀錄列表.md "wikilink") for the women's 20 km walk with her best of 1:26:22 hours from 2001 to 2012, until it was beaten by [刘虹](../Page/刘虹.md "wikilink").\[1\]

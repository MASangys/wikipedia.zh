**百事极度（Pepsi Max）**（在部分国家被称作**百事黑甁（Pepsi Black）**）由百事公司出品，作为[百事可乐和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/百事可乐 "wikilink")[百事健怡的替代品在欧洲和亚洲等市场销售](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/百事健怡 "wikilink")。一种名称相同但配方不同（含有[人参成分和更多咖啡因](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/人参 "wikilink")）的饮料曾今在美国销售，2016年底更名为“无糖百事可乐”。

## 历史

百事可乐于1993年4月在[澳大利亚](../Page/澳大利亚.md "wikilink")、[英国和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/英国 "wikilink")[意大利](../Page/意大利.md "wikilink")首次亮相。随后9月在[爱尔兰](../Page/爱尔兰.md "wikilink")发售，并于次年12月在[法国](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法国 "wikilink")、[希腊](../Page/希腊.md "wikilink")、[西班牙](../Page/西班牙.md "wikilink")、[葡萄牙](../Page/葡萄牙.md "wikilink")和[荷兰](../Page/荷兰.md "wikilink")上市。到1994年底，百事极度在大约20个国家发售。1995年底，这一数字增长了一倍多。由于其中一种主要成分，即乙酰磺胺酸钾尚未得到[美国食品和药物管理局的批准](../Page/美国食品药品监督管理局.md "wikilink")，乙酰磺胺酸钾与[阿斯巴甜](../Page/阿斯巴甜.md "wikilink")组合以提供无糖饮料的甜味，而其他大部分低糖可乐则使用阿斯巴甜加糖提供甜味（百事极度作为无糖饮料，不能添加任何糖分）。正因如此，百事极度直到2006年才在美国本土上市（事实上，美国作为百事可乐的本土市场，才是最大的碳酸软饮料消费者）。

2005年初，Pepsi Max Twist（添加了柠檬，酸橙风味）在[英国和](../Page/英国下议院.md "wikilink")[澳大利亚](../Page/澳大利亚.md "wikilink")上市。2005年秋季，“Pepsi Max Punch”在英国上市，含有生姜和肉桂的产品与Pepsi Holiday Spice相似，后者是一年前在美国上市的含糖甜品百事可乐。 在2005年底和2006年初，[法国](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法国 "wikilink")、[芬兰](../Page/芬兰.md "wikilink")、[爱尔兰](../Page/爱尔兰.md "wikilink")、[挪威](../Page/挪威.md "wikilink")和英国推出了咖啡风味的百事零度。 被称为“百事零度 Cappuccino”（英国为“百事零度 Caino”），该产品由类似的Pepsi Kona（1996年在美国进行短暂试销）和百事可乐Tarik，后在2005年在[马来西亚](../Page/马来西亚.md "wikilink")上市。

百事极度于2006年在韩国、保加利亚和菲律宾上市。并于2006年春季重新在阿根廷上市。 2007年初，在埃及、约旦、黎巴嫩和阿联酋上市。

In October 2008, Pepsi announced it would be redesigning its logo and re-branding many of its products. [Pepsi](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pepsi "wikilink"), [Diet Pepsi](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diet_Pepsi "wikilink"), and Pepsi Max all use lower-case fonts for name brands, [Mountain Dew](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mountain_Dew "wikilink") was renamed "Mtn Dew", and Diet Pepsi Max was re-branded as Pepsi Max. The brand's [blue and red globe trademark](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pepsi_Globe "wikilink") became a series of "smiles", with the central white band arcing at different angles depending on the product. The new imagery is being used. In the case of Pepsi Max, besides renaming of the drink its international name, the logo has a large "smile" likely to emphasize the North American drink's "Wake up people\!" advertising campaign, and also uses [black](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/black "wikilink") in the bottom half of the globe as opposed to the more standard [royal blue](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/royal_blue "wikilink"). The new lower-case font used on Pepsi's products is reminiscent of the font used in Diet Pepsi's logo from the 1960s to the mid-1980s. The website for the "Wake up people\!" campaign now redirects to the [Pepsi Refresh Project](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pepsi_Refresh_Project "wikilink"). It is expected that the version of Pepsi Max outside North America will adopt the new logo used by its US–Canada counterpart; this has now occurred in Australia. In the UK, the cans now have the "Pepsi" text and the new Pepsi globe (with the normal Pepsi "smile" and the blue bottom half, as opposed to the black half used in the US) but the "Max" is in the previous style.

A Pepsi Max Lime version was released in the United States in February 2010 under the name "Pepsi Max Cease Fire" It was introduced in the UK in late-2011. It was cross-promoted with a new flavor series of [Doritos](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doritos "wikilink") chips called "3rd Degree Burn". In July 2010, Pepsi began to move its North American branding for Pepsi Max to match its global branding. It now carries a Max typography similar to that used worldwide, and rolled out a new slogan: "Zero Calories. Maximum Pepsi Taste". Its formula has not changed. In May 2011, Pepsi introduced the drink to Spain. In 2016, the drink was introduced to Venezuela. In 2017 Pepsi Max was launched in Costa Rica\[1\]

In 2013, Pepsi Max collaborated with English magician Steven Frayne, also known as [Dynamo](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dynamo_\(magician\) "wikilink"), as part of their 'Live for Now' campaign which was launched the previous year. As part of a launch event, Dynamo was seen in London hanging from the side of a number 543 double-decker bus on its journey along Millbank, past the Houses of Parliament and across Westminster Bridge, while hundreds of passers by watched.\[2\]\[3\] The partnership also saw Dynamo appear in an on-pack promotion on cans and bottles of Pepsi Max during July and August, and secured 19 million unique impressions & 7 million total video views on social media.\[4\]

## 产品定位

Recent UK/Australia Pepsi Max television advertisements have featured the taglines "Maximum taste, no sugar" and "Don't worry, there's no sugar." Some have incorporated [extreme sports](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/extreme_sport "wikilink") and video games such as [Motocross Mania](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motocross_Mania "wikilink") in an attempt to appeal to young men (in contrast to other diet cola drinks, which tend to target young women). The British advertising campaign involved retouched versions of the American "Do the Dew" commercials for [Mountain Dew](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mountain_Dew "wikilink") (A variant of which is sold in the UK ), rebranded as "Live life to the Max".

[Zero_to_Max.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Zero_to_Max.jpg "fig:Zero_to_Max.jpg")\]\] [Coca-Cola Zero](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coca-Cola_Zero_Sugar "wikilink"), a sugar-free cola from [the Coca-Cola Company](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/the_Coca-Cola_Company "wikilink"), is marketed in a similar manner. In the UK some Coke Zero advertising alluded to Pepsi Max, leading to a robust counter-campaign by Pepsi extolling the virtues of the concept of "maximum" over that of "zero."

### 争议

#### 自杀丑闻

In December 2008, advertisements for Pepsi Max that depicted a cartoon character committing [suicide](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/suicide "wikilink") were run in a German lifestyle magazine, which resulted in a scandal and revocation of the ads.\[5\] One of the three ads featured a bean-shaped character simultaneously shooting himself in the head, with a noose round his neck, and poison in his other hand. The character is supposed to be "one very very very lonely calorie".\[6\]\[7\]

## 加拿大特供版

Beginning in early 1994, an entirely different Pepsi Max was marketed in [Canada](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada "wikilink"). Now regarded as a precursor to [Pepsi Edge](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pepsi_Edge "wikilink"), it was sweetened with a combination of aspartame and [high fructose corn syrup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/high_fructose_corn_syrup "wikilink"). As a result, it contained two-thirds fewer calories than full-sugar colas (including regular Pepsi), but more calories than conventional diet/light colas (or the version of Pepsi Max sold elsewhere). The Canadian product was discontinued in 2002; the [Diet Pepsi Max](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diet_Pepsi_Max "wikilink") product introduced in 2008 has no direct relationship to the earlier formulation.

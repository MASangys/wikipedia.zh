**长颈鹿** () 是非洲[哺乳动物](../Page/哺乳动物.md "wikilink")[偶蹄目](../Page/偶蹄目.md "wikilink")下一个[属](../Page/属.md "wikilink")。该属目前仅由一个物种组成，但已经有七种已经灭绝的长颈鹿的化石被发现，目前仅存的努比亚长颈鹿（*Giraffa camelopardalis*）即为该物种的[模式種](../Page/模式種.md "wikilink")，而基于[线粒体DNA](../Page/线粒体DNA.md "wikilink")和核DNA分析和形态学比较，现存的长颈鹿可以被生物分类为一至八个种*,* 但是[IUCN目前只承认长颈鹿为单一个物种](../Page/國際自然保護聯盟.md "wikilink")（但拥有九个亚种）。长颈鹿也是最高的陆生动物和最大的[反芻动物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/反芻 "wikilink")。

长颈鹿的显著特征在于长脖子、长腿、类角的听小骨、以及它们特有的毛发形态。在[生物分类上](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/科_\(生物\) "wikilink")，它与最近的远亲[㺢㹢狓](../Page/㺢㹢狓.md "wikilink")同属[长颈鹿科](../Page/长颈鹿科.md "wikilink")。长颈鹿的分布北起[乍得](../Page/乍得.md "wikilink")，南达[南非](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/南非 "wikilink")，西到[尼日尔](../Page/尼日尔.md "wikilink")，东至[索马里](../Page/索马里.md "wikilink")，常居于[疏林草原或是](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/疏林草原 "wikilink")[疏林](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/疏林 "wikilink")。它们以食用[含羞草亚科](../Page/含羞草亚科.md "wikilink")为主的木本植物的树叶、水果以及花朵为生，此类植物生长于其他草食动物够不到的高度。它们也可能成为[狮](../Page/狮.md "wikilink")、[豹](../Page/豹.md "wikilink")、[斑鬣狗](../Page/斑鬣狗.md "wikilink")和[非洲野犬](../Page/非洲野犬.md "wikilink")的猎物。长颈鹿为群居动物，与配偶子女为群，或与成年雄性同类为群。雄鹿划分社会等级通过以脖子为武器的角斗，其中，优胜者得以与雌鹿交配。雌鹿的唯一责任为养育幼崽。

长颈鹿以其独特的外观和行为成为了从古至今的各种文化的组成元素，经常出现在绘画，书籍和漫画之中。[國際自然保護聯盟](../Page/國際自然保護聯盟.md "wikilink")认定长颈鹿为[易危物種](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/易危物種 "wikilink")，在部分地区已经[局部地區滅絕](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/局部地區滅絕 "wikilink")。长颈鹿是众多[國家公園](../Page/國家公園.md "wikilink")和主题公园的常见动物，2016年的估计表明，野生长颈鹿大约有97,500只，被饲养的约有1,144只。

## 命名

長頸鹿的名字最早起源於[阿拉伯语詞語](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿拉伯语 "wikilink")*zarāfah* (زرافة)，\[1\] 可能取自與[索馬里語](../Page/索馬里語.md "wikilink")中的動物*geri*.\[2\] 阿拉伯語中這個詞的意思是“走得快的東西”。\[3\] 在[中古英语](../Page/中古英语.md "wikilink")中長頸鹿有多種拼法，例如*jarraf*, *ziraph*和*gerfauntz*。\[4\] [意大利语](../Page/意大利语.md "wikilink")中的*giraffa*出現與20世紀90年代。\[5\] 現代英語中的長頸鹿在1600年左右出現，源自[法语](../Page/法语.md "wikilink")中的*girafe*。\[6\] 驼豹（Camelopard） 是古英语中对长颈鹿的称呼，这个词起源于[古希臘語](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/古希臘語 "wikilink")，用于代指拥有[骆驼](../Page/骆驼.md "wikilink")一样的身体和[豹](../Page/豹.md "wikilink")一样的花纹的动物。\[7\]\[8\]

[中国典籍关于长颈鹿的记载](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/中国 "wikilink")，最早出自[晋代李石所著](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/晋代 "wikilink")《续博物志》，记录[非洲](../Page/非洲.md "wikilink")[索马里](../Page/索马里.md "wikilink")沿岸**拨拔力**古国出产的异兽，身高一丈余颈长九尺。[宋代](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/宋代 "wikilink")[赵汝适](../Page/赵汝适.md "wikilink")著《[诸蕃志](../Page/诸蕃志.md "wikilink")》中称[非洲](../Page/非洲.md "wikilink")长颈鹿为**徂蜡**：“状如驼驰，而大如[牛](../Page/牛.md "wikilink")，色黄，前脚高五尺，后低三尺，头高向上。”\[9\][明朝](../Page/明朝.md "wikilink")永乐十二年（1414），时值[郑和第四次出海](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/郑和 "wikilink")，榜葛剌国（今孟加拉）使臣将一只长颈鹿进贡给明成祖，以“**麒麟**”为名，据说乃东非土语中的长颈鹿名称giri之音译\[10\]\[11\]；在中国传说中，[麒麟](../Page/麒麟.md "wikilink")为祥瑞之兽，[明成祖](../Page/明成祖.md "wikilink")便将其视为祥瑞之兆，命人撰文并绘图记录，以此标榜自己施政的伟大。存世的图像记录仅有传沈度所绘的《麒麟图》。\[12\]该典故流传甚广。长颈鹿在[日語及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/日語 "wikilink")[韓語中的名称皆源于麒麟](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/韓語 "wikilink")（日语：キリン）\[13\]，在[閩南話中也称为](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/閩南話 "wikilink")「麒麟鹿」。

## 分类

将现存的长颈鹿曾被[卡尔·林奈](../Page/卡尔·林奈.md "wikilink")于1758年根据[二名法](../Page/二名法.md "wikilink")归入[鹿屬](../Page/鹿屬.md "wikilink") 。但摩顿·思伦·布兰尼奇（Morten Thrane Brünnich）于1772年重㺢㹢狓将其分类为[长颈鹿属](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/长颈鹿属 "wikilink")\[14\] 种名*camelopardalis* 来源于[拉丁语](../Page/拉丁语.md "wikilink").\[15\]

### 演化

长颈鹿是[偶蹄目](../Page/偶蹄目.md "wikilink")[长颈鹿科](../Page/长颈鹿科.md "wikilink")仅有的两个现存的属中的一个，另一个是[㺢㹢狓](../Page/㺢㹢狓.md "wikilink")。长颈鹿科曾经一度分布非常广泛，有超过10个属。他们关系最近的已知的近亲是已经灭绝的类鹿的[梯角鹿科](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/梯角鹿科 "wikilink") 。他们和叉角羚科一样（现仅存的物种只有[叉角羚](../Page/叉角羚.md "wikilink")）都从属于[长颈鹿总科](../Page/长颈鹿科.md "wikilink") 。这些动物很可能是从已经灭绝的[古鹿科演化过来](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/古鹿科 "wikilink")，而古鹿科很可能是[鹿科](../Page/鹿科.md "wikilink")的祖先\[16\]

长颈鹿脖子的延长似乎开始于长颈鹿谱系的早期。与远古时期的近亲相比，现在的长颈鹿接近颅骨的椎骨较早延长，随后椎骨进一步向下延长。\[17\][Canthumeryx是早期长颈鹿的祖先](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canthumeryx "wikilink")，该物种的历史可以追溯到2500万至2000万年前，并且在利比亚找到了他们的化石。该物种是中等大小，体型苗条，形态类似羚羊。[始长颈鹿属出现在](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/始长颈鹿 "wikilink")1500万年前的印度次大陆，类似于霍加或小长颈鹿，有较长的脖子和类似的听小骨。\[18\] 始长颈鹿属可能与与大量的长颈鹿比如：[西瓦鹿](../Page/西瓦鹿.md "wikilink")和[布拉马兽共享同一个进化分支](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/布拉马兽 "wikilink")。\[19\] [Giraffidcomparison.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Giraffidcomparison.jpg "fig:Giraffidcomparison.jpg")（中）生物与㺢㹢狓（下）和现代长颈鹿相比的图片。 萨摩麟属的生物的解剖学特征明显表现出向长颈鹿状颈部的过渡。\[20\]\]\] 长颈鹿科於 1400 萬年前出現，並且居住於歐亞非大陸全境，代表性的有[古長頸鹿](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/古長頸鹿 "wikilink")、[山西獸屬以及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/山西獸屬_\(Shansitherium\) "wikilink")[萨摩麟](../Page/萨摩麟.md "wikilink") 。这些动物的鹿角状突起不长毛，[鼻竇](../Page/鼻竇.md "wikilink")较小，但头骨更长、更宽。\[21\]\[22\][古麟](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/古麟属 "wikilink")（）和[㺢㹢狓](../Page/㺢㹢狓.md "wikilink")很像，可能是后者的祖先物种。\[23\]也有学者认为㺢㹢狓所在的谱系在进化的过程中分离出去的时间还要更早一些，在始长颈鹿之前。\[24\]萨摩麟是长颈鹿谱系中非常重要的[過渡种](../Page/過渡化石.md "wikilink")，因为萨摩麟的颈椎长度在现代长颈鹿和㺢㹢狓之间，又比㺢㹢狓的要更竖直些。\[25\]**生活在距今900万到700万年前，发源于欧洲东南部，可能是现代长颈鹿的直系祖先之一。*Bohlinia*与现代长颈鹿很像，脖子和腿都很长，ossicones和牙列的结构也很接近。\[26\]

*Bohlinia* entered China and northern India in response to climate change. From there, the genus *[长颈鹿](../Page/长颈鹿.md "wikilink")* evolved and, around 7 mya, entered Africa.\[27\] Further climate changes caused the extinction of the Asian giraffes, while the African giraffes survived and radiated into several new species. Living giraffes appear to have arisen around 1 mya in eastern Africa during the [更新世](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/更新世 "wikilink").\[28\] Some biologists suggest the modern giraffes descended from **;\[29\] others find *[G. gracilis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Giraffa_gracilis "wikilink")* a more likely candidate.\[30\] *G. jumae* was larger and more heavily built while *G. gracilis* was smaller and more lightly built. The main driver for the evolution of the giraffes is believed to have been the changes from extensive forests to more open habitats, which began 8 mya.\[31\] During this time, tropical plants disappeared and were replaced by arid [C4类二氧化碳固定](../Page/C4类二氧化碳固定.md "wikilink")s, and a dry savannah emerged across eastern and northern Africa and western India.\[32\]\[33\] Some researchers have hypothesised that this new habitat coupled with a different diet, including acacia species, may have exposed giraffe ancestors to toxins that caused higher mutation rates and a higher rate of evolution.\[34\] The coat patterns of modern giraffes may also have coincided with these habitat changes. Asian giraffes are hypothesised to have had more okapi-like colourations.\[35\]

十九世紀初， [让-巴蒂斯特·拉马克](../Page/让-巴蒂斯特·拉马克.md "wikilink") 相信長頸鹿的長頸為後天性特徵，因世世代代的長頸鹿祖先奮力探向高處樹葉所發展而來。\[36\] 此理論最終被推翻，科學家現在相信長頸鹿的長頸源自達爾文的[自然选择](../Page/自然选择.md "wikilink")—也就是頸部較長的長頸鹿祖先擁有覓食競爭的優勢 (competing browsers hypothesis)\[37\]，使其較易生存繁殖並延續其基因。\[38\]

The giraffe [基因組](../Page/基因組.md "wikilink") is around 29億 [碱基对](../Page/碱基对.md "wikilink")s in length compared to the 3.3 billion base pairs of the okapi. Of the proteins in giraffe and okapi genes, 19.4% are identical. The two species are equally distantly related to cattle, suggesting the giraffe's unique characteristics are not because of faster evolution. The [趨異演化](../Page/趨異演化.md "wikilink") of giraffe and okapi lineages dates to around 11.5 mya. A small group of s in the giraffe appear to be responsible for the animal's stature and associated circulatory adaptations.\[39\]

### 种及亚种

[Genetic_subdivision_in_the_giraffe_based_on_mitochondrial_DNA_sequences.png](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Genetic_subdivision_in_the_giraffe_based_on_mitochondrial_DNA_sequences.png "fig:Genetic_subdivision_in_the_giraffe_based_on_mitochondrial_DNA_sequences.png")关系示意图，由[线粒体DNA](../Page/线粒体DNA.md "wikilink")确定。图中的圆点代表样本采集地。图中右侧的[系統發生樹](../Page/系統發生樹.md "wikilink")是基于266个长颈鹿样本所得出的[最大似然估计](../Page/最大似然估计.md "wikilink")，其中各个分支上的星号表示该[演化支](../Page/演化支.md "wikilink")的超过90%。演化支末端的星形标记表示马赛长颈鹿和网纹长颈鹿的[並系群](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/並系群 "wikilink")。”\[40\]\]\] 世界自然保护联盟目前仅承认具有9个亚种的一个长颈鹿物种。\[41\]\[42\] 在2001年，有人提出了将长颈鹿分为两种物种的分类建议。\[43\]2007年一篇关于长颈鹿遗传学的研究建议将长颈鹿分为6种：西非，罗斯柴尔德，网状，马赛，安哥拉和南非长颈鹿。\[44\]该研究从和[线粒体DNA](../Page/线粒体DNA.md "wikilink")（mtDNA）的遗传差异推论得出，尽管没有自然障碍阻碍不同种类的长颈鹿的相互接近，但这些种群间具有[生殖隔离](../Page/生殖隔离.md "wikilink")，很少杂交。这包括相邻的罗斯柴尔德，网状和马赛长颈鹿种群。研究同时还建议将马赛长颈鹿分为由[东非裂谷隔开的两个物种](../Page/東非大裂谷.md "wikilink")。\[45\]

和源自东非的长颈鹿类似，网纹长颈鹿和马赛长颈鹿拥有最高的线粒体DNA遗传多样性。Populations further north are more closely related to the former, while those to the south are more related to the latter. Giraffes appear to select mates of the same coat type, which are imprinted on them as calves.\[46\] The implications of these findings for the conservation of giraffes were summarised by David Brown, lead author of the study, who told [英國廣播公司新聞](../Page/英國廣播公司新聞.md "wikilink"): "Lumping all giraffes into one species obscures the reality that some kinds of giraffe are on the brink. Some of these populations number only a few hundred individuals and need immediate protection."\[47\]

A 2016 study also concluded that living giraffes consist of multiple species.\[49\] The researchers suggested the existence of four species, which have not exchanged genetic information between each other for 1 million to 2 million years. Those four species are the [努比亚长颈鹿](../Page/努比亚长颈鹿.md "wikilink") (*G. camelopardalis*),  (*G. giraffa*), [网纹长颈鹿](../Page/网纹长颈鹿.md "wikilink") (*G. reticulata*), and  (*G. tippelskirchi*).\[50\] Since then, a response to this publication has been published, highlighting seven problems in data interpretation, and concludes "the conclusions should not be accepted unconditionally".\[51\]

The coat has dark blotches or patches (which can be orange, , brown, or nearly black in colour\[120\]) separated by light hair (usually white or  in colour\[121\]). Male giraffes become darker as they age.\[122\] The coat pattern has been claimed to serve as [偽裝 (動物學)](../Page/偽裝_\(動物學\).md "wikilink") in the light and shade patterns of savannah woodlands.\[123\] While adult giraffes standing among trees and bushes are hard to see at even a few metres' distance, they actively move into the open to gain the best view of an approaching predator, obviating any benefit that camouflage might bring. Instead, the adults rely on their size and ability to defend themselves. However, camouflage appears to be important for calves, which spend a large part of the day in hiding, away from their mothers; further, over half of all calves die within a year, so predation is certainly important. It appears, therefore, that the spotted coat of the giraffe functions as camouflage for the young, while adults simply inherit this coloration as a by-product.\[124\] The skin underneath the dark areas may serve as windows for [體溫調節](../Page/體溫調節.md "wikilink"), being sites for complex blood vessel systems and large sweat glands.\[125\] Each individual giraffe has a unique coat pattern.\[126\]

长颈鹿的皮肤大部分是灰色的。\[127\] 皮肤的厚度允许长颈鹿穿过刺灌木丛而不受伤。\[128\] The fur may serve as a chemical defence, as its parasite repellents give the animal a characteristic scent. At least 11 main [芳香性](../Page/芳香性.md "wikilink") chemicals are in the fur, although [吲哚](../Page/吲哚.md "wikilink") and [糞臭素](../Page/糞臭素.md "wikilink") are responsible for most of the smell. Because the males have a stronger odour than the females, the odour may also have sexual function.\[129\] Along the animal's neck is a mane made of short, erect hairs.\[130\] The one-metre (3.3-ft) tail ends in a long, dark tuft of hair and is used as a defense against insects.\[131\]

### 颅骨和鹿角状突起（ossicones）

长颈鹿两性都有突出的角状结构，称为鹿角状突起（ossicones） which are formed from ossified cartilage, covered in skin and fused to the skull at the [顶骨](../Page/顶骨.md "wikilink")s.\[132\] Being [血管](../Page/血管.md "wikilink")ized, the ossicones may have a role in thermoregulation,\[133\] and are also used in combat between males.\[134\] Appearance is a reliable guide to the sex or age of a giraffe: the ossicones of females and young are thin and display tufts of hair on top, whereas those of adult males end in knobs and tend to be bald on top.\[135\] Also, a median lump, which is more prominent in males, emerges at the front of the [颅骨](../Page/颅骨.md "wikilink").\[136\] Males develop [钙](../Page/钙.md "wikilink") deposits that form bumps on their skulls as they age.\[137\] A giraffe's skull is lightened by multiple [竇 (動物構造)](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/竇_\(動物構造\) "wikilink").\[138\] However, as males age, their skulls become heavier and more club-like, helping them become more dominant in combat.\[139\] The upper jaw has a grooved [腭](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/腭 "wikilink") and lacks front teeth.\[140\] The giraffe's [大臼齿](../Page/大臼齿.md "wikilink")s have a rough surface.\[141\]

### 腿部, 运动系统和形态姿势

The front and back legs of a giraffe are about the same length. The [桡骨](../Page/桡骨.md "wikilink") and [尺骨](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/尺骨 "wikilink") of the front legs are articulated by the [腕骨](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/腕骨 "wikilink"), which, while structurally equivalent to the human wrist, functions as a knee.\[142\] It appears that a  allows the lanky legs to support the animal's great weight.\[143\] The foot of the giraffe reaches a diameter of , and the [蹄](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/蹄 "wikilink") is  high in males and  in females.\[144\] The rear of each hoof is low and the  is close to the ground, allowing the foot to provide additional support to the animal's weight.\[145\] Giraffes lack s and interdigital glands. The giraffe's pelvis, though relatively short, has an [髂骨](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/髂骨 "wikilink") that is outspread at the upper ends.\[146\]

A giraffe has only two s: walking and galloping. Walking is done by moving the legs on one side of the body at the same time, then doing the same on the other side.\[147\] When galloping, the hind legs move around the front legs before the latter move forward,\[148\] and the tail will curl up.\[149\] The animal relies on the forward and backward motions of its head and neck to maintain balance and the counter momentum while galloping.\[150\] The giraffe can reach a sprint speed of up to ,\[151\] and can sustain  for several kilometres.\[152\]

A giraffe rests by lying with its body on top of its folded legs.\[153\] To lie down, the animal kneels on its front legs and then lowers the rest of its body. To get back up, it first gets on its knees and spreads its hind legs to raise its hindquarters. It then straightens its front legs. With each step, the animal swings its head.\[154\] In captivity, the giraffe sleeps intermittently around 4.6 hours per day, mostly at night.\[155\] It usually sleeps lying down, however, standing sleeps have been recorded, particularly in older individuals. Intermittent short "deep sleep" phases while lying are characterised by the giraffe bending its neck backwards and resting its head on the hip or thigh, a position believed to indicate [快速動眼期](../Page/快速動眼期.md "wikilink").\[156\] If the giraffe wants to bend down to drink, it either spreads its front legs or bends its knees.\[157\] Giraffes would probably not be competent swimmers as their long legs would be highly cumbersome in the water,\[158\] although they could possibly float.\[159\] When swimming, the thorax would be weighed down by the front legs, making it difficult for the animal to move its neck and legs in harmony\[160\]\[161\] or keep its head above the surface.\[162\]

[Flickr_-_Rainbirder_-_High-rise_living.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Flickr_-_Rainbirder_-_High-rise_living.jpg "fig:Flickr_-_Rainbirder_-_High-rise_living.jpg")正在金合欢树下仰头进食，拍摄于[肯尼亚](../Page/肯尼亚.md "wikilink")\]\] The giraffe's neck vertebrae have [杵臼關節](../Page/杵臼關節.md "wikilink")s.\[172\] In particular, the – joint (C1 and C2) allows the animal to tilt its head vertically and reach more branches with the tongue.\[173\] The point of articulation between the cervical and thoracic vertebrae of giraffes is shifted to lie between the first and second thoracic vertebrae (T1 and T2), unlike most other ruminants where the articulation is between the seventh cervical vertebra (C7) and T1.\[174\]\[175\] This allows C7 to contribute directly to increased neck length and has given rise to the suggestion that T1 is actually C8, and that giraffes have added an extra cervical vertebra.\[176\] However, this proposition is not generally accepted, as T1 has other morphological features, such as an articulating [肋骨](../Page/肋骨.md "wikilink"), deemed diagnostic of thoracic vertebrae, and because exceptions to the mammalian limit of seven cervical vertebrae are generally characterised by increased  and maladies.\[177\]

There are several hypotheses regarding the evolutionary origin and maintenance of elongation in giraffe necks.\[178\] The "competing  hypothesis" was originally suggested by [查尔斯·达尔文](../Page/查尔斯·达尔文.md "wikilink") and challenged only recently. It suggests that competitive pressure from smaller browsers, such as , [小岩羚](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/小岩羚 "wikilink") and [高角羚](../Page/高角羚.md "wikilink"), encouraged the elongation of the neck, as it enabled giraffes to reach food that competitors could not. This advantage is real, as giraffes can and do feed up to  high, while even quite large competitors, such as kudu, can feed up to only about  high.\[179\] There is also research suggesting that browsing competition is intense at lower levels, and giraffes feed more efficiently (gaining more leaf biomass with each mouthful) high in the canopy.\[180\]\[181\] However, scientists disagree about just how much time giraffes spend feeding at levels beyond the reach of other browsers,\[182\]\[183\]\[184\]\[185\] and a 2010 study found that adult giraffes with longer necks actually suffered higher mortality rates under drought conditions than their shorter-necked counterparts. This study suggests that maintaining a longer neck requires more nutrients, which puts longer-necked giraffes at risk during a food shortage.\[186\]

Another theory, the [性選擇](../Page/性選擇.md "wikilink") hypothesis, proposes that the long necks evolved as a secondary [两性异形](../Page/两性异形.md "wikilink"), giving males an advantage in "necking" contests (see below) to establish dominance and obtain access to sexually receptive females.\[187\] In support of this theory, necks are longer and heavier for males than females of the same age,\[188\]\[189\] and the former do not employ other forms of combat.\[190\] However, one objection is that it fails to explain why female giraffes also have long necks.\[191\] It has also been proposed that the neck serves to give the animal greater vigilance.\[192\]\[193\]

## 行为与生态

### 栖息地和饮食

[Giraffe_feeding,_Tanzania.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Giraffe_feeding,_Tanzania.jpg "fig:Giraffe_feeding,_Tanzania.jpg") Giraffes usually inhabit [疏林草原s](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/疏林草原 "wikilink") and open [疏林s](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/疏林 "wikilink"). They prefer [含羞草亚科](../Page/含羞草亚科.md "wikilink"), *[没药属](../Page/没药属.md "wikilink")*, *[风车藤属](../Page/风车藤属.md "wikilink")* and open *[榄仁树属](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/榄仁树属 "wikilink")* woodlands over denser environments like ** woodlands.\[212\] The Angolan giraffe can be found in desert environments.\[213\] Giraffes browse on the twigs of trees, preferring trees of the subfamily Acacieae and the genera *Commiphora* and *Terminalia*,\[214\] which are important sources of calcium and protein to sustain the giraffe's growth rate.\[215\] They also feed on shrubs, grass and fruit.\[216\] A giraffe eats around  of foliage daily.\[217\] When stressed, giraffes may chew the bark off branches. Although [食草动物](../Page/食草动物.md "wikilink"), the giraffe has been known to visit carcasses and lick dried meat off bones.\[218\]

在雨季，食物丰富，长颈鹿分布更广，而在旱季，它们聚集在剩下的常绿乔木和灌木丛周围。\[219\] Mothers tend to feed in open areas, presumably to make it easier to detect predators, although this may reduce their feeding efficiency.\[220\] As a [反芻](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/反芻 "wikilink"), the giraffe first chews its food, then swallows it for processing and then visibly passes the half-digested cud up the neck and back into the mouth to chew again.\[221\] It is common for a giraffe to salivate while feeding.\[222\] The giraffe requires less food than many other herbivores because the foliage it eats has more concentrated nutrients and it has a more efficient digestive system.\[223\] The animal's faeces come in the form of small pellets.\[224\] When it has access to water, a giraffe drinks at intervals no longer than three days.\[225\]

Giraffes have a great effect on the trees that they feed on, delaying the growth of young trees for some years and giving "waistlines" to trees that are too tall.\[226\] Feeding is at its highest during the first and last hours of daytime. Between these hours, giraffes mostly stand and ruminate. Rumination is the dominant activity during the night, when it is mostly done lying down.\[227\]

### 社会性

[Giraffe_(Giraffa_camelopardalis)_females.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Giraffe_\(Giraffa_camelopardalis\)_females.jpg "fig:Giraffe_(Giraffa_camelopardalis)_females.jpg")Tswalu Kalahari保护区内的一群南部长颈鹿。长颈鹿通常聚集成群。\]\] 长颈鹿通常成不同规模的群出现，一般这些群体的组成被认为是开放且不断变化的。\[228\] 长颈鹿曾被认为具有很少的社交纽带，出于研究目的，“群体”被定义为“相距不足一公里且朝着相同方向移动的个体集合。”\[229\] 最近的研究发现长颈鹿确实具有长期的社交关系，并可能根据亲属，性别或其他因素形成群体或成对。这些群体可能经常在中与较大群体或子群体相互联系。\[230\]\[231\]\[232\] 同一群体的长颈鹿数量最多可达到44只。\[233\]  长颈鹿群体倾向于性别隔离，\[234\] 尽管已知存在由成年雌性和年轻雄性组成的混合性别群体。特别稳定的长颈鹿群体是由母亲和年幼的长颈鹿组成的群体，\[235\] 并且可以持续数周或数月。\[236\] 这些群体中个体之间的社交凝聚力是由幼年个体之间形成的纽带维系的。\[237\]\[238\] 雌性的交往似乎是以为基础的，并且个体之间可能由[母系制度](../Page/母系制度.md "wikilink")联系。 \[239\] 一般来说，雌性比雄性更倾向于与同性交往。\[240\] 年轻的雄性也会形成一个群体，并会进行打斗。但是，随着年龄的增长，雄性个体变得孤僻，但也有可能与雌性结对或成群。\[241\]\[242\] 长颈鹿不是 [领域 (动物)](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/领域_\(动物\) "wikilink"),\[243\] 但是它们有自己的 。\[244\] 雄性长颈鹿偶尔游荡到远离它们通常常去的地方。\[245\]

尽管长颈鹿通常安静无声，但长颈鹿被听到使用各种声音进行交流。在求爱过程中，雄性会发出大声的咳嗽声。\[246\] 雌性用吼叫来呼唤它们的幼崽。幼年长颈鹿会发出呼噜声、叫声、呻吟声和喵喵声。长颈鹿也会打鼾、嘶嘶、呻吟、咕噜，发出长笛般的声音，\[247\]\[248\] 而且可能会使用[次声波](../Page/次声波.md "wikilink")进行远距离交流\[249\]——尽管这是有争议的。\[250\] 在夜间，长颈鹿似乎会在次声范围以上互相，目的不明。\[251\]

### 繁殖和父母监护行为

Reproduction in giraffes is broadly [多配偶制](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/多配偶制 "wikilink"): a few older males mate with the fertile females. Male giraffes assess female fertility by tasting the female's urine to detect [動情週期](../Page/動情週期.md "wikilink"), in a multi-step process known as the [裂唇嗅反應](../Page/裂唇嗅反應.md "wikilink").\[252\]\[253\] Males prefer young adult females over juveniles and older adults.\[254\] Once an oestrous female is detected, the male will attempt to court her. When courting, dominant males will keep subordinate ones at bay.\[255\] A courting male may lick a female's tail, rest his head and neck on her body or nudge her with his horns. During copulation, the male stands on his hind legs with his head held up and his front legs resting on the female's sides.\[256\]

Giraffe  lasts 400–460 days, after which a single calf is normally born, although twins occur on rare occasions.\[257\] The mother gives birth standing up. The calf emerges head and front legs first, having broken through the s, and falls to the ground, severing the [脐带](../Page/脐带.md "wikilink").\[258\] The mother then grooms the newborn and helps it stand up.\[259\] A newborn giraffe is  tall.\[260\]\[261\]\[262\] Within a few hours of birth, the calf can run around and is almost indistinguishable from a one-week-old. However, for the first 1–3 weeks, it spends most of its time hiding;\[263\] its coat pattern providing camouflage. The ossicones, which have lain flat while it was in the womb, become erect within a few days.\[264\] [Giraffe_Family.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Giraffe_Family.jpg "fig:Giraffe_Family.jpg")的马赛长颈鹿幼崽和成年雌性在进食。抚养后代的主要是雌性长颈鹿。

长颈鹿家庭经常出现在在人类的苗圃中。\]\] Mothers with calves will gather in nursery herds, moving or browsing together. Mothers in such a group may sometimes leave their calves with one female while they forage and drink elsewhere. This is known as a "".\[265\] Adult males play almost no role in raising the young,\[266\] although they appear to have friendly interactions.\[267\] Calves are at risk of predation, and a mother giraffe will stand over her calf and kick at an approaching predator.\[268\] Females watching calving pools will only alert their own young if they detect a disturbance, although the others will take notice and follow.\[269\]

长颈鹿后代与母亲在一起的时间长短不一，尽管可以一直呆到雌性下一次产犊。\[270\] 小长颈鹿的哺乳期一般从最短一个月\[271\] 到最长一年。\[272\]\[273\] 雌性长颈鹿四岁时性成熟，雄性在四到五岁成熟。雄性长颈鹿的[精子发生](../Page/精子发生.md "wikilink")在三到四岁已经开始了。\[274\] 但是雄性必须等到至少七岁才有机会交配。\[275\]\[276\]

### 脖击

[Giraffe_Ithala_KZN_South_Africa_Luca_Galuzzi_2004.JPG](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Giraffe_Ithala_KZN_South_Africa_Luca_Galuzzi_2004.JPG "fig:Giraffe_Ithala_KZN_South_Africa_Luca_Galuzzi_2004.JPG")的[夸祖魯-納塔爾省](../Page/夸祖魯-納塔爾省.md "wikilink")野生动物保护区，雄性南部长颈鹿通过低强度脖击来展示自己优势地位。\]\] [Giraffe-Necking-Etosha.JPG](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Giraffe-Necking-Etosha.JPG "fig:Giraffe-Necking-Etosha.JPG")的一对冲突中的南部长颈鹿，他们采用了更积极的脖击方式。\]\] 雄性长颈鹿在战斗中以脖子为武器，这种行为被称为“脖击”。脖击用于建立支配地位，赢得脖击的雄性具有更大的生殖成功率。\[277\] This behaviour occurs at low or high intensity. In low intensity necking, the combatants rub and lean against each other. The male that can hold itself more erect wins the bout. In high intensity necking, the combatants will spread their front legs and swing their necks at each other, attempting to land blows with their ossicones. The contestants will try to dodge each other's blows and then get ready to counter. The power of a blow depends on the weight of the skull and the arc of the swing.\[278\] A necking duel can last more than half an hour, depending on how well matched the combatants are.\[279\] Although most fights do not lead to serious injury, there have been records of broken jaws, broken necks, and even deaths.\[280\]

After a duel, it is common for two male giraffes to caress and court each other. Such interactions between males have been found to be more frequent than heterosexual coupling.\[281\] In one study, up to 94 percent of observed mounting incidents took place between males. The proportion of same-sex activities varied from 30–75 percent. Only one percent of same-sex mounting incidents occurred between females.\[282\]

## 与人类的联系

[Giraffe_cave_art.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Giraffe_cave_art.jpg "fig:Giraffe_cave_art.jpg")描绘长颈鹿的古老[岩画](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/石洞壁画 "wikilink")\]\] 人类与长颈鹿相处的历史已经有数千年。南非的[萨恩人](../Page/萨恩人.md "wikilink") 有将医疗舞蹈（巫舞）命名为动物的传统，其中就有治疗头部疾病的长颈鹿舞。\[301\]长颈鹿为何长得这么高一直是非洲[民俗](../Page/民俗.md "wikilink")中热议的话题,\[302\] 比如东非某民俗认为这是因为长颈鹿吃太多神奇的草药。\[303\]在整个非洲范围内，长颈鹿是重要的艺术元素，尤其是早期文明比如, [古埃及](../Page/古埃及.md "wikilink")，[麦罗埃](../Page/麦罗埃.md "wikilink")和[努比亚](../Page/努比亚.md "wikilink")。\[304\] 人曾制作了一个包含两个长颈鹿的等身大小的岩石雕刻，这也是世界上最大的岩石岩画.\[305\]\[306\] 古埃及人为长颈鹿创造了[圣书体写法𓃱](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/圣书体 "wikilink")， 在[埃及语](../Page/埃及语.md "wikilink")中代表“sr”之后又代表 “mmy”。\[307\] 古埃及人还将长颈鹿作为宠物饲养并将它们运送到地中海沿岸各地。\[308\] [Tribute_Giraffe_with_Attendant.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Tribute_Giraffe_with_Attendant.jpg "fig:Tribute_Giraffe_with_Attendant.jpg")陈璋根据[明代](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/明代 "wikilink")[沈度的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/沈度 "wikilink")《[瑞应麒麟图》临摹而成的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/瑞应麒麟图 "wikilink")《榜葛剌进麒麟图》，现藏于中国国家博物馆。该图描绘[永乐十二年](../Page/永乐_\(明朝\).md "wikilink")（1414年）榜葛剌（今孟加拉）使臣向明成祖进贡长颈鹿的场景。\]\] 在[古希腊](../Page/古希腊.md "wikilink")和[古罗马](../Page/古罗马.md "wikilink")的记述中也存在长颈鹿，但长颈鹿被认为是骆驼和猎豹的不自然杂交体。\[309\] 长颈鹿是被收集并被送回罗马展览的众多动物之一。已知第一只被带到罗马对公共展览的长颈鹿是公元前46年[尤利烏斯·凱撒](../Page/尤利烏斯·凱撒.md "wikilink")带回的。\[310\] 随着[罗马帝国的衰落](../Page/罗马帝国的衰落.md "wikilink"), 在欧洲被饲养的长颈鹿逐渐绝种\[311\] [中世纪](../Page/中世纪.md "wikilink")期间，欧洲人对于长颈鹿的认识主要来自于和阿拉伯人的交流，阿拉伯人非常独特地尊重长颈鹿，\[312\]

在各地古代史中，被圈养的长颈鹿（数量并不多）一般享有崇高的地位。1414年，一只长颈鹿被从[馬林迪运至](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/馬林迪 "wikilink")[孟加拉 (地區)](../Page/孟加拉_\(地區\).md "wikilink")。之后被[郑和下西洋](../Page/郑和下西洋.md "wikilink")的船队带回并安置在[明朝](../Page/明朝.md "wikilink")的皇室动物园。这种动物令中国人着迷，他们将其与神话中的[麒麟](../Page/麒麟.md "wikilink")联系在一起。\[313\] 1486年，一只被命名为美第奇长颈鹿（）的长颈鹿被敬献给[洛伦佐·德·美第奇](../Page/洛伦佐·德·美第奇.md "wikilink")，它一抵达[佛罗伦萨](../Page/佛罗伦萨.md "wikilink")便引起了轰动。\[314\] 另外一只著名的长颈鹿，, 在十九世纪早期，被[穆罕默德·阿里帕夏](../Page/穆罕默德·阿里帕夏.md "wikilink")送给[查理十世 (法兰西)](../Page/查理十世_\(法兰西\).md "wikilink")。这只长颈鹿在巴黎了引起了轰动，市面上一时流行大量的纪念品，甚至有词汇"giraffanalia"来专门描述它。\[315\]

长颈鹿继续在现代文化中占有一席之地。艺术家[萨尔瓦多·达利](../Page/萨尔瓦多·达利.md "wikilink")在他的超现实主义绘画中将长颈鹿描绘得一种拥有燃烧的鬃毛的生物。达利认为长颈鹿是男子气概的象征，而燃烧的长颈鹿则是“男性宇宙的末日怪物（masculine cosmic apocalyptic monster）”。\[316\] 长颈鹿也是儿童读物中常见的元素，包括大卫·A·乌弗的《怕高的长颈鹿（The Giraffe Who Was Afraid of Heights）》；吉尔斯·安德烈的《长颈鹿不会跳舞（Giraffes Can't Dance）》； [羅爾德·達爾](../Page/羅爾德·達爾.md "wikilink")的《长颈鹿，佩利和我（The Giraffe and the Pelly and Me）》。长颈鹿多次作为动画中的次要角色出现在[华特迪士尼公司](../Page/华特迪士尼公司.md "wikilink")的动画电影中，比如《[狮子王](../Page/狮子王.md "wikilink")》和《[小飞象](../Page/小飞象.md "wikilink")》，并在《[野蛮任务](../Page/野蛮任务.md "wikilink")》和《[馬達加斯加系列電影](../Page/馬達加斯加系列電影.md "wikilink")》中扮演相当重要的角色。苏菲小鹿宝宝（）自1961年推出以来，都是[奶嘴的热门产品](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/奶嘴 "wikilink")。另一个著名的长颈鹿玩具是玩具反斗城的吉祥物 ”R“\[317\]

长颈鹿也已经用于一些科学实验和研究。科学家在为宇航员和战斗机飞行员研制服装的时候广泛研究了长颈鹿的特征，\[318\] 因为这些人士因为处于高重力加速度环境有血液急速流向下半身的危险。 计算机科学家利用反应-扩散机制对几种长颈鹿的外皮模式进行了建模。\[319\]

鹿豹座的星座，于十七世纪问世，描绘的就是一只长颈鹿。\[320\] [茨瓦纳人](../Page/茨瓦纳人.md "wikilink")的传统将[南十字座](../Page/南十字座.md "wikilink")看作两只长颈鹿。其中[十字架二](../Page/十字架二.md "wikilink")和[十字架三](../Page/十字架三.md "wikilink")组成了一只雄性，而[十字架一](../Page/十字架一.md "wikilink")和[十字架四](../Page/十字架四.md "wikilink")构成了雌性长颈鹿。\[321\]

### 开发及保护状况

[Bundesarchiv_Bild_105-DOA0377,_Deutsch-Ostafrika,_Giraffe.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Bundesarchiv_Bild_105-DOA0377,_Deutsch-Ostafrika,_Giraffe.jpg "fig:Bundesarchiv_Bild_105-DOA0377,_Deutsch-Ostafrika,_Giraffe.jpg")被当地部落猎杀的马赛长颈鹿（ ）\]\] 长颈鹿是整个非洲的猎人的共同目标。\[322\]被猎杀后，它们身体的各部分都有不同用途：\[323\]肉主要用于食用；尾毛主要用于制作苍蝇拍，手镯，项链和线；\[324\]\[325\] 皮肤可以被制成盾牌，凉鞋和鼓；部分的筋被用于制作乐器的弦；\[326\] 传统[布干达](../Page/布干达.md "wikilink")的医师使用燃烧长颈鹿皮肤产生的烟雾来治疗流鼻血。\[327\]位于苏丹的Humr人常常饮用认为蕴藏长颈鹿灵魂的Umm Nyolokhh饮料，这种液体主要由长颈鹿的肝脏和骨髓制作，通常包含[二甲基色胺](../Page/二甲基色胺.md "wikilink") 和长颈鹿吃的植物中所包含的精神活性物质，例如长颈鹿常见食物金合欢中一些生物碱已经被证实会引起长颈鹿的幻觉。\[328\]\[329\] 在19世纪，欧洲探险家开始将猎杀长颈鹿作为一种打猎运动。\[330\]

栖息地破坏也影响了长颈鹿的生存：在[萨赫勒](../Page/萨赫勒.md "wikilink")，对牲畜的放牧空间和木柴的需求导致了[森林砍伐](../Page/森林砍伐.md "wikilink")。但通常，长颈鹿可以很好与牲畜共存，因为它们不直接与牲畜竞争。\[331\]2017年，肯尼亚北部的严重干旱导致牧场稀缺的局势加剧，牧民因此杀害了大量野生动物，长颈鹿种群尤其受到打击。\[332\]

航空测量法是监测非洲广袤地区长颈鹿种群情况的最方便也是最常用的方法，但空中方法会低估长颈鹿的数量。\[333\] 地面调查法相对更加准确，与航空调查结合使用的话，可以准确估算种群规模和趋势.\[334\]2010年，长颈鹿被[國際自然保護聯盟](../Page/國際自然保護聯盟.md "wikilink") （IUCN)评估为[无危物种](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/无危物种 "wikilink")，但在2016年又被认定为[易危物種](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/易危物種 "wikilink")。\[335\] 在一些特定地区包括[厄立特里亚](../Page/厄立特里亚.md "wikilink")，[几内亚](../Page/几内亚.md "wikilink")，[毛里塔尼亚](../Page/毛里塔尼亚.md "wikilink")和[塞内加尔](../Page/塞内加尔.md "wikilink")长颈鹿已经[局部地區滅絕](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/局部地區滅絕 "wikilink")。长颈鹿也有可能从[安哥拉](../Page/安哥拉.md "wikilink")，[马里](../Page/马里.md "wikilink")和[奈及利亞](../Page/奈及利亞.md "wikilink")消失， 但是长颈鹿已经被引进到[卢旺达](../Page/卢旺达.md "wikilink")和[斯威士兰](../Page/斯威士兰.md "wikilink")。\[336\] 长颈鹿的两个亚种，西非长颈鹿和[羅氏長頸鹿](../Page/羅氏長頸鹿.md "wikilink")已经被认定为[瀕危物種](../Page/瀕危物種.md "wikilink")，\[337\]\[338\] 因为它们的野外种群数量都已经只剩数百。

1997年，认为努比亚长颈鹿是最受威胁的长颈鹿；\[339\] 2010年不精确的估计显示，该模式种数量已经少于250。私人野生动物保护区对于保护非洲南部的长颈鹿种群起来很大帮助\[340\] 比如长颈鹿庄园（Giraffe Manor）就是[奈洛比](../Page/奈洛比.md "wikilink")的热门酒店，也是罗氏长颈鹿的避难所。

**1963年南美自由盃**是第 4 屆[南美自由盃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/南美自由盃 "wikilink")，為[南美洲](../Page/南美洲.md "wikilink")最頂級的[足球](../Page/足球.md "wikilink")球會賽事，由[南美洲足球協會](../Page/南美洲足球協會.md "wikilink")主辦。

## 第一圈

Eight teams were drawn into two groups of three and one group of two. In each group, teams played against each other home-and-away. The top team in each group advanced to the Semifinals. [山度士](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/山度士足球會 "wikilink"), the title holders, had a [bye](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/bye_\(sports\) "wikilink") to the next round.

**1964年南美自由盃**是第 5 屆[南美自由盃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/南美自由盃 "wikilink")，為[南美洲](../Page/南美洲.md "wikilink")最頂級的[足球](../Page/足球.md "wikilink")球會賽事，由[南美洲足球協會](../Page/南美洲足球協會.md "wikilink")主辦。

## 外圍賽

Due to the uneven amount of teams in the competition, a preliminary round was created to determine who joined the other 9 teams in the competition. As a predominated "weak" side, Venezuela has its representative face off against [Brazil](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazil "wikilink")'s second representative. The winner of the series would advance into the First round. Both matches took place in Caracas.

## 第一圈

Nine teams were drawn into groups of three. In each group, teams played against each other home-and-away. The top team in each group advanced to the Semifinals. [山度士](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/山度士足球會 "wikilink"), the title holders, had a [bye](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/bye_\(sports\) "wikilink") to the next round.

**1965年南美自由盃**是第 6
屆[南美自由盃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/南美自由盃 "wikilink")，為[南美洲最頂級的](../Page/南美洲.md "wikilink")[足球球會賽事](../Page/足球.md "wikilink")，由[南美洲足球協會主辦](../Page/南美洲足球協會.md "wikilink")。

## 第一圈

Nine teams were drawn into three groups. In each group, teams played
against each other home-and-away. The top team in each group advanced to
the Semifinals. [獨立隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/獨立競技俱樂部 "wikilink"),
the title holders, had a
[bye](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/bye_\(sports\) "wikilink") to the
next round.

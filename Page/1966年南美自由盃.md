## 第一圈

Sixteen teams were drawn into two groups of six and one group of four. In each group, teams played against each other home-and-away. The top two teams in each group advanced to the Semifinals. [獨立隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/獨立競技俱樂部 "wikilink"), the title holders, had a [bye](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/bye_\(sports\) "wikilink") to the next round.

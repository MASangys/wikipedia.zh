## 第一圈

Sixteen teams were drawn into two groups of six and one group of four.
In each group, teams played against each other home-and-away. The top
two teams in each group advanced to the Semifinals.
[獨立隊](../Page/獨立競技俱樂部.md "wikilink"), the title
holders, had a [bye](../Page/bye_\(sports\).md "wikilink") to the next
round.

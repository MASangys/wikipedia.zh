## 第一圈

Sixteen teams were drawn into two groups of six and one group of five. In each group, teams played against each other home-and-away. The top two teams in each group advanced to the Second round. [彭拿路](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/佩那羅爾足球俱樂部 "wikilink"), the title holders, had a [bye](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/bye_\(sports\) "wikilink") to the next round.

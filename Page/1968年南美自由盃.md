## 第一圈

Twenty teams were drawn into five groups of four. In each group, teams
played against each other home-and-away. The top two teams in each group
advanced to the Second round. [競賽會](../Page/競賽俱樂部.md "wikilink"), the
title holders, had a [bye](../Page/bye_\(sports\).md "wikilink") to the
Semifinals.

## 第一圈

Twenty teams were drawn into five groups of four. In each group, teams played against each other home-and-away. The top two teams in each group advanced to the Second round. [競賽會](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/競賽俱樂部 "wikilink"), the title holders, had a [bye](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/bye_\(sports\) "wikilink") to the Semifinals.

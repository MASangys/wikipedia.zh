**1989年南美自由盃**是第 30
屆[南美自由盃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/南美自由盃 "wikilink")，為[南美洲最頂級的](../Page/南美洲.md "wikilink")[足球球會賽事](../Page/足球.md "wikilink")，由[南美洲足球協會主辦](../Page/南美洲足球協會.md "wikilink")。

## 分組賽

Twenty teams were divided into five groups of four teams each for the
Group Stage. The top three teams of each group, as well as [Nacional of
Uruguay](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Club_Nacional_de_Football "wikilink")
(who received a bye as winners of the **Copa Libertadores 1988** of the
tournament) qualified to the Round of 16.

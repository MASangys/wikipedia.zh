**1992年南美自由盃**是第 33 屆[南美自由盃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/南美自由盃 "wikilink")，為[南美洲](../Page/南美洲.md "wikilink")最頂級的[足球](../Page/足球.md "wikilink")球會賽事，由[南美洲足球協會](../Page/南美洲足球協會.md "wikilink")主辦。

## 分組賽

### 第 1 組

[高路高路](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/科洛科洛競技俱樂部 "wikilink"), as the previous year champions, should join the competition only in the Round of 16, but they requested to start the competition in the group stage, due to financial reasons, thus five clubs participated in this group.\[1\]

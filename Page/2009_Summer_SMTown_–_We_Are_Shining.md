***2009 Summer SM Town - We are Shining*** 是[SM
Town第六張夏日合輯](../Page/SM_Town.md "wikilink")，除了這是[SHINee第一次參與合輯錄製外](../Page/SHINee.md "wikilink")，還是第一張[SM
Town合輯只收錄男子組合](../Page/SM_Town.md "wikilink")─[東方神起](../Page/東方神起.md "wikilink")、[Super
Junior和](../Page/Super_Junior.md "wikilink")[SHINee的作品](../Page/SHINee.md "wikilink")。

## 曲目列表

1.  Seaside 휴게소 (Boom Boom) - [東方神起](../Page/東方神起.md "wikilink"), [Super
    Junior](../Page/Super_Junior.md "wikilink"),
    [SHINee](../Page/SHINee.md "wikilink")
2.  12시 34분 (Nothing Better) - [東方神起](../Page/東方神起.md "wikilink")
3.  카니발 (Carnival) - [Super Junior](../Page/Super_Junior.md "wikilink")
4.  SCAR - [SHINee](../Page/SHINee.md "wikilink")

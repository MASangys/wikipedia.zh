**2010年大西洋颶風季**泛指在2010年全年內的任何時間，於[大西洋水域所產生的](../Page/大西洋.md "wikilink")[熱帶氣旋](../Page/熱帶氣旋.md "wikilink")。雖然有關方面並沒有設下本颱風季的指定期限，但大部份於[大西洋的](../Page/大西洋.md "wikilink")[熱帶氣旋通常都會於](../Page/熱帶氣旋.md "wikilink")[五月至](../Page/五月.md "wikilink")[十二月期間形成](../Page/十二月.md "wikilink")。本季的第一個被[美國國家颶風中心升格的](../Page/美國國家颶風中心.md "wikilink")[熱帶氣旋在](../Page/熱帶氣旋.md "wikilink")6月25日形成，而第一個被命名的風暴颶風
Alex 則在6月27日被[美國國家颶風中心升為熱帶風暴](../Page/美國國家颶風中心.md "wikilink")。

The table on the right shows the
[ACE](../Page/Accumulated_Cyclone_Energy.md "wikilink") for each storm
in the season. Broadly speaking, the ACE is a measure of the power of a
hurricane multiplied by the length of time it existed, so storms that
last a long time, as well as particularly strong hurricanes, have high
ACEs. ACE is calculated for only full advisories on specifically
tropical systems reaching or exceeding wind speeds of 34 knots (39 mph,
63 km/h), or tropical storm strength. Accordingly, tropical depressions
are not included here. The ACE also does not include subtropical
storms:\[1\] for example, values for Hurricane Otto do not include the
advisories when it was classified as a [subtropical
storm](../Page/subtropical_storm.md "wikilink"). Hurricane Igor's ACE is
the highest for any Atlantic storm since [Hurricane
Ivan](../Page/Hurricane_Ivan.md "wikilink") in the [2004
season](../Page/2004_Atlantic_hurricane_season.md "wikilink"), as Igor
was strong and long-lasting.

the North Atlantic during 2010. Retired names, if any, will be announced
by the [World Meteorological
Organization](../Page/World_Meteorological_Organization.md "wikilink")
in the spring of 2011. The names not retired from this list will be used
again in the 2016 season. This is the same list used in the [2004
season](../Page/2004_Atlantic_hurricane_season.md "wikilink") with the

[Ivan](../Page/Hurricane_Ivan.md "wikilink"), and
[Jeanne](../Page/Hurricane_Jeanne.md "wikilink"), respectively.\[2\]

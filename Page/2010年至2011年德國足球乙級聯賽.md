**2010年至2011年德國足球乙級聯賽**（）是[德國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/德國 "wikilink")[足球聯賽](../Page/足球.md "wikilink")[第二級的](../Page/德國足球聯賽系統.md "wikilink")[德國足球乙級聯賽的第](../Page/德國足球乙級聯賽.md "wikilink")37個球季，於2010年8月21日週未展開，直到2011年5月15日結束。歇冬期由2010年12月18日週未至2011年1月15日\[1\]。

## 概覽

As in the previous year, the league comprise the teams placed fourth
through fifteenth of the [2009–10
season](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2009–10_2._Fußball-Bundesliga "wikilink"),
the worst two teams from the [2009–10
Bundesliga](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2009–10_Fußball-Bundesliga "wikilink"),
the best two teams from the [2009–10 3rd
Liga](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2009–10_3rd_Liga "wikilink"), the
losers of the [Bundesliga relegation
play-off](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2009–10_Fußball-Bundesliga#Relegation_play-off "wikilink")
between the 16th-placed Bundesliga team and the third-placed 2nd
Bundesliga team and the winners of the [2nd Bundesliga relegation
play-off](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2009–10_2._Fußball-Bundesliga#Relegation_play-offs "wikilink")
between the 16th-placed 2nd Bundesliga team and the third-placed 3rd
Liga team.

[2009–10 2.
Fußball-Bundesliga](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2009–10_2._Fußball-Bundesliga "wikilink")
champions [1. FC
Kaiserslautern](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/1._FC_Kaiserslautern "wikilink"),
and runners-up [FC St.
Pauli](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/FC_St._Pauli "wikilink") were
promoted to the
[Bundesliga](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fußball-Bundesliga "wikilink").
They were replaced by [VfL
Bochum](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/VfL_Bochum "wikilink") and [Hertha
BSC](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hertha_BSC "wikilink") who finished
17th and 18th respectively in the [2009–10 Bundesliga
season](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2009–10_Fußball-Bundesliga "wikilink").

[TuS Koblenz](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/TuS_Koblenz "wikilink") and
[Rot-Weiß
Ahlen](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rot_Weiss_Ahlen "wikilink") were
relegated after the [2009–10
season](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2009–10_2._Fußball-Bundesliga "wikilink").
They were replaced by [2009–10 3rd
Liga](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2009–10_3rd_Liga "wikilink")
champions [VfL
Osnabrück](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/VfL_Osnabrück "wikilink") and
runners-up [FC Erzgebirge
Aue](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/FC_Erzgebirge_Aue "wikilink").

Two further spots were available through relegation/promotion play-offs
and taken by [FC
Augsburg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/FC_Augsburg "wikilink") and [FC
Ingolstadt
04](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/FC_Ingolstadt_04 "wikilink"). Augsburg
lost in their promotion play-off against 16th placed Bundesliga team [1.
FC Nuremberg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/1._FC_Nuremberg "wikilink")
and thus retained their 2. Bundesliga spot, while Ingolstadt earned
promotion from the 3rd Liga by defeating [FC Hansa
Rostock](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/FC_Hansa_Rostock "wikilink").

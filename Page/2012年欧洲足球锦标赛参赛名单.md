出场次数、进球数截至2012年5月30日，年龄截至2012年6月8日揭幕战。

## A组

###

主教练:  [弗兰克茨克·斯穆达](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/弗兰克茨克·斯穆达 "wikilink")（Franciszek Smuda）

2012年5月27日, 波兰主帅[斯穆达确定了欧锦赛的参赛名单](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/斯穆达 "wikilink")，[阿森纳门将](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿森纳 "wikilink")[法比安斯基因为肩部受伤退出](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法比安斯基 "wikilink")\[1\]。

###

Head coach:  [Fernando Santos](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fernando_Santos_\(Portuguese_footballer\) "wikilink")

Santos named his first shortlist, composed of players based abroad, on 10 May 2012, and the second, composed of Greece-based players on 17 May 2012, totalling a 25-player provisional list.\[2\] On 28 May 2012, Santos announced his 23-man squad\[3\]

###

Head coach:  [Dick Advocaat](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dick_Advocaat "wikilink")

On 11 May 2012, Advocaat named a provisional list of 26 players for the tournament.\[4\] [Vasili Berezutski](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vasili_Berezutski "wikilink") and [Roman Shishkin](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_Shishkin "wikilink") withdrew on 20 and 24 May respectively, Berezutski with a thigh injury and Shishkin due to a stomach complaint.\[5\]\[6\] [Artem Dzyuba](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artem_Dzyuba "wikilink") and [Magomed Ozdoyev](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magomed_Ozdoyev "wikilink") were excluded from the squad after a friendly game on 25 May, and Shishkin was replaced by Nababkin, thus finalizing the squad.\[7\]

###

Head coach:  [米哈尔·比莱克](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/米哈尔·比莱克 "wikilink")

Michal Bílek announced his 24-man provisional squad on 14 May.\[8\] On 28 May, he replaced [Daniel Pudil](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniel_Pudil "wikilink") with [Vladimír Darida](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vladimír_Darida "wikilink").\[9\] The following day, the 23 man squad was confirmed, with [Tomáš Grigar](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tomáš_Grigar "wikilink") dropping out.\[10\]

## B组

###

主教練： [范马尔维克](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/范马尔维克 "wikilink")（Bert van Marwijk）\[11\]

###

主教練： [摩頓·奧臣](../Page/摩頓·奧臣.md "wikilink")

Denmark coach Morten Olsen named a 20-man squad for the tournament on 16 May, with the three remaining berths to be filled.\[12\] Anders Lindegaard was selected on 19 May.\[13\] Jores Okore and Nicklas Pedersen were selected on 24 May.\[14\] Numbers were given the next day.\[15\] On 29 May, [Kasper Schmeichel](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kasper_Schmeichel "wikilink") replaced the injured [Thomas Sørensen](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas_Sørensen "wikilink").

###

主教练:  [勒夫](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/勒夫 "wikilink")

2012年5月28日，[勒夫公布了德国国家队的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/勒夫 "wikilink")23人名单。\[16\]

###

Head coach:  [Paulo Bento](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paulo_Bento "wikilink")

Paulo Bento named his final 23-man squad on 14 May 2012.\[17\] On 23 May, [Hugo Viana](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hugo_Viana "wikilink") replaced [Carlos Martins](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carlos_Jorge_Neto_Martins "wikilink") after Martins suffered a calf injury.\[18\] On 24 May, the squad numbers were announced.\[19\]

## C组

###

Head coach:  [Vicente del Bosque](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vicente_del_Bosque "wikilink")

Vicente del Bosque named a provisional squad on 15 May 2012, but it did not include any [Barcelona](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/FC_Barcelona "wikilink"), [Athletic Bilbao](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Athletic_Bilbao "wikilink"), or [Chelsea](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chelsea_F.C. "wikilink") players as the two Spanish sides were preparing for the [Copa del Rey](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Copa_del_Rey "wikilink") final on 25 May, while Chelsea were to take on [Bayern Munich](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/FC_Bayern_Munich "wikilink") in the Champions League decider on 19 May.\[20\] On 21 May, del Bosque called-up Chelsea players [Fernando Torres](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fernando_Torres "wikilink") and [Juan Mata](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juan_Mata "wikilink").\[21\] On 27 May, del Bosque gave the final squad list, complete with Barcelona and Athletic Bilbao players.\[22\]

###

主教练:  [切萨雷·普兰德利](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/切萨雷·普兰德利 "wikilink")

[Cesare Prandelli](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cesare_Prandelli "wikilink") named a provisional 32-man squad on 13 May 2012, the final day of the [2011–12 Serie A season](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2011–12_Serie_A "wikilink").\[23\] [Domenico Criscito](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domenico_Criscito "wikilink") was later excluded from the provisional squad due to potential involvement in a match-fixing scandal\[24\]. On 29 May, Prandelli announced the final squad list.\[25\]

###

主教練： [-{zh-hans:吉奥瓦尼·特拉帕托尼;zh-hk:查柏東尼;zh-tw:吉奧瓦尼·特拉帕托尼;}-](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/吉奧瓦尼·特拉帕托尼 "wikilink")

On 7 May 2012, Giovanni Trapattoni announced his 23-man squad list for Euro 2012, along with a five-man stand-by list.\[26\] The Republic of Ireland team is the only squad to contain no players playing in its domestic league, and its captain, Robbie Keane of [LA Galaxy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Los_Angeles_Galaxy "wikilink"), is one of only two players in Euro 2012 who plays in a league outside of UEFA. [Keith Fahey](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keith_Fahey "wikilink") withdrew injured on 26 May and was replaced by [Paul Green](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_Green_\(footballer_born_1983\) "wikilink"). On 29 May [Kevin Foley](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kevin_Foley_\(footballer\) "wikilink") was replaced by [Paul McShane](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_McShane_\(Irish_footballer\) "wikilink") due to injury.\[27\]

|}

###

Head coach:  [Slaven Bilić](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slaven_Bilić "wikilink")

On 10 May 2012 a provisional list of 27 players was announced.\[28\] On 29 May, the final 23 man squad was announced, with [Nikola Kalinić](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nikola_Kalinić "wikilink"), [Šime Vrsaljko](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Šime_Vrsaljko "wikilink"), [Goran Blažević](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Goran_Blažević "wikilink") and [Dejan Lovren](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dejan_Lovren "wikilink") dropped from the provisional list.\[29\]

## D组

###

Head coach:  [Oleh Blokhin](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oleh_Blokhin "wikilink")

On 8 May 2012 Blokhin named a provisional list of 26 players for the tournament.\[30\] On 29 May 2012 Blokhin announced the final squad for the tournament.\[31\]

###

Head coach:  [Erik Hamrén](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erik_Hamrén "wikilink")

Erik Hamrén announced Sweden's 23-man squad on 14 May 2012.\[32\] Christian Wilhelmsson is one of only two players in Euro 2012 based outside UEFA; he currently plays in the [Saudi Professional League](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saudi_Professional_League "wikilink").

###

主教練：  [鶴臣](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鶴臣 "wikilink") 鶴臣公佈了參加2012年歐國盃的23人名單。此外鶴臣還公佈了5名候補球員，利物浦的[-{zh-hans:喬丹·亨德森;zh-hk:佐敦·軒達臣;zh-tw:喬丹·亨德森;}-](../Page/佐敦·軒達臣.md "wikilink")，曼城翼鋒[-{zh-hans:亞當·約翰遜;zh-hk:阿當·莊臣;zh-tw:亞當·強森;}-](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿當莊臣 "wikilink")、車路士前鋒[-{zh-hans:丹尼爾·斯圖裡奇; zh-hk:史杜歷治;}-](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/史杜列治 "wikilink")、愛華頓中堅[-{zh-hans:菲爾·查吉爾卡;zh-hk:菲爾·積基卡;zh-tw:菲爾·積基爾卡;}-入圍](../Page/菲爾·積基爾卡.md "wikilink")\[33\]。5月25日，原定3號門將[約翰·魯迪](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/約翰·魯迪 "wikilink")（John Ruddy）在練習時手指骨折退隊，並由效力伯明翰城的[-{zh-hans:杰克·巴特兰特;zh-hk:畢特蘭;zh-tw:傑克·巴特蘭;}-頂替](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/杰克·巴特兰特 "wikilink")\[34\] 。5月28日中場[-{zh-hans:加雷斯·巴里;zh-hk:加里夫·巴利;zh-tw:加雷斯·巴里;}-在友賽挪威時拉傷鼠蹊](../Page/加雷斯·巴里.md "wikilink")，被迫退出由效力[愛華頓的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/愛華頓 "wikilink")[-{zh-hans:菲爾·查吉爾卡;zh-hk:菲爾·積基卡;zh-tw:菲爾·積基爾卡;}-補上](../Page/菲爾·積基爾卡.md "wikilink")\[35\]。5月31日，中場[-{zh-hans:弗蘭克·蘭帕德;zh-hk:林柏特;zh-tw:法蘭克·蘭帕德;}-練習時拉傷大腿退隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/弗蘭克·蘭帕德 "wikilink")，效力利物浦的[-{zh-hans:喬丹·亨德森;zh-hk:佐敦·軒達臣;zh-tw:喬丹·亨德森;}-將取代林柏特的位置](../Page/佐敦·軒達臣.md "wikilink")\[36\] 。

###

Head coach:  [羅倫特·白蘭斯](../Page/羅倫特·白蘭斯.md "wikilink")

Blanc named his first shortlist on 9 May 2012, consisting of 12 players playing abroad.\[37\] On 15 May 2012 second shortlist of 15 France-based players was announced, while on the same day [Tottenham](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tottenham_Hotspur "wikilink") defender [Younès Kaboul](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Younès_Kaboul "wikilink") was ruled out of the tournament with a knee injury.\[38\] On 29 May 2012 Blanc announced his final 23 man squad\[39\]

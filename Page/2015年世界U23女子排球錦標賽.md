**2015年世界U23女子排球錦標賽**是於2015年8月12日至8月19日期間，在[土耳其](../Page/土耳其.md "wikilink")[安卡拉](../Page/安卡拉.md "wikilink")舉辦的排球賽事。本次為第二屆世界U23女子排球錦標賽。

## 概要

### 參賽國家

The FIVB Sports Events Council confirmed a proposal to streamline the number of teams participating in the Age Group World Championships on 14 December 2013.\[1\]

## Pool standing procedure

1.  Number of matches won
2.  Match points
3.  Sets ratio
4.  Points ratio
5.  Result of the last match between the tied teams

Match won 3–0 or 3–1: 3 match points for the winner, 0 match points for the loser
Match won 3–2: 2 match points for the winner, 1 match point for the loser

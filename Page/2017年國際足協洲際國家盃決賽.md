**2017年國際足協洲際國家盃決賽**是[2017年國際足協洲際國家盃的冠軍戰](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2017年國際足協洲際國家盃 "wikilink")。這場比賽將在2017年7月2日於[圣彼得堡](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/圣彼得堡 "wikilink")[十字架體育場舉行並由準決賽勝方進行](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/十字架體育場 "wikilink")。這是[2003年以來產生新冠軍因為巴西未能晉級](../Page/2003年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")。\[1\]

## 背景

The match is 智利首度進入洲際國家盃決賽 in their inaugural participation in the tournament，having qualified by winning the [2015年美洲盃足球賽](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015年美洲盃足球賽 "wikilink") for the very first time.\[2\]

這也是德國首次晉級聯合會杯的決賽，由於在[2014年國際足協世界盃獲得第四座冠軍而取得參賽資格](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2014年國際足協世界盃 "wikilink")。本次為其第三次在洲際國家杯出賽，德國因獲得[1996年欧洲足球锦标赛](../Page/1996年欧洲足球锦标赛.md "wikilink")的冠軍而取得[1999年洲際國家盃](../Page/1999年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")的參賽資格，最終他們獲得小組第三名的成績。[2005](../Page/2005年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")2005因為是主辦國而自動取得參賽資格，最終獲得季軍。\[3\] Germany were also invited to the tournament in [1992](../Page/1992年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink") as [1990 FIFA World Cup](../Page/1990年國際足協世界盃.md "wikilink") winners, in [1997](../Page/1997年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") as [UEFA Euro 1996](../Page/1996年欧洲足球锦标赛.md "wikilink") winners, and in [2003](../Page/2003年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") as [2002 FIFA World Cup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2002年世界盃足球賽 "wikilink") runners-up, but declined to participate on all occasions.\[4\]

智利與德國曾對決八次，其中四次為[熱身賽而另外四次為](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/熱身賽 "wikilink") competitive matches。德國共獲勝五次，智利獲勝兩次，另有一次的結果是平手。Of the competitive matches between them, Germany remain undefeated with a record of three wins and one draw. West Germany won the first meeting 2–1 in a 1960 friendly. A year later in 1961, 智利在另一場友誼賽中以3-1獲勝. The sides faced each other competitively for the first time another year later at the [1962 FIFA World Cup](../Page/1962年國際足協世界盃.md "wikilink"), which Chile hosted, with West Germany winning 2–0 in the group stage. The sides met again in a friendly in 1968, with Chile winning 2–1. At the [1974 FIFA World Cup](../Page/1974年國際足協世界盃.md "wikilink"), hosts West Germany beat Chile 1–0 in in the first round. They met again at the [1982 FIFA World Cup](../Page/1982年國際足協世界盃.md "wikilink"), with West Germany winning the first round match [4–1](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/1982_FIFA_World_Cup_Group_2#West_Germany_vs_Chile "wikilink"). After 32 years, the two faced each other again in a friendly, with Germany winning 1–0. Their latest meeting took place in the group stage of the tournament, finishing [1–1](../Page/2017年洲際國家盃B組.md "wikilink"), the first draw between the two sides.\[5\]

For the third time, teams from the same group have faced again in the final, previously occurring in [1997](../Page/1997年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") ([Brazil](../Page/巴西國家足球隊.md "wikilink") and [Australia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/澳洲國家足球隊 "wikilink")) and [2009](../Page/2009年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") ([United States](../Page/美國國家足球隊.md "wikilink") and [Brazil](../Page/巴西國家足球隊.md "wikilink")). The final is a record-equalling fourth consecutive with a South American country, and a record-equalling second consecutive with a European country. This is the eighth time a South American country has made the final, including an all-South American final in [2005](../Page/2005年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") between [rivals](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Argentina–Brazil_football_rivalry "wikilink") [Argentina](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿根廷國家足球隊 "wikilink") and [Brazil](../Page/巴西國家足球隊.md "wikilink"). On the other six occasions, the South American team won four times ([Argentina](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿根廷國家足球隊 "wikilink") in [1992](../Page/1992年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink"), [巴西](../Page/巴西國家足球隊.md "wikilink") in [1997](../Page/1997年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), [2009](../Page/2009年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), and [2013](../Page/2013年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")), and lost twice (Argentina in [1995](../Page/1995年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink"), Brazil in [1999](../Page/1999年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")). This is the fifth time a European country has made the final, with three wins ([Denmark](../Page/丹麥國家足球隊.md "wikilink") in [1995](../Page/1995年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink"), [France](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法國國家足球隊 "wikilink") in [2001](../Page/2001年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") and [2003](../Page/2003年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")), and one loss ([Spain](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/西班牙國家足球隊 "wikilink") in [2013](../Page/2013年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")). This is the third final between a South American and European country, with both continents having one win each. The South American country, [Brazil](../Page/巴西國家足球隊.md "wikilink"), defeated [Spain](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/西班牙國家足球隊 "wikilink") in [2013](../Page/2013年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), and the European country, [Denmark](../Page/丹麥國家足球隊.md "wikilink"), defeated [Argentina](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿根廷國家足球隊 "wikilink") in [1995](../Page/1995年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink").\[6\]

For the first time since [2003](../Page/2003年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), a different champion will be declared, as [Brazil](../Page/巴西國家足球隊.md "wikilink"), the defending champions who won the last three editions, failed to qualify (for the first time since [1995](../Page/1995年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink")) following their loss on penalties to [Paraguay](../Page/巴拉圭國家足球隊.md "wikilink") in the quarter-finals of the [2015年美洲盃足球賽](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015年美洲盃足球賽 "wikilink"). For the first time since [法國國家足球隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法國國家足球隊 "wikilink") in [2001](../Page/2001年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), a new country will win the competition, which will be the sixth overall winner.\[7\] A total of two South American teams ([阿根廷國家足球隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿根廷國家足球隊 "wikilink") and [巴西國家足球隊](../Page/巴西國家足球隊.md "wikilink")) and two European countries ([丹麥國家足球隊](../Page/丹麥國家足球隊.md "wikilink") and [法國國家足球隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法國國家足球隊 "wikilink")) have previously won the Confederations Cup. If Chile were to win, they would extend South America's record number of consecutive finals won to four, and overall finals won to six. If Germany were to win, they would extend Europe's number of finals won to four, ranking second only behind South America's five wins.

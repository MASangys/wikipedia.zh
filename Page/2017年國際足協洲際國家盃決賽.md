**2017年國際足協洲際國家盃決賽**是[2017年國際足協洲際國家盃的冠軍戰](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2017年國際足協洲際國家盃 "wikilink")。這場比賽將在2017年7月2日於[圣彼得堡](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/圣彼得堡 "wikilink")[十字架體育場舉行並由準決賽勝方進行](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/十字架體育場 "wikilink")。這是[2003年以來產生新冠軍因為巴西未能晉級](../Page/2003年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")。\[1\]

## 背景

The match is 智利首度進入洲際國家盃決賽 in their inaugural participation in the
tournament，having qualified by winning the
[2015年美洲盃足球賽](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015年美洲盃足球賽 "wikilink")
for the very first time.\[2\]

The match is also Germany's first Confederations Cup
final，在[2014年國際足協世界盃獲得第四座冠軍而取得參賽資格](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2014年國際足協世界盃 "wikilink")。
The tournament is their third appearance in the Confederations Cup. In
[1999](../Page/1999年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), Germany qualified by winning
[UEFA Euro 1996](../Page/1996年欧洲足球锦标赛.md "wikilink"), and finished third
in their group. In [2005](../Page/2005年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), Germany
qualified as hosts, and finished third in the tournament.\[3\] Germany
were also invited to the tournament in
[1992](../Page/1992年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink") as [1990 FIFA
World Cup](../Page/1990年國際足協世界盃.md "wikilink") winners, in
[1997](../Page/1997年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") as [UEFA Euro
1996](../Page/1996年欧洲足球锦标赛.md "wikilink") winners, and in
[2003](../Page/2003年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") as [2002 FIFA World
Cup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2002年世界盃足球賽 "wikilink") runners-up,
but declined to participate on all occasions.\[4\]

Chile and Germany have faced each other on eight occasions prior, four
of which were [friendlies](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/熱身賽 "wikilink")
and four of which were competitive matches. Germany have won a total of
five matches, with Chile winning two, and one match finishing as a draw.
Of the competitive matches between them, Germany remain undefeated with
a record of three wins and one draw. West Germany won the first meeting
2–1 in a 1960 friendly. A year later in 1961, 智利在另一場友誼賽中以3-1獲勝. The
sides faced each other competitively for the first time another year
later at the [1962 FIFA World Cup](../Page/1962年國際足協世界盃.md "wikilink"),
which Chile hosted, with West Germany winning 2–0 in the group stage.
The sides met again in a friendly in 1968, with Chile winning 2–1. At
the [1974 FIFA World Cup](../Page/1974年國際足協世界盃.md "wikilink"), hosts
West Germany beat Chile 1–0 in in the first round. They met again at the
[1982 FIFA World Cup](../Page/1982年國際足協世界盃.md "wikilink"), with West
Germany winning the first round match
[4–1](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/1982_FIFA_World_Cup_Group_2#West_Germany_vs_Chile "wikilink").
After 32 years, the two faced each other again in a friendly, with
Germany winning 1–0. Their latest meeting took place in the group stage
of the tournament, finishing [1–1](../Page/2017年洲際國家盃B組.md "wikilink"),
the first draw between the two sides.\[5\]

For the third time, teams from the same group have faced again in the
final, previously occurring in [1997](../Page/1997年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")
([Brazil](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴西國家足球隊 "wikilink") and
[Australia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/澳洲國家足球隊 "wikilink")) and
[2009](../Page/2009年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") ([United
States](../Page/美國國家足球隊.md "wikilink") and
[Brazil](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴西國家足球隊 "wikilink")). The final
is a record-equalling fourth consecutive with a South American country,
and a record-equalling second consecutive with a European country. This
is the eighth time a South American country has made the final,
including an all-South American final in
[2005](../Page/2005年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") between
[rivals](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Argentina–Brazil_football_rivalry "wikilink")
[Argentina](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿根廷國家足球隊 "wikilink") and
[Brazil](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴西國家足球隊 "wikilink"). On the other
six occasions, the South American team won four times
([Argentina](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿根廷國家足球隊 "wikilink") in
[1992](../Page/1992年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink"),
[Brazil](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴西國家足球隊 "wikilink") in
[1997](../Page/1997年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"),
[2009](../Page/2009年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), and
[2013](../Page/2013年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")), and lost twice (Argentina in
[1995](../Page/1995年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink"), Brazil in
[1999](../Page/1999年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")). This is the fifth time a
European country has made the final, with three wins
([Denmark](../Page/丹麥國家足球隊.md "wikilink") in
[1995](../Page/1995年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink"),
[France](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法國國家足球隊 "wikilink") in
[2001](../Page/2001年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink") and
[2003](../Page/2003年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")), and one loss
([Spain](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/西班牙國家足球隊 "wikilink") in
[2013](../Page/2013年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink")). This is the third final
between a South American and European country, with both continents
having one win each. The South American country,
[Brazil](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴西國家足球隊 "wikilink"), defeated
[Spain](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/西班牙國家足球隊 "wikilink") in
[2013](../Page/2013年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), and the European country,
[Denmark](../Page/丹麥國家足球隊.md "wikilink"), defeated
[Argentina](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿根廷國家足球隊 "wikilink") in
[1995](../Page/1995年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink").\[6\]

For the first time since [2003](../Page/2003年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), a
different champion will be declared, as
[Brazil](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴西國家足球隊 "wikilink"), the
defending champions who won the last three editions, failed to qualify
(for the first time since [1995](../Page/1995年法赫德國王盃.md "wikilink"))
following their loss on penalties to
[Paraguay](../Page/巴拉圭國家足球隊.md "wikilink") in the
quarter-finals of the
[2015年美洲盃足球賽](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015年美洲盃足球賽 "wikilink").
For the first time since
[法國國家足球隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法國國家足球隊 "wikilink")
in [2001](../Page/2001年洲際國家盃.md "wikilink"), a new country will win the
competition, which will be the sixth overall winner.\[7\] A total of two
South American teams
([阿根廷國家足球隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿根廷國家足球隊 "wikilink")
and [巴西國家足球隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴西國家足球隊 "wikilink")) and two
European countries ([丹麥國家足球隊](../Page/丹麥國家足球隊.md "wikilink") and
[法國國家足球隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法國國家足球隊 "wikilink"))
have previously won the Confederations Cup. If Chile were to win, they
would extend South America's record number of consecutive finals won to
four, and overall finals won to six. If Germany were to win, they would
extend Europe's number of finals won to four, ranking second only behind
South America's five wins.

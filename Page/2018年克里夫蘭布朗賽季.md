**[2018年](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2018年NFL賽季 "wikilink")[克里夫蘭布朗賽季](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/克里夫蘭布朗 "wikilink")**是隊伍成立後的第70個賽季，也是球隊加盟[NFL後的第](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/NFL "wikilink")66個賽季。這是[休·傑克遜執教的第三個賽季與新總經理](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/休·傑克遜 "wikilink")[約翰·多西上任後的第一個賽季](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/約翰·多西_\(美式足球\) "wikilink")。[上個賽季的克里夫蘭布朗取得了全聯盟最差的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2017年克里夫蘭布朗賽季 "wikilink")0勝16敗戰績，是自2008年[底特律雄獅之後第二支取得](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/底特律雄獅 "wikilink")16戰全敗的隊伍。球隊也希望終結球隊與聯盟最高紀錄的連續15年不進季後賽的難堪紀錄(布朗隊上次進季後賽為2002年)。

## 休賽季

### 辦公室人員變動

1月2日時，new general manager [約翰·多西](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/約翰·多西_\(美式足球\) "wikilink") hired [Green Bay Packers](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Green_Bay_Packers "wikilink") personnel executive [Alonzo Highsmith](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alonzo_Highsmith "wikilink") as vice president of football operations.\[1\]

On January 10, the Browns announced several front office hirings: [Eliot Wolf](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eliot_Wolf "wikilink") as assistant general manager, Jimmy Noel as assistant director of pro scouting, Matt Donahoe as a scout, and Dan Zegers as personnel coordinator. The Browns also announced that former vice president of player personnel Ken Kovash would switch to a role within the strategy department, and fired senior personnel executive [Ryan Grigson](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ryan_Grigson "wikilink").\[2\]

### 教練團變動

On January 10, the Browns released special teams coordinator Chris Tabor. He had been the longest-tenured coach on the Browns, with the team since 2011.\[3\]

On January 11, the Browns hired [Adam Henry](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adam_Henry_\(American_football\) "wikilink") as wide receivers coach, a position he held with the [New York Giants](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_York_Giants "wikilink") since 2016. Former wide receivers coach [Al Saunders](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al_Saunders "wikilink") will transition into a senior advisory role.\[4\]

On January 12, the Browns hired [Ken Zampese](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ken_Zampese "wikilink") as quarterbacks coach. They also fired running backs coach/run game coordinator [Kirby Wilson](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kirby_Wilson "wikilink"), special teams assistant Shawn Mennenga, and special teams quality control man Stan Watson.\[5\]

On January 24, the Browns confirmed the hiring of [Amos Jones](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amos_Jones "wikilink") as special teams coordinator, [Todd Haley](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Todd_Haley "wikilink") as offensive coordinator, and [Freddie Kitchens](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Freddie_Kitchens "wikilink") as running backs/assistant head coach.\[6\]

On February 8, the Browns hired [Sam Shade](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam_Shade "wikilink") as assistant special teams coach.\[7\]

On March 9, the Browns hired former [return specialist](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/return_specialist "wikilink") [Josh Cribbs](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Josh_Cribbs "wikilink") as special teams intern.\[8\]

#### 退休

  - On March 14, T [Joe Thomas](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joe_Thomas_\(offensive_tackle\) "wikilink") retired after an 11-year career, all with the Browns. Thomas appeared in ten consecutive Pro Bowls from 2007-2016.\[74\]
  - On July 6, T [Donald Stephenson](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Stephenson "wikilink") retired from the Browns, despite having never played for the team.\[75\] Had he not retired, He would have been suspended two games for violating the league's Policy and Program for Substances of Abuse.\[76\] Stephenson played six seasons with the [Kansas City Chiefs](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kansas_City_Chiefs "wikilink") and [Denver Broncos](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denver_Broncos "wikilink").

## 簡介

2018年世界盃足球賽中北美洲及加勒比海區資格賽第二輪定2015年9月4日到8日舉行。由中北美洲及加勒比海足協排名第四檔次國家隊2隊和10支第一輪勝隊共12隊進行主客場二回合交手；勝隊晉級第四輪。

## Seeding

The draw for the third round was held as part of the 2018 FIFA World Cup Preliminary Draw on 25 July 2015, starting 18:00 [MSK](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moscow_Standard_Time "wikilink") ([UTC+3](../Page/UTC+03:00.md "wikilink")), at the Konstantinovsky Palace in [Strelna](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Strelna "wikilink"), [Saint Petersburg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Petersburg "wikilink"), Russia.\[1\]\[2\]

The seeding was based on the [FIFA World Rankings](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/FIFA_World_Rankings "wikilink") of August 2014 (shown in parentheses).\[3\] The 12 teams were seeded into two pots:

  - Pot 3 contains the teams ranked 1–6.
  - Pot 4 contains the teams ranked 7–12.

Each tie contained a team from Pot 1 and a team from Pot 2, with the order of legs decided by draw.

[**孟加拉国**](../Page/孟加拉国.md "wikilink")**大选**於2018年12月30日舉行，選出[國民議會的議員](../Page/國民議會_\(孟加拉國\).md "wikilink")\[1\]。[谢赫·哈西娜领导的](../Page/谢赫·哈西娜.md "wikilink")[人民联盟以压倒性优势赢得了选举](../Page/孟加拉国人民联盟.md "wikilink")。反對派批評選舉被操控，大部分反對派候選人因被拘捕、恐嚇、和驅逐而沒有參選。
反对党领袖卡迈勒·侯赛因否认选举结果，批評選舉「十分滑稽」，要求在中立政府下舉行新一場大選。國家選委會表示得悉有關「全國多處」的舞弊指控，將會作出調查\[2\]\[3\]。

## 选举制度

[國民議會共有](../Page/國民議會_\(孟加拉國\).md "wikilink")350名議員，其中300席以[領先者當選](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/領先者當選 "wikilink")、單選區單議席制度直接選出\[4\]，另外的50席預留予女性。預留的議席以各政黨的得票率而分配予各黨\[5\]。議員的任期為五年。

大約一億名選民會在全國40199個投票站投票\[6\]，其中六個選區將會使用電子投票機\[7\]。

## 背景

由前總理[卡莉達·齊亞領導](../Page/卡莉達·齊亞.md "wikilink")、以[孟加拉國民族主義黨](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/孟加拉國民族主義黨 "wikilink")（BNP）為首的主要反對黨聯盟杯葛了[2014年孟加拉國大選](../Page/2014年孟加拉國大選.md "wikilink")。結果是總理[谢赫·哈西娜領導的](../Page/谢赫·哈西娜.md "wikilink")[孟加拉国人民联盟獲得壓倒性勝利](../Page/孟加拉国人民联盟.md "wikilink")，取得國民議會300個席位中的234席，在選舉中共有153個席位因為無對手而自動當選。\[8\]

2017年7月，民族主義黨表示如果國會解散以及選舉委員會由無黨派成員組成，該黨準備參加來屆大選。\[9\]2017年9月14日，官方總選舉專員確認民族主義黨將參加選舉。\[10\]但是在民族主義黨主席[卡莉達·齊亞被判決腐敗罪成立後](../Page/卡莉達·齊亞.md "wikilink")，\[11\]孟加拉國高等法院認定齊亞不符合參選資格。\[12\]

## 選舉陣營

In August 2017, Jatiya Party (Ershad) chairman and former
[President](../Page/孟加拉国总统.md "wikilink") Hussain Ershad indicated that
he wanted his party to leave the [Awami
League](../Page/孟加拉国人民联盟.md "wikilink")-led [Grand
Alliance](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/大联盟_\(孟加拉国\) "wikilink") and
join a new alliance with democratic left and Islamic democratic
parties.\[13\] However, in November 2018 the party announced that it
would join the Awami League-led alliance.\[14\] On 28 December, Ershad
announced Jatiya Party candidates contesting from “open seats” would
support the ruling Awami League-led grand alliance candidates and
extended his support to "my \[his\] 'sister' Prime Minister Sheikh
Hasina".\[15\] Ershad personally withdrew from contesting the Dhaka-17
seat in favour of the Awami League candidate.\[16\] Ershad said however
that “strong candidates” of his party would stay in the race.\[17\]

On 13 October 2018, the Jatiya Oikya Front (National Unity Front) was
formed,\[18\] consisting primarily of the Gano Forum, [Bangladesh
Nationalist Party](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/孟加拉國民族主義黨 "wikilink"),
Jatiya Samajtantrik Dal-JSD and Nagorik Oikya.\[19\]\[20\] It is led by
former Foreign Minister Kamal Hossain of the Gano Forum. On 18 December
the alliance announced a 14-point manifesto, which included a pledge to
reduce the power of the office of [Prime
Minister](../Page/孟加拉国总理.md "wikilink").\[21\]

There was controversy in the run up to the elections surrounding the
nomination of banned Jamaati candidates under the BNP banner. In 2013,
the hard-line, right-wing, Islamist party, Jamaat-e-Islami was banned
from registering and therefore contesting in elections by the High
Court, citing their charter in violation of the
constitution.\[22\]\[23\] However 25 Jamaati candidates ran in the
election, with 22 nominations for BNP and 3 running as
independents.\[24\]\[25\] An investigation was launched but on 23
December the Election Commission Secretary Helaluddin Ahmed said they
had examined the related law and "there is no scope for rejecting the
Jamaat leaders' candidacy at this moment."\[26\] On 26 December, just
days before the election, Jatiya Oikya Front leader Kamal Hossain
expressed his regret about Jamaat's involvement in the elections under
his alliance, claiming "had I known \[that Jamaat leaders will be given
BNP tickets\] I would not have been part of it."\[27\] The media however
had reported at the end of November that this was happening.\[28\]\[29\]

In accordance with election commission rules, campaigning was halted at
08:00 AM on the morning of 28 December.\[30\]

## 選舉過程

### 暴力事件

2018年12月9日至12日期間，當局接獲47宗暴力事件報告，總共造成8死560傷\[31\]。  According to the BNP
Office, as of 26 December 2018, at least 12,923 people (mostly BNP,
Jatiya Oikya Front and 18 Party Alliance activists) had been injured in
2,833 attacks on opposition candidates.\[32\] Between 8 November and 25
December, at least 1,574 cases were filed against BNP officials in
different districts. During the same period, 15,568 activists were
arrested.\[33\] According to The Daily Star, at least 56 candidates,
mostly from the BNP, were attacked, with around 1,190 people injured and
over 800 BNP-Jamaat officials arrested during the electioneering between
10 and 28 December.\[34\]

On 29 December, the day before the elections, the Bangladesh
Telecommunication Regulatory Commission suspended 3G and 4G connections
across the country and said they would not be resumed until after the
elections.\[35\]

On election day, at least 17 people were killed in clashes between
ruling party supporters and the opposition.\[36\]

### 選舉舞弊

A BBC correspondent reported seeing a ballot box already full of votes
prior to the polling station in [Chittagong](../Page/吉大港市.md "wikilink")
opening.\[37\] Allegations of voting irregularities including polling
booths inexplicably closing for “lunch breaks”, voters being turned away
and ballots being counted unrealistically quickly were widespread.\[38\]
Local media published accounts by correspondents who claimed to have
witnessed Awami League members stuffing ballot boxes in the presence of
police and election officials.\[39\] The Bangladesh Election Commission
promised to investigate numerous reports of vote rigging.\[40\]

**2019年NBA選秀**在2019年6月20日在[紐約](../Page/纽约.md "wikilink")[布魯克林區的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/布鲁克林 "wikilink")[巴克萊中心](../Page/巴克萊中心.md "wikilink")舉行，由ESPN向全美直播。 [國家籃球協會](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/國家籃球協會 "wikilink")(NBA)球隊將輪流挑選美國大學籃球業餘球員和其他符合資格的球員，包括國際球員。這次的選秀使用了新的樂透抽籤機制，三支戰績最差的隊伍（[紐約尼克](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/纽约尼克斯 "wikilink")、[克里夫蘭騎士](../Page/克里夫兰骑士.md "wikilink")、[鳳凰城太陽](../Page/菲尼克斯太阳.md "wikilink")）各可獲得14%抽中狀元籤的機率。

## Combine

The invitation-only [NBA Draft Combine](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/NBA_Draft_Combine "wikilink") was held in Chicago from May 15 to 19. The on-court element of the combine took place on May 17 and 18. This year, 66 players were originally invited to the combine, including top prospects [Zion Williamson](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zion_Williamson "wikilink") and [Ja Morant](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ja_Morant "wikilink").\[2\] The pool of participants also included Croatian [Luka Šamanić](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luka_Šamanić "wikilink"), postgraduate [Jalen Lecque](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jalen_Lecque "wikilink"), and [Darius Bazley](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Darius_Bazley "wikilink"), who took a route similar to [Mitchell Robinson](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mitchell_Robinson "wikilink") with not playing in college or another league for a year before entering the draft. A couple of prospects also returned to the event after entering the previous year's combine, include an injury recovering [Jontay Porter](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jontay_Porter "wikilink") and [Brian Bowen](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brian_Bowen "wikilink"), a player who entered last year as a collegiate participant last year before heading off to play professionally in [Australia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Basketball_League_\(Australia\) "wikilink") this year. In addition, this year also introduced the [NBA G League](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/NBA_G_League "wikilink") Elite Camp, which gave a certain number of draft hopefuls a chance to transfer into the NBA Draft Combine afterward.\[3\] This year, eleven participants from that even joined the combine, increasing the number of total invites up to 77.\[4\] One of the additional invites was [Tacko Fall](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tacko_Fall "wikilink"), who broke combine records for height (reaching  with shoes on), wingspan, and standing reach.\[5\]

## 参选资格和参选者

The draft is conducted under the eligibility rules established in the league's 2017 [collective bargaining agreement](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/NBA_Collective_Bargaining_Agreement "wikilink") (CBA) with its player's union. The previous CBA that ended the [2011 lockout](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2011_NBA_lockout "wikilink") instituted no immediate changes to the draft but called for a committee of owners and players to discuss future changes.

  - All drafted players must be at least 19 years old during the calendar year of the draft. In terms of dates, players who are eligible for the 2019 draft must be born on or before December 31, 2000.
  - Since the 2016 draft, the following rules, as implemented by the [NCAA Division I](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/NCAA_Division_I "wikilink") council for that division, are:\[6\]
      - Declaration for the draft no longer results in an automatic loss of college eligibility. As long as a player does not sign a contract with a professional team outside the NBA, or sign with an agent, he will retain college eligibility as long as he makes a timely withdrawal from the draft.
      - NCAA players have until 10 days after the end of the [NBA Draft Combine](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/NBA_Draft_Combine "wikilink") to withdraw from the draft. Since the combine is held in mid-May, the current deadline is about five weeks after the previous mid-April deadline.
      - NCAA players may participate in the draft combine and are allowed to attend one tryout per year with each NBA team without losing college eligibility.
      - NCAA players may enter and withdraw from the draft up to two times without loss of eligibility. Previously, the NCAA treated the second declaration of draft eligibility as a permanent loss of college eligibility.
  - Starting this year, any undrafted underclassmen in the college system will have the opportunity to return to their college or university for at least one more season, provided they terminate their prior agreements with the agent they signed up with.\[7\]

The NBA has since expanded the draft combine to include players with remaining college eligibility (who, like players without college eligibility, can only attend by invitation).\[8\]

### 早期參選球員

Players who are not automatically eligible have to declare their eligibility for the draft by notifying the NBA offices in writing no later than 60 days before the draft. For the 2019 draft, the date fell on April 21. After that date, "early entry" players are able to attend NBA pre-draft camps and individual team workouts to show off their skills and obtain feedback regarding their draft positions. Under the CBA a player may withdraw his name from consideration from the draft at any time before the final declaration date, which is 10 days before the draft. Under current NCAA rules, players had until May 29 (10 days after the draft combine) to withdraw from the draft and retain college eligibility.\[9\]

A player who has hired an agent forfeits his remaining college eligibility when he is drafted. He can be represented beginning after any basketball season, following a request for an evaluation from the NBA Undergraduate Advisory Committee. From this draft on, players who declare for the NBA draft and are not selected have the opportunity to return to their school for at least another year, only after terminating all agreements with their agents.\[10\]

### 自動獲得資格參加球員

下面僅列出未符合一般標準但自動獲得選秀資格的球員——在勞資協議（CBA）下，國際球員需在選秀當年滿22歲，若非國際球員則須高中畢業至少四年。

球員曾與NBA以外的職業籃球聯盟球隊簽約，並在選秀前終止合約，則「可能」自動獲得選秀資格。而這項資格是否符合端看該球員是否為國際球員：

  - 若為國際球員，則必須曾與美國境內的非NBA球隊簽約。
  - 若非國際球員，則只要曾與NBA以外的任何國家中的職業球隊簽約即可。

在勞資協議下，公民權並非決定該球員是否要依照國際球員標準。在CBA的定義下，必須符合以下所有資格才能稱為國際球員：

  - 在選秀當日之前於美國境外定居超過3年。
  - 並未擁有美國高中籃球資格。
  - 從未就讀美國學院或大學。

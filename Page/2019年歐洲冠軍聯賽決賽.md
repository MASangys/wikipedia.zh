**2019年歐洲聯賽冠軍盃決賽**是[2018–19年歐洲聯賽冠軍盃的決賽](../Page/2018–19年歐洲冠軍聯賽.md "wikilink")，決賽將於2019年6月1日在[西班牙](../Page/西班牙.md "wikilink")[馬德里的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/馬德里 "wikilink")[萬達大都會球場舉行](../Page/萬達大都會球場.md "wikilink")。\[1\]

The **2019 UEFA Champions League Final** will be the final match of the
[2018–19 UEFA Champions
League](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2018–19_UEFA_Champions_League "wikilink"),
the 64th season of Europe's premier club football tournament organised
by [UEFA](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/UEFA "wikilink"), and the 27th
season since it was renamed from the [European Champion Clubs'
Cup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Champion_Clubs'_Cup "wikilink")
to the [UEFA Champions
League](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/UEFA_Champions_League "wikilink").
It will be played at the [Wanda
Metropolitano](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wanda_Metropolitano "wikilink")
in [Madrid](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madrid "wikilink"), Spain on 1
June 2019.\[2\]

The winners will earn the right to play against the winners of the
[2018–19 UEFA Europa
League](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2018–19_UEFA_Europa_League "wikilink")
in the [2019 UEFA Super
Cup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2019_UEFA_Super_Cup "wikilink"). They
will also qualify to enter the group stage of the [2019–20 UEFA
Champions
League](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2019–20_UEFA_Champions_League "wikilink"),
and if they have already qualified through their league performance, the
berth reserved will be given to the champions of the [2018–19 Austrian
Bundesliga](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2018–19_Austrian_Football_Bundesliga "wikilink"),
the 11th-ranked association according to next season's access
list.\[3\]\[4\]

In March 2018, UEFA announced that a [fourth
substitution](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Substitute_\(association_football\) "wikilink")
will be allowed in [extra
time](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Overtime_\(sports\)#Association_football "wikilink")
and that the number of substitutes has been increased from 7 to 12. The
kick-off time has also been changed from 20:45 CEST to 21:00 CEST.\[5\]
The match will also be the first final to use the [video assistant
referee](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/video_assistant_referee "wikilink")
(VAR) system.\[6\]

## 比賽場地

在2017年9月20日於[法國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法國 "wikilink")[里昂舉行的](../Page/里昂.md "wikilink")[歐洲足球協會聯盟執委會議上](../Page/歐洲足球協會聯盟.md "wikilink")，[西班牙甲組足球聯賽球隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/西班牙甲組足球聯賽 "wikilink")[馬德里體育會的主場](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/馬德里體育會 "wikilink")[萬達大都會球場被確認為](../Page/萬達大都會球場.md "wikilink")2019年歐洲聯賽冠軍盃決賽的舉辦場地。\[7\]

**293T**（或**HEK293T**）是一種由[HEK 293細胞系衍生的人類細胞系](../Page/HEK_293.md "wikilink")，是的突變體版本。常用於生物蛋白的表達，以及重組[逆轉錄病毒的製造](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/逆轉錄病毒 "wikilink")。

## Origin

293T was created in Michele Calos's lab at Stanford by [stable transfection](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/transfection#Stable_and_transient_transfection "wikilink") of the HEK 293 cell line with a plasmid encoding a temperature-sensitive mutant of the SV40 large T antigen; it was originally referred to as 293/*tsA1609neo*.\[1\] The first reference to the cell line as "293T" may be its use to create the [BOSC23](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/BOSC23 "wikilink") packaging cell line for producing retroviral particles.\[2\]

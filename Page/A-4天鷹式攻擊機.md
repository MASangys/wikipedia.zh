[AF1_da_Marinha_do_Brasil_2.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:AF1_da_Marinha_do_Brasil_2.jpg "fig:AF1_da_Marinha_do_Brasil_2.jpg") 1952年，美國[道格拉斯飛行器公司贏得合約為美國海軍與陸戰隊製造一款新的輕型噴射戰機](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/道格拉斯飛行器公司 "wikilink")，**A-4天鷹式攻擊機**（）是一架[攻擊機](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/攻擊機 "wikilink")，原型機為**A4D**，最初被設計用來做為美國海軍航空母艦之[艦載機](../Page/艦載機.md "wikilink")。於1954年首次飛行，至今已過六十載，在[越南戰爭中扮演著關鍵的角色](../Page/越南战争.md "wikilink")，亦參加過[福克蘭戰爭](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/福克蘭戰爭 "wikilink")、中東戰爭[贖罪日戰爭](../Page/贖罪日戰爭.md "wikilink")，目前在世界各地僅剩數百架左右的A-4攻擊機正在服役。

## 簡介

本來是以[攻擊機為設計方向](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/攻擊機 "wikilink"),並不是以[戰鬥機設計出發及製造](../Page/战斗机.md "wikilink")，但因為它的運動性能非常卓越且重量只有5.4公噸左右。所以也充作高級飛行員訓練機。與[F-14雄貓或](../Page/F-14雄貓式戰鬥機.md "wikilink")[F-18大黃蜂等](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/F/A-18黃蜂式戰鬥攻擊機 "wikilink")，做不分上下的練習戰鬥。[美國海軍](../Page/美國海軍.md "wikilink")的空中特技表演團藍天使也曾使用過A-4來做特技表演。而在[越南戰爭中](../Page/越南战争.md "wikilink")，A-4曾以不攜帶對空武器的情況下，以火箭彈擊落了俄製米格式[戰鬥機](../Page/战斗机.md "wikilink")，而成為一段佳話。

原型機於1954年試飛成功，時至今日約五十年的歲月，所以種類繁多，目前還有許多國現役，而新加坡當局也擬定計畫把其空軍使用的A-4S型機，修改為A-4SU超級天鷹。

## 機體構型

採單座、單發動機、三角型低單翼設計、進氣口位於機身兩側，機身前段右側設有空中受油管，能夠進行空中加油。

[Wright J65](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wright_J65 "wikilink")-W-20 engine with  of takeoff thrust, 638 built.
    A-4D: Designation not used to avoid confusion with the pre-1962 designation A4D.
    A-4E: Major upgrade, including new [Pratt & Whitney J52-P-6A](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pratt_&_Whitney_J52 "wikilink") engine with  of thrust, strengthened airframe with two more weapon pylons (for a total of five), improved avionics, with [TACAN](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/TACAN "wikilink"), [Doppler navigation radar](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doppler_radar "wikilink"), radar altimeter, toss-bombing computer, and AJB-3A low-altitude bombing system. Many later upgraded with J52-P-8 engine with  thrust; 499 built.
    A-4F: Refinement of A-4E with extra avionics housed in a hump on the fuselage spine (this feature later retrofitted to A-4Es and some A-4Cs) and more powerful J52-P-8A engine with  of thrust, later upgraded in service to J52-P-408 with , 147 built. Some served with [Blue Angels](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blue_Angels "wikilink") acrobatic team from 1973 to 1986.
    [A-4G](../Page/A-4G天鷹式攻擊機.md "wikilink")：該機型首先新建造了8架供[澳大利亞皇家海軍使用](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/澳大利亞皇家海軍 "wikilink")，設計從A-4F作微小變化，使它們沒有駝峰狀凸起的航電位置。後來，還有八架A-4F被改裝到符合澳洲皇家海軍的標準。改裝的主要目的是為了可以在翼下掛載4枚[AIM-9B飛彈以增進該國海軍的航艦防空能力](../Page/AIM-9響尾蛇飛彈.md "wikilink")\[1\]\[2\]
    A-4H: 90 aircraft for the [Israeli Air Force](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Israeli_Air_Force "wikilink") based on the A-4F. Used 30 mm (1.18 in) [DEFA cannon](../Page/德發轉輪式機炮.md "wikilink") with 150 rpg in place of U.S. 20 mm (.79 in) guns. Later, some A-4Es later locally modified to this standard. Subsequently modified with extended jetpipes as protection against heat-seeking missiles.
    A-4K: 10 aircraft for [Royal New Zealand Air Force](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_New_Zealand_Air_Force "wikilink"). In the 1990s, these were upgraded under [Project KAHU](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Project_KAHU "wikilink") with new radar and avionics, provision for [AGM-65 Maverick](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/AGM-65_Maverick "wikilink"), [AIM-9 Sidewinder](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM-9_Sidewinder "wikilink"), and [GBU-16 Paveway II](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/GBU-16_Paveway_II "wikilink") [laser-guided bomb](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/laser-guided_bomb "wikilink"). The RNZAF also rebuilt an A-4C and 10 A-4Gs to A-4K standard.
    A-4M Skyhawk II: Dedicated Marine version with improved avionics and more powerful J52-P-408a engine with  thrust, enlarged cockpit, [IFF](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Identification_friend_or_foe "wikilink") system. Later fitted with Hughes [AN/ASB-19](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/AN/ASB-19 "wikilink") Angle Rate Bombing System (ARBS) with TV and laser spot tracker, 158 built.
    A-4N: 117 modified A-4Ms for the Israeli Air Force.

### 升級、改進及出口型號

  - TA-4E: two A-4Es modified as prototypes of a trainer version.
    EA-4F: four TA-4Fs converted for [ECM](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electronic_countermeasures "wikilink") training.
    A-4L: 100 A-4Cs remanufactured for Marine Corps Reserves and Navy Reserve squadrons. Fitted with A-4F avionics (including the fuselage "hump") but retaining J-65 engine and three-pylon wing.\[3\]
    OA-4M: 23 TA-4Fs modified for [Forward Air Control](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Forward_Air_Control "wikilink") duties for the Marines in Vietnam.\[4\]
    A-4P: Remanufactured A-4Bs sold to [Argentine Air Force](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Argentina "wikilink") known as A-4B by the Argentines.
    A-4Q: Remanufactured A-4Bs sold to Argentine Navy.
    A-4Y: Provisional designation for A-4Ms modified with the [ARBS](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/AN/ASB-19 "wikilink"). Designation never adopted by the U.S. Navy or U.S. Marine Corps.\[5\]
    [A-4AR Fightinghawk](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lockheed_Martin_A-4AR_Fightinghawk "wikilink"): 36 A-4Ms refurbished for Argentina.
    [OA-4AR Fightinghawk](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lockheed_Martin_A-4AR_Fightinghawk "wikilink"): Refurbished two-seat training version for Argentina.\[6\]
    A-4KU: 30 modified A-4Ms for the Kuwaiti Air Force. Brazil purchased 20 of these second-hand and redesignated them **AF-1**. Now used by the [Brazilian Navy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazilian_Navy "wikilink") on carrier duty.
    CA-4F: A proposed variant for the Royal Canadian Navy to replace the retired F-2H3 Banshee on HMCS Bonaventure. Canada expressed little interest and so it was never placed in production.\[7\]
    TA-4KU: three trainer versions of the above. Brazil purchased some of these second-hand and redesignated them **AF-1A**.
    A-4PTM: 40 A-4Cs and A-4Ls refurbished for [Royal Malaysian Air Force](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Malaysian_Air_Force "wikilink"), incorporating many A-4M features (*PTM* stands for *Peculiar to Malaysia*).\[8\]
    TA-4PTM: Unique trainer version for Royal Malaysian Air Force. Converted from A-4C/L airframes with 28" fuselage plug and second cockpit, similar to TA-4F/J (**PTM** stands for **Peculiar to Malaysia**).\[9\]
    [A-4S](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ST_Aerospace_A-4SU_Super_Skyhawk "wikilink"): 50 A-4Bs remanufactured for [Republic of Singapore Air Force](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Singapore_Air_Force "wikilink").
    [TA-4S](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ST_Aerospace_A-4SU_Super_Skyhawk "wikilink"): seven [trainer](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trainer_\(aircraft\) "wikilink") versions of the above. Different from most TA-4 trainers with a common cockpit for the student and instructor pilot, these were essentially rebuilt with a  fuselage plug inserted into the front fuselage and a separate bulged cockpit (giving better all round visibility) for the instructor seated behind the student pilot.
    [A-4S-1](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ST_Aerospace_A-4SU_Super_Skyhawk "wikilink"): 50 A-4Cs remanufactured for [Republic of Singapore Air Force](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Singapore_Air_Force "wikilink").
    [TA-4S-1](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ST_Aerospace_A-4SU_Super_Skyhawk "wikilink"): eight trainer versions of the above. These were designated as **TA-4S-1** to set it apart from the earlier batch of seven airframes.
    [A-4SU Super Skyhawk](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ST_Aerospace_A-4SU_Super_Skyhawk "wikilink"): extensively modified and updated version of the A-4S-1, exclusively for the [Republic of Singapore Air Force](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Republic_of_Singapore_Air_Force "wikilink") (RSAF), fitted with a [General Electric F404](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_Electric_F404 "wikilink") non-afterburning [turbofan](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/turbofan "wikilink") engine, and modernized electronics.
    [TA-4SU Super Skyhawk](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ST_Aerospace_A-4SU_Super_Skyhawk "wikilink"): extensively modified and updated version of the TA-4S & TA-4S-1 to TA-4SU standard.
    AF-1:[Brazilian Navy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazilian_Navy "wikilink") designation applied to 23 A-4KU and TA-4KU aircraft acquired from the [Kuwaiti Air Force](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuwaiti_Air_Force "wikilink").

## 武裝

固定武裝為2門位於翼根處的20mm機砲，最多可掛載4000公斤武器。

## 藍天使 & Top Gun

在1974年-1986年間,擔任美國海軍的藍天使特技飛行小組用機，並為海軍假想敵中隊（Top Gun）所採用，作為訓練學員用的假想敵機。

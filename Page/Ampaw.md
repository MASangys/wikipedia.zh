***Ampaw***是一種，意指用[米香](../Page/米香.md "wikilink")製成的[玉米花](../Page/玉米花.md "wikilink")。

而在[宿霧語](../Page/宿霧語.md "wikilink")中，*Ampaw*也是一種[委婉](../Page/委婉.md "wikilink")詞用法，意思是指一個人空談理論，不能解決實際問題\[1\]。

## 名稱

*Ampaw* means "[puffed grain](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/puffed_grain "wikilink")" in [Philippine languages](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine_languages "wikilink"). Though it applies predominantly to the rice version, [popcorn](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/popcorn "wikilink") can also be referred to as *ampaw* (more accurately as *ampaw na mais*, "puffed corn").\[2\] In [Cebuano](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cebuano_language "wikilink") [slang](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/slang "wikilink"), *ampaw* is also a euphemism roughly equivalent to the English idiom "\[a person\] full of hot air". Ampaw should not be confused with Chinese [red envelopes](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/red_envelope "wikilink"), *ampao* or *ang pao*, a close [homonym](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/homonym "wikilink") in [Philippine Hokkien](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippine_Hokkien "wikilink").\[3\]

## 描述

Ampaw is made with cooked white rice (usually leftovers). It is dried in the sun for around four hours. They are then fried in hot oil to make them [puff up](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puffed_grain "wikilink"). The oil is drained thoroughly after frying. The sugar glazing is cooked separately using [muscovado](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/muscovado "wikilink") sugar or [molasses](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/molasses "wikilink") (or [corn syrup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/corn_syrup "wikilink")), salt, butter, and vinegar or [calamansi](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/calamansi "wikilink") juice. The glazing is poured unto the puffed rice and mixed until the grains are evenly coated. It is then allowed to cool and shaped into the desired form before it fully hardens. They are usually cut into square or rectangular blocks or molded into balls.\[4\]\[5\]\[6\]

Traditional ampao is white in color, but many modern variants are dyed in various colors to appeal more to children.\[7\]

## 變化

Ampaw can be easily modified with added ingredients. Examples include roasted [peanuts](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/peanut "wikilink"), [pinipig](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/pinipig "wikilink") (toasted young rice), and [chocolate](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/chocolate "wikilink").\[8\]\[9\] Ampaw can also be made with other types of rice, like [brown rice](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/brown_rice "wikilink") or [black rice](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/black_rice "wikilink").\[10\]

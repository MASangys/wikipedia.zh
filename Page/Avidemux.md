**Avidumux**是一个[开放源代码的跨平台视频编辑软件](../Page/开放源代码.md "wikilink")，遵循[GPL协议](../Page/GPL.md "wikilink")。

## 功能

## 支持输入格式

文件格式：

  - [AVI](../Page/AVI.md "wikilink")，[OpenDML](https://web.archive.org/web/20080117114737/http://www.matrox.com/video/press/papers/odmlff2.cfm)
  - [ASF](../Page/ASF.md "wikilink")
  - [FLV](../Page/FLV.md "wikilink")
  - 图片（[BMP](../Page/BMP.md "wikilink")，[JPEG](../Page/JPEG.md "wikilink")，[PNG](../Page/PNG.md "wikilink")）
  - [Matroska](../Page/Matroska.md "wikilink")
  - [MPEG](../Page/MPEG.md "wikilink") PS, TS
  - [NuppelVideo](http://freshmeat.net/redir/nuppelvideo/13501/url_homepage/nuppelvideo)
  - [OGM](../Page/OGM.md "wikilink")
  - [QuickTime](../Page/QuickTime.md "wikilink")，[MP4](../Page/MP4.md "wikilink")，3GPP

视频格式：

  - [Cinepak](../Page/Cinepak.md "wikilink")
  - DV
  - FFV1
  - [H.263](../Page/H.263.md "wikilink")
  - [H.264/MPEG-4 AVC](../Page/H.264/MPEG-4_AVC.md "wikilink")
  - [HuffYUV](../Page/HuffYUV.md "wikilink")
  - [MPEG-1](../Page/MPEG-1.md "wikilink")，[MPEG-2](../Page/MPEG-2.md "wikilink")
  - [MPEG-4](../Page/MPEG-4.md "wikilink") Simple Profile/Advanced
    Simple Profile（支持编码[FourCCs](../Page/FourCC.md "wikilink"): DIVX,
    DX50, XVID, FMP4, M4S2）
  - [MJPEG](../Page/MJPEG.md "wikilink")
  - MSMPEG-4 v. 2（FourCC DIV3）
  - Raw RGB
  - SVQ3
  - [VP3](../Page/VP3.md "wikilink")
  - [VP6F](../Page/VP6F.md "wikilink")（通过libavcodec）
  - [WMV](../Page/WMV.md "wikilink") 2

音频格式：

  - [AAC](../Page/AAC.md "wikilink")
  - [AC3](../Page/AC3.md "wikilink")
  - AMR narrow band
  - [MP3](../Page/MP3.md "wikilink")，[MP2](../Page/MP2.md "wikilink")
  - [Vorbis](../Page/Vorbis.md "wikilink")
  - [WAV PCM](../Page/Pulse-code_modulation.md "wikilink")
  - [WAV LPCM](../Page/LPCM.md "wikilink")
  - [WMA](../Page/Windows_Media_Audio.md "wikilink")

## 支持输出格式

文件格式：

  - [AVI](../Page/AVI.md "wikilink")（单声道或双声道）
  - [Flash Video](../Page/Flash_Video.md "wikilink")
  - 图片（[BMP](../Page/BMP.md "wikilink")，[JPEG](../Page/JPEG.md "wikilink")）
  - [Matroska](../Page/Matroska.md "wikilink")（Beta）
  - [MP4](../Page/MP4.md "wikilink")
  - [MPEG](../Page/MPEG.md "wikilink")（[VCD](../Page/VCD.md "wikilink")／[SVCD](../Page/SVCD.md "wikilink")／[DVD兼容](../Page/DVD.md "wikilink")）
  - [OGM](../Page/OGM.md "wikilink")

视频格式：

  - FFV1
  - [H.263](../Page/H.263.md "wikilink")
  - [H.264/MPEG-4
    AVC](../Page/H.264/MPEG-4_AVC.md "wikilink")（使用[x264](../Page/x264.md "wikilink")）
  - [HuffYUV](../Page/HuffYUV.md "wikilink")
  - [MPEG-1](../Page/MPEG-1.md "wikilink")，[MPEG-2](../Page/MPEG-2.md "wikilink")
  - [MPEG-4](../Page/MPEG-4.md "wikilink")（SP/ASP，使用[FFmpeg](../Page/FFmpeg.md "wikilink")
    [MPEG-4或](../Page/MPEG-4.md "wikilink")[Xvid](../Page/Xvid.md "wikilink")）
  - [MJPEG](../Page/MJPEG.md "wikilink")
  - [Snow](../Page/Snow_\(codec\).md "wikilink")

音频格式：

  - [AAC](../Page/AAC.md "wikilink")
  - [AC3](../Page/AC3.md "wikilink")
  - [MP3](../Page/MP3.md "wikilink")，[MP2](../Page/MP2.md "wikilink")
  - [Ogg Vorbis](../Page/Vorbis.md "wikilink")
  - [WAV PCM](../Page/Pulse-code_modulation.md "wikilink")
  - [WAV LPCM](../Page/LPCM.md "wikilink")

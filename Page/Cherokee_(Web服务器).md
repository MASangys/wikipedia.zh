**Cherokee**是一个高性能、灵活、易用的开源Web服务器软件，可以在[Linux](../Page/Linux.md "wikilink")、[Solaris](../Page/Solaris.md "wikilink")、[Mac
OS
X](../Page/Mac_OS_X.md "wikilink")，和<s>[Windows](../Page/Windows.md "wikilink")</s>等操作系统上运行，除了其他Web服务器软件具有的功能外，Cherokee包含了一个名为cherokee-admin的Web配置界面，可以轻易的搭建Web服务器。

## 特点

### Web应用程序\[1\]

Cherokee支持下列动态脚本语言与接口技术：

  - [FastCGI](../Page/FastCGI.md "wikilink")
  - [SCGI](../Page/Simple_Common_Gateway_Interface.md "wikilink")
  - [PHP](../Page/PHP.md "wikilink")
  - [Ruby on Rails](../Page/Ruby_on_Rails.md "wikilink")
  - [ColdFusion](../Page/ColdFusion.md "wikilink")
  - [GlassFish](../Page/GlassFish.md "wikilink")
  - [Django](../Page/Django_\(web_framework\).md "wikilink")
  - [CGI](../Page/Common_Gateway_Interface.md "wikilink")
  - Streaming audio and video

### Web服务器功能

  - [TLS和SSL](../Page/Transport_Layer_Security.md "wikilink")
    \[2\]</ref>
  - [虚拟主机](../Page/虚拟主机.md "wikilink")\[3\]
  - [Authentication](../Page/Authentication.md "wikilink") via htdigest,
    [htpasswd](../Page/htpasswd.md "wikilink")、[LDAP](../Page/LDAP.md "wikilink")、[MySQL](../Page/MySQL.md "wikilink")、[PAM](../Page/PAM.md "wikilink"),
    Plain, and Fixed list. \[4\]
  - [Apache兼容日志格式](../Page/Apache_HTTP_Server.md "wikilink")\[5\]

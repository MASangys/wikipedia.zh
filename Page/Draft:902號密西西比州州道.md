**902號密西西比州州道**（），亦作**35號密西西比州州道**（），是位於[密西西比州](../Page/密西西比州.md "wikilink")中部的[州级公路](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/州级公路 "wikilink")。The route starts at [MS 481](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi_Highway_481 "wikilink") in [Burns](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burns,_Mississippi "wikilink") and it travels northeastwards. It travels within the [Bienville National Forest](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bienville_National_Forest "wikilink") and it ends at [MS 35](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi_Highway_35 "wikilink") in [Lorena](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lorena,_Mississippi "wikilink"). The road was constructed around 1967 and was paved by 1968. The route was signed as MS 902 on state maps by 1998.

[:en:Mississippi Highway 902](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Mississippi_Highway_902 "wikilink")

## Route description

All of MS 902 is located in [Smith County](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Smith_County,_Mississippi "wikilink").\[1\] In 2017, the [Mississippi Department of Transportation](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi_Department_of_Transportation "wikilink") (MDOT) calculated 190 vehicles traveling on MS 902 north of County Road 32-A (CO 32-A) [on average each day](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Annual_average_daily_traffic "wikilink").\[2\] The road is legally defined in Mississippi Code § 65-3-3, as both MS 902 and MS 35 Connector,\[3\] but it is signed as MS 902.\[4\] The route is maintained by MDOT as part of the [Mississippi State Highway System](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mississippi_State_Highway_System "wikilink").\[5\]

MS 902 starts at a [three-way junction](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/three-way_junction "wikilink") with MS 481 in the [unincorporated area](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/unincorporated_area "wikilink") of Burns and travels northward. As the route is completely within the [Bienville National Forest](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bienville_National_Forest "wikilink"),\[6\] the majority of the road is surrounded by forests, with several farms located along the way.\[7\] It then intersects CO 502-A, a [gravel](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/gravel_road "wikilink") [spur route](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/spur_route "wikilink") of CO 502, and CO 32-A, which travels to MS 35 near [Lemon](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lemon,_Mississippi "wikilink"). MS 902 turns northeastwards south of CO 32-B, and continues in that direction until it ends at MS 35 in Lorena. The road continues as CO 32, which ends at CO 541 northwest of [Klein](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Klein,_Mississippi "wikilink").\[8\]

## History

Around 1967, a [gravel road](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/gravel_road "wikilink") from MS 481 to MS 35 was constructed within the Bienville National Forest in Smith County,\[9\]\[10\] and it was paved one year later.\[11\]\[12\] The road was signed as MS 902 on the state highway map by 1998.\[13\]\[14\]

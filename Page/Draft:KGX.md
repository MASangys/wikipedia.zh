KGX can mean:

  - The [SIL code](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethnologue#History) for the [Kamaru language](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KGX) of Sulawesi
  - [FAA identifier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Location_identifier) for the[Grayling Airport](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grayling_Airport) of Grayling, [Alaska](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alaska)
  - The station code for [London King's Cross railway station](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/London_King%27s_Cross_railway_station)
  - The name for [Palm Springs radio station](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Template:Palm_Springs_Radio) [KKGX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KKGX)

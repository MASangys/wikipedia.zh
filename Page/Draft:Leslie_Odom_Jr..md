小萊斯利·奧多姆（[Leslie Odom
Jr.](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Leslie_Odom_Jr. "wikilink")
，生於1981年8月6日）是一名美籍演員與歌手。他曾在美國百老匯、電視劇與電影中演出，並發行過兩張爵士獨唱專輯。他因在百老匯音樂劇「漢密爾頓」中扮演Aaron
Burr而聞名，這使他獲頒為2016年托尼獎最佳音樂劇男主角、作為主唱而獲得葛萊美獎的最佳音樂劇專輯獎

**Leslie Odom Jr.** (, born August 6, 1981) is an American actor and
singer. He has performed on [Broadway](../Page/百老匯劇院.md "wikilink") and
in television and film, and has released two solo jazz albums. He is
known for originating the role of [Aaron
Burr](../Page/阿龙·伯尔.md "wikilink") in the Broadway musical
*[Hamilton](../Page/漢密爾頓_\(音樂劇\).md "wikilink")*, a performance for
which he won the 2016 [Tony Award for Best Actor in a
Musical](../Page/托尼奖最佳音乐剧男主角.md "wikilink") and the [Grammy
Award](../Page/葛萊美獎.md "wikilink") for  as a principal vocalist.\[1\]
His television roles included Sam Strickland in the musical series
*[Smash](../Page/名聲大噪.md "wikilink")* (2012–2013). He is also the author
of the  2018 book *Failing Up*.\[2\]

## Early life

Odom was born in [Queens](../Page/皇后區.md "wikilink"), New York.\[3\] His
family later moved to the  section of
[Philadelphia](../Page/費城.md "wikilink"), where he grew up. He
attended  for middle school and  for high school.\[4\]\[5\] He and his
family attended Canaan Baptist Church in the  section of Philadelphia,
where he sang solos in the church choir.\[6\]

He earned a degree with honors at [Carnegie Mellon
University](../Page/卡内基·梅隆大学.md "wikilink") in Pittsburgh,
Pennsylvania, and then moved to Los Angeles in the summer of 2003.

## Career

### Acting career

#### Theatre

Odom sang as a teenager, and studied musical theatre in college. At the
age of 17 he made his [Broadway](../Page/百老匯劇院.md "wikilink") debut as
Paul in *[Rent](../Page/吉屋出租.md "wikilink")*. In 2000, he appeared in
the ensemble of the one-night Broadway concert version of **.

He spent much of his time doing theatre in LA, including in 2010 with
,\[7\] which moved to Broadway in 2012, with him starring as Isaiah
Sturdevant. The show closed shortly afterwards, however he made the move
to New York and relocated there.

After starring
[off-Broadway](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/外百老匯 "wikilink") in
**, and in ** (later retitled *[Invisible
Thread](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Invisible_Thread_\(musical\) "wikilink")*)
at A.R.T. and in workshops, Odom worked with  in the  production of **
playing Michael. Odom also played [Nat King
Cole](../Page/納·京·高爾.md "wikilink") in the 2015 one-night
benefit concert of **.\[8\]

Odom was nominated for a 2015 \[9\] for the role of [Aaron
Burr](../Page/阿龙·伯尔.md "wikilink") in the off-Broadway production of
*[Hamilton](../Page/漢密爾頓_\(音樂劇\).md "wikilink")* at . He continued in
the same role at the  after the show transferred to
[Broadway](../Page/百老匯劇院.md "wikilink") later that year. He also won
a 2016 Grammy Award for the cast album, and won the [Tony Award for Best
Actor in a Musical](../Page/托尼奖最佳音乐剧男主角.md "wikilink").

Odom's final appearance in the role of Aaron Burr in *Hamilton* occurred
on July 9, 2016.\[10\]

#### Television

Odom had a recurring role on *[CSI:
Miami](../Page/CSI犯罪現場：邁阿密.md "wikilink")* which began
in 2003. He appeared as Joseph Kayle in nine episodes before leaving in
2006.

He followed with a recurring role on **, appearing in 10 episodes as
Maliko Christo. From 2006 to 2007, he held a recurring role as Freddy on
**, appearing on nine episodes prior to its cancellation. He also had a
supporting role in the TV movie *Poe* in 2011.

In the musical television series *[Smash](../Page/名聲大噪.md "wikilink")*,
Odom had a recurring role as Sam Strickland in 2012, and was promoted to
a starring role for the show's final season in 2013.

In late 2013, Odom was offered the leading role of Lucas Newsome on
*[State of Affairs](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/國務危情 "wikilink")*. He
accepted the role, but ended up backing out to take the part of [Aaron
Burr](../Page/阿龙·伯尔.md "wikilink") in the workshops of the musical
*[Hamilton](../Page/漢密爾頓_\(音樂劇\).md "wikilink")*.

In 2013 and 2014, he had a recurring role on *[Person of
Interest](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/疑犯追踪 "wikilink")* as Peter
Collier, appearing in eight episodes. He also played Reverend Curtis
Scott in seven episodes of *[Law & Order: Special Victims
Unit](../Page/法律与秩序：特殊受害者.md "wikilink")* between 2013 and 2015.

Beginning on May 19, 2016, Odom hosted the  web series *Aaron Burr, Sir:
Backstage at Hamilton with Leslie Odom Jr.*, with a new episode being
uploaded every Thursday for eight weeks. Odom was also set to play  in a
2017 miniseries about [New Edition](../Page/新版本合唱團.md "wikilink"), but
ended up leaving the project.

He has made guest appearances on several shows including *[Gilmore
Girls](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/奇異果女孩 "wikilink")*, *[Grey's
Anatomy](../Page/實習醫生_\(電視劇\).md "wikilink")*,
*[Supernatural](../Page/邪恶力量.md "wikilink")*, and *[The Good
Wife](../Page/「法」妻.md "wikilink")*. In 2016, Odom was also featured in a
commercial for .\[11\]\[12\]

On February 4, 2018, Odom sang "[America the
Beautiful](../Page/美丽的阿美利加.md "wikilink")" during the
[NBC](../Page/全国广播公司.md "wikilink") broadcast of .\[13\]

#### Film

Odom has appeared in short films, and had a supporting role as Declan
Hall in the 2012 war film *[Red Tails](../Page/紅色尾翼.md "wikilink")*.

He played Dr. Arbuthnot in [Kenneth
Branagh](../Page/簡尼夫·班納.md "wikilink")'s 2017 adaptation of
*[Murder on the Orient
Express](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/東方快車謀殺案_\(2017年電影\) "wikilink")*.\[14\]

### Recording career

In 2014, Odom self-released his debut album as a jazz singer, ** on CD
and SoundCloud, and promoted the album with several concerts at . In
February 2016, he released a version of [Selena
Gomez](../Page/赛琳娜·戈麦斯.md "wikilink")'s "" featuring his
*Hamilton* castmate .\[15\]

Odom was signed to a four-album deal with  in 2016.\[16\] He and
producer  narrowed down 200 potential tunes to ten tracks, and Odom
recorded an updated and improved version of *Leslie Odom Jr.* during
days off and afternoons before Broadway performances, in order to
release the album before Odom left *Hamilton*.\[17\] Released in June
2016, the album charted at No. 1 on
[Billboard](../Page/告示牌_\(雜誌\).md "wikilink") Jazz and No. 147
on [Billboard 200](../Page/公告牌二百强专辑榜.md "wikilink").

On November 11, 2016, Odom released his second album, **, which featured
jazz interpretations of Christmas standards. A deluxe edition of the
album was released in October 2017, with four additional songs.\[18\]

Odom tours to promote his albums, performing concerts backed by a jazz
quintet that includes a drummer, percussionist, bassist, guitarist, and
a pianist who is also Odom's musical director.\[19\]

### Writing

In June 2017, it was announced that Odom had a book deal for *Failing
Up: How to Take Risks, Aim Higher, and Never Stop Learning*, which he
was writing with the intention to inspire younger readers.\[20\] The
book,  by an imprint of [Macmillan](../Page/麥克米倫出版公司.md "wikilink") in
March 2018, "outlines the setbacks and rejections that preceded his
success."\[21\] Odom modeled his writing on the style of a commencement
speech, exploring what he had learned throughout his life and the
importance of pursuing passions.\[22\]

## Personal life

Odom has been married to actress , since December 1, 2012.\[23\] Their
daughter, Lucille Ruby, was born on April 23, 2017.\[24\]

The couple met in 2008, when she was a student at
[UCLA](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/加利福尼亚大学洛杉矶分校 "wikilink") and
auditioned for ** in Los Angeles.\[25\] When Robinson replaced a cast
member who left unexpectedly, assistant director Odom became responsible
for bringing her quickly up to speed, and a romantic relationship
ensued.\[26\] Robinson has since appeared in the off-Broadway musical
*[Invisible
Thread](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Invisible_Thread_\(musical\) "wikilink")*
(2015) and the television series *[The
Affair](../Page/婚外情事.md "wikilink")*.\[27\]

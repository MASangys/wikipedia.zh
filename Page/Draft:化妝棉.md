**化妝棉**是由[棉花製成的小塊布料](../Page/棉花.md "wikilink")，通常於[化妝時使用或用於醫學領域](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/化妝 "wikilink")。\[1\]於醫療上使用化妝棉可用阻止或避免穿刺造成的[出血](../Page/出血.md "wikilink")，
例如[注射或](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/注射 "wikilink")[採血](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/採血 "wikilink")。\[2\]應使用膠帶固定。化妝棉也應用廣泛，也用於卸除[化妝品](../Page/化妝品.md "wikilink")。\[3\]化妝棉足夠柔軟，以至於能用於清潔[嬰兒](../Page/嬰兒.md "wikilink")。\[4\]**棉球**也有一些用途與化妝棉相同，並可交替使用。

儘管如此，現代棉球和化妝棉不是全部由棉組成，而由成本低的[漂白劑](../Page/漂白劑.md "wikilink")、[合成纖維](../Page/合成纖維.md "wikilink")，例如[聚酯](../Page/聚酯.md "wikilink")\[5\]和[尼龍組成](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/尼龍 "wikilink")。\[6\]在[美国](../Page/美国.md "wikilink")，這些產品因非編織材料通常標示為"棉球"（例如，一次性、[尿片和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/尿片 "wikilink")[衛生棉條等](../Page/衛生棉條.md "wikilink")）
are excluded from the [聯邦貿易委員會](../Page/聯邦貿易委員會.md "wikilink")'s
stringent labeling requirements for [布](../Page/布.md "wikilink")
products ().\[7\]\[8\]

棉球[恐懼症曾有報導](../Page/特殊恐惧症.md "wikilink")，但目前盛行率未知。\[9\]

## 歷史

Use of cotton for sanitary purposes likely dates back to . There is
evidence that [衛生紙](../Page/衛生紙.md "wikilink"), made in part of cotton
and/or other plant fibers such as
[麻部](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/麻部 "wikilink"),\[10\]\[11\]
was used at least as early as
[589年](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/589年 "wikilink") in
[中国历史](../Page/中国历史.md "wikilink").\[12\] Cotton balls have been
used for applying [金箔](../Page/金箔.md "wikilink") since at least as far
back as 1801. An artists' manual from that year recommends using a
"squirrel's tail, or cotton ball" to press the gold leaf into
place.\[13\] There is some evidence that they were being mass produced
as far back as 1816, namely an advertisement taken out of the
*[紐約郵報](../Page/紐約郵報.md "wikilink")* by Palmer, Nichols & Co.
for many different kinds of fabric and products made of cotton which
lists "Cotton Balls" as an item for sale.\[14\] In 1891  ran a story
about women who put cotton balls in their cheeks to make themselves
appear less thin.\[15\] An 1898 patent by Jerome B. Dillon for a new
type of [脐带](../Page/脐带.md "wikilink")
[繃帶](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/繃帶 "wikilink") used an
"antiseptic, absorbent cotton pad" to carry out its function.\[16\]

In 1937, Joseph A. Voss invented a machine which unraveled rolls of
cotton and cut them at a fixed interval into cotton pads,\[17\] starting
the widespread consumption of cotton balls and pads. Companies producing
cotton balls took out ads in newspapers as early as 1948 to promote
their uses to the public.\[18\] In 1965, the  reported that the sanitary
cotton industry in the United States was worth US$60 million (US$460.4
million in 2016 [消費者物價指數](../Page/消費者物價指數.md "wikilink")-
[美元](../Page/美元.md "wikilink")).\[19\] Around this time, there
was industry concern that sanitary products using
[尼龙](../Page/尼龙.md "wikilink"), labeled as cotton balls, were
going to crowd out cotton balls actually containing cotton, harming
cotton-exporting regions.\[20\] In 1986,
[強生公司](../Page/強生公司.md "wikilink"), a manufacturer of
cotton balls, published advertisements stating that "doctors advise"
cotton balls over "synthetic puffs".\[21\] By 2013 however, most
consumer cotton balls and pads outside of specifically labeled "100%
Cotton"  contained mostly polyester and only nominal amounts of
cotton.\[22\]

In 2015, *[Mass Market
Retailers](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mass_Market_Retailers "wikilink")*,
a [超級市場](../Page/超級市場.md "wikilink") and
[連鎖店](../Page/連鎖店.md "wikilink") , estimated that combined
sales of cotton balls and pads in the United States were US$177.7
million for the year 2014,\[23\] down from US$343.1 million in
1999.\[24\] The change could be due to increases of sales of cheaper :
in 1999, only 50.1% of sold cotton balls were store branded,\[25\]
versus 88% in 2014.\[26\] The top three cotton ball brands in the United
States are *Swisspers* (manufactured by U.S. Cotton), *Swiss Beauty*
(U.S. Cotton),\[27\] and *Cotton Cloud* (Wabbit, Inc.).\[28\]

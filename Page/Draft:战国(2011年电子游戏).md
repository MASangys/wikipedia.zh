**战国**是一部由[Paradox Development
Studio制作](../Page/Paradox_Development_Studio.md "wikilink")，[Paradox
Interactive发行的大战略](../Page/Paradox_Interactive.md "wikilink")[电子游戏](../Page/电子游戏.md "wikilink")。

## 游戏概况

在游戏中，玩家可以扮演1467年的一位[战国时代](../Page/战国时代_\(日本\).md "wikilink")[日本贵族](../Page/日本.md "wikilink")。游戏的标题“Sengoku”，来源于日本历史上著名的战国时代。
该游戏在玩法上类似于另一款之后推出的P社游戏《[十字军之王II](../Page/十字军之王II.md "wikilink")》。贵族们拥有各种头衔，并得到来自采邑和封臣的收入。游戏中的人物关系基于其性格特质与情势。从封臣处可征召的军队数量取决于封臣对玩家的喜好程度。
Mercenaries and hired retinues supplement their levies. Hostages are
held to confirm peace treaties.

## Reception

The game received "average" reviews according to the [review
aggregation](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Review_aggregator "wikilink")
website [Metacritic](../Page/Metacritic.md "wikilink").\[1\]

The game was criticized by WarGamer for its lack of missions and the
lack of a flexible starting date, atypical for a Paradox game.\[2\]

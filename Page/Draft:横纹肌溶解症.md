**横纹肌溶解症**（英語：Rhabdomyolysis）是人體[肌肉細胞壞死所造成的疾病](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肌肉細胞 "wikilink")\[1\]。一些肌肉细胞崩坏後释放的产物（如[肌红蛋白](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肌红蛋白 "wikilink")）會进入血液並对[肾脏造成傷害](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肾脏 "wikilink")，导致[肾衰竭](../Page/急性腎損傷.md "wikilink")\[2\]。橫紋肌溶解症的症状包括肌肉疼痛、呕吐和意识混乱\[3\]\[4\]，嚴重程度取决於肌肉受损程度和是否发生肾衰竭。患者也可能會有紅棕色的蛋白尿或[心律不整](../Page/心律不整.md "wikilink")\[5\]。

肌肉受損大多是外部原因導致，如[挤压综合征](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/挤压综合征 "wikilink")、過度鍛鍊、[藥品](../Page/藥品.md "wikilink")或[物質濫用](../Page/物質濫用.md "wikilink")\[6\]；其他成因則包刮[感染](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/感染 "wikilink")、[觸電](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/觸電 "wikilink")、[中暑](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/中暑 "wikilink")、長期不活動、或[蛇咬傷](../Page/蛇咬傷.md "wikilink")\[7\]。一些人則因[遺傳](../Page/遺傳.md "wikilink")性因素，導致其肌肉較容易發病\[8\]。橫紋肌溶解症通常藉由和[顯微鏡](../Page/顯微鏡.md "wikilink")檢查診斷，若尿液試紙為陽性但鏡檢不含[紅血球](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/紅血球 "wikilink")，則可能是橫紋肌溶解\[9\]。[血液检查](../Page/血液检查.md "wikilink")則可見[肌酸激酶濃度大於](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肌酸激酶 "wikilink")1,000 U/L，嚴重時可能高於5,000 U/L\[10\]。

主流的治療方法是大量的液體[靜脈注射](../Page/靜脈注射.md "wikilink")\[11\]，針對重症的其他療法還包含[透析](../Page/透析.md "wikilink")及\[12\]\[13\]。若尿液檢查確定有横纹肌溶解症，常常會使用[碳酸氢钠](../Page/碳酸氢钠.md "wikilink")和[甘露醇](../Page/甘露醇.md "wikilink")來治療，但此治療方式缺乏證據支持\[14\]\[15\]。及早治療的預後通常不錯\[16\]。併發症可能有[高鉀血](../Page/高鉀血症.md "wikilink")、、[弥散性血管内凝血](../Page/弥散性血管内凝血.md "wikilink")與[腔室症候群](../Page/腔室症候群.md "wikilink")\[17\]。

橫紋肌溶解全美每年約發生26,000個案\[18\]。歷史上有許多橫紋肌溶解症的紀錄，第一個近代的描述是在1908年的一次地震之後\[19\]。1941年的[倫敦大空襲事件使人們發現了該病的機制與特徵](../Page/伦敦大轰炸.md "wikilink")\[20\]。這對在[地震](../Page/地震.md "wikilink")中受傷的人們格外的重要，因為在類似[災難中罹患橫紋肌溶解症的人們需要醫療團隊來緩解疾病的影響](../Page/灾害.md "wikilink")\[21\]。

## 症狀與徵候

橫紋肌溶解症的症狀取決於其嚴重度和腎衰竭是否發生。較嚴重的患者可能不會有任何肌肉症狀，通常是因其他問題做了[血液檢查後被診斷出來](../Page/血液检查.md "wikilink")。典型的橫紋肌溶血症則會出現患部[肌肉疼痛](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肌肉痛 "wikilink")、壓痛、和[腫脹](../Page/水肿.md "wikilink")\[22\]。若腫脹發生得很快，當體液快速地從血管流向受損的肌肉時，可能會發生[低血壓](../Page/低血壓.md "wikilink")和[休克](../Page/休克.md "wikilink")，這種情況可能在患者從倒塌的建築物被救出時）。其他的症狀較無特異性，通常是肌肉組織崩解或造成肌肉組織損傷的原因所造成\[23\]\[24\]\[25\]。肌肉組織受損後釋出物質到血液中，可能造成[电解质](../Page/电解质.md "wikilink")不平衡，進一步導致[噁心](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/噁心 "wikilink")、[呕吐](../Page/呕吐.md "wikilink")、、[昏迷](../Page/昏迷.md "wikilink")或[心律不整](../Page/心律不整.md "wikilink")；患者的尿液顏色可能因為含有[肌紅蛋白而變深](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肌紅蛋白 "wikilink")，一般醫學文獻將之描述為「茶色尿」。患者的腎臟可能因[尿量減少或](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/少尿症 "wikilink")[無尿而受損](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/無尿症 "wikilink")，這通常在肌肉最初受損後12至24小時發生\[26\]\[27\]。

受損肌肉腫脹有時也會也成[腔室症候群](../Page/腔室症候群.md "wikilink")，即在同個內壓迫到周圍的[血管](../Page/血管.md "wikilink")和神經等組織，阻斷血液供給而使組織受損或功能喪失。腔室症候群的症狀包括患肢疼痛或感覺減少\[28\]\[29\]。[弥散性血管内凝血](../Page/弥散性血管内凝血.md "wikilink")（DIC）是另一種常見的併發症，會使體內的[凝血](../Page/凝血.md "wikilink")機轉崩潰而造成無法控制的出血\[30\]\[31\]\[32\]。

## 病因

### 遺傳傾向

反覆的橫紋肌溶解可能和患者本身的肌肉酵素缺陷有關，這通常是遺傳性疾病且在幼年時便會發生\[68\]\[69\]。許多肌肉結構的疾病都會因運動、或其他上述因素的刺激而反覆出現橫紋肌溶解\[70\]。遺傳性肌肉病變與感染是孩童橫紋肌溶解最常見的兩個原因\[71\]。

下列的遺傳性疾病會造成肌肉的能量供給不足，進而在肌肉出力時反覆造成橫紋肌溶解症：\[72\]\[73\]\[74\]

  - [糖酵解和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/糖酵解 "wikilink")[糖原分解](../Page/糖原分解.md "wikilink")缺陷：（McArdle氏病）、、[肝醣儲積症](../Page/肝醣儲積症.md "wikilink")第、、、型。

  - ：和、各種亞型的缺陷（、、、、）、缺乏症。

  - [粒線體肌肉病變](../Page/线粒体肌病.md "wikilink")：[琥珀酸脱氢酶](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/琥珀酸脱氢酶 "wikilink")、[细胞色素c氧化酶](../Page/细胞色素c氧化酶.md "wikilink")和[辅酶Q10](../Page/辅酶Q10.md "wikilink")缺乏。

  - 其他：[蠶豆症](../Page/葡萄糖-6-磷酸脫氫酶缺乏症.md "wikilink")、和[肌肉萎缩症](../Page/肌肉萎缩症.md "wikilink")。

## 致病機轉

[Myoglobin.png](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Myoglobin.png "fig:Myoglobin.png")模型，此種含有血質基的[蛋白质](../Page/蛋白质.md "wikilink")在正常肌肉中負責儲存氧氣，但在橫紋肌溶解時會造成腎臟受損。 | alt=Structural drawing of the myoglobin molecule\]\] 骨骼肌的損傷有多種形式。撞擊等物理性傷害可能會造成[肌肉細胞的直接損傷](../Page/肌細胞.md "wikilink")，直接影響血流供應。非物力性的損傷則可能影響肌肉細胞的[代謝機制](../Page/代谢.md "wikilink")。一旦肌肉組織受到損傷，來自血行循環的流體會快速填充入肌肉組織，其中也會包含[鈉離子](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鈉離子 "wikilink")。肌肉細胞的水腫可能會直接瓦解肌細胞，倖存下來的肌細胞也可能因電解質平衡系統受到干擾，而造成胞內鈣離子升高。鈉與鈣離子的升高會導致肌肉去極化，並進而導致的鈣離子流入細胞質內\[75\]。胞質內的鈣離子濃度更高可能會導致持續性[肌肉收縮及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肌肉收缩 "wikilink")[ATP的消耗](../Page/三磷酸腺苷.md "wikilink")\[76\]\[77\]。同時鈣離子的升高也會導致鈣離子依賴性的蛋白酶及磷脂酶活化，造成細胞膜及離子通道的瓦解\[78\]。ATP是細胞的能量來源，ATP的存量下降可能會直接導致鈣離子流入無法控制\[79\]。持續性的肌肉收縮可能導致胞內蛋白質及細胞瓦解\[80\]。

身體中含量最豐富的白血球[嗜中性球會進入肌肉組織](../Page/中性粒细胞.md "wikilink")，啟動[發炎反應並釋放](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/發炎反應 "wikilink")[活性氧化物](../Page/活性氧类.md "wikilink")\[81\]，特別是在承受擠壓傷害的患者\[82\]。若[擠壓症候群的壓迫源解除過快](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/挤压综合征 "wikilink")，血液瞬間灌流回到受損組織，可能在之後導致\[83\]。

水腫及發炎的肌肉可能導致壓力增加，壓迫周邊組織，導致[腔室症候群](../Page/腔室症候群.md "wikilink")。水腫也可能會影響該區域的血流供應，最終導致肌肉細胞損傷，釋放[鉀離子](../Page/鉀離子.md "wikilink")、[磷酸根離子](../Page/磷酸鹽.md "wikilink")、[肌红蛋白](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肌红蛋白 "wikilink")、[肌酸激酶](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肌酸激酶 "wikilink")，及[尿酸](../Page/尿酸.md "wikilink")等物質進入血液循環。若觸發凝血系統可能引起[弥散性血管内凝血](../Page/弥散性血管内凝血.md "wikilink")\[84\]。[血中鉀離子濃度過高會導致致命性的](../Page/高鉀血症.md "wikilink")[心律不整](../Page/心律不整.md "wikilink")，而磷酸根濃度過高也會與血鈣結合，引起[低血鈣](../Page/低鈣血症.md "wikilink")\[85\]。

橫紋肌溶解症能透過多種機制導致[腎衰竭](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/腎衰竭 "wikilink")，其中最主要的原因為肌球蛋白累積於[腎小管中](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nephron "wikilink")\[86\]\[87\]\[88\]。正常來說，血液中的會與肌球蛋白等含有血基質的物質結合，但橫紋肌溶解症的患者的血中肌球蛋白量過高，超越血紅素結合蛋白所能夠結合的量\[89\]。當[血漿中的肌球蛋白含量超過](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/血浆 "wikilink") 0.5–1.5 [mg/dl](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/毫克每公合 "wikilink") 時，尿液中即可能出現肌球蛋白，稱為。當血漿肌球蛋白量超過 100 mg/dl 時，尿液中的顏色會出現變化\[90\]。大約僅要200克的肌肉遭到破壞，血中肌球蛋白濃度即可達到此標準\[91\]。腎小管濾液的水分進行再吸收後，肌球蛋白會與腎元中的[尿調理素](../Page/尿調理素.md "wikilink")起反應，會固化形成[尿液圓柱體](../Page/管型_\(尿液\).md "wikilink")，導致濾液流受到阻滯。若尿酸過高或濾液酸化，會促進尿液圓柱體的形成，可能會使腎衰竭的情形更加惡化\[92\]。此外，血基質釋出的鐵會導致活性氧化物產生，進而傷害腎細胞。除了肌球蛋白尿症以外，低血壓和尿酸也會傷害腎臟。低血壓會導致[血管收縮](../Page/血管收縮.md "wikilink")，會使腎臟的灌流量不足；而尿酸會在腎小管中形成晶體，導致尿流阻塞。整體來說，上述這些機轉都會導致[急性腎小管壞死](../Page/急性腎小管壞死.md "wikilink")，破壞腎小管細胞\[93\]\[94\]。腎小管受損後，腎臟無法正常表現其分泌功能，[腎小管過濾率即會下降](../Page/腎功能.md "wikilink")。這導致電解質平衡失調，血液中鉀含量升高。過高的血磷除了會直接與鈣離子結合沉積之外，還會抑制的作用，使[維生素D無法活化](../Page/维生素D.md "wikilink")，致使血鈣降低\[95\]\[96\]。

## 診斷

In skeletal muscle, creatine kinase exists predominantly in dimers containing two M-subunits, also referred to as "CK-MM".\]\] 遭受外傷、擠壓症候群，或長期無法移動的人，都可能需將横纹肌溶解症列入鑑別診斷。橫紋肌溶解症也可能會到比較後期產生腎功能惡化（血中[肌酸酐](../Page/肌酸酐.md "wikilink")和[尿素氮](../Page/尿素氮.md "wikilink")不正常升高）或尿液呈現紅棕色時才被診斷出來\[97\]\[98\]。

### 初始評估

最可靠的鑑驗指標是血液[肌酸激酶上升](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肌酸激酶 "wikilink")\[99\]。肌酸激酶平時存在於肌肉之中，當肌肉細胞受到損傷時會釋出，若血中含量高於 1000 U/L（最高正常值的5倍）時，則代表發生横纹肌溶解\[100\]。若高於 5,000 U/L 代表發生横纹肌溶解重症，高於100,000 /l 則不太常見\[101\]\[102\]。血中肌酸激酶含量在肌肉受損的前12小時會穩定增加，升高趨勢會維持1–3天後才逐漸下降\[103\]。初始及最高肌酸激酶值與急性腎損傷的風險呈線性正相關\[104\]。肌酸激酶的濃度並不能用於診斷腎功能損傷的發生，但在沒有其他潛在因素下，肌酸激酶低於 20,000 U/l 者通常與腎損傷無關聯性。沒有合併腎損傷的輕微程度肌酸激酶升高稱為「高肌酸激酶血症」\[105\]\[106\]。肌球蛋白的半衰期短，因此在晚期疾病的診斷效益並不顯著\[107\]，其在血液及尿液中的含量與腎損傷呈正相關\[108\]。即使這樣，尿液肌球蛋白含量的測量由於缺乏[特異度且缺乏相關研究](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/特异度 "wikilink")，目前並無實證支持\[109\]。

Elevated concentrations of the enzyme [lactate dehydrogenase](../Page/乳酸脱氢酶.md "wikilink") (LDH) may be detected.\[110\]\[111\] Other markers of muscle damage, such as [aldolase](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/醛縮酶 "wikilink"), [troponin](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肌钙蛋白 "wikilink"),  and  (FABP), are mainly used in chronic muscle diseases.\[112\] The [transaminases](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/转氨酶 "wikilink"), enzymes abundant in both liver and muscle tissue, are also usually increased; this can lead to the condition being confused with acute liver injury, at least in the early stages. The incidence of actual acute liver injury is 25% in people with non-traumatic rhabdomyolysis; the mechanism for this is uncertain.\[113\]

[High potassium levels](../Page/高鉀血症.md "wikilink") tend to be a feature of severe rhabdomyolysis.\[114\] [Electrocardiography](../Page/心电图.md "wikilink") (ECG) may show whether the elevated potassium levels are affecting the conduction system of the heart, as suggested by the presence of [T wave](../Page/T波.md "wikilink") changes or broadening of the .\[115\] Low [calcium](../Page/鈣質.md "wikilink") levels may be present in the initial stage due to binding of free calcium to damaged muscle cells.\[116\]

As detectable levels of [myoglobinemia](../Page/横纹肌溶解症.md "wikilink") and  occur, [blood tests](../Page/血液检查.md "wikilink") and [urine tests](../Page/尿液分析.md "wikilink") may show elevated levels of myoglobin. For example, a  may reveal a positive result for "blood", even though no [red blood cells](../Page/红血球.md "wikilink") can be identified on [microscopy](../Page/顯微鏡學.md "wikilink") of the urine; this occurs because the reagent on the test strip reacts with myoglobin.\[117\] The same phenomenon may happen in conditions that lead to [hemolysis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/溶血反應 "wikilink"), the destruction of red blood cells; in hemolysis the [blood serum](../Page/血清.md "wikilink") is also visibly discolored, while in rhabdomyolysis it is normal.\[118\]\[119\] If kidney damage has occurred, microscopy of the urine also reveals [urinary casts](../Page/管型_\(尿液\).md "wikilink") that appear pigmented and granular.\[120\]

### 併發症

[Compartment syndrome](../Page/腔室症候群.md "wikilink") is a clinical diagnosis, i.e., no  conclusively proves its presence or absence, but direct measurement of the pressure in a fascial compartment,\[121\] and the difference between this pressure and the blood pressure,\[122\] may be used to assess its severity. High pressures in the compartment and a small difference between compartment pressure and blood pressure indicate that the blood supply is likely to be insufficient, and that surgical intervention may be needed.\[123\]\[124\]

## 治療

治療的主要目標為治療休克和保存腎功能，初始的治療方式為大量的[靜脈輸液](../Page/靜脈注射.md "wikilink")（通常為[生理食鹽水](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/生理盐水 "wikilink")，即[重量百分濃度](../Page/质量浓度.md "wikilink")0.9%的[氯化鈉溶液](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/氯化钠 "wikilink")）。針對擠壓症候群的患者，也建議在將他們從崩塌結構中救出前先給予靜脈輸液\[136\]\[137\]，足夠的循環液體體積將能應付肌肉腫脹帶來的問題，並避免肌紅素在腎臟堆積\[138\]，輸液量建議為24小時內6至12升\[139\]\[140\]，若要達到高尿流量（成人200–300 ml/h）則可調高輸液速率\[141\]\[142\]，但高輸液速率也可能造成併發症，例如[心衰竭的患者就需特別注意](../Page/心臟衰竭.md "wikilink")\[143\]。

雖然許多文獻建議在靜脈輸液中添加其他物質以減少腎臟損篩，但多數支持這些處置的證據來自動物研究，仍存有爭議\[144\]。[甘露醇](../Page/甘露醇.md "wikilink")可藉由增加血液的[滲透壓來使尿量上升](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/渗透 "wikilink")，可能能防止肌紅蛋白在腎臟堆積，但目前的研究仍未證實其效果，且甘露醇也有使腎功能惡化的風險\[145\]。添加[碳酸氫鹽能減輕血液](../Page/碳酸氢盐.md "wikilink")[酸中毒](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/酸中毒 "wikilink")、鹼化尿液和防止腎小管[管型形成](../Page/管型_\(尿液\).md "wikilink")\[146\]\[147\]，但研究顯示碳酸氫鹽的幫助有限，且可能造成鈣和磷的沉積，進而惡化低血鈣的問題\[148\]\[149\]\[150\]；若採用尿液鹼化治療，尿液的pH值會維持在6.5以上\[151\]。臨床上常使用[呋塞米](../Page/呋塞米.md "wikilink")（furosemide，一種[環利尿劑](../Page/環利尿劑.md "wikilink")）來確保患者有足夠的尿液\[152\]\[153\]，但目前仍缺乏此舉能預防腎衰竭的研究證據\[154\]。

### 電解質

在疾病發生之初，患者通常會發生電解質異常而需予以矯正。高血鉀可能致命，過多的鉀離子則可藉由增加排尿或腎臟替代療法排除 （見下文）\[155\]。立即處置的措施包括注射鈣離子以避免心律不整、使用[胰島素或](../Page/胰岛素.md "wikilink")[沙丁胺醇](../Page/沙丁胺醇.md "wikilink")（salbutamol）使血液中的鉀離子進入細胞、注射碳酸氫鈉溶液等等\[156\]。

血液鈣離子濃度一開始會偏低，但當患者的情況逐漸好轉後，鈣離子由磷酸鹽中釋出，加上維生素D持續製造，最後導致[高血鈣](../Page/高血鈣.md "wikilink")。約有20–30%發生腎衰竭的患者會產生這種「過度矯正」的現象\[157\]\[158\]。

### 急性腎損傷

[Haemofilter.png](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Haemofilter.png "fig:Haemofilter.png") 腎損傷通常發生在肌肉損傷後一兩天\[159\]，若沒有即時給予適當的處置，可能會需要執行（RRT）\[160\]。RRT能移除血液中過多的鉀、酸和磷，由於腎臟受損時無法排出這些物質，因此在腎功能恢復前需持續進行RRT\[161\]。

最常見的RRT有三種：[血液透析](../Page/血液透析.md "wikilink")、和[腹膜透析](../Page/腹膜透析.md "wikilink")，前兩者須建立血液通路（）處理患者的血液，腹膜透析則在腹腔內灌入透析液，之後再將透析液流出。血液透析用於治療橫紋肌溶解時需每日執行，相較之下，[慢性肾脏病](../Page/慢性肾脏病.md "wikilink")患者一星期僅需透析數次。血液透析比血液過濾好的地方在於透析機器在一天內可以給多個人使用，且不需連續輸注[抗凝剂](../Page/抗凝剂.md "wikilink")\[162\]\[163\]；血液過濾移除血中大分子（如肌紅素）的效率雖然比血液透析高\[164\]，但治療效果似乎沒有顯著的優勢\[165\]\[166\]。腹膜透析較不適用於嚴重腹部創傷的病人\[167\]，且效果可能比其他RRT差\[168\]。

## 預後

The prognosis depends on the underlying cause and whether any complications occur. Rhabdomyolysis complicated by acute kidney impairment in patients with traumatic injury may have a [mortality rate](../Page/死亡率.md "wikilink") of 20%.\[173\] Admission to the intensive care unit is associated with a mortality of 22% in the absence of acute kidney injury, and 59% if kidney impairment occurs.\[174\] Most people who have sustained kidney impairment due to rhabdomyolysis fully recover their kidney function.\[175\]

## 流行病學

The exact number of cases of rhabdomyolysis is difficult to establish, because different definitions have been used.\[176\]\[177\] In 1995, hospitals in the U.S. reported 26,000 cases of rhabdomyolysis.\[178\] Up to 85% of people with major traumatic injuries will experience some degree of rhabdomyolysis.\[179\] Of those with rhabdomyolysis, 10–50% develop acute kidney injury.\[180\]\[181\] The risk is higher in people with a history of illicit drug use, alcohol misuse or trauma when compared to muscle diseases, and it is particularly high if multiple contributing factors occur together.\[182\] Rhabdomyolysis accounts for 7–10% of all cases of acute kidney injury in the U.S.\[183\]\[184\]

Crush injuries are common in major disasters, especially in earthquakes. The aftermath of the [1988 Spitak earthquake](../Page/1988年亞美尼亞大地震.md "wikilink") prompted the establishment, in 1995, of the Renal Disaster Relief Task Force, a working group of the  (a worldwide body of kidney experts). Its volunteer doctors and nurses assisted for the first time in the [1999 İzmit earthquake](../Page/1999年伊茲密特地震.md "wikilink") in [Turkey](../Page/土耳其.md "wikilink"), where 17,480 people died, 5392 were hospitalized and 477 received dialysis, with positive results. Treatment units are generally established outside the immediate disaster area, as [aftershocks](../Page/地震序列.md "wikilink") could potentially injure or kill staff and make equipment unusable.\[185\]\[186\]

Acute exertional rhabdomyolysis happens in 2% to 40% of people going through basic training for the United States military. In 2012, the United States military reported 402 cases.\[187\]

## 歷史

[Blitzaftermath.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Blitzaftermath.jpg "fig:Blitzaftermath.jpg") led to numerous discoveries in the mechanisms underlying kidney impairment in rhabdomyolysis. | alt=Historical photograph of a building severely damaged by air-raid bombing; firefighters are putting out a blaze in the ruins.\]\] The Bible may contain an early account of rhabdomyolysis. In , the Pentateuch says that the Jews demanded meat while traveling in the desert; God sent quail in response to the complaints, and people ate large quantities of quail meat. A plague then broke out, killing numerous people. Rhabdomyolysis after consuming quail was described in more recent times and called ** (after *[Coturnix](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鹑属 "wikilink")*, the main quail genus).\[188\]\[189\] Migrating quail consume large amounts of [hemlock](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/毒参属 "wikilink"), a known cause of rhabdomyolysis.\[190\]

In modern times, early reports from the [1908 Messina earthquake](../Page/1908年墨西拿地震.md "wikilink") and World War I on kidney failure after injury were followed by studies by London physicians  and [Desmond Beall](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Desmond_Beall "wikilink"), working at the  and the , on four victims of [The Blitz](../Page/伦敦大轰炸.md "wikilink") in 1941.\[191\]\[192\]\[193\] Myoglobin was demonstrated in the urine of victims by [spectroscopy](../Page/光谱学.md "wikilink"), and it was noted that the kidneys of victims resembled those of patients who had [hemoglobinuria](../Page/血紅素尿症.md "wikilink") (hemoglobin rather than myoglobin being the cause of the kidney damage). In 1944 Bywaters demonstrated experimentally that the kidney failure was mainly caused by myoglobin.\[194\]\[195\] Already during the war, teams of doctors traveled to bombed areas to provide medical support, chiefly with intravenous fluids, as dialysis was not yet available.\[196\] The prognosis of acute kidney failure improved markedly when dialysis was added to supportive treatment, which first happened during the 1950–1953 [Korean War](../Page/朝鲜战争.md "wikilink").\[197\]

## 獸醫學

Rhabdomyolysis is recognized in horses.\[198\] Horses can develop a number of muscle disorders, many of which may progress to rhabdomyolysis. Of these, some cause isolated attacks of rhabdomyolysis (e.g., dietary deficiency in [vitamin E](../Page/维生素E.md "wikilink") and [selenium](../Page/硒.md "wikilink"), poisoning associated with pasture or agricultural poisons such as [organophosphates](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/磷酸酯 "wikilink")), while others predispose to exertional rhabdomyolysis (e.g., the hereditary condition ).\[199\] 5–10% of [thoroughbred](../Page/純種馬.md "wikilink") horses and some  horses suffer from the condition ; no specific cause has been identified, but an underlying muscle calcium regulation disorder is suspected.\[200\]

Rhabdomyolysis affecting horses may also occur in outbreaks; these have been reported in many European countries, and later in Canada, Australia, and the United States. It has been referred to as "atypical myopathy" or "myoglobinuria of unknown etiology". No single cause has yet been found, but various mechanisms have been proposed,\[201\] and a seasonal pattern has been observed.\[202\] Very high creatine kinase levels are detected, and mortality from this condition is 89%.\[203\]

**清捲舌顫音**是一個[輔音](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/輔音 "wikilink")，[國際音標](../Page/國際音標.md "wikilink")（IPA）寫作。該音在[迪維西語中作為](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/迪維西語 "wikilink")的[同位異音使用](../Page/同位異音.md "wikilink")。\[1\]雖然舌頭在舌下（sub-apical）的位置
Although the tongue starts out in a sub-apical retroflex position,
trilling involves the tip of the tongue and causes it to move forward to
the alveolar ridge; this means that the retroflex trill gives a
preceding vowel retroflex coloration the way other retroflex consonants
do, but the vibration itself is not much different from an [alveolar
trill](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/alveolar_trill "wikilink").

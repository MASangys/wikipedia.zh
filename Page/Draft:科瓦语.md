**科瓦语**(，Khō-wār)，或称**奇特拉尔语**，是使用于[巴基斯坦和](../Page/巴基斯坦.md "wikilink")[印度的一种语言](../Page/印度.md "wikilink")，该语言系属于[印欧语系](../Page/印欧语系.md "wikilink")[印度-伊朗语族的](../Page/印度-伊朗语族.md "wikilink")，为的母语。在[英属印度时期](../Page/英属印度.md "wikilink")，这种语言曾被称作奇特拉尔语（Chitrālī）或Qāshqār
，而现今多称为科瓦语。“科瓦”（Khō-wār）一词为科人对自己语言的称呼，其中“科”（Khō）指科人，“瓦”（wār）意为语言，“科瓦”即科人的语言（Khowar）\[1\]
。

[吉尔吉特-巴尔蒂斯坦](../Page/吉尔吉特-巴尔蒂斯坦.md "wikilink")

**Khowar** (), also known as **Chitrali**, is an
[印度-雅利安语支](../Page/印度-雅利安语支.md "wikilink") of
the|Dardic subbranch.\[2\]

"Kho" means the people of Chitral, "War" means language. It is spoken by
the [Kho people](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kho_people "wikilink") in
the [Chitral
District](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chitral_District "wikilink"),
[吉泽尔县](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/吉泽尔县 "wikilink") district of

(including the , , Phandar  and Gupis) and in parts of Upper
[斯瓦特县](../Page/斯瓦特县.md "wikilink") (Mateltan Village),
[巴基斯坦](../Page/巴基斯坦.md "wikilink"), as well as in
[查谟-克什米尔邦](../Page/查谟-克什米尔邦.md "wikilink"),
[印度](../Page/印度.md "wikilink").\[3\]

modern Indian languages, retaining a great part of

[元音長度](../Page/元音長度.md "wikilink") , , , , and . Sources
are inconsistent on whether length is phonemic, with one author stating
"vowel-length is observed mainly as a substitute one. The vowel-length

neighboring and related , Khowar does not have retroflex vowels.\[9\]

## 书写系统

Since the early twentieth century Khowar has been written in the , which
is based on the [烏爾都字母](../Page/烏爾都字母.md "wikilink") and uses the
[波斯体](../Page/波斯体.md "wikilink"). Prior to that, the language was
carried on through [口头传统](../Page/口头传统.md "wikilink"). Today
[乌尔都语](../Page/乌尔都语.md "wikilink") and English are the official
languages and the only major literary usage of Khowar is in both poetry
and prose composition. Khowar has also been occasionally written in a
version of the Roman script called  since the 1960s.

**舞力全開2019**（英文：**Just Dance 2019**）是 [Ubisoft](../Page/育碧.md "wikilink") 開發的舞蹈[節奏遊戲](../Page/节奏游戏.md "wikilink")。 它於2018年6月11日在[E3新聞發布會上亮相](../Page/E3电子娱乐展.md "wikilink")\[1\]，並於2018年10月23日在北美於 [Nintendo Switch](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_switch "wikilink")、[Wii](../Page/Wii.md "wikilink")、[Wii U](../Page/Wii_U.md "wikilink")、[PlayStation 4](../Page/PlayStation_4.md "wikilink")、[Xbox One](../Page/Xbox_One.md "wikilink") 和 [Xbox 360](../Page/Xbox_360.md "wikilink") 上發布。\[2\]\[3\] 2018年10月26日，此遊戲在[歐洲和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/歐洲 "wikilink")[澳大利亞發布](../Page/澳大利亚.md "wikilink")。\[4\] 這也是 [Ubisoft](../Page/育碧.md "wikilink") 為 [Wii U](../Page/Wii_U.md "wikilink") 和 [Xbox 360](../Page/Xbox_360.md "wikilink") 發布的最後一款[舞力全開系列遊戲](../Page/舞力全开系列.md "wikilink")。

該遊戲的[試玩版於](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/試玩版 "wikilink")2018年11月12日在 [Xbox One](../Page/Xbox_One.md "wikilink")、[PlayStation 4](../Page/PlayStation_4.md "wikilink")、[Wii U](../Page/Wii_U.md "wikilink") 和 [Nintendo Switch](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nintendo_switch "wikilink") 上發布。\[5\]\[6\]

## Gameplay

As with the previous installments of the franchise, players must mimic the on-screen dancer's choreography to a chosen song using either [motion controllers](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/motion_controller "wikilink") or the game's associated smartphone [app](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/mobile_app "wikilink"). The *Just Dance* app is no longer supported on Nintendo platforms, electing to exclusively use the consoles' native motion controls instead. The game's user interface received a significant redesign, with a focus on recommendations and curated playlists, and removing other modes such as minigames to focus on core gameplay.\[7\]

## Reception

*Nintendo Life* gave *Just Dance 2019* a 7 out of 10, noting that its stripped-down experience was "a strange shift to behold, suggesting that the game is trying to adapt to a new [Spotify](../Page/Spotify.md "wikilink")-using, social media-loving, older teen audience, or maybe growing up alongside those who have supported it from the beginning", and also acknowledged Ubisoft's continued emphasis on pushing its subscription-based offerings for the franchise, as well as performance issues and the removal of *Just Dance* Controller app support on the Nintendo Switch version.\[8\]

The game won the award for "Favorite Video Game" at the [2019 Kids' Choice Awards](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2019_Kids'_Choice_Awards "wikilink"),\[9\] and was nominated for "People's Choice" at the Italian Video Game Awards.\[10\]

**西班牙長槍黨 （Falange Española de las JONS）** ([西班牙语](../Page/西班牙语.md "wikilink") for "Spanish Phalanx of the Committees for the National-Syndicalist Offensive", **FE-JONS**)是一個1976年註冊的西班牙政黨，起源於前一政黨[傳統主義西班牙國家工團主義進攻委員會方陣](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/傳統主義西班牙國家工團主義進攻委員會方陣 "wikilink")\[1\] The word  is Spanish for [方陣 (軍事)](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/方陣_\(軍事\) "wikilink"). Members of the party are called **Falangists** (). 該黨主要是[国家工团主义](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/国家工团主义 "wikilink")、[第三位置](../Page/第三位置.md "wikilink")和.

## 历史

在1975年[弗朗西斯科·佛朗哥](../Page/弗朗西斯科·佛朗哥.md "wikilink")死后，也就是[西班牙民主转型](../Page/西班牙民主转型.md "wikilink"),有一部分的右翼人士试图重新采取20世纪30年代的政策。Originally, FE-JONS was linked with the  in [西班牙](../Page/西班牙.md "wikilink"), along with other similar groups.\[2\] A prominent member of the party was linked with the . This strategy continued in the following years,\[3\]\[4\] although the party also participated in elections and fully legal activities. In 1980 an "escuadrilla" (squadron) of the party killed Juan Carlos García Pérez in [線型之都區](../Page/線型之都區.md "wikilink"), [马德里](../Page/马德里.md "wikilink").\[5\]

After the electoral defeat in the general elections of 1977, in which the candidacies openly defending [neo-francoist](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francoism "wikilink") positions gained less than the 1% of the vote, the party begun a gradual distancing from the [佛朗哥时期](../Page/佛朗哥时期.md "wikilink"), highlighting the thoughts of pre-Franco falangists, like [何塞·安东尼奥·普里莫·德里维拉](../Page/何塞·安东尼奥·普里莫·德里维拉.md "wikilink") or .\[6\] In 1979 the [Círculos Doctrinales José Antonio](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Círculos_Doctrinales_José_Antonio "wikilink") joined the organization, in an attempt to unite [长枪党主义](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/长枪党主义 "wikilink") under a single political party. 同一年，西班牙长枪党与以及其他许多[卡洛斯主义](../Page/卡洛斯主义.md "wikilink")政治组织组成一个名叫的政党联盟。在中，该政党联盟共获得378,964张选票(2.11%)，并拿到一个议员席位。西班牙长枪党没有参与23-F，即。

, the "National Chief" of the party since its foundation, resigned in 1983. Diego Márquez Horrillo was elected as the new chief the same year. 自那以后，该党摒弃了[佛朗哥主义](../Page/佛朗哥时期.md "wikilink")，自称其为原[西班牙長槍黨](../Page/西班牙長槍黨.md "wikilink")的继承者，and fully rejecting the "Unification Decree" of 1937.

In 1999 a sector of the party split, forming . In 2004 the small faction  (FEI) joined FE-JONS. In 2011 the organization elected a new national chief, Norberto Pedro Pico Sanabria. Pico was an ex-member of the FEI. In 2012 another small faction, Mesa Nacional Falangista, joined FE-JONS.\[7\]

是[十足目](../Page/十足目.md "wikilink")[短尾下目](../Page/短尾下目.md "wikilink")（[螃蟹](../Page/螃蟹.md "wikilink")）的[綿蟹總科之下三個科的其中一個](../Page/綿蟹總科.md "wikilink")，其成員均為史前已滅絕的[化石種](../Page/化石種.md "wikilink")。
本科生物最早期的[化石是在](../Page/化石.md "wikilink")[白垩纪出現](../Page/白垩纪.md "wikilink")，直到[古新世](../Page/古新世.md "wikilink")\[1\]。

## 分類

現時本科包括有七個屬\[2\]：

  - *[Caloxanthus](../Page/Caloxanthus.md "wikilink")* <small>A.
    Milne-Edwards, 1864</small>
  - *[Etyus](../Page/Etyus.md "wikilink")* <small>Mantell, 1822</small>
  - *[Etyxanthosia](../Page/Etyxanthosia.md "wikilink")* <small>Fraaije,
    Van Bakel, Jagt & Artal, 2008</small>
  - *[Feldmannia](../Page/Feldmannia.md "wikilink")* <small>Guinot &
    Tavares, 2001</small>
  - *[Guinotosia](../Page/Guinotosia.md "wikilink")* <small>Beschin,
    Busulini, De Angeli & Tessier, 2007</small>
  - *[Etyus](../Page/Etyus.md "wikilink")* <small>Mantell, 1822</small>
  - *[Xanthosia](../Page/Xanthosia_\(crab\).md "wikilink")* <small>Bell,
    1863</small>

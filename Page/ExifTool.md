**ExifTool**是Phil
Harvey以[Perl寫成的免費](../Page/Perl.md "wikilink")[开源软件](../Page/开源软件.md "wikilink")，可讀寫及處理[圖像](../Page/圖像.md "wikilink")、[視頻及](../Page/視頻.md "wikilink")[音頻的](../Page/音頻.md "wikilink")[metadata](../Page/metadata.md "wikilink")，例如[Exif](../Page/Exif.md "wikilink")、[IPTC](../Page/IPTC.md "wikilink")、[XMP](../Page/XMP.md "wikilink")、[JFIF](../Page/JFIF.md "wikilink")、[GeoTIFF](../Page/GeoTIFF.md "wikilink")、[ICC
Profile](../Page/ICC_Profile.md "wikilink")。它是[跨平台的](../Page/跨平台.md "wikilink")，可作為[命令列或Perl](../Page/命令列.md "wikilink")[函式庫使用](../Page/函式庫.md "wikilink")。

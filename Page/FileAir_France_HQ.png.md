Due to French law, images of newly constructed buildings inherit the
copyrights of the architectural firms. France does not have the freedom
of panorama, so it is not possible to take a free photograph of this
building

The image is licensed as Creative Commons (CC-BY-SA-2.5), while the
object, and by extent the file, is copyrighted, because there is no
freedom of panorama in France. Both licenses apply to this file.

The usage in Air France is justified since an image of the company's
headquarters is a relevant and important component of the company.

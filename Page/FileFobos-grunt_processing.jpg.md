The Fobos-Grunt spacecraft sitting on top of its main propulsion unit
(MDU) during pre-launch processing in Baikonur Cosmodrome. The Chinese
Yinghuo-1 Mars-orbiter is located inside the truss in the middle
section. The Fobos-Grunt cruise stage is located on top of the truss,
and the return vehicle is located at the very top. See
[1](http://www.roscosmos.ru/img/site/fobos_04.jpg) for a cross-section
diagram.

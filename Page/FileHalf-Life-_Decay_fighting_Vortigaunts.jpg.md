## Summary

A screenshot of *[Half-Life:
Decay](../Page/Half-Life:_Decay.md "wikilink")* in play, in splitscreen
mode. Recorded by the Belkin VideoBus using USBVision 1.1.1, isolated
the individual frame using QuickTime Player 7.1.0 and Mac OS X's
screen-capture feature, and cropped and converted to
[JPEG](../Page/JPEG.md "wikilink") using GraphicConverter 5.8.2.

## Fair use in [Volcano High](../Page/Volcano_High.md "wikilink")

Though this image is subject to copyright, its use is covered by the
U.S. fair use laws because:

1.  It's a low resolution copy of a Film Poster / VHS or DVD Cover.
2.  It doesn't limit the copyright owner's rights to sell the film in
    any way, in fact, it may encourage sales.
3.  Because of the low resolution, copies could not be used to make
    illegal copies of the artwork/image.
4.  The image is itself a subject of discussion in the article or used
    in the infobox thereof.
5.  The image is significant because it was used to promoted a notable

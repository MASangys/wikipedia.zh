**ioquake3**(*ioq3*,*icculus.org/quake3*
)是[雷神之锤3引擎的修改版本](../Page/雷神之锤3.md "wikilink")（[Fork](../Page/Fork.md "wikilink")），但不支持[PunkBuster](../Page/PunkBuster.md "wikilink")。
已经用于[Urban
Terror](../Page/Urban_Terror.md "wikilink")，[OpenArena](../Page/OpenArena.md "wikilink")，[World
of
Padman](../Page/World_of_Padman.md "wikilink")，[Tremulous](../Page/Tremulous.md "wikilink")，[Star
Trek: Voyager: Elite
Force](../Page/Star_Trek:_Voyager:_Elite_Force.md "wikilink")，[OpenMoHAA](../Page/OpenMoHAA.md "wikilink")。而[Smokin'
Guns开发组正在向此引擎迈进](../Page/Smokin'_Guns.md "wikilink")。

## 改进

  - 支持[IPV6和](../Page/IPV6.md "wikilink")[VoIP](../Page/VoIP.md "wikilink")
  - [Ogg Vorbis支持](../Page/Ogg_Vorbis.md "wikilink")
  - [OpenAL音频渲染](../Page/OpenAL.md "wikilink")，支持5.1/7.1声道，改进音效
  - 通过[cURL实现](../Page/cURL.md "wikilink")[HTTP](../Page/HTTP.md "wikilink")[FTP下载数据包](../Page/FTP.md "wikilink")
  - [SDL](../Page/SDL.md "wikilink") 的后台支持
  - [x86 64支持](../Page/x86_64.md "wikilink")
  - 控制台历史，控制台自定义色彩（[POSIX类](../Page/POSIX.md "wikilink")）
  - [PNG](../Page/PNG.md "wikilink")[材质](../Page/纹理映射.md "wikilink")
  - [GUID系统](../Page/GUID.md "wikilink")

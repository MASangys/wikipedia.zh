******是[多板纲](../Page/多板纲.md "wikilink")[新石鳖亞綱](../Page/新石鳖亞綱.md "wikilink")[鳞侧石鳖目的一個](../Page/鳞侧石鳖目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科_\(生物\).md "wikilink")。

## 分類

本科大致可以分為兩個亞科，分別如下\[1\]：

  - Subfamily [Juvenichitoninae](../Page/Juvenichitoninae.md "wikilink")
    <small>Sirenko, 1975</small>
      - *[Juvenichiton](../Page/Juvenichiton.md "wikilink")*
        <small>Sirenko, 1975</small>
      - *[Micichiton](../Page/Micichiton.md "wikilink")* <small>Sirenko,
        1975</small>
      - *[Nanichiton](../Page/Nanichiton.md "wikilink")* <small>Sirenko,
        1975</small>
  - Subfamily [Lepidochitoninae](../Page/Lepidochitoninae.md "wikilink")
    <small>Iredale,
    1914</small>\[2\]\[3\]：食性皆為[啃食性及](../Page/啃食性动物.md "wikilink")[食沉积物](../Page/食沉积物动物.md "wikilink")\[4\]\[5\]。
      - *[Cyanoplax](../Page/Cyanoplax.md "wikilink")* <small>Pilsbry,
        1892</small>
      - *[Lepidochitona](../Page/Lepidochitona.md "wikilink")*
        <small>Gray, 1821</small>
      - *[Nuttallina](../Page/Nuttallina.md "wikilink")*
        <small>([Carpenter](../Page/Phillip_Pearsall_Carpenter.md "wikilink")
        MS) Dall, 1871</small>
      - *[Particulazona](../Page/Particulazona.md "wikilink")*
        <small>Kaas, 1993</small>
      - *[Spongioradsia](../Page/Spongioradsia.md "wikilink")*
        <small>[Pilsbry](../Page/Henry_Augustus_Pilsbry.md "wikilink"),
        1894</small>

此外：

  - *[Craspedochilus](../Page/Craspedochilus.md "wikilink")* <small>G.O.
    Sars, 1878</small>及*Trachydermon* <small>Carpenter,
    1864</small>被認為是*[Lepidochitona](../Page/Lepidochitona.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Gray, 1821</small>）的[同種異名](../Page/同種異名.md "wikilink")\[6\]；
  - WoRMS的分類並未有包括[汉利石鳖屬](../Page/汉利石鳖屬.md "wikilink")\[7\]在內。
  - 舊有的分類還包括以下各屬：
      - *[Boreochiton](../Page/Boreochiton.md "wikilink")* <small>Sars,
        1878</small>：今屬[Tonicellidae](../Page/Tonicellidae.md "wikilink")；
      - *[Tonicella](../Page/Tonicella.md "wikilink")*
        <small>[Carpenter](../Page/Phillip_Pearsall_Carpenter.md "wikilink"),
        1873</small>：今屬[Tonicellidae](../Page/Tonicellidae.md "wikilink")；
      - *[Oligochiton](../Page/Oligochiton.md "wikilink")* <small>Berry,
        1922</small>

***N*-乙酰胞壁酸**（，简称为**MurNAc**或**NAM**）是[N-乙酰葡糖胺与](../Page/N-乙酰葡糖胺.md "wikilink")[乳酸形成的](../Page/乳酸.md "wikilink")[醚类](../Page/醚.md "wikilink")，化学式[C](../Page/碳.md "wikilink")<sub>11</sub>[H](../Page/氢.md "wikilink")<sub>19</sub>[N](../Page/氮.md "wikilink")[O](../Page/氧.md "wikilink")<sub>8</sub>。它与[N-乙酰葡糖胺共同为细菌细胞壁的组成单糖](../Page/N-乙酰葡糖胺.md "wikilink")。

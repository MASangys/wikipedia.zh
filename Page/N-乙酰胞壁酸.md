***N*-乙酰胞壁酸**（，简称为**MurNAc**或**NAM**）是[N-乙酰葡糖胺](../Page/N-乙酰葡糖胺.md "wikilink")与[乳酸](../Page/乳酸.md "wikilink")形成的[醚](../Page/醚.md "wikilink")类，化学式[C](../Page/碳.md "wikilink")<sub>11</sub>[H](../Page/氢.md "wikilink")<sub>19</sub>[N](../Page/氮.md "wikilink")[O](../Page/氧.md "wikilink")<sub>8</sub>。它与[N-乙酰葡糖胺](../Page/N-乙酰葡糖胺.md "wikilink")共同为细菌细胞壁的组成单糖。

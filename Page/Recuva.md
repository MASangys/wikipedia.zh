**Recuva**是一個由[Piriform開發的](../Page/Piriform.md "wikilink")[免費](../Page/免費軟件.md "wikilink")[數據恢復軟件](../Page/數據恢復.md "wikilink")，能夠在Microsoft
Windows執行。該軟體能夠恢復被「永久」刪除以及被作業系統標示為可用空間的檔案。此外，亦能夠恢復在[快閃裝置](../Page/快閃裝置.md "wikilink")、[記憶卡或](../Page/記憶卡.md "wikilink")[MP3播放器中被刪除的檔案](../Page/MP3播放器.md "wikilink")\[1\]\[2\]。支援的[檔案系統有](../Page/檔案系統.md "wikilink")[FAT12](../Page/FAT12.md "wikilink")、[FAT16](../Page/FAT16.md "wikilink")、[FAT32](../Page/FAT32.md "wikilink")、[exFAT](../Page/exFAT.md "wikilink")、[NTFS](../Page/NTFS.md "wikilink")、[NTFS5](../Page/NTFS5.md "wikilink")、[NTFS](../Page/NTFS.md "wikilink")、[EFS](../Page/EFS.md "wikilink")、[ext2](../Page/ext2.md "wikilink")、[ext3](../Page/ext3.md "wikilink")、[ext4](../Page/ext4.md "wikilink")\[3\]。

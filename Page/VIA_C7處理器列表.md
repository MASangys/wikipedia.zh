**[C7](../Page/VIA_C7.md "wikilink")**系列處理器為[VIA推出的第七代處理器](../Page/VIA.md "wikilink")，主要面向客製化處理器與嵌入式處理器市場。

## 桌上型處理器s

### C7系列

#### ["Esther"](../Page/VIA_C7#Esther.md "wikilink") (90 nm)

  - 全系列支援: *[MMX](../Page/MMX_\(instruction_set\).md "wikilink"),
    [SSE](../Page/Streaming_SIMD_Extensions.md "wikilink"),
    [SSE2](../Page/SSE2.md "wikilink"),
    [SSE3](../Page/SSE3.md "wikilink"), [NX
    bit](../Page/NX_bit.md "wikilink"), VIA PadLock (SHA, AES,
    Montgomery Multiplier, RNG)*
  - [VIA PowerSaver](../Page/PowerSaver.md "wikilink") 支援 2

    P-states

[平假名](../Page/平假名.md "wikilink")<span lang="ja">**あ**</span>和[片假名](../Page/片假名.md "wikilink")<span lang="ja">**ア**</span>是[日语的](../Page/日语.md "wikilink")[假名之一](../Page/日語假名.md "wikilink")，由一个[音节组成](../Page/音节.md "wikilink")。在日语[五十音图中排第](../Page/五十音图.md "wikilink")1行第1个（<span lang="ja">[あ行](../Page/あ行.md "wikilink")[あ段](../Page/あ段.md "wikilink")</span>）的位置。

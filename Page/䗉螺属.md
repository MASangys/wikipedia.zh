**䗉螺屬**（学名：**）是[原始腹足目](../Page/原始腹足目.md "wikilink")[钟螺科的一個屬](../Page/钟螺科.md "wikilink")，是[䗉螺亞科的成員之一](../Page/䗉螺亞科.md "wikilink")\[1\]，其棲息地在海裡。

## 物種

以下為部份屬於本屬的物種\[2\]：

  - *[Umbonium callosum](../Page/Umbonium_callosum.md "wikilink")*
    <small> Sowerby, 1887 </small>
  - *[Umbonium conicum](../Page/Umbonium_conicum.md "wikilink")*
    <small>([A. Adams](../Page/Arthur_Adams_\(zoologist\).md "wikilink")
    & [Reeve](../Page/Lovell_Augustus_Reeve.md "wikilink"),
    1850)</small>
  - [肋䗉螺](../Page/肋䗉螺.md "wikilink") *Umbonium costatum*
    <small>([Valenciennes](../Page/Achille_Valenciennes.md "wikilink")
    in Kiener, 1838 in 1834-50)</small>
  - *[Umbonium elegans](../Page/Umbonium_elegans.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Kiener, 1838)</small>
  - *[Umbonium eloiseae](../Page/Umbonium_eloiseae.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Dance, Moolenbeek & Dekker, 1992</small>
  - [巨䗉](../Page/巨䗉.md "wikilink") *Umbonium giganteum*
    <small>([Lesson](../Page/Rene_Primevere_Lesson.md "wikilink"),
    1833)</small>
  - [鍊珠䗉螺](../Page/鍊珠䗉螺.md "wikilink") *Umbonium moniliferum*
    <small>([Lamarck](../Page/Lamarck.md "wikilink"),
    1822)</small>：又名[疣䗉](../Page/疣䗉.md "wikilink")\[3\]。
  - *[Umbonium sagittatum](../Page/Umbonium_sagittatum.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Hinds, 1845)</small>
  - [台灣䗉螺](../Page/台灣䗉螺.md "wikilink") *Umbonium suturale*
    <small>([Lamarck](../Page/Lamarck.md "wikilink"), 1822)</small>
  - [托氏䗉螺](../Page/托氏䗉螺.md "wikilink") *Umbonium thomasi*
    <small>(Crosse, 1862)</small>：又名[湯瑪氏䗉螺](../Page/湯瑪氏䗉螺.md "wikilink")
  - [彩虹䗉螺](../Page/彩虹䗉螺.md "wikilink") *Umbonium vestiarium*
    <small>([Linnaeus](../Page/Linnaeus.md "wikilink"), 1758)</small>

## 免簽證

### 自由流動

[印度](../Page/印度.md "wikilink")公民無須辦理簽證進入不丹，這是基於不丹與印度之間允許兩國人民在互惠的基礎上自由流動的[1949年協議](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/不丹－印度關係#1949年協議 "wikilink")。

Indian citizens can obtain entry into Bhutan by air and land, and may use any of the acceptable documents mentioned below to enter Bhutan:

  - [印度護照](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/印度護照 "wikilink")
  - 附有相片的投票咭\[1\]
  - Identification Slip issued by the Indian Consulate Office in [Phuentsholing](../Page/彭措林.md "wikilink") upon presenting proof of Indian citizenship.\[2\]

## 提前獲得簽證

All foreigners (except for citizens of [Bangladesh](../Page/孟加拉国.md "wikilink"), [India](../Page/印度.md "wikilink"), and [Maldives](../Page/马尔代夫.md "wikilink")) must obtain a visa before visiting Bhutan. If approved, they are given a visa clearance letter, and must present it at the port of entry. The visa is then stamped into their passport. Foreign tourists must use a licensed Bhutanese tour operator or one of their international partners to pre-arrange their visa and book their holiday. A daily fee is also charged for every day of stay. 其他国家的国民可以在首都[廷布](../Page/廷布.md "wikilink")得到落地签证，但是必须在入境前续订好酒店及行程，并且保证每天至少消费250美元(旅遊旺季)或200美元(旅遊淡季)。\[6\]\[7\]

Discounts also apply for minors and larger groups while surcharges exist for groups smaller than 3. The minimum daily package required for visa processing covers accommodation, food, guide and vehicle with driver. Part of it goes towards [free education](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Education_in_Bhutan "wikilink"), [free healthcare](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Health_in_Bhutan "wikilink") and poverty alleviation in Bhutan.\[8\]\[9\] Licensed tour guides accompany tourists during their trips and arrange accommodations - independent tourism by foreigners (except for citizens of [Bangladesh](../Page/孟加拉国.md "wikilink"), [India](../Page/印度.md "wikilink"), and [Maldives](../Page/马尔代夫.md "wikilink")) is prohibited.

The only exceptions for having to book a tour as a condition for being allowed to visit are for those who receive a formal invitation to Bhutan from "a citizen of some standing" or a volunteer organization,\[10\] and those who come as guests of the [Bhutanese government](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bhutanese_government "wikilink").

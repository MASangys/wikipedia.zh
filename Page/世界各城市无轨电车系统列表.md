**Notes** for the two tunnel trolleybus lines:

  - The 6.1 km [Kanden Tunnel
    Trolleybus](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kanden_Tunnel_Trolleybus "wikilink")
    line operates almost entirely in tunnel, through a mountain, and
    connects Ōgizawa Station with Kurobe Dam, for tourists and hikers.
    Ōgizawa Station is in [Ōmachi
    city](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ōmachi,_Nagano "wikilink"),
    [Nagano
    Prefecture](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nagano_Prefecture "wikilink").
  - The affiliated 3.6 km [Tateyama Tunnel
    Trolleybus](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tateyama_Tunnel_Trolleybus "wikilink")
    line, similarly, operates entirely in tunnel and connects Daikanbo
    with Murodō. The line is located in [Tateyama
    town](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tateyama,_Toyama "wikilink"),
    [Toyama
    Prefecture](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Toyama_Prefecture "wikilink").

Both lines are part of the [Tateyama Kurobe Alpine
Route](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tateyama_Kurobe_Alpine_Route "wikilink").
This passes through [Chūbu-Sangaku National
Park](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chūbu-Sangaku_National_Park "wikilink")
(also known in English as "Japan Alps National Park").

**世界轮椅运动会**，全称是**世界轮椅及截肢者运动会**，也叫做**斯托克·曼德维尔**（Stoke Mandeville）**轮椅运动会**，是一个多项目的残疾人运动会。

第一届运动会是由[路德維希·古特曼](../Page/路德維希·古特曼.md "wikilink")在1948年举办。

## 比賽

  - 1948: Stoke Mandeville Games for the Paralyzed \[1\] July 28, 1948, archery competition, 16 competitors\[2\] (14 men, 2 women\[3\])
  - 1949–1951: Stoke Mandeville Games
  - 1952:Stoke Mandeville, England – First International Stoke Mandeville Games\[4\] A Dutch team participated, making it an international event\[5\]
  - 1953–1959: International Stoke Mandeville Games (2nd–8th)
  - 1960: [9th Annual International Stoke Mandeville Games](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/1960年夏季残奥会 "wikilink"), Rome, Italy – later known as the 1st [Paralympic Games](../Page/残奥会.md "wikilink")\[6\]
  - 1961–1963: International Stoke Mandeville Games (10th–12th)
  - 1964: [13th International Stoke Mandeville Games for the Paralysed](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/1964年夏季残奥会 "wikilink"), Tokyo, Japan – later known as the 2nd Paralympic Games\[7\]
  - 1965–1967: International Stoke Mandeville Games (14th–16th)
  - 1968: [17th International Stoke Mandeville Games](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/1968年夏季帕拉林匹克運動會 "wikilink"), [Tel Aviv](../Page/特拉维夫.md "wikilink"), [Israel](../Page/以色列.md "wikilink") \[8\] – later known as the 3rd Paralympic Games
  - 1969–1972: International Stoke Mandeville Games (18th–20th)
  - 1972: [21st International Stoke Mandeville Games](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/1972年夏季帕拉林匹克運動會 "wikilink"),Heidelberg, Germany – later known as the 4th Paralympic Games\[9\]\[10\]

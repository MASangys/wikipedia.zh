**东部省**（[英文](../Page/英文.md "wikilink")：**Eastern
Province**）是[肯尼亚](../Page/肯尼亚.md "wikilink")[八省之一](../Page/肯尼亚行政区划.md "wikilink")，首府[恩布](../Page/恩布.md "wikilink")（英文：Embu）.

## 行政区划

东部省分为26个县：

1.  Chalbi District
2.  [恩布县](../Page/恩布县.md "wikilink") Embu District
3.  Garbatula District
4.  Igembe District
5.  Imenti North District
6.  Imenti South District
7.  [伊西奧洛縣](../Page/伊西奧洛縣.md "wikilink") Isiolo District
8.  Kangundo District
9.  Kibwezi District
10. Kitui District
11. Kyuso District
12. Laisamis District
13. Machakos District
14. Makueni District
15. [馬薩比特縣](../Page/馬薩比特縣.md "wikilink") Marsabit District
16. Mbeere District
17. Mbooni District
18. Meru Central District
19. Meru South District (Nithi District)
20. Moyale District
21. Mutomo District (Kitui South District)
22. Mwala District
23. Mwingi District
24. Tharaka District
25. Tigania District
26. Yatta District

**中部省**（[英文](../Page/英文.md "wikilink")：Central
Province）是[肯尼亚](../Page/肯尼亚.md "wikilink")8[省之一](../Page/省.md "wikilink")，首府[尼耶利](../Page/尼耶利.md "wikilink")（英文：Nyeri）.

## 行政区划

中部省分为11个县：

1.  Gatundu District
2.  [东基安布县Kiambu](../Page/东基安布县.md "wikilink") East District
3.  [西基安布县Kiambu](../Page/西基安布县.md "wikilink") West District
4.  [基里尼亚加县](../Page/基里尼亚加县.md "wikilink") Kirinyaga District
5.  [北穆兰卡县Muranga](../Page/北穆兰卡县.md "wikilink") North District
6.  [南穆兰卡县Muranga](../Page/南穆兰卡县.md "wikilink") South District
7.  [北年达鲁阿县](../Page/北年达鲁阿县.md "wikilink") Nyandarua North District
8.  [南年达鲁阿县Nyandarua](../Page/南年达鲁阿县.md "wikilink") South District
9.  [北尼耶利县Nyeri](../Page/北尼耶利县.md "wikilink") North District
10. [南尼耶利县Nyeri](../Page/南尼耶利县.md "wikilink") South District
11. [锡卡县Thika](../Page/锡卡县.md "wikilink") District

**九里香属**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：**）又名**月橘屬**，是[无患子目](../Page/无患子目.md "wikilink")[芸香科的一](../Page/芸香科.md "wikilink")[屬](../Page/屬.md "wikilink")，該屬植物通稱為**月橘**、**九里香**。通常為常綠性小灌木或小喬木。生育地為海拔1200公尺以下地區之山區闊葉林，株高2-7m。

## 分类

本属归类于[柑橘亚科](../Page/柑橘亚科.md "wikilink")[柑橘族](../Page/柑橘族.md "wikilink")[美栌木亚族](../Page/美栌木亚族.md "wikilink")，已获确认的物种如下：

  - [翼叶九里香](../Page/翼叶九里香.md "wikilink") *Murraya alata*
    [Drake](../Page/Drake.md "wikilink")
  - [蘭嶼月橘](../Page/蘭嶼月橘.md "wikilink") *Murraya crenulata*
    ([Turcz.](../Page/Turcz..md "wikilink"))
    [Oliv.](../Page/Oliv..md "wikilink")
  - [豆叶九里香](../Page/豆叶九里香.md "wikilink") *Murraya euchrestifolia*
    [Hayata](../Page/Hayata.md "wikilink")
  - [调料九里香](../Page/调料九里香.md "wikilink") *Murraya koenigii* (L.)
    [Spreng.](../Page/Spreng..md "wikilink")：即咖喱叶、咖喱树
  - [广西九里香](../Page/广西九里香.md "wikilink") *Murraya kwangsiensis*
    ([C.C.Huang](../Page/C.C.Huang.md "wikilink")) C.C.Huang
  - [小叶九里香](../Page/小叶九里香.md "wikilink") *Murraya microphylla*
    ([Merr.](../Page/Merr..md "wikilink") &
    [Chun](../Page/Chun.md "wikilink"))
    [Swingle](../Page/Swingle.md "wikilink")
  - [月橘](../Page/月橘.md "wikilink") *Murraya paniculata* (L.) Jack
  - [四数九里香](../Page/四数九里香.md "wikilink") *Murraya tetramera* C.C. Huang

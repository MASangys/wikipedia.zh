[Jáchym_Topol_s_knihou_2010-09-22_b.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Jáchym_Topol_s_knihou_2010-09-22_b.jpg "fig:Jáchym_Topol_s_knihou_2010-09-22_b.jpg")**亚希姆·托波尔**（Jáchym
Topol，出生于1962年8月4日），捷克作家。1970年代末和80年代初，他开始为由他的弟弟菲利普领导的摇滚乐团Psí vojáci写歌词。
1982年，他与人共同创办了杂志Violit。他还是七七宪章的签署者之一。

## 作品

### 诗歌

  - *Miluju tě k zbláznění* (I love you madly; samizdat, 1988)
    / Topol's first collection of poetry, published in samizdat,
    received the Tom Stoppard Prize for Unofficial Literature (founded
    in 1983 by [Tom
    Stoppard](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tom_Stoppard "wikilink") and
    awarded by the [Charta
    77](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charta_77 "wikilink") Foundation
    in Stockholm); first non-samizdat edition published by Atlantis in
    1990.

<!-- end list -->

  - *V úterý bude válka* (The war will be on Tuesday; Edice 13x18,
    1992)
    / Five poems from this collection were published in [Alex
    Zucker](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alex_Zucker "wikilink")'s
    translation in the Spring 1994 issue of *Trafika: an international
    literary review*.\[1\]

### 长篇小说

  - *Sestra* (Sister; Atlantis, 1994)
    / Received the 1995 Cena Egona Hostovského\[2\] (Egon Hostovský
    Prize), awarded for a "novel which artistically exceeds the standard
    production"
    / English translation [Alex
    Zucker](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alex_Zucker "wikilink"):
    *[City Sister
    Silver](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/City_Sister_Silver "wikilink")*
    (Catbird Press, 2000)\[3\]
    / Also translated into German, Hungarian, and Polish (*Siostra*,
    trans. [Leszek
    Engelking](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leszek_Engelking "wikilink"),
    Wydawnictwo W.A.B., 2002)
  - *Anděl* (Angel; Hynek, 1995)\[4\]
    / German translation Peter Sacher: *Engel Exit* (Volk und Welt,
    1997)
    / French translation Marianna Canavaggio: *Ange exit* (J'ai lu,
    2002)
    / Turkish translation Martin Alaçam: *Andel* (Norgunk, 2005)
    / Also translated into Hungarian
  - *Noční práce* (Nightwork; Hynek, 2001)\[5\]
    / French translation Marianna Canavaggio: *Missions nocturnes*
    (Laffont, 2002)
    / Polish translation [Leszek
    Engelking](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leszek_Engelking "wikilink"):
    *Nocna praca* (Wydawnictwo W.A.B., 2004)
    / Also translated into Dutch, German, Italian, Spanish and Swedish
  - *Kloktat dehet* (Gargling tar; Torst, 2005)
    / English translation by David Short: *Gargling with Tar*,
    forthcoming from Portobello Books, London, in spring 2010\[6\]
    / French translation Marianna Canavaggio: *Zone cirque* (Editions
    Noir Sur Blanc, 2009)
    / Polish translation [Leszek
    Engelking](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leszek_Engelking "wikilink"):
    *Strefa cyrkowa* (Wydawnictwo W.A.B., 2008)
    / Also translated into Dutch, German and Italian.

**企鵝蘭登書屋**（），於2013年成立的[出版社](../Page/出版社.md "wikilink")，由[德國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/德國 "wikilink")[貝塔斯曼的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/貝塔斯曼 "wikilink")[藍燈書屋及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/藍燈書屋 "wikilink")[英國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/英國 "wikilink")[培生集團](../Page/培生集團.md "wikilink")的[企鵝出版集團合組而成](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/企鵝出版集團 "wikilink")，貝塔斯曼將持有新公司53%股權，培生則持有餘下47%股權\[1\]，合組後的新公司將佔有全球出版業務近1/4的[市場佔有率](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/市場佔有率 "wikilink")\[2\]。

2017年7月11日英國[培生集團](../Page/培生集團.md "wikilink")將以10億美元的作價向合資夥伴德國媒體公司[貝塔斯曼出售](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/貝塔斯曼 "wikilink")[企鵝藍燈書屋](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/企鵝藍燈書屋 "wikilink")22%的股份，交易完成後，培生仍將持有企鵝蘭登書屋的25%股權

## 分支机构

### DK

DK (Dorling Kindersley) 1974年建立于伦敦，是成人与儿童非虚构的读物出版业的标杆。

  - [Alpha](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alpha_Books "wikilink"), publishes [Complete Idiot's Guides](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Complete_Idiot's_Guides "wikilink")
  - [Prima Games](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prima_Games "wikilink"), publishes video game strategy guides
  - [Rough Guides](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rough_Guides "wikilink"), publishes travel guides

2015年，DK 拥有[愤怒的小鸟](../Page/愤怒的小鸟_\(游戏\).md "wikilink")、[漫威和](../Page/漫威漫畫.md "wikilink")[星球大战的出版权](../Page/星際大戰.md "wikilink")\[3\]。

### 皇冠出版集团

皇冠出版最早是 Outlet 图书公司成立于1933年的的打折促销出版社，现在是一个虚构和叙事性非虚构作品的出版社\[4\]。

  - [Amphoto Books](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amphoto_Books "wikilink"), publishes photography books
  - [Broadway Books](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Broadway_Books "wikilink"), founded in 1996 as part of Bantam Doubleday Dell and is now the paperback imprint of Crown
  - Clarkson Potter, produces cookbooks, illustrated gift books, and journals
  - Crown Archetype, hardcover publisher of pop-culture titles
  - Crown Business, publishes business-related content
  - Crown Forum, publishes political discourse
  - [Harmony Books](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harmony_Books "wikilink"), publishes self-help titles
  - [Hogarth Press](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hogarth_Press "wikilink"), partnership between Crown in the U.S. and Windus in the U.K.
  - Convergent, Image Catholic Books (Doubleday Religion), Waterbrook & Multnomah publish Christian non-fiction and fiction titles
  - Pam Krauss Books, founded in 1915 and publishes culinary, food, and lifestyle related titles
  - [Rodale Books](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rodale,_Inc. "wikilink")
  - Ten Speed Press, joined Crown in 2009 as a West Coast publisher of nonfiction and gift titles
  - Tim Duggan Books, founded in 2014
  - [Watson-Guptill](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Watson-Guptill "wikilink"), publishes illustrated art books as part of Ten Speed Press \[5\]

### 克诺夫-道布尔戴出版集团

  - [Sentinel](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sentinel_\(publisher\) "wikilink"), founded in 2003 as a conservative imprint

  - [Firebird](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Firebird_Books "wikilink"), publishes young adult science fiction and fantasy
  - [Frederick Warne](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frederick_Warne_&_Co "wikilink"), publisher founded in 1865 that develops brands based on classic children's literature such as [Peter Rabbit](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Rabbit "wikilink")
  - G.P. Putnam's Sons Books for Young Readers, publisher of picture books
  - [Grosset & Dunlap](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grosset_&_Dunlap "wikilink"), publisher of paperback series, leveled readers, nonfiction, brands, and licenses for ages 0-12
  - Kathy Dawson Books, launched in 2014 by publisher Kathy Dawson to publish hardcover middle-grade and YA fiction
  - [Ladybird Books](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ladybird_Books "wikilink"), publishes books for toddlers
  - Nancy Paulsen Books, launched in 2011 by publisher Nancy Paulsen to publish picture books
  - [Philomel Books](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philomel_Books "wikilink")
  - [Price Stern Sloan (PSS\!)](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Price_Stern_Sloan "wikilink"), founded to publish the [Mad Libs](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mad_Libs "wikilink") books in the 1960s, expanded to publish additional children's, novelty, and humor titles
  - [Puffin Books](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puffin_Books "wikilink"), publishes books for young readers in various formats
  - Razorbill, publishes middle grade and young adult books
  - Speak, launched in 2002 to publish classic and new young adult fiction
  - [Viking](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Viking_Press "wikilink"), publishes books for young readers\[10\]

**伊卜**（，）是[也门的一个城市](../Page/也门.md "wikilink")，是[伊卜省的首府](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/伊卜省 "wikilink")。坐落于山脉的边缘，因被富饶的土地所包围所以被称为“绿城”。它位于[红海港口](../Page/红海.md "wikilink")[摩卡东北方](../Page/摩卡.md "wikilink")117公里处。该地一直被半自治的[埃米尔统治直到](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/埃米尔 "wikilink")1944年被废除。2005年人口为160,000。

伊卜是几个省的交界地：北边是[扎玛尔省](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/扎玛尔省 "wikilink")，南边是[塔伊茲和](../Page/塔伊茲.md "wikilink")[达利省](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/达利省 "wikilink")，东边是[贝达省](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/贝达省 "wikilink")，西边是[荷台達](../Page/荷台達.md "wikilink")。

该市主要的足球队是[Al Sha'ab
Ibb](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al_Sha'ab_Ibb "wikilink")。

## 伊卜歷史古今

[Ibb,_Yemen_(4325126410).jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Ibb,_Yemen_\(4325126410\).jpg "fig:Ibb,_Yemen_(4325126410).jpg")

Ibb has been occupied since ancient times, and due to its strategical
importance, the Ottomans used it as an administrative centre.\[1\] It
thrived as a market town and the Ottomans established the Wednesday
market in Ibb.\[2\] Ibb was governed by a semi-autonomous emir until
1944, when the emirate was abolished. At times in its history many
people have migrated to Ibb from areas of Yemen which had been
experiencing drought, to find work in farming.\[3\] In the mid 1950s
there was reportedly an outbreak of
[smallpox](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/smallpox "wikilink") in the
city.\[4\]

German explorer, [Hermann
Burchardt](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hermann_Burchardt "wikilink"),
photographed the city in 1901, photos now held at the [Ethnological
Museum of
Berlin](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ethnological_Museum_of_Berlin "wikilink").\[5\]

## 伊卜氣候

Ibb is located at an altitude of  on a spur of [Mount
Shamāḥī](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mount_Shamāḥī "wikilink").\[6\]
Due to its high altitude, Ibb has a [subtropical highland
climate](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/subtropical_highland_climate "wikilink")
([Köppen climate
classification](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Köppen_climate_classification "wikilink"):
*Cwa*), and is one of the wettest areas of Yemen,\[7\] typically
receiving 800–1200 mm of rain per annum.

## 伊卜Notable landmarks

[Ibb_flickr02.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Ibb_flickr02.jpg "fig:Ibb_flickr02.jpg")
The city is noted for its towering stone houses, with geometrical
friezes and circular stained glass windows known as
*[qamiriya](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/qamiriya "wikilink")*.\[8\]
The main mosque was built during the Ottoman period, and there are other
mosques and also a fortress in the vicinity, closed to visitors. An
ancient overhead
[aqueduct](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/aqueduct_\(water_supply\) "wikilink")
still remains.\[9\] [Ibb
University](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ibb_University "wikilink") was
established in 1996. Ibb also contains an art centre and museum and
hotels such as Saba Tourist Hotel and Al-Riyad Hotel.\[10\] The main
[football](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Association_football "wikilink")
team is [Al Sha'ab
Ibb](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al_Sha'ab_Ibb "wikilink").

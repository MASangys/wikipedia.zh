**伊朗里亞爾** （波斯語：ریال
；[貨幣編號](../Page/ISO_4217.md "wikilink")**IRR**；標誌：**﷼**）是[伊朗目前的流通](../Page/伊朗.md "wikilink")[貨幣](../Page/貨幣.md "wikilink")。[輔幣單位第納爾](../Page/輔幣.md "wikilink")。1里亞爾=100第納爾。
然而在一般日常生活中，伊朗人習慣以託曼(تومان/toman)來標示價錢，1託曼=10里亞爾。

由於伊朗因為近期[核問題](../Page/伊朗核問題.md "wikilink")，及[油價下跌影響](../Page/油價.md "wikilink")（伊朗82%出口為[石油](../Page/石油.md "wikilink")），因此貶值快速，2016年7月15日時，1[美元可兌換](../Page/美元.md "wikilink")30684伊朗里亞爾，為世界目前流通者價值最低，已有10000舊貨幣換1新貨幣的想法，但目前一直維持當前貨幣流通。

The Iranian rial was [devalued](../Page/貨幣貶值.md "wikilink") in July 2013
to half its previous value as the government reduced subsidisation of
the exchange rate against the dollar.\[1\]\[2\] In December 2016, the
[Iranian government](../Page/Government_of_Iran.md "wikilink") announced
the country's currency will be changed from the rial to the commonly
used [toman](../Page/Iranian_toman.md "wikilink"). It needs the [Iranian
Parliament](../Page/伊斯蘭議會.md "wikilink")'s approval.\[3\]

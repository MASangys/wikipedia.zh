**休尼克省**位於[亞美尼亞](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/亞美尼亞 "wikilink")，與[瓦約茨佐爾省](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/瓦約茨佐爾省 "wikilink")、[伊朗](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/伊朗 "wikilink")、[納戈爾諾-卡拉巴赫](../Page/納戈爾諾-卡拉巴赫.md "wikilink")相鄰。

## Communities and settlements

As of February 2017, Syunik is home to the following 72 administrative communities (*hamaynkner*), of which 6 are urban, and 66 are rural..\[1\]\[2\]\[3\]

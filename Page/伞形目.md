**伞形目**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）又名**繖形花目**，是[被子植物](../Page/被子植物.md "wikilink")[真双子叶植物下的一个目](../Page/真双子叶植物.md "wikilink")。下面包含[五加科](../Page/五加科.md "wikilink")、[繖形花科兩個比較大的科別](../Page/繖形花科.md "wikilink")，這兩個科別的植物有很多著名的药材，例如五加科中有[人參屬與](../Page/人參屬.md "wikilink")[五加屬](../Page/五加屬.md "wikilink")，繖形花科有名的[當歸](../Page/當歸.md "wikilink")、[柴胡與](../Page/柴胡.md "wikilink")[白芷以及可以食用的](../Page/白芷.md "wikilink")[胡蘿蔔與](../Page/胡蘿蔔.md "wikilink")[芹菜等](../Page/芹菜.md "wikilink")。

## 分类

### 内部分类

在[APG系统](../Page/APG系统.md "wikilink")（截至[APG
IV](../Page/APG_IV.md "wikilink")）中，伞形目包含以下7个科：

  - [伞形科](../Page/伞形科.md "wikilink") Apiaceae
    [Lindl.](../Page/Lindl..md "wikilink") (1836), nom. cons.
  - [五加科](../Page/五加科.md "wikilink") Araliaceae
    [Juss.](../Page/Juss..md "wikilink") (1789), nom. cons.
  - [南茱萸科](../Page/南茱萸科.md "wikilink") Griseliniaceae
    [J.R.Forst.](../Page/J.R.Forst..md "wikilink") &
    [G.Forst.](../Page/G.Forst..md "wikilink") ex
    [A.Cunn.](../Page/A.Cunn..md "wikilink") (1839)
  - [裂果枫科](../Page/裂果枫科.md "wikilink") Myodocarpaceae
    [Doweld](../Page/Doweld.md "wikilink") (2001)
  - [毛柴木科](../Page/毛柴木科.md "wikilink") Pennantiaceae
    [J.Agardh](../Page/J.Agardh.md "wikilink") (1858)
  - [海桐科](../Page/海桐科.md "wikilink") Pittosporaceae
    [R.Br.](../Page/R.Br..md "wikilink") (1814), nom. cons.
  - [鞘柄木科](../Page/鞘柄木科.md "wikilink") Torricelliaceae
    [Hu](../Page/Hu.md "wikilink") (1934)

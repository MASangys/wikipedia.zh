[BhutanLhuntse.png](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:BhutanLhuntse.png "fig:BhutanLhuntse.png") **倫奇宗**（[宗卡语](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/宗卡语 "wikilink")：；），是[不丹](../Page/不丹.md "wikilink")二十個宗(dzongkhag)之一，面積達3,462平方公里，人口約28,292人，首府[倫奇](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/倫奇 "wikilink")。与[西藏自治区](../Page/西藏自治区.md "wikilink")[隆子县](../Page/隆子县.md "wikilink")同名。

**倫奇宗**轄下有八個[格窩](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/格窩 "wikilink")(Gewog):

  - [Gangzur Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050817084136/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/lt_Gangzur.pdf)
  - [Jaray Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050816013305/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/lt_jaray.pdf)
  - [Khoma Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050824071209/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/lt_Khoma.pdf)
  - [Kurtoe Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050816164332/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/lt_Kurtoe.pdf)
  - [Menbi Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050816210614/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/lt_Menbi.pdf)
  - [Metsho Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20041022222850/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/lt_Metsho.pdf)
  - [Minjay Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20041022222223/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/lt_Minjay.pdf)
  - [Tsenkhar Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050530151816/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/lt_Tsenkhar.pdf)

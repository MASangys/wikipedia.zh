**伦敦公共交通管理局**（英文：London Transport Executive）是1970年—1984年伦敦的公共交通管理部门，是大伦敦委员会的下属职能部门。\[1\]

## 成立背景与部门职能

大伦敦委员会于1965年成立，但没有公共交通管理权。原因在于大伦敦委员会的前身伦敦郡委员会（London County Council）于1933年将相关职能交由伦敦客运局（Passenger Transport Board）负责。《1969年伦敦交通法案》赋予了**伦敦公共交通管理局**管理[伦敦公交和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/伦敦公交 "wikilink")[伦敦地铁](../Page/伦敦地铁.md "wikilink")的职能，但不包括运行在大伦敦的[英国国铁服务](../Page/英国铁路.md "wikilink")（[大伦敦的部分城市轨道交通服务由](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/大伦敦 "wikilink")[英国国铁提供](../Page/英国铁路.md "wikilink")）。

## 票价政策

The GLC aimed to increase usage of public transport, especially in [Outer London](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outer_London "wikilink"), where car use was high. Fare setting policy was used to increase patronage on the London Underground and London Buses, particularly during less busy times.\[2\] Shortages of staff meant it was also necessary to speed up the rate of boarding on buses that ran without conductors, by introducing pre-purchased and inter-modal tickets. On 4 October 1981, following the [Greater London Council election](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/1981_Greater_London_Council_election "wikilink"), the incoming Labour administration simplified fares in Greater London by introducing four new bus fare zones and two central London Underground zones, named *City* and *West End*, where flat fares applied for the first time.\[3\] This was accompanied by a cut in prices of about a third and was marketed as the *[Fares Fair](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fares_Fair "wikilink")* campaign.\[4\] Following successful legal action against it, on 21 March 1982\[5\] London Buses fares were doubled and London Underground fares increased by 91%.\[6\] The two central area zones were retained and the fares to all other stations were restructured to be graduated at three mile intervals; and thus grouping those stations within three miles of the central zones in an 'inner zone'.\[7\] In 1983, after further legal action, a third revision of fares was undertaken, and a new inter-modal [Travelcard](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Travelcard "wikilink") season ticket was launched covering five new numbered zones; representing an overall cut in prices of around 25%.\[8\] The off-peak *One Day Travelcard* was launched in 1984 and on weekdays was sold for travel after 09.30.\[9\]

## 机构废除及对废除前政策的延续

伦敦公共交通管理局于1986年废除，但运输权在两年前被移除并转移到伦敦地区公共交通管理局（London Regional Transport）。随着[大伦敦政府和其下属职能部门](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/大伦敦政府 "wikilink")[伦敦交通局成立](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/伦敦交通局 "wikilink")，公共交通管理职能于2000年返回伦敦当地政府。在伦敦公共交通管理局存续期间设计的分区票价系统继续在整个大伦敦使用，并且变化不大。

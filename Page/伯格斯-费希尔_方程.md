**伯格斯-費希爾 方程** （Burgers
Fisher）非线性偏微分[方程有如下形式](../Page/方程.md "wikilink")：\[1\]

\[\frac{\partial u}{\partial t}+u^2*\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial u^2}=u*(1-u^2)\]

此偏微分方程的解为：

\(u(x,t)=\frac{1}{2}*\sqrt{1-tanh(\frac{x}{3}-\frac{10t}{9})}\)

\(g[5] := {u(x, t) = _C6*(exp(_C1-(1/3)*x+(2/3)*t))^3}\)

**低泡飛鼠屬**（屬名：***Petinomys***）是一類[飛鼠](../Page/飛鼠.md "wikilink")，包含以下物種：

  - [Basilan Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Basilan_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink") (*Petinomys
    crinitus*)
  - [Whiskered Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Whiskered_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")
    (*Petinomys genibarbis*)
  - [Hagen's Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Hagen's_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink") (*Petinomys
    hageni*)
  - [Siberut Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Siberut_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink") (*Petinomys
    lugens*)
  - [Arrow Flying Squirrel](../Page/Arrow_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")
    (*Petinomys sagitta*)
  - [Temminck's Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Temminck's_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")
    (*Petinomys setosus*)
  - [Vordermann's Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Vordermann's_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")
    (*Petinomys vordermanni*)
  - [Travancore Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Travancore_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")
    (*Petinomys fuscocapillus*)

**佛法僧科**（学名*Coraciidae*）在[动物分类学上是](../Page/动物分类学.md "wikilink")[鸟纲](../Page/鸟纲.md "wikilink")[佛法僧目中的一个](../Page/佛法僧目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科_\(生物\).md "wikilink")，体型类似[乌鸦](../Page/乌鸦.md "wikilink")，但与[翠鸟和](../Page/翠鸟科.md "wikilink")[蜂虎亲缘关系密切](../Page/蜂虎科.md "wikilink")。体色多为蓝色和褐色。以[昆虫为食](../Page/昆虫.md "wikilink")。

佛法僧科一共包括12种，在[中国有](../Page/中国.md "wikilink")3个种分布：

[佛法僧属](../Page/佛法僧属.md "wikilink") *Coracias*

  - [蓝胸佛法僧](../Page/蓝胸佛法僧.md "wikilink") *Coracias garrulus*
  - [蓝喉佛法僧](../Page/蓝喉佛法僧.md "wikilink") *Coracias abyssinica*
  - [紫胸佛法僧](../Page/紫胸佛法僧.md "wikilink") *Coracias caudata*
  - [宽尾佛法僧](../Page/宽尾佛法僧.md "wikilink") *Coracias spatulata*
  - [红冠佛法僧](../Page/红冠佛法僧.md "wikilink") *Coracias noevia*
  - [棕胸佛法僧](../Page/棕胸佛法僧.md "wikilink") *Coracias benghalensis*
  - [紫翅佛法僧](../Page/紫翅佛法僧.md "wikilink") *Coracias temminckii*
  - [蓝腹佛法僧](../Page/蓝腹佛法僧.md "wikilink") *Coracias cyanogaster*

[三宝鸟属](../Page/三宝鸟属.md "wikilink") *Eurystomus*

  - [宽喙三宝鸟](../Page/宽喙三宝鸟.md "wikilink") *Eurystomus glaucurus*
  - [蓝喉三宝鸟](../Page/蓝喉三宝鸟.md "wikilink") *Eurystomus gularis*
  - [紫三宝鸟](../Page/紫三宝鸟.md "wikilink") *Eurystomus azureus*
  - [三宝鸟](../Page/三宝鸟.md "wikilink") *Eurystomus orientalis*

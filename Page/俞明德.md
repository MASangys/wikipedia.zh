**俞明德**，現任[中國科技大學](../Page/中國科技大學.md "wikilink")校長，同時也是一位著名的[財務](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/財務 "wikilink")[金融](../Page/金融.md "wikilink")學者。 1960年生於台南永康，畢業於[台灣大學經濟系](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/台灣大學 "wikilink")，服預備軍官役於澎湖。1984年赴美深造，1990年獲美國[俄亥俄州立大學](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/俄亥俄州立大學 "wikilink")[經濟學博士學位](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/經濟學 "wikilink")，專精於[金融機構管理](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/金融機構 "wikilink")、[衍生性金融商品](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/衍生性金融商品 "wikilink")。

俞明德學經歷豐富，曾任[靜宜大學](../Page/靜宜大學.md "wikilink")校長（2002年－2010年）、[國立台灣大學財務金融學系特聘教授](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/國立台灣大學 "wikilink")、[國立交通大學](../Page/國立交通大學.md "wikilink")管理學院院長、[元智大學](../Page/元智大學.md "wikilink")管理學院院長、[國立中央大學財金系主任暨所長](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/國立中央大學 "wikilink")、美國Drexel大學財務金融系客座教授。學術研究及社會服務並重，2010年羅馬[梵蒂岡頒贈Benemerenti](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/梵蒂岡 "wikilink") Medal獎章，2012年獲中華民國管理科學會頒發「李國鼎管理獎章」，並多次榮獲[中華民國科技部傑出研究獎](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/中華民國科技部 "wikilink")，2018年獲國立交通大學終身講座教授殊榮。亦曾擔任台灣風險與保險學會理事長、台灣財務工程學會理事長、[行政院國家發展基金管理會](../Page/行政院國家發展基金管理會.md "wikilink")委員、行政院金融重建基金管理會委員、[財團法人住宅地震保險基金董事](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/財團法人 "wikilink")、中央存款保險公司與[亞洲開發銀行](../Page/亞洲開發銀行.md "wikilink")顧問、行政院傑出科技貢獻獎審議會委員。

俞明德的學術著作常見於 *Journal of Banking and Finance*, *Journal of Risk and Insurance*, *Journal of Financial Service Research*, *Journal Real Estate Economics and Finance*, *Journal of Business Finance and Accounting*, *Journal of Derivatives*, *Journal of Futures Markets*, *Quantitative Finance*, *Geneva Papers on Risk and Insurance Theory*, *Astin Bulletin*, *Insurance: Mathematics and Economics*, *Pacific Basin Finance Journal*, *Computational Economics* 以及 *ANOR*。


### Min-Teh Yu

Dr. Min-Teh Yu is Chair Professor and President at China University of Technology, Taiwan. He is also Chair Professor at National Chiao Tung University and a research fellow at RIRC, National Cheng-Chi University. Prior to joining CUTe in 2017, he has served as President of Providence University, Dean of National Chiao Tung University, Dean of Yuan Ze University, and Department Head at National Central University. He has been a Distinguished Professor at National Taiwan University and a Visiting Professor at Drexel University. He was also President of the Taiwan Risk and Insurance Association and the Financial Engineering Association of Taiwan. He received his Ph.D. in Economics from Ohio State University and a B.S. from National Taiwan University.

Dr. Yu has been a consultant for the U.S. Congressional Budget Office and the Asian Development Bank. He has won many grants and awards, among them the Distinguished Research Award of the National Science Council, the Distinguished Teaching Award of the Ministry of Education and the MacKay Canadian Studies Faculty Research Award. He has also served as Trustee for Fu-Jen Catholic University and Wenzao Ursuline College of Languages. He received the Benemerenti Medal from Pope Benedict XVI in 2010.

Some of his writings have appeared in *Journal of Banking and Finance*, *Journal of Risk and Insurance*, *Journal of Financial Service Research*, *Journal Real Estate Economics and Finance*, *Journal of Business Finance and Accounting*, *Journal of Derivatives*, *Journal of Futures Markets*, *Quantitative Finance*, *Geneva Papers on Risk and Insurance Theory*, *Astin Bulletin*, *Insurance: Mathematics and Economics*, *Pacific Basin Finance Journal*, *Computational Economics* and *ANOR*.

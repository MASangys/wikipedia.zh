**俞明德**為[靜宜大學](../Page/靜宜大學.md "wikilink")財務金融系講座教授，同時也是[清華大學科技管理學院榮譽講座教授](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/清華大學 "wikilink")、[交通大學終身講座教授](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/交通大學 "wikilink")。生於台灣台南，畢業於[台灣大學經濟系](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/台灣大學 "wikilink")，服預備軍官役於澎湖。1984年赴美深造，1990年獲美國[Ohio State University經濟學博士學位](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ohio_State_University "wikilink")，專精於[金融機構管理](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/金融機構 "wikilink")、[風險管理及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/風險管理 "wikilink")[衍生性金融商品之研究](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/衍生性金融商品 "wikilink")。俞明德教授教育行政與學術研究經歷豐富，曾任[靜宜大學](../Page/靜宜大學.md "wikilink")校長、[中國科技大學](../Page/中國科技大學.md "wikilink")校長、[台灣大學特聘教授](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/台灣大學 "wikilink")、[交通大學管理學院院長](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/交通大學 "wikilink")、[元智大學](../Page/元智大學.md "wikilink")管理學院院長、[中央大學財金系主任暨所長](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/中央大學 "wikilink")、美國Drexel大學財務金融系客座教授，並曾榮獲「李國鼎管理獎章」、「馬偕加拿大研究獎」、台灣[科技部](../Page/科技部.md "wikilink")傑出研究獎。

俞明德教授亦曾擔任「台灣風險與保險學會」理事長、「台灣財務工程學會」理事長、[亞洲開發銀行](../Page/亞洲開發銀行.md "wikilink")顧問、行政院國家發展基金管理委員會委員、行政院金融重建基金管理委員會委員、台灣住宅地震保險基金董事、香港學術基金委員會(HKRGC)的外審委員、[馬來西亞大學](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/馬來西亞大學 "wikilink")(UM)、[拉曼大學](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/拉曼大學 "wikilink")(UTAR)的外部評鑑委員。俞明德教授有超過50篇的SSCI/SCI著作見於國際學術期刊如*Journal of Banking and Finance*, *Journal of Risk and Insurance*, *British Accounting Review*, *Journal of Business Finance and Accounting*, *Journal of Futures Markets*, *Quarterly Review of Economics and Finance*, *Geneva Papers on Risk and Insurance Theory*, *Astin Bulletin*, *Pacific-Basin Finance Journal*, *Insurance: Mathematics and economics*, *Annals of Operation Research*等。

\=== Min-Teh Yu === Dr. Min-Teh Yu is Chair Professor of Finance at Providence University. Currently, he also holds an honorary Chair Professor at National Chiao Tung University and National Tsing Hua University. He was a Distinguished Professor of Finance at National Taiwan University. Dr. Yu has served as President at Providence University and China University of Technology, Dean of College of Management at National Chiao Tung University and Yuan Ze University, Department Chairman at National Central University. He received his Ph.D. in Economics from Ohio State University and a B.A. from National Taiwan University. He was a Visiting Professor of Finance at Drexel University from 1997 to 1998.

His teaching and research focus is in the areas of financial institutions and risk management. His writings have appeared in journals such as *Journal of Banking and Finance*, *Journal of Risk and Insurance*, *British Accounting Review*, *Journal of Financial Service Research*, *Journal of Business Finance and Accounting*, *Journal of Futures Markets*, *Quarterly Review of Economics and Finance*, *Geneva Papers on Risk and Insurance Theory*, *Astin Bulletin*, *Pacific-Basin Finance Journal*, *Insurance: Mathematics and Economics*, *Annals of Operation Research*, etc... He is also an associate editor for more than 10 academic and trade journals in banking, finance and insurance.

Dr. Yu has been a consultant for the U.S. Congressional Budget Office and the Asian Development Bank. He has won many grants and awards, among them the Distinguished Research Award of the National Science Council, the Distinguished Teaching Award of Ministry of Education and the MacKay Canadian Studies Faculty Research Award. He has also served as a trustee for Fu-Jen Catholic University and Wenzao Ursuline University of Languages. He received the Benemerenti Medal from Pope Benedict XVI in 2010. He was the President of Taiwan Risk and Insurance Association, President of Taiwan Financial Engineering Association, and a Board Director for the Taiwan Finance Association.

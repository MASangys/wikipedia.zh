**倉助利** (창조리) (?-?) 是高句丽[烽上王和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/烽上王 "wikilink")[美川王](../Page/美川王.md "wikilink")时代的[国相](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/莫离支 "wikilink")。

## 背景

Chang Jo-ri's origins or ancestry is not mentioned in historical records. It can be inferred that Prime Minister Chang Jo-ri came from a notable noble family because he served in high government positions such as *Daesaja* and *Daejubu*.

## 生平

### [烽上王时代](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/烽上王 "wikilink")

Chang Jo-ri is first mentioned to have served as *Daesaja* of the South Province, and later as *Daejubu*. He rose to the position of Prime Minister in the year 294, succeeding Prime Minister [尙婁](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/尙婁 "wikilink"). 296年，[鲜卑](../Page/鲜卑.md "wikilink")[前燕](../Page/前燕.md "wikilink")[高祖入侵](../Page/慕容廆.md "wikilink")[高句丽](../Page/高句丽.md "wikilink"). With this invasion, Chang Jo-ri urged the [烽上王](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/烽上王 "wikilink") to assign *Daehyeong* general [高奴子](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/高奴子 "wikilink") to the position of Castlelord of [新城](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/辽阳 "wikilink"). The Former Yan forces were defeated due to this assignment. When King Bongsang became corrupt and violent, Chang Jo-ri resigned from the position of Prime Minister and planned a coup to overthrow the tyrant and place [高乙弗](../Page/美川王.md "wikilink"), the King's nephew, on the throne. Chang Jo-ri staged the coup in the year 300, and overthrew King Bongsang, who committed suicide upon being banished.

### [美川王](../Page/美川王.md "wikilink")时代

Chang Jo-ri was reinstated to the position of Prime Minister under King Micheon. Samguk Sagi describes the benign rule of King Micheon, and his advisor Prime Minister Chang Jo-ri. The year of his death is unknown.

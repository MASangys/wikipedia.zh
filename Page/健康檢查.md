**全面醫學檢查**（英文：general medical
examination）又稱為**健康檢查**（英文：Health
Check）是為一種常見的[預防醫學](../Page/預防醫學.md "wikilink")。一個具體的定義為：一個自我感覺[健康良好的](../Page/健康.md "wikilink")[成人定期到院進行身心健康狀況檢查](../Page/成人.md "wikilink")，每次檢查的間隔可以是一年一度或更長。

[中華民國](../Page/中華民國.md "wikilink")[衛生福利部](../Page/衛生福利部.md "wikilink")[國民健康署提供](../Page/國民健康署.md "wikilink")40歲以上未滿65歲民眾每3年1次、55歲以上原住民、罹患小兒麻痺且年在35歲以上者、65歲以上民眾每年1次成人健康檢查。
 In the United Kingdom, the examination remains unpopular with the
general populace.\[1\] \[2\]

The term is generally *not* meant to include visits for the purpose of
newborn checks, [Pap smears](../Page/巴氏涂片检查.md "wikilink") for [cervical
cancer](../Page/子宮頸癌.md "wikilink"), or regular visits for people with
certain [chronic](../Page/慢性病.md "wikilink") medical disorders (for
example, [diabetes](../Page/糖尿病.md "wikilink")).\[3\]The general medical
examination generally involves a [medical
history](../Page/病史.md "wikilink"), a (brief or complete) [physical
examination](../Page/理學檢查.md "wikilink") and sometimes
[laboratory](../Page/檢驗醫學.md "wikilink") tests. Some more advanced tests
include ultrasound and mammography.

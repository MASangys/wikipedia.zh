**《傻瓜大哥再出擊》**（Lage Raho Munna Bhai
）是一部2006年的[寶萊塢](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/寶萊塢 "wikilink")[喜劇電影](../Page/喜劇電影.md "wikilink")，由[](../Page/拉库马·希拉尼.md "wikilink")[拉库马·希拉尼](../Page/拉库马·希拉尼.md "wikilink") 執導，由Vidhu
Vinod Chopra 擔任製片。這部片是2003年的電影《<span>Munna Bhai
M.B.B.S.》的續集，[山齊·杜特](../Page/山齊·杜特.md "wikilink") 再度飾演</span>主角。

## 電影角色

  - [山齊·杜特](../Page/山齊·杜特.md "wikilink") 飾演 慕里as Murli Prasad Sharma or
    "Munna Bhai"
  - [亞薩·瓦西](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/亞薩·瓦西 "wikilink") as Circuit ,
    Munna's sidekick
  - [瑋焍婭·芭蘭](../Page/瑋焍婭·芭蘭.md "wikilink") as Jhanvi, radio-jockey and
    Munna's love-interest
  - [博曼·伊蘭尼](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/博曼·伊蘭尼 "wikilink") as Lucky
    Singh
  - Dilip Prabhavalkar as the image of [Mahatma
    Gandhi](../Page/圣雄甘地.md "wikilink")
  - [迪雅·梅莎](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/迪雅·梅莎 "wikilink") as Simran,
    Lucky's daughter, engaged to Sunny
  - [傑米·瑟吉爾](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/傑米·瑟吉爾 "wikilink") as Victor
    D'Souza, who having lost his father's money, needs Munna's advice
  - Kulbhushan Kharbanda as Kkhurana, a wealthy but superstitious
    businessman
  - Saurabh Shukla as Batuk Maharaj, Kkhurana's astrologer
  - [阿彼锡·巴克罕](../Page/阿彼锡·巴克罕.md "wikilink") as Sunny, Kkhurana's son,
    engaged to Simran
  - Rohitash Gaud as Cuckoo (Lucky's Sidekick)
  - Parikshat Sahni as Victor's father
  - Ashwin Mushran as Hari Desai
  - Hemu Adhikari as the Retired teacher
  - Priya Bapat as the girl who calls Murli
  - Ninad Kamat as the Lawyer
  - Kurush Deboo as Dhansukh Bhai Patel, Gujarati Lawyer
  - Arun Bali as a Second Innings Resident
  - Achyut Potdar as a Second Innings Resident

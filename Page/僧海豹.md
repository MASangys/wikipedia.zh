**僧海豹**（*Monachini*）是[僧海豹族下的](../Page/僧海豹族.md "wikilink")3種[海豹](../Page/海豹.md "wikilink")。包括以下[物種](../Page/物種.md "wikilink")\[1\]：

  - [夏威夷僧海豹](../Page/夏威夷僧海豹.md "wikilink")（*Monachus
    schauinslandi*）：現正處於[極危](../Page/極危.md "wikilink")。\[2\]
  - [地中海僧海豹](../Page/地中海僧海豹.md "wikilink")（*Neomonachus
    monachus*）：現正處於[極危](../Page/極危.md "wikilink")。\[3\]
  - †[加勒比僧海豹](../Page/加勒比僧海豹.md "wikilink")（*Neomonachus
    tropicalis*）：已[滅絕](../Page/滅絕.md "wikilink")。\[4\]

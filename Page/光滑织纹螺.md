**光滑織紋螺**（学名：），是[新腹足目](../Page/新腹足目.md "wikilink")[织纹螺科](../Page/织纹螺科.md "wikilink")*Zeuxis*的一种\[1\]\[2\]\[3\]\[4\]。主要分布于[中国大陆](../Page/中国大陆.md "wikilink")、[台湾](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/台湾 "wikilink")，常栖息在潮下带\[5\]。

## 簡述

殼長從 15 mm 到 45 mm 不等。

The ovate, conical shell is smooth andof a uniform ashy white color. The [spire](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spire_\(mollusc\) "wikilink") is composed of eight [whorls](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Whorl_\(mollusc\) "wikilink"). The upper ones are slightly plaited, the lowest pretty large, marked with more or less straight, longitudinal lines, approximate, and of a color a little deeper and reddish. The base of the shell is ornamented with six or seven furrows. The ovate [aperture](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aperture_\(mollusc\) "wikilink") is, white, fawn-colored within. The thick outer [lip](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lip_\(gastropod\) "wikilink") is arcuated towards the base, elevated exteriorly into a thick, very prominent margin. Within it is striated throughout its whole length. The left lip is thick, and partially covering the [columella](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Columella_\(gastropod\) "wikilink"), which is adorned with guttules one half of its length. The first of these guttules, which is uppermost, is much more marked than the others. At the base of the columella is found a very prominent fold, terminated by a guttule formed like a flattened point.\[6\]

## Distribution

This marine species occurs in the tropical Western Pacific, off [East India](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_India "wikilink"), the [Philippines](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philippines "wikilink"), [Papua New Guinea](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Papua_New_Guinea "wikilink") and Australia ([New South Wales](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_South_Wales "wikilink"), the [Northern Territory](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northern_Territory "wikilink"), [Queensland](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Queensland "wikilink"), [Western Australia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Australia "wikilink"))

**羽毛管蟲屬**（學名：）是[多毛綱](../Page/多毛綱.md "wikilink")[纓鰓蟲目](../Page/纓鰓蟲目.md "wikilink")[纓鰓蟲科之下的一個屬](../Page/纓鰓蟲科.md "wikilink")\[1\]。

## 物種

綜合[WoRMS在](../Page/WoRMS.md "wikilink")2010年列出的七個物種\[2\]及來自其他文獻的內容，以下詳列本屬各物種：

1.  [澳洲光纓蟲](../Page/澳洲光纓蟲.md "wikilink") *[Sabellastarte
    australiensis](../Page/Sabellastarte_australiensis.md "wikilink")*
    (Haswell, 1884)
2.  *[Sabellastarte
    assimilis](../Page/Sabellastarte_assimilis.md "wikilink")*
    (McIntosh, 1885)
3.  *[Sabellastarte fallax](../Page/Sabellastarte_fallax.md "wikilink")*
    (Quatrefages, 1866)
4.  [印度光纓蟲](../Page/印度光纓蟲.md "wikilink")（*[Sabellastarte
    indica](../Page/Sabellastarte_indica.md "wikilink")* (Savigny,
    1822)）：[WoRMS引用](../Page/WoRMS.md "wikilink")2003年文獻，認為本物種跟旋鰓管蟲只是[同種異名](../Page/同種異名.md "wikilink")；但[TaiBIF引](../Page/TaiBIF.md "wikilink")[謝蕙蓮的紀錄](../Page/謝蕙蓮.md "wikilink")，指本物種在形態上有分別，「有約8公分大型觸手羽冠，呈馬蹄形，上面常有紅色到咖啡色，或有灰白及藍子色斑紋」\[3\]，較旋鰓管蟲為鮮艷。
5.  [日本光纓蟲](../Page/日本光纓蟲.md "wikilink") *[Sabellastarte
    japonica](../Page/Sabellastarte_japonica.md "wikilink")*
    (Marenzeller, 1885)：WoRMS認為本物種是*[Branchiomma
    cingulata](../Page/Branchiomma_cingulata.md "wikilink")* (Grube,
    1870)的同種異名。
6.  *[Sabellastarte longa](../Page/Sabellastarte_longa.md "wikilink")*
    (Kinberg, 1866)
7.  [大羽毛管虫](../Page/大羽毛管虫.md "wikilink") *[Sabellastarte
    magnifica](../Page/Sabellastarte_magnifica.md "wikilink")* (Shaw,
    1800)
8.  [胸斑光纓蟲](../Page/胸斑光纓蟲.md "wikilink") *[Sabellastarte
    pectoralis](../Page/Sabellastarte_pectoralis.md "wikilink")*
    (Quatrefages, 1866)
9.  *[Sabellastarte
    sanctijosephi](../Page/Sabellastarte_sanctijosephi.md "wikilink")*
    (Gravier, 1906)
10. [旋鰓管蟲](../Page/旋鰓管蟲.md "wikilink") *[Sabellastarte
    spectabilis](../Page/Sabellastarte_spectabilis.md "wikilink")*
    (Grube,
    1878)：又名[華麗光纓蟲或](../Page/華麗光纓蟲.md "wikilink")[粗壯光纓蟲](../Page/粗壯光纓蟲.md "wikilink")，[棲管皮革質](../Page/棲管.md "wikilink")，[鰓冠大](../Page/鰓冠.md "wikilink")，伸展可達10公分，大多為黃褐色或黃棕色\[4\]。
11. *[Sabellastarte
    zebuensis](../Page/Sabellastarte_zebuensis.md "wikilink")*
    (McIntosh, 1885)

\(K_n(x;p,N)={N \choose n}^{-1}*\)\(\sum_{k=0}^{n}{N-x \choose n-k}*{x \choose k}*(1-1/p)^k\)=\(_2F_1(-n,-x;N;1/p)\)

克拉夫楚克多项式的头几项是

\(K_0(x,p,N)= 1\)

\(K_1(x,p,N)= 1-\frac{x}{p*N}\)

\(K_2(x,p,N)= 1+(\frac{-2}{p*N}+\frac{1}{(p^2*N*(-N+1)})*x-\frac{x^2}{(p^2*N*(-N+1))}\)

\(K_3(x,p,N)= 1+(\frac{-3}{(p*N)}+\frac{3}{(p^2*N*(-N+1))}-\frac{2}{(p^3*N*(-N+1)*(-N+2))})*x+(-3/(p^2*N*(-N+1))+3/(p^3*N*(-N+1)*(-N+2)))*x^2-x^3/(p^3*N*(-N+1)*(-N+2))\)

## 极限关系

[量子Q克拉夫楚克多项式](../Page/量子Q克拉夫楚克多项式.md "wikilink")→ **克拉夫楚克多项式**：

\(\lim_{a \to 1}=K_{n}^{qtm}(q^-{x};p,N;q)=K_{n}(x;p^{-1},N)\)

令[双Q克拉夫楚克多项式](../Page/双Q克拉夫楚克多项式.md "wikilink")\(c=1=p^{-1}\),并令q→1,即得**克拉夫楚克多项式**

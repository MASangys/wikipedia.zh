**八面體數**是能排成[八面體的](../Page/八面體.md "wikilink")[有形數](../Page/有形數.md "wikilink"),
或是由兩個[四角錐疊起來](../Page/四角錐.md "wikilink"), 另一個倒置在下面.
計算**八面體數**\(O_n\)可以用第*n-1*個和第*n*個[四角錐數的](../Page/四角錐數.md "wikilink")[和](../Page/加法.md "wikilink")
, 或是使用下列公式:

\[O_n={n(2n^2 + 1) \over 3}.\]

前幾個八面體數為:

[1](../Page/1.md "wikilink"), [6](../Page/6.md "wikilink"),
[19](../Page/19.md "wikilink"), [44](../Page/44.md "wikilink"),
[85](../Page/85.md "wikilink"), [146](../Page/146.md "wikilink"),
[231](../Page/231.md "wikilink"), [344](../Page/344.md "wikilink"), 489,
670, 891 .

八面體數有一個[母函數](../Page/母函數.md "wikilink")

\[\frac{z(z+1)^2}{(z-1)^4} = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} O_n z^n = z +6z^2 + 19z^3 + \cdots .\]

[波洛克爵士猜想在](../Page/:en:Sir_Frederick_Pollock,_1st_Baronet.md "wikilink")1850之內，每一個數字都可以寫成最多[7八面體數的總和](../Page/7.md "wikilink")(Dickson
2005,
第23頁)參見[波洛克八面體數猜想](../Page/:en:Pollock_octahedral_numbers_conjecture.md "wikilink")。

**八面體數**\(O_n\)可以使用[三角形數](../Page/三角形數.md "wikilink")\(T_n\)表示

\[O_n+4T_{n-1}=T_{2n-1}.\]

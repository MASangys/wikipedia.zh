**冠鰈科**（学名：*Samaridae*）是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鰈亞目的一科](../Page/鰈亞目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**冠鰈科**下分3個屬，如下：

### 斜頜鰈屬(*Plagiopsetta*)

  - [舌形斜頜鰈](../Page/舌形斜頜鰈.md "wikilink")(*Plagiopsetta glossa*)
  - [細身斜頜鰈](../Page/細身斜頜鰈.md "wikilink")(*Plagiopsetta gracilis*)
  - [多斑斜頜鰈](../Page/多斑斜頜鰈.md "wikilink")(*Plagiopsetta stigmosa*)

### 冠鰈屬(*Samaris*)

  - [賈斯特冠鰈](../Page/賈斯特冠鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samaris chesterfieldensis*)
  - [科氏冠鰈](../Page/科氏冠鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samaris costae*)
  - [冠鰈](../Page/冠鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samaris cristatus*)
  - [大鱗冠鰈](../Page/大鱗冠鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samaris macrolepis*)

### 沙鰈屬(*Samariscus*)

  - [阿氏沙鰈](../Page/阿氏沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus asanoi*)
  - [珊瑚沙鰈](../Page/珊瑚沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus corallinus*)
  - [德蘇沙鰈](../Page/德蘇沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus desoutterae*)
  - [絲鰭沙鰈](../Page/絲鰭沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus filipectoralis*)
  - [胡氏沙鰈](../Page/胡氏沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus huysmani*)
  - [短頜沙鰈](../Page/短頜沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus inornatus*)：又稱無斑沙鰈。
  - [日本沙鰈](../Page/日本沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus japonicus*)
  - [滿月沙鰈](../Page/滿月沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus latus*)
  - [醜沙鰈](../Page/醜沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus leopardus*)
  - [長臂沙鰈](../Page/長臂沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus longimanus*)
  - [呂宋沙鰈](../Page/呂宋沙鰈.md "wikilink")(*Samariscus luzonensis*)

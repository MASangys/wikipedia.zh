**雲達·韋索沃斯卡**（；）是一位[波蘭的女性](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/波蘭 "wikilink")[動物地理學家](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/動物地理學家 "wikilink")、[蜘蛛學家和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/蜘蛛學家 "wikilink")[鳥類學家](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鳥類學家 "wikilink")，並以其對[蛛形綱動物的研究而知名](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/蛛形綱 "wikilink")。她專長於[跳蛛科物種的分類](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/跳蛛科 "wikilink")、生物特性及[地理分佈](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/動物地理學 "wikilink")\[1\]。

## 簡歷

雲達·韋索沃斯卡在1968年於的生物學及地球科學學系修讀生物學。她原來的興趣在[鳥類學](../Page/鳥類學.md "wikilink")。之後她轉往[弗罗茨瓦夫大学修讀碩士](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/弗罗茨瓦夫大学 "wikilink")，並於1973年以「**」（於遷徙期在弗沃茨瓦維克附近維斯杜拉大壩水庫濕地的鳥類觀測）為論文題目而取得生物學碩士；而這篇論文亦發表於*[Acta zooogica Cracoviensia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acta_zooogica_Cracoviensia "wikilink")*這份期刊\[2\]。

畢業後，雲達·韋索沃斯卡留在弗罗茨瓦夫大学， After graduating, she took a role at what is now the  (then *Wyższa Szkoła Pedagogiczno-Rolnicza*) in [謝德爾采](../Page/謝德爾采.md "wikilink") where she developed her lifelong interest in the [蠅虎科](../Page/蠅虎科.md "wikilink") family (Salticidae). She stayed at Siedlce until moving to the [弗羅茨瓦夫大學](../Page/弗羅茨瓦夫大學.md "wikilink") in 1978 to start her doctoral study. She achieved a doctorate in natural sciences in 1984 and since then has been successively an Assistant, Assistant Professor and Associate Professor at the University. She was awarded [特许任教资格](../Page/特许任教资格.md "wikilink") on the basis of her work *A Revision of the Spider Genus Menemerus in Africa (Araneae: Salticidae)*, published in the Genus, and in 2009 was made a Professor of the institution. During her career, much of her work has involved work in Africa, including the taxonomy of **, **, **, ** and **, and the biodiversity of spider ecology.\[3\]

She is a member of the African Arachnological Society, , Polskie Towarzystwo Taksonomiczne and Polskie Towarzystwo Zoologiczne.

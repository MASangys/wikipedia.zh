**凹下**村為[魏氏](../Page/魏.md "wikilink")[客家村落](../Page/客家.md "wikilink")，位於[香港](../Page/香港.md "wikilink")[北區近下](../Page/北區_\(香港\).md "wikilink")[禾坑村位置](../Page/禾坑.md "wikilink")，地處[沙頭角公路旁邊之](../Page/沙頭角公路.md "wikilink")[山凹一個寧靜之角落](../Page/山凹.md "wikilink")，因地理位置處於山凹（[山坳](../Page/山坳.md "wikilink")＝[山窩](../Page/山窩.md "wikilink")）之下位置，故[魏氏祖先立村名為](../Page/魏氏.md "wikilink")「凹下」（[au5](http://www.chineselanguage.org/dictionaries/ccdict/sounds/hakka/siyan/au24.wav)[ha5](http://www.chineselanguage.org/dictionaries/ccdict/sounds/hakka/siyan/ha55.wav)或者[ha3](http://www.chineselanguage.org/dictionaries/ccdict/sounds/hakka/siyan/ha31.wav)或者[ka1](http://www.chineselanguage.org/dictionaries/ccdict/sounds/hakka/siyan/kha24.wav)或者[ha1](http://www.chineselanguage.org/dictionaries/ccdict/sounds/hakka/siyan/ha24.wav)），祖先為大種[客家人也](../Page/客家人.md "wikilink")，約兩百年前由[五華](../Page/五華.md "wikilink")（舊稱「[長樂](../Page/長樂.md "wikilink")」遷來），有大部分村民於1928年分支到[上水](../Page/上水.md "wikilink")[古洞南今日之](../Page/古洞.md "wikilink")[長壢村](../Page/長壢.md "wikilink")。

    following pressure from army high command. Mobutu became head of
    government as well as head of state. Born
    [Kasai](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Kasai_region "wikilink")
    1930, Joined Force publique 1949, Sergeant Major by 1960, quickly
    became an officer. 1962 assigned—command the 3rd *Groupement* at
    [Kisangani](../Page/基桑加尼.md "wikilink"). 'Gained international fame
    for.. defence of Bukavu and for conducting one of the most decisive
    battles of the 1964 north-east revolution. When Kisangani was
    recaptured from rebel forces in 1964 he was named military governor
    of the entire northeastern region.' 'General Mulamba has always
    enjoyed great popularity with the troops. He is known for his
    straightforward approach—problems. He has a sizeable farm outside
    Kinshasa—which, he has said, he would like—retire some day.'(Sydney
    Taylor, The New Africans, 1967, p.102)

**刺果树属**（[学名](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/学名 "wikilink")：*Chaetocarpus*）是[大戟科](../Page/大戟科.md "wikilink")下的一个属，为[灌木](../Page/灌木.md "wikilink")或[乔木植物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/乔木 "wikilink")。该属共有10种，分布于[热带](../Page/热带.md "wikilink")[亚洲](../Page/亚洲.md "wikilink")、[非洲](../Page/非洲.md "wikilink")和[美洲](../Page/美洲.md "wikilink")。\[1\]

品種：\[2\]

1.  *Chaetocarpus acutifolius* <small>(Britton & P.Wilson) Borhidi</small> - Sierra de Moa in Cuba
2.  *Chaetocarpus africanus* <small>Pax</small> - C Africa
3.  *Chaetocarpus castanocarpus* <small>(Roxb.) Thwaites</small> - SE Asia, [Yunnan](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yunnan "wikilink"), [Assam](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assam "wikilink"), Bangladesh, Sri Lanka
4.  *Chaetocarpus cordifolius* <small>(Urb.) Borhidi</small> - Cuba, Hispaniola, Jamaica
5.  *Chaetocarpus coriaceus* <small>Thwaites</small> - Sri Lanka
6.  *Chaetocarpus cubensis* <small>Fawc. & Rendle</small> - Cuba
7.  *Chaetocarpus echinocarpus* (Baill.) Ducke - Bolivia, Brazil
8.  *Chaetocarpus ferrugineus* <small>Philcox</small> - Sri Lanka
9.  *Chaetocarpus gabonensis* <small>Breteler</small> - Gabon
10. *Chaetocarpus globosus* <small>(Sw.) Fawc. & Rendle</small> - Jamaica, Cuba, Dominican Rep.
11. *Chaetocarpus myrsinites* <small>Baill.</small> - Bolivia, Brazil
12. *Chaetocarpus parvifolius* <small>Borhidi</small> - Cuba
13. *Chaetocarpus pearcei* <small>Rusby</small> - Bolivia
14. *Chaetocarpus pubescens* <small>(Thwaites) Hook.f.</small> - Sri Lanka
15. *Chaetocarpus rabaraba* <small>Capuron</small> - Madagascar
16. *Chaetocarpus schomburgkianus* <small>(Kuntze) Pax & K.Hoffm.</small> - Colombia, Venezuela, 3 Guianas, NW Brazil

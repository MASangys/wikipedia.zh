**刺猬信号通路**（）是重要的[信号传导通路](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/信号传导 "wikilink")。

刺猬信号通路是动物发育的关键调控之一，在所有的[两侧对称动物](../Page/两侧对称动物.md "wikilink")中都有表达。刺猬信号通路得名于在其多肽配体——Drosophila果蝇中发现的一种名为Hh的细胞间信号分子。Hh是 Drosophila体节极性基因的表达物，是果蝇体形发育形成的基础。该分子在后期胚胎开成及变态期及起重要作用。\[1\]。

共有五種刺蝟因子：[音蝟因子](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/音蝟因子 "wikilink")（Sonic hedgehog，SHH）、[沙漠刺蝟因子](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/沙漠刺蝟因子 "wikilink")（desert hedgehog，DHH）、[印度刺蝟因子](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/印度刺蝟因子 "wikilink")（Indian hedgehog，IHH）、Echidna Hedgehog，EHH和Tiggywinkle Hedgehog，TwHH）。仅在[鱼类中发现有EHH和TwHH](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鱼类 "wikilink")，[哺乳类和其他动物中没有这两种刺蝟因子](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/哺乳类 "wikilink")。

哺乳动物有三种刺猬信号路径同系物，DHH、IHH及SHH,其中Sonic研究得最深入。该路径在脊椎动物胚胎發育中同样的重要。缺乏该路径组件的基因敲除小鼠，其大大脑、骨骼、肌肉系统、胃肠道及肺都未能正正常发育。近期研究表明刺猬信号路径在调节对成人组织起到维护及再生作用的成体干细胞中的作用。同样该路径也与某些癌症的发生的关系。几家制药公司正在积极地开发针对刺猬信号传导以对抗癌症的药物。

## 发现

[Denticlebands.png](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Denticlebands.png "fig:Denticlebands.png")

在20世纪70年代，[发育生物学](../Page/发育生物学.md "wikilink")的基本问题是一个相对简单的受精卵是如何发展成一个复杂的身体的动物的。70年代末，[克里斯汀·纽斯林-沃尔哈德和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/克里斯汀·纽斯林-沃尔哈德 "wikilink")[艾瑞克·威斯乔斯分离了控制果蝇前后体轴发育分节的突变基因](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/艾瑞克·威斯乔斯 "wikilink")\[2\]，他们的“饱和诱变”技术导致了一系列参与体轴[形态发生](../Page/形态发生.md "wikilink")基因的发现。1995年，他们因在对基因突变的研究工作与[爱德华·路易斯共同获得](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/爱德华·路易斯 "wikilink")[诺贝尔奖](../Page/诺贝尔生理学或医学奖.md "wikilink")。\[3\]

黑腹果蠅的刺猬 (*hh*) 基因被發現為其中一種可以造成個體體節前後差異的重要基因。果蠅的刺蝟基因在1992年分別被 [Jym Mohler](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jym_Mohler "wikilink"), [Philip Beachy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Philip_Beachy "wikilink"), 以及 [Thomas B. Kornberg的實驗室所複製出來](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thomas_B._Kornberg "wikilink")。 若其中一些刺猬基因發生突變，將會造成相較於[野生型](../Page/野生型.md "wikilink")來說，較為短小且矮胖的不正常形狀胚胎。刺猬基因的功能为影响体节极性，其突变会会影响幼体的表皮棘刺和成体的附支，如腿和触角\[4\]。Rather than the normal pattern of denticles, hedgehog mutant larvae tend to have "solid lawns" of denticles (Figure 1). 刺蝟基因突變的果蠅幼蟲具有短小且多毛的特徵，形似刺蝟，故此得名。

### 机制

昆蟲細胞表达全长含有[鋅指的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鋅指 "wikilink")[轉錄因子](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/轉錄因子 "wikilink"), 而此蛋白會在細胞質中和类似[kinesin蛋白Costal](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/kinesin "wikilink")-2 (Cos2) 形成複合體並結合到[微管](../Page/微管.md "wikilink")上（圖2）。 [SCF复合物对](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:SCF_complex "wikilink")155 kDa全长Ci蛋白的依赖于[蛋白酶体](../Page/蛋白酶体.md "wikilink")的剪切产生75 kDa片段(CiR)。CiR在细胞内累计并[扩散到](../Page/扩散作用.md "wikilink")[细胞核](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/细胞核 "wikilink")，在那里它作为[刺猬因子靶基因的共](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/刺猬因子 "wikilink")[抑制物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:repressor "wikilink")。\[5\] The steps leading to Ci protein [proteolysis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/proteolysis "wikilink") include [phosphorylation](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/phosphorylation "wikilink") of Ci protein by several [protein kinases](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/protein_kinase "wikilink"); [PKA](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/CAMP-dependent_protein_kinase "wikilink"), [GSK3β](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/GSK-3 "wikilink") and [CK1](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Casein_kinase_1 "wikilink") (Figure 2).\[6\] The *Drosophila* protein Slimb is part of an [SCF complex](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubiquitin#Ubiquitylation "wikilink") that targets proteins for [ubiquitylation](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubiquitin "wikilink"). Slimb binds to [phosphorylated](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/phosphorylated "wikilink") Ci protein.

In the absence of Hh (圖 3), a cell-surface transmembrane protein called [Patched](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/PTCH1 "wikilink") (PTCH) acts to prevent high expression and activity of a [7 membrane spanning receptor](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/7TM_Receptor "wikilink")\[7\] called [Smoothened](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Smoothened "wikilink") (SMO). Patched has sequence similarity to known membrane transport proteins. When extracellular Hh is present (Figure 3), it binds to and inhibits Patched, allowing Smoothened to accumulate and inhibit the proteolytic cleavage of the Ci protein. This process most likely involves the direct interaction of Smoothened and Costal-2 and may involve sequestration of the Ci protein-containing complex to a microdomain where the steps leading to Ci protein proteolysis are disrupted.\[8\] The mechanism by which Hh binding to Patched leads to increased levels of Smoothened is not clear (Step 1 in Figure 3). Following binding of Hh to Patched, Smoothened levels increase greatly over the level maintained in cells when Patched is not bound to Hh.\[9\] It has been suggested that phosphorylation of Smoothened plays a role in Hh-dependent regulation of Smoothened levels.\[10\]

In cells with Hh-activated Patched (Figure 3), the intact Ci protein accumulates in the cell cytoplasm and levels of CiR decrease, allowing transcription of some genes such as [decapentaplegic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/decapentaplegic "wikilink") (dpp, a member of the [BMP](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bone_morphogenetic_protein "wikilink") growth factor family). For other Hh-regulated genes, expression requires not only the loss of CiR but also the positive action of uncleaved Ci to act as a [transcriptional activator](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/transcriptional_activator "wikilink").\[11\] Costal-2 is normally important for holding Ci protein in the cytoplasm, but interaction of Smoothened with Costal-2 allows some intact Ci protein to go to the nucleus. The *Drosophila* protein Fused (Fu in Figure 3) is a protein kinase that binds to Costal-2. Fused can inhibit Suppressor of Fused (SUFU), which in turn interacts with Ci to regulate gene transcription in some cell types.\[12\]

### 作用

[Wingless_and_Hedgehog_reciprocal_signaling_during_segmentation_of_Drosophila_embryos.svg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Wingless_and_Hedgehog_reciprocal_signaling_during_segmentation_of_Drosophila_embryos.svg "fig:Wingless_and_Hedgehog_reciprocal_signaling_during_segmentation_of_Drosophila_embryos.svg") Hedgehog has roles in larval body segment development and in formation of adult appendages. During the formation of body segments in the developing *Drosophila* embryo, stripes of cells that synthesize the [transcription factor](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/transcription_factor "wikilink") [Engrailed](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Engrailed_\(gene\) "wikilink") can also express the cell-to-cell signaling protein Hedgehog (green in Figure 4). Hedgehog is not free to move very far from the cells that make it and so it only activates a thin stripe of cells adjacent to the Engrailed-expressing cells. When acting in this local fashion, hedgehog works as a [paracrine](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/paracrine_signalling "wikilink") factor. Only cells to one side of the Engrailed-expressing cells are competent to respond to Hedgehog following interaction of Hh with the receptor protein Patched (blue in Figure 4).

Cells with Hh-activated Patched receptor synthesize the [Wingless](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wnt_signaling_pathway "wikilink") protein (red in Figure 4). If a *Drosophila* embryo is altered so as to produce Hh in all cells, all of the competent cells respond and form a broader band of Wingless-expressing cells in each segment. The *wingless* gene has an upstream transcription regulatory region that binds the Ci transcription factor in a Hh-dependent fashion resulting in an increase in *wingless* transcription (interaction 2 in Figure 3) in a stripe of cells adjacent to the stripe of Hh-producing cells.\[13\]

Wingless protein acts as an extracellular signal and patterns the adjacent rows of cells by activating its cell surface receptor [Frizzled](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Frizzled "wikilink"). Wingless acts on Engrailed-expressing cells to stabilize the stripes of Engrailed expression. Wingless is a member of the [Wnt](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wnt_signaling_pathway "wikilink") family of cell-to-cell signaling proteins. The reciprocal signaling by Hedgehog and Wingless stabilizes the boundary between parasegments (Figure 4, top). The effects of Wingless and Hedgehog on other stripes of cells in each segment establishes a positional code that accounts for the distinct anatomical features along the anterior-posterior axis of the segments \[14\]

The Wingless protein is called "wingless" because of the [phenotype](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/phenotype "wikilink") of some *wingless* fly mutants. Wingless and Hedgehog functioned together during [metamorphosis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metamorphosis_\(biology\) "wikilink") to coordinate wing formation. Hedgehog is expressed in the posterior part of developing *Drosophila* limbs. Hedgehog also participates in the coordination of eye, brain, gonad, gut and tracheal development. Hedgehog has been implicated in reduced eye development in the amphipod Gammarus minus. Specifically, downregulation of hedgehog results in reduced eyes.\[15\]

## Notable people who declined their invitations

The following are notable people who declined their invitations for various reasons:

  - [Stephen Harper](../Page/史蒂芬·哈珀.md "wikilink") ([Prime Minister of Canada](../Page/加拿大總理.md "wikilink")) and his wife  – the wedding day was three days before the [Canadian federal elections](../Page/2011年加拿大联邦大选.md "wikilink").\[295\]

  -  Sir [Michael Somare](../Page/迈克尔·索马雷.md "wikilink") ([Prime Minister of Papua New Guinea](../Page/巴布亚新几内亚总理.md "wikilink")) and Lady Somare\[296\] – Michael Somare was recovering from surgery.\[297\]

  - [The Baroness Thatcher](../Page/玛格丽特·撒切尔.md "wikilink") (Former [Prime Minister of the United Kingdom](../Page/英国首相.md "wikilink") 1979–1990) – declined due to ill health.\[298\]

  - [The King](../Page/阿卜杜拉二世.md "wikilink") and [Queen of Jordan](../Page/拉尼婭王后_\(約旦\).md "wikilink") – declined due to the [protests](../Page/2011年-2012年約旦反政府示威.md "wikilink") in their country.

  - [The Queen of the Netherlands](../Page/贝娅特丽克丝_\(荷兰\).md "wikilink") – declined due to her nation's observance of [Queen's Day](../Page/國王日.md "wikilink"), which is the day after.\[299\]

  - [The King of the Belgians](../Page/阿尔贝二世_\(比利时\).md "wikilink") and [Queen Paola](../Page/保拉_\(比利時王后\).md "wikilink") – because of the beatification ceremony for [Pope John Paul II](../Page/若望·保祿二世.md "wikilink")

  - [The King of Spain](../Page/胡安·卡洛斯一世.md "wikilink") – could not attend as he was recovering from surgery; was represented by [his wife](../Page/索菲亚_\(西班牙王后\).md "wikilink").\[300\]

  - [The King of Cambodia](../Page/诺罗敦·西哈莫尼.md "wikilink")\[301\]

  - [The Hereditary Prince of Liechtenstein](../Page/阿洛伊斯王储_\(列支敦士登\).md "wikilink") – because of the beatification ceremony for [Pope John Paul II](../Page/若望·保祿二世.md "wikilink").

  - [The Crown Prince of Bahrain](../Page/薩勒曼_\(巴林王儲\).md "wikilink") – declined due to the ensuing [protests](../Page/2011年巴林反政府示威.md "wikilink") in that country.\[302\]

  - [The Crown Prince](../Page/德仁親王.md "wikilink") and [Crown Princess of Japan](../Page/皇太子妃雅子.md "wikilink") – declined due to the [earthquake and tsunami](../Page/2011年日本東北地方太平洋近海地震.md "wikilink") that devastated their country.\[303\]

**副仙女魚科**（），又稱**準青眼魚科**，是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[仙女魚目](../Page/仙女魚目.md "wikilink")[合齒魚亞目的其中一](../Page/合齒魚亞目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

本科其下分1個屬：

### 副仙女魚屬(*Paraulopus*)

  - [白尾副仙女魚](../Page/白尾副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(*Paraulopus atripes*)
  - [黑邊副仙女魚](../Page/黑邊副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(''Paraulopus balteatus '')
  - [短吻副仙女魚](../Page/短吻副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(''Paraulopus brevirostris '')
  - [絲鰭副仙女魚](../Page/絲鰭副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(*Paraulopus filamentosus*)
  - [日本副仙女魚](../Page/日本副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(''Paraulopus japonicus '')
  - [萊氏副仙女魚](../Page/萊氏副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(''Paraulopus legandi '')
  - [長臀副仙女魚](../Page/長臀副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(''Paraulopus longianalis '')
  - [大斑副仙女魚](../Page/大斑副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(*Paraulopus maculatus*)
  - [黑紋副仙女魚](../Page/黑紋副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(*Paraulopus melanogrammus*)
  - [黑口副仙女魚](../Page/黑口副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(*Paraulopus melanostomus*)
  - [黑鰭副仙女魚](../Page/黑鰭副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(*Paraulopus nigripinnis*)
  - [新西蘭副仙女魚](../Page/新西蘭副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(''Paraulopus
    novaeseelandiae '')
  - [大鱗副仙女魚](../Page/大鱗副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(*Paraulopus oblongus*)
  - [岡村副仙女魚](../Page/岡村副仙女魚.md "wikilink")(''Paraulopus okamurai '')

**加比涅·穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)**（西班牙语：**Garbiñe Muguruza
Blanco**，），出生於[委内瑞拉](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/委内瑞拉 "wikilink")[首都](../Page/首都.md "wikilink")[卡拉卡斯](../Page/卡拉卡斯.md "wikilink")，是[西班牙職業女子](../Page/西班牙.md "wikilink")[網球](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/網球 "wikilink")[運動員](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/運動員 "wikilink")，她於[2014年法國網球公開賽擊敗時任世界第一](../Page/2014年法國網球公開賽.md "wikilink")[小威廉絲](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/小威廉絲 "wikilink")，打入八強而一戰成名，並且在[2016年法國網球公開賽闖入](../Page/2016年法國網球公開賽.md "wikilink")[決賽](../Page/2016年法國網球公開賽女子單打比賽.md "wikilink")，而再度擊敗[小威廉絲](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/小威廉絲 "wikilink")，奪得生涯首座大滿貫女單冠軍。[2017年溫布頓網球錦標賽](../Page/2017年溫布頓網球錦標賽.md "wikilink")[決賽](../Page/2017年溫布頓網球錦標賽女子單打比賽.md "wikilink")，梅古露莎擊敗賽會五屆冠軍[大威廉絲奪冠](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/大威廉絲 "wikilink")，成為21世紀以來，威廉絲姊妹以外第五位溫網女單冠軍，更是史上第一位在女單大滿貫，於決賽成功擊敗[大威廉絲與](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/大威廉絲 "wikilink")[小威廉絲兩人的女子網球員](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/小威廉絲 "wikilink")。梅古露莎在2017年9月11日的WTA排名登上[女子單打第一位](../Page/WTA單打與雙打世界第一列表.md "wikilink")，成為史上第24位世界球-{后}-。

## 個人生活

穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)的父親José Antonio是西班牙人，母親Scarlet Blanco是委內瑞拉人。

## 職業生涯

### 2012

2012年索尼愛立信公開賽，穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)收到外卡得以參賽，第二輪直落兩盤擊敗曾排名前世界第二的[薇拉·茲沃納列娃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/薇拉·茲沃納列娃 "wikilink")，第三輪擊敗[弗拉維婭·佩內塔](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/弗拉維婭·佩內塔 "wikilink")，第四輪敗給最終奪冠的[阿格涅什卡·拉德萬斯卡](../Page/阿格涅什卡·拉德萬斯卡.md "wikilink")。

### 2013

2013年巴黎銀行公開賽，穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)從資格賽打進到會內賽的第四輪，才輸給[安赫利奎·克柏](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/安赫利奎·克柏 "wikilink")，接著又收到2013年索尼愛立信公開賽的外卡，第三輪擊敗前界第一[卡羅琳·沃茲尼亞奇](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/卡羅琳·沃茲尼亞奇 "wikilink")，第四輪敗給世界第二[李娜](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/李娜_\(網球運動員\) "wikilink")。

### 2014：突破

穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)在澳網前的熱身賽，荷巴特國際賽闖入決賽，以直落二盤擊敗[克拉拉·扎科帕洛娃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/克拉拉·扎科帕洛娃 "wikilink")，獲得職業生涯第一個WTA級別冠軍\[1\]。

[2014年澳洲網球公開賽](../Page/2014年澳洲網球公開賽.md "wikilink")，穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)在[第三輪再次擊敗前界第一](../Page/2014年澳洲網球公開賽女子單打比賽.md "wikilink")[卡羅琳·沃茲尼亞奇](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/卡羅琳·沃茲尼亞奇 "wikilink")，但在第四輪再次敗給[阿格涅什卡·拉德萬斯卡](../Page/阿格涅什卡·拉德萬斯卡.md "wikilink")。

澳網後，穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)參加[巴西](../Page/巴西.md "wikilink")[弗洛里亞諾波利斯舉行的巴西網球盃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/弗洛里亞諾波利斯 "wikilink")（Brasil
Tennis
Cup），她闖入決賽，但取得一盤領先的情況下，被對手[克拉拉·扎科帕洛娃連得兩盤](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/克拉拉·扎科帕洛娃 "wikilink")，獲得亞軍。

[2014年法國網球公開賽](../Page/2014年法國網球公開賽.md "wikilink")，穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)在[第二輪爆冷擊敗上屆冠軍](../Page/2014年法國網球公開賽女子單打比賽.md "wikilink")[沙蓮娜·威廉絲](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/沙蓮娜·威廉絲 "wikilink")\[2\]，接著一路打進八強，敗給最終奪冠的[瑪麗亞·莎拉波娃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/瑪麗亞·莎拉波娃 "wikilink")，但已創下大滿貫最佳成績。

### 2015

[Muguruza_RG15_(24)_(19311051291).jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Muguruza_RG15_\(24\)_\(19311051291\).jpg "fig:Muguruza_RG15_(24)_(19311051291).jpg")
quarterfinal at the [2015 French
Open](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_French_Open "wikilink").\]\]
Muguruza's first event of the year was to be the [Brisbane
International](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Brisbane_International "wikilink")
but she was forced to withdraw from the event due to an ankle
injury.\[3\] The following week, Muguruza chose not to defend her title
at the [Hobart
International](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Hobart_International "wikilink")
and competed at the [Sydney
International](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Apia_International_Sydney "wikilink")
instead, where she reached the quarterfinals, defeating [Agnieszka
Radwańska](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agnieszka_Radwańska "wikilink")
for the first time in her career en route.\[4\] At the [Australian
Open](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Australian_Open "wikilink"),
she progressed to the fourth round for the second consecutive year with
wins over [Marina
Erakovic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marina_Erakovic "wikilink"),
[Daniela
Hantuchová](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniela_Hantuchová "wikilink")
and [Timea
Bacsinszky](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timea_Bacsinszky "wikilink")
before falling to the eventual champion, [Serena
Williams](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serena_Williams "wikilink") in
three sets.

Muguruza won both of her singles rubbers in the [2015 Fed Cup World
Group
II](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Fed_Cup_World_Group_II "wikilink"),
defeating [Irina-Camelia
Begu](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Irina-Camelia_Begu "wikilink") and
world No. 3 [Simona
Halep](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simona_Halep "wikilink") but Spain
lost the tie 3–2 after Muguruza and her compatriot, [Anabel Medina
Garrigues](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anabel_Medina_Garrigues "wikilink")
lost the deciding doubles rubber. At the [Dubai Tennis
Championships](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Dubai_Tennis_Championships "wikilink"),
Muguruza reached her first WTA Premier 5 semi-final in singles,
defeating qualifier [Jarmila
Gajdošová](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jarmila_Gajdošová "wikilink"),
twelfth seed [Jelena
Janković](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jelena_Janković "wikilink"),
fifth seed [Agnieszka
Radwańska](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agnieszka_Radwańska "wikilink")
and thirteenth seed and doubles partner [Carla Suárez
Navarro](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carla_Suárez_Navarro "wikilink")
before falling to the seventeenth seed and eventual runner-up, [Karolína
Plíšková](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karolína_Plíšková "wikilink") in
three sets. She also reached the final of the
[doubles](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Dubai_Tennis_Championships_–_Women's_Doubles "wikilink")
with Suárez Navarro. The following week, Muguruza retired from her first
round match against Suárez Navarro at the [Qatar
Open](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Qatar_Total_Open "wikilink")
while trailing 6–5 in the first set.

In March, Muguruza lost to Ka. Plíšková in the third round of the
[Indian Wells
Masters](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_BNP_Paribas_Open "wikilink")
after a second round win over American wildcard [Irina
Falconi](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Irina_Falconi "wikilink").
Despite this, she rose to a career high singles ranking of world No. 19
after the event. A fortnight later, Muguruza reached the third round of
the [Miami
Open](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Miami_Open "wikilink") after
defeating qualifier [Sesil
Karatantcheva](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sesil_Karatantcheva "wikilink")
but lost to the eleventh seed [Sara
Errani](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sara_Errani "wikilink") after
winning the first set.

Muguruza reached the second round of the three clay court events that
she competed in leading into the [French
Open](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_French_Open "wikilink"). She
lost to second seed Simona Halep in
[Stuttgart](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Porsche_Tennis_Grand_Prix "wikilink"),
[Kristina
Mladenovic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kristina_Mladenovic "wikilink")
in
[Marrakech](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Grand_Prix_SAR_La_Princesse_Lalla_Meryem "wikilink")
and the eventual finalist [Svetlana
Kuznetsova](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Svetlana_Kuznetsova "wikilink")
in
[Madrid](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Mutua_Madrid_Open "wikilink").
Muguruza and Suárez Navarro also reached the
[doubles](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Mutua_Madrid_Open_–_Women's_Doubles "wikilink")
final of the latter for the second consecutive year but the pair lost in
three sets to [Casey
Dellacqua](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Casey_Dellacqua "wikilink") and
[Yaroslava
Shvedova](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yaroslava_Shvedova "wikilink").
Despite failing to win back-to-back matches since February, Muguruza
reached her second consecutive French Open quarterfinal, defeating
[Angelique
Kerber](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angelique_Kerber "wikilink")\[5\]
and [Flavia
Pennetta](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flavia_Pennetta "wikilink")\[6\]
en route, before losing to the eventual runner-up, [Lucie
Šafářová](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lucie_Šafářová "wikilink").\[7\]

Muguruza made a poor start to the [grass
court](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/grass_court "wikilink") season,
losing in the first round of the [Birmingham
Classic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Aegon_Classic "wikilink")
and the third round of the [Eastbourne
International](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Aegon_International "wikilink")
after a first round bye. However, she and Suárez Navarro won their first
grass court
[doubles](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Aegon_International_–_Doubles "wikilink")
title in Birmingham, defeating [Andrea
Hlaváčková](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andrea_Hlaváčková "wikilink")
and [Lucie
Hradecká](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lucie_Hradecká "wikilink") in
straight sets in the final. At the [Wimbledon
Championships](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Wimbledon_Championships "wikilink"),
Muguruza defeated 10th seed Kerber in the third round,\[8\] 5th seed
[Caroline
Wozniacki](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caroline_Wozniacki "wikilink")
in the fourth round,\[9\] 15th seed [Timea
Bacsinszky](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timea_Bacsinszky "wikilink")
in the quarterfinals and 13th seed [Agnieszka
Radwańska](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agnieszka_Radwańska "wikilink")
in the semi-finals to advance to her maiden Grand Slam final. She played
against world No. 1 [Serena
Williams](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serena_Williams "wikilink") for
the title, but lost in straight sets.\[10\] After the tournament,
Muguruza entered the Top 10 for the first time, moving up to world No. 9
in the [WTA
Rankings](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/WTA_Rankings "wikilink").

During the [US Open
series](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_US_Open_Series "wikilink"),
Muguruza did not enjoy much success, dropping her opening matches in
[Toronto](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Rogers_Cup "wikilink") and
[Cincinnati](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Western_&_Southern_Open "wikilink")
to qualifiers [Lesia
Tsurenko](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lesia_Tsurenko "wikilink") and
[Yaroslava
Shvedova](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yaroslava_Shvedova "wikilink")
respectively. At the [US
Open](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_US_Open_\(tennis\) "wikilink"),
she recorded her first win at the tournament by defeating German [Carina
Witthöft](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carina_Witthöft "wikilink") in
the first round. However, she fell short to [Johanna
Konta](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johanna_Konta "wikilink") in the
following round in three sets, the match was a total time of 3 hours and
23 minutes, the longest women's singles match in that tournament's
history since the introduction of tiebreaker in 1970.

Muguruza kicked off the Asian swing at the [Pan Pacific
Open](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Toray_Pan_Pacific_Open "wikilink")
where she successfully advanced to the last eight after defeating
[Barbora
Strýcová](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barbora_Strýcová "wikilink")
before losing to eventual finalist [Belinda
Bencic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belinda_Bencic "wikilink") in
straight sets. At the [Wuhan
Open](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Wuhan_Open "wikilink"),
Muguruza successfully cruised into her second straight quarterfinal at
the event after dispatching [Sloane
Stephens](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sloane_Stephens "wikilink") and
[Ana Ivanovic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ana_Ivanovic "wikilink").
She then went on to defeat the likes of [Anna Karolina
Schmiedlova](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anna_Karolina_Schmiedlova "wikilink")
and Kerber to reach the final where she faced [Venus
Williams](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Venus_Williams "wikilink") but
was forced to retire in the second set with an ankle injury. Muguruza
was able to crack the Top 5 for the first time in her career after the
tournament's conclusion.\[11\] At the [China
Open](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_China_Open_\(tennis\) "wikilink"),
Muguruza breezed by [Irina
Falconi](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Irina_Falconi "wikilink") before
going the distance against [Mirjana
Lučić-Baroni](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mirjana_Lučić-Baroni "wikilink")
to advance to the quarterfinals. With the win, Muguruza qualified for
the [WTA
Finals](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_WTA_Finals "wikilink") for
the first time. She will be the first Spaniard to compete at the
year-end championships since [Arantxa Sánchez
Vicario](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arantxa_Sánchez_Vicario "wikilink")
in 2001. She then beat [Bethanie
Mattek-Sands](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bethanie_Mattek-Sands "wikilink")
and Radwańska to advance to her maiden Premier Mandatory final. With her
semifinal win over Radwańska, Muguruza cracked the Top 4 rankings where
she reached a career high of world No. 4. She went on to defeat
Bacsinszky in straight sets to claim her first Premier Mandatory title
and her biggest title of her career.

Muguruza then traveled to Singapore for the [WTA
Finals](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_WTA_Finals "wikilink"), where
she went undefeated in the round robin play; beating [Petra
Kvitová](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petra_Kvitová "wikilink"),
[Angelique
Kerber](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angelique_Kerber "wikilink"), and
[Lucie
Šafářová](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lucie_Šafářová "wikilink").
She then lost to eventual champion [Agnieszka
Radwańska](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agnieszka_Radwańska "wikilink")
in the semifinals. Muguruza also competed in doubles in Singapore, where
she and her compatriot [Carla Suárez
Navarro](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carla_Suárez_Navarro "wikilink")
reached the final. However, they lost to the number one seeds [Martina
Hingis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Martina_Hingis "wikilink") and
[Sania Mirza](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sania_Mirza "wikilink").

### 2016

[Muguruza_US16_(6)_(29827653006).jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Muguruza_US16_\(6\)_\(29827653006\).jpg "fig:Muguruza_US16_(6)_(29827653006).jpg")\]\]
穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)在2015年后半年迎来井喷式的状态爆发后，2016年年初陷入了状态低迷，布里斯班站退赛后她在澳网第三轮输给了捷克人斯特里科娃，随后她在迪拜和多哈两站公开赛中接连负于斯维托丽娜和佩特科维奇。来到强制赛印第安维尔斯，她首轮轮空后第二轮就输给了本土选手麦克哈尔，迈阿密站第二轮苦战击败齐布尔科娃之后在第四轮负于北美背靠背夺冠的阿扎伦卡。

进入红土赛季，穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)的状态有所恢复，在斯图加特站打进8强负于科维托娃，马德里站第二轮负于罗马尼亚人贝古，罗马站打入本年度首个半决赛，直落两盘负于美国人凯斯。进入法网，首轮和赛季连败的施米德洛娃陷入苦战，最终三盘淘汰对手，之后穆古魯薩(瑪格瑞莎)状态神勇，接连战胜库兹涅佐娃，斯托瑟等强手，决赛直落两盘击败连续四天作战的头号种子小威廉姆斯，取得对对手法网两连胜的同时也获得了职业生涯首个单打大满贯冠军。

### 2017

Muguruza started the season at the [Brisbane
International](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2017_Brisbane_International "wikilink"),
being the no. 4 seed. She made a run to the quarterfinals after
three-set wins over [Sam
Stosur](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam_Stosur "wikilink") and [Daria
Kasatkina](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daria_Kasatkina "wikilink"),
and a straight sets win over [Svetlana
Kuznetsova](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Svetlana_Kuznetsova "wikilink").
She then retired in the first set of her semifinal match against [Alizé
Cornet](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alizé_Cornet "wikilink") due to
injury. At the [Australian
Open](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2017_Australian_Open "wikilink"),
Muguruza beat [Marina
Erakovic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marina_Erakovic "wikilink"),
[Samantha
Crawford](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samantha_Crawford "wikilink"),
Anastasija Sevastova and [Sorana
Cîrstea](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sorana_Cîrstea "wikilink") en
route to her first ever quarterfinal at the tournament. She then lost to
[Coco
Vandeweghe](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coco_Vandeweghe "wikilink") in
straight sets.

Muguruza then played the Fed Cup quarter-finals, where she beat [Barbora
Strycova](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barbora_Strycova "wikilink") in
three sets before losing to [Karolina
Pliskova](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karolina_Pliskova "wikilink").
Spain ended up losing the tie 2-3. During the Gulf Swing, she beat
[Cagla
Buyukakcay](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cagla_Buyukakcay "wikilink")
in
[Doha](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2017_Qatar_Total_Open "wikilink"),
before succumbing to [Zhang
Shuai](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhang_Shuai_\(tennis\) "wikilink")
in three sets. She then retired in her first match at
[Dubai](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2017_Dubai_Tennis_Championships "wikilink")
against [Kateryna
Bondarenko](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kateryna_Bondarenko "wikilink"),
due to injury. At [Indian
Wells](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2017_BNP_Paribas_Open "wikilink"),
Muguruza advanced to the quarterfinals, beating [Kirsten
Flipkens](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kirsten_Flipkens "wikilink"),
17-year-old [Kayla
Day](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kayla_Day "wikilink"), and [Elina
Svitolina](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elina_Svitolina "wikilink") en
route. She then lost to world no.3 [Karolina
Pliskova](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karolina_Pliskova "wikilink") in
two tiebreaks.

**北美联盟**（**North American
Union**，**NAU**）是一个理论上的北美经济联盟，在适当程度上也有可能发展成为美国，加拿大，墨西哥三个政治上的松散联盟。其理念效仿欧盟，有传闻一种被称为「[北美共同貨幣](../Page/Amero.md "wikilink")」或北美元的新货币将在未来代替现今流通的[美元](../Page/美元.md "wikilink")、[加元和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/加元 "wikilink")[墨西哥比索](../Page/墨西哥比索.md "wikilink")。

## 外部連結

  - [Annexation.ca](http://www.annexation.ca) is a political discussion
    forum dedicated to the exploration of the potentialities for a
    democratic annexation of Canada to the USA.
  - American University's [Center for North American Studies
    (CNAS)](http://www.american.edu/ia/cnas/index.cfm) educates and
    promotes policy debates between governments on the North American
    Region.
  - Arizona State University's [North American Center for Transborder
    Studies
    (NACTS)](https://web.archive.org/web/20090220085059/http://nacts.asu.edu/)
    is a center for scholars regarding the trilateral issues in North
    America.
  - [H. Con.
    Res. 40](http://www.govtrack.us/congress/bill.xpd?bill=hc110-40):
    Expressing the sense of Congress that the United States should not
    engage in the construction of a North American Free Trade Agreement
    (NAFTA) Superhighway System or enter into a North American Union
    with Mexico and Canada.
  - [The North American Union
    Farce](http://www.globalresearch.ca/index.php?context=viewArticle&articleId=8247)
    by Laura Carlsen, March 3, 2008.
  - SPP in the
    [U.S.](https://web.archive.org/web/20090204083302/http://spp.gov/),
    [Canada](https://web.archive.org/web/20081220122409/http://www.spp-psp.gc.ca/menu-en.aspx)
    and [Mexico](http://www.sre.gob.mx/eventos/aspan/).
  - [*The Trojan
    Horse*](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Trojan_Horse_\(miniseries\) "wikilink"),
    ([Official website](http://www.cbc.ca/trojanhorse/index.html), [IMDb
    profile](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0883102/)). On 30 March 2008,
    [CBC
    Television](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/CBC_Television "wikilink")
    aired this two-part [science
    fiction](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/science_fiction "wikilink")
    [miniseries](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/miniseries "wikilink")
    portraying the merger of Canada and the United States.
  - [United North
    America](https://web.archive.org/web/20080509104819/http://www.unitednorthamerica.org/)
    is a non-profit organization that advocates the admittance of
    Canadian provinces into the United States as new states of the
    Union, however rejects the idea of Mexico being part of the union.

**十八般兵器**的说法来自**十八般武艺**，最早见于[南宋](../Page/南宋.md "wikilink")[华岳编的](../Page/华岳.md "wikilink")[兵书](../Page/兵书.md "wikilink")《[翠微北征录](../Page/翠微北征录.md "wikilink")》，但是未有具体地列出各种类。中國兵器的种类远超18这一数量，因此十八般兵器乃列舉最具代表性的十八種兵器概括描述。

## 各种流传的说法

[今人復原唐代“寶刀”（橫刀）.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:今人復原唐代“寶刀”（橫刀）.jpg "fig:今人復原唐代“寶刀”（橫刀）.jpg")

  - [明朝](../Page/明朝.md "wikilink")[福建人](../Page/福建.md "wikilink")[謝肇淛编纂的](../Page/謝肇淛.md "wikilink")《[五杂俎](../Page/五杂俎.md "wikilink")》列出“十八般武艺”：“一[弓](../Page/弓.md "wikilink")、二[弩](../Page/弩.md "wikilink")、三[枪](../Page/枪.md "wikilink")、四[刀](../Page/刀.md "wikilink")、五[剑](../Page/剑.md "wikilink")、六[矛](../Page/矛.md "wikilink")、七[盾](../Page/盾.md "wikilink")、八[斧](../Page/斧.md "wikilink")、九[钺](../Page/钺.md "wikilink")、十[戟](../Page/戟.md "wikilink")、十一[鞭](../Page/鞭.md "wikilink")、十二[锏](../Page/锏.md "wikilink")、十三[挝](../Page/挝.md "wikilink")、十四[殳](../Page/殳_\(武器\).md "wikilink")、十五[叉](../Page/叉.md "wikilink")、十六[耙](../Page/耙.md "wikilink")、十七[绵绳套索](../Page/繩.md "wikilink")、十八白打\[1\]。”
  - 同样成书于[明朝的](../Page/明朝.md "wikilink")《[水浒传](../Page/水浒传.md "wikilink")》写到的十八般兵器：“[矛](../Page/矛.md "wikilink")[锤](../Page/锤_\(武器\).md "wikilink")[弓](../Page/弓.md "wikilink")[弩](../Page/弩.md "wikilink")[铳](../Page/铳.md "wikilink")，[鞭](../Page/鞭.md "wikilink")[锏](../Page/锏.md "wikilink")[剑](../Page/剑.md "wikilink")[链](../Page/链.md "wikilink")[挝](../Page/挝.md "wikilink")；[斧](../Page/斧.md "wikilink")[钺並](../Page/钺.md "wikilink")[戈](../Page/戈.md "wikilink")[戟](../Page/戟.md "wikilink")，[牌](../Page/牌.md "wikilink")[棒與](../Page/棍.md "wikilink")[枪](../Page/枪.md "wikilink")[杈](../Page/耙.md "wikilink")。”
  - 另说十八般兵器：“[刀](../Page/刀.md "wikilink")、[枪](../Page/枪.md "wikilink")、[剑](../Page/剑.md "wikilink")、[戟](../Page/戟.md "wikilink")、[棍](../Page/棍.md "wikilink")、[棒](../Page/棒.md "wikilink")、[槊](../Page/槊.md "wikilink")、[镋](../Page/镋.md "wikilink")、[斧](../Page/斧.md "wikilink")、[钺](../Page/钺.md "wikilink")、[铲](../Page/铲.md "wikilink")、[耙](../Page/耙.md "wikilink")、[鞭](../Page/鞭.md "wikilink")、[锏](../Page/锏.md "wikilink")、[锤](../Page/锤_\(武器\).md "wikilink")、[叉](../Page/叉.md "wikilink")、[戈](../Page/戈.md "wikilink")、[矛](../Page/矛.md "wikilink")”等十八种武术器械。
  - 还有一种说法十八般兵器包括“九长九短”：九长是[枪](../Page/枪.md "wikilink")、[戟](../Page/戟.md "wikilink")、[棍](../Page/棍.md "wikilink")、[钺](../Page/钺.md "wikilink")、[叉](../Page/叉.md "wikilink")、[钂](../Page/钂.md "wikilink")、[钩](../Page/钩.md "wikilink")、[槊](../Page/槊.md "wikilink")、[鏟](../Page/鏟.md "wikilink")；九短是[刀](../Page/刀.md "wikilink")、[剑](../Page/剑.md "wikilink")、[拐](../Page/拐.md "wikilink")、[斧](../Page/斧.md "wikilink")、[鞭](../Page/鞭.md "wikilink")、[锏](../Page/锏.md "wikilink")、[锤](../Page/锤_\(武器\).md "wikilink")、[杵](../Page/杵.md "wikilink")、[棒](../Page/棒.md "wikilink")。
  - 然而最常见的说法是十八般兵器：[刀](../Page/刀.md "wikilink")[枪](../Page/枪.md "wikilink")[剑](../Page/剑.md "wikilink")[戟](../Page/戟.md "wikilink")，[斧](../Page/斧.md "wikilink")[钺](../Page/钺.md "wikilink")[钩](../Page/钩.md "wikilink")[叉](../Page/叉.md "wikilink")，[镋](../Page/镋.md "wikilink")[棍](../Page/棍.md "wikilink")[槊](../Page/槊.md "wikilink")[棒](../Page/棒.md "wikilink")，[鞭](../Page/鞭.md "wikilink")[锏](../Page/锏.md "wikilink")[锤](../Page/锤_\(武器\).md "wikilink")[撾](../Page/撾.md "wikilink")，[拐子](../Page/拐.md "wikilink")[流星](../Page/流星錘.md "wikilink")。
  - 戲劇中所使用的十八般武藝：三尖兩刃刀、蛇矛、槊、棍、斧、钂、-{双}-頭槍、戟、叉、槍、刀、虎頭牌、錘、籐牌、腰刀、單刀、-{扑}-刀、杵、劍、鞭、鐧。

## 影响

在[日本](../Page/日本.md "wikilink")[江户时代](../Page/江户时代.md "wikilink")，受中国的“十八般兵器”或者“十八般武艺”的影响，日本有“”的说法，包括[剑道](../Page/剑道.md "wikilink")、[骑术](../Page/骑术.md "wikilink")、[游泳等](../Page/游泳.md "wikilink")，而“”一词更已被用于指代某人擅长的技能。

现代十八般兵器一词常被作为全套必备用品的代名词而使用，如“恋爱中的十八般兵器”、“办公自动化的十八般兵器”，等等。

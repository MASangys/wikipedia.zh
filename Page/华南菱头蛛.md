**华南菱头蛛**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）为[跳蛛科](../Page/跳蛛科.md "wikilink")[菱头蛛属的动物](../Page/菱头蛛属.md "wikilink")。分布于[越南](../Page/越南.md "wikilink")、[印度以及](../Page/印度.md "wikilink")[中国大陆的](../Page/中国大陆.md "wikilink")[陕西](../Page/陕西.md "wikilink")、[山东](../Page/山东.md "wikilink")、[河南](../Page/河南.md "wikilink")、[江苏](../Page/江苏.md "wikilink")、[安徽](../Page/安徽.md "wikilink")、[浙江](../Page/浙江.md "wikilink")、[江西](../Page/江西.md "wikilink")、[湖南](../Page/湖南.md "wikilink")、[广东](../Page/广东.md "wikilink")、[广西](../Page/广西.md "wikilink")、[四川](../Page/四川.md "wikilink")、[云南](../Page/云南.md "wikilink")、[贵州等地](../Page/贵州.md "wikilink")，常见于农田。\[1\]

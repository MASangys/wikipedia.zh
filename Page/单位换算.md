**单位换算**是指通过乘以**换算系数**实现不同[计量单位](../Page/计量单位.md "wikilink")间的等[量换算](../Page/量_\(物理\).md "wikilink")。

## 换算技术

### 过程

单位换算过程与用户具体情况和预期目的相关。可能受法令、[合同](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/合同 "wikilink")、[技术规格或其它出版物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/技术规格 "wikilink")[技术标准的限制](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/技术标准 "wikilink")。工程判断可能包括以下因素:

  - 计量[准确与精密度与相关测量的不准确度](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/准确与精密 "wikilink")
  - 初始量测统计[置信区间](../Page/置信区间.md "wikilink")或[容许区间](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/容许区间 "wikilink")
  - 计量[有效数字](../Page/有效数字.md "wikilink")位数
  - 包括工程容差计量的预期用途

某些从一个单位系统需要不增减精度地向另一个单位体系严密转换第一个量度。有时被称为“软转换”。它不涉及更改被计量的物理配置。

与之相比，“硬转换”或“适应转换”可能不完全相等。它更改计量以适合在新体系内的有效数字与单位。它有时涉及项目的稍微不同的配置或尺寸替代。有时允许并使用[标称值](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/标称值 "wikilink")。

### 换算系数

此条目内不列出在[公制单位](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/公制 "wikilink")[国际单位制词头](../Page/国际单位制词头.md "wikilink")间的转换（例如：1 千克 = 1000 克, 1 毫克 = 0.001 克）。除非单位还有其他常见名称。

Often, information entropy is measured in [shannons](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Claude_Shannon "wikilink"), whereas the (discrete) storage space of digital devices is measured in bits. | Name of unit                                                                              | Symbol | Definition     | Relation to SI units |
| ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------ | -------------- | -------------------- |

| [sievert](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/sievert "wikilink") (SI unit)                     | Sv     | ≡ 1 J/kg\[96\] | \= 1 Sv              |

Radiation - [equivalent dose](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/equivalent_dose "wikilink")

Although the definitions for sievert (Sv) and gray (Gy) would seem to indicate that they measure the same quantities, this is not the case. The effect of receiving a certain dose of radiation (given as Gy) is variable and depends on many factors, thus a new unit was needed to denote the biological effectiveness of that dose on the body; this is known as the equivalent dose and is shown in Sv. The general relationship between absorbed dose and equivalent dose can be represented as

  -
    *H = Q · D*

where *H* is the equivalent dose, *D* is the absorbed dose, and *Q* is a dimensionless quality factor. Thus, for any quantity of *D* measured in Gy, the numerical value for *H* measured in Sv may be different.\[97\]

## Software tools

Home and office computers come with converters in bundled spreadsheet applications or can access free converters via the Internet. Units and measurements can be easily converted using these tools, but only if the units are explicitly defined and the conversion is compatible (e.g., cmHg to kPa).

### General commercial sources of converters

  - [Advanced electronic calculators](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/calculator "wikilink") have unit-conversion functionality.
  - [Spreadsheet](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spreadsheet "wikilink") programs usually provide conversion [functions](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/function_\(computer_science\) "wikilink") or [formulas](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/formula "wikilink") or the user can write their own.
  - Commercial mathematical, scientific and technical applications often include converters.

## In fiction

When a measure is converted to another unit in a fiction work like a novel or film, the value often loses meaning (as in [numerology](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/numerology "wikilink")) or gets too many significant digits ("40 miles" turns into "64 km"). For this reason, authors sometimes choose numbers that can be converted easily. Most notably, distances measured in miles often begin with 3 or 5, so the value ghjcfugffyhfdfgjvcgconverted to km also has one significant digit (5 or 8).

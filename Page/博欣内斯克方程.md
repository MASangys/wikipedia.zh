**博欣内斯克方程**是一个二元非线性偏微分方程：\[1\]

\(\frac{\partial^2 u }{\partial t^2}-\frac{\partial^2 u }{\partial x^2 }-\frac{\partial^2 u^2 }{\partial^2 y^2 }+\frac{\partial^4 u }{\partial x^4}=0\)

博欣内斯克方程有如下行波解：\[2\]

\(u(x,t)= (3/2*(1-c^2))*sech((1/2)*\sqrt{(1-c^2)*(x-c*t))^2}\)

**印度扁卷螺属**（学名：）為一種會呼吸空氣的[淡水螺的一個](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/淡水螺 "wikilink")[屬](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/屬_\(生物\) "wikilink")，屬於[軟體動物門](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/軟體動物門 "wikilink")[腹足綱](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/腹足綱 "wikilink")[有肺類](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/有肺類 "wikilink")[扁蜷科](../Page/扁蜷科.md "wikilink")的动物。本屬的[唯一物種為](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/單型 "wikilink")**印度扁卷螺**（）。本物種廣泛分佈於熱帶地區，包括[印度](../Page/印度.md "wikilink")、[缅甸](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/缅甸 "wikilink")、[斯里兰卡](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/斯里兰卡 "wikilink")、[印度尼西亞](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/印度尼西亞 "wikilink")、[泰国](../Page/泰国.md "wikilink")、[马来西亚](../Page/马来西亚.md "wikilink")以及[中国大陆](../Page/中国大陆.md "wikilink")的[云南等地](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/云南 "wikilink")，主要生活于湖泊沿岸带、池塘以及沼泽地等淡水和微咸水域\[1\]。本物種是多種[寄生](../Page/寄生.md "wikilink")[吸虫的重要](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/吸虫 "wikilink")[中間宿主](../Page/中間宿主.md "wikilink")\[2\]。其[入侵性本質及環境的高容耐度使之成為醫學上及獸醫學上的重要研究課題](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/入侵物種 "wikilink")\[3\]。

## 分類

儘管本物種有着悠久的歷史和廣泛的地理分佈範圍，牠們一直都被認為是屬內唯一的物種\[4\]；而過往亦有文獻建議本屬跟其他關係較密切的屬合併。然而，Liu et al. (2010)根據[系統地理學研究](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/系統地理學 "wikilink")\[5\]，發現在系統發育方面，印度分支和東南亞分支之間有相當的分歧；加上棲息地和[寄生蟲學的差異](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/寄生蟲學 "wikilink")，表明這個物種實際可能由多於一個物種構成\[6\]。

從系統發育的角度看，與本屬關係最密切的是*[Bulinus](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulinus "wikilink")*屬\[7\]。

## 分佈

印度扁卷螺本身是一種淡水螺，在[中亞的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/中亞 "wikilink")[伊朗](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/伊朗 "wikilink")\[8\]與[阿富汗](../Page/阿富汗.md "wikilink")\[9\]，[南亞的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/南亞 "wikilink")[印度](../Page/印度.md "wikilink")、[尼泊爾](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/尼泊爾 "wikilink")\[10\]及[斯里蘭卡](../Page/斯里蘭卡.md "wikilink")\[11\]均見其踪影。在[東南亞](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/東南亞 "wikilink")，例如：[泰國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/泰國 "wikilink"))\[12\]，甚至遠至阿拉伯地區及非洲均見其踪影\[13\]。

The type locality of *Indoplanorbis exustus* is marshes on the coast of [Malabar](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malabar_\(Northern_Kerala\) "wikilink") in southwestern India.\[14\]

*Indoplanorbis exustus* is a common snail across Southeast Asia and the Indian sub-continent.\[15\] The snail is also found in the Middle East ([Oman](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oman "wikilink")\[16\] and [Socotra](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Socotra "wikilink")\[17\]) and [Nigeria](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_non-marine_molluscs_of_Nigeria "wikilink") and the Ivory Coast;\[18\] these findings were attributed by Brandt (1974)\[19\] to recent introductions by human activities (Brandt's view has been frequently cited in the literature on *Indoplanorbis*).\[20\]\[21\]\[22\]\[23\] In contrast to Asia, the well documented appearance of the snail in Africa (e.g., Nigeria\[24\] and Ivory Coast\[25\]) and more recently (2002) in the Lesser Antilles,\[26\] is almost certainly the result of introductions through human activities over the last 50–100 years.\[27\]

This species is already established in the USA, and is considered to represent a potentially serious threat as a [pest](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/pest_\(organism\) "wikilink"), an [invasive species](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/invasive_species "wikilink") which could negatively affect agriculture, natural ecosystems, human health or commerce. Therefore it has been suggested that this species be given top national [quarantine](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/quarantine "wikilink") significance in the USA.\[28\]

### 生物地理學

Meier-Brook (1984)\[29\] adopted an African ([Gondwanan](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gondwana "wikilink")) origin for *Indoplanorbis* with rafting to Asia since the [Cretaceous](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cretaceous "wikilink") on the northward migrating [Indian craton](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_craton "wikilink"); this author also considered a Europe to Southwest Asia tract or an Africa to South India dispersal.\[30\] Morgan et al. (2002)\[31\] attributed the occurrence of *Indoplanorbis* in India to colonization (from Africa) via the Middle East land connection.\[32\] Clearly the two different dispersal mechanisms imply very different chronologies; the Gondwanan vicariance hypothesis implies that proto-*Indoplanorbis* has been present in India since the late Eocene (35 Ma; India: Asia collision), whereas dispersal via the [Sinai-Levant](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sinai-Levant "wikilink") suggests a Plio-[Pleistocene](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pleistocene "wikilink") arrival.\[33\] The results by Liu et al. (2010) indicated a radiation beginning in the late Miocene with a divergence of an ancestral bulinine lineage into [Assam](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Assam "wikilink") and peninsular India clades.\[34\] A Southeast Asian clade diverged from the peninsular India clade late-Pliocene; this clade then radiated at a much more rapid pace to colonize all of the sampled range of *Indoplanorbis* in the mid-Pleistocene.\[35\]

## 簡述

與其他同屬[扁卷螺科的物種一樣](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/扁卷螺科 "wikilink")，其外殼都是[平旋壳](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/平旋壳 "wikilink")，但其實是螺把殼上下顛倒來揹在身上，使[左旋的外殼看起來像是右旋](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/左旋 "wikilink")。本物種的外殼呈圓盤形，所以\[大小的增長很快\[36\]。每個螺層的高比其闊增長得較快\[37\]：其闊約為 5\[38\]-25 mm\[39\]，其高約為 4.5\[40\]-13 mm\[41\]。

## 生態學

當印度扁卷螺被圈養時，可以以[莴苣](../Page/莴苣.md "wikilink")\[45\]及[菠菜](../Page/菠菜.md "wikilink")來圈養。有時候其膳食會輔以[羊肝](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/羊肝 "wikilink")，在繁殖時可提供一般寵物鼠的食糧。

### Habitat

The snail is found in small ponds, pools, and less commonly in [rice paddy fields](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/paddy_field "wikilink").\[46\] The snail may also occur in semi-permanent pools formed in flooded areas of fields, where it can survive the dry season buried in mud.\[47\] The [desiccation tolerance](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/desiccation_tolerance "wikilink") of adult snails is high, while the resistance of juvenile snails is very low.\[48\] Consequently, dispersal may occur in clumps of mud adhered to the bodies of cattle or across water in vegetation mats. It is possible it could be transported by birds.\[49\]

### Life cycle

*Indoplanorbis exustus* is a [hermaphroditic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/hermaphroditic "wikilink") [invasive](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Invasive_species "wikilink") snail species with high [fecundity](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/fecundity "wikilink").\[50\] Within one year of introduction the snail is able to colonize habitats with well established populations of other pulmonate and prosobranch snails.\[51\] The snail requires a water temperature in excess of 15 °C for maturation.\[52\] At the optimum temperature of 30 °C each snail can lay up to 800 eggs.\[53\] There is from 2 to 43 eggs in one cluster with an average 20 eggs in one cluster.\[54\] The capacity for self-fertilization and high fecundity probably underlies the invasive potential of the species.\[55\] The average life span of *Indoplanorbis exustus* is 4 months\[56\] and during this time it lays about 60 egg clusters.\[57\]

### 捕食者

現時本物種並沒有已知的天敵，不過有文獻透過實驗去測試一些物種會否進食印度扁卷螺的卵。Raut & Aditya (2002)在實驗裡讓一種屬於[南美洲](../Page/南美洲.md "wikilink")[福壽螺科的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/福壽螺科 "wikilink")*[Pomacea bridgesii](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pomacea_bridgesii "wikilink")*去攻擊和進食印度扁卷螺的卵，以期望未來可以讓這種福壽螺來控制印度扁卷螺的數量\[58\]。

### 寄生蟲

*Indoplanorbis* is of economic importance in that it is responsible for the transmission of several species of the genus *[Schistosoma](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schistosoma "wikilink")* which infect cattle and cause reduced [livestock](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/livestock "wikilink") productivity.\[59\] The snail is also of medical importance as a source of [cercarial dermatitis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/cercarial_dermatitis "wikilink") among rural workers, particularly in India.\[60\]

*Indoplanorbis exustus* is best known as the intermediate host responsible for the [transmission](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transmission_\(medicine\) "wikilink") of *[Schistosoma nasale](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schistosoma_nasale "wikilink")* and *[S. spindale](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schistosoma_spindale "wikilink")*, as well as other trematodes such as *[Echinostoma](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Echinostoma "wikilink")* spp. and some [spirorchids](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/spirorchiidae "wikilink").\[61\] A third species of *Schistosoma*, *[S. indicum](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schistosoma_indicum "wikilink")* (Montgomery, 1906), is also transmitted by *I. exustus*.\[62\] Other snails have been implicated in transmission of these three *Schistosoma* species (such as *[Lymnaea luteola](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lymnaea_luteola "wikilink")*, a host for *S. indicum* and *S. nasale*, and *[L. acuminata](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lymnaea_acuminata "wikilink")*, a host for *S. nasale* and *S. spindale*), *I. exustus* is the most important host for *S. nasale* and *S. spindale*, as well as for *S. indicum* in certain regions. *I. exustus* may be the sole natural intermediate host for these three *Schistosoma* species on the Indian sub-continent.\[63\]

*Indoplanorbis exustus* is also an intermediate host for:

  - *[Artyfechinostomum malayanum](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artyfechinostomum_malayanum "wikilink")* - as the first intermediate host\[64\]
  - [似锥低颈吸虫](../Page/似锥低颈吸虫.md "wikilink") *[Hypoderaeum conoideum](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hypoderaeum_conoideum "wikilink")* - as the first intermediate host\[65\]
  - [巨片吸虫](../Page/巨片吸虫.md "wikilink") *[Fasciola gigantica](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fasciola_gigantica "wikilink")*\[66\]
  - *[Paramphistomum mehrai](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paramphistomum_mehrai "wikilink")*\[67\]
  - *[Paramphistomum explanatum](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paramphistomum_explanatum "wikilink")*\[68\]
  - *[Gastrodiscus secundus](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gastrodiscus_secundus "wikilink")*\[69\]
  - *[Petagifer srivastavi](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Petagifer_srivastavi "wikilink")*\[70\]
  - *[Plasmiorchis orientalis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plasmiorchis_orientalis "wikilink")*\[71\]
  - *[Pseudodiscus collinsi](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pseudodiscus_collinsi "wikilink")*\[72\]
  - *[Gastrothylax crumenifer](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gastrothylax_crumenifer "wikilink")*\[73\]
  - *[Enterohaemotrema paleorticum](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Enterohaemotrema_paleorticum "wikilink")*\[74\]
  - *[Cotylophoron cotylophorum](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cotylophoron_cotylophorum "wikilink")*\[75\]
  - *[Cotylophoron indica](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cotylophoron_indica "wikilink")*\[76\]
  - *[Cotylophoron bhaleraoi](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cotylophoron_bhaleraoi "wikilink")*\[77\]
  - *[Cotylophoron mathurapurensis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cotylophoron_mathurapurensis "wikilink")*\[78\]

*Indoplanorbis exustus* has been implicated in outbeaks of [cercarial dermatitis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/cercarial_dermatitis "wikilink") in human populations in India, Laos, Malaysia and Thailand.\[79\] Cercarial dermatitis results from the cutaneous [allergic reaction](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/allergic_reaction "wikilink") in people exposed to larval schistosomes ([cercariae](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/cercariae "wikilink")) shed by infected snails into freshwater bodies such as lakes, ponds, and paddy fields.\[80\] The cercariae cause [pruritis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/pruritis "wikilink") and papular eruptions, with often severe secondary infections, as they attempt to infect a non-permissive definitive host and die in the skin.\[81\]

### 毒理學

一種在[印度](../Page/印度.md "wikilink")生長的[大戟科](../Page/大戟科.md "wikilink")植物[紅雀珊瑚](../Page/紅雀珊瑚.md "wikilink")（）可用作植物性[殺螺劑](../Page/殺螺劑.md "wikilink")，有助對抗印度扁卷螺\[82\]。

[黄果茄](../Page/黄果茄.md "wikilink")的酒精提取物亦可對本物種（[LC<sub>50</sub>](../Page/半數致死量.md "wikilink") = 198.00 mg/L and LC<sub>90</sub> = 236.80 mg/L）\[83\]。

此外，另一種大戟科物種[虎刺梅](../Page/虎刺梅.md "wikilink")（*[Euphorbia milii](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euphorbia_milii "wikilink")*），其雜交物種株的乳汁對於本物種的活躍度有所抑制，也可作殺螺劑使用\[84\]。除了虎刺梅的雜交種，印度的科學家Singh & Sunil在2005年發表的文獻亦研究過[黃花夾竹桃](../Page/黃花夾竹桃.md "wikilink")（*[Cascabela thevetia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cascabela_thevetia "wikilink")*）、[黑板樹](../Page/黑板樹.md "wikilink")（*[Alstonia scholaris](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alstonia_scholaris "wikilink")*）兩種[夾竹桃科](../Page/夾竹桃科.md "wikilink")植物及另一種較常見的大戟科植物[一品紅](../Page/一品紅.md "wikilink")（*[Euphorbia pulcherrima](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euphorbia_pulcherrima "wikilink")*）的乳汁對印度扁卷螺的抑制效果\[85\]。

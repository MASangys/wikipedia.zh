**双重哈恩多项式**(Dual Hahn polynomials)是一个正交多项式，定义如下\[1\]

\[R_n(\lambda(x);\gamma,\delta,N)= {}_3F_2(-n,-x,x+\gamma+\delta+1;\gamma+1,-N;1).\]
其中0≤*n*≤*N*

## 正交性

双重哈恩多项式满足下列正交关系：\[2\]

\(\sum_{x=0}^{N} \frac{(2x+\gamma+\delta+1)(\gamma+1)_{x}(-N)_{x}N!}{  (-1)^x(x+\gamma+\delta+1)_{N+1}(\delta+1)_{x}x!}\)\*
\(R_{m}(\lambda(x);\gamma,\delta,N)R_{n}(\lambda(x);\gamma,\delta,N)=\frac{\delta_{mn}}{{\gamma+n \choose n}{\delta+N-n \choose  N-n}}\)

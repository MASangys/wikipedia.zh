**叙马库斯**（），（）。\[1\][古罗马](../Page/古罗马.md "wikilink")[政治家](../Page/政治家.md "wikilink")、[演说家以及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/演说家 "wikilink")[文学家](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/文学家 "wikilink")。出生于[贵族家庭](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/贵族 "wikilink")，后参加[教会](../Page/教会.md "wikilink")事务，并开始进行[文学创作](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/文学 "wikilink")，以此获得了巨额财富。不久他因为卷入[罗马帝国](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/罗马帝国 "wikilink")[政治](../Page/政治.md "wikilink")纷争而死于非命，他的作品传世至今，反映了公元4[世纪中期至公元](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/世纪 "wikilink")5世纪初期混乱的[欧洲](../Page/欧洲.md "wikilink")状况。

## 扩展阅读

  - *Q. Aurelii Symmachi quae supersunt*, ed. by [Otto Seeck](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Otto_Seeck "wikilink") (Berlin, 1883; reprinted Munich, 2001), ISBN 3-921575-19-2. All surviving writings of Symmachus: letters, speeches and official reports, in the original Latin. This volume is Volume 6 of the series [Monumenta Germaniae Historica](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monumenta_Germaniae_Historica "wikilink"). The letters are also published in a supplementary volume XIII in the [Patrologia Latina](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patrologia_Latina "wikilink"). More recently, *Symmaque: Lettres*, ed. by [Jean-Pierre Callu](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jean-Pierre_Callu "wikilink") in four volumes (Paris, 1972–2002) published by [Les Belles Lettres](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Budé "wikilink") contains the letters of Symmachus in Latin with facing-page French translation. This has the fullest text and translation.
  - Richard Klein: *Symmachus. Eine tragische Gestalt des ausgehenden Heidentums*. Darmstadt (Wissenschaftliche Buchgesellschaft \[Impulse der Forschung, Band 2\]) 1971, ISBN 3-534-04928-4.
  - Richard Klein: *Der Streit um den Victoriaaltar*. Darmstadt (WBG \[Texte zur Forschung Band 7\]) 1972, ISBN 3-534-05169-6.
  - J.F. Matthews, "The Letters of Symmachus" in *Latin Literature of the Fourth Century* (edited by J.W. Binns), pp. 58–99. London: Routledge and Kegan Paul, 1974, discusses them.
  - J.F. Matthews, *Western Aristocracies and Imperial Court, AD 364-425*. Oxford: Clarendon Press, 1990. ISBN 0-19-814499-7
  - Cristiana Sogno, *Q. Aurelius Symmachus: A Political Biography*. Ann Arbor: University of Michigan Press, 2006. ISBN 978-0-472-11529-7

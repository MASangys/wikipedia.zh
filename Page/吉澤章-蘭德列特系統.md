**吉澤章-蘭德列特系統**（）是一種[摺紙步驟圖](../Page/摺紙步驟圖.md "wikilink")表示系統。許多摺紙書籍的[摺紙步驟圖](../Page/摺紙步驟圖.md "wikilink")都是使用這個系統來描述[摺紙](../Page/摺紙.md "wikilink")模型的基本摺紙技術。也有一些是基本動作，它通常用在[摺紙](../Page/摺紙.md "wikilink")模型的第一步驟。

## 歷史

這種[摺紙步驟圖](../Page/摺紙步驟圖.md "wikilink")表示方法的概念最早起源於1797年出版的書“Senbazuru
Orikata”，這是最早的摺紙書。在這本書中的[摺紙步驟圖](../Page/摺紙步驟圖.md "wikilink")很清楚，且常僅表現在折疊過程的最終結果，留下的摺痕不能確定模型是如何被創建的。後來書就開始制定出一個模型，正是如何折疊的系統。

## 參考文獻

  -
  - [Robert J.
    Lang](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_J._Lang "wikilink"). *The
    Complete Book of Origami: step-by-step instructions in over 1000
    diagrams*. Dover Publications, Mineola, NY. Copyright 1988 by Robert
    J. Lang. ISBN 0-486-25837-8 (pbk.)

      -
        Pages 1–30 are an excellent introduction to most of these
        skills. Each of these 13 models is designed to let you practice
        one skill several times. Unfortunately, the remaining 24 models
        leave out lots of pre-creases.

  - [Peter Engel](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Engel "wikilink").
    *Origami from Angelfish to Zen*. Dover Publications, Mineola, NY.
    Copyright 1989 by Peter Engel. ISBN 0-486-28138-8 (pbk.)

      -
        Pages 1–88 explain how the crease patterns of the classic bases
        are related to each other, how they are examples of fractal
        design, and how it is possible to generate arbitrarily complex
        origami designs.

  - [Robert J.
    Lang](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_J._Lang "wikilink").
    *Origami Design Secrets: mathematical methods for an ancient art*. A
    K Peters, Natick, MA. Copyright 2003 by A K Peters. ISBN
    1-56881-194-2 (pbk)

      -
        This book shows how crease patterns and folding techniques are
        related to the large-scale structure of a model. It also shows
        how closed-unsinking was derived, using Lang's *TreeMaker*
        software for prototyping arbitrarily complex origami designs.

  - [Tomoko Fuse](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tomoko_Fuse "wikilink").
    *Unit Origami: multidimensional transformations*. Japan
    Publications, inc. Tokyo. Copyright 1990 by Tomoko Fuse. ISBN
    0-87040-852-6

      -
        This is an excellent book about [modular
        origami](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/modular_origami "wikilink"),
        and serves as a great introduction to geometric models and
        designs.

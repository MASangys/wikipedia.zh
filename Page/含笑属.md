**含笑属**（[学名](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/学名 "wikilink")：*Michelia*）是[木兰科](../Page/木兰科.md "wikilink")下的一个属，为[灌木](../Page/灌木.md "wikilink")或[乔木植物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/乔木 "wikilink")。该属共有约50余种，分布于[亚洲](../Page/亚洲.md "wikilink")[热带](../Page/热带.md "wikilink")、[亚热带](../Page/亚热带.md "wikilink")及[温带](../Page/温带.md "wikilink")。\[1\]

## 物种

  - [白蘭](../Page/白蘭.md "wikilink") *Michelia × alba* DC. ([syn.](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/异名 "wikilink") *M. longifolia* Blume). **White Champaca**或**White Sandalwood**是由黄玉兰（*Michelia champaca* L.）和山含笑（*Michelia montana* Blume）自然杂交得到
  - *Michelia angustioblonga* Y.-W. Law & Y.-F. Wu
  - *Michelia balansae* (A. DC.) Dandy (基本异名：*Magnolia balansae* A. DC.)

  - *Michelia cavaleriei* Finet & Gagnep.

  - [黄玉兰](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/黄玉兰 "wikilink") *Michelia champaca* L. **Champak**. 原产地为印度、[爪哇岛](../Page/爪哇岛.md "wikilink")和菲律宾，小乔木或灌木，高及蓬径3－6米，叶革质，翠绿色，达16厘米长；花芬芳，橙黄色或乳白色，春季开花，可提取精油用於制作香水
  - *Michelia chapensis* Dandy
  - *Michelia compressa* (Maxim.) Sarg. (基本异名：*Magnolia compressa* Maxim.)
  - *Michelia coriacea* Chang & Chen
  - *Michelia crassipes* Y.-W. Law
  - [南亞含笑](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/南亞含笑 "wikilink") *Michelia doltsopa* Buch.-Ham. ex DC. Large shrubs and trees, growing to 30 meters. Native to the eastern [Himalayas](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Himalayas "wikilink") and [Meghalaya subtropical forests](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Meghalaya_subtropical_forests "wikilink"). Varies in form from bushy to narrow and upright. Leathery dark-green leaves, 6 to 17 cm in length. Clusters of creamy white flowers in winter. Growing in popularity as a street tree in coastal [California](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/California "wikilink").
  - *Michelia elegans* Y.-W. Law & Y.-F. Wu (视为*Michelia cavaleriei* Finet & Gagnep. by Chen & Nooteboom (1993)\[5\]的异名)
  - *Michelia elliptilimba* Chen & Noot. (视为*Michelia sphaerantha* C.Y. Wu ex Z.S. Yue by J. Li (1997)\[6\]的异名)
  - [含笑花](../Page/含笑花.md "wikilink") *Michelia figo* (Lour.) Spreng. (基本异名：*Liriodendron figo* Lour.) **Banana Shrub**. A slow growing shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters high and nearly as wide. Densely covered with small glossy green leaves. Bears clusters of large, white, sometimes purple-streaked, flowers with a potent, sweet banana scent. **Port Wine** is a variety that bears rose to maroon flowers.

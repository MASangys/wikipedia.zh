**周雲蓬 ZHOU YUNPENG**

民謠歌手、詩人、作家

1970年出生於遼寧瀋陽，9歲失明。1980年就讀於瀋陽盲童學校；1991年考入長春大學特教學院中文專業；1994年畢業，背著吉他去北京唱歌。
發行唱片：《沈默如迷的呼吸》（2004）、《中國孩子》（2007）、《牛羊下山》（2010）、《四月舊州》（2014）。
出版書籍：個人詩文集《春天責備》（2010）、雜文集《綠皮火車》（2011）、詩集《午夜起來聽寂靜》（2017）。
參與電影：2011年參演高群書導演電影《神探亨特張》，該片獲金馬獎最佳電影獎。2016年參演李睿珺導演電影《路過未來》，該片入圍2017年第70屆戛納國際電影節「一種關注」競賽單元。2017年為韓東導演電影《在碼頭》製作電影原聲音樂，該片參加2017年釜山電影節。
2008年被《南方人物週刊》評為「青年領袖」；曾獲得過華語傳媒音樂大獎「最佳民謠藝人」、獲得「最佳作詞人」；2011年因詩作《不會說話的愛情》獲《人民文學》年度「詩歌獎」。
2009年、2012年策劃民謠合輯《紅色推土機》、《金色推土機》，銷售所得用於幫助貧困盲童。

ZHOU YUNPENG

Folk singer, poet, writer.

Born in 1970, Shenyang, LIaoning, was blind at 9 years old.

Went to Shenyang Blind Children School in 1980, went to Special
Education Institute in Changchun University in 1991, major in Chinese
Language, went to Beijing began singing career in 1994.

His Albums: The Breath Silent as a secret(2004), Chinese Children(2007),
Cattle and Sheep Down From Mountain(2010), Old Town in April(2014). His
Books: Spring Blame(2010), Green Train(2011), Get up to Listen the
Silence in the Midnight(2017).

Films: In 2011, took part in film”Beijing Blues”(Director Gao Qunshu),
which was awarded as Best Film by Golden Horse Film Festival. In 2016,
took part in film “Walking Past the Future”(Director Li Ruijun), which
has been short-listed for “The Attention” Part in 70th Cannes Film
Festival(2017). In 2017, made the movie soundtrack of “One Night on the
Wharf”(Director Han Dong), which was shown in 2017 Busan Film Festival.

Awarded as “Leader of Young Men” by Southern Figures Week in 2008, “Best
Folk Singer” “Best Lyric Writer”by Chinese Media Music, his poem “The
Love that can not speak”was awarded as“Best Poem” by People Literature
in 2011.

Planned Folk albums “Red Bulldozer” (2009)and “Golden Bulldozer”(2012),
the income was used to help blind children.

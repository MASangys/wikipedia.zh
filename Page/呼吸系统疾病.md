**呼吸系统疾病** (Respiratory Diseases)。
是指局限于[呼吸系统的疾病](../Page/呼吸系统.md "wikilink")。从生理上分为两类：[阻塞性肺病和](../Page/阻塞性肺病.md "wikilink")[限制性肺疾病](../Page/限制性肺疾病.md "wikilink")。
从解剖学上可分为：
上呼吸道疾病，下呼吸道疾病，[肺间质疾病和](../Page/肺间质疾病.md "wikilink")[血管性肺病](../Page/血管性肺病.md "wikilink")。

## 阻塞性肺病（Obstructive lung diseases）

  - [支气管炎](../Page/支气管炎.md "wikilink")(Bronchitis)
  - [小支气管炎](../Page/小支气管炎.md "wikilink")(Bronchiolitis)
  - [肺气肿](../Page/肺气肿.md "wikilink")(Emphysema)
  - [哮喘](../Page/哮喘.md "wikilink")(Asthma)
  - [慢性阻塞性肺病](../Page/慢性阻塞性肺病.md "wikilink")\[Chronic obstructive
    pulmonary disease (COPD)\]
  - [支气管扩张](../Page/支气管扩张.md "wikilink")(Bronchiectasis)
  - [棉尘症](../Page/棉尘症.md "wikilink")(Byssinosis)

## 限制性肺疾病(Restrictive lung diseases)

  - [肺纤维化](../Page/肺纤维化.md "wikilink")(Fibrosis)
  - [结节病](../Page/结节病.md "wikilink")(Sarcoidosis)
  - [胸腔积液](../Page/胸腔积液.md "wikilink")(Pleural effusion)
  - [过敏性肺炎](../Page/过敏性肺炎.md "wikilink")(Hypersensitivity pneumonitis)
  - [石棉肺](../Page/石棉肺.md "wikilink")(Asbestosis)
  - [胸膜炎](../Page/胸膜炎.md "wikilink")(Pleurisy)

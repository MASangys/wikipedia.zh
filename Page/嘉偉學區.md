**嘉偉學區**\[1\](**Garvey School
District**)是[洛杉矶县的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/洛杉矶县_\(加利福尼亚州\) "wikilink")[学区](../Page/学区.md "wikilink"),
总部在[柔似蜜](../Page/柔似蜜.md "wikilink")。\[2\]

## 学校

初中:

  - Garvey Intermediate School
  - Temple Intermediate School

小学:

  - Bitely Elementary School
  - Dewey Elementary School
  - Emerson Elementary School
  - Hillcrest Elementary School
  - Marshall Elementary School
  - Monterey Vista Elementary School
  - Rice Elementary School
  - Sanchez Elementary School
  - Willard Elementary School

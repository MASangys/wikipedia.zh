**國際班迪球聯合會**（）是一個[國際性的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/國際 "wikilink")[班迪球](../Page/班迪球.md "wikilink")[體育組織](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/體育 "wikilink")，由世界各國的班迪球協會组成。總部設於[瑞典](../Page/瑞典.md "wikilink")[卡特琳娜霍爾姆](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/卡特琳娜霍爾姆 "wikilink")。目前共有26個會員協會。[1](https://web.archive.org/web/20170127123051/http://www.worldbandy.com/members.html)
[世界班迪球錦標賽創立於](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/世界班迪球錦標賽 "wikilink")1957年，女子賽是在2004年才加入。

### 前成員

**Soviet Union 1955–1991**: When the international federation was
founded in 1955, the [Soviet Union](../Page/苏联.md "wikilink") was one of
its founding members. The Soviet national federation was called the
[Federation of bandy and field hockey
USSR](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federation_of_bandy_and_field_hockey_USSR "wikilink")
(Федерация хоккея с мячом и хоккея на траве СССР). The Soviet Union had
a [national bandy team for
men](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union_national_bandy_team "wikilink")
and a [national bandy team for
women](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union_women's_national_bandy_team "wikilink").
When the Soviet Union was dissolved in December 1991, the national team
came to represent the [Commonwealth of Independent
States](../Page/獨立國家聯合體.md "wikilink") for some months. In
1992, Russia took over its place in FIB.

**West Germany/Germany 1990–1991**: Before the present German Bandy
Association was founded in 2013, there had been an earlier, short-lived
German federation, which was a member of FIB from January 1990 until
March 1991, when it voluntarily choose to leave.\[1\]\[2\] In its short
life span, it still overlived the [German
reunification](../Page/兩德統一.md "wikilink") in October 1990.

[BandyCountries2015.png](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:BandyCountries2015.png "fig:BandyCountries2015.png")
A number of national federations have been admitted as members during
the years\[3\] but then for different reasons not survived, often
because of a lack of interest in the sport in their countries. In early
2017, these federations were removed from the member list of FIB.\[4\]
These defunct national federations were:

  - , Bandy Federation of Argentina, admitted to FIB in 2008.

  - , Australian Bandy League, admitted to FIB in 2006.

  - , [Bandy Federation of
    Denmark](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bandy_Federation_of_Denmark "wikilink"),
    admitted to FIB in 2014

  - , Bandy Federation of Ireland, admitted to FIB in 2006

  - , [Italian Bandy
    Federation](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italian_Bandy_Federation "wikilink"),
    admitted to FIB in 2003

  - , Bandy Federation of Poland, admitted to FIB in 2005

  - , Bandy Federation of Serbia, admitted to FIB in 2006

**圖卡諾安語系**（**Tucanoan**；**Tukanoan**；**Tukánoan**）是一種在[南美洲東部通行的](../Page/南美洲.md "wikilink")25種語言的合稱，主要在[哥倫比亞流傳](../Page/哥倫比亞.md "wikilink")，亦有部份語言人口位於[巴西](../Page/巴西.md "wikilink")、[厄瓜多尔及](../Page/厄瓜多爾.md "wikilink")[秘魯](../Page/秘魯.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

現存的圖卡諾安語系語言大致可以分為以下15種：

I. 西圖卡諾安語（Western Tucanoan）

  -
    1\. **[Correguaje](../Page/Correguaje.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a.
    Coreguaje, Caquetá, Korewahe, Koreguaje)
    2\. **[Macaguaje](../Page/Macaguaje.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a.
    Kakawahe, Piohé, Secoya, Siona-Secoya) *(†)*
    3\. **[Teteté](../Page/Teteté.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a. Tetete,
    Eteteguaje)
    4\. **[Orejón](../Page/Orejón.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a. Coto,
    Payoguaje, Payaguá, Koto, Payowahe, Payawá)
    5\. **[Yauna](../Page/Yauna.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a. Jaúna, Yahuna,
    Yaúna)

II. 中圖卡諾安語（Central Tucanoan）

  -
    6\. **[Cubeo](../Page/Cubeo.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a. Cuveo, Kobeua,
    Kubewa)

III. 東圖卡諾安語（Eastern Tucanoan）

  -
    7\. **[Macuna](../Page/Macuna.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a. Buhagana,
    Wahana, Makuna-Erulia, Makuna)
    8\.
    **[Yupuá](../Page/Yupuá.md "wikilink")-[Durina](../Page/Durina.md "wikilink")**
    *(†)*
    9\. **[Cueretú](../Page/Cueretú.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a. Kueretú)
    *(†)*
    10\.
    **[Desano](../Page/Desano.md "wikilink")-[Siriano](../Page/Siriano.md "wikilink")**
    (a.k.a. Desano)
    11\.
    **[Bará](../Page/Bará.md "wikilink")-[Tuyuka](../Page/Tuyuka.md "wikilink")**
    (a.k.a. Pocanga, Pakang, Tejuca, Teyuka, Tuyuca, Bara)
    12\. **[Carapano](../Page/Carapano.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a.
    Carapana, Karapana)
    13\. **[圖卡諾語](../Page/圖卡諾語.md "wikilink")** (Tucano，a.k.a. Tukana,
    Dasea)
    14\. **[Guanano](../Page/Guanano.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a. Wanana,
    Kotedia, Wanana-Pirá)
    15\. **[Piratapuyo](../Page/Piratapuyo.md "wikilink")** (a.k.a.
    Waikina, Uiquina)

Macaguaje、Yupuá-Durina及Cueretú[已消亡](../Page/已消亡語言.md "wikilink")。另外，除特別註明通行地點者，其他語言皆在哥倫比亞某地通行。

**塞拉利昂行政区划**把全国分为3个[省](../Page/省.md "wikilink")（Province）和1个区（Area）。3个省之下设有12个行政区（District）；1个区之下设有2个行政区（District）。

[Sierra_Leone_Districts_zh_en.png](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Sierra_Leone_Districts_zh_en.png "fig:Sierra_Leone_Districts_zh_en.png")

  - 东方省 Eastern Province
      - [凯拉洪区](../Page/凯拉洪区.md "wikilink") Kailahun District
      - [凯内马区](../Page/凯内马区.md "wikilink") Kenema District
      - [科诺区](../Page/科诺区.md "wikilink") Kono District
  - 北方省 Northern Province
      - [邦巴利区](../Page/邦巴利区.md "wikilink") Bombali District 　
      - [坎比亚区](../Page/坎比亚区.md "wikilink") Kambia District 　
      - [洛科港区](../Page/洛科港区.md "wikilink") Port Loko District 　
      - [科伊纳杜古区](../Page/科伊纳杜古区.md "wikilink") Koinadugu District 　
      - [通科利利区](../Page/通科利利区.md "wikilink") Tonkolili District
  - 南方省 Southern Province
      - [博城区](../Page/博城区.md "wikilink") Bo District 　
      - [邦特区](../Page/邦特区.md "wikilink") Bonthe District 　
      - [莫扬巴区](../Page/莫扬巴区.md "wikilink") Moyamba District 　
      - [普杰洪区](../Page/普杰洪区.md "wikilink") Pujehun District
  - 西部区 Western Area
      - [西部区市区](../Page/西部区市区.md "wikilink") Western Area Urban District
      - [西部区郊区](../Page/西部区郊区.md "wikilink") Western Area Rural District

[Anti-reflective_coating_comparison.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Anti-reflective_coating_comparison.jpg "fig:Anti-reflective_coating_comparison.jpg") **增透膜**（英语：Anti-reflective coating，AR）是一种表面[光学](../Page/光学.md "wikilink")[镀层](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/镀层 "wikilink")，它通过减少[光](../Page/光.md "wikilink")的反射而增加透过率。在复杂的光学系统中，它可以通过减少系统中的[散射光来提高](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/散射光 "wikilink")[对比度](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/对比度 "wikilink")，例如[望远镜](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/望远镜 "wikilink")，这对天文学十分重要。其他方面，增透膜能减少暗处[双筒望远镜](../Page/双筒望远镜.md "wikilink")的闪光。

很多涂层都包括[折射率](../Page/折射率.md "wikilink")不同的透明的薄膜结构[thin film](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/thin-film_optics "wikilink") structures with alternating layers of contrasting. 薄膜的厚度决定了其作用的反射光波长。当光线在增透膜上产生二次反射时，会和原反射光发生干涉，从而减弱反射光。而根据能量守恒，光的能量不变。因此当反射光减少时，透射光便增多。这就是增透膜的原理。一般，选择增透膜时需确定波长，如红外线，可见光以及紫外线。

## 应用

### (视力)校正镜片

[Opticians](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optician "wikilink") dispense "anti-reflection lenses" because the decreased reflection makes them look better, and they produce less [glare](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/light_pollution#Glare "wikilink"), which is particularly noticeable when driving at night or working in front of a [computer monitor](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/computer_monitor "wikilink"). The decreased glare means that wearers often find their eyes are less tired, particularly at the end of the day. Allowing more light to pass through the lens also increases [contrast](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/contrast_\(vision\) "wikilink") and therefore increases visual acuity.

Antireflective ophthalmic lenses should not be confused with [polarized lenses](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/polarizer "wikilink"), which decrease (by absorption) the visible glare of sun reflected off of surfaces such as sand, water, and roads. The term "antireflective" relates to the reflection from the surface of the lens itself, not the origin of the light that reaches the lens.

Many anti-reflection lenses include an additional coating that repels [water](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/water "wikilink") and [grease](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fat "wikilink"), making them easier to keep clean. Anti-reflection coatings are particularly suited to high-[index](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/refractive_index "wikilink") lenses, as these reflect more light without the coating than a lower-index lens (a consequence of the [Fresnel equations](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fresnel_equations "wikilink")). It is also generally easier and cheaper to coat high index glasses.

### 光刻

在微电子技术的[光刻](../Page/光刻.md "wikilink")工艺中， 增透膜可以减少光斑的畸变。不同的增透膜适用不同的 [光致抗蚀剂](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/光致抗蚀剂 "wikilink"), 另外，也可以减少[驻波](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/驻波 "wikilink")，薄膜干涉以及镜面反射\[1\]\[2\]

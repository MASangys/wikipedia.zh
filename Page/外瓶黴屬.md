**外瓶黴屬**（*Exophiala*）是一種[真菌屬](../Page/真菌.md "wikilink")，屬於[Herpotrichiellaceae科](../Page/Herpotrichiellaceae.md "wikilink")，是一種在自然界呈一定的地域性分布的[条件性致病菌](../Page/条件性致病菌.md "wikilink")\[1\]\[2\]，亦是在魚類上出現的廣義的[黑色真菌症](../Page/黑色真菌症.md "wikilink")\[3\]及人類皮膚出現的[皮下黑色真菌症](../Page/皮下黑色真菌症.md "wikilink")\[4\]的病原體。

外瓶黴屬於1966年由J.W.
Carmichael確定\[5\]，當時被歸類為[半知菌亞門](../Page/半知菌亞門.md "wikilink")；[腐生](../Page/腐生.md "wikilink")，可引起[腹膜炎](../Page/腹膜炎.md "wikilink")（Deuteromycetes）\[6\]。

  - *[Exophiala

  - *[Exophiala
    calicioides](../Page/Exophiala_calicioides.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Exophiala
    castellanii](../Page/Exophiala_castellanii.md "wikilink")*
  - [皮炎外瓶黴](../Page/皮炎外瓶黴.md "wikilink") *Exophiala
    dermatitidis*\[9\]：：會分泌黑色分泌物\[10\]，可引起[暗色絲孢霉病](../Page/暗色絲孢霉病.md "wikilink")\[11\]、[皮下黑色真菌症和](../Page/皮下黑色真菌症.md "wikilink")[著色芽生黴菌症](../Page/著色芽生黴菌症.md "wikilink")\[12\]
  - [多匹可樂外瓶黴](../Page/多匹可樂外瓶黴.md "wikilink") *Exophiala dopicola*\[13\]
  - *[Exophiala exophialae](../Page/Exophiala_exophialae.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Exophiala
    heteromorpha](../Page/Exophiala_heteromorpha.md "wikilink")*
  - [甄氏外瓶黴](../Page/甄氏外瓶黴.md "wikilink")　*Exophiala
    jeanselmei*\[14\]：可引起一種名為的\[15\]，This is usually an
    asymtomatic disease which presents with black or brown macular
    lesions which enlarge by peripheral extension. The lesion is darkest
    at the periphery and very distinct margins. Lab diagnosis- using a
    KOH mount. Typically seen is brown septate branching hyphae or dark
    brown budding cells. Treatment is topical antifungal: miconazole or
    econazole；亦可引起[暗色絲孢霉病](../Page/暗色絲孢霉病.md "wikilink")\[16\]和[皮下黑色真菌症](../Page/皮下黑色真菌症.md "wikilink")\[17\]；
  - *[Exophiala
    lecanii-corni](../Page/Exophiala_lecanii-corni.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Exophiala mansonii](../Page/Exophiala_mansonii.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Exophiala mesophila](../Page/Exophiala_mesophila.md "wikilink")*
  - [叢梗孢外瓶黴](../Page/叢梗孢外瓶黴.md "wikilink") *Exophiala moniliae*\[18\]；
  - *[Exophiala negronii](../Page/Exophiala_negronii.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Exophiala
    phaeomuriformis](../Page/Exophiala_phaeomuriformis.md "wikilink")*
  - [嗜魚外瓶黴](../Page/嗜魚外瓶黴.md "wikilink") *Exophiala
    pisciphila*：見於魚類的黑色真菌\[19\]；
  - *[Exophiala
    psychrophila](../Page/Exophiala_psychrophila.md "wikilink")*
  - [鮭魚外瓶黴](../Page/鮭魚外瓶黴.md "wikilink") *Exophiala
    salmonis*：[模式種](../Page/模式種.md "wikilink")，見於魚類的黑色真菌\[20\]；
  - [棘狀外瓶黴](../Page/棘狀外瓶黴.md "wikilink") *Exophiala
    spinifera*：會引起[著色芽生黴菌症](../Page/著色芽生黴菌症.md "wikilink")\[21\]和
  - [威尼克外瓶黴](../Page/威尼克外瓶黴.md "wikilink") *Exophiala
    werneckii*\[22\]：[黑癬的病原體](../Page/黑癬.md "wikilink")。部份文獻把這種黴與
    *Hortaea werneckii*、*Cladosporium werneckii*及*Phaeoannellomyces
    werneckii*等同\[23\]。

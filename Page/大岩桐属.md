**大岩桐属**（[学名](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/学名 "wikilink")：*Gloxinia*），又名**红鸟苣苔属**\[1\]，是[苦苣苔科](../Page/苦苣苔科.md "wikilink")下的一个属，只有三個品種，均為[開花植物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/開花植物 "wikilink")。三個品種有兩個生長於[南美洲](../Page/南美洲.md "wikilink")[安第斯山脈](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/安第斯山脈 "wikilink")，但*Gloxinia perennis*亦有在[中美洲](../Page/中美洲.md "wikilink")及[加勒比地区](../Page/加勒比地区.md "wikilink")發現。

*Gloxinia perennis*是本屬的[模式種](../Page/模式種.md "wikilink")，亦是本屬最原始的品種。本屬原來有約20個品種，這是基於[Hans Wiehler在](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Hans_Wiehler "wikilink")1976年的分類而劃分\[2\]。不過，近年對分子生物學及相關領域對本屬及其相關屬的分析，發現Wiehler當年的分類太廣泛，把很多不相關的品種亦包括進來，因而把這些品種重新分類。

## 品種

  - *[Gloxinia perennis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gloxinia_perennis "wikilink")*在中南美洲廣泛分佈。The plant is an herb with large nodding, purple, mint-scented flowers. It is sometimes known as "Canterbury Bells" (not to be confused with members of the genus *[Campanula](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Campanula "wikilink")*, which go by the same name). It is cultivated in tropical regions and its original range is unknown.
  - *[Gloxinia erinoides](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gloxinia_erinoides "wikilink")* (syn. *Koellikeria erinoides*) occurs from Costa Rica to Bolivia. The plant is a small herb with tiny white and maroon flowers. This diminutive plant is notable for having tiny but distinctly coconut-scented flowers and is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant.
  - *[Gloxinia xanthophylla](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gloxinia_xanthophylla "wikilink")* (syn. *Anodiscus xanthophyllus*) occurs in Ecuador and Peru. The plant is a shrubby herb with small white flowers and is rarely encountered in cultivation. Unlike the other two species it lacks scaly rhizomes.

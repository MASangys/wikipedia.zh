**大飛鼠**（屬名：***Petaurista***）是一類[飛鼠](../Page/飛鼠.md "wikilink")，包含以下物種：

  - [Red And White Giant Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Red_And_White_Giant_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")
    (*Petaurista alborufus*)
  - [Spotted Giant Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Spotted_Giant_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")
    (*Petaurista elegans*)
  - [栗褐鼯鼠](../Page/栗褐鼯鼠.md "wikilink") (*Petaurista magnificus*)
  - [Bhutan Giant Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Bhutan_Giant_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")
    (*Petaurista nobilis*)
  - [Indian Giant Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Indian_Giant_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")
    (*Petaurista philippensis*)
  - [Chinese Giant Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Chinese_Giant_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")
    (*Petaurista xanthotis*)
  - *[Japanese giant flying
    squirrel](../Page/Japanese_giant_flying_squirrel.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Red Giant Flying
    Squirrel](../Page/Red_Giant_Flying_Squirrel.md "wikilink")*

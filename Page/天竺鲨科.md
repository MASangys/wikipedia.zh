**天竺鮫科**（學名*Hemiscylliidae*）又稱**竹鮫科**，是[板鰓亞綱](../Page/板鰓亞綱.md "wikilink")[鬚鮫目的其中一科](../Page/鬚鮫目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**天竺鮫科**下分為2個屬，如下：

  - 班竹鯊屬(*Chiloscyllium*)
      - [阿拉伯斑竹鯊](../Page/阿拉伯斑竹鯊.md "wikilink")(*Chiloscyllium arabicum*)
        <small>[Gubanov](../Page/Evhen_Pavlovich_Gubanov.md "wikilink"),
        1980</small>

      - [緬甸斑竹鯊](../Page/緬甸斑竹鯊.md "wikilink")(*Chiloscyllium burmensis*)
        <small>[Dingerkus](../Page/Guido_Dingerkus.md "wikilink") &
        [DeFino](../Page/Terry_C._DeFino.md "wikilink"), 1983</small>

      - [灰斑竹鯊](../Page/灰斑竹鯊.md "wikilink")(*Chiloscyllium griseum*)
        <small>[J. P. Müller](../Page/約翰內斯·彼得·繆勒.md "wikilink") &
        [Henle](../Page/Friedrich_Gustav_Jakob_Henle.md "wikilink"),
        1838</small>：又稱灰斑狗鮫。

      - (*Chiloscyllium hasselti*)
        <small>[Bleeker](../Page/Pieter_Bleeker.md "wikilink"),
        1852</small>

      - [印度斑竹鯊](../Page/印度斑竹鯊.md "wikilink")(*Chiloscyllium indicum*)
        <small>[J. F.
        Gmelin](../Page/Johann_Friedrich_Gmelin.md "wikilink"),
        1789</small>：又稱印度狗鮫。

      - [條紋斑竹鯊](../Page/條紋斑竹鯊.md "wikilink")(*Chiloscyllium plagiosum*)
        <small>Anonymous, referred to
        [Bennett](../Page/Edward_Turner_Bennett.md "wikilink"),
        1830</small>：又稱斑竹狗鮫。

      - [點紋斑竹鯊](../Page/點紋斑竹鯊.md "wikilink")(*Chiloscyllium punctatum*)
        <small>[J. P. Müller](../Page/約翰內斯·彼得·繆勒.md "wikilink") &
        [Henle](../Page/Friedrich_Gustav_Jakob_Henle.md "wikilink"),
        1838</small>：又稱狗鮫。
  - 長尾鬚鯊屬(*Hemiscyllium*)
      - [印度印西亞長尾鬚鯊](../Page/印度印西亞長尾鬚鯊.md "wikilink")(*Hemiscyllium
        freycineti*)
        <small>([Quoy](../Page/Jean_René_Constant_Quoy.md "wikilink") &
        [Gaimard](../Page/Joseph_Paul_Gaimard.md "wikilink"),
        1824)</small>

      - (*Hemiscyllium galei*) <small>[G. R.
        Allen](../Page/Gerald_R._Allen.md "wikilink") &
        [Erdmann](../Page/Mark_van_Nydeck_Erdmann.md "wikilink"),
        2008</small>\[1\]

      - [巴布亞長尾鬚鯊](../Page/巴布亞長尾鬚鯊.md "wikilink")(*Hemiscyllium
        hallstromi*)
        <small>[Whitley](../Page/Gilbert_Percy_Whitley.md "wikilink"),
        1967</small>

      - (*Hemiscyllium halmahera*) <small>[G. R.
        Allen](../Page/Gerald_R._Allen.md "wikilink"),
        [Erdmann](../Page/Mark_van_Nydeck_Erdmann.md "wikilink") &
        [Dudgeon](../Page/Christine_L._Dudgeon.md "wikilink"),
        2013</small>\[2\]

      - (*Hemiscyllium henryi*) <small>G. R. Allen & Erdmann,
        2008</small>\[3\]

      - (*Hemiscyllium michaeli*) <small>G. R. Allen & Dudgeon,
        2010</small>\[4\]

      - [斑點長尾鬚鯊](../Page/斑點長尾鬚鯊.md "wikilink")(*Hemiscyllium ocellatum*)
        <small>([Bonnaterre](../Page/Pierre_Joseph_Bonnaterre.md "wikilink"),
        1788)</small>

      - [斯氏長尾鬚鯊](../Page/斯氏長尾鬚鯊.md "wikilink")(*Hemiscyllium strahani*)
        <small>Whitley, 1967</small>

      - [項斑長尾鬚鯊](../Page/項斑長尾鬚鯊.md "wikilink")(*Hemiscyllium
        trispeculare*) <small>[J.
        Richardson](../Page/John_Richardson_\(naturalist\).md "wikilink"),
        1843</small>

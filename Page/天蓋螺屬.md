**天蓋螺屬**（學名：**）是[裂螺科](../Page/裂螺科.md "wikilink")[天蓋螺亞科之下的一個由小型到中型](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/天蓋螺亞科 "wikilink")[海螺組成的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/海螺 "wikilink")[屬](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/屬 "wikilink")。

## Life habits

Like all other fissurellids, *Diodora* species are herbivores, and use the [齒舌](../Page/齒舌.md "wikilink") to scrape algae from rocks. An exception is *D. apertura*, which grazes on sponges\[1\]\[2\] such as **.\[3\]

Water for respiration and excretion is drawn in under the edge of the shell and exits through the "keyhole" at or near the apex.

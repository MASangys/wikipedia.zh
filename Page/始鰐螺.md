**始鰐螺**（學名：**）是一類已[滅絕的](../Page/滅絕.md "wikilink")[腹足綱](../Page/腹足綱.md "wikilink")[軟體動物](../Page/軟體動物.md "wikilink")[化石](../Page/化石.md "wikilink")。它們生存於[始新世的](../Page/始新世.md "wikilink")[亞洲](../Page/亞洲.md "wikilink")、[歐洲及](../Page/歐洲.md "wikilink")[北美洲](../Page/北美洲.md "wikilink")。

## 棲息地

這種[肉食性](../Page/肉食性.md "wikilink")[海洋生物棲息於沙質土壤中較深的海域](../Page/海洋生物.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

  - *[Eosurcula moorei](../Page/Eosurcula_moorei.md "wikilink")* Gabb,
    1860 from [Bartonian](../Page/Bartonian.md "wikilink") in
    [Texas](../Page/Texas.md "wikilink")\[1\]
  - *[Eosurcula stena](../Page/Eosurcula_stena.md "wikilink")* (Edwards)
    from [Hampshire](../Page/Hampshire.md "wikilink")\[2\]

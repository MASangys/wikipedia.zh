**富衛**（）是一個總部設於[香港](../Page/香港.md "wikilink")的[保險](../Page/保險.md "wikilink")集團，於[香港](../Page/香港.md "wikilink")、[澳門](../Page/澳門.md "wikilink")、[新加坡](../Page/新加坡.md "wikilink")及[泰國提供](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/泰國 "wikilink")[人壽保險](../Page/人壽保險.md "wikilink")、、[退休金](../Page/退休金.md "wikilink")[信託及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/信託 "wikilink")[財務策劃服務](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/財務策劃 "wikilink")，是由香港富商[李澤楷](../Page/李澤楷.md "wikilink")私人持有的[盈科拓展](../Page/盈科拓展.md "wikilink")集團擁有。

FWD is a Hong Kong-based insurance group that provides life insurance, general insurance, pension trust and financial planning services in Hong Kong, Macau, Singapore and Thailand. It is owned by Pacific Century Regional Developments Limited, which is privately held by Hong Kong businessman Richard Li Tzar Kai.

## 歷史

前身為[ING](../Page/ING.md "wikilink")於[亞洲的保險業務](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/亞洲 "wikilink")。2012年10月，李澤楷的盈科拓展集團以21.4億美元向ING收購香港、澳門及泰國保險業務。\[1\]2013年4月，富衛購入李澤楷減持的[香港電訊信託部份股權](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/香港電訊信託 "wikilink")。\[2\]2013年7月，ING香港、澳門及泰國保險業務易名為富衛。\[3\]

2013年10月，[瑞士再保险](../Page/瑞士再保险.md "wikilink")（Swiss Re）以4.25億美元購入富衛12.3%股份。\[4\]

2016年6月7日，富衛獲批准收購大東方人壽（越南）有限公司。\[5\]

2016年8月4日，富衛出售旗下[強制性公積金](../Page/強制性公積金.md "wikilink")及職業退休計劃業務，包括「富衛退休金信託有限公司」给香港[永明金融](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/永明金融 "wikilink")。雙方亦達成為期15年的策略分銷協議，富衛的專屬代理人將可於香港分銷永明金融的強積金及公積金產品。

2016年11月15日美國保險集團[AIG](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIG "wikilink")，出售日本人壽保險業務AIG富士生命保險公司给富衛。

2018年10月23日富衛以4.26億澳元（約23.62億港元）收購[澳洲聯邦銀行](../Page/澳洲聯邦銀行.md "wikilink")旗下印尼人壽保險業務PT Commonwealth Life（PTCL）的80%股權。雙方亦達成為期15年的策略分銷協議，澳洲聯邦銀行的專屬代理人將可分銷相關產品。

2019年富衛冠名贊助於4月28日在[沙田馬場](../Page/沙田馬場.md "wikilink")舉行的冠軍賽馬日，並冠名贊助[冠軍一哩賽和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/冠軍一哩賽 "wikilink")[女皇盃兩項國際一級賽](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/女皇盃 "wikilink")。\[6\]

2019年6月27日富衛以4億美元（31.2億港元）收購美國[大都會人壽保險](../Page/大都會人壽保險.md "wikilink")香港業務，即大都會人壽保險有限公司及美商大都會人壽保險香港有限公司。

2019年7月1日[富衛](../Page/富衛.md "wikilink")以30億美元927億泰銖(約236.8億港元)及其他額外收益收購[泰國匯商銀行](../Page/泰國匯商銀行.md "wikilink")人壽保險公司（SCB Life）全部股權，同時訂立為期15年獨家分銷協議，將向其泰國客戶分銷富衛人壽保險產品。

In October 2012, Pacific Century Regional Developments Limited acquired Hong Kong, Macau and Thailand insurance business from ING for US$2.14 billion.

In July 2013, ING Hong Kong, Macau and Thailand's insurance business was renamed FWD.

In October 2013, Swiss Re purchased a 12.3% stake in FWD for US$425 million.

On June 7, 2016, FWD was approved to acquire Great Eastern Life (Vietnam) Co. Ltd.

On August 4, 2016, FWD sold its mandatory provident fund and occupational retirement scheme business, including “FWD Trust Co., Ltd.” to Hong Kong Sun Life Financial Inc. The two parties have also reached a 15-year strategic distribution agreement. The exclusive agent of FWD will be able to distribute the liquidity and provident fund products of Sun Life Financial in Hong Kong.

On November 15, 2016, American insurance group (AIG) sold AIG Fuji Life Insurance Co., a Japanese life insurance company, to FWD.

On October 23, 2018, FWD acquired an 80% stake in PT Commonwealth Life (PTCL) from the Bank of Australia, for US$426 million (approximately HK$2,362 million). The two parties also reached a 15-year strategic distribution agreement, and the exclusive agent of the Commonwealth Bank of Australia will be able to distribute related products.

In 2019, FWD sponsored the champion horse race held at Sha Tin Racecourse on April 28th, and was named to sponsor the champion squad and the Queen's Cup two international first-class matches.

On June 27, 2019, FWD acquired US Metropolitan Life Insurance's Hong Kong business for US$400 million (HK$3.12 billion), namely Metropolitan Life Insurance Co., Ltd. and American Metropolitan Life Insurance Hong Kong Limited.

On July 1, 2019, FWD acquired the entire share capital of SCB Life for US$35.7 billion (HK$23.68 billion) and other additional income, and entered into a 15-year exclusive distribution agreement. Its Thai customers distribute Fu Wei Life Insurance products.

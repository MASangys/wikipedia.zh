**察合台汗**是[察合台汗国的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/察合台汗国 "wikilink")[可汗](../Page/可汗.md "wikilink")，第一任可汗是[成吉思汗次子](../Page/成吉思汗.md "wikilink")[察合台](../Page/察合台.md "wikilink")，1227年察合台获得原属[西辽的封地](../Page/西辽.md "wikilink")。1680年察合台系的[葉爾羌汗國被蒙古](../Page/葉爾羌汗國.md "wikilink")[准噶尔汗国吞并](../Page/准噶尔汗国.md "wikilink")，该汗位终止。

***注意：**以下列表并不完整，且有时会不完全准确。而且此列表未包含一些只统治了很少或不太重要的土地的察合台旁系。*

During the reign of <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/拉失德" title="wikilink">Abdur-Rashid Khan</a> a certain <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/纳克什班迪教团" title="wikilink">Naqshbandi</a> Sufi teacher <strong>Ahmad Kasan</strong> (1462–1542), known as <em>Makhdum-i-Azam</em> (the Great Master) came to <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/喀什" title="wikilink">Kashgar</a> from <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/撒马尔罕" title="wikilink">Samarkand</a>. Makhdum's eldest son <strong>Muhammad Amin's</strong> (d.1598), son <strong>Muhammad Yūsuf</strong> settled in <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/喀什" title="wikilink">Kashgar</a> and his son <strong>Khoja</strong> <strong>Hidayatullah</strong> better known as <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/阿帕克和卓" title="wikilink">Afaq Khoja</a> became the founder of the <em>Afaqiya</em> Sufi order (<a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/:en:Tariqa" title="wikilink">tariqa</a>), and his followers became known as the <em><a href="../Page/和卓.md" title="wikilink">Aq Taghliks</a></em>, those of the "White Mountains", because of their close approximation to <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/天山" title="wikilink">Tangri Tagh</a> (Tian Shan). Makhdum's second son, <strong>Muhammad Ishaq Wali</strong> (d.1599), founder of <em>Ishaqiya</em> Sufi order, with his followers established themselves in <a href="../Page/莎车县.md" title="wikilink">Yarkand</a> and became known as the <em><a href="../Page/和卓.md" title="wikilink">Qara Taghliks</a></em>, those of the "Black Mountains", because of their close approximation to the <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/帕米尔高原" title="wikilink">Pamir</a>, <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/喀喇崑崙山脈" title="wikilink">Karakoram</a> and <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/崑崙山" title="wikilink">Kunlun</a>.

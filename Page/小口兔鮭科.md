**小口兔鮭科**是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[水珍魚目的一個科](../Page/水珍魚目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**小口兔鮭科**其下分11個屬，如下：

### 淵珍魚屬(*Bathylagichthys*)

  - [澳洲淵珍魚](../Page/澳洲淵珍魚.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagichthys australis*)
  - [格雷淵珍魚](../Page/格雷淵珍魚.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagichthys greyae*)
  - [長鰭淵珍魚](../Page/長鰭淵珍魚.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagichthys longipinnis*)
  - [帕氏淵珍魚](../Page/帕氏淵珍魚.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagichthys parini*)
  - [西南非洲淵珍魚](../Page/西南非洲淵珍魚.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagichthys
    problematicus*)

### 似深海鮭屬(*Bathylagoides*)

  - [銀腹似深海鮭](../Page/銀腹似深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagoides argyrogaster*)
  - [黑似深海鮭](../Page/黑似深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagoides nigrigenys*)
  - [韋氏似深海鮭](../Page/韋氏似深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagoides wesethi*)

### 深海鮭屬(*Bathylagus*)

  - [安氏深海鮭](../Page/安氏深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagus andriashevi*)
  - [南極深海鮭](../Page/南極深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagus antarcticus*)
  - [冰島寬深海鮭](../Page/冰島寬深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagus euryops*)
  - [細深海鮭](../Page/細深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagus gracilis*)
  - [黑體深海鮭](../Page/黑體深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagus niger*)
  - [太平洋深海鮭](../Page/太平洋深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagus
    pacificus*)：又稱細體海珍魚。
  - [狹深海鮭](../Page/狹深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Bathylagus tenuis*)

### 長兔鮭屬(*Dolicholagus*)

  - [大吻長兔鮭](../Page/大吻長兔鮭.md "wikilink")(*Dolicholagus longirostris*)

### 光舌鮭屬(*Leuroglossus*)

  - [卡氏光舌鮭](../Page/卡氏光舌鮭.md "wikilink")(*Leuroglossus callorhini*)
  - [施氏光舌鮭](../Page/施氏光舌鮭.md "wikilink")(*Leuroglossus schmidti*)
  - [滑舌光舌鮭](../Page/滑舌光舌鮭.md "wikilink")(*Leuroglossus stilbius*)

### 深海脂鮭屬(*Lipolagus*)

  - [鄂霍次克深海脂鮭](../Page/鄂霍次克深海脂鮭.md "wikilink")(*Lipolagus ochotensis*)

### 黑淵鮭屬(*Melanolagus*)

  - [黑淵鮭](../Page/黑淵鮭.md "wikilink")(*Melanolagus bericoides*)

### 小口兔鮭屬(*Microstoma*)

  - [小口兔鮭](../Page/小口兔鮭.md "wikilink")(*Microstoma microstoma*)

### 南氏魚屬(*Nansenia*)

  - [阿爾南氏魚](../Page/阿爾南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia ahlstromi*)
  - [南極南氏魚](../Page/南極南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia antarctica*)
  - [南氏魚](../Page/南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia ardesiaca*)
  - [大西洋南氏魚](../Page/大西洋南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia atlantica*)
  - [光耀南氏魚](../Page/光耀南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia candida*)
  - [厚身南氏魚](../Page/厚身南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia crassa*)
  - [大眼南氏魚](../Page/大眼南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia groenlandica*)
  - [西班牙南氏魚](../Page/西班牙南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia iberica*)
  - [印度南氏魚](../Page/印度南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia indica*)
  - [長尾南氏魚](../Page/長尾南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia longicauda*)
  - [大鱗南氏魚](../Page/大鱗南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia macrolepis*)
  - [大南氏魚](../Page/大南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia megalopa*)
  - [遺南氏魚](../Page/遺南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia oblita*)
  - [暗色南氏魚](../Page/暗色南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia obscura*)
  - [海南氏魚](../Page/海南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia pelagica*)
  - [桑南氏魚](../Page/桑南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia tenera*)
  - [薄尾南氏魚](../Page/薄尾南氏魚.md "wikilink")(*Nansenia tenuicauda*)

### 擬深海鮭屬(*Pseudobathylagus*)

  - [短吻擬深海鮭](../Page/短吻擬深海鮭.md "wikilink")(*Pseudobathylagus milleri*)

### 奇眼珍魚屬(*Xenophthalmichthys*)

  - [奇眼珍魚](../Page/奇眼珍魚.md "wikilink")(*Xenophthalmichthys danae*)

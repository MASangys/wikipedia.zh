**小土蜗**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）为[椎实螺科](../Page/椎实螺科.md "wikilink")[土蜗属的动物](../Page/土蜗属.md "wikilink")，俗名鸭姆螺、小椎实螺、椎实螺、痕螺。分布于[俄罗斯](../Page/俄罗斯.md "wikilink")、[蒙古](../Page/蒙古.md "wikilink")、[朝鲜](../Page/朝鲜.md "wikilink")、[日本](../Page/日本.md "wikilink")、[菲律宾](../Page/菲律宾.md "wikilink")、[印度](../Page/印度.md "wikilink")、[缅甸](../Page/缅甸.md "wikilink")、马里亚纳群岛、[台湾岛以及](../Page/台湾岛.md "wikilink")[中国大陆的](../Page/中国大陆.md "wikilink")[黑龙江](../Page/黑龙江.md "wikilink")、[吉林](../Page/吉林.md "wikilink")、[辽宁](../Page/辽宁.md "wikilink")、[内蒙古](../Page/内蒙古.md "wikilink")、[河北](../Page/河北.md "wikilink")、[河南](../Page/河南.md "wikilink")、[山东](../Page/山东.md "wikilink")、[山西](../Page/山西.md "wikilink")、[陕西](../Page/陕西.md "wikilink")、[甘肃](../Page/甘肃.md "wikilink")、[青海](../Page/青海.md "wikilink")、[新疆](../Page/新疆.md "wikilink")、[西藏](../Page/西藏.md "wikilink")、[安徽](../Page/安徽.md "wikilink")、[江苏](../Page/江苏.md "wikilink")、[浙江](../Page/浙江.md "wikilink")、[江西](../Page/江西.md "wikilink")、[湖北](../Page/湖北.md "wikilink")、[湖南](../Page/湖南.md "wikilink")、[福建](../Page/福建.md "wikilink")、[广东](../Page/广东.md "wikilink")、[广西](../Page/广西.md "wikilink")、[贵州](../Page/贵州.md "wikilink")、[云南](../Page/云南.md "wikilink")、[四川等地](../Page/四川.md "wikilink")，常栖息于湖泊以及小溪沿岸的静水带。\[1\]

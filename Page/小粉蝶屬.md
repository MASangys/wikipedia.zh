**小粉蝶屬**（學名：***Leptidea***）是[粉蝶科](../Page/粉蝶科.md "wikilink")[袖粉蝶亞科裡的一個屬](../Page/袖粉蝶亞科.md "wikilink")。共有7個物種，分佈於[古北界](../Page/古北界.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

下列部分本屬物種：

  - [突角小粉蝶](../Page/突角小粉蝶.md "wikilink") *[Leptidea
    amurensis](../Page/Leptidea_amurensis.md "wikilink")*
  - [杜波小粉蝶](../Page/杜波小粉蝶.md "wikilink") *L. duponcheli*
  - [圆翅小粉蝶](../Page/圆翅小粉蝶.md "wikilink") *[Leptidea
    gigantea](../Page/Leptidea_gigantea.md "wikilink")*
  - *L. juvernica*
  - [乳色小粉蝶](../Page/乳色小粉蝶.md "wikilink") *L. lactea*
  - [莫氏小粉蝶](../Page/莫氏小粉蝶.md "wikilink") *[Leptidea
    morsei](../Page/Leptidea_morsei.md "wikilink")*
  - [雷小粉蝶](../Page/雷小粉蝶.md "wikilink") *L. reali*
  - [锯纹小粉蝶](../Page/锯纹小粉蝶.md "wikilink") *[Leptidea
    serrata](../Page/Leptidea_serrata.md "wikilink")*
  - [条纹小粉蝶](../Page/条纹小粉蝶.md "wikilink") *[Leptidea
    sinapis](../Page/Leptidea_sinapis.md "wikilink")*

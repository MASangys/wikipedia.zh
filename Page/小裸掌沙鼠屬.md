**小裸蹠沙鼠屬**（學名**''Taterillus**''）又稱**長尾沙鼠屬**。是一種鼠科動物，包含下列物種：

  - [沙地長尾沙鼠](../Page/沙地長尾沙鼠.md "wikilink") (*Taterillus arenarius*)
  - [剛果長尾沙鼠](../Page/剛果長尾沙鼠.md "wikilink") (*Taterillus congicus*)
  - [小長尾沙鼠](../Page/小長尾沙鼠.md "wikilink") (*Taterillus emini*)
  - [瘦長尾沙鼠](../Page/瘦長尾沙鼠.md "wikilink") (*Taterillus gracilis*)
  - [哈林頓長尾沙鼠](../Page/哈林頓長尾沙鼠.md "wikilink") (*Taterillus harringtoni*)
  - [乍湖長尾沙鼠](../Page/乍湖長尾沙鼠.md "wikilink") (*Taterillus lacustris*)
  - [彼得沙鼠](../Page/彼得沙鼠.md "wikilink") (*Taterillus petteri*)
  - [白臀長尾沙鼠](../Page/白臀長尾沙鼠.md "wikilink") (*Taterillus pygargus*)
  - *Taterillus tranieri*

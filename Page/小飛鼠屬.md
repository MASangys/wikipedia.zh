**小飛鼠**（屬名：**''Petaurillus**''；英語名：**Pygmy flying
squirrels**）是[鼯鼠中的一個屬](../Page/鼯鼠.md "wikilink")，包含以下物種：

  - [Lesser pygmy flying
    squirrel](../Page/Lesser_pygmy_flying_squirrel.md "wikilink")
    *[Petaurillus emiliae](../Page/Petaurillus_emiliae.md "wikilink")*
    Thomas, 1908
  - [Hose's pygmy flying
    squirrel](../Page/Hose's_pygmy_flying_squirrel.md "wikilink")
    *[Petaurillus hosei](../Page/Petaurillus_hosei.md "wikilink")*
    (Thomas, 1900)
  - [Selangor pygmy flying
    squirrel](../Page/Selangor_pygmy_flying_squirrel.md "wikilink")
    *[Petaurillus
    kinlochii](../Page/Petaurillus_kinlochii.md "wikilink")* (Robinson
    and Kloss, 1911)

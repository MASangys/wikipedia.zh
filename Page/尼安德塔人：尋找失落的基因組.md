[Plos_paabo.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Plos_paabo.jpg "fig:Plos_paabo.jpg") **尼安德塔人：尋找失落的基因組**（）是[瑞典](../Page/瑞典.md "wikilink")[演化人類學](../Page/演化人類學.md "wikilink")者[斯万特·帕博](../Page/斯万特·帕博.md "wikilink")（Svante Pääbo）所著的一部书\[1\]。本书讲述了帕博對生活在欧洲和近东的[尼安德塔人DNA的探索](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/尼安德塔人 "wikilink")。此书以[回忆录的形式写成](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/回忆录 "wikilink")\[2\]，畅談了其25年來的研究歷程。

## 反响

[科普作家](../Page/科学普及.md "wikilink")在《[紐約時報](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/紐約時報 "wikilink")》撰文称其为“令人着迷的书”（"a fascinating account"）\[3\] The book made the paper's Sunday Book Review Editors' Choice List.\[4\] 《[衛報](../Page/衛報.md "wikilink")》的Peter Forbes, remarking on the book's characterization of the research process, stated that:

"His book is also valuable for showing just how modern research teams work together and, sometimes, fall out. Along the way, a key collaborator became a competitor and, midstream, Pääbo abandoned one collaborating gene-sequencing company for another. The race for the Neanderthal genome is reminiscent of the human genome story of 10 years previously, as recounted in James Shreeve's *The Genome War*."\[5\]

**尼爾·約翰·泰勒**（，），是一名[威爾士足球運動員](../Page/威爾士.md "wikilink")，司職[左後衞](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/後衞_\(足球\) "wikilink")。於[威爾士](../Page/威爾士.md "wikilink")[聖亞薩出世](../Page/聖亞薩.md "wikilink")，現效力[英冠球隊](../Page/英格蘭足球冠軍聯賽.md "wikilink")[阿士東維拉](../Page/阿士東維拉足球會.md "wikilink")，並為[威爾斯代表](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/威爾斯足球代表隊 "wikilink")。

作為一名[曼城青訓](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/曼城 "wikilink")，他於2007年在[雷克斯漢姆展開其職業生涯並在](../Page/雷克斯汉姆足球俱乐部.md "wikilink")2010年以15萬鎊加盟史雲斯，並在綽號「天鵝」的史雲斯上陣超過150場。母親是一名印度移民，他是在英國職業足球中相對小數的[亞裔英國人之一](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/亞裔英國人 "wikilink")。\[1\]

泰勒在2010年開始為[威爾斯出戰國際賽事](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/威爾斯足球代表隊 "wikilink")，他目前已經累積超過30頂喼帽，也獲選為[2016年歐洲足球錦標賽](../Page/2016年歐洲足球錦標賽.md "wikilink")23人名單之一。他也在[2012年夏季奧林匹克運動會代表](../Page/2012年夏季奧林匹克運動會男子足球比賽.md "wikilink")[英國出賽](../Page/英国奥林匹克足球队.md "wikilink")。

## 俱樂部生涯

### 雷克斯漢姆

Taylor began his career at [Manchester
City](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manchester_City_F.C. "wikilink"),
but left the club at the age of 15.\[2\] He instead moved to
[Wrexham](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wrexham_F.C. "wikilink") at the
age of 16, progressing through the youth system at the club,\[3\]
signing a professional contract in July 2007.\[4\] He made his
professional debut on 28 August 2007 in the second round of the [League
Cup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Football_League_Cup "wikilink"), as a
79th-minute substitute for [Eifion
Williams](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eifion_Williams "wikilink") in a
0–5 home loss to [Aston
Villa](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aston_Villa "wikilink") at the
[Racecourse
Ground](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Racecourse_Ground "wikilink").\[5\]
On 22 September, he made his league debut, starting in a 2–1 [League
Two](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Football_League_Two "wikilink") loss
at [Stockport
County](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stockport_County "wikilink"),
crossing for the opening goal by [Marc
Williams](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marc_Williams "wikilink").\[6\]
He finished the 2007–08 season when he made 27 league and cup
appearances.\[7\] He signed an extension to his contract on 13 March
2008, keeping him at Wrexham until 2010.\[8\] Wrexham finished the
season with relegation out of [The Football
League](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Football_League "wikilink").

2008年10月7日， Taylor scored his first career goal, in a 3–1 home win over
[York City](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/York_City "wikilink") in the
[Conference
Premier](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conference_Premier "wikilink"), a
match in which he was
[captain](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Captain_\(association_football\) "wikilink"),\[9\]
and followed it up with another in a 5–0 rout of [Eastbourne
Borough](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eastbourne_Borough "wikilink") on
20 December.\[10\] His only other Wrexham goal came in his last
appearance on 10 April 2010, volleying to conclude a 2–0 win at
relegated [Grays
Athletic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grays_Athletic "wikilink").\[11\]

### 斯旺西

2010年6月30日，剛約滿[域斯咸的](../Page/雷克斯汉姆足球俱乐部.md "wikilink")21歲[威爾斯足球代表隊代表泰萊加盟另一支](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/威爾斯足球代表隊 "wikilink")[威爾士球隊](../Page/威爾士.md "wikilink")[英冠的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/英冠 "wikilink")[斯旺西](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/斯旺西城足球俱乐部 "wikilink")，合約為期三年\[12\]。但由於球員轉會年齡於24歲以下，就算約滿轉會，青訓球會仍有權向新球會索取賠償金，兩間球會仍商討中。

2010年9月30日，[斯旺西與](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/斯旺西城足球俱乐部 "wikilink")[域斯咸達成協議](../Page/雷克斯汉姆足球俱乐部.md "wikilink")，[斯旺西會支付](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/斯旺西城足球俱乐部 "wikilink")15萬[英鎊賠償金給](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/英鎊 "wikilink")[域斯咸](../Page/雷克斯汉姆足球俱乐部.md "wikilink")，另加如日後[斯旺西出售泰萊中所得到轉會費盈利](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/斯旺西城足球俱乐部 "wikilink")10%\[13\]。

2011年6月7日，[斯旺西證實收到](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/斯旺西城足球俱乐部 "wikilink")[紐卡素報價提出收購泰萊](../Page/纽卡斯尔联足球俱乐部.md "wikilink")\[14\]。

2011年7月14日，泰萊與[斯旺西簽署一份新的四年合約](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/斯旺西城足球俱乐部 "wikilink")\[15\]。

[Robin_Van_Persie_Nathan_Dyer_&_Neil_Taylor_2011.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Robin_Van_Persie_Nathan_Dyer_&_Neil_Taylor_2011.jpg "fig:Robin_Van_Persie_Nathan_Dyer_&_Neil_Taylor_2011.jpg")在2011年對陣阿仙奴的比賽聯合對[罗宾·范佩西進行攔截](../Page/罗宾·范佩西.md "wikilink")\]\]
Taylor suffered a broken ankle in the game against
[Sunderland](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sunderland_A.F.C. "wikilink")
on 1 September 2012 after falling awkwardly during a challenge on [Craig
Gardner](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Craig_Gardner "wikilink") in the
early stages of the game and was ruled out until the end of the
season.\[16\] On 14 December 2012, Taylor signed a new
three-and-a-half-year contract with Swansea, lasting until 2016.\[17\]

In late February, Taylor returned to full training after six months out
with injury.\[18\] Shortly before his return, Swansea City won the
[League Cup
Final](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2013_Football_League_Cup_Final "wikilink")
following a 5–0 win over League Two side [Bradford
City](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bradford_City_A.F.C "wikilink").\[19\]
In late April, he played 80 minutes for [Swansea City
U21s](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swansea_City_A.F.C._Reserves "wikilink")
as part of his rehabilitation.\[20\] Taylor made his first senior
appearance since his injury as a substitute for [Ben
Davies](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ben_Davies_\(footballer_born_1993\) "wikilink")
in a 2–0 loss against
[Chelsea](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chelsea_F.C. "wikilink") on 28
April 2013.\[21\] After the match, he expressed relief at his return to
the first team.\[22\]

After Taylor's return, Swansea manager [Michael
Laudrup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Laudrup "wikilink")
described the battle for the left-back slot between Taylor and Davies as
“a beautiful problem for a manager to have”.\[23\] Following the
transfer of Davies to [Tottenham
Hotspur](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tottenham_Hotspur_F.C. "wikilink")
in 2014, Taylor reestablished himself as first choice left-back for
Swansea. Taylor signed a new four year contract in June 2015, tying him
to the club until 2019.\[24\]

## 國家隊生涯

### 威爾斯

[Neil_Taylor_Wales.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Neil_Taylor_Wales.jpg "fig:Neil_Taylor_Wales.jpg")
Taylor was also eligible for
[India](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/India_national_football_team "wikilink")
through his mother.\[25\] He made his debut for
[Wales](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wales_national_football_team "wikilink")
on 23 May 2010 in a friendly against
[Croatia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Croatia_national_football_team "wikilink")
at the [Stadion Gradski
vrt](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stadion_Gradski_vrt "wikilink"),
replacing [Andy
Dorman](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andy_Dorman "wikilink") for the
final 23 minutes of the 2–0 loss; he and [Mark
Bradley](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mark_Bradley "wikilink") had been
promoted from the under-21 team in an emergency.\[26\] In May 2011, he
played two games at the [Nations
Cup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2011_Nations_Cup "wikilink") in
Dublin.\[27\]

On 9 September 2014, in Wales' first match of [UEFA Euro 2016
qualification](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2016_qualification "wikilink"),
he gave away a penalty to
[Andorra](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andorra_national_football_team "wikilink")
in the fifth minute, converted by [Cristian
Martínez](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cristian_Martínez_\(Andorran_footballer\) "wikilink"),
but the Welsh fought back for a 2–1 victory.\[28\]

### 英國

[Stuart Pearce](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stuart_Pearce "wikilink")
named Taylor in his 18-man squad for the [2012 Summer
Olympics](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2012_Summer_Olympics "wikilink")
in [London](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/London "wikilink") as one of
three [Swansea
City](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Swansea_City_A.F.C. "wikilink")
players.\[29\] He played his first game for [Great
Britain](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Britain_Olympic_football_team "wikilink")
in a friendly against
[Brazil](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Brazil_national_under-23_football_team "wikilink")
at the [Riverside
Stadium](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Riverside_Stadium "wikilink") on
20 July.\[30\] He then went on to appear in all of the team's group
stage fixtures, helping to secure progression into the knockout stages.

## 個人生活

泰勒出生在[登比郡](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/登比郡 "wikilink")[聖亞薩並在鄰近的](../Page/聖亞薩.md "wikilink")[魯思因成長](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/魯思因 "wikilink")，\[31\]
where he attended Ysgol Brynhyfryd.\[32\]
他是[威爾斯和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/威爾斯人 "wikilink")[印度的混血兒](../Page/印度.md "wikilink");
他的母親Shibani
Chakraborty為[印度](../Page/印度.md "wikilink")[加爾各答的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/加爾各答 "wikilink")[孟加拉族人](../Page/孟加拉族.md "wikilink")，\[33\]\[34\]
He is married to Genna, with whom he has two daughters, and the family
live in [Killay,
Swansea](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Killay,_Swansea "wikilink"); in a
2016 spring clean, they donated furniture worth thousands of pounds to
be sold by the [British Heart
Foundation](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_Heart_Foundation "wikilink").\[35\]
Taylor travels to India to promote football, and has said that due to
his name, the population are surprised to learn of his ancestry.\[36\]

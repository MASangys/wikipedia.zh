**山姆·阿布拉姆斯**（Sam
Abrams，）出生于[布鲁克林](../Page/布鲁克林.md "wikilink")，是一名美国[作家](../Page/作家.md "wikilink")。\[1\]\[2\]\[3\]阿布拉姆斯曾以[福布萊特教授的身份於](../Page/福布萊特.md "wikilink")[雅典大学任教](../Page/雅典大学.md "wikilink")[美国文学](../Page/美国文学.md "wikilink")，現於[羅徹斯特理工學院的文學院擔任语言与文学](../Page/羅徹斯特理工學院.md "wikilink")[名誉](../Page/名誉.md "wikilink")[教授](../Page/教授.md "wikilink")。阿布拉姆斯是[布鲁克林](../Page/布鲁克林.md "wikilink")[詹姆斯·麦迪逊高中](../Page/詹姆斯·麦迪逊高中.md "wikilink")、[布魯克林大學](../Page/布魯克林大學.md "wikilink")
（1958年文学学士学位）及[伊利諾伊大學厄巴納-香檳分校](../Page/伊利諾伊大學厄巴納-香檳分校.md "wikilink")（1950文學碩士）的毕业生。

## 著作

**书籍**：
\* *Barbara*. Ferry Press (London, 1966), 1st Edition. 32 pp. Poems,
limited edition of 350 copies.

  - *The Neglected Walt Whitman*. Sam Abrams, Editor. Sixty-five poems,
    fragments, and three prose pieces by Whitman.
  - *The Old Pothead Poems*. Louis Armstrong said it, "Marijuana is an
    assistant, a friend." These poems riff off that theme, a
    fifty-year-long set of improv-collaborations between two old
    friends, Miss Mary Jane and her man, Sam. Poems too of a classicist,
    on familiar terms with Sappho, Archilochus, Horace, Socrates --
    regulars in the audience along with Miles, Billy, Bessie,...
  - *The Post-American Cultural Congress.*
  - *Book of Days*, with Paul Blackburn

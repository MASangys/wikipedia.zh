**岩鹨科**（学名*Prunellidae*）是[鸟纲](../Page/鸟纲.md "wikilink")[雀形目中的一个](../Page/雀形目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科_\(生物\).md "wikilink")，其下仅有**岩鹨属**(*Prunella*)一属。分布在[欧洲和](../Page/欧洲.md "wikilink")[亚洲](../Page/亚洲.md "wikilink")，是[候鸟](../Page/候鸟.md "wikilink")。体型与[麻雀相似](../Page/麻雀.md "wikilink")，但关系较远。夏天以昆虫为食，冬天也吃种子和浆果。

包括以下几种：

  - [领岩鹨](../Page/领岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella collaris*
  - [高原岩鹨](../Page/高原岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella himalayana*
  - [鸲岩鹨](../Page/鸲岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella rubeculoides*
  - [棕胸岩鹨](../Page/棕胸岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella strophiata*
  - [棕眉山岩鹨](../Page/棕眉山岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella montanella*
  - [褐岩鹨](../Page/褐岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella fulvescens*
  - [拉氏岩鹨](../Page/拉氏岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella ocularis*
  - [黑喉岩鹨](../Page/黑喉岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella atrogularis*
  - [贺兰山岩鹨](../Page/贺兰山岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella koslowi*
  - [林岩鹨](../Page/林岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella modularis*
  - [日本岩鹨](../Page/日本岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella rubida*
  - [栗背岩鹨](../Page/栗背岩鹨.md "wikilink") *Prunella immaculata*

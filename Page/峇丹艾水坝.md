**峇丹艾水坝**(Batang Ai Dam)是[马来西亚](../Page/马来西亚.md "wikilink")[砂拉越](../Page/砂拉越.md "wikilink")第一個[水力發電站和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/水力發電站 "wikilink")[concrete-face rock-fill dam興建之處](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/concrete-face_rock-fill_dam "wikilink")，位于[Batang Ai National Park](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Batang_Ai_National_Park "wikilink")。The power station comprises four  [turbines](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/water_turbine "wikilink"), totalling the installed capacity to . The station is operated by [Sarawak Electricity Supply Corporation](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sarawak_Electricity_Supply_Corporation "wikilink"). Preparations for the dam began as early as 1975, before the design was published in 1977. Construction started 1982 with the river diversion work and the last turbine completed in 1985. The Batang Ai project, a relatively modest dam financed by the Asian Development Bank, caused the displacement of approximately 3,000 people from 26 longhouses. (These people have since been accommodated in the Batang Ai Resettlement Scheme to cultivate cocoa and rubber but the programme has not been successful.與建好後的蓄水湖環境優美而成了州內一旅遊景點．湖中央興建了一間星級度假酒店。湖內淡水魚養殖業極其發達。

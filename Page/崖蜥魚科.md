**崖蜥鱼科**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）又名**大鱗蜥魚科**，是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[仙女魚目的其中一](../Page/仙女魚目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**崖蜥魚科**其下分3個屬，如下：

  - 阿爾蜥燈魚屬(*Ahliesaurus*)
      - [貝氏阿爾蜥燈魚](../Page/貝氏阿爾蜥燈魚.md "wikilink")(*Ahliesaurus berryi*)
      - [短尾阿爾蜥燈魚](../Page/短尾阿爾蜥燈魚.md "wikilink")(*Ahliesaurus brevis*)
  - 光柱魚屬(*Luciosudis*)
      - [諾氏光柱魚](../Page/諾氏光柱魚.md "wikilink")(*Luciosudis normani*)
  - 弱蜥魚屬(*Scopelosaurus*)
      - [艾氏弱蜥魚](../Page/艾氏弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus adleri*)
      - [阿氏弱蜥魚](../Page/阿氏弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus ahlstromi*)
      - [銀弱蜥魚](../Page/銀弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus argenteus*)
      - [克氏弱蜥魚](../Page/克氏弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus craddocki*)
      - [吉布斯弱蜥魚](../Page/吉布斯弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus gibbsi*)
      - [漢氏弱蜥魚](../Page/漢氏弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus hamiltoni*)
      - [哈氏弱蜥魚](../Page/哈氏弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus harryi*)
      - [大眼弱蜥魚](../Page/大眼弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus herwigi*)
      - [霍氏弱蜥魚](../Page/霍氏弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus hoedti*)
      - [哈布斯弱蜥魚](../Page/哈布斯弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus hubbsi*)
      - [長胸弱蜥魚](../Page/長胸弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus lepidus*)
      - [莫氏弱蜥魚](../Page/莫氏弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus mauli*)
      - [米氏弱蜥魚](../Page/米氏弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus meadi*)
      - [史氏弱蜥魚](../Page/史氏弱蜥魚.md "wikilink")(*Scopelosaurus smithii*)

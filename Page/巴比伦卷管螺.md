**巴比倫捲管螺**（学名：**）是一個[海螺的物種](../Page/海螺.md "wikilink")，一種屬於[新腹足類支序的](../Page/新腹足類.md "wikilink")[捲管螺科](../Page/捲管螺科.md "wikilink")[捲管螺屬的](../Page/捲管螺屬.md "wikilink")[海洋](../Page/海洋生物.md "wikilink")[腹足綱](../Page/腹足綱.md "wikilink")[軟體動物](../Page/軟體動物.md "wikilink")。

## 分佈

本物種見於[西太平洋海域](../Page/西太平洋.md "wikilink")，北起[台湾](../Page/台湾.md "wikilink")、[菲律賓](../Page/菲律賓.md "wikilink")、[印度尼西亚](../Page/印度尼西亚.md "wikilink")、[東帝汶](../Page/東帝汶.md "wikilink")、[巴布亞新畿內亞到](../Page/巴布亞新畿內亞.md "wikilink")[所羅門群島](../Page/所羅門群島.md "wikilink")；以及[印度洋的](../Page/印度洋.md "wikilink")[毛里求斯海岸與](../Page/毛里求斯.md "wikilink")[马斯克林群岛](../Page/马斯克林群岛.md "wikilink")[海盆](../Page/海盆.md "wikilink")\[1\]\[2\]。

## 棲息地

這些熱帶的腹足綱物種常棲息於潮下帶的岩石及砂質海底\[3\]\[4\]。

## 型態描述

[_Turridae_-_Turris_babylonia.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:_Turridae_-_Turris_babylonia.jpg "fig:_Turridae_-_Turris_babylonia.jpg")
of *Turris babylonia* from Philippines\]\] The size of an adult shell
varies between 63 mm and 100 mm. \[5\] The shell shows somewhat angular
[whorls](../Page/Whorl_\(mollusc\).md "wikilink"), caused by the greater
prominence of one of the revolving ribs. Its
[sculpture](../Page/Sculpture_\(mollusc\).md "wikilink") shows large
revolving ribs, with intermediate raised lines. The color of the shell
is whitish, with large dark brown or nearly black spots upon the ribs.
\[6\]

## Biology

Embryos of *Turris babylonia* develop into free-swimming planktonic
marine larvae ([trocophore](../Page/trocophore.md "wikilink")) and later
into juvenile [veligers](../Page/veligers.md "wikilink"). Adults feed on
marine worms chased by means of their venom, similarly to the cone
snails. \[7\]\[8\]

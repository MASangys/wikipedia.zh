《**布兰妮：星踪可考**》（英语：*Britney: For the Record*），是美国女歌手[布兰妮·斯皮尔斯的一部纪录片](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/布兰妮·斯皮尔斯 "wikilink")，发行于布兰妮个人事业的低谷期后，讲述了布兰妮回归乐坛的事情。纪录片拍摄于2008年第3季度的[比佛利山和](../Page/比佛利山_\(加利福尼亞州\).md "wikilink")[纽约](../Page/纽约.md "wikilink")。主要部分开始拍摄于布兰妮在[2008年MTV音乐录像带大奖表演两日前](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2008年MTV音乐录像带大奖 "wikilink")（2008年9月5日）。本纪录片由[菲尔·葛瑞芬导演](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/菲尔·葛瑞芬_\(导演\) "wikilink")。[MTV于](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/MTV "wikilink")2008年10月9日发布了纪录片的预告片。

《布兰妮：星踪可考》于2008年11月30日（布兰妮第6张录音室专辑《[妮裳马戏团](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/妮裳马戏团 "wikilink")》发行前两日）在MTV首播，作为专辑宣传的一部分。纪录片获得了褒贬不一的评价，但获得了较高的收视率和粉丝的好评。\[1\]

## 评价

Critical reaction to the documentary was mixed. *[Los Angeles Times](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Los_Angeles_Times "wikilink")* gave the film a positive reaction calling the film "an unstoppable documentary, Spears can finally tell her side of the story without periods, or commas".\[6\] [MSNBC](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/MSNBC "wikilink") was negative in its review, writing that Spears provided "very little insight about her life, her decision-making, or her mistakes", and that Spears responses were "vague, occasionally contradictory". MSNBC concluded that "All that *Britney: For the Record* leaves us with, then, is a faint portrait of a 26-year-old whose life is unlike any other 26-year-old's, even though she wants to convince herself otherwise".\[7\] The *[Hartford Courant](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hartford_Courant "wikilink")* criticized the documentary for being "an [infomercial](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/infomercial "wikilink") about rehabilitating the image of Britney Spears." The review would go on to note "'No topic was off limits,' insisted a title at the beginning of the film, which explained that MTV cameras got to follow her for 60 days this fall. 'No question went unanswered.' But of course not every question was asked in the first place.\[8\] Australian newspaper *[The Age](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Age "wikilink")* described it as "nothing more than a miserable, anxious limp through an entirely unsteady 'comeback'.\[9\]

## 收视率

[MTV](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/MTV "wikilink") reported that "Britney: For the Record" accumulated the largest average viewers on a regular Sunday with averaged 3.7 million viewers, per the network.\[10\]\[11\] Combined with the [rerun](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/rerun "wikilink") immediately after the first showing, it received 5.6 million viewers overall. The documentary received three times more viewers than various MTV series including their number one hit series *[The Hills](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hills "wikilink")*.\[12\] In Australia, the primetime broadcast on [Network Ten](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Network_Ten "wikilink") attracted a national audience of 1.42 million viewers, which equates to just under 7% of the entire country's population. The documentary, as such, became the fourth most watched programme of the week. In the United Kingdom, the broadcast received 391,000 viewers and 1.8% share. Moreover, it also received 4.6% amongst adults 16-34 and 5.1% among women 16-34. Aside from a repeat of *[Doctor Who](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doctor_Who "wikilink")* on [BBC Three](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/BBC_Three "wikilink"), "Britney: For the Record" was the highest rated multichannel (cable) show for the night (December 1, 2008).\[13\] According [Televisa Views](../Page/Televisa.md "wikilink") in Mexico the documentary scored the number one spot two nights in a row, on its debut night the documentary was viewed by more than 2.3 million viewers and on its second night it scored 2 million for a total of 4.3 millions views in the two showings, the documentary beat *[Desperate Housewives](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Desperate_Housewives "wikilink")* for the Sunday showing in Mexico by 1.25 million.

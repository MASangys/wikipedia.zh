## 恆等式

\[a\Rightarrow b = \lnot a \lor b\]

\[a\Leftrightarrow b = \lnot a \lor b\]

\[a\oplus b = \lnot a\cdot b \lor a\cdot\lnot b\]

\[a\oplus 1 = \lnot a\]

## 布林函數恆等式

\[x_{i}^{\sigma_{i}}=\begin{cases}x_{i}\;,\sigma_{i}=1\;,\\ \lnot x_{i}\;,\sigma_{i}=0\;,\end{cases}x_{i}\!\in\{0,1\}\]

**希瑟·安·奈特**（英語：Heather Ann
Nauert，1970年1月27日－）是美国记者和政府官员，自2017年4月24日起擔任[美国国务院发言人](../Page/美国国务院.md "wikilink")\[1\]。她是前[ABC新聞的新闻记者以及](../Page/ABC新聞.md "wikilink")[福斯新聞頻道](../Page/福斯新聞頻道.md "wikilink")《福克斯和朋友》前[新聞主播](../Page/新聞主播.md "wikilink")。她还在2018年3月至10月代理副国务卿（公共外交和公共事务）。

## 早期生活

奈特出生於[伊利諾伊州的](../Page/伊利诺伊州.md "wikilink")[罗克福德](../Page/罗克福德.md "wikilink")\[2\]。其父彼得·奈特曾經是保險業界的行政人員。奈特有三名兄弟：賈斯汀、喬納森和約瑟夫\[3\]。

奈特在[罗克福德上日校](../Page/罗克福德.md "wikilink")，再到[麻省](../Page/麻薩諸塞州.md "wikilink")[栗树山的](../Page/栗树山_\(马萨诸塞州\).md "wikilink")，之後到[亞利桑那州立大學上學](../Page/亞利桑那州立大學.md "wikilink")。After
landing an internship hosting a country music video program in
Washington, DC, she stayed there to finish
school，最終在畢業並獲得[文學士的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/文学士 "wikilink")[通信學位](../Page/通信.md "wikilink")。之後在[哥倫比亞大學獲得](../Page/哥伦比亚大学.md "wikilink")[新聞學](../Page/新闻学.md "wikilink")[文學碩士](../Page/文學碩士.md "wikilink")\[4\]。

## 職業生涯

### 記者

In 1996, Nauert was a reporter for the syndicated weekly business
program *First Business*. She worked for [Fox
News](../Page/福斯新聞頻道.md "wikilink") from 1998 to 2005, first
as a contributor for three years and then as a correspondent for four
years. During her time as a correspondent, she regularly contributed to
*The Big Story*.\[5\]

From 2005 to 2007, Nauert held positions at several news organizations,
including [ABC News](../Page/ABC新聞.md "wikilink") as a general
assignment correspondent, where she contributed to *[ABC World News
Tonight](../Page/ABC世界新聞.md "wikilink")*, *[Good Morning
America](../Page/早安美國.md "wikilink")*, and
*[Nightline](../Page/夜線.md "wikilink")*. While at ABC, she was
nominated for an [Emmy Award](../Page/艾美奖.md "wikilink") for her work on
the special series *13 Around the World*.

In 2007, Nauert returned to Fox News as co-host with John Gibson of the
weekday edition of *The Big Story*.

Nauert also co-anchored the newscasts *Good Day Early Call* and *Good
Day New York Wake Up* with co-anchor Steve Lacy weekday mornings for
[Fox Broadcasting Company](../Page/福斯廣播公司.md "wikilink")
owned-and-operated station WNYW in [New York
City](../Page/纽约.md "wikilink"). In October 2012, Nauert left *Good
Day Wake Up* and became a [news presenter](../Page/新聞主播.md "wikilink")
for *Fox & Friends*.\[6\]\[7\]

### 顧問

Nauert has also worked as a [government](../Page/政府.md "wikilink")
affairs consultant on issues including [health
insurance](../Page/醫療保險.md "wikilink"), [Social
Security](../Page/美国社会安全保险.md "wikilink") and
taxes。奈特亦是[美国外交关系协会會員](../Page/美国外交关系协会.md "wikilink")。

### 美國國務院

[缩略图](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Heather_Nauert_Regional_Media_Day.png "fig:缩略图")。\]\]
[缩略图](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Secretary_Pompeo_Participates_in_a_Bilateral_Meeting_With_Foreign_Minister_Wang_Yi_\(28921695728\).jpg "fig:缩略图")與中國國務委員[王毅舉行會面](../Page/王毅.md "wikilink")。攝於2018年6月14日，中國北京。\]\]
On April 24, 2017, the [United States Department of
State](../Page/美国国务院.md "wikilink") announced that Nauert
would be the new State Department spokesperson, her first role in
government.\[8\]She held her first [press
briefing](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/記者會 "wikilink") in that role
five weeks later, on June 6, 2017.\[9\]Following the dismissal of Steve
Goldstein on March 13, 2018, Nauert was named acting Under Secretary of
State for Public Diplomacy and Public Affairs.\[10\]

In April 2018, Nauert voiced support for [Saudi Arabian-led intervention
in Yemen](../Page/沙特领导的干预也门行动.md "wikilink"). She also condemned "Iran’s
malign influence" in [Yemen](../Page/也门.md "wikilink").\[11\]In May
2018, Nauert said in response to the [Gaza border
protests](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2018年加沙邊境抗議事件 "wikilink"): "We
oppose actions against Israel at the International Criminal Court (...)
because it does not help the cause for peace."\[12\]

In July 2018, Nauert stirred up controversy when citing the [Invasion of
Normandy](../Page/诺曼底战役.md "wikilink") as evidence of a strong
relationship between the United States and
[Germany](../Page/德国.md "wikilink") on the anniversary of the D Day
invasion.\[13\]

In August 2018, [Canada](../Page/加拿大.md "wikilink") called for the
immediate release of Saudi [human rights](../Page/人权.md "wikilink")
activist Raif Badawi and his sister, Samar Badawi. In response to
Canada's criticism, Saudi Arabia expelled the Ambassador of Canada and
froze trade with Canada, leading to a decline in Canada–Saudi Arabia
relations.\[14\]Nauert said: "It is up for the Government of Saudi
Arabia and the Canadians to work this out. Both sides need to
diplomatically resolve this together. We cannot do it for them."\[15\]

On November 1, 2018, it was reported that President Trump offered Nauert
the position of [United States Ambassador to the United
Nations](../Page/美国常驻联合国代表列表.md "wikilink") to replace the outgoing
officeholder, [Nikki
Haley](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/妮基·黑利 "wikilink"). Sources
reported that Trump previously had former presidential advisor Dina
Powell on the top of his list of people under consideration for the
position before she withdrew her name.\[16\]

### 美國駐聯合國大使

2018年12月6日，多個媒體報道指總統特朗普將會任命奈特為下任[美国常驻联合国代表](../Page/美国常驻联合国代表列表.md "wikilink")\[17\]。特朗普向記者表示奈特是一個「出色的人」，「長期支持我的施政」\[18\]。

## 個人生活

奈特的丈夫為[摩根士丹利私人信貸和股權執行董事史葛](../Page/摩根士丹利.md "wikilink")·諾比，諾比曾經在國家獸醫協會、[瑞銀集團](../Page/瑞银集团.md "wikilink")、[高盛和](../Page/高盛.md "wikilink")[嘉古公司工作](../Page/嘉吉公司.md "wikilink")。二人育有兩個兒子。

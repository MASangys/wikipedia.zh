**帚尾袋貂屬**是屬於[袋貂科的](../Page/袋貂科.md "wikilink")[有袋類動物](../Page/有袋類.md "wikilink")，包含如下物種：

  - [北帚尾袋貂](../Page/北帚尾袋貂.md "wikilink") *Trichosurus arnhemensis*
  - [短耳帚尾袋貂](../Page/短耳帚尾袋貂.md "wikilink") *Trichosurus caninus*
  - [山帚尾袋貂](../Page/山帚尾袋貂.md "wikilink") *Trichosurus cunninghami*
  - [銅色帚尾袋貂](../Page/銅色帚尾袋貂.md "wikilink") *Trichosurus johnstonii*
  - [帚尾袋貂](../Page/帚尾袋貂.md "wikilink") *Trichosurus vulpecula*

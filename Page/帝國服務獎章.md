**帝國服務獎章**（，簡稱ISM」）是一枚附属于[帝國服務勳章](../Page/帝國服務勳章.md "wikilink")的奖章。该奖章是[爱德华七世](../Page/爱德华七世.md "wikilink")于1902年8月8日根据帝国服务令章程设立。\[1\]它颁发给退休时完成至少25年服务的选定公务员。

## 外形

When originally created the Imperial Service Medal was a seven-pointed star, or a laurel wreath for women, in the same pattern as the Imperial Service Order, but with the star or laurel in bronze.\[2\] In 1920 an amendment of the statutes changed the appearance of the medal to its current form of as a circular silver medal,  in diameter, bearing the effigy of the reigning sovereign on the obverse.\[3\] To date, here have been six versions:\[4\]

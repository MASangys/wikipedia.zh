*' 广义耦合KdV方程组*'(Generalized coupled KdV equations)是一个三元非线性偏微分方程组：\[1\]

\(U_{t}-0.25*U_{xxx}-3*U*U_{x}-3*(W-V^2)_{x}=0,\)

\(V_{t}+0.5*V_{xxx}+3*U*V_{x}=0,\)

\(W_{t}+0.5*W_{xxx}+3*U*W_{x}=0\)

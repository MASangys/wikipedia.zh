**开放新拟钉螺**（學名：**）是[新拟钉螺属](../Page/新拟钉螺属.md "wikilink")的其中一個物種，屬於[盖螺科](../Page/盖螺科.md "wikilink")的[淡水生](../Page/水生動物.md "wikilink")[腹足纲](../Page/腹足纲.md "wikilink")[软体动物](../Page/软体动物.md "wikilink")。 過往這個物種是本屬唯一的一個物種；但現時此屬已有至少兩個已描述的物種。

## 形態描述

A detailed account of the anatomy of *Neotricula aperta* was given by Davis et al. in 1976.\[6\]

Three strains of *Neotricula aperta* have been recognized (called α, β and γ), on the basis of shell size and body pigmentation.\[7\] Although all three strains are susceptible to ** (γ\> \> β \> α), only the γ-strain is epidemiologically significant.\[8\]

## 生態學

*Neotricula aperta* is exclusively [岩生植物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/岩生植物 "wikilink") or [epixylic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/epixylic "wikilink") (on rotting wood).\[9\] *Neotricula aperta* is found only in shallow areas (typically 0.5 to 3 m deep) of the Mekong river and some of its tributaries.\[10\] The snails are restricted to areas where the current is moderate (around 2 × 10<sup>3</sup> m<sup>3</sup>s<sup>−1</sup>), the water is clear and the bed rock forms (almost flat) platforms where agal [aufwuchs](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/aufwuch "wikilink") is extensive.\[11\] Such conditions exist only during the [旱季](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/旱季 "wikilink") in the lower Mekong (March to May) and so *Neotricula aperta* populations persist mostly by recruitment (from eggs laid on stones in the previous year) or re-colonization from other rivers, and transmission of ** is seasonal.\[12\] [人口密度](../Page/人口密度.md "wikilink") can reach up to 4734 snails per m².\[13\]

In addition, *Neotricula aperta* is not known from waters of low [電導率](../Page/電導率.md "wikilink") or pH and is [calciphilic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/calciphilic "wikilink"); the pH of all *Neotricula aperta* habitats sampled to date is \> 7.5 and the river systems in which the snails are found have always been those draining [喀斯特地形](../Page/喀斯特地形.md "wikilink") areas.\[14\] More recently, *Neotricula aperta* has been found in the primary streams emerging from karst springs, close to the origins of the streams.\[15\] The snail *Neotricula aperta* requires hard water to provide the [钙](../Page/钙.md "wikilink") needed for rapid [手性 (腹足綱)](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/手性_\(腹足綱\) "wikilink") growth as populations re-establish in the Mekong river each year following the annual flood.\[16\]

The ecological requirements of *Neotricula aperta* indicate that this snail will not become established in the reservoirs or downstream channels of dams in the region and that flooding of habitats by impoundment will eliminate all *Neotricula aperta* populations from the affected area.\[17\]

*Neotricula aperta* grazes the algal [epilithon](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/epilithon "wikilink") and therefore, unlike species such as ** (African vector of other kind of schistosomiasis), cannot survive in areas where sediment is depositing and preventing the growth of the algae upon which it feeds.\[18\] Indeed, ecological studies of *Neotricula aperta* have shown that this snail is found only on stones covered with fine sediments and that this species is highly sensitive to .\[19\]

**弗朗西斯·惠恩**（，生於1957年1月22日）是[英國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/英國 "wikilink")[記者](../Page/記者.md "wikilink")、[作家和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/作家 "wikilink")[播音員](../Page/播音員.md "wikilink")。

## 著作

  - *The Sixties* (1982) ISBN 0-7126-0018-3
  - *Television: A History* (1984) ISBN 0-7126-0929-6
  - *Battle for London* (1985) ISBN 0-7453-0054-5
  - *[Tom Driberg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tom_Driberg "wikilink"): His Life and Indiscretions* (1990) ISBN 0-7011-3143-8
  - *The Chatto Book of Cats (Chatto Anthologies)* Francis Wheen, editor, John O'Connor, illustrator (1993) ISBN 0-7011-4005-4
  - *Lord Gnome's Literary Companion* (1994) ISBN 1-85984-945-8
  - *Karl Marx* (1999) ISBN 1-85702-637-3
  - *Who Was [Dr. Charlotte Bach](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karoly_Hajdu "wikilink")?* (2002) ISBN 1-904095-39-9
  - *Hoo-hahs and Passing Frenzies: Collected Journalism, 1991–2001* (2002) ISBN 1-903809-42-8 (mainly consisting of columns written for *The Guardian*)
  - *The Irresistible Con: The Bizarre Life of a Fraudulent Genius* (2004) ISBN 1-904095-74-7
  - *Shooting Out the Lights* (2004) ISBN 0-00-714943-3
  - *[How Mumbo-Jumbo Conquered the World](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/How_Mumbo-Jumbo_Conquered_the_World "wikilink")* (2004) ISBN 0-00-714096-7; in the USA and Canada: *Idiot Proof: A Short History of Modern Delusions* (2004) ISBN 1-58648-247-5
  - *Marx's [Das Kapital](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Das_Kapital "wikilink"): A Biography* (2006) ISBN 978-1-84354-400-5
  - *Strange Days Indeed: The Golden Age of Paranoia* (2009) ISBN 978-0-00-724427-0

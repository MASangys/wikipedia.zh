**後貝綿蟹屬**（學名：）为[貝綿蟹科下的一个屬](../Page/貝綿蟹科.md "wikilink")，也是**後貝綿蟹亞科**（）之下的唯一一個屬\[1\]。本屬的模式種是*[Dynomene
devaneyi](../Page/Dynomene_devaneyi.md "wikilink")*
<small>[Takeda](../Page/Takeda.md "wikilink"), 1977</small>\[2\]。

## 物種

本屬有四個物種\[3\]\[4\]：

  - *[Metadynomene
    crosnieri](../Page/Metadynomene_crosnieri.md "wikilink")*
    <small>McLay, 1999</small>
  - *[Metadynomene
    devaneyi](../Page/Metadynomene_devaneyi.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Takeda, 1977)</small>
  - [種島後貝綿蟹](../Page/種島後貝綿蟹.md "wikilink") *Metadynomene tanensis*
    <small>(Yokoya, 1933)</small>\[5\]
  - *[Metadynomene tuamotu](../Page/Metadynomene_tuamotu.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Ng & McLay, 2010</small>

**德克萨斯中央铁路**是一條由Texas Central Partners, LLC規劃的[高速鐵路](../Page/高速鐵路.md "wikilink")線並將串聯德州最大的兩個都市 [達拉斯–沃斯堡都會區以及](../Page/德州三角地帶.md "wikilink")[休士頓](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/休士頓 "wikilink")。該鐵路將運用 [東海旅客鐵道](../Page/東海旅客鐵道.md "wikilink")\[1\] 的相關技術並將縮短兩地之間的旅程到僅有90分鐘。\[2\]\[3\] 倘若該鐵道順利完成建設的話將會是繼 [Acela特快之後](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Acela特快 "wikilink")[美國高速鐵路以及第一條具有專用路軌的美國高速鐵路](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/美國第二條高速鐵路 "wikilink")。

## 路線

2015年8月10日，美國美國運輸部和聯邦鐵路總署出具一份報告並正式批准這條稱之為"utility corridor"的鐵路興建計畫。\[4\]

該路線的路軌將大致與290號公路--Hempstead Highway以及以一條貨運鐵道平行的模式進入休士頓市區。除了頭尾兩站之外原先還預計將在布拉索斯郡設站以服務鄰近的[德克薩斯州農工大學但遭到當地市政府反對](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/德克薩斯州農工大學 "wikilink")。

2018年1月，該公司正式公布達拉斯站選站計畫並將在西達斯郡西側設立終點站以及一座連接達拉斯會議中心的行人穿越道，而該計畫也是依據聯邦鐵路總署依據環境影響評估報告建議的位置進行規劃。

## 使用車種

該路線將選用出口版的[新幹線N700系電聯車](../Page/新幹線N700系電聯車.md "wikilink")作為營運車輛。\[5\] 該車輛與日本新幹線不一樣的是將僅有八節車廂而非十六節車廂以符合營運需求。\[6\]

## 承包商與建設計畫

2017年1月，川普政府將該計畫列為國家基礎建設優先計畫之一。\[7\]

In June 2017, it was stated that construction would begin in 2018 and would support 10,000 jobs during each year of the construction process and 1,000 jobs in each year after that.\[8\] Also in June 2017, the company announced that it had reached agreement with 福陸公司to advance the preliminary development of the project. Fluor will support the project during the preliminary development by refining the project’s engineering, design-build cost estimate, schedule and construction planning, and potentially further phases of development. Fluor and its partners would be the preferred [design-builder](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Design–build "wikilink") of the project.\[9\] On September 13 2018, the company earned a $300 million loan for permitting, design and engineering.\[10\] The company selected [Salini Impregilo](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salini_Impregilo "wikilink") to lead the civil construction consortium that will build the Texas passenger line, to the top of the rails, including viaducts, embankments and drainage. Construction is now slated to begin in 2019 and end in 2024.\[11\]\[12\]\[13\]

the Federal permitting dashboard shows that the date for the Record of Decision is March 27, 2020.\[14\] Since construction cannot start until the completion of the NEPA process, it would appear that earliest work could start would be late 2019 or early 2020 and would take up to 6 years.\[15\]\[16\] the company currently has 30% of needed land acquisitions needed for construction.\[17\]

## 法律爭議

In a December 2016 summary judgement, a Harris County court ruled that the Texas Central Railroad does not have the power to require private landowners to allow their land to be surveyed for possible future eminent domain purchases. The case, *Texas central Railroad and Infrastructure vs Calvin House*, was prosecuted by the railroad against a landowner that refused to allow survey crews onto his land.\[18\]

In 2017, Grimes County won an injunction against Texas Central Railroad that prevents the company from performing any surveys that damage or alter any county roads or right-of-ways.\[19\]

In February 2019, a Leon County district judge ruled that Texas Central is not a railroad company and therefore does not have the right to conduct surveys on private land.\[20\]

## 營運進度

目前該公司還在就依據[國家環境政策法規定須進行的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/國家環境政策法 "wikilink")[環境影響評估細節與](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/環境影響評估 "wikilink")[聯邦鐵路總署](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/聯邦鐵路總署 "wikilink")、[德克薩斯州運輸部等政府部門進行後續作業程序](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/德克薩斯州運輸部 "wikilink")。\[21\]不過就初期的人事規劃來看，[執行長Tim](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/執行長 "wikilink") Keith將負責該公司的財政、開發、營運以及日常運轉。

2015年7月，該公司表示已經獲得7500萬美元的私募基金並將有助於該公司持續推進該公司經營但資金來源為何尚屬未知。\[22\]

In December 2015, the company announced that it had appointed three new executives to help develop the project, all of whom will report to Tim Keith:\[23\]

  - Managing Director, External Affairs – Holly Reed. She was previously Regional vice president of external affairs for AT\&T.
  - Chief Finance Officer (CFO) – Lori Willox. She is a certified public accountant, and was previously a Senior vice president and CFO for [Balfour Beatty](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Balfour_Beatty "wikilink")’s Central region.\[24\]
  - Managing Director, Design-Build Program – Doug Jones. He has been in the construction industry for 38 years and was previously with Balfour Beatty Construction in Dallas.

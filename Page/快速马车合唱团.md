**快速马车合唱团(REO Speedwagon)**是美国1970-80年代活跃的摇滚乐队。他们广受好评的歌曲有："[Keep On
Loving You](../Page/Keep_On_Loving_You.md "wikilink")", "[Can't Fight
This Feeling](../Page/Can't_Fight_This_Feeling.md "wikilink")"等等。

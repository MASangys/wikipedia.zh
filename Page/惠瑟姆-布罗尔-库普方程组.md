**惠瑟姆-布罗尔-库普方程组**（Whitham-Broer-Kaup equation）是非线性偏微分方程组\[1\]

\[u_{t}+uu_{x}+v_{x}+\beta*u_{xx}=0\]

\[v_{t}+(v*u)_{x}+\alpha*u_{xxx}=0\]

《**憂鬱的解剖**》（；全稱：*The Anatomy of Melancholy, What it is: With all the
Kinds, Causes, Symptomes, Prognostickes, and Several Cures of it. In
Three Maine Partitions with their several Sections, Members, and
Subsections. Philosophically, Medicinally, Historically, Opened and Cut
Up*）一書是由在[牛津大學裡](../Page/牛津大學.md "wikilink")[牧師兼](../Page/牧師.md "wikilink")[學者的](../Page/學者.md "wikilink")[羅伯特·伯頓撰寫](../Page/羅伯特·伯頓.md "wikilink")
，於1621年首次出版。

## 概述

這本書從封面外表來看，不過就是一本探討[憂鬱症的](../Page/憂鬱症.md "wikilink")[醫學](../Page/醫學.md "wikilink")[教科書](../Page/教科書.md "wikilink")（其中還包括現在被稱為的[臨床抑鬱症](../Page/臨床抑鬱症.md "wikilink")）。雖然作為一個[醫生介紹的文字](../Page/醫生.md "wikilink")，《憂鬱的解剖》其實是一本相當特殊的[文學作品](../Page/文學.md "wikilink")，因為其內容以[哲學和](../Page/哲學.md "wikilink")[科學居多](../Page/科學.md "wikilink")，往往超越該書探討的主題。而且此書所謂的「憂鬱」和「解剖」其實是用憂鬱的筆調來剖析[人類所有情感和思想表達方式](../Page/人類.md "wikilink")。

羅伯特伯頓把這本書的序言就寫的帶有諷刺的意味，伯頓的人物德謨克利特少年解釋說：「我寫的是繁忙的憂鬱，以避免憂鬱。『剖析』是一個範圍廣泛的文件，其中載有離題和評論。無論其強項，醫療文本或作為一個歷史文件，它是解剖的巨大廣度-解決話題，如消化，小妖精，地理美洲等
和聲音特色，特別是它的作者是最普遍提及其崇拜者為主要來源的吸引力。 」

## 出版

伯頓的一生中將《憂鬱的解剖》修定和擴充了五次，並經常再版，最近的一次再版是在2001年依據1932年出版的版本重印。此次再版（2001年）和伯頓首次出版年分1621年相差380年。

## 內容簡介

伯頓定義他的主題如下：

> *Melancholy*, the subject of our present discourse, is either in
> disposition or in [habit](../Page/Habit_\(psychology\).md "wikilink").
> In disposition, is that transitory *Melancholy* which goes and comes
> upon every small occasion of sorrow, need, sickness, trouble, fear,
> grief, passion, or perturbation of the mind, any manner of care,
> discontent, or thought, which causes anguish, dulness, heaviness and
> vexation of spirit, any ways opposite to pleasure, mirth, joy,
> delight, causing frowardness in us, or a dislike. In which equivocal
> and improper sense, we call him melancholy, that is dull, sad, sour,
> lumpish, ill-disposed, solitary, any way moved, or displeased. And
> from these melancholy dispositions no man living is free, no Stoick,
> none so wise, none so happy, none so patient, so generous, so godly,
> so divine, that can vindicate himself; so well-composed, but more or
> less, some time or other, he feels the smart of it. Melancholy in this
> sense is the character of Mortality. . . . This *Melancholy* of which
> we are to treat, is a habit, a serious ailment, a settled
> [humour](../Page/The_four_humours.md "wikilink"), as Aurelianus and
> others call it, not errant, but fixed: and as it was long increasing,
> so, now being (pleasant or painful) grown to a habit, it will hardly
> be removed.

《憂鬱的解剖》是一個特別[長的](../Page/長.md "wikilink")[書](../Page/書.md "wikilink")，第一版是[四開量近](../Page/四開.md "wikilink")[900](../Page/900.md "wikilink")[頁](../Page/頁.md "wikilink")，後來的版本甚至更長。該文本共分為三個主要部分，另外還有ㄧ序言，序言整個寫出伯頓的廣闊的風格。

## 崇拜者

此書從首刷到現在累積了不少贊同伯頓理念的崇拜者，從古到今的崇拜者有：塞繆爾約翰遜、勞倫斯斯特恩、查爾斯蘭姆和約翰濟慈、菲利浦普爾曼、豪爾赫路易斯、博爾赫斯尼克洞和塞繆爾貝克特
等。

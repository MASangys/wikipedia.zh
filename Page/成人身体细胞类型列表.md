[人体内有数百种特异的](../Page/人体.md "wikilink")[细胞类型](../Page/细胞.md "wikilink")\[1\]\[2\]<ref>

Vickaryous MK, Hall BK, *Human cell type diversity, evolution,
development, and classification with special reference to cells derived
from the neural crest*, BIOLOGICAL REVIEWS, Volume: 81, Pages: 425-455,
2006 </ref>，总共有多于一百兆的细胞。

细胞类型可以依照其组织来源进行分类。然而，某些细胞的行为可能是受其外周组织影响的。

**桶头勋爵**（英文：Lord Buckethead） 是一名英国。这名角色在三次[英国大选](../Page/英国大选.md "wikilink")中分别和三名不同的[保守党党魁同台竞选](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/保守党党魁_\(英国\) "wikilink")[英国首相](../Page/英国首相.md "wikilink")职务。\[1\]  Representing the Gremloids, a [frivolous political party](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_frivolous_political_parties "wikilink"), he ran against [Margaret Thatcher](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Margaret_Thatcher "wikilink") for parliament in [Finchley](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finchley_\(UK_Parliament_constituency\)#Elections_in_the_1980s "wikilink") in 1987, against [John Major](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Major "wikilink") in [Huntingdon](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Huntingdon_\(UK_Parliament_constituency\)#Elections_in_the_1990s "wikilink") in 1992, and against [Theresa May](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theresa_May "wikilink") in [Maidenhead](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maidenhead_\(UK_Parliament_constituency\)#Elections_in_the_2010s "wikilink") in 2017.

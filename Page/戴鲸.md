**戴鲸**（），[字](../Page/表字.md "wikilink")**時鳴**，[號](../Page/號.md "wikilink")**南江**，[浙江](../Page/浙江.md "wikilink")[鄞县人](../Page/鄞县.md "wikilink")，[明朝政治人物](../Page/明朝.md "wikilink")、学者，同[進士出身](../Page/進士.md "wikilink")。

## 生平

[嘉靖二年](../Page/嘉靖.md "wikilink")（1523年）登癸未科[进士](../Page/进士.md "wikilink")，任[广东](../Page/广东.md "wikilink")[番禺县](../Page/番禺县.md "wikilink")[知县](../Page/知县.md "wikilink")。升任[湖南佥宪](../Page/湖南.md "wikilink")[提督](../Page/提督.md "wikilink")\[1\]。历任[工部](../Page/工部.md "wikilink")、[刑部](../Page/刑部.md "wikilink")[员外郎](../Page/员外郎.md "wikilink")。出任[江西](../Page/江西.md "wikilink")[按察](../Page/按察.md "wikilink")[佥事](../Page/佥事.md "wikilink")。谪任[邓州](../Page/邓州.md "wikilink")[通判](../Page/通判.md "wikilink")，终[福建](../Page/福建.md "wikilink")[左参议](../Page/左参议.md "wikilink")。[致仕归乡后致力于学术著述](../Page/致仕.md "wikilink")\[2\]。

## 家族

祖父[戴浩](../Page/戴浩.md "wikilink")，[字](../Page/表字.md "wikilink")**彥廣**，[號](../Page/號.md "wikilink")**默菴**，[永乐](../Page/永乐.md "wikilink")[举人](../Page/举人.md "wikilink")，曾任[雷州](../Page/雷州.md "wikilink")、[永州](../Page/永州.md "wikilink")[知州](../Page/知州.md "wikilink")。父戴槚，[字](../Page/表字.md "wikilink")**育之**，[號](../Page/號.md "wikilink")**茂軒**，为[儒学](../Page/儒学.md "wikilink")[教谕](../Page/教谕.md "wikilink")\[3\]。戴鲸與兄[戴鰲](../Page/戴鰲.md "wikilink")、弟[戴鱀](../Page/戴鱀.md "wikilink")、[戴䲀俱登](../Page/戴䲀.md "wikilink")[黃甲](../Page/黃甲.md "wikilink")，为[明朝罕有的](../Page/明朝.md "wikilink")“一门兄弟四进士”\[4\]。

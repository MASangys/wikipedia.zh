**打石湖村**（[da3](http://www.chineselanguage.org/dictionaries/ccdict/sounds/hakka/siyan/ta31.wav)[sak8](http://www.chineselanguage.org/dictionaries/ccdict/sounds/hakka/siyan/sak5.wav)[fu2](http://www.chineselanguage.org/dictionaries/ccdict/sounds/hakka/siyan/fu11.wav)[cun1](http://www.chineselanguage.org/dictionaries/ccdict/sounds/hakka/siyan/tshun24.wav)）是位於[香港](../Page/香港.md "wikilink")[新界](../Page/新界.md "wikilink")[元朗區](../Page/元朗區.md "wikilink")[八鄉的](../Page/八鄉.md "wikilink")[原居民村落](../Page/新界原居民.md "wikilink")，居民主要為[張氏與](../Page/張.md "wikilink")[宋氏之](../Page/宋.md "wikilink")[客家人](../Page/客家人.md "wikilink")。

宋張兩氏百多年前由[嘉應](../Page/嘉應.md "wikilink")[五華遷來](../Page/五華.md "wikilink")[寶安](../Page/寶安.md "wikilink")，最先於南[大刀屻向西山坡](../Page/大刀屻.md "wikilink")[牛牯角立村搭](../Page/牛牯角.md "wikilink")[茅寮而居](../Page/茅寮.md "wikilink")（落擔者字派為「達」），後[人丁繁衍](../Page/人丁.md "wikilink")，且[山上](../Page/山.md "wikilink")[耕地不足](../Page/耕地.md "wikilink")，宋張兩氏經商討後，遂決定沿[擔水坑舉村遷到今晡日](../Page/擔水坑.md "wikilink")（[客家話](../Page/客家話.md "wikilink")：今天）之打石湖新村，張氏更分支到[八鄉](../Page/八鄉.md "wikilink")[河背附近的](../Page/河背.md "wikilink")[大窩](../Page/大窩.md "wikilink")、[蓮花地](../Page/蓮花地.md "wikilink")；另外[宋氏分支到位於](../Page/宋.md "wikilink")[蕉徑河谷附近現之](../Page/蕉徑河谷.md "wikilink")[蓮塘尾村定居](../Page/蓮塘尾.md "wikilink")，與同樣[先祖兩百年前來自](../Page/先祖.md "wikilink")[嘉應](../Page/嘉應.md "wikilink")[五華之](../Page/五華.md "wikilink")[長壢村](../Page/長壢.md "wikilink")（[沙頭角](../Page/沙頭角.md "wikilink")[凹下村分支](../Page/凹下.md "wikilink")）[魏氏客家](../Page/魏氏.md "wikilink")[村民隔](../Page/村民.md "wikilink")[雙魚河為毗鄰相望](../Page/雙魚河.md "wikilink")。<small>\[1\]</small>

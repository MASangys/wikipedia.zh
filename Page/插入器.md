**插入器**（學名：**），又名**交尾刺**、**中葉突**，**陽莖器**等，是[雄性](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/雄性 "wikilink")[節肢動物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/節肢動物 "wikilink") (主要是[昆蟲](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/昆蟲 "wikilink")）的[生殖器](../Page/生殖器.md "wikilink")，與雌性動物[交配](../Page/交配.md "wikilink")時通過插入器來分泌由[睪丸產生的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/睪丸 "wikilink")[精子](../Page/精子.md "wikilink")。這個器官可以類比[哺乳類動物的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/哺乳類 "wikilink")[陰莖](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/陰莖 "wikilink")，儘管插入器的構造其實更為複雜。部分昆蟲的插入器有莢包裹，就好像陰莖的[包皮](../Page/包皮.md "wikilink")那樣，但不叫「陰莖」，而是叫作「陽莖」。

## 語源

「插入器」的學名「**」源於[古希臘語的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/古希臘語 "wikilink")（*aidos*）或（*aidoion*）、眾數作（*aidoia*）。

## 解剖構造

插入器是雄性昆蟲腹部的一部分，即其整個身體三個主要部分的最後方的部分。插入器有生殖管連接往其體內的一對睪丸。插入器是雄性昆蟲的陽莖的部分，其構造複雜，而且不同物種間會有不同的排列方式。 complex and often species-specific arrangement of more or less sclerotized (hardened) flaps and hooks which also includes in some species the [valvae](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/valva "wikilink") (clasper), which are paired organs which help the male hold on to the female during copulation. During copulation, the aedeagus connects with the [ovipore](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ovipore "wikilink") of a female. The aedeagus can be quite pronounced or *de minimis*.

The base of the aedeagus may be the partially sclerotized phallotheca, also called the phallosoma or theca. In some species the phallotheca contains a space, called the endosoma (internal holding pouch), into which the tip end of the aedeagus may be withdrawn (retracted). The vas deferens is sometimes drawn into (folded into) the phallotheca together with a seminal vesicle.\[1\]\[2\]

[节肢动物](../Page/节肢动物.md "wikilink")的精子往雌蟲傳送時不會以液態 with free-swimming [spermatozoa傳送](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/spermatozoa "wikilink")，而是contains capsules called [spermatophores](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/spermatophore "wikilink") in which the actual spermatozoa are enclosed. In addition to the spermatophores, in some [species](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/species "wikilink") the aedeagus also discharges a [spermatophylax](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/spermatophylax "wikilink"), a ball of  精弓[nutritious](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/nutrition "wikilink") secretions to aid the female in producing offspring.

[鱗翅目](../Page/鱗翅目.md "wikilink")的大多數雄性物種，其插入器均有鞘， which is supported by an organ called the [juxta](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/juxta "wikilink"), which is located between the aforementioned valvae.

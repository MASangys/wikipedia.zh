**撒拉斐爾**
([希伯來語](../Page/希伯來語.md "wikilink")：<span style="font-family:SBL Hebrew, Ezra SIL SR, Ezra SIL, Cardo, Chrysanthi Unicode, TITUS Cyberbit Basic, Arial Unicode MS, Narkisim, Times New Roman"><big>**שאלתיאל**</big></span>，[英語](../Page/英語.md "wikilink")：**Selaphiel**或**Selatiel**)，名字意為「向神祈禱」或「神之禱告」，是[東正教會和](../Page/正教會.md "wikilink")傳統中的七大[天使長之一](../Page/天使長.md "wikilink")。\[1\]

## 形像

祂在[聖像中被描繪為一位雙目低垂](../Page/聖像畫.md "wikilink")，雙臂交叉於胸前，正在虔誠[祈禱的天使形像](../Page/禱告.md "wikilink")。\[2\]祂有時也被描繪為以下跪之姿祈禱，祈禱被認為是祂的特性。\[3\]

## 聖經文獻

## 主保

在[東正教和某些](../Page/正教會.md "wikilink")[天主教傳統中](../Page/天主教.md "wikilink")，撒拉斐爾通常被看作是[祈禱和團體服侍的](../Page/禱告.md "wikilink")[守護聖者](../Page/主保聖人.md "wikilink")。

## 禱文

在[東正教和](../Page/正教會.md "wikilink")傳統中經常被誦唸的一篇聖撒拉斐爾代禱禱文：

*O Pure and Holy Archangel St. Selaphiel, you bow before the Almighty
Lord offerinng angelic salutations of praise and thanksgiving. Guide us
in our prayer. Like you, we would like to unceasingly pray and worship
God the right way. May our lives be like incense pleasing to God. While
awaiting for the inevitable time of separation from this material world,
may we praise the Holy Trinity in the spirit of true love and humility
throughout the days of our lives in eternity. Obtain for us these favors
(name them) and present to God the Father all these petitions through
Jesus Christ our Lord together with the Holy Spirit forever and ever.
Amen.*

**擬纓鰓蟲屬**（學名：）是[纓緦蟲科之下的一個屬](../Page/纓緦蟲科.md "wikilink")\[1\]\[2\]，包括以下各個物種：

  - [短胸擬纓鰓蟲](../Page/短胸擬纓鰓蟲.md "wikilink") *Parasabella brevithoracicus*
    <small>(Pillai, 1961)</small>
  - [擬纓鰓蟲](../Page/擬纓鰓蟲.md "wikilink") *Parasabella krusensterni*
    <small>(Kinberg, 1867)</small>
  - [白亮擬纓鰓蟲](../Page/白亮擬纓鰓蟲.md "wikilink") *Parasabella leucaspis*
    <small>(Kinberg, 1867)</small>
  - [中擬纓鰓蟲](../Page/中擬纓鰓蟲.md "wikilink") *Parasabella medius*
    <small>Bush, 1905</small>
  - [小眼擬纓鰓蟲](../Page/小眼擬纓鰓蟲.md "wikilink") *Parasabella microphthalmus*
    <small>(Verrill, 1873)</small>
  - [蒼白擬纓鰓蟲](../Page/蒼白擬纓鰓蟲.md "wikilink") *Parasabella pallidus*
    <small>(Moore, 1923)</small>

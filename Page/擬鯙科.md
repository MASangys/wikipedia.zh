**擬鯙科**為[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鰻鱺目的其中一個](../Page/鰻鱺目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**擬鯙科**其下分為9個屬，如下：

  - 貝龜鰻屬(*Boehlkenchelys*)
      - [貝龜鰻](../Page/貝龜鰻.md "wikilink")(*Boehlkenchelys longidentata*)
  - 卡特斯草鰻屬(*Catesbya*)
      - [卡特斯草鰻](../Page/卡特斯草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Catesbya pseudomuraena*)
  - 唇鼻鰻屬(*Chilorhinus*)
      - [扁吻唇鼻鰻](../Page/扁吻唇鼻鰻.md "wikilink")(*Chilorhinus
        platyrhynchus*)：又稱扁吻擬鯙。
      - [休氏唇鼻鰻](../Page/休氏唇鼻鰻.md "wikilink")(*Chilorhinus suensonii*)
  - 草鰻屬(*Chlopsis*)
      - [無鰭草鰻](../Page/無鰭草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Chlopsis apterus*)
      - [雙項草鰻](../Page/雙項草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Chlopsis bicollaris*)
      - [雙色草鰻](../Page/雙色草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Chlopsis bicolor*)
      - [裸胸草鰻](../Page/裸胸草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Chlopsis dentatus*)
      - [墨西哥草鰻](../Page/墨西哥草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Chlopsis kazuko*)
      - [長齒草鰻](../Page/長齒草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Chlopsis longidens*)
      - [奧洛草鰻](../Page/奧洛草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Chlopsis olokun*)
      - (*Chlopsis slusserorum*)
  - 眶鼻鰻屬(*Kaupichthys*)
      - [黑吻眶鼻鰻](../Page/黑吻眶鼻鰻.md "wikilink")(*Kaupichthys atronasus*)
      - [短鰭眶鼻鰻](../Page/短鰭眶鼻鰻.md "wikilink")(*Kaupichthys brachychirus*)
      - [雙齒眶鼻鰻](../Page/雙齒眶鼻鰻.md "wikilink")(*Kaupichthys diodontus*)
      - [高體眶鼻鰻](../Page/高體眶鼻鰻.md "wikilink")(*Kaupichthys hyoproroides*)
      - (*Kaupichthys japonicus*)
      - [頸環眶鼻鰻](../Page/頸環眶鼻鰻.md "wikilink")(*Kaupichthys nuchalis*)
  - 鮑氏鰻屬(*Powellichthys*)
      - [鮑氏鰻](../Page/鮑氏鰻.md "wikilink")(*Powellichthys ventriosus*)
  - 羅賓斯草鰻屬(*Robinsia*)
      - [羅賓斯草鰻](../Page/羅賓斯草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Robinsia catherinae*)
  - 龜草鰻屬(*Thalassenchelys*)
      - [科氏龜草鰻](../Page/科氏龜草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Thalassenchelys coheni*)
      - [葉狀龜草鰻](../Page/葉狀龜草鰻.md "wikilink")(*Thalassenchelys
        foliaceus*)
  - 奇康吉鰻屬(*Xenoconger*)
      - [弗氏奇康吉鰻](../Page/弗氏奇康吉鰻.md "wikilink")(*Xenoconger fryeri*)

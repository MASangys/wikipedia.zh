**攀雀科**（学名*Remizidae*）是[鸟纲](../Page/鸟纲.md "wikilink")[雀形目中的一个](../Page/雀形目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科_\(生物\).md "wikilink")，与[山雀是近亲](../Page/山雀科.md "wikilink")。本科的物种归属仍有一定的争议。

根据Harrap & Quinn (1996)的文献，认为可分为5属13种：

[攀雀属](../Page/攀雀属.md "wikilink") *Remiz*

  - [欧洲攀雀](../Page/欧洲攀雀.md "wikilink") *Remiz pendulinus*
  - [攀雀](../Page/攀雀.md "wikilink")（中华攀雀） *Remiz consobrinus*
  - [白冠攀雀](../Page/白冠攀雀.md "wikilink") *Remiz coronatus*

*Anthoscopu*

  - [非洲攀雀](../Page/非洲攀雀.md "wikilink") *Anthoscopus caroli*
  - [林攀雀](../Page/林攀雀.md "wikilink") *Anthoscopus flavifrons*
  - [南方攀雀](../Page/南方攀雀.md "wikilink") *Anthoscopus minutus*)
  - [鼠色攀雀](../Page/鼠色攀雀.md "wikilink") (*Anthoscopus musculus*
  - [黄攀雀](../Page/黄攀雀.md "wikilink") (*Anthoscopus parvulus*)
  - *Anthoscopus punctifrons*

[火帽攀雀属](../Page/火帽攀雀属.md "wikilink") ''Cephalopyrus ''

  - [火帽攀雀](../Page/火帽攀雀.md "wikilink") *Cephalopyrus flammiceps*

''Auriparus ''

  - *Auriparus flaviceps*

''Pholidornis ''

  - *Pholidornis rushiae*

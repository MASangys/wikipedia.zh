**散囊菌目**（[學名](../Page/學名.md "wikilink")：）是[子囊菌門](../Page/子囊菌門.md "wikilink")[盘菌亚门之下的一個](../Page/盘菌亚门.md "wikilink")[綱](../Page/綱_\(生物\).md "wikilink")。

## 分類

散囊菌目原來分為以下三個分類：

  - [刺盾炱亞綱](../Page/刺盾炱亞綱.md "wikilink") Chaetothyriomycetidae\[1\]
      - [刺盾炱目](../Page/刺盾炱目.md "wikilink") Chaetothyriales
      - [Pyrenulales](../Page/Pyrenulales.md "wikilink")
      - [瓶口衣目](../Page/瓶口衣目.md "wikilink") Verrucariales
  - [散囊菌亞綱](../Page/散囊菌亞綱.md "wikilink") Eurotiomycetidae
      - [Coryneliales](../Page/Coryneliales.md "wikilink")
      - [散囊菌目](../Page/散囊菌目.md "wikilink") Eurotiales
      - [爪甲團囊菌目](../Page/爪甲團囊菌目.md "wikilink") Onygenales
  - [Mycocaliciomycetidae](../Page/Mycocaliciomycetidae.md "wikilink")
      - [Mycocaliciales](../Page/Mycocaliciales.md "wikilink")

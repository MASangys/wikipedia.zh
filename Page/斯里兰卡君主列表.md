## 康提王国

  - [Jayaweera Bandãra](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jayaweera_Bandãra "wikilink"), accepted baptism as Dom Manoel, 1546年3月9日, as part of alliance with Portugal for protection from Mayadunne of Sitawaka
  - [Karalliyaddë Bandãra](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karalliyaddë_Bandãra "wikilink") (baptized as Dom Joao)
  - Kandy conquered by King Rãjasimha II of Sitawake, 1582
  - Rebellion by Kandyans regains independence from Sitawaka, 1592
  - [Dom Filipe Yamasimha](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dom_Filipe_Yamasimha "wikilink") 1592年, nephew of Karalliyadde Bandara, died of suspected poisoning by his general Konnapu Bandara
  - [Dom João](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dom_João "wikilink"), 12-year-old son of Yamasimha, throne usurped by his general Konnapu Bandara (Dom Joao da Austria) as *Vimala Dharma Surya I*; later ordained a Catholic priest, lived & died at Telheiras near Lisbon, 1642年 at the age of 64
  - [Dona Catarina](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dona_Catarina "wikilink"), daughter of Karalliyadde Bandara, defeated and captured by Konnapu Bandara, October 9, 1594, [Battle of Danturë](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Battle_of_Danturë "wikilink"), and forcibly made his 'queen'; she remained Catholic till her death

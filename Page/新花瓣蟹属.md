**新花瓣蟹屬**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）为[十足目](../Page/十足目.md "wikilink")[扇蟹科的动物](../Page/扇蟹科.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

本屬包括以下物種\[1\]：

  - *[Neoliomera cerasinus](../Page/Neoliomera_cerasinus.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Ng, 2002</small>
  - *[Neoliomera demani](../Page/Neoliomera_demani.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Forest & Guinot, 1961</small>
  - *[Neoliomera insularis](../Page/Neoliomera_insularis.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Adams & White, 1849)</small>
  - *[Neoliomera
    intermedia](../Page/Neoliomera_intermedia.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Odhner, 1925</small>
  - *[Neoliomera lippa](../Page/Neoliomera_lippa.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Nobili, 1905)</small>
  - *[Neoliomera nobilii](../Page/Neoliomera_nobilii.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Odhner, 1925</small>
  - *[Neoliomera ovata](../Page/Neoliomera_ovata.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Tweedie, 1950</small>
  - *[Neoliomera praetexta](../Page/Neoliomera_praetexta.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Rathbun, 1906)</small>
  - [柔毛新花瓣蟹](../Page/柔毛新花瓣蟹.md "wikilink") *Neoliomera pubescens*
    <small>(H. Milne Edwards, 1834)</small>
  - *[Neoliomera
    richteroides](../Page/Neoliomera_richteroides.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Sakai, 1969</small>
  - *[Neoliomera richtersi](../Page/Neoliomera_richtersi.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(De Man, 1889)</small>
  - *[Neoliomera sabaea](../Page/Neoliomera_sabaea.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Nobili, 1905)</small>
  - *[Neoliomera striata](../Page/Neoliomera_striata.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Buitendijk, 1941</small>
  - *[Neoliomera sundaica](../Page/Neoliomera_sundaica.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(De Man, 1888)</small>
  - *[Neoliomera themisto](../Page/Neoliomera_themisto.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(De Man, 1889)</small>
  - *[Neoliomera variolosa](../Page/Neoliomera_variolosa.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(A. Milne-Edwards, 1873)</small>

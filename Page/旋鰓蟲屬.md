**旋鰓蟲屬**（[學名](../Page/學名.md "wikilink")：）是[龍介蟲科下一](../Page/龍介蟲科.md "wikilink")[屬細小的](../Page/屬.md "wikilink")[環節動物](../Page/環節動物.md "wikilink")\[1\]。

## 物種

現時已知旋鰓蟲屬下有數十個[物種](../Page/物種.md "wikilink")，比較常見的如下\[2\]\[3\]：

  - [大旋鰓蟲](../Page/大旋鰓蟲.md "wikilink")（*[Spirobranchus
    giganteus](../Page/Spirobranchus_giganteus.md "wikilink")*
    ）<small>[Pallas](../Page/彼得·西蒙·帕拉斯.md "wikilink"), 1766</small>
  - [盤蓋旋鰓蟲](../Page/盤蓋旋鰓蟲.md "wikilink")（*[Spirobranchus
    latiscapus](../Page/Spirobranchus_latiscapus.md "wikilink")*）
    <small>[Marenzeller](../Page/Marenzeller.md "wikilink"),
    1885</small>
  - [四犄旋鰓蟲](../Page/四犄旋鰓蟲.md "wikilink")（*[Spirobranchus
    tetraceros](../Page/Spirobranchus_tetraceros.md "wikilink")*）\[4\]

**明綃蝶屬**（學名：*Hyalenna*）是[蛺蝶科](../Page/蛺蝶科.md "wikilink")[斑蝶亞科](../Page/斑蝶亞科.md "wikilink")[綃蝶族中的一個](../Page/綃蝶族.md "wikilink")[屬](../Page/屬_\(生物\).md "wikilink")。物種分佈於[南美洲](../Page/南美洲.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

  - *[Hyalenna paradoxa](../Page/Hyalenna_paradoxa.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Hyalenna hugia](../Page/Hyalenna_hugia.md "wikilink")*
  - [明綃蝶](../Page/明綃蝶.md "wikilink") *Hyalenna perasippa*
  - *[Hyalenna buckleyi](../Page/Hyalenna_buckleyi.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Hyalenna sulmona](../Page/Hyalenna_sulmona.md "wikilink")*
      - 裂明綃蝶 *H. s. lobusa*
  - *[Hyalenna pascua](../Page/Hyalenna_pascua.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Hyalenna alidella](../Page/Hyalenna_alidella.md "wikilink")*
      - 滴明綃蝶 *H. a. dirama*

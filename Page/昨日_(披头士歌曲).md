“**昨日**”（英语：**Yesterday**）是一首[披头士乐队的歌曲](../Page/披头士乐队.md "wikilink")，原本为1965年的专辑《[Help\!](../Page/Help!.md "wikilink")》所作。虽然署名为[列侬-麦卡特尼](../Page/列侬-麦卡特尼.md "wikilink")，该曲是由[保罗·麦卡特尼独自创作](../Page/保罗·麦卡特尼.md "wikilink")。这首歌流行至今，有超过2200个翻唱版本，是录音史上被翻唱次数最多的歌曲之一{{\#tag:ref|At
one time, *[Guinness World
Records](../Page/Guinness_World_Records.md "wikilink")* cited
"Yesterday" with the most [cover
versions](../Page/cover_version.md "wikilink") of any song ever written
– 2,200. However,
"[Summertime](../Page/Summertime_\(song\).md "wikilink")", an aria
composed by [George Gershwin](../Page/George_Gershwin.md "wikilink") for
the 1935 opera [Porgy and Bess](../Page/Porgy_and_Bess.md "wikilink")
has been claimed to have well over 30,000 recorded performances, far
more than the 1,600 claimed for "Yesterday".\[1\]|group="note"}}。

## 創作過程

據麥卡尼憶述，歌曲旋律的靈感來自[夢中](../Page/夢.md "wikilink")，一覺醒來後他立即走到鋼琴前彈奏出來，並以[錄音機記錄下來](../Page/錄音機.md "wikilink")。\[2\]他詢問音樂界人士，確定旋律是原創的，並非他不經意抄襲他人，之後他便着手填詞；他曾以"Scrambled
eggs, Oh, baby how I love your legs."（炒蛋，噢，寶貝我愛煞你的腿。）作為非正式歌詞。

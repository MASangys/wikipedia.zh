**普利特维拉·卡浦尔**（[印地語](../Page/印地語.md "wikilink")：पृथ्वीराज
कपूर，*Prithviraj
Kapoor*）是[印度](../Page/印度.md "wikilink")[寶萊塢電影演員](../Page/寶萊塢.md "wikilink")。他的兒子[拉吉](../Page/拉吉·卡浦尔.md "wikilink")（*Raj
Kapoor*）和[沙希·卡浦爾後來也是寶萊塢演員](../Page/沙希·卡浦爾.md "wikilink")、導演。

## 演出電影

  - Alam Ara (1931)
  - Vidyapati (1937)
  - Sikander (1941)
  - [Awaara](../Page/Awaara.md "wikilink") (1951)
  - [Mughal-e-Azam](../Page/Mughal-e-Azam.md "wikilink") (1960)
  - Zindagi (1964)
  - Heer Raanjha (1970)
  - Kal Aaj Aur Kal (1971)
  - Pardesi (1957 film)
  - Sakshatkara - A Kannada movie

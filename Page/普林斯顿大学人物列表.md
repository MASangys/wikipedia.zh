## 历任校长

[Princeton University](../Page/Princeton_University.md "wikilink") is
led by a President selected by the Board of Trustees. A number of noted
American statesmen, clerics, and revolutionaries led the University in
the eighteenth century; today, the office is usually held by prominent
academics.

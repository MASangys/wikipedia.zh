## 历任校长

[Princeton University](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Princeton_University "wikilink") is led by a President selected by the Board of Trustees. A number of noted American statesmen, clerics, and revolutionaries led the University in the eighteenth century; today, the office is usually held by prominent academics.

**曼迪市**（英文名：Munday）\[1\]\[2\]是位于[美国](../Page/美国.md "wikilink")[德克萨斯州](../Page/德克萨斯州.md "wikilink")[诺克斯县](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/诺克斯县_\(德克萨斯州\) "wikilink")）的一座城市。[2000年美国人口普查](../Page/2000年美国人口普查.md "wikilink")时，人口为1527人。[美国人口调查局](../Page/美国人口调查局.md "wikilink")2010年时的数据为1300人。\[3\]

20世纪初，德克萨斯州政治家奥维尔·布灵顿（Orville Bullington）在曼迪市从事法律工作。1932年，他作为[共和党州长候选人与米里亚姆](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/共和党_\(美国\) "wikilink")·弗格森（Miriam Ferguson）竞选州长，之前是[诺克斯县检察长](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/诺克斯县_\(德克萨斯州\) "wikilink")。



## 地理

[左](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:MundayCottonSunset.jpg "fig:左") 曼迪市的坐标为： （33.449292, -99.626028）\[4\]，位于**美国277国道**、**德克萨斯222号州内公路**、**美国1581号乡村公路**、**美国2811号乡村公路**在[诺克斯县南部的交点](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/诺克斯县_\(德克萨斯州\) "wikilink")。\[5\]

曼迪市与[阿比林和](../Page/阿比林_\(得克萨斯州\).md "wikilink")[威奇托福尔斯的距离均为](../Page/威奇托福尔斯_\(得克萨斯州\).md "wikilink")120千米左右\[6\]

根据[美国人口调查局](../Page/美国人口调查局.md "wikilink")的数据，该市管辖面积为，全部为陆地面积。

## 历史

该市的历史可追溯至1893年，当时该区域存在一个公共建筑。原名“莫德”（Maud），这个名字来自早期居民莫德·伊斯贝尔（Maud Isbell）的名字。1894年当地邮局成立时由时任局长R·P·曼迪（R.P. Munday）改为现在的名字。\[7\]1903年时，曼迪城区分为不相连的东西两部分，两者相距大约1000米。同年，西曼迪搬至东曼迪，大致形成了今天曼迪市城区的格局。\[8\]\[9\]威奇托谷铁路（Wichita Valley Railroad）于1906年进入曼迪，同年曼迪进行了社区合并。1910年，曼迪总人口为968人，因而成为[诺克斯县最大的镇](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/诺克斯县_\(德克萨斯州\) "wikilink")。1950年，曼迪人口达到了鼎盛时期的2270人。此后的20世纪后半叶人口缓慢减少。曼迪市吸引人的地方包括：一座9洞高尔夫球场、县级图书馆、公园、精品店等。\[10\]

棉花至今依然是曼迪市的主要农作物及支柱产业。W·A·欧内斯特（W. A. Earnest）于1900年在曼迪市建造了一台[轧棉机](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/轧棉机 "wikilink")，自此棉花加工业成为曼迪市支柱产业。在灌溉农业大发展后，曼迪市同时成为了[蔬菜](../Page/蔬菜.md "wikilink")、[水果生产及深加工中心](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/水果 "wikilink")，本地农民种植的蔬菜、水果包括：[洋葱](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/洋葱 "wikilink")、[马铃薯](../Page/马铃薯.md "wikilink")、[黄瓜](../Page/黄瓜.md "wikilink")等。[德克萨斯州农工大学](../Page/德克萨斯州农工大学.md "wikilink")也于1971年在曼迪市建立了农业研究中心。\[11\]

## 人口

### 2010年人口普查数据

根据[2010年美国人口普查数据](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2010年美国人口普查 "wikilink")，曼迪市总人口为1300人，比[2000年美国人口普查](../Page/2000年美国人口普查.md "wikilink")时减少了14.87%（227人）。各族裔人口及比重为：白人70.46%（916人）、[非裔美国人](../Page/非裔美国人.md "wikilink")7%（91人）、[美洲原住民](../Page/美洲原住民.md "wikilink") 0.46%（6人）、混血3.23%（42人）、其他族裔18.85%（245人）。[西班牙裔](../Page/西班牙裔.md "wikilink")或者拉丁裔占总人口的38.23%（497人）。

### 2000年人口普查数据

根据[2000年美国人口普查](../Page/2000年美国人口普查.md "wikilink")数据\[12\]，曼迪市总人口为1,527人，拥有597个户口，有404个家庭在此实际居住。[人口密度](../Page/人口密度.md "wikilink")为415.2人/km²。There were 701 housing units at an average density of 495.1 per square mile (190.6/km²). The racial makeup of the city was 69.48% [White](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/White_\(U.S._Census\) "wikilink") (1,061 people), 8.51% [African American](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/African_American_\(U.S._Census\) "wikilink") (130 people), 1.05% [Native American](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Native_American_\(U.S._Census\) "wikilink") (16 people), 0.20% [Pacific Islander](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Islander_\(U.S._Census\) "wikilink") (3 people), 17.81% from [other races](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Race_\(United_States_Census\) "wikilink") (272 people), and 2.95% from two or more races (45 people). [Hispanic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hispanic_\(U.S._Census\) "wikilink") or [Latino](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latino_\(U.S._Census\) "wikilink") of any race were 30.58% of the population (467 people).

There were 597 households out of which 33.5% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 53.6% were [married couples](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marriage "wikilink") living together, 11.2% had a female householder with no husband present, and 32.2% were non-families. 31.0% of all households were made up of individuals and 18.6% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.47 and the average family size was 3.12.

In the city, the population was spread out with 30.0% under the age of 18, 5.4% from 18 to 24, 23.7% from 25 to 44, 17.6% from 45 to 64, and 23.2% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 37 years. For every 100 females, there were 87.4 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 82.1 males.

The median income for a household in the city was $22,708, and the median income for a family was $28,158. Males had a median income of $22,303 versus $20,769 for females. The [per capita income](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/per_capita_income "wikilink") for the city was $11,593. About 21.8% of families and 29.1% of the population were below the [poverty line](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/poverty_line "wikilink"), including 43.5% of those under age 18 and 17.0% of those age 65 or over.

## 教育

曼迪市全市[学区](../Page/学区.md "wikilink")均属[曼迪联合独立学区](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/曼迪联合独立学区 "wikilink")。

[Munday High School](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Munday_High_School "wikilink") is rated an Recognized campus by the Texas Education Agency (2010). The High School's mascot is the Moguls and school colors are purple and gold. The Mogul football team won the Class A State Championship in 1984, 2007, and 2012. The Mogulette track team won the State Championship 5 times; in '88,'89,'90,'91, and '99. They were also State Champions in Cross Country twice; in '88 and '89. The Mogul track team won State in '85,'88,'90,'91 and '11. They still hold the State record in the 1600 meter relay and were named to the UIL All Century Track Team. The Munday High School Purple Cloud Band has won the Texas 1A Honor Band Competition twice, in 1994 and 2000. Munday's One Act Play has advanced to the State OAP Meet 9 times, winning the State Championship in '93, 2nd runner-up in '97, and 1st runner-up in 2008. The school also has 3 State CX Debate championships.

## Climate

The climate in this area is characterized by hot, humid summers\[13\] and generally mild to cool winters. According to the [Köppen Climate Classification](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Köppen_Climate_Classification "wikilink") system, Munday has a [humid subtropical climate](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/humid_subtropical_climate "wikilink"), abbreviated "Cfa" on climate maps.\[14\]

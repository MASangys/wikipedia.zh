**會談邊界控制器**（Session Border Controller,
SBC），一種[NAT穿透的方式](../Page/NAT穿透.md "wikilink")。SBC可確保[VoIP](../Page/VoIP.md "wikilink")
安全，又可提供媒體代理伺服器的套件。

SBC架構於[IMS網路之上](../Page/IMS.md "wikilink")，可作為IMS網路的[SIP和](../Page/SIP.md "wikilink")[RTSP的](../Page/RTSP.md "wikilink")
Proxy
Server，所有的SIP與RTSP訊息都會透過SBC來處理，SBC更具備NAT穿透的功能。目前[思科與](../Page/思科.md "wikilink")
Kagoor 對會談邊界控制技術有突破性的發展。

## 功能

SBCs 會維持整個會議狀態並提供以下功能:

  - [网络安全](../Page/网络安全.md "wikilink") – 保護網路以及其他裝置:

:\* Malicious 攻擊, 像是[分散式阻斷服務攻擊](../Page/分散式阻斷服務攻擊.md "wikilink") (DoS)
或是分散式 DoS

:\* Toll fraud via rogue media streams

:\* 拓樸隱藏（Topology hiding）

:\* Malformed packet protection

:\* 訊號加密 (經由 [TLS](../Page/傳輸層安全協議.md "wikilink") 以及
[IPSec](../Page/IPSec.md "wikilink")) 以及媒體
([SRTP](../Page/安全实时传输协议.md "wikilink"))

  - 連結 – 允許不同網域之間的溝通:

:\* NAT 穿透

:\* SIP 正規化 via SIP message and header manipulation

:\* IPv4 轉 IPv6

:\* VPN 連結

:\* 協定轉換, 如 [SIP](../Page/Session_Initiation_Protocol.md "wikilink"),
SIP-I, [H.323](../Page/H.323.md "wikilink")

  - [服务质量](../Page/QoS.md "wikilink") – QoS 的政策是網路狀態的優先取捨問題. 其功能如下:

:\* Traffic policing

:\* Resource allocation

:\* Rate limiting

:\* Call admission control

:\* TOS/DSCP bit setting

  - Regulatory – many times the SBC is expected to provide support for
    regulatory requirements such as:

:\* [emergency calls](../Page/emergency_calls.md "wikilink")
prioritization and

:\* [lawful interception](../Page/lawful_interception.md "wikilink")

  - Media services – many of the new generation of SBCs also provide
    built-in digital signal processors (DSPs) to enable them to offer
    border-based media control and services such as:

:\* DTMF 中繼與互連

:\* 媒體轉碼

:\* Tones 與 announcements

:\* 數據與內網傳真

:\* 支援音訊與影訊

  - 統計與傳單資訊 – since all sessions that pass through the edge of the
    network pass through the SBC, it is a natural point to gather
    statistics and usage based information on these sessions.

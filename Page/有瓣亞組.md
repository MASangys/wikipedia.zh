**有瓣類**（學名：）是[雙翅目](../Page/雙翅目.md "wikilink")[昆虫](../Page/昆虫.md "wikilink")之下的一個分類單元，[家蠅下目](../Page/家蠅下目.md "wikilink")的成員。根據[WoRMS](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/WoRMS "wikilink")，有瓣類屬於之下。It consists of those flies which possess a  that covers the s, among which are some of the most familiar of all flies, such as the [家蠅](../Page/家蠅.md "wikilink")。

目前這個群組共有約18,000個已描述的物種，佔所有已描述的蒼蠅約12%\[1\]。

**果蝠屬**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：**），[哺乳綱](../Page/哺乳綱.md "wikilink")[翼手目](../Page/翼手目.md "wikilink")[狐蝠科的一屬](../Page/狐蝠科.md "wikilink")，而與果蝠屬（棕果蝠）同科的動物尚有[灰果蝠屬](../Page/灰果蝠屬.md "wikilink")（灰果蝠）、[毬果蝠屬](../Page/毬果蝠屬.md "wikilink")（布氏毬果蝠）、[狐蝠屬](../Page/狐蝠屬.md "wikilink")（遊狐蝠）、[錐齒狐蝠屬](../Page/錐齒狐蝠屬.md "wikilink")（錐齒狐蝠）等之數種[哺乳動物](../Page/哺乳動物.md "wikilink")。\[1\]

1.  [北非果蝠](../Page/北非果蝠.md "wikilink")（[埃及果蝠](../Page/埃及果蝠.md "wikilink")）
    *Rousettus aegyptiacus* (Egyptian rousette)
2.  [抱尾果蝠](../Page/抱尾果蝠.md "wikilink") *Rousettus amplexicaudatus*
    (Geoffroy's rousette)
3.  [安哥拉果蝠](../Page/安哥拉果蝠.md "wikilink") *Rousettus angolensis* (Angolan
    rousette)
4.  [蘇拉威西果蝠](../Page/蘇拉威西果蝠.md "wikilink")（[西里伯斯果蝠](../Page/西里伯斯果蝠.md "wikilink")）
    *Rousettus celebensis* (Sulawesi rousette)
5.  [狭齿果蝠](../Page/狭齿果蝠.md "wikilink")（[多毛果蝠](../Page/多毛果蝠.md "wikilink")）
    *Rousettus lanosus* (Long-haired rousette)
6.  [棕果蝠](../Page/棕果蝠.md "wikilink")（[列氏果蝠](../Page/列氏果蝠.md "wikilink")）
    *Rousettus leschenaulti* (Leschenault's rousette)
7.  [马达加斯加果蝠](../Page/马达加斯加果蝠.md "wikilink")（[馬島果蝠](../Page/馬島果蝠.md "wikilink")）
    *Rousettus madagascariensis* (Madagascan rousette)
8.  [科摩羅果蝠](../Page/科摩羅果蝠.md "wikilink") *Rousettus obliviosus* (Comoro
    rousette)
9.  [棘果蝠](../Page/棘果蝠.md "wikilink")（[光背果蝠](../Page/光背果蝠.md "wikilink")）
    *Rousettus spinalatus* (Bare-backed rousette)

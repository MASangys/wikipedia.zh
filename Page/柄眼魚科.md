**柄眼魚科**（学名：*Dactyloscopidae*）為[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鱸形目](../Page/鱸形目.md "wikilink")[䲁亞目的其中一個](../Page/䲁亞目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**柄眼魚科**其下分9個屬，如下：

### 粗指鰧屬(*Dactylagnus*)

  - [粗指鰧](../Page/粗指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactylagnus mundus*)
  - [哥斯達黎加粗指鰧](../Page/哥斯達黎加粗指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactylagnus peratikos*)

### 指鰧屬(*Dactyloscopus*)

  - [墨西哥指鰧](../Page/墨西哥指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactyloscopus amnis*)
  - [拜氏指鰧](../Page/拜氏指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactyloscopus byersi*)
  - [大眼指鰧](../Page/大眼指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactyloscopus crossotus*)
  - [厄瓜多爾指鰧](../Page/厄瓜多爾指鰧.md "wikilink") (*Dactyloscopus fimbriata*)
  - [乳色指鰧](../Page/乳色指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactyloscopus lacteus*)
  - [新月指鰧](../Page/新月指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactyloscopus lunaticus*)
  - [穆氏指鰧](../Page/穆氏指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactyloscopus moorei*)
  - [鰭指鰧](../Page/鰭指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactyloscopus pectoralis*)
  - [短頜指鰧](../Page/短頜指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactyloscopus poeyi*)
  - [指鰧](../Page/指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactyloscopus tridigitatus*)
  - [嫉指鰧](../Page/嫉指鰧.md "wikilink")(*Dactyloscopus zelotes*)

**查爾斯·亞歷山大·伊士曼**（Charles Alexander Eastman，），乳名**Hakadah**，後更名****，為 was a 內科[医生](../Page/医生.md "wikilink")。伊士曼畢業於[波士頓大學](../Page/波士頓大學.md "wikilink")，為第一位取得西方醫學學位的[美國原住民](../Page/美國原住民.md "wikilink")。他同時也是位作家、民族演說家，以及改革者。他是20世紀蘇族民族史領域，著作最豐富的作家暨演說家之一\[1\]。

伊士曼擁有、，及法國人血統。在南達科達的保留地行醫一段時間後，他開始熱衷政治，並關注[美國原住民](../Page/美國原住民.md "wikilink")相關議題。他試圖改善青年生活，並成立了32個以原住民為主的[基督教青年会](../Page/基督教青年会.md "wikilink")（YMCA）分會，同時也協助[美國童軍](../Page/美國童軍.md "wikilink")的成立。伊士曼被認為是首位以美國原住民觀點去記錄美國歷史的原住民作家。

## 著作

  - [*Memories of an Indian Boyhood*](http://pinkmonkey.com/dl/library1/digi152.pdf), autobiography; McClure, Philips, 1902.
  - *Indian Boyhood*, New York; McClure, Phillips & Co., 1902. [Online at Webroots](https://web.archive.org/web/20081120033133/http://www.webroots.org/library/usanativ/iboy0000.html).
  - *Red Hunters and Animal People*, legends; Harper and Brothers, 1904.
  - *[The Madness of Bald Eagle](https://web.archive.org/web/20030116012449/http://etext.lib.virginia.edu/etcbin/toccer-new2?id=EasMadn.sgm&images=images%2Fmodeng&data=%2Ftexts%2Fenglish%2Fmodeng%2Fparsed&tag=public&part=all)*, legend; 1905.
  - *[Old Indian Days](https://web.archive.org/web/20050405223013/http://etext.lib.virginia.edu/cgibin/toccer?id=EasDays&tag=public&images=images%2Fmodeng&data=%2Ftexts%2Fenglish%2Fmodeng%2Fparsed&part=0)*, legends; McClure, 1907.
  - *Wigwam Evenings: Sioux Folk Tales Retold* (co-author with his wife Ellen Goodale Eastman), legends; Little, Brown, 1909.
  - *Smoky Day's Wigwam Evenings* (co-written with Ellen Goodale Eastman), 1910
  - *[The Soul of the Indian: An Interpretation](https://web.archive.org/web/20050405223025/http://etext.lib.virginia.edu/cgibin/toccer?id=EasSoul&tag=public&images=images%2Fmodeng&data=%2Ftexts%2Fenglish%2Fmodeng%2Fparsed&part=0)*, Houghton, 1911.
  - *Indian Child Life*, nonfiction, Little, Brown, 1913.
  - *Indian Scout Talks: A Guide for Scouts and Campfire Girls*, nonfiction, Little, Brown, 1914. (retitled *Indian Scout Craft and Lore*, Dover Publications). A 1914 reviewer writes, "If one should follow this guide, one would soon begin to doubt he is a white man".\[2\]
  - *The Indian Today: The Past and Future of the Red American*, Doubleday-Page, 1915.
  - *From the Deep Woods to Civilization: Chapters in the Autobiography of an Indian*, autobiography; Little, Brown, 1916.
  - *[Indian Heroes and Great Chieftains](http://www.gutenberg.org/etext/336)*, Little, Brown, 1918. Also [Online at Webroots](https://web.archive.org/web/20021116110147/http://www.webroots.org/library/usanativ/ihagc000.html).

**查菲 - 堙方特方程**（Chaffee-Infante equation）是一个非线性偏微分方程：\[1\]

\(u_{t}-u_{xx}+\lambda*(u^3-u)=0\)

## 解析解

\[u(x, t) = -1/2-(1/2*I)*cot(_C1-(1/4)*\sqrt(-2*\lambda)*x+(3/4*I)*\lambda*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = -1/2-(1/2*I)*tan(_C1-(1/4)*\sqrt(-2*\lambda)*x-(3/4*I)*\lambda*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = -1/2+(1/2*I)*cot(_C1+(1/4)*\sqrt(-2*\lambda)*x-(3/4*I)*\lambda*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = -1/2+(1/2*I)*tan(_C1+(1/4)*\sqrt(-2*\lambda)*x+(3/4*I)*\lambda*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = -1/2-(1/2)*coth(_C1-(1/4)*\sqrt(2)*\sqrt(\lambda)*x+(3/4)*\lambda*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = -1/2+(1/2)*tanh(_C1-(1/4)*\sqrt(2)*\sqrt(\lambda)*x-(3/4)*\lambda*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = 1/2-(1/2*I)*cot(_C1-(1/4)*\sqrt(-2*\lambda)*x-(3/4*I)*\lambda*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = 1/2-(1/2*I)*tan(_C1+(1/4)*\sqrt(-2*\lambda)*x+(3/4*I)*\lambda*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = 1/2-(1/2)*coth(_C1-(1/4)*\sqrt(2)*\sqrt(\lambda)*x-(3/4)*\lambda*t)\]

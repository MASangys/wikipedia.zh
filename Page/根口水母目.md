**根口水母目**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）是[刺胞动物门](../Page/刺胞动物门.md "wikilink")[钵水母纲的一个目](../Page/钵水母纲.md "wikilink")。代表种包括[海蜇](../Page/海蜇.md "wikilink")（Rhopilema）、[硝水母](../Page/硝水母.md "wikilink")（Mastigias）等。本目物種的特色是其體腔的邊緣沒有[觸手或其他構造](../Page/觸手.md "wikilink")。相反，從牠們的口會伸出八條高度分支的長臂，並長有吸盤構造的小口。

## 分類

，本目計有92個已確認的[現存物種](../Page/現存物種.md "wikilink")\[1\]，分為以下十個科\[2\]\[3\]：

  - Suborder: [Daktyliophorae](../Page/Daktyliophorae.md "wikilink")
      - Family [Catostylidae](../Page/Catostylidae.md "wikilink")
      - Family [Lobonematidae](../Page/Lobonematidae.md "wikilink")
      - Family [Lychnorhizidae](../Page/Lychnorhizidae.md "wikilink")
      - Family [Rhizostomatidae](../Page/Rhizostomatidae.md "wikilink")
      - Family [Stomolophidae](../Page/Stomolophidae.md "wikilink")
  - Suborder: [Kolpophorae](../Page/Kolpophorae.md "wikilink")
      - Family [Cassiopeidae](../Page/Cassiopeidae.md "wikilink")
      - Family [Cepheidae](../Page/Cepheidae.md "wikilink")
      - Family [Mastigiidae](../Page/Mastigiidae.md "wikilink")
      - Family
        [Thysanostomatidae](../Page/Thysanostomatidae.md "wikilink")
      - Family [Versurigidae](../Page/Versurigidae.md "wikilink")

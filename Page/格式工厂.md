**格式工厂**（Format
Factory）是一款多功能的[多媒体格式转换软件](../Page/多媒体.md "wikilink")，适用于[Windows](../Page/Windows.md "wikilink")。可以实现大多数[视频](../Page/视频.md "wikilink")、[音频以及](../Page/音频.md "wikilink")[图像不同格式之间的相互转换](../Page/图像.md "wikilink")。转换可以具有设置文件输出配置、增添[数字水印等功能](../Page/数字水印.md "wikilink")。

2008年2月，上海格詩網路科技有限公司成立。

## 主要功能

格式工厂支持以下[格式](../Page/文件格式.md "wikilink")：

| [视频格式](../Page/视频文件格式.md "wikilink")                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | [音频格式](../Page/音频文件格式.md "wikilink")                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     | [图片格式](../Page/图形文件格式比较.md "wikilink")            |
| ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| [3GP](../Page/3GP.md "wikilink") (.3gp){{-}}[MPEG-1](../Page/MPEG-1.md "wikilink") (.mpg){{-}}[Matroska](../Page/Matroska.md "wikilink") (.mkv){{-}}[Flash Video](../Page/Flash_Video.md "wikilink") (.flv){{-}}[SWF](../Page/Swf.md "wikilink") (.swf){{-}}[MPEG-4 Part 14](../Page/MPEG-4_Part_14.md "wikilink") (.mp4){{-}}[Audio Video Interleave](../Page/Audio_Video_Interleave.md "wikilink") (.avi){{-}}[Windows Media Video](../Page/Windows_Media_Video.md "wikilink") (.wmv){{-}}[RealMedia Variable Bitrate](../Page/RMVB.md "wikilink") (.rmvb){{-}}[VOB](../Page/VobSub.md "wikilink") (Video Object) (DVD file) (.vob){{-}}[QuickTime File Format (QTFF)](../Page/QuickTime文件格式.md "wikilink") (.mov){{-}} | [MP3](../Page/MP3.md "wikilink"){{-}}[Ogg](../Page/.ogg.md "wikilink"){{-}}[Wave Sound](../Page/.wav.md "wikilink") (.wav){{-}}[Adaptive Multi-Rate](../Page/自适应多速率音频压缩.md "wikilink") (.amr){{-}}[Windows Media Audio](../Page/Windows_Media_Audio.md "wikilink") (.wma){{-}}[Advanced Audio Coding](../Page/進階音訊編碼.md "wikilink") (.aac){{-}}[MIDI](../Page/MIDI.md "wikilink") (.mid) | {{-}}[.TGA](../Page/Truevision_TGA.md "wikilink") |

  - 所有类型视频轉換為
    [MP4](../Page/MP4.md "wikilink")、[3GP](../Page/3GP.md "wikilink")、[MPG](../Page/MPEG.md "wikilink")、[AVI](../Page/AVI.md "wikilink")、[WMV](../Page/WMV.md "wikilink")、[FLV](../Page/FLV.md "wikilink")、[SWF](../Page/SWF.md "wikilink")、[RMVB](../Page/RMVB.md "wikilink")（rmvb需要安裝[Realplayer或相關的](../Page/Realplayer.md "wikilink")[解碼器](../Page/解碼器.md "wikilink")）等常用格式（包括開放格式）
  - 所有类型音频轉換為
    [MP3](../Page/MP3.md "wikilink")、[WMA](../Page/WMA.md "wikilink")、[AMR](../Page/AMR.md "wikilink")、[OGG](../Page/OGG.md "wikilink")、[AAC](../Page/AAC.md "wikilink")、[WAV](../Page/WAV.md "wikilink")
    等常用格式（包括開放格式）
  - 所有类型图像轉換為
    [JPG](../Page/JPG.md "wikilink")、[BMP](../Page/BMP.md "wikilink")、[PNG](../Page/PNG.md "wikilink")、[TIF](../Page/TIF.md "wikilink")、[ICO](../Page/ICO.md "wikilink")、[GIF](../Page/GIF.md "wikilink")、[TGA](../Page/TGA.md "wikilink")
    等格式（包括開放格式）
  - 转换过程中可修复某些损坏的視頻
  - 媒體文件[壓縮](../Page/壓縮.md "wikilink")
  - 支援[iPhone](../Page/iPhone.md "wikilink")、[iPod](../Page/iPod.md "wikilink")、[PSP等媒体定制格式](../Page/PSP.md "wikilink")
  - 转换[圖像](../Page/圖像.md "wikilink")[檔案支援缩放](../Page/檔案.md "wikilink")、旋转、數碼水印等功能
  - 支援從[DVD-Video複製視頻](../Page/DVD-Video.md "wikilink")
  - 支援從[CDDA複製音樂](../Page/CDDA.md "wikilink")\[1\]
  - 支援62种语言\[2\]

## 版權爭議

由於格式工廠使用了[FFmpeg的](../Page/FFmpeg.md "wikilink")[解碼](../Page/解碼.md "wikilink")/[編碼](../Page/編碼.md "wikilink")[算法](../Page/算法.md "wikilink")，卻以免費軟體的形式發布并且沒有[公開源代碼](../Page/公開源代碼.md "wikilink")，違反FFmpeg的[LGPL協議](../Page/LGPL.md "wikilink")\[3\]，于2009年7月22日登上了FFmpeg的“恥辱柱”。\[4\]開發方
Freetime 至今仍沒有回應。

**格雷文馬赫區**（[盧森堡語](../Page/盧森堡語.md "wikilink")：**Distrikt
Gréiwemaacher**、[德語](../Page/德語.md "wikilink")**Distrikt
Grevenmacher**、[法語](../Page/法語.md "wikilink")**District de
Grevenmacher**）是[西歐國家](../Page/西歐.md "wikilink")[盧森堡的的三個](../Page/盧森堡.md "wikilink")[区之一](../Page/区_\(卢森堡\).md "wikilink")，于2015年10月3日废除。曾下設有3個縣和26個市鎮。

1.  [埃希特納赫縣](../Page/埃希特納赫縣.md "wikilink") - Beaufort - Bech - Berdorf -
    Consdorf - Echternach - Mompach - Rosport - Waldbillig
2.  [格雷文馬赫縣](../Page/格雷文馬赫縣.md "wikilink") - Betzdorf - Biwer -
    Flaxweiler - Grevenmacher - Junglinster - Manternach - Mertert -
    Wormeldange
3.  [雷米希縣](../Page/雷米希縣.md "wikilink") - Bous - Burmerange - Dalheim -
    Lenningen - Mondorf-les-Bains - Remerschen - Remich - Stadtbredimus
    - Waldbredimus - Wellenstein

西與[盧森堡區](../Page/盧森堡區.md "wikilink")、北與[迪基希區](../Page/迪基希區.md "wikilink")、東與[德國的](../Page/德國.md "wikilink")[萊茵蘭-普法爾茨州和](../Page/萊茵蘭-普法爾茨州.md "wikilink")[薩爾州](../Page/薩爾州.md "wikilink")、南與[法國的](../Page/法國.md "wikilink")[摩澤爾省接壤](../Page/摩澤爾省.md "wikilink")。

**次黃嘌呤-鳥嘌呤磷酸核苷轉移酶**（Hypoxanthine-guanine phosphoribosyltransferase，簡稱HGPRT）為人體內一個轉譯自*HPRT1*[基因](../Page/基因.md "wikilink")的[酵素](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/酵素 "wikilink")\[1\]\[2\]

HGPRT為一種[轉移酶](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/轉移酶 "wikilink")，可以催化將[次黃嘌呤轉換為](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/次黃嘌呤 "wikilink")[肌苷酸](../Page/肌苷酸.md "wikilink")（IMP），也可將[鳥嘌呤的反應轉為](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鳥嘌呤 "wikilink")[單磷酸鳥苷](../Page/單磷酸鳥苷.md "wikilink")。這兩個反應都是將[PRPP的](../Page/磷酸核糖焦磷酸.md "wikilink")5-磷酸核苷轉移至嘌呤上。HGPRT在[核苷酸再利用合成途徑中扮演重要角色](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/核苷酸再利用合成途徑 "wikilink")。

HGPRTase functions primarily to salvage purines from degraded DNA to reintroduce into purine synthetic pathways. In this role, it catalyzes the reaction between [guanine](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鳥嘌呤 "wikilink") and [phosphoribosyl pyrophosphate](../Page/磷酸核糖焦磷酸.md "wikilink") (PRPP) to form [GMP](../Page/單磷酸鳥苷.md "wikilink"), or between [hypoxanthine](../Page/次黄嘌呤.md "wikilink") and [phosphoribosyl pyrophosphate](../Page/磷酸核糖焦磷酸.md "wikilink") (PRPP) to form [inosine monophosphate](../Page/肌苷酸.md "wikilink").

## 疾病中的角色

此基因的突變往往導致[高尿酸血症](../Page/高尿酸血症.md "wikilink")：

  - 一些男性帶有部分程度的HGPRT缺陷（約低於正常活動量20%）並因此導致血液中高濃度的[尿酸](../Page/尿酸.md "wikilink")。隨之而來的是[痛風以及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/痛風 "wikilink")[腎結石](../Page/腎結石.md "wikilink")。這症狀稱為[凱利-塞米勒症候群](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/萊希-尼亨症候群#輕微症狀 "wikilink")。 \[4\]
  - [萊希-尼亨症候群](../Page/萊希-尼亨症候群.md "wikilink")起源由HPRT1突變導致的HGPRT缺陷。 \[5\]
  - 某些基因突變可能導致[痛風](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/痛風 "wikilink")。發病的風險與hypoxanthine-guanine phosphoribosyltransferase的缺陷程度成正比。

[thumbtime=3](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:PRTFDC1-Is-a-Genetic-Modifier-of-HPRT-Deficiency-in-the-Mouse-pone.0022381.s008.ogv "wikilink")

  - HPRT expression on the mRNA and protein level is induced by hypoxia inducible factor 1 (). HIF-1 is a [transcription factor](../Page/转录因子.md "wikilink") that directs an array of cellular responses that are used for adaptation during oxygen deprivation. This finding implies that HPRT is a critical pathway that helps preserve the cell's [purine](../Page/嘌呤.md "wikilink") nucleotide resources under hypoxic conditions as found in pathology such as [myocardial ischemia](../Page/冠狀動脈疾病.md "wikilink").\[6\]

The [in silico](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/in_silico "wikilink") and [in-vitro](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/In_vitro "wikilink") correlation of these compounds were test in [Leishmania](../Page/利什曼原蟲.md "wikilink") HGPRT and validates the result.\[7\]

**歐亨尼奧·蒙特霍**是一名[委內瑞拉](../Page/委內瑞拉.md "wikilink")詩人，出生於[卡拉卡斯](../Page/卡拉卡斯.md "wikilink")，曾於羅慕洛·加列戈斯中心從事[拉丁美洲](../Page/拉丁美洲.md "wikilink")的研究工作。詩作上，他曾於1998年獲得委內瑞拉的國家文學獎，及2004年獲得奧克塔維奧·帕斯獎。2003年墨西哥導演[艾力謝路·高沙里斯·依拿力圖執導的劇情片](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/艾力謝路·高沙里斯·依拿力圖 "wikilink")《[靈魂的重量](../Page/靈魂的重量.md "wikilink")》，當中男主角引述了蒙特霍的詩作《地球轉動讓我們靠近》，令更多人認識到歐亨尼奧·蒙特霍。

## 著作

  - Elegos (1967)
  - Muerte y memoria (1972)
  - Algunas palabras (1977)
  - Terredad (1978)
  - Trópico absoluto (1982)
  - Alfabeto del mundo (1986)
  - Adiós al Siglo XX (1992, 1997 a complete version)
  - Papiros amorosos (2002)
  - Chamario (2003, for children)
  - Fábula del escriba (2006)
  - La ventana oblicua (1974)
  - El taller blanco (1983)
  - El cuaderno de Blas Coll (1981)
  - [The Trees](https://web.archive.org/web/20080627150855/http://www.saltpublishing.com/books/smpt/1844710335.htm) (selected poems 1967-2004) - an anthology of selected poems with English translations by the Australian poet Peter Boyle. Original Spanish text appears alongside English translation (Salt Publishing, 2004).
  - [Alphabet of the World](http://www.oupress.com/ECommerce/Book/Detail/1467/alphabet%20of%20the%20world.htm) (Selected Works by Eugenio Montejo). An anthology of selected poems and prose pieces, translated and edited by Kirk Nesset. A bilingual book; the text in Spanish appears also. Critical introduction by Wilfredo Hernandez and Kirk Nesset (University of Oklahoma Press, 2010).

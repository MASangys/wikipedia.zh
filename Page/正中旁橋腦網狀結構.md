**中線旁橋腦網狀系統**（），簡稱**PPRF**或**外旋旁核**（Paraabducens
nucleus）屬於[橋腦](../Page/橋腦.md "wikilink")[網狀結構的一部分](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/網狀結構 "wikilink")。PPRF與的調控有關，特別負責水平眼動，與。

## Input, Output, and Function

The PPRF is located
[anterior](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/人体解剖学方位 "wikilink")
and lateral to the  (MLF). It receives input from the  via the predorsal
bundle and from the  via . The rostral PPRF probably coordinates
vertical saccades; the caudal PPRF may be the generator of horizontal
saccades. In particular, activity of the excitatory burst
[neurons](../Page/神經元.md "wikilink") (EBNs) in the PPRF generates the
"pulse" movement that initiates a saccade. In the case of horizontal
saccades the "pulse" information is conveyed via
[axonal](../Page/轴突.md "wikilink") fibers to the , initiating
lateral . The [angular velocity](../Page/角速度.md "wikilink") of the eye
during horizontal saccade ranges from 100 to 700
[degrees](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/度_\(角\) "wikilink") per second.
Larger saccades have faster pulses; the PPRF is involved in this
determination.

## 受損影響

單側PPRF會造成下列問題：

  - 眼球往病灶處水平轉動的功能喪失，不論受損時眼球的位置為何。
  - 視野偏向受損對側。
  - 眼球向受損對側[震颤](../Page/眼球震颤.md "wikilink")。

雙側受損則會導致，且垂直轉動也會變慢。

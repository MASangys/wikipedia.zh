[原始南島語](../Page/原始南島語.md "wikilink") \*e is realized as /o/ in . Two
Bikol dialects have unique additional consonants, namely , which has an
[边音](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/边音 "wikilink") consonant /l̟/ (also
transcribed as l̪͆ ),\[3\]\[4\] and Buhi-non, which has the
[濁軟顎擦音](../Page/濁軟顎擦音.md "wikilink") /ɣ/.\[5\]

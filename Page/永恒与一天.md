《**永恒与一天**》（）是1998年的一部[希腊](../Page/希腊.md "wikilink")电影，布鲁诺·甘兹主演，[泰奧·安哲羅普洛斯](../Page/泰奧·安哲羅普洛斯.md "wikilink")导演。这部影片赢得了1998年[坎城影展](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/坎城影展 "wikilink")[金棕榈奖](../Page/金棕榈奖.md "wikilink")。

## 演员表

  - [Bruno Ganz](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bruno_Ganz "wikilink") as Alexandre
  - [Isabelle Renauld](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Isabelle_Renauld "wikilink") as Anna
  - [Fabrizio Bentivoglio](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fabrizio_Bentivoglio "wikilink") as the poet
  - Achileas Skevis as the child
  - Alexandra Ladikou as Anna's mother
  - Despina Bebedelli as Alexandre's mother
  - Helene Gerasimidou as Urania
  - Iris Chatziantoniou as Alexandre's daughter
  - Nikos Kouros as Anna's uncle
  - Alekos Oudinotis as Anna's father
  - Nikos Kolovos as the doctor

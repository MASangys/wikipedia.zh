**沃尔多蛤属**（學名：）為[帘蛤目](../Page/帘蛤目.md "wikilink")[鼬眼蛤總科](../Page/鼬眼蛤總科.md "wikilink")[鼬眼蛤科](../Page/鼬眼蛤科.md "wikilink")\[1\]之下的一個屬，[模式種是](../Page/模式種.md "wikilink")*[Lepton
parasiticum](../Page/Lepton_parasiticum.md "wikilink")* <small>Dall,
1876</small>。本屬物種均為在海洋生活的小型蛤類，與[海膽共生](../Page/海膽.md "wikilink")。目前本屬物種基本只在[南大西洋和](../Page/南大西洋.md "wikilink")[南冰洋海域發現](../Page/南冰洋.md "wikilink")，就只有*[Waldo
arthuri](../Page/Waldo_arthuri.md "wikilink")*在東北部太平洋海域發現。

## 物種

本屬有五個物種，分別如下：

  - *[Waldo arthuri](../Page/Waldo_arthuri.md "wikilink")*
    <small>[Valentich-Scott](../Page/Paul_Valentich-Scott.md "wikilink"),
    [Ó Foighil](../Page/Diamard_Ó_Foighil.md "wikilink"), &
    [Li](../Page/Jingchun_Li.md "wikilink"), 2013</small>
  - *[Waldo digitatus](../Page/Waldo_digitatus.md "wikilink")*
    <small>[Zelaya](../Page/Diego_G._Zelaya.md "wikilink") &
    [Ituarte](../Page/Cristián_F._Ituarte.md "wikilink"), 2013</small>
  - *[Waldo parasiticus](../Page/Waldo_parasiticus.md "wikilink")*
    <small>([Dall](../Page/William_Healey_Dall.md "wikilink"),
    1876)</small>
  - *[Waldo
    paucitentaculatus](../Page/Waldo_paucitentaculatus.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Zelaya & Ituarte, 2013</small>
  - *[Waldo trapezialis](../Page/Waldo_trapezialis.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Zelaya & Ituarte, 2002 </small>

**法伯病**（），又名**法伯脂肪肉芽肿病**、**神经酰胺酶缺乏症**、**纤维细胞性异常粘多糖病**、**脂肪肉芽肿病**\[1\]，是一种极为罕见的（迄今为止全球已报道80例\[2\]）常染色体隐性[溶小體儲積症](../Page/溶小體儲積症.md "wikilink")，其特征为缺乏，导致脂肪物质[鞘脂积累](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鞘脂 "wikilink")，进而导致[关节](../Page/关节.md "wikilink")、肝、喉、组织及[中樞神經系統](../Page/中樞神經系統.md "wikilink")异常。正常情况下，可分解人体细胞中的脂肪，而法伯病患者体内负责产生此酶的基因发生突变，因此脂肪物质不能分解，积聚在人体各个部位。

## 命名

法伯病以美国儿科病理学家的姓氏命名\[3\]\[4\]。

## 遺傳病學

法伯病被認為与基因缺陷有关\[5\]。

## 診斷

Disease onset is typically in early infancy but may occur later in life. Children who have the classic form of Farber disease develop symptoms within the first few weeks of life. These symptoms may include moderately impaired mental ability and problems with swallowing. The [liver](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/liver "wikilink"), [heart](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/heart "wikilink") and [kidneys](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/kidney "wikilink") may also be affected. Other symptoms may include [vomiting](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/vomiting "wikilink"), [arthritis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/arthritis "wikilink"), swollen [lymph nodes](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/lymph_node "wikilink"), swollen joints, joint [contractures](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/contracture "wikilink") (chronic shortening of muscles or tendons around joints), [hoarseness](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/hoarseness "wikilink") and [xanthomas](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/xanthoma "wikilink") which thicken around joints as the disease progresses. Patients with breathing difficulty may require a [breathing tube](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tracheal_tube "wikilink").

## 治療

There is no specific treatment for Farber disease. [Corticosteroids](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corticosteroid "wikilink") may be prescribed to relieve pain. [Bone marrow transplants](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bone_marrow_transplant "wikilink") may improve [granulomas](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/granuloma "wikilink") (small masses of inflamed tissue) on patients with little or no lung or nervous system complications. Older patients may have granulomas surgically reduced or removed.

## 预测

多数法伯病患儿2岁便死于肺病。最严重者出生后不久便诊断出，後者通常6个月内死亡。

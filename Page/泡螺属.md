**泡螺属**（学名：）为[腹足綱的一個](../Page/腹足綱.md "wikilink")[海螺的](../Page/海螺.md "wikilink")[属](../Page/属_\(生物\).md "wikilink")。原屬[後鰓目或](../Page/後鰓目.md "wikilink")[頭楯目](../Page/頭楯目.md "wikilink")（Cephalaspidea）的[泡螺科](../Page/泡螺科.md "wikilink")，今屬[異鰓類](../Page/異鰓類.md "wikilink")[下異鰓類支序的](../Page/下異鰓類.md "wikilink")[捻螺總科](../Page/捻螺總科.md "wikilink")[船尾螺科](../Page/船尾螺科.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

本屬物種計有：

  - [白带泡螺](../Page/白带泡螺.md "wikilink")（*[Hydatina
    albocincta](../Page/Hydatina_albocincta.md "wikilink")* <small>Van
    der Hoeven, 1839</small>）：又稱[三带泡螺](../Page/三带泡螺.md "wikilink")
  - *[Hydatina cinctoria](../Page/Hydatina_cinctoria.md "wikilink")*
    ：分佈於[南非](../Page/南非.md "wikilink")；也有意見認為本物種是[黑带泡螺的異名](../Page/黑带泡螺.md "wikilink")，只是兩個物種的顏色有所不同。
  - [精美泡螺](../Page/精美泡螺.md "wikilink") *[Hydatina
    exquisita](../Page/Hydatina_exquisita.md "wikilink")* Voskuil, 1995
  - *[Hydatina montilai](../Page/Hydatina_montilai.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Delsaerdt, 1996</small>: Distribution: Philippines.
  - [泡螺](../Page/泡螺.md "wikilink") *[Hydatina
    physis](../Page/Hydatina_physis.md "wikilink")*
    <small>([Linnaeus](../Page/Carolus_Linnaeus.md "wikilink"),
    1758)</small>
  - *[Hydatina velum](../Page/Hydatina_velum.md "wikilink")*
    <small>[Gmelin](../Page/Johann_Friedrich_Gmelin.md "wikilink"),
    1791</small> (probably color variant and synonym of *Hydatina
    zonata*)
      - Distribution: Indian Ocean, East Africa
      - Length: 25–50 mm
  - *[Hydatina vesicaria](../Page/Hydatina_vesicaria.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Lightfoot, 1786)</small> **brown-lined paper bubble**
      - Distribution: Florida, Mexico, Caribbean.
      - Length: 17–45 mm
      - Description: lives on the bottom at the depth of 8 m to 64 m;
        very fragile and delicate shell with fine brown spiral lines
  - [黑带泡螺](../Page/黑带泡螺.md "wikilink") *[Hydatina
    zonata](../Page/Hydatina_zonata.md "wikilink")*

## 寄生蟲

1964年在[馬拉維湖的](../Page/馬拉維湖.md "wikilink")（Monkey
Bay）發現的一種泡螺屬物種被一種[血吸蟲的幼體](../Page/血吸蟲.md "wikilink")[寄生](../Page/寄生.md "wikilink")\[1\]。

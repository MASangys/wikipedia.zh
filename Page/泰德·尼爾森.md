**泰德·尼爾森**（，）是美國資訊科技與社會學家，他於1963年創造[超文字與](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/超文字 "wikilink")[超媒體這兩個名詞](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/超媒體 "wikilink")，於1965年公布。

## 著作

  - *Life, Love, College, etc.* (1959)
  - *[Computer Lib](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_Lib "wikilink"): You can and must understand computers now / [Dream Machines](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_Lib_/_Dream_Machines "wikilink"): New freedoms through computer screens—a minority report* (1974), Microsoft Press, revised edition 1987: ISBN 0-914845-49-7\[1\]
  - *The Home Computer Revolution* (1977)
  - *[Literary Machines](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Literary_Machines "wikilink"): The report on, and of, Project Xanadu concerning word processing, electronic publishing, hypertext, thinkertoys, tomorrow's intellectual revolution, and certain other topics including knowledge, education and freedom* (1981), Mindful Press, Sausalito, California; publication dates as listed in the 93.1 (1993) edition: 1980–84, 1987, 1990–93
  - *The Future of Information* (1997)
  - *[A Cosmology for a Different Computer Universe: Data Model, Mechanisms, Virtual Machine and Visualization Infrastructure](https://archive.is/20041009214354/http://www.ics.uci.edu/~ejw/csr/nelson_pg.html)*. Journal of Digital Information, Volume 5 Issue 1. Article No. 298, July 16, 2004
  - *[Geeks Bearing Gifts](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geeks_Bearing_Gifts "wikilink"): How The Computer World Got This Way* (2008; [Chapter summaries](https://archive.is/20041009214354/http://www.ics.uci.edu/~ejw/csr/nelson_pg.html))
  - *POSSIPLEX: Movies, Intellect, Creative Control, My Computer Life and the Fight for Civilization* (2010), autobiography\[2\]\[3\]

\-{zh-cn:**泽[字节](../Page/字节.md "wikilink")**或**泽它字节**;
zh-tw:**皆[位元組](../Page/位元組.md "wikilink")**;}-（，縮寫為**ZB**）是一種資訊計量[單位](../Page/单位_\(度量衡\).md "wikilink")，現今通常在標示網路硬碟總容量，或具有大容量的儲存媒介之儲存容量時使用。

## 與其他儲存單位的換算

  - 1ZB = 1,000 [EB](../Page/EB.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZB = 1,000,000 (10<sup>6</sup>)[PB](../Page/PB.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZB = 1,000,000,000 (10<sup>9</sup>)[TB](../Page/TB.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZB = 1,000,000,000,000
    (10<sup>12</sup>)[GB](../Page/GB.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZB = 1,000,000,000,000,000
    (10<sup>15</sup>)[MB](../Page/MB.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZB = 1,000,000,000,000,000,000
    (10<sup>18</sup>)[KB](../Page/KB.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZB = 1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000
    (10<sup>21</sup>)[B](../Page/位元組.md "wikilink")

另外，Zettabyte往往亦可以指[Zebibyte](../Page/Zebibyte.md "wikilink")，其换算公式是：

  - 1ZiB = 1,024 [EiB](../Page/Exbibyte.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZiB = 1,048,576
    (1024<sup>2</sup>)[PiB](../Page/Pebibyte.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZiB = 1,073,741,824
    (1024<sup>3</sup>)[TiB](../Page/Tebibyte.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZiB = 1,099,511,627,776
    (1024<sup>4</sup>)[GiB](../Page/Gibibyte.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZiB = 1,125,899,906,842,624
    (1024<sup>5</sup>)[MiB](../Page/Mebibyte.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZiB = 1,152,921,504,606,846,976
    (1024<sup>6</sup>)[KiB](../Page/Kibibyte.md "wikilink")
  - 1ZiB = 1,180,591,620,717,411,303,424
    (1024<sup>7</sup>)[B](../Page/Byte.md "wikilink")

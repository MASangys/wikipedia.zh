**派勒特岩**（）是[俄勒冈州](../Page/俄勒冈州.md "wikilink")[阿什兰附近的一个突起的](../Page/阿什兰_\(俄勒冈州\).md "wikilink")[火山栓](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/火山栓 "wikilink")，位于[喀斯喀特山脉](../Page/喀斯喀特山脉.md "wikilink")西部的东端，即以东。 Rising thousands of feet above the [Shasta](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shasta_Valley "wikilink") and [Rogue](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rogue_Valley "wikilink") valleys, it is a [landmark](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/landmark "wikilink") distinguishable from over  away. At 25.6 million years old, it is one of the oldest formations of the Cascade Range. The rock is protected by the [Cascade–Siskiyou National Monument](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cascade–Siskiyou_National_Monument "wikilink") and the [Soda Mountain Wilderness](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soda_Mountain_Wilderness "wikilink"). Several trails pass near Pilot Rock, including the [Pacific Crest Trail](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Crest_Trail "wikilink").

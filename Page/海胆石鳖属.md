**海胆石鳖属**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：），是[石鳖目](../Page/石鳖目.md "wikilink")[石鳖科的一属](../Page/石鳖科.md "wikilink")。
本屬的特色是其有尖刺，有八塊\[1\]。

## 物種

根據[WoRMS](../Page/WoRMS.md "wikilink")，本屬有物種八種，分別如下\[2\]

  - *[Acanthopleura
    brevispinosa](../Page/Acanthopleura_brevispinosa.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Sowerby, 1840)</small>
  - *[Acanthopleura
    echinata](../Page/Acanthopleura_echinata.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Barnes, 1824)</small>
  - [鬼石鳖](../Page/鬼石鳖.md "wikilink") *Acanthopleura gemmata* <small>(de
    Blainville, 1825)</small>
  - *[Acanthopleura
    granulata](../Page/Acanthopleura_granulata.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Gmelin, 1791)</small>
  - [琉球石鳖](../Page/琉球石鳖.md "wikilink") *Acanthopleura loochooana*
    <small>(Broderip & Sowerby, 1829)</small>
  - *[Acanthopleura
    planispina](../Page/Acanthopleura_planispina.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Bergenhayn, 1933</small>
  - [海胆石鳖](../Page/海胆石鳖.md "wikilink") *Acanthopleura spinosa*
    <small>(Bruguiere, 1792)</small>
  - *[Acanthopleura
    vaillantii](../Page/Acanthopleura_vaillantii.md "wikilink")*
    <small>de Rochebrune, 1882</small>

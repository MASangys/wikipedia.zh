**淤泥**（），又稱**沉泥**或**粉土**，是[泥土的基本組成成份之一](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/泥土 "wikilink")。地質學中，淤泥是介於[沙土及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/沙土 "wikilink")[黏土](../Page/黏土.md "wikilink")之間，長約2到62微米、直徑4到9微米的一種，主要由[石英](../Page/石英.md "wikilink")及[长石](../Page/长石.md "wikilink")這兩種[礦物](../Page/礦物.md "wikilink")組成\[1\]。淤泥可能以[土壤](../Page/土壤.md "wikilink")的成份或[悬浊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/悬浊液 "wikilink")[水體內的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/水體 "wikilink")[沉積物](../Page/沉積物.md "wikilink")兩種形式出現。當河道泛濫，又或被[山崩](../Page/山崩.md "wikilink")時的[泥石流](../Page/泥石流.md "wikilink")帶動，淤泥會在流動時隨同水體在沿途沉積成為土壤。由於淤泥的[比表面积屬中度](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/比表面积 "wikilink")，非黏粘，有好像塑膠那樣帶彈性。當完全乾燥時，淤泥會變回粉塵般的幼細，但潮濕後立即又變回黏滑。在手持的放大鏡下，淤泥清晰可見，往往帶有尖銳的外表。當以牙齒咬或以舌頭觸摸時，這種棱角亦能感覺到，而這亦是分辨水中的淤泥或黏土的一種方法。

## 來源

淤泥是通過對其原石施以各種物理過程而產生。這些物理過程能夠通過利用原石內沙粒大小的石英[晶体中的缺陷](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/晶体结构 "wikilink")，將這些石英沙粒再細分\[2\]。這些物理過程包括有：岩石[表岩屑](../Page/表岩屑.md "wikilink")的[風化作用](../Page/風化作用.md "wikilink")\[3\]、\[4\]和\[5\]。當中主要的過程是岩石在被水體運輸時造成的[磨損作用](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/磨損作用_\(地質學\) "wikilink")，例如：[河流](../Page/河流作用.md "wikilink")[粉碎](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/粉碎 "wikilink")、[風成](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/風成 "wikilink")和[冰川](../Page/冰川.md "wikilink")研磨\[6\]。在半乾旱環境\[7\]中，產生了大量的淤泥。乾燥後的淤泥又名**粉砂**，特別是由冰川作用形成的淤泥。礦物學上，淤泥的主要成份是[石英](../Page/石英.md "wikilink")和[长石](../Page/长石.md "wikilink")。主要由淤泥組成的[沉积岩](../Page/沉积岩.md "wikilink")被稱為。強烈地震造成的[液化是懸浮在水中的淤泥](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/液化 "wikilink")，水流動力從地下開始向上推。

## 粒徑大小的條件

按[巫登–溫特瓦分級](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巫登–溫特瓦分級 "wikilink")（Udden–Wentworth scale），淤泥的[粒徑](../Page/粒徑.md "wikilink")屬於[粉砂級的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/粉砂 "wikilink")3.9至62.5 µm之間，比[黏土](../Page/黏土.md "wikilink")大，但比[沙](../Page/沙.md "wikilink")粒小。[國際標準化組織](../Page/國際標準化組織.md "wikilink")的[ISO 14688將淤泥的粒徑定為](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_14688 "wikilink")2 µm-6 µm 到 20 µm-63 µm，然後再分為幼粒、中等和粗粒三等。實際上，淤泥在化學上的成份與黏土完全不同；此外淤泥的[粒徑](../Page/粒徑.md "wikilink")在各個方向都大致相同，這一點亦是與黏土不同。再者，淤泥的尺寸往往重疊，即有多種不同粒徑的粉砂混合在一起。反而黏土由通過靜電力保持在一起的薄板狀顆粒形成，因此具有內聚力；相反淤泥並沒有這種內聚力。根據[美国农业部](../Page/美国农业部.md "wikilink")（[USDA](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/USDA "wikilink")）的[土壤質地分類系統](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/土壤質地分類系統 "wikilink")（）沙土和淤泥以0.05 mm的顆粒為分野\[8\]。這套由美国农业部開發的系統後來亦為[联合国粮食及农业组织](../Page/联合国粮食及农业组织.md "wikilink")（[FAO](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/FAO "wikilink")）所採納。In the  (USCS) and the  Soil Classification system, the sand–silt distinction is made at the 0.075 mm particle size (i.e., material passing the \#200  [筲箕](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/筲箕 "wikilink")). Silts and clays are distinguished mechanically by their [塑性變形](../Page/塑性變形.md "wikilink").

## 環境影響

[Burgwall_Jatzke3.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Burgwall_Jatzke3.jpg "fig:Burgwall_Jatzke3.jpg")[艾肖尔斯特](../Page/艾肖尔斯特.md "wikilink")的淤泥湖。\]\]  Silt is easily transported in [水](../Page/水.md "wikilink") or other liquid and is fine enough to be carried long distances by air in the form of [灰塵](../Page/灰塵.md "wikilink"). Thick deposits of silty material resulting from deposition by [風蝕](../Page/風蝕.md "wikilink") are often called [黃土](../Page/黃土.md "wikilink"). Silt and clay contribute to [濁度](../Page/濁度.md "wikilink") in water. Silt is transported by [溪s](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/溪 "wikilink") or by water currents in the [洋](../Page/洋.md "wikilink"). When silt appears as a pollutant in water the phenomenon is known as .

Silt, deposited by annual floods along the [尼罗河](../Page/尼罗河.md "wikilink"), created the rich, fertile soil that sustained the [古埃及](../Page/古埃及.md "wikilink")ian civilization. Silt deposited by the [密西西比河](../Page/密西西比河.md "wikilink") throughout the 20th century has decreased due to a system of [堤](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/堤 "wikilink"), contributing to the disappearance of protective [湿地](../Page/湿地.md "wikilink") and  in the [密西西比河三角洲](../Page/密西西比河三角洲.md "wikilink") region surrounding [新奥尔良](../Page/新奥尔良.md "wikilink").\[9\]

In southeast Bangladesh, in the [諾阿卡利縣](../Page/諾阿卡利縣.md "wikilink"), cross dams were built in the 1960s whereby silt gradually started forming new land called "chars". The district of Noakhali has gained more than  of land in the past 50 years.

With Dutch funding, the Bangladeshi government began to help develop older chars in the late 1970s, and the effort has since become a multi-agency operation building roads, [涵洞s](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/涵洞 "wikilink"), embankments, cyclone shelters, toilets and ponds, as well as distributing land to settlers. By fall 2010, the program will have allotted some  to 21,000 families.\[10\]

A main source of silt in urban rivers is disturbance of soil by [建筑施工](../Page/建筑施工.md "wikilink") activity.\[11\] A main source in rural rivers is [侵蚀作用](../Page/侵蚀作用.md "wikilink") from plowing of farm fields, [皆伐](../Page/皆伐.md "wikilink") or [刀耕火耨](../Page/刀耕火耨.md "wikilink") treatment of [森林](../Page/森林.md "wikilink")s.

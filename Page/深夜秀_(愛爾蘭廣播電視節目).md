《**深夜秀**》（）有時候英語又被稱作「The Late
Late」\[1\]，或者是簡寫成為「LLS」\[2\]\[3\]，是目前世界上播放時間最長且由同一主持人進行的[脱口秀節目](../Page/脱口秀.md "wikilink")\[4\]\[5\]\[6\]\[7\]\[8\]\[9\]\[10\]，同時這也是[愛爾蘭共和國政府國營的廣播機構](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/愛爾蘭共和國 "wikilink")[愛爾蘭廣播電視其主要節目內容](../Page/愛爾蘭廣播電視.md "wikilink")\[11\]。該節目是以現場直播2個多小時的形式，在每年5月至9月期間的星期五晚間21時30分於電視上播出。包含海外地區在內都將《深夜秀》視為愛爾蘭共和國的重要電視節目\[12\]，甚至被視為「愛爾蘭脫口秀」\[13\]。

9.  Questions are sometimes raised over whether *The Tonight Show* on US
    television, which began broadcasting in 1954, should be described as
    the longest running. However there is little continuity between the
    show launched in 1954 and the current format, with the show existing
    under different guises and names ('Tonight' and 'Tonight\! America
    after Dark' are just two of its names in its early years). The show
    in its different formats ran as a variety show, then as a news show
    that was modelled on breakfast show, before adopting the current
    format and the name *The Tonight Show* when [Johnny
    Carson](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johnny_Carson "wikilink") took
    over as permanent presenter in October 1962, some months after *The
    Late Late Show* was launched. Having kept the same name and format
    continuously, *The Late Late Show* is perceived in the media as more
    entitled to the term "the longest running show".

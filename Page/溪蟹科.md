**溪蟹科**（学名：）是[短尾下目](../Page/短尾下目.md "wikilink")([螃蟹](../Page/螃蟹.md "wikilink"))[溪蟹總科之下一個由](../Page/溪蟹總科.md "wikilink")[淡水蟹組成的](../Page/淡水蟹.md "wikilink")[科級](../Page/科_\(生物\).md "wikilink")[分類單元](../Page/分類單元.md "wikilink")。本科包括超過650個物種，分屬接近100個屬。按其第八片[胸腹片](../Page/胸腹片.md "wikilink")(Thoracic
sternite)的不同，我們大致可以分為兩個亞科：

## 分類

[印度北部及](../Page/印度北部.md "wikilink")[緬甸的水域同時有兩個亞科的蟹種存在](../Page/緬甸.md "wikilink")。

### 溪蟹亞科

[溪蟹亞科](../Page/溪蟹亞科.md "wikilink")（[Potaminae](../Page/Potaminae.md "wikilink")
）物種分佈於從環[地中海](../Page/地中海.md "wikilink")(包括[南歐](../Page/南歐.md "wikilink")、[近東](../Page/近東.md "wikilink")、[中東到](../Page/中東.md "wikilink")[北非](../Page/北非.md "wikilink"))、[也門](../Page/也門.md "wikilink")[索科特拉岛往東到](../Page/索科特拉岛.md "wikilink")[印度北部及](../Page/印度北部.md "wikilink")[緬甸](../Page/緬甸.md "wikilink")，包括以下各屬：

  - *[Acanthopotamon](../Page/Acanthopotamon.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Kemp, 1918</small>
  - *[Alcomon](../Page/Alcomon.md "wikilink")* <small>Yeo & Ng,
    2007</small>
  - *[Himalayapotamon](../Page/Himalayapotamon.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Pretzmann, 1966</small>
  - *[Lobothelphusa](../Page/Lobothelphusa.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Bouvier, 1917</small>
  - *[Paratelphusula](../Page/Paratelphusula.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Alcock, 1909</small>
  - [溪蟹屬](../Page/溪蟹屬.md "wikilink")
    *[Potamon](../Page/Potamon.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Socotra](../Page/Socotra_pseudocardisoma.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Cumberlidge & Wranik, 2002</small>
  - *[Socotrapotamon](../Page/Socotrapotamon.md "wikilink")* <small>Apel
    & Brandis, 2000</small>

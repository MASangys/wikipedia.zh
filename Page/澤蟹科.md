**澤蟹科**(學名：)，又名**陸溪蟹科**或**擬地蟹科**，是[短尾下目](../Page/短尾下目.md "wikilink")([螃蟹](../Page/螃蟹.md "wikilink"))的一個[淡水蟹科](../Page/淡水蟹.md "wikilink")\[1\]。

本科在與[束腰蟹科合併之後](../Page/束腰蟹科.md "wikilink")，成為了**澤蟹總科**(）之下的唯一一個科；而本科舊有物屬，現時被劃歸旗下新設的[澤蟹亞科](../Page/澤蟹亞科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

以下為本科旗下的兩個亞科和屬：

  - [澤蟹亞科](../Page/澤蟹亞科.md "wikilink") Gecarcinucinae
      - *[Barytelphusa](../Page/Barytelphusa.md "wikilink")*
      - *[Ceylonthelphusa](../Page/Ceylonthelphusa.md "wikilink")*
      - *[Cylindrotelphusa](../Page/Cylindrotelphusa.md "wikilink")*
      - [澤蟹屬](../Page/澤蟹屬.md "wikilink")
        *[Geothelphusa](../Page/Geothelphusa.md "wikilink")*
      - *[Gubernatoria](../Page/Gubernatoria.md "wikilink")*
      - *[Mahatha](../Page/Mahatha.md "wikilink")*
      - *[Oziothelphusa](../Page/Oziothelphusa.md "wikilink")*
      - *[Pastilla](../Page/Pastilla_\(genus\).md "wikilink")*
      - *[Perbrinckia](../Page/Perbrinckia.md "wikilink")*
      - [塞舌爾蟹屬](../Page/塞舌爾蟹屬.md "wikilink")
        *[Seychellum](../Page/Seychellum.md "wikilink")*
      - *[Spiralothelphusa](../Page/Spiralothelphusa.md "wikilink")*
      - *[Travancoriana](../Page/Travancoriana.md "wikilink")*
  - [束腰蟹亞科](../Page/束腰蟹亞科.md "wikilink") Parathelphusinae ：包括有40個屬。

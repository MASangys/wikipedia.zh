这是[火星](../Page/火星.md "wikilink")的[山脉的列表](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/山脉 "wikilink")，高度以[海平面](../Page/海平面.md "wikilink")为标准。火星的海平面的基准是根据火星的平均气压以及半径确定的。高度并不是指相对于周边地区的高度。

  - A **[Mons](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mons_\(astrogeology\) "wikilink")** (plural *montes*) is a term used in [astrogeology](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/astrogeology "wikilink") to name extraterrestrial mountains and may or may not be of [volcanic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/volcanic "wikilink") origin.
  - A **[Patera](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pateru_\(disambiguation\) "wikilink")** refers to the dish-shaped depression atop a volcano that is not very high compared to its diameter.
  - In planetary geology, a **Tholus** (pl. tholi) is the term used to describe a small domical mountain or hill.
  - Listed Mons elevation is the highest point (at 16 pixels/degree) within the feature.
  - Listed Patera elevation is the average elevation of the shallow dish-shaped depression (the actual 'patera') at the summit.

**灯蛾科**（）是[鱗翅目](../Page/鱗翅目.md "wikilink")[大鱗翅亞目](../Page/大鱗翅亞目.md "wikilink")[夜蛾總科中的一個大科](../Page/夜蛾總科.md "wikilink")，包含目前世界上的1.1萬種飛蛾，其中的6000多种为[新熱帶種](../Page/新熱帶.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

  - [燈蛾群](../Page/燈蛾群.md "wikilink") *Arctiinina*
      - [苔蛾亞科](../Page/苔蛾亞科.md "wikilink") *Lithosiinae*
      - [燈蛾亞科](../Page/燈蛾亞科.md "wikilink") *Arctiinae*
          - [麗燈蛾族](../Page/麗燈蛾族.md "wikilink") *Callimorphini*
          - [燈蛾族](../Page/燈蛾族.md "wikilink") *Arctiini*
      - *Pericopinae*
  - [鹿子蛾群](../Page/鹿子蛾群.md "wikilink") *Ctenuchinina*
      - [鹿子蛾亞科](../Page/鹿子蛾亞科.md "wikilink") *Syntominae*
          - [鹿子蛾族](../Page/鹿子蛾族.md "wikilink") *Syntomini*
          - [豐彩鹿子蛾族](../Page/豐彩鹿子蛾族.md "wikilink") *Euchromiini*
      - [蜂蛾亞科](../Page/蜂蛾亞科.md "wikilink") *Ctenuchinae*\[1\]\[2\]

## 知名品種

以下為燈蛾科裡比較常見的種\[3\]：

  - [红缘灯蛾](../Page/红缘灯蛾.md "wikilink") *Amsacta lactinea*
  - [豹燈蛾](../Page/豹燈蛾.md "wikilink") *Arctia caja*
  - [紅腹蜂蛾](../Page/紅腹蜂蛾.md "wikilink") *Euchromia elegantissima
    formosana*
  - [美国白蛾](../Page/美国白蛾.md "wikilink") *Hyphantria cunea*
  - [亚麻篱灯蛾](../Page/亚麻篱灯蛾.md "wikilink") *Phragmatobia fulginosa*
  - [燈蛾](../Page/燈蛾.md "wikilink") *Pyrrharctia isabella*
  - [红点浑黄灯蛾](../Page/红点浑黄灯蛾.md "wikilink") *Rhyparioides subvaria*
  - [肖浑黄灯蛾](../Page/肖浑黄灯蛾.md "wikilink") *Rhyparioides amurensis*
  - [筋紋燈蛾](../Page/筋紋燈蛾.md "wikilink") *Spilarctia seriatopunctata*
  - [人紋污燈蛾](../Page/人紋污燈蛾.md "wikilink") *Spilarctia subcarnea*
  - [星白雪灯蛾](../Page/星白雪灯蛾.md "wikilink") *Spilosoma menthastri*
  - [红腹灯蛾](../Page/红腹灯蛾.md "wikilink") *Spilosoma punctaria*

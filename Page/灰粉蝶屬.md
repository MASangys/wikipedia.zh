**灰粉蝶屬**（學名：***Hypsochila***）是[粉蝶科](../Page/粉蝶科.md "wikilink")[粉蝶亞科](../Page/粉蝶亞科.md "wikilink")[粉蝶族](../Page/粉蝶族.md "wikilink")[粉蝶亞族裡的一個屬](../Page/粉蝶亞族.md "wikilink")。共有11個物種，分佈於[新熱帶界](../Page/新熱帶界.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

  - [銀灰粉蝶](../Page/銀灰粉蝶.md "wikilink") *Hypsochila argyrodice*
  - [明灰粉蝶](../Page/明灰粉蝶.md "wikilink") *Hypsochila galactodice*
  - [箭紋灰粉蝶](../Page/箭紋灰粉蝶.md "wikilink") *Hypsochila huemul*
  - [美脈唇粉蝶](../Page/美脈唇粉蝶.md "wikilink") *Hypsochila microdice* =
    *Tatochila microdice*
  - [彭灰粉蝶](../Page/彭灰粉蝶.md "wikilink") *Hypsochila penai*
  - [灰粉蝶](../Page/灰粉蝶.md "wikilink") *Hypsochila wagenknechti*
  - *Hypsochila \[n. sp.\]*

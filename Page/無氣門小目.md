**無氣門小目**（），
是[蛛形綱](../Page/蛛形綱.md "wikilink")[蜱蟎亞綱](../Page/蜱蟎亞綱.md "wikilink")[蟎形總目](../Page/蟎形總目.md "wikilink")[恙蟎目](../Page/恙蟎目.md "wikilink")[甲蟎亞目](../Page/甲蟎亞目.md "wikilink")[Desmonomata下目之下的三個小目之一](../Page/Desmonomata.md "wikilink")\[1\]。
原來與甲蟎亞目同屬[疥蟎亞目](../Page/疥蟎亞目.md "wikilink")（Sarcoptiformes）\[2\]之下。

本分類的物種大多數都是[哺乳動物身上的](../Page/哺乳動物.md "wikilink")[寄生蟲](../Page/寄生蟲.md "wikilink")。
Most notorious among these are the
[Psoroptidia](../Page/Psoroptidia.md "wikilink"), which contain such
notable [taxa](../Page/taxa.md "wikilink") as [feather
mites](../Page/feather_mite.md "wikilink") and *[Sarcoptes
scabiei](../Page/Sarcoptes_scabiei.md "wikilink")*.

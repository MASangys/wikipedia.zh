**無臂鰨科**是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鰈亞目的其中一科](../Page/鰈亞目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**無臂鰨科**下分7個屬，如下：

### 無臂鰨屬(*Achirus*)

  - [亞馬遜河無臂鰨](../Page/亞馬遜河無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Achirus achirus*)
  - [斜體無臂鰨](../Page/斜體無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Achirus declivis*)
  - [克氏無臂鰨](../Page/克氏無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Achirus klunzingeri*)
  - [線紋無臂鰨](../Page/線紋無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Achirus lineatus*)
  - [馬薩無臂鰨](../Page/馬薩無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Achirus mazatlanus*)
  - [馬氏無臂鰨](../Page/馬氏無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Achirus mucuri*)
  - [南美無臂鰨](../Page/南美無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Achirus novoae*)
  - [盾紋無臂鰨](../Page/盾紋無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Achirus scutum*)
  - [斑紋無臂鰨](../Page/斑紋無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Achirus zebrinus*)

### 無鋸鰨屬(*Apionichthys*)

  - [杜氏無鋸鰨](../Page/杜氏無鋸鰨.md "wikilink")(*Apionichthys dumerili*)
  - [鰭狀無鋸鰨](../Page/鰭狀無鋸鰨.md "wikilink")(*Apionichthys finis*)
  - [梅氏無鋸鰨](../Page/梅氏無鋸鰨.md "wikilink")(*Apionichthys menezesi*)
  - [納特氏無鋸鰨](../Page/納特氏無鋸鰨.md "wikilink")(*Apionichthys nattereri*)
  - [羅氏無鋸鰨](../Page/羅氏無鋸鰨.md "wikilink")(*Apionichthys rosai*)
  - [索爾氏無鋸鰨](../Page/索爾氏無鋸鰨.md "wikilink")(*Apionichthys sauli*)
  - [塞氏無鋸鰨](../Page/塞氏無鋸鰨.md "wikilink")(*Apionichthys seripierriae*)

### 甲鰨屬(*Catathyridium*)

  - [加曼氏甲鰨](../Page/加曼氏甲鰨.md "wikilink")(*Catathyridium garmani*)
  - [格氏甲鰨](../Page/格氏甲鰨.md "wikilink")(*Catathyridium grandirivi*)
  - [詹氏甲鰨](../Page/詹氏甲鰨.md "wikilink")(*Catathyridium jenynsii*)
  - [洛倫氏甲鰨](../Page/洛倫氏甲鰨.md "wikilink")(*Catathyridium lorentzii*)

### 裸無臂鰨屬(*Gymnachirus*)

  - [黑裸無臂鰨](../Page/黑裸無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Gymnachirus melas*)
  - [裸無臂鰨](../Page/裸無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Gymnachirus nudus*)
  - [特氏裸無臂鰨](../Page/特氏裸無臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Gymnachirus texae*)

### 下線鰨屬(*Hypoclinemus*)

  - [亞馬遜河下線鰨](../Page/亞馬遜河下線鰨.md "wikilink")(*Hypoclinemus mentalis*)

### 醜臂鰨屬(*Pnictes*)

  - [貪食醜臂鰨](../Page/貪食醜臂鰨.md "wikilink")(*Pnictes asphyxiatus*)

### 三鰭鰨屬(*Trinectes*)

  - [纓三鰭鰨](../Page/纓三鰭鰨.md "wikilink")(*Trinectes fimbriatus*)
  - [河溪三鰭鰨](../Page/河溪三鰭鰨.md "wikilink")(*Trinectes fluviatilis*)
  - [豐澤三鰭鰨](../Page/豐澤三鰭鰨.md "wikilink")(*Trinectes fonsecensis*)

  - [斑點三鰭鰨](../Page/斑點三鰭鰨.md "wikilink")(*Trinectes maculatus*)
  - [小眼三鰭鰨](../Page/小眼三鰭鰨.md "wikilink")(*Trinectes microphthalmus*)
  - [蓋三鰭鰨](../Page/蓋三鰭鰨.md "wikilink")(*Trinectes opercularis*)

  - [黃尾三鰭鰨](../Page/黃尾三鰭鰨.md "wikilink")(*Trinectes xanthurus*)

**狂野夏洛特**（Good
Charlotte），1996年在[美国](../Page/美国.md "wikilink")[马里兰州成立的流行摇滚乐队](../Page/马里兰州.md "wikilink")，名称由克罗·比奇·约克（Carol
Beach York）所著儿童书《Good Charlotte: The Girls of Good Day
Orphanage》的名称而得来，现有五名成员。

    (2000)
  - *[The Young and the
    Hopeless](../Page/The_Young_and_the_Hopeless.md "wikilink")* (2002)
  - *[The Chronicles of Life and

  - *[Good Morning Revival](../Page/Good_Morning_Revival.md "wikilink")*
    (2007)
  - *[Greatest Remixes](../Page/Greatest_Remixes.md "wikilink")* (2008)
  - *[Cardiology](../Page/Cardiology_\(album\).md "wikilink")* (2010)
  - *[Youth Authority](../Page/Youth_Authority.md "wikilink")* (2016)

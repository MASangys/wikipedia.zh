### 特别版

On September 30, 2003, Disney released a Special Edition of the
soundtrack with two newly-added tracks:

1.

    <li value=13>

    "The Morning Report" – performed by [James Earl
    Jones](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Earl_Jones "wikilink"),
    [Jeff
    Bennett](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeff_Bennett "wikilink") and
    Evan Saucedo

2.  "[Can You Feel the Love
    Tonight](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Can_You_Feel_the_Love_Tonight "wikilink")
    (Remix)" – performed by Elton John

In addition, on the same day, a vinyl record picture disc of the
original soundtrack was released in a limited edition of 4,000.

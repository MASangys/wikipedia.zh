**狼蛛總科**是[新蛛亞目中八眼蜘蛛的一個](../Page/新蛛亞目.md "wikilink")[總科](../Page/總科.md "wikilink")，包含12個科：

  - [櫛蛛科](../Page/櫛蛛科.md "wikilink") Ctenidae
  - [狼蛛科](../Page/狼蛛科.md "wikilink") Lycosidae
  - [Neolanidae](../Page/Neolanidae.md "wikilink")
  - [貓蛛科](../Page/貓蛛科.md "wikilink") Oxyopidae
  - [跑蛛科](../Page/跑蛛科.md "wikilink") Pisauridae
  - [褸網蛛科](../Page/褸網蛛科.md "wikilink") Psechridae
  - [Senoculidae](../Page/Senoculidae.md "wikilink")
  - [Stiphidiidae](../Page/Stiphidiidae.md "wikilink")
  - [行蛛科](../Page/行蛛科.md "wikilink") Trechaleidae
  - [佐蛛科](../Page/佐蛛科.md "wikilink") Zoridae
  - [Zorocratidae](../Page/Zorocratidae.md "wikilink")
  - [逸蛛科](../Page/逸蛛科.md "wikilink") Zoropsidae

根據 Fang et. al. (2000) 的研究(2000)\[1\]，由粒線體的12S和16S rDNA可以知道一些科之間的關係，如下：

`       +---- 褸網蛛科 `*`Psechridae`*
`    +--|`
`    |  +---- 貓蛛科 `*`Oxyopidae`*
`----|`
`    |  +---- 跑蛛科 `*`Pisauridae`*
`    +--|`
`       +---- 狼蛛科 `*`Lycosidae`*

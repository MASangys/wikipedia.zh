**貓亞科**是[貓科中的一個](../Page/貓科.md "wikilink")[亞科](../Page/亞科.md "wikilink")，其包括了如下的屬和種。

  - [獵豹屬](../Page/獵豹屬.md "wikilink") *Acinoynx*
      - [獵豹](../Page/獵豹.md "wikilink") *Acinoynx jubatus*
  - [獰貓屬](../Page/獰貓屬.md "wikilink") *Caracal*
      - [獰貓](../Page/獰貓.md "wikilink") *Caracal caracal*
  - [金貓屬](../Page/金貓屬.md "wikilink") *Catopuma*
      - [婆羅洲金貓](../Page/婆羅洲金貓.md "wikilink") *Catopuma badia*
      - [金貓](../Page/金貓.md "wikilink") *Catopuma temminckii*
  - [草原貓屬](../Page/草原貓屬.md "wikilink") *Oncifelis*
      - [南美草原貓](../Page/南美草原貓.md "wikilink") *Oncifelis colocolo*
      - [喬氏貓](../Page/喬氏貓.md "wikilink") *Oncifelis geoffroyi*
      - [南美林貓](../Page/南美林貓.md "wikilink") *Oncifelis guigna*
  - [山原貓屬](../Page/山原貓屬.md "wikilink") *Oreailurus*
      - [山原貓](../Page/山原貓.md "wikilink") *Leopardus jacobita*
  - [虎貓屬](../Page/虎貓屬.md "wikilink") *Leopardus*
      - [虎貓](../Page/虎貓.md "wikilink") *Leopardus pardalis*
      - [小斑虎貓](../Page/小斑虎貓.md "wikilink") *Leopardus tigrinus*
      - [長尾虎貓](../Page/長尾虎貓.md "wikilink") *Leopardus wiedii*
  - [藪貓屬](../Page/藪貓屬.md "wikilink") *Leptailurus*
      - [藪貓](../Page/藪貓.md "wikilink") *Leptailurus serval*
  - [猞猁屬](../Page/猞猁屬.md "wikilink") *Lynx*
      - [加拿大猞猁](../Page/加拿大猞猁.md "wikilink") *Lynx canadensis*
      - [猞猁](../Page/歐亞猞猁.md "wikilink") *Lynx lynx*
      - [西班牙猞猁](../Page/西班牙猞猁.md "wikilink") *Lynx pardinus*
      - [短尾貓](../Page/短尾貓.md "wikilink") *Lynx rufus*
  - [紋貓屬](../Page/紋貓屬.md "wikilink") *Pardofelis*
      - [紋貓](../Page/紋貓.md "wikilink") *Pardofelis marmorata*
  - [豹貓屬](../Page/豹貓屬.md "wikilink") *Prionailurus*
      - [豹貓](../Page/豹貓.md "wikilink") *Prionailurus bengalensis*
      - [扁頭貓](../Page/扁頭貓.md "wikilink") *Prionailurus planiceps*
      - [鏽斑豹貓](../Page/鏽斑豹貓.md "wikilink") *Prionailurus rubiginosus*
      - [漁貓](../Page/漁貓.md "wikilink") *Prionailurus viverrinus*
  - [非洲金貓屬](../Page/非洲金貓屬.md "wikilink") *Profelis*
      - [非洲金貓](../Page/非洲金貓.md "wikilink") *Profelis aurata*
  - [美洲金貓屬](../Page/美洲金貓屬.md "wikilink") *Puma*
      - [美洲獅](../Page/美洲獅.md "wikilink") *Puma concolor*
      - [細腰貓](../Page/細腰貓.md "wikilink") *Puma yagouaroundi*
  - [貓屬](../Page/貓屬.md "wikilink") *Felis*
      - [荒漠貓](../Page/荒漠貓.md "wikilink") *Felis bieti*
      - [叢林貓](../Page/叢林貓.md "wikilink") *Felis chaus*
      - [沙貓](../Page/沙貓.md "wikilink") *Felis margarita*
      - [黑足貓](../Page/黑足貓.md "wikilink") *Felis nigripes*
      - [斑貓](../Page/斑貓.md "wikilink") *Felis sylvestris*
          - [家貓](../Page/家貓.md "wikilink") *Felis catus*
      - [兔猻](../Page/兔猻.md "wikilink") *Felis manul*

下列的屬有時會被歸在[豹亞科裡](../Page/豹亞科.md "wikilink")：

  - [獰貓屬](../Page/獰貓屬.md "wikilink") *Caracal*
  - [藪貓屬](../Page/藪貓屬.md "wikilink") *Leptailurus*
  - [非洲金貓屬](../Page/非洲金貓屬.md "wikilink") *Profelis*

下列的屬之前被歸在[豹亞科裡](../Page/豹亞科.md "wikilink")：

  - [金貓屬](../Page/金貓屬.md "wikilink") *Catopuma*
  - [猞猁屬](../Page/猞猁屬.md "wikilink") *Lynx*

[Catdryfood.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Catdryfood.jpg "fig:Catdryfood.jpg") **猫粮**是[猫](../Page/猫.md "wikilink")吃的食物。

猫对饮食有着特定的营养需求。\[1\]某些营养成分，包括多种[维生素](../Page/维生素.md "wikilink")和[氨基酸](../Page/氨基酸.md "wikilink")，会因为制造过程中的温度、压强和化学处理而被降低有效成分，因此必须在制造后再添加，以免破坏营养成分而导致营养缺乏。\[2\]\[3\]例如，在肉类中被发现的[胺基牛磺酸](../Page/牛磺酸.md "wikilink")，在加工过程中会被降解，因此，[合成牛磺酸通常是后期添加的](../Page/牛磺酸.md "wikilink")。长期的牛磺酸缺乏，例如长期喂食缺乏牛磺酸的[狗粮](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/狗粮 "wikilink")，可能会导致[视网膜](../Page/视网膜.md "wikilink")变性，[失明](../Page/失明.md "wikilink")和[心脏损害](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/心脏 "wikilink")。

## 商业猫粮

大多数店售的猫粮是干的，在美国也被称为*kibble*，或湿罐装的形式。有些生产商销售冷冻原料饲料和预混料产品，以迎合那些自制猫粮的猫主们的需要。

### 干粮

[Katzenfutter.JPG](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Katzenfutter.JPG "fig:Katzenfutter.JPG") 干粮（水分8-10%）通常由高热量和压力下挤压蒸煮，又或為熱風乾燥、急凍乾燥製成。主要成分為各式澱粉（玉米、穀物、其他類澱粉），添加少許肉沫、香料，以增加适口性，和其他的微量成分，其中对热敏感的维生素，在挤出过程中可能被破坏，可能適度增加份量。另外會添加油脂、防腐劑，用以增加保存期限。

### 湿粮

[Canned_Cat_Food1.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Canned_Cat_Food1.jpg "fig:Canned_Cat_Food1.jpg") [罐头或湿粮](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/罐头 "wikilink")（湿度75-78%）通常是3[盎司](../Page/盎司.md "wikilink")（85克），5.5盎司（156克），13盎司（369克）的规格。也会以铝箔袋的形式出售。

採用濕食是增加寵物飲水量的有效方法，可降低寵物因缺水而罹患疾病（如慢性腎衰竭、尿結石）的風險。以貓來說，一隻貓每天所需的水分，每公斤（體重）就需要60毫升的水分，若以罐頭為主食，就已攝取了93％每天所需水分；反之，若主食為僅含10%水分的乾飼料，一隻4公斤的貓，還需要自發性的喝200毫升的水，然而由於貓的口渴機制並不發達，往往會因此缺水。\[6\]

### 素食或纯素食品

目前有[素食和](../Page/素食主義.md "wikilink")[純素食的猫粮销售](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/純素食主義 "wikilink")。猫為肉食动物，不能从植物食品中合成这些养分，如[牛磺酸](../Page/牛磺酸.md "wikilink")和[花生四烯酸](../Page/花生四烯酸.md "wikilink")，故長期食用可能將對貓產生負面效果。\[7\]有些素食猫粮品牌都标有其制造商遵循AAFCO猫粮营养标准。

## 标签

In the United States, cat foods labeled as "complete and balanced" must meet standards established by the [Association of American Feed Control Officials](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Association_of_American_Feed_Control_Officials "wikilink") (AAFCO) either by meeting a nutrient profile or by passing a feeding trial. Cat Food Nutrient Profiles were established in 1992 and updated in 1995 by the AAFCO's Feline Nutrition Expert Subcommittee. The updated profiles replaced the previous recommendations set by the [National Research Council](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_National_Research_Council "wikilink") (NRC). Certain manufacturers label their products with terms such as premium, ultra premium, natural and holistic. Such terms currently have no legal definitions.  However, "While most of the food supplied comes from within the US, the FDA ensures that standards are met within our borders even when components come from countries with less stringent ideas of safety or label integrity."\[8\]

## 能量需求

成年猫的能量需求范围是，懒猫每天每公斤体重消耗60-70千卡的代谢能量，活泼的则要消耗80-90千卡。5周龄的小猫要250千卡。随着年龄的增长，30周到50周成年的需求下降至100千卡。怀孕的母猫需要约90-100千卡，哺乳期的母猫需要90-270千卡视产崽数量而定。\[9\]

## 营养和功能

[维生素](../Page/维生素.md "wikilink")缺乏可导致广泛的临床异常反映其代谢作用的多样性。十二种矿物质被称为是猫科动物的必需营养素。钙，磷是强健骨骼和牙齿的关键。猫需要其他矿物质，如镁，钾，钠，神经冲动的传递，肌肉收缩，细胞信号转导。许多矿物质在体内的微量存在，包括硒，铜，钼，作为佣工多种酶促反应。\[10\]

## 饮食和疾病

### 素食和纯素食饮食

素食或为猫准备的素食是有争议的。据[美国国家科学研究委员会称](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/美国国家科学研究委员会 "wikilink")，“猫需要特定的营养，而不是特定的饲料（Cats require specific nutrients, not specific feedstuffs.）”\[11\] 。[国际素食联盟](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/国际素食联盟 "wikilink")\[12\]，[素食协会](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/完全素食协会_\(英国\) "wikilink")\[13\]和[PETA](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/PETA "wikilink")\[14\]\[15\]是一些支持猫的素食和纯素食饮食的组织。不推荐喂猫吃素食，\[16\]，[美国爱护动物协会也不推荐这么做](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/美国爱护动物协会 "wikilink")。\[17\]

Vegan or vegetarian diets for cats are controversial. According to the [United States National Research Council](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_National_Research_Council "wikilink"), "Cats require specific nutrients, not specific feedstuffs."\[18\] The [International Vegetarian Union](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Vegetarian_Union "wikilink"),\[19\] the [Vegan Society](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vegan_Society "wikilink")\[20\] and [PETA](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/PETA "wikilink")\[21\]\[22\] are some of the organizations that support a vegan or vegetarian diet for cats. The [Animal Protection Institute](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Animal_Protection_Institute "wikilink") does not recommend a vegetarian diet for cats,\[23\] and neither does the [American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Society_for_the_Prevention_of_Cruelty_to_Animals "wikilink") (ASPCA).\[24\]

Not all animal advocacy groups take a firm position either way. The Association of Veterinarians for Animal Rights (now [Humane Society Veterinary Medical Association](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Humane_Society_Veterinary_Medical_Association "wikilink")) accepts that it is possible for a plant-based diet to be nutritionally adequate but stated in August 2006 that such diets "cannot at this time be reliably assured".\[25\] This position was based on a 2004 study demonstrating that of two commercially available vegetarian cat diets tested, both were nutritionally deficient.\[26\] The formulation error in one of these diets was promptly identified and corrected.\[27\] Nevertheless, it remains likely that formulation errors will result in nutritional deficiencies in a wide range of commercially available diets from time to time, whether meat-based, vegetarian or vegan. Hence, regular (at least, annual) veterinary checkups of all companion animals is recommended, and brands may be occasionally varied.\[28\]

In 2006, the first study of the health of a population of long-term vegetarian cats was published in the Journal of the American Veterinary Medical Association. Most of the cats were fed a commercially-available vegan diet, though 35% were allowed outdoors. The study consisted of telephone questionnaires of the caregivers of 32 cats, and analysis of blood samples from some of them. The blood samples were tested for [taurine](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/taurine "wikilink") and [cobalamin](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/cobalamin "wikilink") deficiencies. Cobalamin levels were normal in all cats. Taurine levels were low in 3 out of 17 cats tested, but not low enough to be considered deficient. 97% of the caregivers perceived their cats to be healthy, including those with low taurine levels.\[29\]

### 家庭自制猫粮

很多宠物主人用自制食品来喂养猫。这些食品通常由某种形式的生或熟的肉、骨头、蔬菜，和补充品（如[牛磺酸](../Page/牛磺酸.md "wikilink")和多种[维生素](../Page/维生素.md "wikilink")）。

### 双酚A

一份2004年的研究报告显示，罐装食品外包装含有[双酚A跟](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/双酚A "wikilink")[甲亢猫有关](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/甲亢 "wikilink")。\[30\]

### 食物过敏

Food allergy is a non-seasonal disease with skin and/or gastrointestinal disorders. The main complaint is excessive scratching ([Pruritus](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pruritus "wikilink")) which is usually resistant to treatment by [steroidal](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glucocorticoid "wikilink") [anti-inflammatory](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/anti-inflammatory "wikilink") [drugs](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/medication "wikilink"). The exact prevalence of food allergy in cats remains unknown. In 20 to 30% of the cases, cats have concurrent allergic diseases ([atopy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/atopy "wikilink") / flea-allergic [dermatitis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/dermatitis "wikilink")). A reliable diagnosis can only be made with [dietary elimination-challenge trials](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elimination_diet "wikilink"). [Allergy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Food_allergy "wikilink") [testing](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allergy_testing "wikilink") is necessary for the identification of the causative food component(s). Therapy consists of avoiding the offending food component(s).\[31\]

### 营养不良

非常规饮食喂猫，营养不良可能是一个问题，但因飼料發明僅約六十年，各家飼料產品因成本考量、價格廉價而不可能製作出符合健康之飼料，故貓咪是否應食用加工飼料而有很大爭議。用单一的食物喂猫，[淡水鱼](../Page/淡水鱼.md "wikilink")会得[硫胺素缺乏症](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/硫胺素 "wikilink")。只喂对肝就会[维生素A中毒](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/维生素A "wikilink")。此外，专门肉食为主的饮食可能含有过量的[蛋白质](../Page/蛋白质.md "wikilink")和[磷](../Page/磷.md "wikilink")而缺乏[钙](../Page/钙.md "wikilink")，[维生素E](../Page/维生素E.md "wikilink")，[微量元素如](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/微量元素 "wikilink")[铜](../Page/铜.md "wikilink")，[锌](../Page/锌.md "wikilink")，[钾](../Page/钾.md "wikilink")等。[能量密度](../Page/能量密度.md "wikilink")也必须保持相对于其他的营养成分。当[植物油用来维持能量平衡](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/植物油 "wikilink")，猫可能无法找到的食物适口性。\[32\]

### 猫粮召回

The broad [pet food recalls](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Product_recall "wikilink") starting in March 2007 came in response to reports of [renal failure](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/renal_failure "wikilink") in pets consuming mostly wet pet foods made with [wheat gluten](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/wheat_gluten "wikilink") from a single [Chinese](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/People's_Republic_of_China "wikilink") company beginning in February 2007. Overall, several major companies recalled more than 100 brands of pet foods with most of the recalled product coming from Menu Foods. The most likely cause according to the FDA is the presence of [melamine](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/melamine "wikilink") in the wheat gluten of the affected foods. Melamine is known to falsely inflate the protein content rating of substances in laboratory tests. The economic impact on the pet food market has been extensive, with Menu Foods alone losing roughly [$](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Dollar "wikilink")30 Million from the recall.

Rations greater than 4.5 <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/calorie" title="wikilink">kcal</a>/<a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/gram_(unit)" title="wikilink">g</a> should be corrected for <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/energy_density" title="wikilink">energy density</a>; rations less than 4.0 <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/calorie" title="wikilink">kcal</a>/<a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/gram_(unit)" title="wikilink">g</a> should <em>not</em> be corrected for energy.</li>
<li>Although a true requirement for <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/fat" title="wikilink">fat</a> per se has not been established, the minimum level was based on recognition of <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/fat" title="wikilink">fat</a> as a source of <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/essential_fatty_acids" title="wikilink">essential fatty acids</a>, as a carrier of <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/fat-soluble_vitamins" title="wikilink">fat-soluble vitamins</a>, to enhance palatability, and to supply an adequate <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/caloric_density" title="wikilink">caloric density</a>.</li>
<li>If the mean <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/urine_pH" title="wikilink">urine pH</a> of cats fed <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ad_libitum" title="wikilink">ad libitum</a> is not below 6.4, the risk of <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/struvite_urolithiasis" title="wikilink">struvite urolithiasis</a> increases as the <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/magnesium" title="wikilink">magnesium</a> content of the diet increases.</li>
<li>Because of very poor <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/bioavailability" title="wikilink">bioavailability</a>, <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/iron" title="wikilink">iron</a> from <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/carbonate" title="wikilink">carbonate</a> or <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/oxide" title="wikilink">oxide</a> sources that are added to the diet should not be considered as components in meeting the minimum nutrient level.</li>
<li>Because of very poor <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/bioavailability" title="wikilink">bioavailability</a>, <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/copper" title="wikilink">copper</a> from <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/oxide" title="wikilink">oxide</a> sources that are added to the diet should not be considered as components in meeting the minimum nutrient level.</li>
<li>Add 10 <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_units" title="wikilink">IU</a> <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/vitamin_E" title="wikilink">vitamin E</a> above minimum level per <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/gram_(unit)" title="wikilink">gram</a> of <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/fish_oil" title="wikilink">fish oil</a> per kilogram of diet.</li>
<li><a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vitamin_K" title="wikilink">Vitamin K</a> does not need to be added unless diet contains greater than 25 <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/percent" title="wikilink">percent</a> fish on a dry matter basis.</li>

<li><a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biotin" title="wikilink">Biotin</a> does not need to be added unless diet contains <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/antimicrobial" title="wikilink">antimicrobial</a> or <a href="https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/antivitamin" title="wikilink">antivitamin</a> compounds.</li>

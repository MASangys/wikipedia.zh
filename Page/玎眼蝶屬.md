**玎眼蝶屬**（學名：***Dingana***）是[蛺蝶科](../Page/蛺蝶科.md "wikilink")[眼蝶亞科](../Page/眼蝶亞科.md "wikilink")[絡眼蝶族中的一個屬](../Page/絡眼蝶族.md "wikilink")。物種分佈於非洲南部\[1\]。

## 物種

  - *[Dingana fraterna](../Page/Dingana_fraterna.md "wikilink")*
  - [紅帶玎眼蝶](../Page/紅帶玎眼蝶.md "wikilink") *Dingana alaedeus*
  - *[Dingana alticola](../Page/Dingana_alticola.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Dingana angusta](../Page/Dingana_angusta.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Dingana clara](../Page/Dingana_clara.md "wikilink")*
  - [玎眼蝶](../Page/玎眼蝶.md "wikilink") *Dingana dingana*
  - [黃斑玎眼蝶](../Page/黃斑玎眼蝶.md "wikilink") *Dingana jerinae*

**玫瑰山**（）是美国[纽约市](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/纽约市 "wikilink")[曼哈顿的一片最近恢复名称的街区](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/曼哈顿 "wikilink")\[1\]。它南到25街，北到30街，东到[第三大道](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/第三大道 "wikilink")，西到[麦迪逊大道或](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/麦迪逊大道 "wikilink")[第五大道](../Page/第五大道.md "wikilink")。毗邻的街区，北面是茉莉山（Murray
Hill），东面是基普湾（Kips
Bay），南面是[熨斗区和](../Page/熨斗区.md "wikilink")[葛莱美西公园](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/葛莱美西公园 "wikilink")，西面是诺麦狄（NoMad）\[2\]。

2.  Neighborhoods in New York City do not have official status, and
    their boundaries are not specifically set by the city. (There are a
    number of [Community
    Boards](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Community_Boards_of_Manhattan "wikilink"),
    whose boundaries are officially set, but these are fairly large and
    generally contain a number of neighborhoods, and the [neighborhood
    map](http://www.nyc.gov/html/dcp/html/neighbor/neigh.shtml) issued
    by the Department of City Planning only shows the largest ones.)
    Because of this, the definition of where neighborhoods begin and end
    is subject to a variety of forces, including the efforts of real
    estate concerns to promote certain areas, the use of neighborhood
    names in media news reports, and the everyday usage of people. The
    eastern boundary of
    [NoMad](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/NoMad "wikilink"), an
    up-and-coming neighborhood north and west of Madison Square Park,
    and the western boundary of Rose Hill, is thus uncertain, and
    proponents of both may make claim to the assets that lie in the
    borderlands between them.

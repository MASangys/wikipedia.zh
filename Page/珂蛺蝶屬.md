**珂蛺蝶屬**（學名：*Evena*）是[線蛺蝶亞科](../Page/線蛺蝶亞科.md "wikilink")[翠蛺蝶族中的一](../Page/翠蛺蝶族.md "wikilink")[屬](../Page/屬_\(生物\).md "wikilink")。物種分佈於[中非及](../Page/中非.md "wikilink")[西非的森林](../Page/西非.md "wikilink")\[1\]。

## 物種

  - [安珂蛺蝶](../Page/安珂蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Evena angustatum*
  - [珂蛺蝶](../Page/珂蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Evena crithea*
  - *[Evena niji](../Page/Evena_niji.md "wikilink")*
  - [奧珂蛺蝶](../Page/奧珂蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Evena oberthueri*
  - [白帶珂蛺蝶](../Page/白帶珂蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Evena sikorana*

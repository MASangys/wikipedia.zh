**琼·瓦拉赫·斯科特**（，
1941年12月18日-）是一位[美国](../Page/美国.md "wikilink")[历史学家](../Page/史家.md "wikilink")，亦是[普林斯顿高等研究院](../Page/普林斯顿高等研究院.md "wikilink")[教授](../Page/教授.md "wikilink")，专业为法国史和[性别史](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/性別_\(文化\) "wikilink")。

1962年，
她于[布兰代斯大学毕业](../Page/布兰戴斯大学.md "wikilink")。1969年，于[威斯康星大学麦迪逊分校获得](../Page/威斯康星大学麦迪逊分校.md "wikilink")[博士学位](../Page/博士.md "wikilink")。1985年后，分别担任[伊利諾大學芝加哥分校副教授](../Page/伊利諾大學芝加哥分校.md "wikilink")、[西北大学副教授以及](../Page/西北大学_\(伊利诺伊州\).md "wikilink")[布朗大学教授](../Page/布朗大学.md "wikilink")。

## 著作

  - *The glassworkers of Carmaux: French craftsmen and political action
    in a nineteenth-century city*, Harvard University Press, 1974.
  - *Gender and the politics of history*, Columbia University Press,
    1988.
  - *Only paradoxes to offer: French feminists and the rights of man*,
    Harvard University Press, 1996.
  - *Parité\!: sexual equality and the crisis of French universalism*,
    University of Chicago Press, 2005.
  - *The politics of the veil*, Princeton University Press, 2007.

### 合作著作

  - *Women, work, and family*, Louise A. Tilly and Joan W. Scott, Holt,
    Rinehart and Winston, 1978.

### 编著

  - *Feminists theorize the political*, edited by Judith Butler and Joan
    W. Scott, Routledge, 1992.
  - *Feminism and history*, edited by Joan Wallach Scott, Oxford
    University Press, 1996.
  - *Transitions, environments, translations: feminisms in international
    politics*, edited by Joan W. Scott, Cora Kaplan, Debra Keates,
    Routledge, 1997.
  - *Schools of thought: twenty-five years of interpretive social
    science*, edited by Joan W. Scott, Debra Keates, Princeton
    University Press, 2001.
  - *Going public: feminism and the shifting boundaries of the private
    sphere*, edited by Joan W. Scott and Debra Keates, University of
    Illinois Press, 2004.
  - *Women's studies on the edge*, edited by Joan Wallach Scott, Duke
    University Press, 2008.

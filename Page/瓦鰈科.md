**瓦鰈科**（[學名](../Page/學名.md "wikilink")：），有分類作**瓦鰈亞科**（），是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鰈亞目的其中一科](../Page/鰈亞目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

瓦鰈科其下有三個屬\[1\]\[2\]：

### 馬來鰈屬（*Marleyella* <small>Fowler, 1925</small>）

  - [絲鰭馬來鰈](../Page/絲鰭馬來鰈.md "wikilink") (*Marleyella bicolorata*)
  - [馬爾地夫馬來鰈](../Page/馬爾地夫馬來鰈.md "wikilink")(*Marleyella maldivensis*)

### 線鰭瓦鰈屬（*Nematops* <small>Günther, 1880</small>）

  - [大鱗線鰭瓦鰈](../Page/大鱗線鰭瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Nematops grandisquama*)
  - [大手線鰭瓦鰈](../Page/大手線鰭瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Nematops macrochirus*)
  - [小口線鰭瓦鰈](../Page/小口線鰭瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Nematops microstoma*)
  - [細鱗線鰭瓦鰈](../Page/細鱗線鰭瓦鰈.md "wikilink")(*Nematops nanosquama*)

### 瓦鰈屬（*Poecilopsetta* <small>Günther, 1880</small>）

  - [白斑瓦鰈](../Page/白斑瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Poecilopsetta albomaculata*)
  - [比恩氏瓦鰈](../Page/比恩氏瓦鰈.md "wikilink")(*Poecilopsetta beanii*)
  - [黑斑瓦鰈](../Page/黑斑瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Poecilopsetta colorata*)
  - [高背瓦鰈](../Page/高背瓦鰈.md "wikilink")(*Poecilopsetta dorsialta*)
  - [夏威夷瓦鰈](../Page/夏威夷瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Poecilopsetta hawaiiensis*)
  - [無刺瓦鰈](../Page/無刺瓦鰈.md "wikilink")(*Poecilopsetta inermis*)
  - [大頭瓦鰈](../Page/大頭瓦鰈.md "wikilink")(*Poecilopsetta macrocephala*)
  - [多輻瓦鰈](../Page/多輻瓦鰈.md "wikilink")(*Poecilopsetta multiradiata*)
  - [南非瓦鰈](../Page/南非瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Poecilopsetta natalensis*)
  - [諾氏瓦鰈](../Page/諾氏瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Poecilopsetta normani*)
  - [大胸瓦鰈](../Page/大胸瓦鰈.md "wikilink")(*Poecilopsetta pectoralis*)
  - [雙斑瓦鰈](../Page/雙斑瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Poecilopsetta plinthus*)
  - [長體瓦鰈](../Page/長體瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Poecilopsetta praelonga*)
  - [沃氏瓦鰈](../Page/沃氏瓦鰈.md "wikilink")(*Poecilopsetta vaynei*)
  - [桑給巴爾瓦鰈](../Page/桑給巴爾瓦鰈.md "wikilink") (*Poecilopsetta
    zanzibarensis*)

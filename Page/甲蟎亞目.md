**甲蟎亞目**（），又名**隱氣門亞目**（），
是[蛛形綱](../Page/蛛形綱.md "wikilink")[蜱蟎亞綱](../Page/蜱蟎亞綱.md "wikilink")[蟎形總目](../Page/蟎形總目.md "wikilink")[恙蟎目之下的四個亞目之一](../Page/恙蟎目.md "wikilink")\[1\]，以[青苔或](../Page/青苔.md "wikilink")[甲蟲為食](../Page/甲蟲.md "wikilink")\[2\]。亦有其他文獻把本分類歸為[目級](../Page/目_\(生物\).md "wikilink")，學名不變\[3\]\[4\]。牠們的大小從0.2至1.4毫米\[5\]\[6\]\[7\]。

甲蟎的[新陳代謝速率一般比較低](../Page/新陳代謝.md "wikilink")，發展也慢，繁殖力也低\[8\]。 Species
are
[iteroparous](../Page/Reproduction#Other_types_of_reproductive_strategies.md "wikilink")
with adults living a relatively long time; for example, estimates of
development time from [egg](../Page/Egg_\(biology\).md "wikilink") to
adult vary from several months to two years in [temperate
forest](../Page/temperate_forest.md "wikilink") soils.\[9\] Oribatid
mites have six active [instars](../Page/instar.md "wikilink"): prelarva,
[larva](../Page/larva.md "wikilink"), 3
[nymphal](../Page/nymph_\(biology\).md "wikilink") instars and the
adult.\[10\] All these stages after the prelarva feed on a wide variety
of material including living and dead
[plant](../Page/plant.md "wikilink") and
[fungal](../Page/fungus.md "wikilink") material,
[lichens](../Page/lichen.md "wikilink") and
[carrion](../Page/carrion.md "wikilink"); some are
[predatory](../Page/predator.md "wikilink"), but none is
[parasitic](../Page/parasitism.md "wikilink") and feeding habits may
differ between immatures and adults of the same species.

甲蟎是多種[絛蟲綱物種的宿主](../Page/絛蟲綱.md "wikilink")，有助泥土內的有機物質分解，就好像[蚯蚓那樣](../Page/蚯蚓.md "wikilink")，所以在農業經濟上有其重要之處\[11\]。

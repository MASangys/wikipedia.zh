**留园网** ()，又称为6park.com,是一家综合性海外中文社区网站，中国大陆用户无法登录。有新闻，论坛，博客，网络游戏等。 It
has been mentioned in news sources such as [Hua
Sheng](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hua_Sheng "wikilink") Online as a
way for Chinese people living overseas to get news.\[1\] The website
does provide news from
[China](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/China "wikilink"), besides also
translate news from foreign media, such as newspaper, television and
websites.

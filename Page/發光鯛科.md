**發光鯛科**為[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鱸形目的其中一個](../Page/鱸形目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**發光鯛科**下分9個屬，如下：

### 發光鯛屬(*Acropoma*)

  - [銀發光鯛](../Page/銀發光鯛.md "wikilink")(''Acropoma argentistigma '')
  - [前肛發光鯛](../Page/前肛發光鯛.md "wikilink")(''Acropoma boholensis '')
  - [羽根田氏發光鯛](../Page/羽根田氏發光鯛.md "wikilink")(*Acropoma
    hanedai*)：又稱[圓鱗發光鯛](../Page/圓鱗發光鯛.md "wikilink")。
  - [日本發光鯛](../Page/日本發光鯛.md "wikilink")(*Acropoma japonicum*)
  - [勒氏發光鯛](../Page/勒氏發光鯛.md "wikilink")(''Acropoma lecorneti '')
  - [深發光鯛](../Page/深發光鯛.md "wikilink")(''Acropoma profundum '')

### 擬發光鯛屬(*Apogonops*)

  - [棘臀擬發光鯛](../Page/棘臀擬發光鯛.md "wikilink")(*Apogonops anomalus*)

### 赤鯥屬(*Doederleinia*)

  - [赤鯥](../Page/赤鯥.md "wikilink")(*Doederleinia
    berycoides*)：又稱[赤鮭](../Page/赤鮭.md "wikilink")。

### 軟魚屬(*Malakichthys*)

  - [鬚軟魚](../Page/鬚軟魚.md "wikilink")(''Malakichthys barbatus '')
  - [美軟魚](../Page/美軟魚.md "wikilink")(*Malakichthys elegans*)
  - [灰軟魚](../Page/灰軟魚.md "wikilink")(*Malakichthys griseus*)
  - [光滑軟魚](../Page/光滑軟魚.md "wikilink")(''Malakichthys levis '')
  - [莫氏軟魚](../Page/莫氏軟魚.md "wikilink")(*Malakichthys mochizuki*)
  - [寶石軟魚](../Page/寶石軟魚.md "wikilink")(''Malakichthys similis '')
  - [脅谷軟魚](../Page/脅谷軟魚.md "wikilink")(*Malakichthys wakiyae*)

### 新鯥屬(*Neoscombrops*)

  - [大西洋新鯥](../Page/大西洋新鯥.md "wikilink")(*Neoscombrops atlanticus*)
  - [犬齒新鯥](../Page/犬齒新鯥.md "wikilink")(*Neoscombrops cynodon*)
  - [太平洋新鯥](../Page/太平洋新鯥.md "wikilink")(*Neoscombrops pacificus*)

### 尖牙鱸屬(*Synagrops*)

  - [艾氏尖牙鱸](../Page/艾氏尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops adeni*)
  - [多棘尖牙鱸](../Page/多棘尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops analis*)
  - [銀光尖牙鱸](../Page/銀光尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops argyrea*)
  - [黑口尖牙鱸](../Page/黑口尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops bellus*)
  - [日本尖牙鱸](../Page/日本尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops japonicus*)：又稱光棘尖牙鱸。
  - [馬來尖牙鱸](../Page/馬來尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops malayanus* )
  - [小鱗尖牙鱸](../Page/小鱗尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops microlepis*)
  - [菲律賓尖牙鱸](../Page/菲律賓尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops
    philippinensis*)：又稱腹棘尖牙鱸。
  - [擬小鱗尖牙鱸](../Page/擬小鱗尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops pseudomicrolepis*)
  - [鋸棘尖牙鱸](../Page/鋸棘尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops serratospinosus*)
  - [棘尖牙鱸](../Page/棘尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops spinosus*)
  - [三刺尖牙鱸](../Page/三刺尖牙鱸.md "wikilink")(*Synagrops trispinosus*)

### 尖牙發光鯛屬(*Verilus*)

  - [古巴尖牙發光鯛](../Page/古巴尖牙發光鯛.md "wikilink")(*Verilus sordidus*)

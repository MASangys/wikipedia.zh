**百事零度（Papsi
Next）**是[百事可乐公司生产的](../Page/百事公司.md "wikilink")[可乐型](../Page/可乐.md "wikilink")[碳酸饮料](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/碳酸饮料 "wikilink")，是[百事可乐系列的变种](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/百事可乐系列 "wikilink")。百事零度含有[果葡糖浆](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/果葡糖浆 "wikilink")、[蔗糖和](../Page/蔗糖.md "wikilink")[三氯蔗糖](../Page/三氯蔗糖.md "wikilink")。面向与减肥者以及不喜欢太甜可乐的消费者。

2013年6月15日，百事公司在Facebook上告知消费者：新配方的百事可乐将不再含有阿斯巴甜，这种人造甜味剂用于包括百事清怡在内的大量产品。所以，在此之后美国销售的部分百事可乐包装上写着“不含阿斯巴甜”，但是，作为甜味剂的乙酰磺胺酸钾仍然存在于百事可乐配方之中。

除美国外，百事零度还在[加拿大](../Page/加拿大.md "wikilink")、[荷兰](../Page/荷兰.md "wikilink")、[芬兰和](../Page/芬兰.md "wikilink")[新西兰等地销售](../Page/新西兰.md "wikilink")。

## 研制

[Pepsi Edge](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pepsi_Edge "wikilink"),
Pepsi's first mid-calorie cola, was introduced in 2004, and discontinued
only two years later. PepsiCo, towards the end of the 2000s found new
interest in mid-calorie beverages, and then released G2, a version of
Gatorade with less than half the calories, and in the year 2011, Trop50,
a juice blend, with 50% the calories of regular juice.

In September 2007, PepsiCo filed for trademarks in the [US Patent and
Trademark
Office](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/US_Patent_and_Trademark_Office "wikilink"),
for "Pepsi Next", and "Diet Pepsi Next".

In 2010, PepsiCo began to test Pepsi Next via market research groups. In
June 2011, PepsiCo announced that Pepsi Next was going to be moved into
two test markets, [Cedar Rapids,
Iowa](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cedar_Rapids,_Iowa "wikilink"), and
[Eau Claire,
Wisconsin](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eau_Claire,_Wisconsin "wikilink").
After testing, on February 27, 2012, PepsiCo announced the launch of
Pepsi Next.\[1\]

In the Australian market, Pepsi Next was bottled by [Schweppes
Australia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schweppes_Australia "wikilink")
and is sweetened by
[stevia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/stevia "wikilink") which reduces
the sugar content by 30% compared to regular Pepsi.\[2\] Pepsi Next was
first introduced in
[France](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/France "wikilink") in March 2013,
and in [Finland](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finland "wikilink") and
[Canada](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canada "wikilink") in March 2014.
Stevia extract is used in all three markets.

## 销售

After the launch of Pepsi Next, a [television
commercial](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/television_commercial "wikilink")
was launched that depicted a young couple, enjoying Pepsi Next.

[Eva Longoria](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eva_Longoria "wikilink"),
[Paula Patton](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paula_Patton "wikilink"),
and [Nicki Minaj](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicki_Minaj "wikilink")
all have featured in advertisements for Pepsi Next. Paula Patton has
filmed a television commercial for Pepsi Next, as has [Nicki
Minaj](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicki_Minaj "wikilink").\[5\]

A series of Internet and print ads were made, with a picture of a can of
Pepsi Next, with the words: *Real Cola Taste, 60% Less Sugar, Drink It
To Believe It.*

In the 2013 Super Bowl, Pepsi used its advertising minutes right before
the halftime show (which featured Beyonce, a "brand ambassador" for
Pepsi) to advertise Pepsi Next. The presentation by Pepsi (the halftime
show plus the advertising) was unique in that it featured photos of
Pepsi customers. Pepsi had asked customers to send in photos before the
Super Bowl.\[6\]

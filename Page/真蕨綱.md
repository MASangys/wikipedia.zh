**真蕨綱**是[蕨類植物門裡的一綱](../Page/蕨類植物門.md "wikilink")，包括所有的[薄囊蕨類](../Page/薄囊蕨類.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

下列為 Smith et al. 所提出的完整分類架構\[1\]：

  - [紫萁目](../Page/紫萁目.md "wikilink") Osmundales
      - [紫萁科](../Page/紫萁科.md "wikilink") Osmundaceae
  - [膜蕨目](../Page/膜蕨目.md "wikilink") Hymenophyllales
      - [膜蕨科](../Page/膜蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Hymenophyllaceae（包含Trichomanaceae）
  - [裏白目](../Page/裏白目.md "wikilink") Gleicheniales
      - [裏白科](../Page/裏白科.md "wikilink")
        Gleicheniaceae（包含Dicranopteridaceae、[羽葉裏白科](../Page/羽葉裏白科.md "wikilink")
        Stromatopteridaceae）
      - [雙扇蕨科](../Page/雙扇蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Dipteridaceae（包含[燕尾蕨科](../Page/燕尾蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Cheiropleuriaceae）
      - [馬頓蕨科](../Page/馬頓蕨科.md "wikilink") Matoniaceae
  - [莎草蕨目](../Page/莎草蕨目.md "wikilink") Schizaeales
      - [海金沙科](../Page/海金沙科.md "wikilink") Lygodiaceae
      - [密穗蕨科](../Page/密穗蕨科.md "wikilink") Anemiaceae（包含Mohriaceae）
      - [莎草蕨科](../Page/莎草蕨科.md "wikilink") Schizaeaceae
  - [槐葉蘋目](../Page/槐葉蘋目.md "wikilink") Salviniales
      - [蘋科](../Page/蘋科.md "wikilink") Marsileaceae（包含Pilulariaceae）
      - [槐葉蘋科](../Page/槐葉蘋科.md "wikilink")
        Salviniaceae（包含[滿江紅科](../Page/滿江紅科.md "wikilink")
        Azollaceae）
  - [桫欏目](../Page/桫欏目.md "wikilink") Cyatheales
      - [傘序蕨科](../Page/傘序蕨科.md "wikilink") Thyrsopteridaceae
      - [偏環蕨科](../Page/偏環蕨科.md "wikilink") Loxomataceae
      - [墊囊蕨科](../Page/墊囊蕨科.md "wikilink") Culcitaceae
      - [瘤足蕨科](../Page/瘤足蕨科.md "wikilink") Plagiogyriaceae
      - [金狗毛蕨科](../Page/金狗毛蕨科.md "wikilink") Cibotiaceae
      - [桫欏科](../Page/桫欏科.md "wikilink")
        Cyatheaceae（包含Alsophilaceae、Hymenophyllopsidaceae）
      - [蚌殼蕨科](../Page/蚌殼蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Dicksoniaceae（包含[毛囊蕨科](../Page/毛囊蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Lophosoriaceae）
      - [蚌沙蕨科](../Page/蚌沙蕨科.md "wikilink") Metaxyaceae
  - [水龍骨目](../Page/水龍骨目.md "wikilink") Polypodiales
      - [鱗始蕨科](../Page/鱗始蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Lindsaeaceae（包含Cystodiaceae、Lonchitidaceae）
      - [袋囊蕨科](../Page/袋囊蕨科.md "wikilink") Saccolomataceae
      - [碗蕨科](../Page/碗蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Dennstaedtiaceae（包含[姬蕨科](../Page/姬蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Hypolepidaceae、[稀子蕨科](../Page/稀子蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Monachosoraceae、[蕨科](../Page/蕨科.md "wikilink") Pteridiaceae）
      - [鳳尾蕨科](../Page/鳳尾蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Pteridaceae(包含[鹵蕨科](../Page/鹵蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Acrostichaceae、[輻射蕨科](../Page/輻射蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Actiniopteridaceae、[鐵線蕨科](../Page/鐵線蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Adiantaceae、Anopteraceae、[車前蕨科](../Page/車前蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Antrophyaceae、[水蕨科](../Page/水蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Ceratopteridaceae、Cheilanthaceae、[珠蕨科](../Page/珠蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Cryptogrammaceae、[裸子蕨科](../Page/裸子蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Hemionitidaceae、Negripteridaceae、[水蕨科](../Page/水蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Parkeriaceae、Platyzomataceae、[中國蕨科](../Page/中國蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Sinopteridaceae、[竹葉蕨科](../Page/竹葉蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Taenitidaceae、[書帶蕨科](../Page/書帶蕨科.md "wikilink") Vittariaceae）
      - [鐵角蕨科](../Page/鐵角蕨科.md "wikilink") Aspleniaceae
      - [金星蕨科](../Page/金星蕨科.md "wikilink") Thelypteridaceae
      - [岩蕨科](../Page/岩蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Woodsiaceae（包含[蹄蓋蕨科](../Page/蹄蓋蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Athyriaceae、Cystopteridaceae）
      - [烏毛蕨科](../Page/烏毛蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Blechnaceae（包含[光葉藤蕨科](../Page/光葉藤蕨科.md "wikilink")\]
        Stenochlaenaceae)
      - [球子蕨科](../Page/球子蕨科.md "wikilink") Onocleaceae
      - [鱗毛蕨科](../Page/鱗毛蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Dryopteridaceae（包含[叉蕨科](../Page/叉蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Aspidiaceae、[實蕨科](../Page/實蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Bolbitidaceae、[舌蕨科](../Page/舌蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Elaphoglossaceae、[腫足蕨科](../Page/腫足蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Hypodematiaceae、[球盖蕨科](../Page/球盖蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Peranemataceae）
      - [藤蕨科](../Page/藤蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Lomariopsidaceae(包含[腎蕨科](../Page/腎蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Nephrolepidaceae)
      - [三叉蕨科](../Page/三叉蕨科.md "wikilink") Tectariaceae
      - [條蕨科](../Page/條蕨科.md "wikilink") Oleandraceae
      - [骨碎補科](../Page/骨碎補科.md "wikilink") Davalliaceae
      - [水龍骨科](../Page/水龍骨科.md "wikilink")
        Polypodiaceae（包含[槲蕨科](../Page/槲蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Drynariaceae、[禾葉蕨科](../Page/禾葉蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Grammitidaceae、[雨蕨科](../Page/雨蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Gymnogrammitidaceae、[劍蕨科Loxogrammaceae](../Page/劍蕨科.md "wikilink")、[鹿角蕨科](../Page/鹿角蕨科.md "wikilink")
        Platyceriaceae、Pleurisoriopsidaceae）

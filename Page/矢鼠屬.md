**矢鼠屬**（學名：）是[哺乳綱](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/哺乳綱 "wikilink")[囓齒目](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/囓齒目 "wikilink")[鼠科](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鼠科 "wikilink")[鼠亞科的一屬](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鼠亞科 "wikilink")，旗下只有**長尾矢鼠**一個物種。這個屬只生活於[非洲大陸](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/非洲大陸 "wikilink")，分佈於[貝寧](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/貝寧 "wikilink")、[喀麥隆](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/喀麥隆 "wikilink")、[中非共和國](../Page/中非共和國.md "wikilink")、[剛果共和國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/剛果共和國 "wikilink")、[剛果民主共和國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/剛果民主共和國 "wikilink")、[赤道畿內亞](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/赤道畿內亞 "wikilink")、[加蓬](../Page/加蓬.md "wikilink")、[尼日利亞](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/尼日利亞 "wikilink")、[多哥](../Page/多哥.md "wikilink")和[烏干達](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/烏干達 "wikilink")。牠們的自然[棲息地](../Page/棲息地.md "wikilink")是[亞熱帶或](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/亞熱帶 "wikilink")[熱帶潮濕低地](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/熱帶 "wikilink")[森林](../Page/森林.md "wikilink")。

同科的動物尚有[大巽他鼠屬](../Page/大巽他鼠屬.md "wikilink")（[巴氏大巽他鼠](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴氏大巽他鼠 "wikilink")）、[西里伯斯鼠屬](../Page/西里伯斯鼠屬.md "wikilink")、[狹鼠屬](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/狹鼠屬 "wikilink")（[神女狹鼠](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/神女狹鼠 "wikilink")）、[狹顱鼠屬](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/狹顱鼠屬 "wikilink")（[灰尾狹顱鼠](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/灰尾狹顱鼠 "wikilink")）等之數種[哺乳動物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/哺乳動物 "wikilink")\[1\]。

## Description

The target rat is so named because it has a number of long bristles projecting from its rump which resemble the shafts of arrows in a target. The synonym *Mus sebastianus* also refers to this, [Saint Sebastian](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saint_Sebastian "wikilink") being an early Christian martyr said to have been tied to a tree and shot with arrows. The fur is dark reddish-brown and the tail very long.\[2\]

## 分佈及習性

矢鼠屬原生於中非西部的潮濕熱帶雨林，分佈範圍從多哥到貝寧及尼日利亞，然後到剛果盆地，再往東到烏干達西部\[3\]。 It occurs in swampy areas of forests and in gallery forests and beside streams. It has also been observed in secondary forests around villages and in banana plantations.\[4\]

## Ecology

The target rat is largely nocturnal and feeds on the ground on green vegetable material and fallen fruit, supplementing these with some insects. Little is known of the social organisation of this species but in Cameroon a group of thirteen was caught at one location in an [oil palm](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elaeis "wikilink") plantation. This rat seems to breed at any time of year.\[5\]

## Status

The target rat has a wide range and is a common species. It is presumed to have a large population and no particular threats have been identified. The [International Union for Conservation of Nature](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Union_for_Conservation_of_Nature "wikilink") has rated its conservation status as being of "[least concern](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Least-concern_species "wikilink")".\[6\]

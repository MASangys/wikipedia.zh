**秘密战争**（或）可能指：

**历史**上：

  - **Secret War**, a term used in some sources to describe American clandestine involvement in the [寮國內戰](../Page/寮國內戰.md "wikilink")（1953年–1975年），which was in part a theater of the larger [Vietnam War](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vietnam_War "wikilink").

**漫画**中

  - 《[秘密战争](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/秘密战争_\(1984年漫画\) "wikilink")》，1984年[漫威漫画吉姆](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/漫威漫画 "wikilink")·舒特和迈克·泽克创作的超级英雄交叉迷你系列。
      - [*Secret Wars* (toyline)](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secret_Wars_\(toyline\) "wikilink"), an action figure tie-in to the miniseries produced by Mattel.
  - *[Secret Wars II](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secret_Wars_II "wikilink")*, the 1985 Marvel Comics sequel to *Secret Wars* by Jim Shooter and Al Milgrom.
  - 《[秘密战争](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/秘密战争_\(2004年漫画\) "wikilink")》, the 2004 Marvel Comics superhero crossover limited series by Brian Michael Bendis and Gabriele Dell'Otto.
  - 《[秘密战争](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/秘密战争_\(2015年漫画\) "wikilink")》, the 2015 Marvel Comics superhero crossover event by Jonathan Hickman and Esad Ribic.

In **television**:

  - ["Secret Wars" (Spider-Man)](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secret_Wars_\(Spider-Man\) "wikilink"), a three episode storyline of *Spider-Man: The Animated Series*, based on the *Secret Wars* miniseries by Marvel Comics.
  - [The Secret War (TV Series)](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Secret_War_\(TV_Series\) "wikilink"), a documentary series from 1977 highlighting technical developments made during the [Second World War](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Second_World_War "wikilink").

In **music**:

  - [*Secret Wars* (album)](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Secret_Wars_\(album\) "wikilink"), an album by Oneida.

In **video games**:

  - The Secret War is part of the MMO [The Secret World](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Secret_World "wikilink")'s pre-launch campaign.

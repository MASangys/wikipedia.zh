《**第4击**》（）是美国歌手[碧昂丝的第四张录音室专辑](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/碧昂丝 "wikilink")，由与[哥伦比亚唱片发行于](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/哥伦比亚唱片 "wikilink")2011年6月24日。Following a career hiatus that reignited her creativity, Beyoncé was inspired to create a record with a basis in traditional [rhythm and blues](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/rhythm_and_blues "wikilink") that stood apart from contemporary [popular music](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pop_music "wikilink"). Her collaborations with songwriters and record producers [The-Dream](../Page/The-Dream.md "wikilink"), [Tricky Stewart](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tricky_Stewart "wikilink") and [Shea Taylor](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shea_Taylor "wikilink") produced a mellower tone, developing diverse vocal styles and influences from [funk](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/funk "wikilink"), [hip hop](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hip_hop_music "wikilink"), and [soul music](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/soul_music "wikilink").

Severing professional ties with father and manager [Mathew Knowles](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathew_Knowles "wikilink"), Beyoncé eschewed the music of her previous releases in favor of an intimate, personal album. *4*{{'}}s lyrics emphasize monogamy, female empowerment and self-reflection, a result of Beyoncé considering a maturer message to contend artistic credibility. In May 2011, Beyoncé submitted seventy-two songs to Columbia Records for consideration, twelve of which appeared on the standard edition.

*4* was promoted in mid-2011 by television performances and festival appearances, such as Beyoncé's headlining [Glastonbury Festival](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glastonbury_Festival_2011 "wikilink") set. The album received generally positive reviews by music critics; several publications included it on their year-end lists. It was her fourth consecutive album to debut at number one on the US [*Billboard* 200](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Billboard_200 "wikilink"), and it also reached number one in Brazil, France, Ireland, South Korea, Spain, Switzerland and the United Kingdom. *4* spawned the international singles "[Run the World (Girls)](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Run_the_World_\(Girls\) "wikilink")", "[Best Thing I Never Had](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Best_Thing_I_Never_Had "wikilink")", "[Party](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Party_\(Beyoncé_song\) "wikilink")", "[Love on Top](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Love_on_Top "wikilink")" and "[Countdown](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Countdown_\(Beyoncé_song\) "wikilink")". "Love on Top" won the [Grammy Award for Best Traditional R\&B Performance](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grammy_Award_for_Best_Traditional_R&B_Performance "wikilink") at the [55th annual ceremony](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/55th_Annual_Grammy_Awards "wikilink"). As of December 2015, *4* has sold 1.5 million copies in the United States.

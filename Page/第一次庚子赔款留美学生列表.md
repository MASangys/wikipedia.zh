## 1909年第一批

[Boxer_Rebellion_Indemnity_Scholarship_recipients_1909.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Boxer_Rebellion_Indemnity_Scholarship_recipients_1909.jpg "fig:Boxer_Rebellion_Indemnity_Scholarship_recipients_1909.jpg")}-总办[周自齐](../Page/周自齐.md "wikilink")（前中）、会办[唐国安](../Page/唐国安.md "wikilink")（前右）和[范源濂](../Page/范源濂.md "wikilink")（前左）合影\]\]

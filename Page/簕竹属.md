**簕竹属**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：**），又称**刺竹属**，[禾本目](../Page/禾本目.md "wikilink")[禾本科的一](../Page/禾本科.md "wikilink")[属](../Page/属.md "wikilink")，是[竹亚科](../Page/竹亚科.md "wikilink")[竹族中的一个大属](../Page/竹族.md "wikilink")，有约137[种和多数培育变种](../Page/种.md "wikilink")，原产于[亚洲的](../Page/亚洲.md "wikilink")[热带和](../Page/热带.md "wikilink")[亚热带的](../Page/亚热带.md "wikilink")[季风湿热地区](../Page/季风.md "wikilink")，多数品种植株矮小丛生，也有几种高大品种，有多种园艺观赏品种。在[中国](../Page/中国.md "wikilink")，主要生长在[长江以南各地](../Page/长江.md "wikilink")，但目前许多观赏品种已经被引种到北方，以及[欧洲](../Page/欧洲.md "wikilink")、[美洲各地](../Page/美洲.md "wikilink")。

## 分类

本属至少有137个受承认的物种，部份物种如下：

  - *Bambusa arnhemica*
  - *Bambusa arundinacea* [印度簕竹](../Page/印度簕竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa balcooa*
  - *Bambusa bambos* [小刺竹](../Page/小刺竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa basihirsuta* [苦绿竹](../Page/苦绿竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa beecheyana* [吊丝球竹](../Page/吊丝球竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa blumeana*
  - *Bambusa boniopsis* [妈竹](../Page/妈竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa burmanica* [缅竹](../Page/缅竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa chungii* [粉单竹](../Page/粉单竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa emeiensis* [慈竹](../Page/慈竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa forbesii*
  - *Bambusa glaucophylla* [马来簕竹](../Page/马来簕竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa lako* [帝汶黑竹](../Page/帝汶黑竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa longispiculata* [花眉竹](../Page/花眉竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa multiplex* [孝顺竹](../Page/孝顺竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa pachinensis* [长毛米筛竹](../Page/长毛米筛竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa sinospinosa* [车筒竹](../Page/车筒竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa tulda* [马甲竹](../Page/马甲竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa ventricosa* [佛肚竹](../Page/佛肚竹.md "wikilink")
  - *Bambusa vulgaris* [大佛肚竹](../Page/大佛肚竹.md "wikilink")

\(E_{a,b}(z)=\sum_{k=0}^{\infty}\frac{z^k}{\Gamma(ak+b)}\)

## 特例

对应 \(a=0,1/2,1,2\) 有

\[E_{0,1}(z) = \sum_{k=0}^\infty z^k = \frac{1}{1-z}.\]

[指数函数](../Page/指数函数.md "wikilink"):

\[E_{1,1}(z) = \sum_{k=0}^\infty \frac{z^k}{\Gamma (k + 1)} = \sum_{k=0}^\infty \frac{z^k}{k!} = \exp(z).\]

[误差函数](../Page/误差函数.md "wikilink"):

\[E_{1/2,1}(z) = \exp(z^2)\operatorname{erfc}(-z).\]

[双曲余弦](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/双曲余弦 "wikilink"):

\[E_{2,1}(z) = \cosh(\sqrt{z}).\]

对应 \(a=0,1,2\), ：

\[\int_0^{z}E_{\alpha,1}(-s^2){\mathrm d}s\] 有下列积分式

\[\arctan(z)\],

\[\tfrac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}\operatorname{erf}(z)\],

\[\sin(z)\].

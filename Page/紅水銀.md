**紅水銀**（Red
Mercury，或称**红汞**）是一种虚构的物质，傳聞为[蘇聯在](../Page/蘇聯.md "wikilink")[冷戰时期研製](../Page/冷戰.md "wikilink")，通过純[水銀和氧化汞銻放置在核反應堆內](../Page/水銀.md "wikilink")[輻照二十天](../Page/輻照.md "wikilink")，则製造出的這種呈紅色的廉價但輻射度極高的物料。传说紅水銀每立方公分重達二十點二[公克](../Page/公克.md "wikilink")（20.2
g/cm<sup>3</sup>），密度更勝於[鈾](../Page/鈾.md "wikilink")（19.1
g/cm<sup>3</sup>）；只需把[棒球大小的份量製成](../Page/棒球.md "wikilink")[骯髒彈](../Page/骯髒彈.md "wikilink")，就已足以造成災難性破壞。但由於至今仍无力證據證明紅水銀的存在，科學家對紅水銀到底是甚麼莫衷一是。質疑，以如此高能量密度的輻射物，穩定性不足，難以製造炸彈。所有相關討論都與蘇聯相關連，故部份專家認為是有心人士散播的[謠言](../Page/謠言.md "wikilink")。

## 大眾文化

在[布鲁斯·威利斯](../Page/布鲁斯·威利斯.md "wikilink")2013年主演的《[超危險特工2：狠戰](../Page/超危險特工2：狠戰.md "wikilink")》中，红色水银是冷战时期被研制出来并被藏在[克里姆林宫内的一个秘密武器](../Page/克里姆林宫.md "wikilink")。

## 延伸閱讀

  - , Steve McQuillan, *The Mini-Nuke Conspiracy: How Mandela Inherited
    a Nuclear Nightmare*, Viking Adult（October 1, 1995）ISBN
    0-670-86925-2. This book made the claim that [South
    Africa](../Page/South_Africa.md "wikilink") had made red mercury,
    and used it to construct a thousand miniature tactical nuclear
    weapons, that were now in the hands of non-governmental South
    African right-wing elements which intended to make use of them in
    the near future (of 1995).

  - Henry Stevens, *Hitler's Suppressed and Still-Secret Weapons,
    Science and Technology* ISBN 978-1-931882-73-6. in Chapter 21,
    citing sources from above, Stevens makes a case that Germany may
    have developed technology of Red Mercury during [World War
    II](../Page/World_War_II.md "wikilink").

**紋蛺蝶屬**（學名：***Anartia***）是[蛺蝶亞科](../Page/蛺蝶亞科.md "wikilink")[維蛺蝶族中的一個](../Page/維蛺蝶族.md "wikilink")[屬](../Page/屬.md "wikilink")。物種分佈於[新熱帶界](../Page/新熱帶界.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

  - [白斑紅紋蛺蝶](../Page/白斑紅紋蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Anartia amathea* (Linnaeus,
    1758)
  - [金紋蛺蝶](../Page/金紋蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Anartia chrysopelea* Hübner,
    \[1831\]
  - [白紋蛺蝶](../Page/白紋蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Anartia fatima* (Fabricius,
    1793)
  - [褐紋蛺蝶](../Page/褐紋蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Anartia jatrophae* (Linnaeus,
    1763)
  - [黑紋蛺蝶](../Page/黑紋蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Anartia lytrea* (Godart, 1819)

**納卡卜**（**Naqaab**）是2007年[寶萊塢製作的驚險劇情影片](../Page/寶萊塢.md "wikilink")。

主要演員為：[波比·德奧利](../Page/波比·德奧利.md "wikilink")（[Bobby
Deol](../Page/Bobby_Deol.md "wikilink")）和[阿克夏·庫馬](../Page/阿克夏·庫馬.md "wikilink")（[Akshaye
Khanna](../Page/Akshaye_Khanna.md "wikilink")），擔任女主角的是前模特兒、電影新人[烏沃瓦什·沙馬](../Page/烏沃瓦什·沙馬.md "wikilink")（[Urvashi
Sharma](../Page/Urvashi_Sharma.md "wikilink")）。

## 故事梗概

三角戀愛故事。模特兒蘇菲亞（Sophia）已經和億萬富翁卡蘭（Karan
Oberoi）訂婚，此時出現了維克（Vicky），蘇菲亞要在愛她的卡蘭和她所愛的維克之間作出選擇
。。。

## 影評

*Naqaab* received mixed reviews. Critics were generally positive to the
performances of the three lead actors. Taran Adarsh of Indiafm.com said
the *Naqaab belongs to Akshaye, who delivers yet another powerful
performance. He's splendid, the real scene stealer, the soul of the
enterprise. Bobby is relegated to the backseat in the first hour, but
manages to make his presence felt in the second half. Urvashi Sharma
gets a dream launch and she utilizes the golden opportunity completely*.
\[4\] Bollywoodarchive.com said *Akshaye Khanna’s stands out most
outstandingly. His chemistry with Urvashi is superb. Bobby Deol does his
part well but offers nothing vulnerable. Newcomer Urvashi Sharma acts as
if she has the experience of many films behind her*. \[5\]

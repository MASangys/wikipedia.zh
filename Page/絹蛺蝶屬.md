**絹蛺蝶屬**（[學名](../Page/學名.md "wikilink")：*Calinaga*）是[蛺蝶科裡](../Page/蛺蝶科.md "wikilink")**絹蛺蝶亞科**（[學名](../Page/學名.md "wikilink")：*Calinaginae*）的唯一一個屬，分佈於[古北區及](../Page/古北區.md "wikilink")[東洋區](../Page/東洋區.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

  - [黑絹蛺蝶](../Page/黑絹蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Calinaga lhatso*
  - [絹蛺蝶](../Page/絹蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Calinaga buddha*
  - [臘絹蛺蝶](../Page/臘絹蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Calinaga cercyon*
  - [大衞絹蛺蝶](../Page/大衞絹蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Calinaga davidis*
  - [格奧絹蛺蝶](../Page/格奧絹蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Calinaga gautama*
  - [阿波絹蛺蝶](../Page/阿波絹蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Calinaga aborica*
  - [大絹蛺蝶](../Page/大絹蛺蝶.md "wikilink") *Calinaga sudassana*

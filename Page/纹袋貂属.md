**紋袋貂屬**是屬於[袋鼯科的](../Page/袋鼯科.md "wikilink")[有袋類動物](../Page/有袋類.md "wikilink")，包含如下物種：

  - [大尾袋貂](../Page/大尾袋貂.md "wikilink") *Dactylopsila megalura*
  - [长指袋貂](../Page/长指袋貂.md "wikilink") *Dactylopsila palpator*
  - [费岛袋貂](../Page/费岛袋貂.md "wikilink") *Dactylopsila tatei*
  - [纹袋貂](../Page/纹袋貂.md "wikilink") *Dactylopsila trivirgata*

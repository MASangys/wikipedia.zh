**线粒体16S
RNA**是一种存在于[线粒体核糖体](../Page/线粒体核糖体.md "wikilink")（mitoribosome，MR）[39S核糖体亚基中的](../Page/39S核糖体亚基.md "wikilink")[rRNA](../Page/rRNA.md "wikilink")。\[1\]\[2\]在[人类](../Page/人类.md "wikilink")[细胞的](../Page/细胞.md "wikilink")[线粒体中](../Page/线粒体.md "wikilink")，该rRNA由[MT-RNR2](../Page/MT-RNR2.md "wikilink")[基因编码](../Page/基因.md "wikilink")。MT-RNR2基因还同时编码了能抑制[阿尔茨海默症相关](../Page/阿尔茨海默症.md "wikilink")[基因表达的](../Page/基因表达.md "wikilink")[humanin](../Page/humanin.md "wikilink")。\[3\]\[4\]

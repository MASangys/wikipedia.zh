**絞口鯊科**（學名*Ginglymostomatidae*）又稱**鏽鬚鮫科**，是[板鰓亞綱](../Page/板鰓亞綱.md "wikilink")[鬚鯊目的其中一科](../Page/鬚鯊目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**絞口鯊科**下分為3個屬，如下：

  - [絞口鯊屬](../Page/絞口鯊屬.md "wikilink")(*Ginglymostoma*) <small>[J. P.
    Müller](../Page/約翰內斯·彼得·繆勒.md "wikilink") &
    [Henle](../Page/Friedrich_Gustav_Jakob_Henle.md "wikilink"),
    1837</small>
      - [鉸口鯊](../Page/鉸口鯊.md "wikilink")(*Ginglymostoma cirratum*)
        <small>[Bonnaterre](../Page/Pierre_Joseph_Bonnaterre.md "wikilink"),
        1788</small>：俗名护士鲨
      - [太平洋護士鯊](../Page/太平洋護士鯊.md "wikilink")(*Ginglymostoma unami*)
        <small>[Del-Moral-Flores](../Page/Luis_Fernando_Del-Moral-Flores.md "wikilink"),
        [Ramírez-Antonio](../Page/Emmanuel_Ramírez-Antonio.md "wikilink"),
        [Angulo](../Page/Arturo_Angulo_Sibaja.md "wikilink") &
        [Pérez-Ponce de
        León](../Page/Gerardo_Pérez-Ponce_de_León.md "wikilink"),
        2015</small> \[1\]
  - [光鱗鯊屬](../Page/光鱗鯊屬.md "wikilink")(*Nebrius*)
    <small>[Rüppell](../Page/Eduard_Rüppell.md "wikilink"),
    1837</small>
      - [鏽鬚鮫](../Page/鏽鬚鮫.md "wikilink")(*Nebrius ferrugineus*)
        <small>[Lesson](../Page/勒內-普里梅韋勒·萊松.md "wikilink"),
        1831</small>：又稱光鱗鯊。
  - [護士鯊屬](../Page/護士鯊屬.md "wikilink")(*Pseudoginglymostoma*)
    <small>[Dingerkus](../Page/Guido_Dingerkus.md "wikilink"),
    1986</small>
      - [短尾護士鯊](../Page/短尾護士鯊.md "wikilink")(*Pseudoginglymostoma
        brevicaudatum*) <small>[Günther](../Page/阿爾伯特·甘瑟.md "wikilink"),
        1867</small>：俗名短尾鉸口鯊、短尾光鱗鯊

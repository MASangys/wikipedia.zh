**漂鹬**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：，原名：）又名**-{zh-cn:美洲黄足鹬;zh-tw:漂鷸;}-**，为[鹬科](../Page/鹬科.md "wikilink")[鹬属的](../Page/鹬属.md "wikilink")[鸟类](../Page/鸟类.md "wikilink")。分布于[台湾等地](../Page/台湾.md "wikilink")。该物种的模式产地在太平洋[摩丽雅岛](../Page/摩丽雅岛.md "wikilink")（[Moorea](../Page/:en:Moorea.md "wikilink")）和[帕默斯顿](../Page/帕默斯顿.md "wikilink")（[Palmerston](../Page/:en:Palmerston_Island.md "wikilink")）群岛。\[2\]

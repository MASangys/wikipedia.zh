**翠鸟属**（学名*Alcedo*）在[动物分类学上是](../Page/动物分类学.md "wikilink")[鸟纲](../Page/鸟纲.md "wikilink")[佛法僧目](../Page/佛法僧目.md "wikilink")[翠鸟科中的一个](../Page/翠鸟科.md "wikilink")[属](../Page/属_\(生物\).md "wikilink")，包括了翠鸟科的大部分种，约有15种以上。

  - [斑头大翠鸟](../Page/斑头大翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo hercules*
  - [普通翠鸟](../Page/普通翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo atthis*
  - [半领翠鸟](../Page/半领翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo semitorquata*
  - [闪蓝翠鸟](../Page/闪蓝翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo quadribrachys*
  - [蓝耳翠鸟](../Page/蓝耳翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo meninting*
  - [碧翠鸟](../Page/碧翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo azurea*
  - [俾斯翠鸟](../Page/俾斯翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo websteri*
  - [蓝带翠鸟](../Page/蓝带翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo euryzona*
  - [青带翠鸟](../Page/青带翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo cyanopecta*
  - [银翠鸟](../Page/银翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo argentata*
  - [冠翠鸟](../Page/冠翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo cristata*
  - [马岛翠鸟](../Page/马岛翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo vintsioides*
  - [白腹翠鸟](../Page/白腹翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo leucogaster*
  - [蔚蓝翠鸟](../Page/蔚蓝翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo coerulescens*
  - [小翠鸟](../Page/小翠鸟.md "wikilink") *Alcedo pusilla*
  - *Alcedo nais*
  - *Alcedo thomensis*

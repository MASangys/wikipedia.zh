**翼法螺**（学名：**）是一種[掠食性的](../Page/掠食性動物.md "wikilink")[海螺](../Page/海螺.md "wikilink")，是一種[海洋](../Page/海洋生物.md "wikilink")[腹足綱](../Page/腹足綱.md "wikilink")[軟體動物](../Page/軟體動物.md "wikilink")。舊屬[异足目](../Page/异足目.md "wikilink")[法螺科](../Page/法螺科.md "wikilink")[翼法螺属](../Page/翼法螺属.md "wikilink")，今屬[鶉螺總科的](../Page/鶉螺總科.md "wikilink")[法螺科](../Page/法螺科.md "wikilink")。
***Biplex perca*** is a [species](../Page/species.md "wikilink") of
predatory [sea snail](../Page/sea_snail.md "wikilink"), a
[marine](../Page/marine_\(ocean\).md "wikilink")
[gastropod](../Page/gastropod.md "wikilink")
[mollusk](../Page/mollusk.md "wikilink") in the family
[Ranellidae](../Page/Ranellidae.md "wikilink"), the tritons. It is more
commonly known as the **winged frog shell** or the*' maple leaf
shell*'.\[1\]\[2\]

## Description

Shells of *Biplex perca* can reach a length of .\[3\] These unusual
shells are quite flattened, well adapted to laying on a soft substrate,
with large flanges close to the outer lips and a blade-like outpouching
of the outer shell layer, forming two longitudinal folds (hence the
genus name). Shell surface may be whitish, yellowish or pale
brown.\[4\]\[5\]

## 分佈

本物種廣泛分佈於從[東非到](../Page/東非.md "wikilink")[日本的](../Page/日本.md "wikilink")[印度洋](../Page/印度洋.md "wikilink")-[西太平洋海域](../Page/西太平洋.md "wikilink")\[6\]\[7\]\[8\]，包括[中国大陆](../Page/中国大陆.md "wikilink")及[台湾](../Page/台湾.md "wikilink")。常栖息在潮间带的岩礁区。

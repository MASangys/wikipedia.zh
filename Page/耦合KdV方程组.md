**耦合KdV方程组**(Coupled KdV equation)是一个二元非线性偏微分方程组：\[1\]

\(u_{t}+6*\alpha*u*u_{x}-6*v*v_{x}+\alpha*u_{xxx}=0\)

\(v_{t}+3*\alpha*u*v_{x}+\alpha*v_{xxx}=0\)

## 解析解

\[u(x, t) = (1/3)*(-_C3+\alpha*_C2^3)/(\alpha*_C2)-2*_C2^2*csc(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)^2, v(x, t) = -(1/3)*\sqrt(12*\alpha*_C2^4+6*_C2*_C3)*csc(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = (1/3)*(-_C3+\alpha*_C2^3)/(\alpha*_C2)-2*_C2^2*sec(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)^2, v(x, t) = -(1/3)*\sqrt(12*\alpha*_C2^4+6*_C2*_C3)*sec(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = (1/3)*(-_C3+2*\alpha*_C2^3)/(\alpha*_C2)-2*_C2^2*coth(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)^2, v(x, t) = -(1/3)*\sqrt(6*_C2*_C3+24*\alpha*_C2^4)*coth(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = (1/3)*(-_C3+2*\alpha*_C2^3)/(alpha*_C2)-2*_C2^2*tanh(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)^2, v(x, t) = -(1/3)*\sqrt(6*_C2*_C3+24*alpha*_C2^4)*tanh(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = -(1/3)*(_C3+\alpha*_C2^3)/(\alpha*_C2)-2*_C2^2*csch(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)^2, v(x, t) = -(1/3)*\sqrt(6*_C2*_C3-12*alpha*_C2^4)*csch(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)\]

\[u(x, t) = -(1/3)*(_C3+8*\alpha*_C2^3)/(\alpha*_C2)-4*_C2^2*tan(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)^2, v(x, t) = -(1/6)*(_C3+8*\alpha*_C2^3)*\sqrt(2)/(_C2*\sqrt(\alpha))-2*\sqrt(2)*\sqrt(\alpha)*_C2^2*tan(_C1+_C2*x+_C3*t)^2\]

\[u(x, t) = (1/3)*(-_C4+\alpha*_C3^3+\alpha*_C3^3*_C1^2)/(\alpha*_C3)-2*_C3^2*JacobiNS(_C2+_C3*x+_C4*t, _C1)^2, v(x, t) = -(1/3)*\sqrt(12*\alpha*_C3^4*_C1^2+12*\alpha*_C3^4+6*_C3*_C4)*JacobiNS(_C2+_C3*x+_C4*t, _C1)\]

\[u(x, t) = (1/3)*(-_C4+\alpha*_C3^3+\alpha*_C3^3*_C1^2)/(\alpha*_C3)-2*_C3^2*_C1^2*JacobiSN(_C2+_C3*x+_C4*t, _C1)^2, v(x, t) = -(1/3)*\sqrt(12*\alpha*_C3^4*_C1^2+12*\alpha*_C3^4+6*_C3*_C4)*_C1*JacobiSN(_C2+_C3*x+_C4*t, _C1)\]

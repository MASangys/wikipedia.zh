**耶胡迪爾**
([希伯來語](../Page/希伯來語.md "wikilink")：<span style="font-family:SBL Hebrew, Ezra SIL SR, Ezra SIL, Cardo, Chrysanthi Unicode, TITUS Cyberbit Basic, Arial Unicode MS, Narkisim, Times New Roman"><big>**יהודיאל**</big></span>，[英語](../Page/英語.md "wikilink")：**Jegudiel**或**Jehudiel**)，名字意為「神之讚美」，是[東正教會和](../Page/正教會.md "wikilink")[東儀天主教會傳統中的七大](../Page/東儀天主教會.md "wikilink")[天使長之一](../Page/天使長.md "wikilink")。\[1\]

## 形像

祂在[聖像中被描繪為手持](../Page/聖像畫.md "wikilink")[皇冠和帶有三根鞭條的](../Page/皇冠.md "wikilink")[鞭子](../Page/鞭.md "wikilink")。\[2\]皇冠象徵[上帝給予義人的獎賞](../Page/基督教的神.md "wikilink")，鞭子象徵對罪人的懲罰。[東正教對祂的傳統表現形式是站立之像](../Page/正教會.md "wikilink")，右手持[皇冠](../Page/皇冠.md "wikilink")，左手持[權杖](../Page/權杖.md "wikilink")。

## 主保

耶胡迪爾是那些努力工作之人的[守護聖者](../Page/主保聖人.md "wikilink")，祂手持之皇冠即象徵著對那些成功的[靈性勞作者的獎勵](../Page/靈性.md "wikilink")。\[3\]

## 禱文

作為努力工作和領導地位的[守護聖者而獻予耶胡迪爾的禱文](../Page/主保聖人.md "wikilink")：

*Saint Jegudiel the Archangel, angel of praise to God, pray for us, that
in every act, in every job, in every work, and in every labor we may
constantly carry out the will of the Lord gladly and in praise for all
He has given us. Amen.*

作為神之慈悲的[守護聖者而獻予耶胡迪爾的禱文](../Page/主保聖人.md "wikilink")：

*O merciful Archangel, St. Jhudiel, dispenser of God's eternal and
abundant Mercy. Because of our sinfulness, we do not deserve God's
forgiveness. Yet, He continually grants us forbearance freely and
lovingly. Help us in our determination to overcome our sinful habits and
be truly sorry for them. Bring each one of us to true conversion of
heart. That we may experience the joy of reconciliation which it brings,
without which neither we, as individuals, nor the whole world can know
true peace. You who continually intercede for us, listen to our prayers.
Present to God our Father all these petitions. Through Jesus Christ our
Lord, together with the Holy Spirit forever and ever. Amen.*

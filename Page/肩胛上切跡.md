**肩胛上切跡**（）位於[肩胛骨](../Page/肩胛骨.md "wikilink")上緣，位於的內側。

## 解剖構造

肩胛上切跡的上側會被封住，形成一個。讓通過（但並沒有通過，而是從韌帶上方通過）。有時韌帶會被[骨化](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/骨化 "wikilink")。\[1\]。

根據，肩胛上切跡分為六個基本型：\[2\]

  - 第一型（8%）：無切跡。The superior border forms a wide depression from the medial angle to the coracoid process.
  - 第二型（31%）：Notch is a blunted V-shape occupying the middle third of the superior border.
  - 第三型（48%）：Notch is U-shaped with nearly parallel margins.
  - 第四型（3%）：Notch is V-shaped and very small. A shallow groove is frequently formed for the suprascapular nerve adjacent to the notch.
  - 第五型（6%）：Notch is minimal and U-shaped with a partially ossified ligament.
  - 第六型（4%）：Notch is a foramen as the ligament is completely ossified.

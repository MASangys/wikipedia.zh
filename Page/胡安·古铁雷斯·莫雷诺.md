**胡安·古铁雷斯·莫雷诺**（，），通常稱**华尼托**（），前[西班牙足球運動員](../Page/西班牙.md "wikilink")，擔任[中堅](../Page/中堅.md "wikilink")。

A player of physical display, he is also noted for his excellent aerial
ability. He spent the bulk of his career with
[Betis](../Page/Real_Betis.md "wikilink"), appearing in more than 300
official games and winning one [Copa del
Rey](../Page/Copa_del_Rey.md "wikilink").

With the [Spanish national
team](../Page/Spain_national_football_team.md "wikilink"), Juanito
appeared in one [World Cup](../Page/FIFA_World_Cup.md "wikilink") and
two [European
Championships](../Page/UEFA_European_Championship.md "wikilink"),
contributing to the conquest of [Euro
2008](../Page/UEFA_Euro_2008.md "wikilink").

## 國家隊生涯

Juanito made his international debut for
[Spain](../Page/Spain_national_football_team.md "wikilink") on 21 August
2002 against
[Hungary](../Page/Hungary_national_football_team.md "wikilink"), in a
[testimonial](../Page/Testimonial_match.md "wikilink") for [Ferenc
Puskás](../Page/Ferenc_Puskás.md "wikilink"). His first goal for the
national side came against [Côte
d'Ivoire](../Page/Côte_d'Ivoire_national_football_team.md "wikilink")
in a 3–2 win.

Juanito represented Spain at [UEFA Euro
2004](../Page/UEFA_Euro_2004.md "wikilink") and [Euro
2008](../Page/UEFA_Euro_2008.md "wikilink") (playing in the 2–1 win over
[Greece](../Page/Greece_national_football_team.md "wikilink") in the
latter tournament\[1\]) and the [2006 FIFA World
Cup](../Page/2006_FIFA_World_Cup.md "wikilink"), scoring with his head
in the 1–0 triumph over [Saudi
Arabia](../Page/Saudi_Arabia_national_football_team.md "wikilink").\[2\]
That goal was the first ever scored by a Betis player in the
competition.

In the [2010 World Cup
qualifier](../Page/2010_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_\(UEFA\).md "wikilink")
against [Estonia](../Page/Estonia_national_football_team.md "wikilink")
on 11 October 2008, he netted through another header in a 3–0 away
success.\[3\] He was overlooked however for the finals in South Africa,
as the national side emerged victorious.

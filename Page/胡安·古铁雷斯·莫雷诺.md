**胡安·古铁雷斯·莫雷诺**（，），通常稱**华尼托**（），前[西班牙](../Page/西班牙.md "wikilink")足球運動員，擔任[中堅](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/中堅 "wikilink")。

A player of physical display, he is also noted for his excellent aerial ability. He spent the bulk of his career with [Betis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Real_Betis "wikilink"), appearing in more than 300 official games and winning one [Copa del Rey](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Copa_del_Rey "wikilink").

With the [Spanish national team](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain_national_football_team "wikilink"), Juanito appeared in one [World Cup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/FIFA_World_Cup "wikilink") and two [European Championships](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/UEFA_European_Championship "wikilink"), contributing to the conquest of [Euro 2008](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2008 "wikilink").

## 國家隊生涯

Juanito made his international debut for [Spain](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spain_national_football_team "wikilink") on 21 August 2002 against [Hungary](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungary_national_football_team "wikilink"), in a [testimonial](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Testimonial_match "wikilink") for [Ferenc Puskás](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ferenc_Puskás "wikilink"). His first goal for the national side came against [Côte d'Ivoire](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Côte_d'Ivoire_national_football_team "wikilink") in a 3–2 win.

Juanito represented Spain at [UEFA Euro 2004](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2004 "wikilink") and [Euro 2008](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/UEFA_Euro_2008 "wikilink") (playing in the 2–1 win over [Greece](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greece_national_football_team "wikilink") in the latter tournament\[1\]) and the [2006 FIFA World Cup](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2006_FIFA_World_Cup "wikilink"), scoring with his head in the 1–0 triumph over [Saudi Arabia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saudi_Arabia_national_football_team "wikilink").\[2\] That goal was the first ever scored by a Betis player in the competition.

In the [2010 World Cup qualifier](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2010_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_\(UEFA\) "wikilink") against [Estonia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Estonia_national_football_team "wikilink") on 11 October 2008, he netted through another header in a 3–0 away success.\[3\] He was overlooked however for the finals in South Africa, as the national side emerged victorious.

**臭鼬科**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）是[食肉目下的一个科](../Page/食肉目.md "wikilink")。

## 分类

本科现存4属12种\[1\]：

  - [臭鼬属](../Page/臭鼬属.md "wikilink") *Mephitis*
      - [臭鼬](../Page/臭鼬.md "wikilink") *Mephitis mephitis*
      - [大尾臭鼬](../Page/大尾臭鼬.md "wikilink") *Mephitis macroura*
  - [斑臭鼬属](../Page/斑臭鼬属.md "wikilink") *Spilogale*
      - [西部斑臭鼬](../Page/西部斑臭鼬.md "wikilink") *Spilogale gracilis*
      - [东部斑臭鼬](../Page/东部斑臭鼬.md "wikilink") *Spilogale putorius*
      - [南部斑臭鼬](../Page/南部斑臭鼬.md "wikilink") *Spilogale angudtifrons*
      - [侏斑臭鼬](../Page/侏斑臭鼬.md "wikilink") *Spilogale pygmaea*
  - [獾臭鼬属](../Page/獾臭鼬属.md "wikilink") *Conepatus*
      - [智利獾臭鼬](../Page/智利獾臭鼬.md "wikilink") *Conepatus chinga*
      - [巴塔戈尼亚獾臭鼬](../Page/巴塔戈尼亚獾臭鼬.md "wikilink") *Conepatus
        humboldtii*
      - [獾臭鼬](../Page/獾臭鼬.md "wikilink") *Conepatus leuconotus*
      - [墨西哥獾臭鼬](../Page/墨西哥獾臭鼬.md "wikilink") *Conepatus semistriatus*
  - [臭獾属](../Page/臭獾属.md "wikilink") *Mydaus*
      - [巽他臭獾](../Page/巽他臭獾.md "wikilink") *Mydaus javanensis*
      - [臭獾](../Page/臭獾.md "wikilink") *Mydaus marchei*

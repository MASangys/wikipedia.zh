**舌形貝屬**（[學名](../Page/學名.md "wikilink")：）是[舌形貝科下的一個](../Page/舌形貝科.md "wikilink")[屬](../Page/屬.md "wikilink")，俗名**海豆芽**，[模式種是](../Page/模式種.md "wikilink")[鴨嘴海豆芽](../Page/鴨嘴海豆芽.md "wikilink")（）。早在[寒武紀中期](../Page/寒武紀.md "wikilink")，舌形貝屬[動物就已存在](../Page/動物.md "wikilink")\[1\]。一些生物學家認為舌形貝屬生物是一種[活化石](../Page/活化石.md "wikilink")，因為這一類[物種從遠古一直生存到現在](../Page/物種.md "wikilink")。跟其他[腕足類一樣](../Page/腕足類.md "wikilink")，舌形貝屬動物也是生物。

舌形貝屬生活在[溫帶和](../Page/溫帶.md "wikilink")[熱帶海域](../Page/熱帶.md "wikilink")，[肉莖粗大](../Page/肉莖.md "wikilink")，能在[海底鑽洞穴居住](../Page/海底.md "wikilink")，肉足可以在洞穴裡自由伸縮絕大部分。時間在洞穴裡，只靠[外套膜上面的三個管子和外界接觸](../Page/外套膜.md "wikilink")。

## 特徵

這類較小的無[鉸腕足動物的殼很薄](../Page/鉸齒.md "wikilink")，兩瓣微凸，外廓呈[舌形](../Page/舌.md "wikilink")。腳狀瓣的[嘴有一個](../Page/嘴.md "wikilink")[三角形槽用來支撐](../Page/三角形.md "wikilink")[腳](../Page/腳.md "wikilink")。瓣內沒有[牙和](../Page/牙.md "wikilink")[眼窩](../Page/眼.md "wikilink")。其殼上的花紋只有一條纖細的線。\[2\]

## 物種

舌形貝屬動物包括以下現存物種\[3\]：

  - [亞氏海豆芽](../Page/亞氏海豆芽.md "wikilink") *Lingula adamsi* <small>Dall,
    1873</small>
  - [鴨嘴海豆芽](../Page/鴨嘴海豆芽.md "wikilink") *Lingula anatina*
    <small>Lamarck, 1801</small>
  - *[Lingula dregeri](../Page/Lingula_dregeri.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Andreae, 1893</small>
  - *[Lingula eocenica](../Page/Lingula_eocenica.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Moroz & Ermokhyna, 1990</small>
  - *[Lingula parva](../Page/Lingula_parva.md "wikilink")* <small>Smith,
    1871</small>
  - *[Lingula reevii](../Page/Lingula_reevii.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Davidson, 1880</small>
  - *[Lingula rostrum](../Page/Lingula_rostrum.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(Shaw, 1798)</small>
  - *[Lingula tenuis](../Page/Lingula_tenuis.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Sowerby</small>
  - *[Lingula translucida](../Page/Lingula_translucida.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Dall, 1921</small>
  - *[Lingula tumidula](../Page/Lingula_tumidula.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Reeve, 1841</small>
  - *[Lingula waikatoensis](../Page/Lingula_waikatoensis.md "wikilink")*
    <small>Pen, 1930</small>

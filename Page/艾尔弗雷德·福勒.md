**艾尔弗雷德·福勒**，[CBE](../Page/大英帝國勳章.md "wikilink")
[FRS](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/皇家学会会士 "wikilink")\[1\]（，），出生于[约克郡](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/约克郡 "wikilink")，英国[天文学家](../Page/天文学家.md "wikilink")。

## 奖项和荣誉

**奖项**

  - 法国科学院Valz奖\[2\] (1913年)
  - [皇家天文学会金质奖章](../Page/英國皇家天文學會金質獎章.md "wikilink") (1915年)
  - [皇家奖章](../Page/皇家獎章.md "wikilink") (1918年)
  - [皇家学会会士](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/皇家学会会士 "wikilink")
  - [美国国家科学院](../Page/美国国家科学院.md "wikilink")[亨利·德雷伯奖章](../Page/亨利·德雷伯獎章.md "wikilink")
    (1920)\[3\]
  - [布鲁斯奖章](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/布鲁斯奖 "wikilink") (1934年)
  - [大英帝国勋章](../Page/大英帝國勳章.md "wikilink") (1935年)

**以他的名字命名的事物**

  - [月球上的](../Page/月球.md "wikilink")[福勒环形山](../Page/福勒环形山.md "wikilink")（与[拉尔夫·福勒共同命名](../Page/拉爾夫·福勒.md "wikilink")）

## 发表的论文

  - *The Spectra of Metallic Arcs in an Exhausted Globe* (with H Page,
    (Proc Roy Soc, vol lxxii);
  - *Formulae for Spectrum Series* (with H Shaw, Astrophys Journ, vols
    xviii, xxi);
  - *The Spectra of Antarian Stars in relation to the Fluted Spectrum of
    Titanium* (Proc Roy Soc, vol lxxiii, 1904);
  - *Observations of the Spectra of Sunspots, Region C to D* (Monthly
    Notices Roy Astron Soc, vol lxv, 1905);
  - *Spectroscopic Observations of the Great Sunspot (February, 1905)
    and Associated Prominences* (ibid, vol lxv, 1905);
  - *Total Solar Eclipse, 1905, August 30* (with H L Callendar) (Proc
    Roy Soc, vol lxxvii, 1905);
  - *High Level Chromosperic Lines and their Behaviour in Sunspot
    Spectra* (Monthly Notices Roy Astron Soc, vol lxvi, 1906);
  - *Observations and Discussion of the Spectra of Sunspots, Region B to
    E* (Trans Internat Union Solar Research, vol i, 1906);
  - *Enhanced Lines of Iron in the Region F to C, and Note on Silicon in
    the Chromosphere* (Monthly Notices, Roy Astron Soc vol lxvii, 1906);
  - *The Fluted Spectrum of Titanium Oxide* (Proc Roy Soc, vol lxxx,
    1907);
  - *The Origin of certain Bands in the Spectra of Sunspots* (Monthly
    Notices, Roy Astron Soc, vol lxvii, 1907);
  - *Report of Committee on Sunspot Spectra* (Trans Internat Union Solar
    Research, vol ii, 1908);
  - *The Spectrum of Scandium and its relation to Solar Spectra* (Phil
    Trans, A, 1908);
  - *The Reproduction of Prismatic Spectrum Photographs on a Uniform
    Scale of Wave-lengths* (Astrophys Journ, vol xxviii, 1908);
  - *Spectroscopic Comparison of o Ceti with Titanium Oxide* (Monthly
    Notices, Roy Astron Soc, vol lxix, 1909).

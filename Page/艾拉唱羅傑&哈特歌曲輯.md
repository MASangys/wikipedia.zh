***艾拉唱羅傑&哈特歌曲輯***（Ella Fitzgerald Sings the Rodgers and Hart
Songbook）是1956年美國爵士女歌手[艾拉·費茲傑拉與由巴迪](../Page/艾拉·費茲傑拉.md "wikilink")·布雷格曼（Buddy
Bregman）指揮的室內管弦樂團合作錄製的專輯，演唱由[理查·勞傑斯作曲](../Page/理查·勞傑斯.md "wikilink")，[勞倫茲·哈特作詞的作品](../Page/勞倫茲·哈特.md "wikilink")。

This album was inducted into the [Grammy Hall of
Fame](../Page/Grammy_Hall_of_Fame_Award.md "wikilink") in 1999, which is
a special Grammy award established in 1973 to honor recordings that are
at least twenty-five years old, and that have "qualitative or historical
significance."\[1\]

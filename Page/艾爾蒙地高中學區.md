**愛滿地高中學區**\[1\](**El Monte Union High School District**,
**EMUHSD**)是[洛杉矶县的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/洛杉矶县_\(加利福尼亚州\) "wikilink")[学区](../Page/学区.md "wikilink"),
总部在[愛滿地](../Page/艾爾蒙地.md "wikilink")。\[2\]

## 学校

  - Arroyo High School
  - El Monte High School
  - Mountain View High School
  - 羅斯密高中 (Rosemead High School) ([柔似蜜](../Page/柔似蜜.md "wikilink"))\[3\]
  - South El Monte High School
  - Fernando R. Ledesma High School
  - San Gabriel Valley Conservation Corps
  - El Monte Union High School Community Day
  - El Monte-Rosemead Adult School

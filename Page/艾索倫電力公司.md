**艾索倫電力公司（Exelon Corporation）**是[美國一家公共事業控股公司](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/美國 "wikilink")，也是美國目前最大的[核電公司](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/核電 "wikilink")。其擁有10座[發電廠](../Page/發電廠.md "wikilink")，每年發電可達到1.8萬[兆瓦](../Page/瓦特.md "wikilink")，佔美國核電力發電量的20%。它的子公司主要針對住宅、商業、工業和批發顧客從事電力的生產、 購買、 傳輸、發行和銷售。並同時針對住宅、商業和工業顧客從事分配和銷售天然氣業務。 \[1\]

## 歷史

艾索倫電力公司成立於2000年10月，由（PECO Energy Company）及 兩家電力公司合併以成。\[2\] Unicom 的最早前身為芝加哥愛迪生公司（Chicago Edison Company），1907年與聯邦電力（Commonwealth Electric）合併，成為[聯邦愛迪生公司](../Page/聯邦愛迪生電力公司.md "wikilink")（Commonwealth Edison Company，ComEd）。後來，聯邦愛迪生公司又經數次合併成為 Unicom。\[3\] The merger was overseen by the CEO of Unicom, John Rowe who joined the corporation in 1998 and went on to lead the newly-formed Exelon until 2012, becoming the nation's longest-serving utility executive.\[4\]\[5\]\[6\]

Under Rowe, Exelon explored a number of mergers and acquisitions. On June 30, 2005, the [Federal Energy Regulatory Commission](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Energy_Regulatory_Commission "wikilink") approved the merger of Exelon and [Public Service Enterprise Group Inc.](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Public_Service_Enterprise_Group_Inc. "wikilink"), a [New Jersey](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Jersey "wikilink") utility. Under this merger, Exelon would have become the largest utility in the [United States](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "wikilink").\[7\] The two companies later broke off the agreement\[8\] due to pressure put on the NJ Board of Public Utilities by public interest groups, including New Jersey Citizen Action.\[9\] The merger sat pending in front of the NJBPU for nineteen months before Exelon concluded that they were fighting a losing battle.\[10\] On April 28, 2011, Exelon announced a merger with [Constellation Energy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constellation_Energy "wikilink") for $7.9 billion with the combined company owning more than 34 gigawatts of power generation (55 percent nuclear, 24 percent natural gas, 8 percent renewable including hydro, 7 percent oil and 6 percent coal).\[11\]\[12\] The merger was completed on March 12, 2012.\[13\]

In 2012, when announcing the cancellation of new nuclear construction for [Victoria County Station](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Victoria_County_Station "wikilink"), Texas, Exelon stated that economic and market conditions, especially low natural gas prices, made the "construction of new merchant nuclear power plants in competitive markets uneconomical now and for the foreseeable future".\[14\]

In 2008, Christopher Crane was named CEO following Rowe's retirement and the completion of the merger with Constellation Energy.\[15\]\[16\] Exelon announced the proposed purchase of [Pepco Holdings](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pepco_Holdings "wikilink"), Inc on April 30, 2014, for $6.8 billion in an all-cash transaction. The merger was rejected by the [District of Columbia Public Service Commission](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/District_of_Columbia_Public_Service_Commission "wikilink") in August 2015, though it was approved by other federal and state regulators. The companies appealed the decision.\[17\] On March 23, 2016, the merger was approved by the Washington DC Public Service Commission, under a revised set of terms. The merger was completed later that day making Exelon the largest regulated utility in the United States by customer count and total revenue.\[18\]

## 艾索倫旗下公司及產業

### 艾索倫產能（Exelon Generation）

艾索倫產能（Exelon Generation）是艾索倫電力旗下的能源供應商。其下有兩大主要業務部門，分別為艾索倫核能（Exelon Nuclear）及艾索倫能源（Exelon Power）。\[19\]

#### Wind

Exelon has 47 wind projects in ten states, totaling nearly 1,500 megawatts (MW).\[35\] In 2010 Exelon purchased [John Deere](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Deere "wikilink") Renewables for $860 million adding 735 MW to Exelon's capacity.\[36\]

#### Exelon PowerLabs, LLC

Exelon PowerLabs, LLC, a wholly owned subsidiary of Exelon Generation, provides quality laboratory services to Exelon and its subsidiaries, as well as other organizations across multiple industries. Exelon PowerLabs operates at four locations nationwide. The [Plattsburgh, New York](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plattsburgh,_New_York "wikilink") and [Madison, Pennsylvania](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Madison,_Pennsylvania "wikilink") facilities specialize in calibration services; the [Wilmington, Illinois](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilmington,_Will_County,_Illinois "wikilink") facility specializes in failure analysis and component testing, and the [Coatesville, Pennsylvania](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coatesville,_Pennsylvania "wikilink") (headquarters) location provides all services. Their quality system is ISO 9001:2000 registered for the Coatesville and Plattsburgh facilities. The Coatesville, Madison and Plattsburgh facilities are also [ISO/IEC 17025](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO/IEC_17025 "wikilink") accredited through the American Association for Laboratory Accreditation (A2LA).\[37\] Exelon PowerLabs has been operating since 1911.\[38\]

#### Constellation Energy

Constellation, an Exelon Company, provides energy sales in deregulated markets, known as competitive energy sales, to business, residential and government customers across the United States. Their customers include two-thirds of Fortune 100 companies.\[39\]

**Atlantic City Electric**

Atlantic City Electric is a regulated utility located in [New Jersey](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Jersey "wikilink") with 545,000 electric customers.\[40\]

#### Baltimore Gas and Electric

Baltimore Gas and Electric (BGE) is a regulated utility located in [Maryland](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maryland "wikilink") with 1.25 million+ electric customers and 650,000+ natural gas customers.\[41\]

#### Commonwealth Edison

Commonwealth Edison (ComEd) is regulated utility located in [Illinois](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Illinois "wikilink") with 4 million electric customers.\[42\]

#### Delmarva Power

Delmarva Power is a regulated utility located in [Delaware](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delaware "wikilink") and the Eastern Shore of Maryland with 515,000 electric customers over 5,000 square miles of service territory. They provide natural gas to 130,000 customers in northern Delaware.\[43\]

#### Philadelphia Electric Company

Philadelphia Electric Company (PECO) is regulated utility located in eastern [Pennsylvania](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pennsylvania "wikilink") with 1.6 million electric customers and more than 500,000 natural gas customers.\[44\]

#### Potomac Electric Power Company

Potomac Electric Power Company (PEPCO) is a regulated utility in [Washington, D.C.](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Washington,_D.C. "wikilink") that serves 842,000 electric customers in the district and parts of Maryland.\[45\]

#### Exelon Transmission Company

Exelon Transmission Company is specifically focused on the development of power transmission lines—specifically the RITELine project which involves work in [Ohio](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ohio "wikilink"), [Indiana](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indiana "wikilink"), and [Illinois](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Illinois "wikilink").\[46\]\[47\]

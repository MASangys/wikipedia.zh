**芒蛤科**（学名：），又名**蛏螂科**，是[雙殼綱](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/雙殼綱 "wikilink")[原鳃亚纲](../Page/原鳃亚纲.md "wikilink")[芒蛤目之下的一個](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/芒蛤目 "wikilink")[科](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/科_\(生物\) "wikilink")，其物種皆為[海洋蛤蜊類動物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/海洋生物 "wikilink")。

## 生物學

本科物種的消化管道 are remarkable in that their digestive tract is either
extremely small or non-existent, and their feeding appendages are too
short to reach outside the shell.\[1\]

It has been shown that these clams host
[sulphur](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/sulphur "wikilink")-[oxidizing](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/oxidizing "wikilink")
[bacteria](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/bacteria "wikilink")
intracellularly within their
[gill](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/gill "wikilink") filaments. As
[chemoautotrophs](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/chemoautotrophs "wikilink"),
these bacterial
[symbionts](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/symbionts "wikilink")
synthesize organic matter from CO<sub>2</sub> and are the primary source
of nutrition for the whole organism.\[2\]\[3\] In turn, the animal host
provides its symbionts a habitat in which they have access to the
substrates of chemoautotrophy (O<sub>2</sub>, CO<sub>2</sub>, and
reduced inorganic compounds such as H<sub>2</sub>S). Together, these
partners create "animals" with novel metabolic capabilities.

**芝加哥唐人街**，是位于[芝加哥盔甲广场](../Page/芝加哥.md "wikilink")（Armour
Square）社区的[唐人街](../Page/唐人街.md "wikilink")，以舍麦路（Cermak
Road）和永活街（Wentworth
Avenue）为中心。根据[2000年人口普查](ftp://ftp2.census.gov/census_2000/datasets/demographic_profile/Illinois/2kh17.pdf),
[1](http://www.census.gov/population/estimates/metro-city/99mfips.txt芝加哥大都会统计区)拥有68,021名华人。在华人因迫害逃离加州之后，芝加哥成为美国第二古老的华人社区。

在芝加哥另有一处“新唐人街”，位于北区亚皆老街周围。

## 教育

[芝加哥公立學校營運興氏學校](../Page/芝加哥公立學校.md "wikilink") (John C. Haines
School)和。\[1\]\[2\]

興氏學校邊界包括唐人街和(公營住宅)。2001年70%的學生是亞洲人和28%是黑人。\[3\] Connie Laureman of the
*[Chicago
Tribune](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chicago_Tribune "wikilink")*
stated that Haines, in 1990, was "crowded and dilapidated".\[4\] Until
Gandy Easton became principal in 1990, the school had [de facto racial
segregation](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/de_facto_segregation "wikilink")
as ethnic Chinese students stayed in a bilingual program while black
students took regular classes. Easton combined the two levels together,
despite protests from ethnic Chinese parents.\[5\] By 2001 school
authorities instituted programs to combat racism and ensure Chinese and
black students socialized with one another.\[6\]

The [Roman Catholic Archdiocese of
Chicago](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Archdiocese_of_Chicago "wikilink")
supports the St. Therese Chinese Catholic School, a K-8 private Catholic
school.\[7\]  almost all of the students were ethnic Chinese.\[8\]

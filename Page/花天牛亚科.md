**花天牛亚科**（學名： ）是[天牛科](../Page/天牛科.md "wikilink")[昆蟲的一個](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/昆蟲 "wikilink")[亚科](../Page/亚科.md "wikilink")，在全世界包括9個族、約150個屬，當中有在中國分佈的有4族63属173种。 這一血統在[北半球](../Page/北半球.md "wikilink")最為多樣化。過往亞科的物種被併入本亞科，但近年再被認為是一個單獨的亞科。 2016年，舊屬[木花天牛族的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/#木花天牛族 "wikilink")[无瘤花天牛属](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/无瘤花天牛属 "wikilink")（*[Caraphia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caraphia "wikilink")*）獨立出來成為[无瘤花天牛族](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/无瘤花天牛族 "wikilink")（[Caraphiini](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caraphiini "wikilink")），是本亞科的第十個族。此外，仍有部分屬仍處於地位未定。

Usually among the smaller members of their family, these beetles are of a slender shape – particularly the [胸節 (昆蟲)](../Page/胸節_\(昆蟲\).md "wikilink") is markedly less wide than the wings, while the [翅鞘](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/翅鞘 "wikilink") tips are often pointed. They differ from most other longhorn beetles in that the [觸角](../Page/觸角.md "wikilink")e are not directly adjacent to the [複眼](../Page/複眼.md "wikilink")s. Hence, the latter are generally oval in outline, rather than having an indentation where the antennae originate, or even being divided by them. In addition, [两性异形](../Page/两性异形.md "wikilink") is not infrequently seen in lepturines; usually, longhorn beetles are not [dimorphic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/sexual_dimorphism "wikilink") or only have longer antennae in males.

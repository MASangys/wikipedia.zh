**莽草酸脱氢酶**（，[EC](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/EC编号 "wikilink")
[1.1.1.25](http://enzyme.expasy.org/EC/1.1.1.25)）是一种以[NADP<sup>+</sup>为](../Page/烟酰胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸磷酸.md "wikilink")[受体](../Page/受体_\(生物化学\).md "wikilink")、作用于[供体](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/供体 "wikilink")[CH](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/次甲基 "wikilink")-[OH](../Page/羟基.md "wikilink")[基团上的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/基团 "wikilink")[氧化还原酶](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/氧化还原酶 "wikilink")。这种[酶能](../Page/酶.md "wikilink")[催化以下](../Page/催化.md "wikilink")[酶促反应](../Page/酶促反应.md "wikilink")：

  -
    [莽草酸](../Page/莽草酸.md "wikilink") +
    [NADP<sup>+</sup>](../Page/烟酰胺腺嘌呤二核苷酸磷酸.md "wikilink")
    \(\rightleftharpoons\) [3-脱氢莽草酸](../Page/3-脱氢莽草酸.md "wikilink") +
    NADH + [H<sup>+</sup>](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/氢离子 "wikilink")

上述反应中的NADP<sup>+</sup>不能以NAD<sup>+</sup>替代\[1\]。莽草酸脱氢酶主要参与苯丙氨酸、[酪氨酸和](../Page/酪氨酸.md "wikilink")[色氨酸的](../Page/色氨酸.md "wikilink")[生物合成过程](../Page/生物合成.md "wikilink")——[莽草酸途径](../Page/莽草酸途径.md "wikilink")。在较高等的[生物](../Page/生物.md "wikilink")[细胞中](../Page/细胞.md "wikilink")，这种酶与[3-脱氢奎尼酸脱水酶一同构成一种多酶](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/3-脱氢奎尼酸脱水酶 "wikilink")[复合物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/复合物 "wikilink")。\[2\]

## 功能

Shikimate dehydrogenase is an enzyme that catalyzes one step of the
[shikimate
pathway](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/shikimic_acid "wikilink"). This
pathway is found in bacteria, plants, fungi, algae, and parasites and is
responsible for the
[biosynthesis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/biosynthesis "wikilink") of
aromatic amino acids
([phenylalanine](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/phenylalanine "wikilink"),
[tyrosine](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/tyrosine "wikilink"), and
[tryptophan](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/tryptophan "wikilink")) from
the metabolism of carbohydrates. In contrast, animals and humans lack
this pathway hence products of this biosynthetic route are [essential
amino
acids](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/essential_amino_acid "wikilink")
that must be obtained through an animal's diet.

There are seven enzymes that play a role in this pathway. Shikimate
dehydrogenase (also known as 3-dehydroshikimate dehydrogenase) is the
fourth step of the seven step process. This step converts
3-dehydroshikimate to shikimate as well as reduces NADP<sup>+</sup> to
NADPH.

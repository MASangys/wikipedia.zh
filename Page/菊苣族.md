**菊苣族**（学名：）为[菊科](../Page/菊科.md "wikilink")[舌状花亚科的一](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/舌状花亚科 "wikilink")[族](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/族_\(生物\) "wikilink")，全族约93属、1600多個可交配的物种、超過7000
種。广布[東](../Page/東半球.md "wikilink")[北半球](../Page/北半球.md "wikilink")[温带地区](../Page/温带.md "wikilink")\[1\]
。常见的如[生菜](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/生菜 "wikilink")，[蒲公英等植物就属于该族](../Page/蒲公英.md "wikilink")。
Cichorieae all have milky latex and flowerheads that only contain one
type of . The genera
*[Gundelia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gundelia "wikilink")* and **
only have [菊科](../Page/菊科.md "wikilink"), while all other genera only
have [菊科](../Page/菊科.md "wikilink"). The genera that contain most
species are
*[Taraxacum](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taraxacum "wikilink")*
(Crepidinae subtribe) with about 1600 apomictic species,
*[Hieracium](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hieracium "wikilink")* with
about 770 sexually reproducing and 5200 apomictic species, and
*[山柳菊属](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/山柳菊属 "wikilink")* with 110
sexually reproducing and 700 apomictic species (both Hieraciinae).\[2\]
Well known members include [莴苣](../Page/莴苣.md "wikilink"),
[chicory](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/chicory "wikilink"),
[蒲公英](../Page/蒲公英.md "wikilink"), and
[婆罗门参属](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/婆罗门参属 "wikilink").

## Description

Most species are [草本植物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/草本植物 "wikilink"),
[多年生植物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/多年生植物 "wikilink"), short-lived or
[一年生植物s](../Page/一年生植物.md "wikilink"), rarely
[亚灌木s](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/亚灌木 "wikilink"),
[灌木s](../Page/灌木.md "wikilink") or
[攀緣植物s](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/攀緣植物 "wikilink"). All
Cichorieae-species have  in both the roots, stems and leaves, and this
occurs to be a unique character among the Asteraceae, although latex as
such occurs rather widespread in this family. The leaves are in a  or
[葉](../Page/葉.md "wikilink") along the stem, but this is the dominant
situation in the Asteraceae. The only exception in the Cichorieae are
the opposite lower leaves of **. Traditionally, the Cichorieae consisted
of taxa with flowerheads only containing
[植物生殖型態學](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/植物生殖型態學 "wikilink")
ligulate florets (having a strap-shaped
[花瓣](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/花瓣 "wikilink") with five teeth at
its tip), a rare character that is further present only in the genera
**, **, ** (), and ** ( [向日葵族](../Page/向日葵族.md "wikilink")). However,
recently the genera *Gundelia* and *Warionia* have been included in the
Cichorieae, and those two genera have heads containing only disk
flowers.\[3\]

## 分類

In his *Elemens de botanique ou methode pour connoître les plantes* of
1694,  first described this group as a
[分類單元](../Page/分類單元.md "wikilink"), calling it the "13th
class of the plant kingdom". He only assigned
[分類單元](../Page/分類單元.md "wikilink") to it that are still
regarded part of the Cichorieae today.  gave this group the name
"Cichoracées" in 1723. Since the name predates the start of the
[二名法](../Page/二名法.md "wikilink") in 1753, it is not , but
[让-巴蒂斯特·拉马克](../Page/让-巴蒂斯特·拉马克.md "wikilink") and
[Augustin Pyramus de
Candolle](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Augustin_Pyramus_de_Candolle "wikilink")
used the name Cichorieae in the *Synopsis Plantarium in Flora Gallica
Descriptarum*, published in 1806. The name Lactuceae that was coined by
[亨利·卡西尼](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/亨利·卡西尼 "wikilink") in 1819,
comprises the same group of taxa and is thus a
[異名](../Page/異名.md "wikilink"). Obviously, over the centuries
since the group was first identified, numerous new taxa have been
described that are now included in the Cichorieae, and the group has
been divided in different subgroups using various morphological
character states by authors
例如：卡西尼、[大卫·顿](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/大卫·顿 "wikilink"),
, A.P. De Candolle, [乔治·边沁](../Page/乔治·边沁.md "wikilink") 及.\[4\]，

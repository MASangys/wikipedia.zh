**華潤·萬象城**（Mixc），簡稱**萬象城**，首個項目位於[中國](../Page/中國.md "wikilink")[深圳市](../Page/深圳市.md "wikilink")[羅湖區](../Page/羅湖區.md "wikilink")\[1\]，由[華潤置地有限公司旗下](../Page/華潤置地.md "wikilink")[華潤（深圳）有限公司經營](../Page/華潤（深圳）有限公司.md "wikilink")\[2\]。目前，萬象城已分布在深圳（3）、[杭州](../Page/杭州.md "wikilink")、[沈陽](../Page/沈陽.md "wikilink")、[成都](../Page/成都.md "wikilink")、[南寧](../Page/南寧.md "wikilink")、[鄭州](../Page/鄭州.md "wikilink")、[重慶](../Page/重慶.md "wikilink")、[無錫](../Page/無錫.md "wikilink")、[青島](../Page/青島.md "wikilink")、[贛州](../Page/贛州.md "wikilink")、[合肥](../Page/合肥.md "wikilink")、[溫州](../Page/溫州.md "wikilink")、[西安](../Page/西安.md "wikilink")、[上海](../Page/上海.md "wikilink")、[泰州](../Page/泰州.md "wikilink")、[太原](../Page/太原.md "wikilink")、[南通](../Page/南通.md "wikilink")、[柳州](../Page/柳州.md "wikilink")、[石家莊](../Page/石家莊.md "wikilink")、[廈門](../Page/廈門.md "wikilink")、[桂林](../Page/桂林.md "wikilink")、[寧波](../Page/寧波.md "wikilink")、[濟南](../Page/濟南.md "wikilink")、[汕頭](../Page/汕頭.md "wikilink")、[福州](../Page/福州.md "wikilink")、[武漢](../Page/武漢.md "wikilink")、[昆明](../Page/昆明.md "wikilink")、[南京等三十余座城市](../Page/南京.md "wikilink")。

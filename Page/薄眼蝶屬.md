**薄眼蝶屬**（學名：***Erycinidia***）是[眼蝶亞科](../Page/眼蝶亞科.md "wikilink")[眼蝶族](../Page/眼蝶族.md "wikilink")[珍眼蝶亞族中的一個屬](../Page/珍眼蝶亞族.md "wikilink")。物種分佈於[新畿內亞中部高地](../Page/新畿內亞.md "wikilink")\[1\]。

## 物種

  - *[Erycinidia ducis](../Page/Erycinidia_ducis.md "wikilink")*
  - [薄眼蝶](../Page/薄眼蝶.md "wikilink") *Erycinidia gracilis*
  - [半白薄眼蝶](../Page/半白薄眼蝶.md "wikilink") *Erycinidia hemileuca*
  - [莫德薄眼蝶](../Page/莫德薄眼蝶.md "wikilink") *Erycinidia maudei*
  - [罕薄眼蝶](../Page/罕薄眼蝶.md "wikilink") *Erycinidia tenera*
  - *[Erycinidia virgo](../Page/Erycinidia_virgo.md "wikilink")*

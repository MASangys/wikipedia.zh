**蘑菇科**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）又名**伞菌科**，是[担子菌门](../Page/担子菌门.md "wikilink")[伞菌目的一科](../Page/伞菌目.md "wikilink")，包含了[伞菌属等合共](../Page/伞菌属.md "wikilink")85個屬、1340個品種\[1\]。

## 屬

[白环蘑属](../Page/白环蘑属.md "wikilink")（）跟[白鬼伞属](../Page/白鬼伞属.md "wikilink")（）這兩個屬均已知與[螞蟻](../Page/螞蟻.md "wikilink")[共生](../Page/共生.md "wikilink")\[2\]。The
extinct genus *[Coprinites](../Page/Coprinites.md "wikilink")* is one of
four known agaricalean genera in the fossil record.\[3\]Others include
*[Aureofungus](../Page/Aureofungus.md "wikilink")*,
*[Protomycena](../Page/Protomycena.md "wikilink")*, and
*[Archaeomarasmius](../Page/Archaeomarasmius.md "wikilink")*.
*Archaeomarasmius leggeti*, from [Atlantic Coastal
Plain](../Page/Atlantic_Coastal_Plain.md "wikilink") amber, is
90–94 [Ma](../Page/Million_years_ago.md "wikilink")); the other
fossil genera are from [Dominican
amber](../Page/Dominican_amber.md "wikilink") and date to 15–20 Ma.\[4\]

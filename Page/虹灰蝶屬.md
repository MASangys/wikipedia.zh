**虹灰蝶屬**（學名：***Arcas***）是[灰蝶科](../Page/灰蝶科.md "wikilink")[線灰蝶亞科](../Page/線灰蝶亞科.md "wikilink")[美灰蝶族中的一個屬](../Page/美灰蝶族.md "wikilink")。物種分佈於[新熱帶界](../Page/新熱帶界.md "wikilink")\[1\]。幼蟲以[番荔枝科](../Page/番荔枝科.md "wikilink")[樓林果屬](../Page/樓林果屬.md "wikilink")（*Rollinia*）植物作[寄主](../Page/寄主.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

  - [彩虹灰蝶](../Page/彩虹灰蝶.md "wikilink")*Arcas cypria*
  - [帝王虹灰蝶](../Page/帝王虹灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Arcas imperialis*
  - [二尾虹灰蝶](../Page/二尾虹灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Arcas ducalis*
  - *Arcas alleluia*
  - *Arcas gozmanyi*
  - *Arcas splendor*
  - *Arcas jivaro*
  - *Arcas delphia*
  - [圖虹灰蝶](../Page/圖虹灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Arcas tuneta*

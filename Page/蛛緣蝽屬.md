## 物種

本屬現時包括以下六個物種：

  - ** <small>(Linnaeus, 1758)</small> (red-backed bug)
  - ** <small>Montandon, 1893</small>
  - ** <small>(Say, 1825)</small>
  - ** <small>Herrich-Schaeffer, 1847</small>
  - *[Alydus scutellatus](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alydus_scutellatus "wikilink")* <small>Van Duzee, 1903</small>
  - ** <small>Fracker, 1918</small>

**蜘蛛螺屬**（學名：）是[鳳凰螺科的一個](../Page/鳳凰螺科.md "wikilink")[腹足綱](../Page/腹足綱.md "wikilink")[軟體動物的](../Page/軟體動物.md "wikilink")[屬](../Page/屬_\(生物\).md "wikilink")\[1\]，舊屬[中腹足目](../Page/中腹足目.md "wikilink")，今屬[新生腹足類](../Page/新生腹足類.md "wikilink")[高腹足類](../Page/高腹足類.md "wikilink")[玉黍螺類支序](../Page/玉黍螺類.md "wikilink")\[2\]。

## 簡述

本屬物種都屬於大型的物種\[3\]，殼口有7根突起的長管，因像蜘蛛形狀，故名。高約17公分，淡褐色。棲於[台灣](../Page/台灣.md "wikilink")、[琉球等暖海沙泥中](../Page/琉球.md "wikilink")。其虹管（[siphonal
canal](../Page/siphonal_canal.md "wikilink")）長而彎，and the [apertural
lip](../Page/apertural_lip.md "wikilink") is adorned with
[circumapertural](../Page/circumapertural.md "wikilink") projections,
which are fingerlike
[processes](../Page/Process_\(anatomy\).md "wikilink") emanating from
the edge of the [shell
aperture](../Page/Aperture_\(mollusc\).md "wikilink").\[4\]

**蝗莺属**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink"):
）是[雀形目](../Page/雀形目.md "wikilink")[鶲科的一个](../Page/鶲科.md "wikilink")[属](../Page/属_\(生物\).md "wikilink")，包括以下於東亞地區較常見的物种：

  - [鴝蝗莺](../Page/鴝蝗莺.md "wikilink"),''Locustella luscinoides ''
  - [小蝗莺](../Page/小蝗莺.md "wikilink"), *Locustella certhiola*
  - [北蝗莺](../Page/北蝗莺.md "wikilink"), *Locustella ochotensis*
  - [矛斑蝗莺](../Page/矛斑蝗莺.md "wikilink"), *Locustella lanceolata*
  - [河蝗莺](../Page/河蝗莺.md "wikilink"),*Locustella fluviatilis*
  - [苍眉蝗莺](../Page/苍眉蝗莺.md "wikilink"), *Locustella fasciolata*
  - [黑斑蝗莺](../Page/黑斑蝗莺.md "wikilink"), *Locustella naevia*
  - [史氏蝗莺](../Page/史氏蝗莺.md "wikilink"), *Locustella pleskei*
  - [斑背大尾莺](../Page/斑背大尾莺.md "wikilink"), *Locustella pryeri*,
    从2004年\[1\]
  - [四川蝗莺](../Page/四川蝗莺.md "wikilink"), *Locustella chengi*
  - [臺灣短翅鶯](../Page/臺灣短翅鶯.md "wikilink"), *Locustella alishanensis*

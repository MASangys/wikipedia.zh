**螔螺屬**（學名：**）是[翼足目](../Page/翼足目.md "wikilink")[螔螺科之下的一個](../Page/螔螺科.md "wikilink")[海洋](../Page/海洋生物.md "wikilink")[腹足綱](../Page/腹足綱.md "wikilink")[軟體動物門屬](../Page/軟體動物門.md "wikilink")，即俗稱「[海蝴蝶](../Page/海蝴蝶.md "wikilink")」的一種。本屬物種是腹足綱之下其中一個物種非常豐富的屬\[1\]\[2\]。

本屬的學名「*Limacina*」本身的意思其實就是「像[蝸牛](../Page/蝸牛.md "wikilink")」\[3\]

作為一種在[海洋的](../Page/海洋.md "wikilink")[浮游生物界生活的腹足綱物種](../Page/浮游生物界.md "wikilink")，螔螺屬透過拍動牠們的一雙[擬足](../Page/擬足.md "wikilink")（亦作疣足，parapoida）來在水中暢泳，所以得來「海蝴蝶」的美名。

海蝴蝶是[有殼翼足類](../Page/有殼翼足類.md "wikilink")[支序](../Page/支序.md "wikilink")（Thecosomata）的成員，與之相對的是屬於[裸殼翼足類支序](../Page/裸殼翼足類.md "wikilink")（Gymnosomata）的[海天使](../Page/海天使.md "wikilink")。這兩個支序的成員合組成為[翼足類](../Page/翼足類.md "wikilink")。兩者的分別在於有殼翼足類的成員的背上還留有一個或大或小的殼，但裸殼翼足類的殼已完全褪化。

## 物種

本屬物種同時有化石種及生存種，分別如下：

  - † *[Limacina atypica](../Page/Limacina_atypica.md "wikilink")*
    (Laws, 1944)
  - (recent) *[Limacina
    bulimoides](../Page/Limacina_bulimoides.md "wikilink")*
    ([d'Orbigny](../Page/Alcide_d'Orbigny.md "wikilink"),
    1836)\[4\]\[5\] - Bulimoid pteropod. Distribution: Red Sea, Pacific.
    Length: 1.2 mm.
  - † *[Limacina erasmiana](../Page/Limacina_erasmiana.md "wikilink")*
    Janssen, 2010\[6\]
  - † *[Limacina dilatata](../Page/Limacina_dilatata.md "wikilink")*
    (Koenen, 1892)
  - † *[Limacina ferax](../Page/Limacina_ferax.md "wikilink")* (Laws,
    1944)
  - † *[Limacina gormani](../Page/Limacina_gormani.md "wikilink")*
    (Curry, 1982)\[7\]
  - † *[Limacina guersi](../Page/Limacina_guersi.md "wikilink")*
    Janssen, 2010\[8\]
  - [螔螺](../Page/螔螺.md "wikilink") *Limacina helicina* (Phipps,
    1774)：[模式種](../Page/模式種.md "wikilink")\[9\]。
  - (recent) *[Limacina
    lesueurii](../Page/Limacina_lesueurii.md "wikilink")* (d'Orbigny,
    1836) - Distribution: North America, Western Atlantic.
  - † *[Limacina limata](../Page/Limacina_limata.md "wikilink")*
    (Marwick, 1926)
  - † *[Limacina pygmaea](../Page/Limacina_pygmaea.md "wikilink")*
    (Lamarck, 1805)\[10\]\[11\]
  - (recent) *[Limacina rangii](../Page/Limacina_rangii.md "wikilink")*
    <small>(d'Orbigny, 1834)</small>
  - [后弯螔螺](../Page/后弯螔螺.md "wikilink") *Limacina retroversa* (Fleming,
    1823)：分布於北美洲的西大西洋及北冰洋，屬於是亚寒带种。
  - † *[Limacina sculptilis](../Page/Limacina_sculptilis.md "wikilink")*
    (Maxwell, 1992)
  - † *[Limacina taylori](../Page/Limacina_taylori.md "wikilink")*
    (Curry, 1965)\[12\]
  - (recent) *[Limacina
    trochiformis](../Page/Limacina_trochiformis.md "wikilink")*
    (d'Orbigny, 1836) - Trochiform pteropod. Distribution: North
    America, Western Atlantic, Red Sea, Pacific. Length: 1 mm.
  - † *[Limacina valvatina](../Page/Limacina_valvatina.md "wikilink")*
    (Reuss, 1867)\[13\]
  - † *[Limacina? vegrandis](../Page/Limacina?_vegrandis.md "wikilink")*
    Cahuzac & Janssen, 2010\[14\]

下列物種皆為[同種異名](../Page/同種異名.md "wikilink")：

  - (recent) *Limacina antarctica* Woodward, 1854::\[15\] synonym of
    *Limacina rangii* f. *antarctica* Woodward, 1854: synonym of
    *Limacina rangii* (d'Orbigny, 1834)
  - *Limacina balea* Møller, 1841: synonym of *Limacina retroversa*
    (Fleming, 1823)
  - *Limacina contorta* Sykes, 1905: synonym of *Limacina trochiformis*
    (d'Orbigny, 1834)
  - *Limacina crossei* Pelseneer, 1888: synonym of *Limacina lesueurii*
    (d'Orbigny, 1835)
  - *Limacina cucullata* Gould, 1852: synonym of *Limacina rangii* f.
    *antarctica* Woodward, 1854: synonym of *Limacina rangii*
    (d'Orbigny, 1834)
  - *Limacina helicialis* Lamarck, 1819: synonym of *Limacina helicina*
    (Phipps, 1774)
  - (recent) *Limacina helicoides* Jeffreys, 1877 - synonym of *[Thielea
    helicoides](../Page/Thielea_helicoides.md "wikilink")* (Jeffreys,
    1877)
  - (recent) *Limacina inflata* (d'Orbigny, 1836) - Planorbid pteropod.
    Distribution: circumglobal, Red Sea, Pacific. Length: 1 mm.
    Description: the shell is flatly twisted, resembling the shell of
    the cephalopod [Nautilus](../Page/Nautilus.md "wikilink"). -
    synonym: *[Heliconoides
    inflata](../Page/Heliconoides_inflata.md "wikilink")* (d’Orbigny,
    1836)
  - *Limacina naticoides* Souleyet, 1852: synonym of *Limacina
    trochiformis* (d'Orbigny, 1834)
  - *Limacina pacifica* Dall, 1871: synonym of *Limacina helicina
    pacifica* Dall, 1871
  - *Limacina scaphoidea* Gould, 1852: synonym of *[Heliconoides
    inflatus](../Page/Heliconoides_inflatus.md "wikilink")* (d'Orbigny,
    1834)

在[分类学意义上](../Page/分类学.md "wikilink")，**衣藻科**是[藻类下的一个科](../Page/藻类.md "wikilink")，其隶属于[胶毛藻目](../Page/胶毛藻目.md "wikilink")\[1\]。

## 分类

  - *Brachiomonas*
  - [四鞭藻属](../Page/四鞭藻属.md "wikilink") *Carteria*
  - *Chlainomonas*
  - [衣藻属](../Page/衣藻属.md "wikilink") *Chlamydomonas*
  - [拟衣藻属](../Page/拟衣藻属.md "wikilink") *Chloromonas*
  - *Halosarcinochlamys*
  - *[Lobochlamys](../Page/Lobochlamys.md "wikilink")*
  - [叶衣藻属](../Page/叶衣藻属.md "wikilink") *Lobomonas*
  - *Oogamochlamys*
  - [素衣藻属](../Page/素衣藻属.md "wikilink") *Polytoma*
  - *Polytomella*
  - *Pseudocarteria*
  - *Vitreochlamys*

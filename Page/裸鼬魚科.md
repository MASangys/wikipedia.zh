**裸鼬魚科**為[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鼬魚目的其中一科](../Page/鼬魚目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**裸鼬魚科**下分7個屬：

  - 膠胎鳚屬(Aphyonus)
      - [博林膠胎鳚](../Page/博林膠胎鳚.md "wikilink")(Aphyonus bolini)
      - [短背膠胎鳚](../Page/短背膠胎鳚.md "wikilink")(Aphyonus brevidorsalis)
      - [膠胎鳚](../Page/膠胎鳚.md "wikilink")(Aphyonus gelatinosus)
      - [拉氏膠胎鳚](../Page/拉氏膠胎鳚.md "wikilink")(Aphyonus rassi)
  - 盲鼬鳚屬(Barathronus)
      - [安芬盲鼬鳚](../Page/安芬盲鼬鳚.md "wikilink")(Barathronus affinis)
      - [雙色盲鼬鳚](../Page/雙色盲鼬鳚.md "wikilink")(Barathronus bicolor)
      - [布氏盲鼬鳚](../Page/布氏盲鼬鳚.md "wikilink")(Barathronus bruuni)
      - [盲鼬鳚](../Page/盲鼬鳚.md "wikilink")(Barathronus diaphanus)
      - [太平洋盲鼬鳚](../Page/太平洋盲鼬鳚.md "wikilink")(Barathronus pacificus)
  - 白膠鳚屬(Leucochlamys)
      - [白膠鳚](../Page/白膠鳚.md "wikilink")(Leucochlamys cryptophthalmus)
  - 懸胶鳚屬(Meteoria)
      - [紅身懸胶鳚](../Page/紅身懸胶鳚.md "wikilink")(Meteoria erythrops)
  - 寬臀膠鳚屬(Nybelinella)
      - [寬臀膠鳚](../Page/寬臀膠鳚.md "wikilink")(Nybelinella erikssoni)
  - 副柄臂膠鳚屬(Parasciadonus)
      - [副柄臂膠鳚](../Page/副柄臂膠鳚.md "wikilink")(Parasciadonus
        brevibrachium)
      - [少輻副柄臂膠鳚](../Page/少輻副柄臂膠鳚.md "wikilink")(Parasciadonus
        pauciradiatus)
  - 柄臂膠鳚屬(Sciadonus)
      - [柄臂膠鳚](../Page/柄臂膠鳚.md "wikilink")(Sciadonus pedicellaris)

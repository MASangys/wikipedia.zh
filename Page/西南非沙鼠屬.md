**西南非沙鼠屬**（**''Gerbillurus**''）又稱**南非小沙鼠屬**，是[鼠科中的一個](../Page/鼠科.md "wikilink")[屬](../Page/屬.md "wikilink")，包含下列[物種](../Page/物種.md "wikilink")：

  - [Hairy-footed Gerbil](../Page/Hairy-footed_Gerbil.md "wikilink")
    (*Gerbillurus paeba*)
  - [Namib Brush-tailed
    Gerbil](../Page/Namib_Brush-tailed_Gerbil.md "wikilink")
    (*Gerbillurus setzeri*)
  - [Dune Hairy-footed
    Gerbil](../Page/Dune_Hairy-footed_Gerbil.md "wikilink")
    (*Gerbillurus tytonis*)
  - [Bushy-tailed Hairy-footed
    Gerbil](../Page/Bushy-tailed_Hairy-footed_Gerbil.md "wikilink")
    (*Gerbillurus vallinus*)

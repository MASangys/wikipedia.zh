[汉武帝](../Page/汉武帝.md "wikilink")[建元](../Page/建元.md "wikilink")[癸卯三年](../Page/癸卯.md "wikilink")（公元前138年）[张骞出使西域寻访](../Page/张骞.md "wikilink")[大月氏联合抗击](../Page/大月氏.md "wikilink")[匈奴](../Page/匈奴.md "wikilink")。虽然出使未获成功，但是却沟通了[中原与](../Page/中原.md "wikilink")[西域诸国的联系和交往](../Page/西域.md "wikilink")。

[Xiyu_City-States_of_Tarim_basin_(BC1C).jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Xiyu_City-States_of_Tarim_basin_\(BC1C\).jpg "fig:Xiyu_City-States_of_Tarim_basin_(BC1C).jpg")

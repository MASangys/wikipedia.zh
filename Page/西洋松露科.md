**西洋松露科**（[學名](../Page/學名.md "wikilink")：；），又名**块菌科**，是一種[子囊菌門](../Page/子囊菌門.md "wikilink")[mycorrhizal類的](../Page/mycorrhizal.md "wikilink")[真菌](../Page/真菌.md "wikilink")。美食中的極品[松露就是這一科的生物](../Page/松露.md "wikilink")。

It includes the genus *[Tuber](../Page/Tuber_\(fungus\).md "wikilink")*,
which includes the so-called "true"
[truffles](../Page/truffle_\(fungi\).md "wikilink"). It was
characterized by the Belgian botanist [Barthélemy Charles Joseph du
Mortier](../Page/Barthélemy_Charles_Joseph_du_Mortier.md "wikilink") in
1822. A [molecular](../Page/molecular_phylogenetics.md "wikilink") study
of [ribosomal DNA](../Page/ribosomal_DNA.md "wikilink") by mycologist
Kerry O'Donnell in 1997 found that a small
[clade](../Page/clade.md "wikilink") now redefined as
[Helvellaceae](../Page/Helvellaceae.md "wikilink") is most closely
related to the Tuberaceae\[1\]。

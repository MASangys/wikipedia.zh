**西福尔**（**Vestfold**）[挪威东南部一郡](../Page/挪威.md "wikilink")，毗邻[布斯克吕和](../Page/布斯克吕.md "wikilink")[泰勒马克](../Page/泰勒马克.md "wikilink")。面积2224平方公里,人口239,907。位于奥斯陆峡湾西岸。郡首府[滕斯贝格](../Page/滕斯贝格.md "wikilink")（Tønsberg）。

## 历史

挪威第一位國王[金发哈拉尔](../Page/哈拉尔一世.md "wikilink")(Harald
Fairhair)的家乡，听说来自这个地区。那时西福尔包括[Lier和](../Page/Lier,_Norway.md "wikilink")[Eiker](../Page/Eiker.md "wikilink")，是一个狭窄王国。西福尔的国王包括:

  - Erik Agnarsson
  - [Halfdan Hvitbeinn](../Page/Halfdan_Hvitbeinn.md "wikilink")
    (西福尔的一部分)
  - [Eystein Halfdansson](../Page/Eystein_Halfdansson.md "wikilink")
  - [Halfdan the Mild](../Page/Halfdan_the_Mild.md "wikilink")
  - [Gudrød the Hunter](../Page/Gudrød_the_Hunter.md "wikilink")
  - [Halfdan the
    Black](../Page/Halfdan_the_Black.md "wikilink")，与他的兄弟[Olaf
    Gudrødsson一起](../Page/Olaf_Gudrødsson.md "wikilink")
  - [Ragnvald the
    Mountain-High](../Page/Ragnvald_the_Mountain-High.md "wikilink")
  - [Harald Fairhair](../Page/Harald_Fairhair.md "wikilink")
  - [Bjørn Farmann](../Page/Bjørn_Farmann.md "wikilink")
  - [Olaf Haraldsson
    Geirstadalf](../Page/Olaf_Haraldsson_Geirstadalf.md "wikilink")，Bjørn的兄弟
  - [Harald Gudrødsson
    Grenske](../Page/Harald_Gudrødsson_Grenske.md "wikilink"),
    [976](../Page/976.md "wikilink")-[987](../Page/987.md "wikilink")

[Kaupang](../Page/Kaupang.md "wikilink"), a town from the [Viking
Age](../Page/Viking_Age.md "wikilink") is believed to be the first town
in Norway, although [Tønsberg](../Page/Tønsberg.md "wikilink") is the
oldest town in Norway still existing. By the [10th
century](../Page/10th_century.md "wikilink") the local kings had
established themselves as the first dynasty to begin the unification of
Norway.

在1918年之前西福尔郡叫*[Jarlsberg og Larvik
Amt](../Page/Jarlsberg_og_Larvik_Amt.md "wikilink")*。

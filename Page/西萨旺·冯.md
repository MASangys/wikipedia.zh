**西萨旺·冯**（ *Sisavang
Vong*，），[老挝国王](../Page/老挝.md "wikilink")，生于[琅勃拉邦](../Page/琅勃拉邦.md "wikilink")，父亲[扎卡林](../Page/扎卡林.md "wikilink")（Zakarine），[老挝王国第一位国王和](../Page/老挝王国.md "wikilink")[琅勃拉邦王国最后一位国王](../Page/琅勃拉邦王国.md "wikilink")。

他的妻子包括:

  - [加穆尼公主](../Page/坎温第一.md "wikilink") (1885-1915), daughter of Prince
    Ko and Princess Duangbadani
  - [坎潘](../Page/坎潘.md "wikilink") (1896-1983), his half sister by his
    father's wife Mom La; they had no children
  - [坎拉](../Page/坎拉.md "wikilink"), 平民
  - [坎播](../Page/坎播.md "wikilink"), 平民
  - [坎迪](../Page/坎迪.md "wikilink"), 平民
  - [坎敦公主](../Page/坎敦公主.md "wikilink") of Luang Prabang, his half sister
    (one son)
  - [加玛杜尼公主](../Page/加玛杜尼公主.md "wikilink") of the Vang Hnaxxs family
  - [甘标](../Page/甘标.md "wikilink"), 平民
  - [因德拉加玛公主](../Page/因德拉加玛公主.md "wikilink"), a daughter of Prince
    Jayasena, Prince Sri Dibudinha and his wife, Pong
  - [加穆尼公主](../Page/坎温第二公主.md "wikilink")
  - [坎标公主](../Page/坎标公主.md "wikilink"), the daughter of his half-brother
  - [占西](../Page/占西.md "wikilink")（Chansy） (1900-1984), 平民

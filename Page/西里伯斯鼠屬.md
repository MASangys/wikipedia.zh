**西里伯斯鼠屬**（）是[哺乳綱](../Page/哺乳綱.md "wikilink")[囓齒目](../Page/囓齒目.md "wikilink")[鼠科的一屬](../Page/鼠科.md "wikilink")\[1\]\[2\]，而與西里伯斯鼠屬同科的動物尚有[扁顱鼠屬](../Page/扁顱鼠屬.md "wikilink")（[刺扁顱鼠](../Page/刺扁顱鼠.md "wikilink")）、[塔特鼠屬](../Page/塔特鼠屬.md "wikilink")（[塔特鼠](../Page/塔特鼠.md "wikilink")）、[大巽他鼠屬](../Page/大巽他鼠屬.md "wikilink")（[巴氏大巽他鼠](../Page/巴氏大巽他鼠.md "wikilink")）、[矢鼠屬](../Page/矢鼠屬.md "wikilink")（[長尾矢鼠](../Page/長尾矢鼠.md "wikilink")）等之數種[哺乳動物](../Page/哺乳動物.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

本屬包括下列各物種：

  - *[Taeromys arcuatus](../Page/Taeromys_arcuatus.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Taeromys
    callitrichus](../Page/Taeromys_callitrichus.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Taeromys celebensis](../Page/Taeromys_celebensis.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Taeromys hamatus](../Page/Taeromys_hamatus.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Taeromys
    microbullatus](../Page/Taeromys_microbullatus.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Taeromys punicans](../Page/Taeromys_punicans.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Taeromys taerae](../Page/Taeromys_taerae.md "wikilink")*

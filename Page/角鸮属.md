**角鸮属**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：**）是[鸱鸮科下一个种类众多的属](../Page/鸱鸮科.md "wikilink")，大约包括65种角鸮。主要有：

  - [普通角鸮](../Page/普通角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus scops*
  - [印度领角鸮](../Page/领角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus bakkamoena*
  - [烟色角鸮](../Page/烟色角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus capnodes*
  - [白额角鸮](../Page/白额角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus sagittatus*
  - [红角鸮](../Page/红角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus rufescens*
  - [黄嘴角鸮](../Page/黄嘴角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus spilocephalus*
  - [纵纹角鸮](../Page/纵纹角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus brucei*
  - [兰屿角鸮](../Page/兰屿角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus elegans*
  - [西部角鸮](../Page/西部角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus kennicottii*
  - [科摩罗角鸮](../Page/科摩罗角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus pauliani*
  - [东部角鸮](../Page/东部角鸮.md "wikilink") *Otus asio*
  - [斯歐角鴞](../Page/斯歐角鴞.md "wikilink") *Otus siaoensis*
  - [莫島角鴞](../Page/莫島角鴞.md "wikilink") *Otus moheliensis*

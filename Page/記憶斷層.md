<span>**《記憶斷層》**</span> 是 2003 年的一部心理驚悚片，由 Roland Suso
Richter執導，編劇為 Michael Cooney 根據自己的戲劇《*Point of
Death》*所寫。這部片和Alan Dean Foster的科幻小說*《The I Inside》*無關。

## 劇情

賽門‧凱博在醫院中醒來，腦中困惑、也不知道自己為何身在此處。在經過醫生的諮詢之後，他發現自己患有[](../Page/失憶症.md "wikilink")[失憶症並不記得過去兩年間發生的所有事情](../Page/失憶症.md "wikilink")。於是，他開始調查自己到底發生了什麼事，並逐漸將自己神秘的過去拼湊起來。

## 重要評價

  - <span>《記憶斷層</span>》可以和<span>[記憶拼圖](../Page/凶心人.md "wikilink")</span>\[1\]* 、*<span>[蝴蝶效應](../Page/連鎖蝶變.md "wikilink")</span>,\[2\]''
    <span>eXistenZ</span>,*\[3\] 和 *Jacob's Ladder''\[4\]
    等電影相比較。這些電影都以類似的元素為主題，並試著模糊化現實和奇幻之間的分界。

<!-- end list -->

  - The film has generally received luke-warm reviews from online
    critics. It holds a 33% rating on Rotten Tomatoes. Review sites have
    commended the film for a rather involving first two-thirds, with the
    mystery slowly being revealed layer by layer, drawing in the viewer.
    However, some critics found the ending a let down, calling it a
    "gotcha" gimmick\[5\] that causes "the past 70 or so minutes (to go)
    out the window".\[6\]

<!-- end list -->

  - Christopher Null of *Filmcritic.com* 提出了一個特別但是有趣的觀點： "The movie is
    based on a play with a much different title, one that actually gives
    away the surprise ending."\[7\]

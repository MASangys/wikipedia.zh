The 7110 was the first Nokia phone with a
[WAP](../Page/Wireless_Application_Protocol.md "wikilink") browser. WAP
was significantly hyped up during the 1998–2000 Internet boom. However
WAP did not meet these expectations and uptake was limited. Another
industry first was the flap, which slid from beneath the phone with a
push from the release button. Unfortunately the cover was not too
durable. The 7110 was also the only phone to feature a navi-roller key.

The 7250i was a slightly improved version of the Nokia 7250. It includes
[XHTML](../Page/XHTML.md "wikilink") and OMA Forward lock [digital
rights management](../Page/digital_rights_management.md "wikilink"). The
phone has exactly the same design as the 7250. This phone is far more
popular than the 7250 and has been made available on pre-paid packages
and therefore it is very popular amongst youths in the UK and other
European countries.

The 7510 Supernova was a phone exclusive to [T-Mobile
USA](../Page/T-Mobile_USA.md "wikilink"). Only some units of this model
have Wi-Fi chips with
[UMA](../Page/Unlicensed_Mobile_Access.md "wikilink"). The Wi-Fi adapter
on this phone supports up to
[WPA2](../Page/Wi-Fi_Protected_Access.md "wikilink") encryption if
present. This phone uses Xpress-On Covers.

The 7650 was the first [Series 60](../Page/Series_60.md "wikilink")
[smartphone](../Page/smartphone.md "wikilink") of Nokia. It was quite
basic compared to smartphones, it didn't have
[MMC](../Page/MultiMediaCard.md "wikilink") slot, but it had a camera.

The 7610 was Nokia's first smartphone featuring a
[megapixel](../Page/megapixel.md "wikilink") camera (1,152x864 pixels),
and is targeted towards the fashion conscious individual.
[End-users](../Page/End-user.md "wikilink") can also use the 7610 with
[Nokia Lifeblog](../Page/Nokia_Lifeblog.md "wikilink"). Other
pre-installed applications include the
[Opera](../Page/Opera_\(web_browser\).md "wikilink") and Kodak Photo
Sharing. It is notable for its looks, having opposite corners rounded
off. It comes with a 64 MB [Reduced Size
MMC](../Page/Multi_Media_Card.md "wikilink"). The main
[CPU](../Page/Central_processing_unit.md "wikilink") is an
[ARM](../Page/ARM_architecture.md "wikilink") compatible chip (ARM4T
architecture) running at 123 MHz.

##### 诺基亚8xxx – 高端系列（1996–2007）

This series is characterized by ergonomics and attractiveness. The
internals of the phone are similar to those in different series and so
on that level offer nothing particularly different, however the physical
handset itself offers a level of functionality which appeals to users
who focus on ergonomics. The front slide keypad covers offered a
pseudo-flip that at the time Nokia were unwilling to make. Materials
used increased the cost and hence exclusivity of these handsets.

The only exception to the rule (there are many in different series) is
the 82xx, 8310 which were very small and light handsets.

**謝拉度·加西亞·利安**(**Gerardo García
León**)簡稱**謝拉度**於()，岀生在[西班牙](../Page/西班牙.md "wikilink")[安達魯西亞的](../Page/安達魯西亞.md "wikilink")[塞维利亚的西班牙足球員](../Page/塞维利亚.md "wikilink")，現效力西班牙足球甲级联赛的皇家蘇斯達，司職後衞。

## 隊伍

  - 1992-1995: [Real Madrid
    Castilla](../Page/Real_Madrid_Castilla.md "wikilink")
  - 1995-1996: [CD Leganés](../Page/CD_Leganés.md "wikilink")
  - 1996-1997: [UE Lleida](../Page/UE_Lleida.md "wikilink")
  - 1997-1998: [CD Badajoz](../Page/CD_Badajoz.md "wikilink")
  - 1998-2000: [Villarreal CF](../Page/Villarreal_CF.md "wikilink")
  - 2000-2001: [CA Osasuna](../Page/CA_Osasuna.md "wikilink")
  - 2001-2006: [Málaga CF](../Page/Málaga_CF.md "wikilink")
  - 2006-2009: [皇家蘇斯達](../Page/皇家蘇斯達.md "wikilink")
  - 2009-2011: [Córdoba CF](../Page/Córdoba_CF.md "wikilink")
  - 2011-2013: [SD Logroñés](../Page/SD_Logroñés.md "wikilink")

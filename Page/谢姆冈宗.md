[BhutanZhemgang.png](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:BhutanZhemgang.png "fig:BhutanZhemgang.png") **謝姆岡宗**（གཞལམ་སྒང་རྫོང་ཁག་），前稱**Shemgang**，現稱Zhemgang，是[不丹](../Page/不丹.md "wikilink")二十個宗(dzongkhag)之一，面積達2,579平方公里，人口約27,506人，首府[謝姆岡](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/謝姆岡 "wikilink")。

**謝姆岡宗**轄下有八個[格窩](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/格窩 "wikilink")(Gewog):

  - [Bardho Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050820021109/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/zh_Bardho.pdf)
  - [Bjoka Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050816115904/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/zh_Bjoka.pdf)
  - [Goshing Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050817032118/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/zh_Goshing.pdf)
  - [Nangkor Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20041022220406/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/zh_Nangkor.pdf)
  - [Ngangla Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20041022224621/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/zh_Ngangla.pdf)
  - [Pangkhar Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050816124716/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/zh_Pangkhar.pdf)
  - [Shingkhar Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050816074936/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/zh_Shingkhar.pdf)
  - [Trong Gewog](https://web.archive.org/web/20050824050735/http://www.dop.gov.bt/fyp/09/zh_Trong.pdf)

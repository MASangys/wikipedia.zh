**貝克·里根·梅菲爾德**()）是[美國職業美式足球運動員](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/美國 "wikilink")，現效力於[國家美式足球聯盟的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/國家美式足球聯盟 "wikilink")[克里夫蘭布朗](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/克里夫蘭布朗 "wikilink")，場上位置為[四分衛](../Page/四分衛.md "wikilink")。他曾為[奧克拉荷馬大學打過大學足球](../Page/奧克拉荷馬大學.md "wikilink")，並參加了[2018年NFL選秀](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2018年NFL選秀 "wikilink")，以首輪第一順位被選中。

## 高中生涯

梅菲爾德是[特拉維斯湖高中騎士](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/特拉維斯湖高中 "wikilink")[美式足球隊的先發四分衛](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/高中美式足球 "wikilink")。他帶領特拉維斯湖高中在兩個賽季中拿到了25勝2敗的戰績，且贏得了2011年4A州冠軍。他在整個高中生涯中，總共傳出6,255碼，67個達陣與8次被攔截。\[1\]

## 大學生涯

### 德克薩斯理工大學

在[2013賽季開季前不久](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2013年德克薩斯理工大學紅突擊者美式足球隊 "wikilink")，梅菲爾德接替受傷的[邁克爾·布魯爾成為先發四分衛](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/邁克爾·布魯爾 "wikilink")。\[2\]梅菲爾德被認為是第一個在開幕戰實際擔任四分衛位置的一年級生。\[3\]

在首次對陣[南方衛理會大學時](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/南方衛理會大學 "wikilink")，梅菲爾德傳出了413碼與四次達陣，並以60傳43中的成績打破由[比利·喬·托利亞保持的學校紀錄](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/比利·喬·托利亞 "wikilink")，且是[NCAA第一級別FBS僅有完成此紀錄的四名大一新生之一](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/NCAA第一級別美式足球碗賽分區 "wikilink")。\[4\]\[5\]
For his performance, Mayfield was named [Big 12
Conference](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_12_Conference "wikilink")
Offensive Player of the Week – the first freshman Texas Tech quarterback
to be named so since current Red Raider head coach [Kliff
Kingsbury](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kliff_Kingsbury "wikilink") in
[1999](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/1999_Texas_Tech_Red_Raiders_football_team "wikilink").\[6\]
The game featured the last four former Lake Travis quarterbacks combined
on both teams: [Garrett
Gilbert](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Garrett_Gilbert "wikilink"),
[Michael
Brewer](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Brewer "wikilink"), Collin
Lagasse, and Mayfield.\[7\]

Following the Red Raiders' second victory over [Stephen F.
Austin](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2013_Stephen_F._Austin_Lumberjacks_football_team "wikilink"),
Mayfield's 780 season yards and seven touchdowns already exceeded the
755 yards and six touchdowns accrued by Texas Tech's last true freshman
quarterback, Aaron Keesee, in 10 games.\[8\] After being affected by
injury and losing the starting job to fellow true freshman [Davis
Webb](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Davis_Webb "wikilink"), Mayfield
finished the season with 2,315 yards on 218-of-340 completions with 12
touchdowns and 9 interceptions.

Mayfield was named one of 10 semifinalists for the [Burlsworth
Trophy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burlsworth_Trophy "wikilink") in
November; the award is given to the best player in Division I football
who began his college career as a walk-on.\[9\]

Mayfield earned Big 12 Conference Freshman Offensive Player of the Year
for the 2013 season. Mayfield announced that he would be leaving the
program due to a 'miscommunication' with the coaching staff.\[10\]\[11\]

### 奧克拉荷馬大學

梅菲爾德在2014年1月轉學至[奧克拉荷馬大學](../Page/奧克拉荷馬大學.md "wikilink")，but had not
contacted the Sooner coaching staff. Mayfield further elaborated in an
interview with [ESPN](../Page/ESPN.md "wikilink") that he sought to
transfer due to scholarship issues and a perception that he had earned
the starting position and that further competition was not "really
fair".\[12\]\[13\] The alleged scholarship issues were denied by Texas
Tech coach Kliff Kingsbury.\[14\]

In February 2014, Oklahoma head coach [Bob
Stoops](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bob_Stoops "wikilink") confirmed
that Mayfield would be walking on for the [Oklahoma
Sooners](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oklahoma_Sooners_football "wikilink").
Mayfield was not eligible to play until the 2015 season, and lost a
season of eligibility due to Big 12 Conference transfer rules following
an unsuccessful appeal of his transfer restrictions.\[15\]

#### 2015年賽季

On August 24, 2015, Mayfield was named the starting quarterback for the
Sooners after winning an open quarterback competition against [Trevor
Knight](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trevor_Knight "wikilink"). On
September 6, 2015, Mayfield started against
[Akron](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Akron_Zips_football_team "wikilink").
Mayfield totaled 388 yards through the air with 3 touchdowns on 23
completions in the 41–3 win.\[16\] In the second game of the 2015
season, Mayfield started at
[Tennessee](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Tennessee_Volunteers_football_team "wikilink")
at [Neyland
Stadium](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neyland_Stadium "wikilink"). The
Sooners were ranked 19th at the time and the Volunteers were ranked
23rd. Mayfield started off very slow in the game, not even reaching
midfield until the 13-minute mark of the 4th quarter. Oklahoma came back
from a 17-point deficit to win the game in double overtime. Mayfield
threw for 187 yards and three touchdowns on 19 completions while
throwing two interceptions early in the game in the 31–24 2OT win.\[17\]
In the third game of the season, Mayfield got the start against
[Tulsa](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Tulsa_Golden_Hurricane_football_team "wikilink").
He had a career day throwing for 487 yards and four touchdowns,
including 316 yards at half. Mayfield also ran for 85 yards and two
touchdowns in the 52–38 win.\[18\]\[19\]

Mayfield finished the year with 3,700 passing yards, 36 touchdowns, and
seven interceptions. He finished fourth in the [Heisman
Trophy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heisman_Trophy "wikilink") voting
and helped lead Oklahoma to the [College Football
Playoff](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/College_Football_Playoff "wikilink")
semifinal ([2015 Orange
Bowl](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Orange_Bowl "wikilink")).
However, they lost to
[Clemson](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2015_Clemson_Tigers_football_team "wikilink")
by a score of 37–17.\[20\]

#### 2016年賽季

Mayfield started the 2016 season off with 323 passing yards and two
touchdowns in a 33–23 loss to \#15 Houston.\[21\] In the rivalry game
against Texas on October 8, he had 390 passing yards, three touchdowns,
and two interceptions in the 45–40 victory.\[22\] On October 22, in a
66–59 victory over Texas Tech, Mayfield had 545 passing yards and
seven touchdowns in the historical matchup against [Patrick
Mahomes](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patrick_Mahomes "wikilink").
Mahomes tallied 734 passing yards and five touchdowns to go along with
Mayfield's numbers in a game that broke various single-game passing
records.\[23\] Over the final five games of the regular season, Mayfield
totaled 1,321 passing yards, 15 passing touchdowns, and three
interceptions to go along with three rushing touchdowns. All five games
were victories for the Sooners.\[24\]

It was announced in December 2016 that Mayfield and his top receiving
target, [Dede
Westbrook](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dede_Westbrook "wikilink"),
would be finalists for the 2016 [Heisman
Trophy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Heisman_Trophy "wikilink"). It was
also announced that they would play in the [2017 Sugar
Bowl](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2017_Sugar_Bowl "wikilink").
Mayfield ended up finishing third in the Heisman voting.\[25\]

In the 2017 Sugar Bowl, Mayfield helped lead the Sooners to a 35–19
victory over
[Auburn](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2016_Auburn_Tigers_football_team "wikilink").
He finished the bowl game 19-for-28 with 296 passing yards and two
touchdowns earning him the MVP award.\[26\]

#### 2017年賽季

2017年9月9日，梅菲爾德在球隊獲勝後，將奧克拉荷馬大學的旗幟插在[俄亥俄體育場草地的O上面](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/俄亥俄體育場 "wikilink")，這樣的舉動讓球迷們十分不滿，而本人也在不久後道歉。\[27\]\[28\]

2017年11月4日，梅爾爾德在對上同州世仇[奧克拉荷馬州立大學時](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/奧克拉荷馬州立大學 "wikilink")，繳出了生涯新高的598碼傳球，並以36傳24中5次傳球達陣的成績，帶領球隊以62-52擊潰對手。梅菲爾德同時作為奧克拉荷馬大學的首發四分衛，在瘋人大戰(奧克拉荷馬大學與奧克拉荷馬州立大學的世仇對決)中，達成生涯3-0的橫掃成績。

[2017-0717-Big12MD-BakerMayfield.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:2017-0717-Big12MD-BakerMayfield.jpg "fig:2017-0717-Big12MD-BakerMayfield.jpg")
In November 2017, Mayfield was under fire again after an interaction
during the game against
[Kansas](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kansas_Jayhawks_football "wikilink").
Mayfield was seen grabbing his crotch and mouthing "Fuck you\!" at the
coach of the opposing team. He also told their fans to "Go cheer on
basketball." In response, Mayfield issued another public
apology.\[29\]\[30\] Days after the 41–3 victory over Kansas, Sooners
head coach Lincoln Riley announced that Mayfield would not start or be
the captain during the upcoming game against West Virginia due to his
actions against Kansas.

2017年12月2日，12大聯盟睽違六年重新舉辦聯盟冠軍賽。梅菲爾德帶領球隊在冠軍賽中，以41-17擊敗了德克薩斯基督教大學。賽後梅菲爾德被選為冠軍賽MVP。奧克拉荷馬大學也取得了三年內第二個季後賽的資格。但最後在[2018年玫瑰盃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/2018年玫瑰盃 "wikilink")(季後賽半決賽)時以54-48輸給了喬治亞大學。\[31\]

梅菲爾德以囊括大多數選票的優勢拿下海斯曼奖，他總共拿到了732張第一名選票，最終獲得2398分。換算下來，他總共拿下了86%的得票率，這在海斯曼奖歷史上可以排到第三高。此外，梅菲爾德是唯一以試訓球員身分拿下海斯曼奖的人。\[32\]\[33\]

### 「梅菲爾德條款」

當梅菲爾德打完他的大一球季之後，便從德克薩斯理工大學轉至奧克拉荷馬大學。梅菲爾德向NCAA上訴，希望能立即取得為奧克拉荷馬大學上場比賽的資格，因為他是藉由試訓管道進入德克薩斯理工大學，且沒有接受任何獎學金。梅菲爾德主張他並不適用跟領取獎學金的球員一樣的轉學制度。但上訴被駁回了，NCAA認為他並不符合立即上場的資格。[12大聯盟的转校规则](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/12大聯盟 "wikilink")，他只能到2015-16赛季才能出战，而且将会损失一个赛季的大学球员资格。\[34\]\[35\]

奧克拉荷馬大學官方要求德州理工大學同意梅菲爾德上場，但德州理工大學拒絕並反對了這項要求。\[36\]梅菲爾德被迫在2014年停賽，也損失了12大聯盟規則中要求的一年大學球員資格。\[37\]\[38\]

2016年6月1日，12大聯盟的代表們提出，讓像是梅菲爾德這樣藉由試訓且沒有領取獎學金的球員能夠擺脫轉學的限制，這項提案在第二天的投票中以7-3的比數通過，梅菲爾德也得以繼續參加2017年的比賽。而梅菲爾德的前學校德州理工大學也投下同意票。\[39\]\[40\]

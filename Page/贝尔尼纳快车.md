**贝尔尼纳快车**是连接[瑞士](../Page/瑞士.md "wikilink")[库尔与](../Page/库尔.md "wikilink")[意大利波斯基亚沃和蒂拉诺的特快列车](../Page/意大利.md "wikilink")，从北到南穿越瑞士阿尔卑斯山。其大部分线路通过[世界遗产](../Page/世界遗产.md "wikilink")[雷蒂亚铁路阿尔布拉](../Page/雷蒂亚铁路.md "wikilink")/贝尔尼纳路段风景。

贝尔尼纳快车由[雷蒂亚铁路公司运营](../Page/雷蒂亚铁路.md "wikilink")。这并不是一个高速火车，而是为一段长时间的旅程提供服务。贝尔尼纳快车由全景车厢组成，提供多语言的解说。该列车很受旅行者欢迎，在蒂拉诺可以转乘邮政巴士经过意大利的[科莫湖到达瑞士的](../Page/科莫湖.md "wikilink")[卢加诺](../Page/卢加诺.md "wikilink")。\[1\]

2008年，贝尔尼纳快车线路中的阿尔布拉/贝尔尼纳路段被列入[世界遗产](../Page/世界遗产.md "wikilink")。\[2\]该路段乘坐时间为4小时，经过196座桥梁，通过55个隧道，\[3\]最高处海拔为2253米。

阿尔布拉铁路线于1898-1904年建造，一直由雷塔恩铁路公司运营。贝尔尼纳铁路线于1908-1910年建造，贝尔尼纳铁路公司独立运营直到1940年，之后被雷蒂亚铁路公司接管。

### 阿尔布拉线

Main article: [阿尔布拉铁路](../Page/阿尔布拉铁路.md "wikilink")

The train leaves the city of [Chur](../Page/Chur.md "wikilink") (585 m)
in [Graubünden](../Page/Graubünden.md "wikilink") and follows the course
of the [Rhine](../Page/Rhine.md "wikilink") to
[Bonaduz](../Page/Bonaduz.md "wikilink") (655 m). From there it enters
the Domleschg Valley and follows the [Posterior
Rhine](../Page/Hinterrhein_\(river\).md "wikilink") from
[Rhäzüns](../Page/Rhäzüns.md "wikilink") (658 m) to
[Thusis](../Page/Thusis.md "wikilink") (697 m). The train continues
toward [Tiefencastel](../Page/Tiefencastel.md "wikilink") (851 m)
following the [Albula river](../Page/Albula_river.md "wikilink") and
then crosses the [Landwasser
Viaduct](../Page/Landwasser_Viaduct.md "wikilink") before arriving at
[Filisur](../Page/Filisur.md "wikilink") (1032 m). Shortly after Filisur
the train passes its first
[spiral](../Page/Spiral_\(railway\).md "wikilink") tunnel then continues
to [Bergün/Bravuogn](../Page/Bergün/Bravuogn.md "wikilink"). Between
Bergün/Bravuogn (1373 m) and [Preda](../Page/Preda.md "wikilink") (1789
m), at the end of the valley, the train has to achieve a difference in
height of about 400 meters with an horizontal distance of 5 km without
using [rack-and-pinion](../Page/Rack_railway.md "wikilink"), but with
many spirals. Then the train enters the [Albula
Tunnel](../Page/Albula_Tunnel.md "wikilink") at 1,815 m under the
[Albula Pass](../Page/Albula_Pass.md "wikilink"). It emerges in the Val
Bever, where it reaches
[Bever](../Page/Bever,_Switzerland.md "wikilink") (1,708 m) on the
[Engadin](../Page/Engadin.md "wikilink") plain. The train continues
toward [Samedan](../Page/Samedan.md "wikilink") (1,721 m) and arrives at
the [St. Moritz](../Page/St._Moritz.md "wikilink") station (1,775 m).

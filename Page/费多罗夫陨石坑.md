**费多罗夫陨石坑**(Fedorov)是[月球正面](../Page/月球正面.md "wikilink")位于[雨海](../Page/雨海.md "wikilink")西侧的一处小型地貌特征(国际天文联合会的术语为"陨石坑")，以[俄罗斯](../Page/俄罗斯.md "wikilink")[火箭](../Page/火箭.md "wikilink")[工程师](../Page/工程师.md "wikilink")"亚历山大·彼得罗维奇·费多罗夫"(1872年-1920年)之名命名，1979年被[国际天文联合会正式批准接受](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/国际天文联合会 "wikilink")<ref>In general IAU assigned official names to craters on the near side in 1935, to craters on the far side in 1970, and finally have some small craters (previously considered as satellite craters) given new names from 1973 onwards. see:

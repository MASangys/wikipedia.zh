**赤刀魚科**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）為[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[大眼鲷目的其中一個](../Page/大眼鲷目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**赤刀魚科**下分5個屬，如下：

  - [棘赤刀魚屬](../Page/棘赤刀魚屬.md "wikilink") *Acanthocepola*
      - [小棘赤刀魚](../Page/小棘赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Acanthocepola abbreviata*
      - [楔形棘赤刀魚](../Page/楔形棘赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Acanthocepola cuneata*
      - [印度棘赤刀魚](../Page/印度棘赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Acanthocepola indica*
      - [克氏棘赤刀魚](../Page/克氏棘赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Acanthocepola
        krusensternii*
      - [背點棘赤刀魚](../Page/背點棘赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Acanthocepola limbata*
  - [赤刀魚屬](../Page/赤刀魚屬.md "wikilink") *Cepola*
      - [澳洲赤刀魚](../Page/澳洲赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Cepola australis*
      - [哈氏赤刀魚](../Page/哈氏赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Cepola haastii*
      - [赤刀魚](../Page/赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Cepola macrophthalma*
      - [少輻赤刀魚](../Page/少輻赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Cepola pauciradiata*
      - [史氏赤刀魚](../Page/史氏赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Cepola
        schlegelii*：又稱頜斑赤刀魚。
  - [歐氏鰧屬](../Page/歐氏鰧屬.md "wikilink") *Owstonia*
      - [粒牙歐氏鰧](../Page/粒牙歐氏鰧.md "wikilink") *Owstonia grammodon*
      - [麥克歐氏鰧](../Page/麥克歐氏鰧.md "wikilink") *Owstonia maccullochi*
      - [梳歐氏鰧](../Page/梳歐氏鰧.md "wikilink") *Owstonia pectinifer*
      - [仰吻歐氏鰧](../Page/仰吻歐氏鰧.md "wikilink") *Owstonia simoterus*
      - [土佐歐氏鰧](../Page/土佐歐氏鰧.md "wikilink") *Owstonia tosaensis*
      - [歐氏鰧](../Page/歐氏鰧.md "wikilink") *Owstonia totomiensis*：又稱圖圖歐氏鰧。
      - [韋勃歐氏鰧](../Page/韋勃歐氏鰧.md "wikilink") *Owstonia weberi*
  - [擬赤刀魚屬](../Page/擬赤刀魚屬.md "wikilink") *Pseudocepola*
      - [擬赤刀魚](../Page/擬赤刀魚.md "wikilink") *Pseudocepola taeniosoma*
  - [楔花鮨鰧屬](../Page/楔花鮨鰧屬.md "wikilink") *Sphenanthias*
      - [楔花鮨鰧](../Page/楔花鮨鰧.md "wikilink") *Sphenanthias sibogae*

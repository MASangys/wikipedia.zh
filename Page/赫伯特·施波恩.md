**赫伯特·施波恩**（，），[德国](../Page/德国.md "wikilink")[数学家和](../Page/数学.md "wikilink")[物理学家](../Page/物理学.md "wikilink")，[慕尼黑工业大学应用概率论教授](../Page/慕尼黑工业大学.md "wikilink")。

## 部分出版物

  - *Kinetic equations from Hamiltonian dynamics.* In: *Reviews of
    Modern Physics.* Vol. 52, 1980, S. 569–615.
  - mit Joel L. Lebowitz: [*A Gallavotti-Cohen type fluctuation theorem
    for stochastic dynamics.*](http://arxiv.org/abs/cond-mat/9811220)
    [In: *Journal of Statistical Physics.* Vol. 95, 1999, S.
    333–365.](http://arxiv.org/abs/cond-mat/9811220).
  - mit Michael Prähofer: [*Scale invariance of the PNG droplet and the
    Airy process.*](http://arxiv.org/abs/math/0105240) In: *Journal of
    Statistical Physics.* Vol. 108, 2002, S. 1071–1106.\[1\]
  - mit Patrik L. Ferrari: [*Step fluctuations for a faceted
    crystal.*](http://arxiv.org/abs/cond-mat/0212456) In: *Journal of
    Statistical Physics.* Vol. 113, 2003, S. 1–46.
  - mit Gianluca Panati und Stefan Teufel: [*Effective dynamics for
    Bloch electrons. Peierls substitution and
    beyond.*](http://arxiv.org/abs/math-ph/0212041) In: *Communications
    in Mathematical Physics.* Vol. 242, 2003, S. 547–578.
  - *Dynamics of Charged Particles and Their Radiation Field.* Cambridge
    University Press, Cambridge 2004, ISBN 0-521-83697-2.
  - mit Volker Betz: *A central limit theorem for Gibbs measures
    relative to Brownian motion.* In: *Probability Theory and Related
    Fields.* Vol. 131, 2005, S. 459–478.

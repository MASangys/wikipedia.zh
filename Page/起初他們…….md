[Poem_by_Martin_Niemoeller_at_the_the_Holocaust_memorial_in_Boston_MA.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Poem_by_Martin_Niemoeller_at_the_the_Holocaust_memorial_in_Boston_MA.jpg "fig:Poem_by_Martin_Niemoeller_at_the_the_Holocaust_memorial_in_Boston_MA.jpg")[麻薩諸塞州](../Page/麻薩諸塞州.md "wikilink")[波士頓的](../Page/波士頓.md "wikilink")[新英格蘭猶太人大屠殺紀念碑石碑](../Page/新英格蘭猶太人大屠殺紀念碑.md "wikilink")\]\]
**起初他們……**（）是[德國著名](../Page/德國.md "wikilink")[神學家兼](../Page/神學家.md "wikilink")[信義宗](../Page/信義宗.md "wikilink")[牧師](../Page/牧師.md "wikilink")[馬丁·尼莫拉的一首詩](../Page/馬丁·尼莫拉.md "wikilink")（忏悔文），描述忽視與自己無關的團體所造成的結果。該詩後來常被引用，作為對不關心[政治的人之呼籲](../Page/政治.md "wikilink")。

這首詩被鐫刻在[美國](../Page/美國.md "wikilink")[麻薩諸塞州](../Page/麻薩諸塞州.md "wikilink")[波士頓的](../Page/波士頓.md "wikilink")[新英格蘭猶太人大屠殺紀念碑石碑上](../Page/新英格蘭猶太人大屠殺紀念碑.md "wikilink")。

<td><p>Als die Nazis die Kommunisten holten,<br />

ich war ja kein Kommunist.<br />

Als sie die Sozialdemokraten einsperrten,<br />

<td><p>起初，納粹抓了共產黨人的時候，<br />
我沉默了；<br />
因為我不是共產黨人。<br />
<br />
當他們關了猶太人的時候，<br />
我沉默了；<br />
因為我不是猶太人。<br />
<br />
當他們抓工會成員的時候，<br />
我沒有抗議；<br />
因為我不是工會成員。<br />
<br />
當他們抓天主教徒的時候，<br />
我沉默了；<br />
因為我不是天主教徒。<br />
<br />
最後當他們奔向我而來，<br />
再也沒有人站起來為我說話了。</p></td>
<td><p>THEY CAME FIRST for the Communists,<br />
and I didn't speak up<br />
because I wasn't a Communist.<br />

THEN THEY CAME for the Jews,<br />
and I didn't speak up<br />
because I wasn't a Jew.<br />
<br />
THEN THEY CAME for the trade unionists,<br />
and I didn't speak up<br />
because I wasn't a trade unionist.<br />
<br />
THEN THEY CAME for the Catholics,<br />
and I didn't speak up<br />

<br />
THEN THEY CAME for me,<br />
and by that time no one was left to speak up.</p></td>

Because I was not a trade unionist.<br />
<br />
Then they came for the Jews,<br />
and I did not speak out—<br />
Because I was not a Jew.<br />
<br />
Then they came for me—<br />

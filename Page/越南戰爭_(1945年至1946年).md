**越南戰爭**，英國稱為**Operation Masterdom**（支配行動）\[1\]，越南稱為**Nam Bộ kháng
chiến**（南方抗戰）\[2\]，是在[第二次世界大戰](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/第二次世界大戰 "wikilink")[日本投降](../Page/日本投降.md "wikilink")後在[越南發生的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/越南 "wikilink")[戰爭](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/戰爭 "wikilink")，由[英属印度](../Page/英属印度.md "wikilink")及[法國的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/法國 "wikilink")[特遣隊](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/特遣隊 "wikilink")、以及[日本](../Page/日本.md "wikilink")[南方軍的部隊對抗](../Page/南方軍_\(日本陸軍\).md "wikilink")[越南](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/越南 "wikilink")[共产主义](../Page/共产主义.md "wikilink")的[越南獨立同盟會](../Page/越南獨立同盟會.md "wikilink")，其目的是要爭奪越南的控制權。

## 傷亡人數

根據官方統計，英國有介入的第一次越南戰爭之傷亡人數為：40名大英及印第安士兵，法國及日本傷亡人數略多於此，[越盟死傷人數估計為](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/越盟 "wikilink")2700，其中約有600人死於英國士兵之手，其餘則是被法國及日本士兵殺死。非官方的統計數字或許會較多，但考慮到越盟的統計方法，正確的數字應該永遠無法得知了。

## 重要性

Four more bloody decades of fighting lay ahead which would end in defeat
for two major world players. From March to July, 1946, armed and backed
up by the French, the Viet Minh systematically set about executing
leaders and members of nationalist Vietnamese groups, as Ho's lieutenant
Lê Duẩn said, "(to) wipe out the reactionaries." Known as the "Great
Purge", the goal was to eliminate everyone thought dangerous to the
Communist Party of Vietnam, and tens of thousands of nationalists,
Catholics and others were massacred from 1946–1948.\[13\]

Between May and December, Ho Chi Minh spent four months in France
attempting to negotiate full independence and unity for Vietnam, but
failed to obtain any guarantee from the French. After a series of
violent clashes with Viet Minh, French forces bombarded Hai Phong harbor
and occupied Hanoi, forcing Viet Minh forces to retreat into the jungle.
On December 19, 1946, 30,000 Viet Minh under Giap initiated the National
Resistance War with an attack on French troops at Hanoi.\[13\] The War
in Vietnam of 1946 – 1954, had begun.

WING所屬](../Page/EARLY_WING.md "wikilink")。以前隸屬於[Kaleidoscope](../Page/Kaleidoscope.md "wikilink")。[石川縣出身](../Page/石川縣.md "wikilink")。[血型是A型](../Page/血型.md "wikilink")。

**软舌螺动物**（[学名](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/学名 "wikilink")：）是生活在[古生代](../Page/古生代.md "wikilink")的一类神秘[動物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/動物 "wikilink")，具有小圓錐形的[螺殼](../Page/螺殼.md "wikilink")。這些物種目前都已全部[滅絕](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/滅絕 "wikilink")；其[化石](../Page/化石.md "wikilink")一般只能保存锥壳、[口盖和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/#口蓋 "wikilink")[附肢三个部分](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/附肢 "wikilink")，外壳为[钙质成分](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/钙质 "wikilink")，两侧对称。根據形態學的分類，本物種是一类海生有壳的[无脊椎动物](../Page/无脊椎动物.md "wikilink")，但再具體一點的分类众说纷纭：

  - 一种意见认为它是[软体动物门之下的一个纲](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/软体动物门 "wikilink")，
  - 另一种意见则认为它与软件动无本质上的联系，应属一个独立的[门](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/門_\(生物\) "wikilink")。

近期以軟組織化石分析發現軟舌螺動物具有[觸手冠](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lophophore "wikilink") (Lophophore) 這種進食器官，指出軟舌螺動物其實應該獨立為一門，而且和包含[腕足動物門在內的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/腕足動物門 "wikilink")[觸手冠動物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/觸手冠動物 "wikilink") (Lophophorata) 較相近\[1\]。[Hyoliths02.JPG](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Hyoliths02.JPG "fig:Hyoliths02.JPG")的软舌螺动物门内部铸件，[爱沙尼亚](../Page/爱沙尼亚.md "wikilink")北部\]\]

## 形態學

软舌螺动物的[碳酸鈣](../Page/碳酸鈣.md "wikilink")質外殼很大可能是由[霰石](../Page/霰石.md "wikilink")組成\[2\]。 有保護，還有兩條又名[海倫體的彎彎](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/#附肢 "wikilink")[附肢](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/附肢 "wikilink")（）去支撐身體，以的形式成長\[3\]。 體長一般在一公分到四公分，橫切面呈三角形或橢圓形。部份物種有環紋或條紋。

### 外殼

原状壳具有带横向束微结构的内层和包含纵向束的外层。\[4\]

### 附肢

本物種的附肢叫作「海倫體」， Helens are long structures that taper as they logarithmically coil gently in a ventral direction.\[5\]

The helens were calcareous, with an organic component, and had an organic-rich central core surrounded by concentric laminae of calcite. They grew by the addition of new material at their base, on the cavity side, leaving growth lines.\[6\] They were originally described by Walcott as separate fossils under the genus name *Helenia*, (Walcott's wife was named Helena and his daughter Helen); [Bruce Runnegar](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bruce_Runnegar "wikilink") adopted the name helen when they were recognized as part of the hyolith organism.\[7\]

### 口蓋

緊緊的把殼關閉。 The operculum closes perfectly over the aperture of the shell, leaving two gaps through which the helens can protrude.\[8\]It comprises two parts: the cardinal shield, a flat region at the top of the shell; and the conical shield, the bottom part, which is more conical.\[9\]The inside of the shell bears a number of protrusions, notably the dorsal cardinal processes and the radially-arranged clavicles.\[10\]

## 分类

[Hyoliths02.JPG](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Hyoliths02.JPG "fig:Hyoliths02.JPG") of northern [Estonia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Estonia "wikilink"); these are internal molds.\]\] 人们依据有无口唇将软舌螺动物门动物分成两个目：

  - †[有唇软舌螺目](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/有唇软舌螺目 "wikilink") Hyolithida
  - †[直管螺目](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/直管螺目 "wikilink") Orthothecida

Hyolitha have dorso-ventrally differentiated opercula, with the ventral surface of the shell extending forwards to form a shelf termed the ligula.\[11\]

The Orthothecida are somewhat more problematic, and probably contain a number of non-hyoliths simply because they are so difficult to identify with confidence, especially if their operculum is absent.\[12\]They have a straight (planar) opening, sometimes with a notch on the bottom side, and sealed with an operculum that has no ligula, clavicles, furrow or rooflets.\[13\]

*[Hyptiotheca](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyptiotheca "wikilink")* is an unusual hyolithid, in that it lacks clavicles.\[14\]

Orthothecids fall into two groups: one, the orcothecida *sensu stricto*,\[15\]is kidney or heart shaped in cross-section due to a longitudinal groove on its ventral surface, and its opercula bear cardinal processes; the other has a rounded cross-section and often lacks cardinal processes, making them difficult to distinguish from other cornet-shaped calcareous organisms.\[16\]All were sessile and benthic; some may have been filter feeders.\[17\]

## Phylogenetic position

[Haplophrentis_Burgess_Shale.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Haplophrentis_Burgess_Shale.jpg "fig:Haplophrentis_Burgess_Shale.jpg") (Middle [Cambrian](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cambrian "wikilink")), Burgess Pass, British Columbia, Canada.\]\] Because hyoliths are extinct and do not obviously resemble any [extant](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extant_taxon "wikilink") group, it is unclear which living group they are most closely related to. They may be molluscs; authors who suggest that they deserve their own phylum do not comment on the position of this phylum in the tree of life.\[18\]Their grade of organization is considered to be of the 'mollusc-annelid-sipunculid' level,\[19\]consistent with a Lophotrochozoan affinity, and comparison is usually drawn with the molluscs or [sipunculids](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/sipunculid "wikilink").\[20\]\[21\]Older studies (predating the Lophotrochozoan concept) consider hyoliths to represent a stem lineage of the clade containing (Mollusca + Annelida + Arthropoda).\[22\]

## 生态

软舌螺可能是[底栖生物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/底栖生物 "wikilink")。 寒武纪软舌螺是全球分布，没有迹象显示有地区差异，有迹象显示软舌螺有长寿命浮游幼虫生活期；但在奥陶系不同的组合变得越来越普遍。\[23\]

## 出现

最早的软舌螺壳体化石大约出现在5亿4000万年前的[西伯利亚](../Page/西伯利亚.md "wikilink") Purella antiqua Zone 的[Nemakit-Daldynian阶](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nemakit-Daldynian "wikilink")，其类似物发现于[中国](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/中国 "wikilink")*Paragloborilus subglobosus–Purella squamulosa* Zone 的 [梅树村阶](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/梅树村阶 "wikilink")（[寒武纪](../Page/寒武纪.md "wikilink")第一个阶）。\[24\]。软舌螺动物门物種無論在其數量及其多樣性都在[寒武紀達至巔峰](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/寒武紀 "wikilink")，之後逐步遞減，直到滅絕。\[25\]\[26\]

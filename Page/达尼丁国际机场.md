## 大陸航空

[大陸航空](../Page/大陸航空.md "wikilink") is based at the airport, and operate
scenic, charter and ambulance flights. Mainland Aviation College, a
division of [Mainland Air](../Page/Mainland_Air.md "wikilink"), operate
a flight training school.

**迪特·京特尔·波伦**（，）是德国歌曲作者、歌手、音乐人、制作人、企业家、电视名人。波伦以著名的流行重唱组合[Modern
Talking](../Page/Modern_Talking.md "wikilink")（1983–1987年与1998–2003年），以及女歌手[C.C.Catch的歌曲作者与制作人闻名](../Page/C._C._Catch.md "wikilink")。是（“德国偶像”）节目各季的现场评委与制片人、歌曲创作人。

## 生平

Bohlen was born in
[Berne](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Berne,_Germany "wikilink"), [West
Germany](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/West_Germany "wikilink"), and
grew up in
[Oldenburg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oldenburg "wikilink"). His
father was a supplier to the construction business. In 1973, Dieter
Bohlen was member of the band
[Monza](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monza_\(German_band\) "wikilink").
In 1981 he was member of trio
[Sunday](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sunday_\(band\) "wikilink"), and
they had a hit "[Hale Hey
Louise](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hale_Hey_Louise "wikilink")",
which has been covered by [Ricky
King](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ricky_King "wikilink") and others.
From 1983 to 1987 and from 1998 to 2003, Bohlen was one half of the pop
duo [Modern Talking](../Page/Modern_Talking.md "wikilink") as producer,
songwriter and composer. From 1985 to 1988, he produced and wrote songs
for the disco singer [C. C. Catch](../Page/C._C._Catch.md "wikilink").

In 1988, Dieter Bohlen signed 15-year-old singer [Anna
Garcia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anna_Garcia "wikilink") whose
stage name was "Sheree".\[1\] In 1988 she recorded a song "Ronnie – Talk
To Russia\!" as Sheree,\[2\] written by German songwriter and former
member of Modern Talking, Dieter Bohlen.

After Modern Talking's first split in 1987, Bohlen formed [Blue
System](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blue_System "wikilink"). For most
projects Bohlen had session singers for the background vocals to produce
a high-pitched chorus. He frequently worked in the studio with the
producer [Luis
Rodríguez](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luis_Rodríguez_\(producer\) "wikilink").
[Rivalen Der
Rennbahn](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rivalen_Der_Rennbahn "wikilink")
is a soundtrack written by Bohlen. It was one of the best-selling albums
in 1989 in Germany. Bohlen produced the German boygroup
[Touché](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Touché_\(band\) "wikilink")
which was founded by one of the group's five members, [Karim
Maataoui](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karim_Maataoui "wikilink"). In
1986, Bohlen wrote and produced [Chris
Norman](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chris_Norman "wikilink")'s
"[Midnight
Lady](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Midnight_Lady "wikilink")" for an
episode of the German crime show
*[Tatort](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tatort "wikilink")*. This song
became a major hit in central Europe.

In the 1990s, Bohlen became producer for [Bonnie
Tyler](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bonnie_Tyler "wikilink") under
[Hansa Records](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hansa_Records "wikilink").
They released three albums;
*[Bitterblue](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bitterblue "wikilink")*,
*[Angel
Heart](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Angel_Heart_\(Bonnie_Tyler_album\) "wikilink")*
and *[Silhouette in
Red](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Silhouette_in_Red "wikilink")*. Tyler
had great success with her single
"[Bitterblue](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bitterblue_\(song\) "wikilink")",
which was written and produced by Bohlen.

He has been a judge on all the seasons of the talent show
*[德国寻找超级明星](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deutschland_sucht_den_Superstar "wikilink")*
and has worked with acts discovered in the show, yielding several major
hits. He is criticized for controversial and insulting comments ("You
sing like a garden gnome on ecstasy", "Your voice sounds like Kermit
getting his ass kicked"). Bohlen has also released five books. Two of
these are autobiographical and describe his career, romantic
involvements and experiences with singers. The first book was an
unprecedented success in Germany. Both books were also released as audio
books.

In 2006, Bohlen released an album called *[Dieter: Der
Film](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dieter:_Der_Film_\(soundtrack\) "wikilink")*
that was a soundtrack to the [movie of the same
name](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dieter:_Der_Film "wikilink").

In 2011, he founded the clothing label "Needs Clothing" along with Felix
Werner.

He has also been a judge the talent show [Das
Supertalent](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Das_Supertalent "wikilink"),
the German version of [Britain's Got
Talent](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Britain's_Got_Talent "wikilink").
He is the only judge to have been in every season of the show.

## 个人生活

Dieter is the son of Edith and Hans Bohlen. His maternal grandmother is
originally from
[Königsberg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Königsberg "wikilink").\[2\]
He has a sibling, a younger brother named Uwe. He was educated in
Oldenburg, Göttingen and Hamburg, culminating in a Business
Administration degree.

Bohlen was married to Erika Sauerland from 1983 to 1994 and has three
children from the marriage: Marc, Marvin and Marielin.
In 1996 he married model and singer [Verona
Feldbusch](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Verona_Feldbusch "wikilink")
but divorced her after thirty days, propelling Verona to celebrity
status (she went on to host television shows such as *Peep* and *Veronas
Welt*). Later, Bohlen lived with the 1979 born model [Stefanie
Küster](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stefanie_Küster "wikilink") until
2006. They have one son, Maurice-Cassian, born mid-2005.

He met his current girlfriend Carina Walz in
[Majorca](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Majorca "wikilink"), Spain,
where one of the casting events for *[Deutschland sucht den
Superstar](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deutschland_sucht_den_Superstar "wikilink")*
(the German version of *[American
Idol](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Idol "wikilink")* and *[Pop
Idol](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pop_Idol "wikilink")*) was held.
They have a daughter, Amelie (born 2011) and a son, Maximilian (born
2013).

Bohlen lives in the village of
[Tötensen](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tötensen "wikilink") near
Hamburg. He is left-handed, but was forced to learn how to write with
his right hand at grammar school. He plays tennis with his left hand
because he started tennis after his school years.

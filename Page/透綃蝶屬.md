**透綃蝶屬**（學名：*Hyalyris*）是[蛺蝶科](../Page/蛺蝶科.md "wikilink")[斑蝶亞科](../Page/斑蝶亞科.md "wikilink")[綃蝶族中的一個](../Page/綃蝶族.md "wikilink")[屬](../Page/屬_\(生物\).md "wikilink")。物種分佈於[南美洲](../Page/南美洲.md "wikilink")。

## 物種

  - [安提閂綃蝶](../Page/安提閂綃蝶.md "wikilink") *Hyalyris antea*
      - *H. a. frater* = 絲閂綃蝶 *H. statilla*
  - [白斑透綃蝶](../Page/白斑透綃蝶.md "wikilink") *Hyalyris coeno*
      - 黑緣透綃蝶 *H. c. avinoffi*
  - [紅裙透綃蝶](../Page/紅裙透綃蝶.md "wikilink") *Hyalyris excelsa*
  - *[Hyalyris fiammetta](../Page/Hyalyris_fiammetta.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Hyalyris juninensis](../Page/Hyalyris_juninensis.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Hyalyris latitimbata](../Page/Hyalyris_latitimbata.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Hyalyris leptalina](../Page/Hyalyris_leptalina.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Hyalyris mestra](../Page/Hyalyris_mestra.md "wikilink")*
  - [鈍閂綃蝶](../Page/鈍閂綃蝶.md "wikilink") *Hyalyris ocna*
      - 美特閂綃蝶 *H. o. metella*
  - [四色透綃蝶](../Page/四色透綃蝶.md "wikilink") *Hyalyris oulita*
      - *H. o. lurida* = 三色閂綃蝶 *H. tricolor*
  - *[Hyalyris praxilla](../Page/Hyalyris_praxilla.md "wikilink")*
  - *[Hyalyris schlingeri](../Page/Hyalyris_schlingeri.md "wikilink")*
  - *Hyalyris \[n. sp.\]*

**速掘鼠屬**（**''Tachyoryctes**''）又稱**東非鼴鼠屬**，是[鼴形鼠科](../Page/鼴形鼠科.md "wikilink")（Spalacidae）中的一個屬。含有以下物種：

  - [乌干达鼹鼠](../Page/乌干达鼹鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes ankoliae*)
  - [肯尼亚鼹鼠](../Page/肯尼亚鼹鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes annectens*)
  - [勇鼹鼠](../Page/勇鼹鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes audax*)
  - [奇异鼹鼠](../Page/奇异鼹鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes daemon*)
  - [Kenyan African Mole
    Rat](../Page/Kenyan_African_Mole_Rat.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes
    ibeanus*)
  - [大东非鼹鼠](../Page/大东非鼹鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes macrocephalus*)
  - [奈湖鼹鼠](../Page/奈湖鼹鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes naivashae*)
  - [小鼹鼠](../Page/小鼹鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes rex*)
  - [卢旺达鼹鼠](../Page/卢旺达鼹鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes ruandae*)
  - [拉德鼹鼠](../Page/拉德鼹鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes ruddi*)
  - [高山鼹鼠](../Page/高山鼹鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes spalacinus*)
  - [東非鼴鼠](../Page/東非鼴鼠.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes splendens*)
  - [Storey's African Mole
    Rat](../Page/Storey's_African_Mole_Rat.md "wikilink") (*Tachyoryctes
    storeyi*)

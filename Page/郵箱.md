**郵箱**，或稱**郵筒**，是常見於[街道上](../Page/街道.md "wikilink")，收集外寄[信件的](../Page/信件.md "wikilink")[郵政設施](../Page/郵政.md "wikilink")，寄信人若果不便去[郵政局](../Page/郵政局.md "wikilink")，可以把信件投入到就近的郵箱，[郵差會定時來郵筒收集信件運回郵局](../Page/郵差.md "wikilink")，再分類、[運輸及派送](../Page/運輸.md "wikilink")。
郵箱是郵政局的[固定資產](../Page/固定資產.md "wikilink")，它是便民設施。

为了向公众展示统一的形象，邮政管理部门通常选择特定的颜色，使公众从总体上识别其服务。从目前各国邮政邮箱的主流颜色来看，[红色是使用最为广泛的颜色](../Page/红色.md "wikilink")。[中华人民共和国的邮箱颜色统一为](../Page/中华人民共和国.md "wikilink")[绿色](../Page/绿色.md "wikilink")。[中華郵政則同時使用綠色](../Page/中華郵政.md "wikilink")（平信）及紅色（限時信件與國際郵件）。

郵箱有多種類型，包括：

  - [圓筒形的邮箱又称郵筒](../Page/圓柱體.md "wikilink")（Pillar
    box），多見於[英國或前](../Page/英國.md "wikilink")[英聯邦地區](../Page/英聯邦.md "wikilink")
  - 掛在[柱子上的](../Page/柱子.md "wikilink")（Lamp box）
  - 裝在[牆上的](../Page/牆.md "wikilink")（Wall box）

## 恐怖襲擊

1939年間，[愛爾蘭共和軍曾於](../Page/愛爾蘭共和軍.md "wikilink")[英格蘭的郵筒放下數個](../Page/英格蘭.md "wikilink")[炸彈](../Page/炸彈.md "wikilink")，作為他們的部份計劃。

## 国家地区

### 中国

中国邮箱颜色为绿色。

### 香港

香港邮箱颜色在 1997 年前为红色，1997 年后改为绿色。

### 澳门

澳门邮箱为红色。

### 美国

美国邮箱为蓝色。

1.  All Royal Mail / GPO post boxes were painted BS 538 Post Office Red
    between 1874 and 1969. With the introduction of the K8 [Telephone
    kiosk](../Page/Telephone_booth.md "wikilink") in 1969, a new "red"
    colour was adopted for GPO street furniture, designated
    [BS](../Page/British_Standards.md "wikilink") 539 Post Haste Red.
    After [British Telecom](../Page/British_Telecom.md "wikilink") and
    [Royal Mail](../Page/Royal_Mail.md "wikilink") were split by the
    British Government, BT continued to use BS539 exclusively, whilst
    Royal Mail use both BS538 and BS539 in a seemingly random way. Prior
    to 1859 there was no standard colour although there is a document in
    the [BPMA](../Page/BPMA.md "wikilink") archive indicating that
    optionally, the lettering and Royal cypher could be picked out in
    white or black. In 1859, a bronze green colour became standard until
    1874. It took ten years for every box to be repainted during this
    period).
2.  In 2012 UK post boxes mostly in the hometowns of [Team
    GB](../Page/Team_GB.md "wikilink") gold medal winners in the [2012
    Summer Olympics](../Page/2012_Summer_Olympics.md "wikilink") were
    painted gold.

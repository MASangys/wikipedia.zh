**酯酶**（英語：**Esterase**）是一種[水解酶](../Page/水解酶.md "wikilink")[催化劑](../Page/催化劑.md "wikilink")，可在[水分子的參與下](../Page/水分子.md "wikilink")，經由[水解作用](../Page/水解.md "wikilink")，將[酯類切割成](../Page/酯類.md "wikilink")[酸類與](../Page/酸.md "wikilink")[醇類](../Page/醇.md "wikilink")。此類酶參與多種[生物化學反應](../Page/生物化學反應.md "wikilink")，依其專屬[受質](../Page/受質.md "wikilink")、[蛋白質結構](../Page/蛋白質結構.md "wikilink")，以及功能而有不同。

## EC酶分類

  - *EC 3.1.1*：[羧酸酯水解酶類](../Page/羧酸酯水解酶.md "wikilink")（[Carboxylic ester
    hydrolases](../Page/Carboxylic_ester_hydrolases.md "wikilink")）
      - [乙酰酯酶](../Page/乙酰酯酶.md "wikilink")（[Acetylesterase](../Page/Acetylesterase.md "wikilink")，EC
        3.1.1.6），可分開有機化合物的[乙酰基](../Page/乙酰基.md "wikilink")[官能基](../Page/官能基.md "wikilink")。
          - [膽鹼酯酶](../Page/膽鹼酯酶.md "wikilink")（[Cholinesterase](../Page/Cholinesterase.md "wikilink")）
              - [乙酰膽鹼酯酶](../Page/乙酰膽鹼酯酶.md "wikilink")（[Acetylcholinesterase](../Page/Acetylcholinesterase.md "wikilink")）：能抑制[神经递质](../Page/神经递质.md "wikilink")[乙酰膽鹼酯酶](../Page/乙酰膽鹼酯酶.md "wikilink")（[acetylcholine](../Page/acetylcholine.md "wikilink")）的能力。
              - [Pseudocholinesterase](../Page/Butyrylcholinesterase.md "wikilink"),
                broad substrate specificity, found in the [blood
                plasma](../Page/blood_plasma.md "wikilink") and in the
                [liver](../Page/liver.md "wikilink")
      - [Pectinesterase](../Page/Pectinesterase.md "wikilink") (EC
        3.1.1.11), clarifies fruit juices
  - *EC 3.1.2*: [硫酯水解酶類](../Page/硫酯水解酶.md "wikilink")（[Thiolester
    hydrolases](../Page/Thiolester_hydrolases.md "wikilink")）
      - [硫酯酶類](../Page/硫酯酶.md "wikilink")（[Thioesterase](../Page/Thioesterase.md "wikilink")）
          - [Ubiquitin carboxy-terminal hydrolase
            L1](../Page/Ubiquitin_carboxy-terminal_hydrolase_L1.md "wikilink")
  - *EC 3.1.3*: [Phosphoric monoester
    hydrolases](../Page/Phosphoric_monoester_hydrolases.md "wikilink")
      - [碱性磷酸酶](../Page/碱性磷酸酶.md "wikilink")（[Phosphatase](../Page/Phosphatase.md "wikilink")，EC
        3.1.3.x）：將[磷酸單酯](../Page/磷酸單酯.md "wikilink")（[phosphoric acid
        monoesters](../Page/phosphoric_acid_monoesters.md "wikilink")）轉化為[磷酸離子和醇類物質](../Page/磷酸離子.md "wikilink")。
          - [碱性磷酸酶](../Page/碱性磷酸酶.md "wikilink")（[Alkaline
            phosphatase](../Page/Alkaline_phosphatase.md "wikilink")）：可在[核苷酸](../Page/核苷酸.md "wikilink")、蛋白质、[生物碱等分子上去除](../Page/生物碱.md "wikilink")[磷酸基](../Page/磷酸基.md "wikilink")，进行去磷酸化作用。
          - [Phosphodiesterase](../Page/Phosphodiesterase.md "wikilink")
            (PDE), inactivates the [second
            messenger](../Page/second_messenger.md "wikilink")
            [cAMP](../Page/环腺苷酸.md "wikilink")
              - [cGMP specific phosphodiesterase type
                5](../Page/cGMP_specific_phosphodiesterase_type_5.md "wikilink"),
                is inhibited by
                [Sildenafil](../Page/Sildenafil.md "wikilink") (Viagra)
      - [果糖磷酸二酯酶](../Page/果糖-1,6-磷酸二酯酶.md "wikilink") (3.1.3.11),
        converts fructose-1,6-bisphosphate to fructose-6-phosphate in
        [gluconeogenesis](../Page/gluconeogenesis.md "wikilink")
  - *EC 3.1.4*: [Phosphoric diester
    hydrolases](../Page/Phosphoric_diester_hydrolases.md "wikilink")
  - *EC 3.1.5*: [Triphosphoric monoester
    hydrolases](../Page/Triphosphoric_monoester_hydrolases.md "wikilink")
  - *EC 3.1.6*: [Sulfuric ester
    hydrolases](../Page/Sulfuric_ester_hydrolases.md "wikilink")
    ([sulfatases](../Page/sulfatases.md "wikilink"))
  - *EC 3.1.7*: [Diphosphoric monoester
    hydrolases](../Page/Diphosphoric_monoester_hydrolases.md "wikilink")
  - *EC 3.1.8*: [Phosphoric triester
    hydrolases](../Page/Phosphoric_triester_hydrolases.md "wikilink")
  - [Exonucleases](../Page/Exonuclease.md "wikilink")
    ([deoxyribonucleases](../Page/deoxyribonuclease.md "wikilink") and
    [ribonucleases](../Page/ribonuclease.md "wikilink"))
      - *EC 3.1.11*:
        [Exodeoxyribonucleases](../Page/Exodeoxyribonucleases.md "wikilink")
        producing
        [5'-phosphomonoesters](../Page/5'-phosphomonoesters.md "wikilink")
      - *EC 3.1.13*:
        產生[5′-磷酸二酯的](../Page/5′-磷酸二酯.md "wikilink")[核糖核酸外切酶](../Page/核糖核酸外切酶.md "wikilink")
      - *EC 3.1.14*: 產生[3′-磷酸二酯的核糖核酸外切酶](../Page/3′-磷酸二酯.md "wikilink")
      - *EC 3.1.15*: [Exonucleases](../Page/Exonucleases.md "wikilink")
        active with either ribo- or deoxy-
  - [核酸內切酶](../Page/內切酶.md "wikilink")（[脫氧核糖核酸及](../Page/脫氧核糖核酸.md "wikilink")[核糖核酸](../Page/核糖核酸.md "wikilink")）
      - [去氧核糖核酸內切酶](../Page/去氧核糖核酸內切酶.md "wikilink")（[Endodeoxyribonuclease](../Page/Endodeoxyribonuclease.md "wikilink")
      - [核糖核酸內切酶](../Page/核糖核酸內切酶.md "wikilink")（[Endoribonuclease](../Page/Endoribonuclease.md "wikilink")
      - 能同時擔任上述兩種角色的酶

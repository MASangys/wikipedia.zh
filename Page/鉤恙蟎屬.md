**鉤恙蟎屬**（學名：；(），又名**纤恙螨属**，是[蛛形綱](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/蛛形綱 "wikilink")[蜱蟎亞綱](../Page/蜱蟎亞綱.md "wikilink")[蟎形總目](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/蟎形總目 "wikilink")[恙蟎目](../Page/恙蟎目.md "wikilink")[恙蟎總科](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/恙蟎總科 "wikilink")[恙蟎科](../Page/恙蟎科.md "wikilink")之下的一個屬，可透過叮咬人類而令人類感染[恙蟲東方體](../Page/恙蟲東方體.md "wikilink")（*O. tsutsugamushi*）而患上[恙蟲病](../Page/恙蟲病.md "wikilink")（亦作[叢林斑疹傷寒](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/叢林斑疹傷寒 "wikilink")）\[1\]。鉤恙蟎屬的幼虫主要宿主是[啮齿类](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/啮齿类 "wikilink")，但有时也能寄生于人类或其他大型哺乳类动物中。

過往曾以為啮齿类動物是恙蟲東方體的宿主，而恙蟎只是感染的[载体](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/载体_\(生物学\) "wikilink")\[2\]\[3\]，但現時我們知道恙蟎一生只會進食一次，這使病原從啮齿类動物傳播至人類這種假設不可能，因為恙蟎要達成載體的話，需要先後嚙咬鼠隻取得病原、然後再嚙咬人類以將病原傳給人類\[4\]。所以病菌只可能一開始就已在恙蟎的體內，透過的過程從母體傳染給蟎的蟲卵\[5\]\[6\]\[7\]。所以鉤恙蟎屬物種同時是  Leptotrombidium mites are therefore both vector and reservoir for *O. tsutsugamushi*.\[8\] The infection predominantly affects female mites,\[9\] and does not appear to otherwise harm the mites.

## Life history

The larva is pale orange in colour and feeds on liquified skin tissue, not blood, as their mouth parts (chelicerae) are too short to reach the blood vessels.\[10\] They have 3 pairs of legs. The larvae most commonly target rodents, but will also attach to humans.\[11\] For humans, the bite is painless, but pain commonly develops only after the larva detaches from the skin, leaving a red papule that may then develop into an eschar.\[12\]

The larval stage lasts for 1 to 2 weeks. After feeding, the larvae drop to the ground and become nymphs. Nymph is brick-red in colour and has 4 pairs of legs. Nymphal stage lasts for 1 to 3 weeks. Nymphs mature into adults which have 4 pairs of legs, first pair being the largest. They are harmless to humans. In the post larval stage, they are not parasitic and feed on plant materials.\[13\] 雌蟲每次產卵只產一枚，約需一星期孵化。成蟲壽命約六個月。

## 物種

以下詳列本屬物種：

  - *[L. akamushi](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leptotrombidium_akamushi "wikilink")*：日本特有種，and is the reservoir for the Kato [serotype](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/serotype "wikilink") of *[Orientia tsutsugamushi](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Orientia_tsutsugamushi "wikilink")*;\[14\]
  - [纖恙蟎](../Page/纖恙蟎.md "wikilink")（*[L. deliense](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leptotrombidium_deliense "wikilink")*）：是[華南](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/華南 "wikilink")\[15\]及[泰國](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/泰國 "wikilink")\[16\]的主要病毒載體。it is also endemic to , in the [Northern Territory, Australia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Northern_Territory,_Australia "wikilink"), where is carries the Litchfield serotype.\[17\]
  - *[L. pallidum](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leptotrombidium_pallidum "wikilink")* is endemic to Japan and is the reservoir for Karp and Gilliam serotypes;\[18\]\[19\]
  - [小盾纤恙螨](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/小盾纤恙螨 "wikilink") *[L. scutellare](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leptotrombidium_scutellare "wikilink")*：原生於日本，是 endemic to Japan and is the reservoir for Kawasaki and Kuroki serotypes.\[20\]\[21\]，[肾综合征出血热](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/肾综合征出血热 "wikilink")（, HFRS）的載體。

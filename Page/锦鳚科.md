**锦鳚科**為[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鱸形目的其中一個](../Page/鱸形目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**锦鳚科**其下8個屬，如下：

  - 異錦鳚屬(*Allopholis*)
      - [異錦鳚](../Page/異錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Allopholis laetus*)
  - 無足鳚屬(*Apodichthys*)
      - [黃色無足鳚](../Page/黃色無足鳚.md "wikilink")(*Apodichthys flavidus*)
      - [不美無足鳚](../Page/不美無足鳚.md "wikilink")(*Apodichthys inornatus*)
      - [多血無足鳚](../Page/多血無足鳚.md "wikilink")(*Apodichthys sanguineus*)
      - [綠無足鳚](../Page/綠無足鳚.md "wikilink")(*Apodichthys virescens*)
  - 繸鳚屬(*Azuma*)
      - [繸鳚](../Page/繸鳚.md "wikilink")(*Azuma emmnion*)
  - 雲鳚屬(*Enedrias*)
      - [粗棘雲鳚](../Page/粗棘雲鳚.md "wikilink")(*Enedrias crassispina*)
      - [雲鳚](../Page/雲鳚.md "wikilink")(*Enedrias nebulosa*)
  - 擬錦鳚屬(*Pholis*)
      - [長鰭錦鳚](../Page/長鰭錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Pholis clemensi*)
      - [條紋錦鳚](../Page/條紋錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Pholis fasciatus*)
      - [吉氏錦鳚](../Page/吉氏錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Pholis gilli*)
      - [錦鳚](../Page/錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Pholis gunnellus*)
      - [樂錦鳚](../Page/樂錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Pholis laeta*)
      - [雲斑錦鳚](../Page/雲斑錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Pholis nebulosus*)
      - [鞍斑錦鳚](../Page/鞍斑錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Pholis ornatus*)
      - [眼帶錦鳚](../Page/眼帶錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Pholis picta*)
      - [紅錦鳚](../Page/紅錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Pholis schultzi*)
  - 銀帶錦鳚屬(*Rhodymenichthys*)
      - [銀帶錦鳚](../Page/銀帶錦鳚.md "wikilink")(*Rhodymenichthys
        dolichogaster*)
  - 棲苣鳚屬(*Ulvicola*)
      - [棲苣鳚](../Page/棲苣鳚.md "wikilink")(*Ulvicola sanctaerosae*)
  - 爬鳚屬(*Xererpes*)
      - [爬鳚](../Page/爬鳚.md "wikilink")(*Xererpes fucorum*)

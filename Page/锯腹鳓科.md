**锯腹鳓科**為[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鯡形目的其中一科](../Page/鯡形目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**锯腹鳓科**下分9個屬38种，部分如下：

### 鰳屬(*Ilisha*)

  - [西非鰳](../Page/西非鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha africana*)
  - [亞馬遜鰳](../Page/亞馬遜鰳.md "wikilink")(''Ilisha amazonica '')
  - [窄身鰳](../Page/窄身鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha compressa*)
  - [長鰳](../Page/長鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha
    elongata*)：又稱[鰳](../Page/鰳.md "wikilink")。
  - [絲鰳](../Page/絲鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha filigera*)
  - [富爾氏鰳](../Page/富爾氏鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha furthii*)
  - [凱氏鰳](../Page/凱氏鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha kampeni*)
  - [新月鰳](../Page/新月鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha lunula*)
  - [大鰭鰳](../Page/大鰭鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha macrogaster*)
  - [大眼鰳](../Page/大眼鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha megaloptera*)
  - [黑口鰳](../Page/黑口鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha melastoma*)
  - [緬甸鰳](../Page/緬甸鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha novacula*)
  - [孟買鰳](../Page/孟買鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha obfuscata*)
  - [鋸肚鰳](../Page/鋸肚鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha pristigastroides*)
  - [薛氏鰳](../Page/薛氏鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha sirishai*)
  - [縱帶鰳](../Page/縱帶鰳.md "wikilink")(*Ilisha striatula*)

### 後鰭魚屬(*Opisthopterus*)

  - [杜氏後鰭魚](../Page/杜氏後鰭魚.md "wikilink")(*Opisthopterus dovii*)
  - [高身後鰭魚](../Page/高身後鰭魚.md "wikilink")(*Opisthopterus effulgens*)
  - [厄瓜多爾後鰭魚](../Page/厄瓜多爾後鰭魚.md "wikilink")(*Opisthopterus
    equitorialis*)
  - [大眼後鰭魚](../Page/大眼後鰭魚.md "wikilink")(*Opisthopterus macrops*)
  - [後鰭魚](../Page/後鰭魚.md "wikilink")(*Opisthopterus
    tardoore*)：又稱[塔氏長鰭鯡](../Page/塔氏長鰭鯡.md "wikilink")。
  - [伐氏後鰭魚](../Page/伐氏後鰭魚.md "wikilink")(*Opisthopterus valenciennesi*)

### 多齒鰳屬(*Pellona*)

  - [秘魯多齒鰳](../Page/秘魯多齒鰳.md "wikilink")(*Pellona altamazonica*)
  - [近視多齒鰳](../Page/近視多齒鰳.md "wikilink")(*Pellona castelnaeana*)
  - [戴氏多齒鰳](../Page/戴氏多齒鰳.md "wikilink")(*Pellona dayi*)
  - [印度多齒鰳](../Page/印度多齒鰳.md "wikilink")(*Pellona ditchela*)：又稱庇隆鰳。
  - [黃鰭多齒鰳](../Page/黃鰭多齒鰳.md "wikilink")(*Pellona flavipinnis*)
  - [哈氏多齒鰳](../Page/哈氏多齒鰳.md "wikilink")(*Pellona harroweri*)

### 鋸腹鰳屬(*Pristigaster*)

  - [卡耶鋸腹鰳](../Page/卡耶鋸腹鰳.md "wikilink")(*Pristigaster cayana*)
  - [懷氏鋸腹鰳](../Page/懷氏鋸腹鰳.md "wikilink")(*Pristigaster whiteheadi*)

**长棘星螺**（学名：**），又名**長刺螺**，是一種深海[海螺的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/海螺 "wikilink")[物種](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/物種 "wikilink")，屬於[海洋](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/海洋生物 "wikilink")[腹足綱](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/腹足綱 "wikilink")[軟體動物的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/軟體動物 "wikilink")[蝾螺科](../Page/蝾螺科.md "wikilink")[刺螺屬的一個物種](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/刺螺屬 "wikilink")。

## 分佈與棲息地

這種熱帶的海洋生物物種棲息於西太平洋的日本和菲律賓對開海域，見於水深的海洋；
也見於[中国大陆](../Page/中国大陆.md "wikilink")、[台湾](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/台湾 "wikilink")。常栖息在潮下带。

## 型態描述

The size of the shell varies between . The color pattern of the shell
varies from very light brownish to purple-brown. Some specimens contain
only 6
[whorls](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Whorl_\(mollusc\) "wikilink")
instead of 7 as in the holotype. The upper whorls are smooth, then
follows the [body
whorl](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/body_whorl "wikilink") with
scarcely 2 rows of granules, instead of 7 or 8. Towards the keel it has
very irregular radiating ribs, which leave however a nearly smooth zone
above the keel, with only a few spiral striae.

The base of the shell is less convex than the upper part. Some specimens
lack the rose-coloured line round the umbilical callosity. The
[aperture](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aperture_\(mollusc\) "wikilink")
is oval, thick, with the nucleus in the external lower corner. The outer
surface is slightly rugose, by irregular wrinkles, almost parallel with
the basal margin of the
[operculum](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Operculum_\(gastropod\) "wikilink").
The nucleus is marked by an olive spot.\[1\]

## Biology

Embryos of *Guildfordia yoka* develop into free-swimming planktonic
larvae with several bands of cilia
([trocophore](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/trocophore "wikilink")).
Later they develop into juvenile
[veligers](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/veliger "wikilink"), finally
into fully grown adults.\[2\]

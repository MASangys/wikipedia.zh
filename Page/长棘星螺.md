**长棘星螺**（学名：**），又名**長刺螺**，是一種深海[海螺的](../Page/海螺.md "wikilink")[物種](../Page/物種.md "wikilink")，屬於[海洋](../Page/海洋生物.md "wikilink")[腹足綱](../Page/腹足綱.md "wikilink")[軟體動物的](../Page/軟體動物.md "wikilink")[蝾螺科](../Page/蝾螺科.md "wikilink")[刺螺屬的一個物種](../Page/刺螺屬.md "wikilink")。

## 分佈與棲息地

這種熱帶的海洋生物物種棲息於西太平洋的日本和菲律賓對開海域，見於水深的海洋；
也見於[中国大陆](../Page/中国大陆.md "wikilink")、[台湾](../Page/台湾.md "wikilink")。常栖息在潮下带。

## 型態描述

The size of the shell varies between . The color pattern of the shell
varies from very light brownish to purple-brown. Some specimens contain
only 6 [whorls](../Page/Whorl_\(mollusc\).md "wikilink") instead of 7 as
in the holotype. The upper whorls are smooth, then follows the [body
whorl](../Page/body_whorl.md "wikilink") with scarcely 2 rows of
granules, instead of 7 or 8. Towards the keel it has very irregular
radiating ribs, which leave however a nearly smooth zone above the keel,
with only a few spiral striae.

The base of the shell is less convex than the upper part. Some specimens
lack the rose-coloured line round the umbilical callosity. The
[aperture](../Page/Aperture_\(mollusc\).md "wikilink") is oval, thick,
with the nucleus in the external lower corner. The outer surface is
slightly rugose, by irregular wrinkles, almost parallel with the basal
margin of the
[operculum](../Page/Operculum_\(gastropod\).md "wikilink"). The nucleus
is marked by an olive spot.\[1\]

## Biology

Embryos of *Guildfordia yoka* develop into free-swimming planktonic
larvae with several bands of cilia
([trocophore](../Page/trocophore.md "wikilink")). Later they develop
into juvenile [veligers](../Page/veliger.md "wikilink"), finally into
fully grown adults.\[2\]

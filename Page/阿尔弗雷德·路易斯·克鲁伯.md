**阿尔弗雷德·路易斯·克鲁伯**（**Alfred Louis Kroeber**，），美国[人类学家](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/人类学家 "wikilink")，20世纪前半期美国人类学的领军人物之一。小说家娥苏拉·勒瑰恩的父亲。

## 部分著作

  - *Indian Myths of South Central California* (1907), in *University of California Publications in American Archaeology and Ethnology* 4:167-250. Berkeley (Six *Rumsien Costanoan* myths, pp. 199-202); online at [Sacred Texts](http://www.sacred-texts.com/nam/ca/scc).
  - *The Religion of the Indians of California* (1907), in *University of California Publications in American Archaeology and Ethnology* 4:6. Berkeley, sections titled "Shamanism", "Public Ceremonies", "Ceremonial Structures and Paraphernalia", and "Mythology and Beliefs"; available at [Sacred Texts](http://www.sacred-texts.com/nam/ca/ric)
  - *Handbook of the Indians of California* (1925). Washington, D.C: *Bureau of American Ethnology Bulletin* No. 78
  - *Anthropology: Culture Patterns & Processes* (1963). Harcourt: Brace & World, Inc.

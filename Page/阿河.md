**阿河**可以指：

  - 河流:

**阿河**（**Aa**）是[北歐不少小河的名稱](../Page/北歐.md "wikilink")。

  - [法国](../Page/法国.md "wikilink"):
      - [阿河 (法国)位于法国北方](../Page/阿河_\(法国\).md "wikilink")，在格拉沃利讷注入北海
  - [瑞士](../Page/瑞士.md "wikilink"):
      - Sarner Aa, a tributary of [Lake
        Lucerne](../Page/Lake_Lucerne.md "wikilink").
      - Engelberger Aa, also a tributary of [Lake
        Lucerne](../Page/Lake_Lucerne.md "wikilink").
      - Aabach, in the cantons of
        [Lucerne](../Page/Canton_of_Lucerne.md "wikilink") and
        [Aargau](../Page/Aargau.md "wikilink"), which carries the waters
        of Lakes [Baldegg](../Page/Lake_Baldegg.md "wikilink") and
        [Hallwil](../Page/Lake_Hallwil.md "wikilink") into the
        [Aar](../Page/Aar.md "wikilink") near
        [Lenzburg](../Page/Lenzburg.md "wikilink").
  - [德国](../Page/德国.md "wikilink"):
      - the Westphalian Aa (Westfälische Aa), rising in the [Teutoburg
        Forest](../Page/Teutoburg_Forest.md "wikilink"), and joining the
        [Werre](../Page/Werre.md "wikilink") at
        [Herford](../Page/Herford.md "wikilink").
      - the Münster Aa (Münstersche Aa), a tributary of the
        [Ems](../Page/Ems.md "wikilink") near
        [Münster](../Page/Münster.md "wikilink").
      - the Great Aa (Große Aa), a tributary of the
        [Ems](../Page/Ems.md "wikilink") near
        [Lingen](../Page/Lingen.md "wikilink").
      - and several others (Ahauser Aa, Alstätter Aa, Bocholter Aa,
        Grenzaa, Ibbenbürener Aa, Speller Aa, Steinfurter Aa).
  - [荷兰](../Page/荷兰.md "wikilink"):
      - Drentse Aa, a small river in the
        [Drenthe](../Page/Drenthe.md "wikilink") and
        [Groningen](../Page/Groningen_\(province\).md "wikilink")
        provinces that also flows through
        [Groningen](../Page/Groningen_\(city\).md "wikilink") city.
      - Aa, a river in
        [Noord-Brabant](../Page/Noord-Brabant.md "wikilink"), flowing
        through [Helmond](../Page/Helmond.md "wikilink") and
        ['s-Hertogenbosch](../Page/'s-Hertogenbosch.md "wikilink").
      - Aa or Weerijs, also in
        [Noord-Brabant](../Page/Noord-Brabant.md "wikilink"), a small
        river near [Breda](../Page/Breda_\(Netherlands\).md "wikilink").
      - several small rivers and canals in
        [Groningen](../Page/Groningen_\(province\).md "wikilink")
        province, such as Pekel Aa, Ruiten Aa, Mussel Aa.
  - [拉脱维亚](../Page/拉脱维亚.md "wikilink"):
      - [Lielupe](../Page/Lielupe.md "wikilink") (in
        [German](../Page/German_\(language\).md "wikilink") Kurländische
        Aa)
      - [Gauja](../Page/Gauja.md "wikilink") (in
        [German](../Page/German_\(language\).md "wikilink") Livländische
        Aa), both falling into the [Gulf of
        Riga](../Page/Gulf_of_Riga.md "wikilink"), near
        [Riga](../Page/Riga.md "wikilink")

**随机偏微分方程**（英文：Stochastic partial differential equations (SPDEs)）[类似于一般的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/类似 "wikilink")[随机微分方程](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/随机微分方程 "wikilink")。其本质上是带有[随机](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/随机 "wikilink")[项和随机](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/项 "wikilink")[系数的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/系数 "wikilink")[偏微分方程](../Page/偏微分方程.md "wikilink")。随机微分方程在[量子场论](../Page/量子场论.md "wikilink")、[统计力学](../Page/统计力学.md "wikilink")、[金融数学中有着广泛的应用](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/金融数学 "wikilink")。

## Examples

One of the most studied SPDEs is the stochastic [heat equation](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/heat_equation "wikilink"), which may formally be written as

\[\partial_t u = \Delta u + \xi\;,\]

where \(\Delta\) is the [Laplacian](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laplacian "wikilink") and \(\xi\) denotes space-time [white noise](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/white_noise "wikilink").

## Discussion

One difficulty is their lack of regularity. In one space dimension, solutions to the stochastic heat equation are only almost 1/2-[Hölder continuous](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hölder_continuous "wikilink") in space and 1/4-Hölder continuous in time. For dimensions two and higher, solutions are not even function-valued, but can be made sense of as random [distributions](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Distribution_\(mathematics\) "wikilink").

**隱棘杜父魚科**是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鮋形目的一個科](../Page/鮋形目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**隱棘杜父魚科**下分8個屬，如下：

### 蟾眼杜父魚屬(*Ambophthalmos*)

  - [窄頭蟾眼杜父魚](../Page/窄頭蟾眼杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Ambophthalmos angustus*)
  - [新西蘭蟾眼杜父魚](../Page/新西蘭蟾眼杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Ambophthalmos
    eurystigmatephoros*)
  - [大鬚蟾眼杜父魚](../Page/大鬚蟾眼杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Ambophthalmos
    magnicirrus*)

### 擬杜父魚屬(*Cottunculus*)

  - [大頭擬杜父魚](../Page/大頭擬杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Cottunculus granulosus*)
  - [康氏擬杜父魚](../Page/康氏擬杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Cottunculus konstantinovi*)
  - [小眼擬杜父魚](../Page/小眼擬杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Cottunculus microps*)
  - [裸身擬杜父魚](../Page/裸身擬杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Cottunculus nudus*)
  - [極地擬杜父魚](../Page/極地擬杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Cottunculus sadko*)
  - [棘擬杜父魚](../Page/棘擬杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Cottunculus spinosus*)
  - [湯氏擬杜父魚](../Page/湯氏擬杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Cottunculus thompsonii*)
  - [管棲擬杜父魚](../Page/管棲擬杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Cottunculus tubulosus*)

### 鬚杜父魚屬(*Dasycottus*)

  - [棘頭鬚杜父魚](../Page/棘頭鬚杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Dasycottus setiger*)

### 紅杜父魚屬(*Ebinania*)

  - [澳大利亞紅杜父魚](../Page/澳大利亞紅杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Ebinania australiae*)
  - [短頭紅杜父魚](../Page/短頭紅杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Ebinania brephocephala*)
  - [科氏紅杜父魚](../Page/科氏紅杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Ebinania costaecanariae*)
  - [螺旋紅杜父魚](../Page/螺旋紅杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Ebinania gyrinoides*)
  - [馬克薩斯紅杜父魚](../Page/馬克薩斯紅杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Ebinania macquariensis*)
  - [軟頭紅杜父魚](../Page/軟頭紅杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Ebinania malacocephala*)
  - [蟲紋紅杜父魚](../Page/蟲紋紅杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Ebinania vermiculata*)

### 寬杜父魚屬(*Eurymen*)

  - [巴氏寬杜父魚](../Page/巴氏寬杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Eurymen bassargini*)
  - [蝌蚪寬杜父魚](../Page/蝌蚪寬杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Eurymen gyrinus*)

### 吉氏軟杜父魚屬(*Gilbertidia*)

  - [多爾吉氏軟杜父魚](../Page/多爾吉氏軟杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Gilbertidia dolganovi*)
  - [丘疣吉氏軟杜父魚](../Page/丘疣吉氏軟杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Gilbertidia pustulosa*)

### 軟杜父魚屬(*Malacocottus*)

  - [阿留申軟杜父魚](../Page/阿留申軟杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Malacocottus aleuticus*)
  - [駝背軟杜父魚](../Page/駝背軟杜父魚.md "wikilink")(*Malacocottus gibber*)

### 蟾杜父魚屬(*Neophrynichthys*)

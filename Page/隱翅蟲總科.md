**隱翅蟲總科**（學名：）是[多食亞目](../Page/多食亞目.md "wikilink")[甲蟲之下的一個總科](../Page/甲蟲.md "wikilink")，屬於[隐翅虫下目的一員](../Page/隐翅虫下目.md "wikilink")。本總科物種分佈全世界，而且多樣性很大，單單是已經命名的物種就有五萬八千種以上\[1\]。

## 特徵

大多數隱翅蟲類物種皆為中型到小型的甲蟲，有一對變短了的[鞘翅](../Page/鞘翅.md "wikilink")（[elytra](../Page/翅鞘.md "wikilink")）。The
hind wings have no accessory posterior ridge (locking device), no wedge
cell and apical cell. Abdominal 8th segment not entirely invaginated in
the 7th. Head mostly without coronal suture.\[2\]

## 系統分類及演化

根據Beutel & Leschen
(2005)透過比對隱翅蟲下目各物種的成蟲及若蟲的121項外型特徵，綜合出本總科之下應包括下列七個科\[3\]\[4\]：

  - C.G. Thomson 1859

  - Mulsant 1844

  - [球蕈甲科](../Page/球蕈甲科.md "wikilink")（[Leiodidae](../Page/球蕈甲科.md "wikilink")
    <small>Fleming, 1821</small> =
    [Anisotomidae](../Page/Anisotomidae.md "wikilink")）

  - Erichson 1845

      - [Cephaloplectinae](../Page/Cephaloplectinae.md "wikilink") or
        [Limulodidae](../Page/Limulodidae.md "wikilink") Sharp 1883

  - Leach 1815

  - [埋葬虫科](../Page/埋葬虫科.md "wikilink")（[Silphidae](../Page/葬甲科.md "wikilink")
    <small>Latreille, 1807</small>）

  - [隐翅虫科](../Page/隐翅虫科.md "wikilink")（[Staphylinidae](../Page/隱翅蟲科.md "wikilink")
    <small>Latreille, 1802</small>）

儘管本總科物種的明确化石记录可以追溯到[三叠纪](../Page/三叠纪.md "wikilink")\[5\]，但可能起源於[中生代早期](../Page/中生代.md "wikilink")\[6\]。

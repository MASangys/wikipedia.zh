## 自制游戏

[Stay_Frosty_(SpiceWare)_1.png](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Stay_Frosty_\(SpiceWare\)_1.png "fig:Stay_Frosty_(SpiceWare)_1.png")

With more than 49 titles in this category, the Atari 2600 is a popular platform for [homebrew](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/homebrew_\(video_games\) "wikilink") projects. Unlike later systems, the Atari 2600 does not require a [modchip](../Page/破解芯片.md "wikilink") to run homebrew cartridges. Although there is one high-level [compiler](../Page/編譯器.md "wikilink") available — [batari Basic](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Atari_2600_homebrew#batari_Basic "wikilink")\[23\] — most development for the Atari 2600 is still done in [6502](../Page/MOS_6502.md "wikilink") [assembly language](../Page/汇编语言.md "wikilink"). The 2600 lacks video memory, has only 128 [bytes](../Page/字节.md "wikilink") of RAM, and requires precise timing; homebrews written in assembly are typically considered programming challenges.

In 2003, [动视](../Page/动视.md "wikilink") selected several homebrew 2600 titles for inclusion in the [Game Boy Advance](../Page/Game_Boy_Advance.md "wikilink") version of their *[Activision Anthology](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Activision_Anthology "wikilink")*, as indicated below.\[24\]

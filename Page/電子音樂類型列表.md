現代電子音樂包括了下列這些音樂類型或風格：

  - [Ambien](../Page/氛围音乐.md "wikilink")（氛圍音樂）
      - [Ambient groove](../Page/Ambient#Ambient_groove.md "wikilink")
      - [Illbient](../Page/Illbient.md "wikilink")
      - [Organic ambient](../Page/Ambient#Organic_ambient.md "wikilink")
      - [Isolationist](../Page/Ambient#Isolationist_ambient.md "wikilink")
  - [Breakbeat](../Page/Breakbeat.md "wikilink")（碎拍）
      - [Darkstep](../Page/Darkstep.md "wikilink")
      - [2Step](../Page/2Step.md "wikilink")，又稱[Speed
        garage](../Page/Speed_garage.md "wikilink")
      - [Breakbeat hardcore](../Page/Breakbeat_hardcore.md "wikilink")
      - [Breakcore](../Page/Breakcore.md "wikilink")
      - [Brokenbeat](../Page/Brokenbeat.md "wikilink")
      - [Drill and bass](../Page/Drill_and_bass.md "wikilink")
      - [Drum and bass](../Page/Drum_and_bass.md "wikilink") (鼓打貝斯)
      - [Jungle](../Page/早期叢林舞曲.md "wikilink")（叢林音樂）
  - [Darkwave](../Page/Darkwave.md "wikilink")
  - [Downtempo](../Page/Downtempo.md "wikilink")
      - [Nu jazz](../Page/新爵士.md "wikilink")
      - [Trip hop](../Page/Trip_hop.md "wikilink")（又稱"Bristol Sound"）
  - [Electro](../Page/Electro.md "wikilink") (電子放克)
  - [Electronica](../Page/Electronica.md "wikilink")
      - [Big beat](../Page/Big_beat.md "wikilink")
  - [Hardcore](../Page/Hardcore.md "wikilink")（硬蕊）
      - [4-beat](../Page/4-beat.md "wikilink")
      - [Gabba](../Page/Gabba.md "wikilink")
      - [Happy hardcore](../Page/Happy_hardcore.md "wikilink")
      - [Freeform hardcore](../Page/Freeform_hardcore.md "wikilink")
      - [Makina](../Page/Makina.md "wikilink")
  - [Hi-NRG](../Page/Hi-NRG.md "wikilink")
      - [Nu-NRG](../Page/Nu-NRG.md "wikilink")
  - [浩室](../Page/浩室.md "wikilink")（House）
      - [Acid house](../Page/Acid_house.md "wikilink")（迷幻浩室）
      - [Chicago house](../Page/Chicago_house.md "wikilink")
      - [Deep house](../Page/Deep_house.md "wikilink")
      - [Freestyle house](../Page/Freestyle_house.md "wikilink")
      - [Garage](../Page/Garage.md "wikilink")（車庫音樂）
      - [Ghetto house](../Page/Ghetto_house.md "wikilink")
      - [Hard house](../Page/Hard_house.md "wikilink")
      - [Hip house](../Page/Hip_house.md "wikilink")
      - [Microhouse](../Page/Microhouse.md "wikilink")
      - [New Beat](../Page/New_Beat.md "wikilink")
      - [Progressive house](../Page/Progressive_house.md "wikilink")
      - [Tech house](../Page/Tech_house.md "wikilink")
      - [Vocal house](../Page/Vocal_house.md "wikilink")
  - [Industrial](../Page/Industrial.md "wikilink")（工業音樂）
      - [Electronic body
        music](../Page/Electronic_body_music.md "wikilink")（EBM）
      - [Futurepop](../Page/Futurepop.md "wikilink")
      - [Old-school EBM](../Page/Old-school_EBM.md "wikilink")
  - [Intelligent dance
    music](../Page/Intelligent_dance_music.md "wikilink")（IDM）
      - [Bitpop](../Page/Bitpop.md "wikilink")
      - [Chip music](../Page/Chip_music.md "wikilink")
      - [Glitch](../Page/Glitch.md "wikilink")
  - [Miami Bass](../Page/Miami_Bass.md "wikilink")
  - [Musique concrète](../Page/Musique_concrète.md "wikilink")
  - [New Age](../Page/New_Age.md "wikilink")
  - [Noise](../Page/Noise.md "wikilink")
  - [Nortec](../Page/Nortec.md "wikilink")（從[提瓦納](../Page/提瓦納.md "wikilink")、[墨西哥來的電子音樂風格](../Page/墨西哥.md "wikilink")）
  - [瑞舞](../Page/瑞舞.md "wikilink")（Rave）
  - [Synth pop](../Page/Synth_pop.md "wikilink")（電子合成流行音樂）
      - [Electroclash](../Page/Electroclash.md "wikilink")（1990年代晚期、2000年代初期）
      - [Electropop](../Page/Electropop.md "wikilink")（1980年代）
  - [Synthpunk](../Page/Synthpunk.md "wikilink")
  - [Techno](../Page/Techno.md "wikilink")（鐵克諾）
      - [Detroit techno](../Page/Detroit_techno.md "wikilink") (底特律鐵克諾)
      - [Freetekno](../Page/Freetekno.md "wikilink")
      - [Ghettotech](../Page/Ghettotech.md "wikilink")
      - [Hardcore techno](../Page/Hardcore_techno.md "wikilink")
          - [Rotterdam techno](../Page/Rotterdam_techno.md "wikilink")
  - [Trance](../Page/Trance.md "wikilink")（出神/傳世）
      - [Hard trance](../Page/Hard_trance.md "wikilink")
      - [Goa trance](../Page/Goa_trance.md "wikilink")
      - [Melodic trance](../Page/Melodic_trance.md "wikilink")
      - [Minimalist trance](../Page/Minimalist_trance.md "wikilink")
      - [Progressive trance](../Page/Progressive_trance.md "wikilink")
      - [Psychedelic trance](../Page/迷幻出神.md "wikilink")
      - [Uplifting trance](../Page/Uplifting_trance.md "wikilink")
  - [蒸汽波](../Page/蒸汽波.md "wikilink")（Vaporwave）

**震旦角石**（[學名](../Page/學名.md "wikilink")：）是一[屬已](../Page/屬.md "wikilink")[滅絕的](../Page/滅絕.md "wikilink")[鸚鵡螺類](../Page/鸚鵡螺亞綱.md "wikilink")。牠們的[化石廣泛分佈在](../Page/化石.md "wikilink")[中國各地](../Page/中國.md "wikilink")，包括[貴州](../Page/貴州.md "wikilink")、[湖北](../Page/湖北.md "wikilink")、[湖南](../Page/湖南.md "wikilink")、[四川](../Page/四川.md "wikilink")、[甘肅](../Page/甘肅.md "wikilink")、[江蘇](../Page/江蘇.md "wikilink")、[安徽](../Page/安徽.md "wikilink")、[山西](../Page/山西.md "wikilink")、[西藏](../Page/西藏.md "wikilink")、[新疆](../Page/新疆.md "wikilink")、[浙江](../Page/浙江.md "wikilink")、[陝西及](../Page/陝西.md "wikilink")[雲南等地](../Page/雲南.md "wikilink")。其下皆是未定種。

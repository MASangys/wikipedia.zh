**青眼魚科**是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[仙女魚目的其中一](../Page/仙女魚目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**青眼魚科**其下分2個屬，如下：

### 青眼魚屬(Chlorophthalmus)

  - [尖額青眼魚](../Page/尖額青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus acutifrons*)
  - [尖吻青眼魚](../Page/尖吻青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus agassizi*)
  - [大眼青眼魚](../Page/大眼青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus albatrossis*)
  - [大西洋青眼魚](../Page/大西洋青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus atlanticus*)
  - [北域青眼魚](../Page/北域青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus borealis*)
  - [巴西青眼魚](../Page/巴西青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus brasiliensis*)
  - [鉛色青眼魚](../Page/鉛色青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus chalybeius*)
  - [角青眼魚](../Page/角青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus corniger*)
  - [伊氏青眼魚](../Page/伊氏青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus ichthyandri*)
  - [蒙圖青眼魚](../Page/蒙圖青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus mento*)
  - [黑緣青眼魚](../Page/黑緣青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus
    nigromarginatus*)
  - [日本青眼魚](../Page/日本青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus pectoralis*)
  - [前齒青眼魚](../Page/前齒青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus proridens*)
  - [斑點青眼魚](../Page/斑點青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus punctatus*)
  - [智利青眼魚](../Page/智利青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Chlorophthalmus zvezdae*)

### 副青眼魚屬(*Parasudis*)

  - [布氏副青眼魚](../Page/布氏副青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Parasudis fraserbrunneri*)
  - [巴西副青眼魚](../Page/巴西副青眼魚.md "wikilink")(*Parasudis truculenta*)

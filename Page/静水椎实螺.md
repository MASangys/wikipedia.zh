**静水椎实螺**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）动物，俗名椎实螺，是一種大型會呼吸空氣的[淡水螺](../Page/淡水螺.md "wikilink")，是[椎实螺科](../Page/椎实螺科.md "wikilink")[椎实螺属的](../Page/椎实螺属.md "wikilink")[有肺類](../Page/有肺類.md "wikilink")[腹足綱](../Page/腹足綱.md "wikilink")[軟體動物](../Page/軟體動物.md "wikilink")\[1\]。

## 寄生蟲

静水椎实螺是下列多種寄生蟲的[中間宿主](../Page/中間宿主.md "wikilink")：

  - *[Moliniella anceps](../Page/Moliniella_anceps.md "wikilink")*
    ([Molin](../Page/Raffaele_Molin.md "wikilink"), 1859) Hubner,
    1939\[9\]

其他寄生蟲包括：

  - *[Echinoparyphium
    aconiatum](../Page/Echinoparyphium_aconiatum.md "wikilink")*\[10\]
  - *[Echinoparyphium
    recurvatum](../Page/Echinoparyphium_recurvatum.md "wikilink")*\[11\]
  - *[Opisthioglyphe
    ranae](../Page/Opisthioglyphe_ranae.md "wikilink")*\[12\]
  - *[Plagiorchis
    elegans](../Page/Plagiorchis_elegans.md "wikilink")*\[13\]
  - *[Diplostomum
    pseudospathaceum](../Page/Diplostomum_pseudospathaceum.md "wikilink")*\[14\]
  - [卷棘口吸虫](../Page/卷棘口吸虫.md "wikilink") *[Echinostoma
    revolutum](../Page/Echinostoma_revolutum.md "wikilink")*\[15\]
  - *[Trichobilharzia
    szidati](../Page/Trichobilharzia_szidati.md "wikilink")*\[16\]

*Lymnaea stagnalis*亦曾在實驗中被安排受*[Elaphostrongylus
rangiferi](../Page/Elaphostrongylus_rangiferi.md "wikilink")*感染\[17\]。

**非线性扩散方程**（Nonlinear Diffusion equation）是一个非线性偏微分方程：\[1\]

\(u_{t}=\alpha*u_{xx}-\beta*u^3-\gamma*u^2\)

## 解析解

\[u(x, t) = -(1/2)*\gamma/\beta-(1/2)*\gamma*coth(_C1-(1/2)*\gamma*x/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)-(1/4)*(-2*\gamma*\beta/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+\gamma^2)*t/\beta)/\beta\]

\[{u(x, t) = -(1/2)*\gamma/\beta+(1/2)*\gamma*coth(_C1-(1/2)*\gamma*x/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+(1/4)*(2*\gamma*\beta/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+\gamma^2)*t/\beta)/\beta}\]

\[{u(x, t) = -(1/2)*\gamma/\beta+(1/2)*\gamma*coth(_C1+(1/2)*\gamma*x/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+(1/4)*(-2*\gamma*\beta/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+\gamma^2)*t/\beta)/\beta}\]

\[{u(x, t) = -(1/2)*\gamma/\beta-(1/2)*\gamma*coth(_C1+(1/2)*\gamma*x/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)-(1/4)*(2*\gamma*\beta/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+\gamma^2)*t/\beta)/\beta}\]

\[{u(x, t) = -(1/2)*\gamma/\beta-(1/2)*\gamma*tanh(_C1-(1/2)*\gamma*x/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)-(1/4)*(-2*\gamma*\beta/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+\gamma^2)*t/\beta)/\beta}\]

\[{u(x, t) = -(1/2)*\gamma/\beta+(1/2)*\gamma*tanh(_C1-(1/2)*\gamma*x/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+(1/4)*(2*\gamma*\beta/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+\gamma^2)*t/\beta)/\beta}\]

\[{u(x, t) = -(1/2)*\gamma/\beta+(1/2)*\gamma*tanh(_C1+(1/2)*\gamma*x/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+(1/4)*(-2*\gamma*\beta/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+\gamma^2)*t/\beta)/\beta}\]

\[{u(x, t) = -(1/2)*\gamma/\beta-(1/2)*\gamma*tanh(_C1+(1/2)*\gamma*x/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)-(1/4)*(2*\gamma*\beta/\sqrt(-2*\beta*\alpha)+\gamma^2)*t/\beta)/\beta}\]

\[{u(x, t) = -(1/2)*\gamma/\beta+(1/2*I)*\gamma*tan(_C1+(1/4)*\gamma*\sqrt(2)*x/\sqrt(\beta*\alpha)-(1/4)*(I*\gamma^2+\gamma*\sqrt(2)*\beta/\sqrt(\beta*\alpha))*t/\beta)/\beta}\]

\[{u(x, t) = -(1/2)*\gamma/\beta+(1/2*I)*\gamma*cot(_C1+(1/4)*\gamma*\sqrt(2)*x/\sqrt(\beta*\alpha)+(1/4)*(I*\gamma^2-\gamma*\sqrt(2)*\beta/\sqrt(\beta*\alpha))*t/\beta)/\beta}\]

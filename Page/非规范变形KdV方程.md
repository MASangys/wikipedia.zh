'''非规范变形KdV方程 '''(Unnormalized modified KdV equation)是一个非线性偏微分方程：\[1\]

\(u_{t}+u_{xxx}+\alpha*u^2*u_{x}=0\)

## 解析解

\[{u(x, t) = \sqrt(6)*_C2*sech(_C1+_C2*x-_C2^3*t)/\sqrt(\alpha)}\]

\[u(x, t) = \sqrt(6)*_C3*JacobiDN(_C2+_C3*x+(-2*_C3^3+_C3^3*_C1^2)*t, _C1)/\sqrt(\alpha)\]

\[u(x, t) = \sqrt(-6*\alpha*(-1+_C1^2))*_C3*JacobiND(_C2+_C3*x+(-2*_C3^3+_C3^3*_C1^2)*t, _C1)/\alpha\]

\[u(x, t) = \sqrt(6)*_C1*_C3*JacobiCN(-_C2-_C3*x-(-2*_C3^3*_C1^2+_C3^3)*t, _C1)/\sqrt(\alpha)\]

\[{u(x, t) = \sqrt(6)*\sqrt(\alpha*(-1+_C1^2))*_C3*JacobiNC(-_C2-_C3*x-(-2*_C3^3*_C1^2+_C3^3)*t, _C1)/\alpha}\]

\[u(x, t) = -6*_C3*JacobiNS(_C2+_C3*x+(_C3^3*_C1^2+_C3^3)*t, _C1)/\sqrt(-6*\alpha)\]

\[u(x, t) = -\sqrt(6)*_C3*JacobiDN(_C2+_C3*x+(-2*_C3^3+_C3^3*_C1^2)*t, _C1)/\sqrt(\alpha)\]

**鞭冠鮟鱇科**，又稱**疏刺角鮟鱇科**是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鮟鱇目的其中一科](../Page/鮟鱇目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**鞭冠鮟鱇科**下分1個屬，如下：

  - 鞭冠鮟鱇屬(*Himantolophus*)
      - [大西洋鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/大西洋鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        albinares*)
      - [阿氏鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/阿氏鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus appelii*)
      - [藍光鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/藍光鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        azurlucens*)
      - [寶蓮鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/寶蓮鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus borealis*)
      - [短吻鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/短吻鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        brevirostris*)
      - [窄鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/窄鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus compressus*)
      - [角鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/角鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus cornifer*)
      - [毛鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/毛鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus crinitus*)
      - [西太平洋鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/西太平洋鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        danae*)
      - [多指鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/多指鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        groenlandicus*)：又稱疏刺角鮟鱇。
      - [新西蘭鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/新西蘭鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        litoceras*)
      - [大角鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/大角鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        macroceras*)
      - [擬大角鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/擬大角鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        macroceratoides*)
      - [莫爾鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/莫爾鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus mauli*)
      - [黑鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/黑鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        melanolophus*)：又稱深海鮟鱇、黑冠疏刺角鮟鱇。
      - [多叉鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/多叉鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        multifurcatus*)
      - [黑角鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/黑角鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        nigricornis*)
      - [少絲鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/少絲鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        paucifilosus*)
      - [南非鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/南非鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        pseudalbinares*)
      - [相模灣鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/相模灣鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus
        sagamius*)
      - [斯氏鞭冠鮟鱇](../Page/斯氏鞭冠鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Himantolophus stewarti*)

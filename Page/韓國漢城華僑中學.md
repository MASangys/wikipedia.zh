**韓國漢城華僑中學**（, 或**Hanseong Chinese Middle and High School**\[1\]\[2\]）是一所[韩国](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/韩国 "wikilink")[首爾](../Page/首爾.md "wikilink")[西大門區](../Page/西大門區.md "wikilink")的华僑高中，\[3\]用台湾的[课程和](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/课程 "wikilink")[课本](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/课本 "wikilink")。\[4\]

It is a *hwagyo*, or an ethnic Chinese school in South Korea. The South Korean government began recognizing *hwagyo* as educational institutions in 1998.\[5\]

The area primary school serving Chinese students is [Seoul Chinese Primary School](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seoul_Chinese_Primary_School "wikilink") in [Jung-gu](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jung-gu,_Seoul "wikilink").

## 历史

1942年**光华中学** 建筑，1945年本学校关门因为[第二次世界大战](../Page/第二次世界大战.md "wikilink")，1948年重新开启在中华民国大使馆。\[6\]

Sun Shu-i became the principal in 1984 and discontinued tracking students into classes A, B, and C.\[7\]

, the school had 1,100 students. About half of the families of the students made their living as owners or employees of Chinese restaurants.\[8\]

## 營運

The school has painting and [Chinese calligraphy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_calligraphy "wikilink") classes.\[9\]

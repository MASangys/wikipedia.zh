**頂冠節蜱屬**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）为[蛛形綱](../Page/蛛形綱.md "wikilink")[蜱蟎亞綱](../Page/蜱蟎亞綱.md "wikilink")[絨蟎目](../Page/絨蟎目.md "wikilink")[Prostigmata亞目](../Page/Prostigmata.md "wikilink")（亦有作[Prostigmata目](../Page/Prostigmata.md "wikilink")）[節蜱科下的一個屬](../Page/節蜱科.md "wikilink")。

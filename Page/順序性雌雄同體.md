**順序性雌雄同體**
是一種發生在許多[魚類](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/魚類 "wikilink")[腹足綱](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/腹足綱 "wikilink")、[植物之中的](../Page/植物.md "wikilink")[雌雄同體現象](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/雌雄同體 "wikilink")，例如[小丑魚](../Page/小丑魚.md "wikilink")。「順序性雌雄同體」的發生時機為一個生物個體轉變性別的時候。性別轉換的方向可以是雌性變為雄性，也可以是由雄性變為雌性。該生物的性腺在轉換性別時，可以同時擁有雌性和雄性配子，也可以完全改變性別。該現象發生於其生命周期的後期。\[1\]
花朵的個體也可以稱為是順序性雌雄同體的一種， 即使該株植物的雄花以及雌花可能是分開的。

## 動物學

### 雄性先熟

[Anemone_purple_anemonefish.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Anemone_purple_anemonefish.jpg "fig:Anemone_purple_anemonefish.jpg"),
一個雄性先熟的物種。\]\] **雄性先熟雌雄同體**
意指出生時為雄性，並在其生命的一個時間點轉換性別為雌性的生物。[小丑魚即為雄性先熟的動物之一](../Page/小丑魚.md "wikilink")。
小丑魚擁有非常結構化的社會習性。 *[海葵雙鋸魚](../Page/海葵雙鋸魚.md "wikilink")*
有零到四個個體被排除在育種個體，以及居住於海葵內的一對育種配對之外。
支配地位則視體型大小。雌魚是最大型的，而雄魚則是第二大型的。 群體內的其他小丑魚則是體型較小且不育種，
其性腺並沒有任何功能。\[2\]
如果雌性小丑魚死亡，則其配偶，雄性小丑魚則會增加體型大小，並轉變為該群體之中的雌性育種小丑魚。
群體之中最大型的不育種小丑魚則開始性成熟，並成為該群體之中的雄性育種小丑魚。\[3\]

其他雄性先熟的魚類包括[鯡形目](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鯡形目 "wikilink")，[鮎形目](../Page/鮎形目.md "wikilink")，[巨口魚目](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巨口魚目 "wikilink")，
以及[鱸形目之下的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鱸形目 "wikilink")[雀鯛科以及](../Page/雀鯛科.md "wikilink")[鯛科](../Page/鯛科.md "wikilink")。
這些生物的親緣關係較遠，而且中間還有許多不是雄性先熟的近緣生物， 其表示雄性先熟的模式已經多次演化。\[4\] 其他雄性先熟的物種還包括：

    該生物的雌性個體較雄性個體大，而且只能於夏季時觀察到。 而雄性個體任何時候都能觀察到。
  - 扁形動物門的* retenuova*以及* cardii*。
  - **，為一
    [腹足綱生物](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/腹足綱 "wikilink")，為生殖功能上的雄性先熟。
    其[精子在冬末以及春初的時候成熟](../Page/精子.md "wikilink")，而其[卵子在初夏的時候成熟](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/卵子 "wikilink")，並只於六月時交配。
    其表示雄性在雌性成熟並出現之前並不能交配或生殖，為該物種在功能上被列入雄性先熟的原因。\[5\]\[6\]
  - **，是一類雄性先熟的蝴蝶。 在該物種之中為功能上的雄性先熟，雄性成體比雌性成體早2\~3周出現。\[7\]

### 雌性先熟

[thumb](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/image:Thalassoma_lunare_1.jpg "wikilink")，一個雌性先熟的物種。\]\]

**雌性先熟雌雄同體**意指出生時為雌性，並在其生命的一個時間點轉換性別為雄性的生物。 隨著該生物成長，將因內部或外部刺激，
而改變性別成為雄性。雄性的[fecundity隨著年齡大幅增加](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/fecundity "wikilink")，和雌性不同。

雌性先熟是大自然之中，最常見的順序性雌雄同體型態。\[8\] 500種順序性雌雄同體的魚類之中，大概有75% 的物種是雌性先熟的。
\[9\]\[10\] 這類型的順序性雌雄同體的普遍生物為[隆頭魚科的](../Page/隆頭魚科.md "wikilink")。 They
are one of the largest families of coral reef fish and belong to the
family Labridae. 隆投頭魚位於世界上所有marine habitats and tend to bury themselves
in sand at night or when they feel threatened.\[11\]
在隆頭魚科的動物中，最大的兩條魚為雄性，而較小型的為雌性。
在多數狀況下，雌性魚和未熟的魚有著均一，較單調的顏色，而雄性魚最後則有著體色雙色的階段。 \[12\] Large males
hold territories and try to pair spawn while small to mid-size
initial-phase males live with females and group
[spawn](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spawn_\(biology\) "wikilink").\[13\]
In other words, both the initial and terminal phase males can breed;
they differ however in the way they do it.

In the [California
sheephead](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/California_sheephead "wikilink")
(*Semicossyphus pulcher*)，一種隆頭魚，當雌性個體轉變為雄性時， the ovaries degenerate and
spermatogenic crypts appear in the gonads. The general structure of the
gonads remains ovarian after the transformation and the sperm is
transported through a series of ducts on the periphery of the gonad and
[oviduct](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/oviduct "wikilink"). 性別的改變和年齡相關。
For example, the California sheephead stays a female for four years
before changing sex.\[14\]

[Blue-headed
wrasse在生命開始時可以是雄性或雌性](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thalassoma_bifasciatum "wikilink")，但雌性可以改變性別並擁有雄性生殖功能。
年幼的雌性及雄性魚擁有不同的體色，known as the "Initial Phase" before progressing into
the "Terminal Phase" coloration, which has a change in intensity
of顏色、條紋。 Initial Phase males have larger
[testes](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/testes "wikilink") than larger,
terminal phase males, which enables the initial phase males to produce a
large amount of sperm. This strategy is able to compete with that of the
larger male, who is able to guard his own harem.

*[Botryllus
schlosseri](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Botryllus_schlosseri "wikilink")*,
a colonial
[tunicate](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/tunicate "wikilink"), is a
protogynous hermaphrodite. In a colony, eggs are ovulated about two days
before the peak of sperm emission.\[15\] Although self-fertilization is
avoided and cross-fertilization favored by this strategy,
self-fertilization is still possible. Self-fertilized eggs develop with
a substantially higher frequency of anomalies during cleavage than
cross-fertilized eggs (23% vs. 1.6%).\[16\] Also a significantly lower
percentage of larvae derived from self-fertilized eggs metamorphose, and
the growth of the colonies derived from their metamorphosis is
significantly lower. These findings suggest that self-fertilization
gives rise to inbreeding depression associated with developmental
deficits that are likely caused by expression of deleterious recessive
mutations.\[17\]

### 最終後果

[Michael
Ghiselin](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Ghiselin "wikilink")
proposed three models for hermaphroditism in 1969 in his paper "The
evolution of hermaphroditism among animals".\[24\]

1.  The 'low-density model' states that individuals have characteristics
    that reduce the opportunity for mating; this model cannot be applied
    to sequential hermaphroditism.
2.  The 'gene dispersal model' is based on the idea that limitations on
    dispersal may influence population structure or genetical
    environment and it can be separated into two versions: the
    inbreeding version and the sampling-error version. This theory of
    gene dispersal can be applied to sequential hermaphrodites,
    especially the inbreeding version. The inbreeding version is based
    upon the fact that both protandry and protogyny help prevent
    inbreeding in plants and thus one can make the same assumption that
    in animals it works by reducing the probability of this occurring
    among siblings. The sampling-error version is based on the reality
    that the genetical environment is influenced by genetic drift and
    similar phenomena in small populations. The two aspects of these
    hypotheses influenced by hermaphroditism, that is inbreeding and
    sampling-error, result in the same thing — reduction of genetic
    variability. In other words, being a hermaphrodite would increase
    genetic variability and thus be considered advantageous to the
    population.
3.  Lastly, the 'size-advantage model' states that reproductive
    functions are carried out better if the individual is a certain
    size/age. To create selection for hermaphroditism, small individuals
    must have higher reproductive fitness as one sex and larger
    individuals must have higher reproductive fitness as the opposite
    sex. With this size-distribution pattern, an individual would
    maximize its fitness if it reproduced as a sequential hermaphrodite.
    For example, eggs are larger than sperm, thus if you are a big you
    are able to make more eggs so being female when big is advantageous,
    however the size advantage relationship is really not as simple as
    the example just mentioned, but it allows for a better
    understanding.

In most
[ectotherms](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/ectotherms "wikilink") body
size and female fecundity are positively correlated.\[25\] This supports
Ghiselin’s size-advantage model, which is still widely accepted today.
Kazancioglu and Alonzo (2010) performed the first comparative analysis
of sex change in
[Labridae](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Labridae "wikilink"). Their
analysis supports the size-advantage model by Ghiselin and suggest that
sequential hermaphroditism is correlated to the size-advantage. They
determined that
[dioecy](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/dioecy "wikilink") was less
likely to occur when the size advantage is stronger than other
advantages\[26\]

Warner suggests that selection for protandry may occur in populations
where female fecundity is augmented with age and individuals mate
randomly. Selection for protogyny may occur where there are traits in
the population that depress male fecundity at early ages
(territoriality, mate selection or inexperience) and when female
fecundity is decreased with age, the latter seems to be rare in the
field.\[27\] An example of territoriality favoring protogyny occurs when
there is a need to protect their habitat and being a large male is
advantageous for this purpose. In the mating aspect, a large male has a
higher chance of mating, while this has no effect on the female mating
fitness.\[28\] Thus, he suggests that female fecundity has more impact
on sequential hermaphroditism than the age structures of the
population.\[29\]

The size-advantage model predicts that sex change would only be absent
if the relationship between size/age with reproductive potential is
identical in both sexes. With this prediction one would assume that
hermaphroditism is very common, but this is not the case. Sequential
hermaphroditism is very rare and according to scientists this is due to
some cost that decreases fitness in sex changers as opposed to those who
don’t change sex. Some of the hypotheses proposed for the dearth of
hermaphrodites are the energetic cost of sex change, genetic and/or
physiological barriers to sex change, and sex-specific mortality
rates.\[30\]\[31\]\[32\]

In 2009, Kazanciglu and Alonzo found that dioecy was only favored when
the cost of changing sex was very large. This indicates that the cost of
sex change does not explain the rarity of sequential hermaphroditism by
itself.\[33\]

### 近因

Many studies have focused on the proximate causes of sequential
hermaphroditism. The role of
[aromatase](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/aromatase "wikilink") has been
widely studied in this area. Aromatase is an
[enzyme](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/enzyme "wikilink") that controls
the
[androgen](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/androgen "wikilink")/[estrogen](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/estrogen "wikilink")
ratio in animals by catalyzing the conversion of
[testosterone](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/testosterone "wikilink")
into [oestradiol](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/oestradiol "wikilink"),
which is irreversible. It has been discovered that the aromatase pathway
mediates sex change in both directions.\[34\] Many studies also involve
understanding the effect of aromatase inhibitors on sex change. One such
study was performed by Kobayashi et al. In their study they tested the
role of estrogens in male three-spot wrasses (*Halichoeres
trimaculatus*). They discovered that fish treated with aromatase
inhibitors showed decreased gonodal weight, plasma estrogen level and
spermatogonial proliferation in the testis as well as increased androgen
levels. Their results suggest that estrogens are important in the
regulation of
[spermatogenesis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/spermatogenesis "wikilink")
in this protogynous hermaphrodite.\[35\]

### 遺傳後果

順序性雌形同體的物種的性別比通常都近似於其出生時的性別,
而且於轉換性別之後出現更多的成功繁殖。理論上它們會減少[遺傳多樣性以及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/遺傳多樣性 "wikilink")[有效種群大小](../Page/有效種群大小.md "wikilink")。
然而，一個在南非海域對於兩個生態學上相似的物種，[紫背冬鯛](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/紫背冬鯛 "wikilink")
([兩性異形](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/兩性異形 "wikilink"))以及[隆背麗眼鯛](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/隆背麗眼鯛 "wikilink")
(雌性先熟) 的調查發現，該兩物種的物種多樣性十分相似，而當有效族群大小在變性期間快速下降時，它們在相對較短的時間內依然是相似的。\[36\]

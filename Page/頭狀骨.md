**頭狀骨**（**capitate
bone**）是人類[掌骨中最大的一塊](../Page/掌骨.md "wikilink")，為腕骨中央的骨頭。較圓的一端位於[手舟骨及](../Page/手舟骨.md "wikilink")[月骨所組成的凹槽上](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/月骨 "wikilink")\[1\]。

許多[哺乳類動物身上也可以找到這塊骨頭](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/哺乳類 "wikilink")，與[爬蟲類及](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/爬蟲類 "wikilink")[兩棲類身上的第三遠端腕骨為](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/兩棲類 "wikilink")[同源構造](../Page/同源.md "wikilink")。

## 構造

頭狀骨是腕骨中最大的一塊\[2\]，位於腕骨遠端列。頭狀骨的旁邊接的是無名指掌骨，，尺骨側連接的是[鉤骨](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鉤骨 "wikilink")，橈側連接[三角骨](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/三角骨 "wikilink")，腕側結月骨跟手舟骨。\[3\]

### 表面

The *superior surface* is round, smooth, and articulates with the
[lunate
bone](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/lunate_bone "wikilink").\[4\]

The *inferior surface* is divided by two ridges into three facets, for
articulation with the second, third, and fourth [metacarpal
bones](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/metacarpal_bone "wikilink"), that
for the third being the largest.\[5\]

The *dorsal surface* is broad and rough.\[6\]

The *palmar surface* is narrow, rounded, and rough, for the attachment
of ligaments and a part of the [adductor pollicis
muscle](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/adductor_pollicis_muscle "wikilink").\[7\]

The *lateral surface* articulates with the lesser multangular by a small
facet at its anterior inferior angle, behind which is a rough depression
for the attachment of an [interosseous
ligament](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Interosseous_intercarpal_ligaments "wikilink").
Above this is a deep, rough groove, forming part of the neck, and
serving for the attachment of ligaments; it is bounded superiorly by a
smooth, convex surface, for articulation with the [scaphoid
bone](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/scaphoid_bone "wikilink").\[8\]

The *medial surface* articulates with the [hamate
bone](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/hamate_bone "wikilink") by a smooth,
concave, oblong facet, which occupies its posterior and superior parts;
it is rough in front, for the attachment of an interosseous
ligament.\[9\]

### 變形

The capitate bone variably articulates with the metacarpal of the index
finger.\[10\]

### 骨化

頭狀骨的骨化始於1至5個月大時。\[11\]

## 功能

為構成腕骨的一部分。\[12\]

## 歷史

辭源來自於[拉丁文的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/拉丁文 "wikilink")「頭」（**）。\[13\]

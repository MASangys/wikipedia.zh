**页面描述语言**（简称
PDL）是一种在较高层次上描述实际输出结果[位图的描述页面内容的语言](../Page/位图.md "wikilink")。
一些页面描述语言并不完备，很难称为是一种[编程语言](../Page/编程语言.md "wikilink")，但是作为页面描述语言之一的
[PostScript](../Page/PostScript.md "wikilink") 则是一个非常完备的编程语言。

## 列表

  - [AFP](../Page/Advanced_Function_Presentation.md "wikilink")
    (Advanced Function Presentation)
  - [DVI](../Page/DVI_file_format.md "wikilink") (stands for DeVice
    Independent)
  - [EPL](../Page/Eltron_Programming_Language.md "wikilink") (Eltron
    Programming Language)
  - [ESC/P2](../Page/ESC/P2.md "wikilink")
  - [IPDS](../Page/IPDS.md "wikilink") (Intelligent Printer Data Stream)
  - Metacode
  - [PCL](../Page/PCL.md "wikilink") (Printer Command Language)
  - [PDF](../Page/PDF.md "wikilink") (Portable Document Format)
  - [PLT](../Page/PLT.md "wikilink")(HPGL)
  - [PostScript](../Page/PostScript.md "wikilink")
  - [PPDS](../Page/IBM_Personal_Printer_Data_Stream.md "wikilink")
    (Personal Printer Data Stream).
  - [SVG](../Page/Scalable_Vector_Graphics.md "wikilink") (Scalable
    Vector Graphics).
  - [XSL-FO](../Page/XSL-FO.md "wikilink") (XSL Formating Objects)
  - [XPS](../Page/XML纸张规范.md "wikilink")（XML Paper Specification）

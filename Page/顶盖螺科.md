**顶盖螺科**（学名：），又名 **晃蓋螺科**（学名：）及**马掌螺科**，
[软体动物门](../Page/软体动物门.md "wikilink")[腹足纲下的一科](../Page/腹足纲.md "wikilink")，舊屬[中腹足目](../Page/中腹足目.md "wikilink")的[马掌螺总科](../Page/马掌螺总科.md "wikilink")，今屬[玉黍螺類支序的](../Page/玉黍螺類.md "wikilink")[白彫螺總科](../Page/白彫螺總科.md "wikilink")。

## 屬

以下為本科的屬：

  - *[Antisabia](../Page/Antisabia.md "wikilink")*
    [Iredale](../Page/Tom_Iredale.md "wikilink"), 1937
  - [晃盖螺属](../Page/晃盖螺属.md "wikilink")
    *[Cheilea](../Page/Cheilea.md "wikilink")* Modeer, 1793 - synonym:
    *Mitrularia* Schumacher, 1817\[1\]
  - *[Hipponix](../Page/Hipponix.md "wikilink")* DeFrance, 1819\[2\]
  - *[Leptonotis](../Page/Leptonotis.md "wikilink")* Conrad, 1866\[3\]
  - *[Malluvium](../Page/Malluvium.md "wikilink")* Melvill, 1909 \[4\]
  - *[Milicheilea](../Page/Milicheilea.md "wikilink")* Espinosa & Ortea,
    2011
  - *[Neojanacus](../Page/Neojanacus.md "wikilink")*
    [Suter](../Page/Henry_Suter.md "wikilink"), 1907\[5\]
  - *[Sabia](../Page/Sabia_\(gastropod\).md "wikilink")* Gray, 1841\[6\]

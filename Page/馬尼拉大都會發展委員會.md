**馬尼拉大都會發展委員會**或稱MMDA是菲律賓共和國綜合規劃與治理[馬尼拉](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/馬尼拉市 "wikilink")、[奎松](../Page/奎松市.md "wikilink")、[加洛坎](../Page/加洛坎市.md "wikilink")、[巴萨](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴萨市 "wikilink")、[曼达路永](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/曼达路永市 "wikilink")、[马卡迪](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/马卡迪市 "wikilink")、[帕西](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/帕西市 "wikilink")、[馬利金納](../Page/馬利金納市.md "wikilink")、[文珍俞巴](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/文珍俞巴市 "wikilink")、[拉斯皮納斯](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/拉斯皮納斯市 "wikilink")、[帕拉納克](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/帕拉納克市 "wikilink")、[描仁瑞拉](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴倫蘇埃拉_\(菲律賓\) "wikilink")、[馬拉邦](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/馬拉邦市 "wikilink")、[達義](../Page/達義市.md "wikilink")、[納沃達斯](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/納沃達斯市 "wikilink")、[仙範與自治市](../Page/仙範市.md "wikilink")－[巴提洛斯的機關](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/巴提洛斯自治市 "wikilink")，負責[首都區](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/首都區 "wikilink")－[馬尼拉大都會](../Page/馬尼拉大都會.md "wikilink")內[都市計劃](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/都市計劃 "wikilink")、監督、協調機-{制}-、運作一般政策或執行總體政令的機關；論權限和統籌範圍乃高於首都區內各地方政府的層級；發展委員會的委員長乃由[菲律賓總統指派](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/菲律賓總統 "wikilink")，屬[內閣](../Page/內閣.md "wikilink")成員。

\<\!-- The Secretary is assisted by a General Manager, an Assistant
General Manager for Finance and Administration, an Assistant General
Manager for Planning and Assistant General Manager for Operation, all of
whom are appointed by the President with the consent and concurrence of
the majority of the Council, subject to civil service laws, rules and
regulations. They enjoy security of tenure and may be removed for cause
in accordance with law.

The Assistant General Manager for Planning should have not less than
five (5) years extensive experience in development and planning or must
hold a master's degree in urban planning or similar disciplines.

## History

  - **February 27, 1975.** President [Ferdinand
    Marcos](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ferdinand_Marcos "wikilink")
    issued [Presidential
    Decree](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Presidential_Decree "wikilink")
    824 creating the Metropolitan Manila Commission (MMC). It integrates
    the Philippine capital
    [Manila](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manila "wikilink") and
    adjacent [Quezon
    City](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quezon_City "wikilink") with 2
    cities and 12 municipalities of the province of
    [Rizal](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rizal "wikilink") and 1
    municipality of the province of
    [Bulacan](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bulacan "wikilink"). Marcos
    appointed his wife [Imelda
    Marcos](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imelda_Marcos "wikilink") as
    governor and Ismael Mathay, Jr. as vice-governor. The office was
    located in front of the GMA-7 TV station at
    [EDSA](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/EDSA "wikilink") corner Timog
    Avenue, Diliman, Quezon City.

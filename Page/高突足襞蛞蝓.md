**高突足襞蛞蝓**（学名：）为[腹足綱](../Page/腹足綱.md "wikilink")[有肺類](../Page/有肺類.md "wikilink")[足襞蛞蝓科](../Page/足襞蛞蝓科.md "wikilink")[襞蛞蝓属的动物](../Page/襞蛞蝓属.md "wikilink")。分布于[日本](../Page/日本.md "wikilink")、[印度](../Page/印度.md "wikilink")、马来群岛以及东南亚各国、[台湾以及](../Page/台湾.md "wikilink")[中国的](../Page/中国.md "wikilink")[广东](../Page/广东.md "wikilink")、[海南](../Page/海南.md "wikilink")、[广西](../Page/广西.md "wikilink")、[香港](../Page/香港.md "wikilink")、[澳门等地](../Page/澳门.md "wikilink")，生活环境为陆地，多生活于山区、丘陵地带灌木丛、草丛中、公园、住宅附近乱石堆裡、石块下以及喜潮湿、多腐殖质的环境。\[1\]

## 簡述

本物種是一種有着圓圓、深色的身體，沒有外殼的蛞蝓。長約7到8厘米。它的皮膚有輕微結節狀。中央龍骨是彩色米色。
這蛞蝓有一個獨特的，非常窄腳;少年標本有腳1毫米寬和成人標本有腳的寬度僅為4或5毫米。
觸角是小的，2或3毫米長，並且它們很少延伸超過罩的邊緣。

*Laevicaulis alte* is a round, dark-coloured slug with no shell, 7 or
8 cm long. Its skin is slightly tuberculated. The central
[keel](../Page/keel_\(slug\).md "wikilink") is beige in colour.

This slug has a unique, very narrow foot; juvenile specimens have a foot
1 mm wide and adult specimens have a foot that is only 4 or 5 mm wide.

The [tentacles](../Page/tentacle.md "wikilink") are small, 2 or 3 mm
long, and they are only rarely extended beyond the edge of the
[mantle](../Page/mantle_\(mollusc\).md "wikilink").
[Laevecaulis.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Laevecaulis.jpg "fig:Laevecaulis.jpg")

## 寄生物

本蛞蝓是[廣東住血線蟲](../Page/廣東住血線蟲.md "wikilink")（*[Angiostrongylus
cantonensis](../Page/Angiostrongylus_cantonensis.md "wikilink")*）的[中間宿主](../Page/中間宿主.md "wikilink")，可引起[广州管圆线虫病](../Page/广州管圆线虫病.md "wikilink")\[2\]\[3\]。

## 捕食者

本物種會被[蛙類](../Page/蛙類.md "wikilink")*[Rana
tigrina](../Page/Rana_tigrina.md "wikilink")*獵食，從而亦成為了[廣東住血線蟲](../Page/廣東住血線蟲.md "wikilink")（*[Angiostrongylus
cantonensis](../Page/Angiostrongylus_cantonensis.md "wikilink")*）的[中間宿主](../Page/中間宿主.md "wikilink")\[4\]\[5\]。

## 分佈

本物種的原產地很大機會會在[西非或](../Page/西非.md "wikilink")[東非](../Page/東非.md "wikilink")。目前分佈在下列各地：

  - [坦桑尼亞](../Page/坦桑尼亞.md "wikilink")\[6\]

在以下各地被認為是[入侵物種](../Page/入侵物種.md "wikilink")\[7\]：

  - [南亞](../Page/南亞.md "wikilink")
  - [台灣](../Page/台灣.md "wikilink")[東沙島](../Page/東沙島.md "wikilink")\[8\]
  - [United States](../Page/United_States.md "wikilink") (Hawaii)\[9\]
  - islands in the [Indian Ocean](../Page/Indian_Ocean.md "wikilink")
  - [Australia](../Page/Australia.md "wikilink") (since 1889)
  - [Samoa](../Page/Samoa.md "wikilink")
  - and others

This species is already established in the USA, and is considered to
represent a potentially serious threat as a
[pest](../Page/pest_\(organism\).md "wikilink"), an [invasive
species](../Page/invasive_species.md "wikilink") which could negatively
affect agriculture, natural ecosystems, human health or commerce.
Therefore it has been suggested that this species be given top national
[quarantine](../Page/quarantine.md "wikilink") significance in the
USA.\[10\]

## Habitat

This species lives in dry areas, mostly at lower altitudes.
[Laevecaulis-small.JPG](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Laevecaulis-small.JPG "fig:Laevecaulis-small.JPG")

## Feeding habits

What this slug eats in the wild is not known. In captivity this slug
will eat:

  - [Dandelion](../Page/Dandelion.md "wikilink") leaves
  - [Tomatoes](../Page/Tomato.md "wikilink") - it nibbles the skin first
    and then the pulp
  - [cucumber](../Page/cucumber.md "wikilink")
  - [apples](../Page/apple.md "wikilink")
  - [spinach](../Page/spinach.md "wikilink")
  - When other items are not available, it will eat
    [dill](../Page/dill.md "wikilink")

In captivity it will not eat:

  - [grapes](../Page/grape.md "wikilink")

## Life cycle

This slug hatches from eggs. This species has several adaptations for
living in dry conditions: a rounded shape with as small as possible
surface area, and a narrow foot to reduce evaporation.

Juvenile specimens search for food nearly always at night, and stay
buried in the soil during the day. Larger specimens are active during
the day sometimes. This slug can grow up from 0.5 cm to approximately
4 cm in length in 7 months.

**鬚鰃目**又稱**銀眼鯛目**，是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鬚鰃總目](../Page/輻鰭魚#鬚鰃總目_Polymyxiomorpha.md "wikilink")(Polymyxiomorpha)下唯一一目。

鬚鰃目其下僅有**鬚鰃科**(*Polymixiidae*)一科。

## 分類

鬚鰃科其下僅有一屬：

  - **鬚鰃屬**(*Polymixia*)
      - [貝氏銀眼鯛](../Page/貝氏銀眼鯛.md "wikilink")(*Polymixia
        berndti*)：又稱[短鬚鬚鰃](../Page/短鬚鬚鰃.md "wikilink")。
      - [布氏鬚鰃](../Page/布氏鬚鰃.md "wikilink")(*Polymixia busakhini*)
      - [暗色鬚鰃](../Page/暗色鬚鰃.md "wikilink")(*Polymixia fusca*)
      - [銀眼鯛](../Page/銀眼鯛.md "wikilink")(*Polymixia
        japonica*)：又稱[日本鬚鰃](../Page/日本鬚鰃.md "wikilink")。
      - [長棘銀眼鯛](../Page/長棘銀眼鯛.md "wikilink")(*Polymixia
        longispina*)：又稱[長棘鬚鰃](../Page/長棘鬚鰃.md "wikilink")。
      - [羅氏鬚鰃](../Page/羅氏鬚鰃.md "wikilink")(*Polymixia lowei*)
      - [長鬚鬚鰃](../Page/長鬚鬚鰃.md "wikilink")(*Polymixia nobilis*)
      - [東太平洋鬚鰃](../Page/東太平洋鬚鰃.md "wikilink")(*Polymixia
        salagomeziensis*)
      - [薩氏鬚鰃](../Page/薩氏鬚鰃.md "wikilink")(*Polymixia sazonovi*)
      - [尤氏鬚鰃](../Page/尤氏鬚鰃.md "wikilink")(*Polymixia yuri*)

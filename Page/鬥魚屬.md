**鬥魚屬**（*Macropodus*），又稱[中國鬥魚屬](../Page/中國鬥魚屬.md "wikilink")，是[絲足鱸科下的一個](../Page/絲足鱸科.md "wikilink")[屬](../Page/屬_\(生物\).md "wikilink")，目前該屬下所有物種皆原產自[亞洲](../Page/亞洲.md "wikilink")。本屬的模式種是[叉尾鬥魚](../Page/叉尾鬥魚.md "wikilink")。

鬥魚屬目前已知的物種包括：

  - [叉尾鬥魚](../Page/叉尾鬥魚.md "wikilink")'' Macropodus opercularis''
    ([Linnaeus](../Page/Linnaeus.md "wikilink"), 1758)
  - [圓尾鬥魚](../Page/圓尾鬥魚.md "wikilink") *Macropodus ocellatus*
    [Cantor](../Page/Cantor.md "wikilink"), 1842
  - [黑叉尾鬥魚](../Page/黑叉尾鬥魚.md "wikilink") *Macropodus spechti*
    [Schreitmüller](../Page/Schreitmüller.md "wikilink"), 1936
    （又名[越南鬥魚](../Page/越南鬥魚.md "wikilink")）
  - [紅鰭黑叉尾鬥魚](../Page/紅鰭黑叉尾鬥魚.md "wikilink") *Macropodus erythropterus*
    [Freyhof](../Page/Freyhof.md "wikilink") &
    [Herder](../Page/Herder.md "wikilink"), 2002
  - [香港鬥魚](../Page/香港鬥魚.md "wikilink") *Macropodus hongkongensis*
    [Freyhof](../Page/Freyhof.md "wikilink") &
    [Herder](../Page/Herder.md "wikilink"), 2002
  - *Macropodus baviensis* Nguyen & Nguyen, 2005 (有效性存疑)
  - *Macropodus lineatus* Nguyen, Ngo & Nguyen, 2005 (有效性存疑)
  - *Macropodus oligolepis* Nguyen, Ngo & Nguyen, 2005 (有效性存疑)
  - *Macropodus phongnhaensis* Ngo, Nguyen & Nguyen , 2005 (有效性存疑)

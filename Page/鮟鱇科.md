**鮟鱇科**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）為[鮟鱇目的其中一科](../Page/鮟鱇目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**鮟鱇科**下分4個屬，如下：

### 擬鮟鱇屬(*Lophiodes*)

  -   - [貝羅擬鮟鱇](../Page/貝羅擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes beroe*)
      - [夏威夷擬鮟鱇](../Page/夏威夷擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes bruchius*)
      - [莖鼻擬鮟鱇](../Page/莖鼻擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes caulinaris*)
      - [遠藤擬鮟鱇](../Page/遠藤擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes
        endoi*)：又稱[遠藤氏擬鮟鱇](../Page/遠藤氏擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")。
      - [長瓣擬鮟鱇](../Page/長瓣擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes fimbriatus*)
      - [細身擬鮟鱇](../Page/細身擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes gracilimanus*)
      - [褐擬鮟鱇](../Page/褐擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes
        infrabrunneus*)：又稱隱棘擬鮟鱇。
      - [南非擬鮟鱇](../Page/南非擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes
        insidiator*)：又稱[納塔爾擬鮟鱇](../Page/納塔爾擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")。
      - [岩本擬鮟鱇](../Page/岩本擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes iwamotoi*)
      - [長棘擬鮟鱇](../Page/長棘擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes kempi*)
      - [斑點擬鮟鱇](../Page/斑點擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes maculatus*)
      - [少棘擬鮟鱇](../Page/少棘擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes miacanthus*)
      - [莫氏擬鮟鱇](../Page/莫氏擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes monodi*)
      - [大眼擬鮟鱇](../Page/大眼擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes
        mutilus*)：又稱[光擬鮟鱇](../Page/光擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")。
      - [奈氏擬鮟鱇](../Page/奈氏擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes naresi*)
      - [網紋擬鮟鱇](../Page/網紋擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes reticulatus*)
      - [線鰭擬鮟鱇](../Page/線鰭擬鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiodes spilurus*)

### 黑鮟鱇屬(*Lophiomus*)

  -   - [黑鮟鱇](../Page/黑鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophiomus setigerus*)

### 鮟鱇屬(*Lophius*)

  -   - [美洲鮟鱇](../Page/美洲鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophius americanus*)
      - [蕾鮟鱇](../Page/蕾鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophius budegassa*)：又稱歐洲鮟鱇
      - [長鰭鮟鱇](../Page/長鰭鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophius gastrophysus*)
      - [黃鮟鱇](../Page/黃鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophius litulon*)
      - [釣鮟鱇](../Page/釣鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophius piscatorius*)：又稱鮟鱇。
      - [伐氏短棘鮟鱇](../Page/伐氏短棘鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophius vaillanti*)
      - [鋤齒鮟鱇](../Page/鋤齒鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Lophius vomerinus*)：又稱犁齒鮟鱇。

### 寬鰓鮟鱇屬(*Sladenia*)

  -   - [褐寬鰓鮟鱇](../Page/褐寬鰓鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Sladenia remiger*)
      - [薛氏寬鰓鮟鱇](../Page/薛氏寬鰓鮟鱇.md "wikilink")(*Sladenia shaefersi*)

**鱸亞目**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）為[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鱸形目的一個](../Page/鱸形目.md "wikilink")[亞目](../Page/亞目.md "wikilink")，包含了与[河鲈科最亲近的类群](../Page/河鲈科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

### 内部分类

依2017年《[硬骨鱼支序分类法](../Page/硬骨鱼支序分类法.md "wikilink")》，鲈亚目只包含3个科，约有14个属，如下：

  - [东洋鲈科](../Page/东洋鲈科.md "wikilink") Niphonidae
      - [东洋鲈属](../Page/东洋鲈属.md "wikilink") *Niphon*
  - [河鲈科](../Page/河鲈科.md "wikilink") Percidae
      - [河鲈亚科](../Page/河鲈亚科.md "wikilink") Percinae
          - [晶鲈属](../Page/晶鲈属.md "wikilink") *Crystallaria*
          - [梅花鲈属](../Page/梅花鲈属.md "wikilink") *Gymnocephalus*
          - [鲈属](../Page/鲈属.md "wikilink") *Perca*
          - [腔鲈属](../Page/腔鲈属.md "wikilink") *Percarina*
      - [镖鲈亚科](../Page/镖鲈亚科.md "wikilink") Etheostomatinae
          - [隐鲈属](../Page/隐鲈属.md "wikilink") *Ammocrypta*
          - [镖鲈属](../Page/镖鲈属.md "wikilink") *Etheostoma*
          - [铜颊镖鲈属](../Page/铜颊镖鲈属.md "wikilink") *Nothonotus*
          - [小鲈属](../Page/小鲈属.md "wikilink") *Percina*
      - [梭吻鲈亚科](../Page/梭吻鲈亚科.md "wikilink") Luciopercinae
          - [罗马尼亚鲈属](../Page/罗马尼亚鲈属.md "wikilink") *Romanichthys*
          - [梭吻鲈属](../Page/梭吻鲈属.md "wikilink") *Sander*
          - [金吉鲈属](../Page/金吉鲈属.md "wikilink") *Zingel*
  - [鳄𬶮科](../Page/鳄𬶮科.md "wikilink") Trachinidae
      - [毒蛇䲢属](../Page/毒蛇䲢属.md "wikilink") *Echiichhtys*
      - [龙䲢属](../Page/龙䲢属.md "wikilink") *Trachinus*

**鳞侧石鳖目**（学名：****），是[新石鳖亞綱的一個目](../Page/新石鳖亞綱.md "wikilink")\[1\]\[2\]。

## 分類

本目之下只有一個亞目\[3\]，包含下列各科\[4\]：

  - 鳞侧石鳖亞目 Lepidopleurina\[5\]
      - [Abyssochitonidae](../Page/Abyssochitonidae.md "wikilink")
        <small>Dell'Angelo & Palazzi, 1989</small>
      - [漢利石鱉科](../Page/漢利石鱉科.md "wikilink") Hanleyidae
        <small>Bergenhayn, 1955</small>
      - [鳞侧石鳖科](../Page/鳞侧石鳖科.md "wikilink") Leptochitonidae
        <small>Dall, 1889</small>
      - [Nierstraszellidae](../Page/Nierstraszellidae.md "wikilink")
        <small>Sirenko, 1992</small>
      - [原石鳖科](../Page/原石鳖科.md "wikilink")
        [Protochitonidae](../Page/Protochitonidae.md "wikilink")
        <small>Ashby, 1925</small> †

此外\[6\]，

  - [Ferreiraellidae](../Page/Ferreiraellidae.md "wikilink") Dell'Angelo
    & Palazzi, 1991
    被認為是[Abyssochitonidae](../Page/Abyssochitonidae.md "wikilink")
    Dell'Angelo & Palazzi, 1989的同種異名；
  - [Lepidopleuridae](../Page/Lepidopleuridae.md "wikilink") Pilsbry,
    1892 被認為是[Leptochitonidae](../Page/Leptochitonidae.md "wikilink")
    Dall, 1889的同種異名；
  - [Xylochitonidae](../Page/Xylochitonidae.md "wikilink")
    Gowlett-Holmes & Jones, 1992
    被認為是[Abyssochitonidae](../Page/Abyssochitonidae.md "wikilink")
    Dell'Angelo & Palazzi, 1989的同種異名。

**鴨嘴鰻科**為[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鰻鱺目的其中一個](../Page/鰻鱺目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**鴨嘴鰻科**下分7個屬，如下：

### 小鴨嘴鰻屬(*Facciolella*)

  - [卡氏小鴨嘴鰻](../Page/卡氏小鴨嘴鰻.md "wikilink")(*Facciolella castlei*)
  - [赤道小鴨嘴鰻](../Page/赤道小鴨嘴鰻.md "wikilink")(*Facciolella equatorialis*)
  - [吉氏小鴨嘴鰻](../Page/吉氏小鴨嘴鰻.md "wikilink")(*Facciolella gilberti*)
  - [卡勒氏小鴨嘴鰻](../Page/卡勒氏小鴨嘴鰻.md "wikilink")(*Facciolella karreri*)
  - [尖吻小鴨嘴鰻](../Page/尖吻小鴨嘴鰻.md "wikilink")(*Facciolella oxyrhyncha*)
  - [似蜥小鴨嘴鰻](../Page/似蜥小鴨嘴鰻.md "wikilink")(*Facciolella
    saurencheloides*)

### 長犁(鋤)齒鰻屬(*Hoplunnis*)

  - [黑尾長犁齒鰻](../Page/黑尾長犁齒鰻.md "wikilink")(*Hoplunnis diomedianus*)
  - [大尾長犁齒鰻](../Page/大尾長犁齒鰻.md "wikilink")(*Hoplunnis macrurus*)
  - [大長犁齒鰻](../Page/大長犁齒鰻.md "wikilink")(*Hoplunnis megista*)
  - [太平洋長犁齒鰻](../Page/太平洋長犁齒鰻.md "wikilink")(*Hoplunnis pacifica*)
  - [斑長犁齒鰻](../Page/斑長犁齒鰻.md "wikilink")(*Hoplunnis punctatus*)
  - [施氏犁齒鰻](../Page/施氏犁齒鰻.md "wikilink")(*Hoplunnis schmidti*)
  - [招潮長犁齒鰻](../Page/招潮長犁齒鰻.md "wikilink")(*Hoplunnis sicarius*)
  - [似長犁齒鰻](../Page/似長犁齒鰻.md "wikilink")(*Hoplunnis similis*)
  - [細尾長犁齒鰻](../Page/細尾長犁齒鰻.md "wikilink")(*Hoplunnis tenuis*)

### 弱頭鰻屬(*Leptocephalus*)

  - [貝氏弱頭鰻](../Page/貝氏弱頭鰻.md "wikilink")(*Leptocephalus bellottii*)
  - [似蛇弱頭鰻](../Page/似蛇弱頭鰻.md "wikilink")(*Leptocephalus ophichthoides*)

### 絲鰻(鴨嘴鰻)屬(*Nettastoma*)

  - [鐮形絲鰻](../Page/鐮形絲鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettastoma falcinaris*)：又稱鐮形鴨嘴鰻。
  - [黑尾絲鰻](../Page/黑尾絲鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettastoma melanurum*)：又稱黑尾鴨嘴鰻。
  - [小頭絲鰻](../Page/小頭絲鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettastoma parviceps*)：又稱小頭鴨嘴鰻。
  - [前鼻絲鰻](../Page/前鼻絲鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettastoma
    solitarium*)：又稱[前鼻鴨嘴鰻](../Page/前鼻鴨嘴鰻.md "wikilink")。
  - [古巴絲鰻](../Page/古巴絲鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettastoma syntresis*)：又稱古巴鴨嘴鰻。

### 鴨蛇鰻屬(*Nettenchelys*)

  - [戴氏鴨蛇鰻](../Page/戴氏鴨蛇鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettenchelys dionisi*)
  - [印度洋鴨蛇鰻](../Page/印度洋鴨蛇鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettenchelys erroriensis*)
  - [野鴨舌鰻](../Page/野鴨舌鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettenchelys exoria*)
  - [鴨蛇鰻](../Page/鴨蛇鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettenchelys gephyra*)
  - [大枕鴨蛇鰻](../Page/大枕鴨蛇鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettenchelys inion*)
  - [帕氏鴨蛇鰻](../Page/帕氏鴨蛇鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettenchelys paxtoni*)
  - [矮身鴨蛇鰻](../Page/矮身鴨蛇鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettenchelys pygmaeus*)
  - [泰勒鴨蛇鰻](../Page/泰勒鴨蛇鰻.md "wikilink")(*Nettenchelys taylori*)

### 蜥鰻屬(*Saurenchelys*)

  - [細蜥鰻](../Page/細蜥鰻.md "wikilink")(*Saurenchelys cancrivora*)
  - [野蜥鰻](../Page/野蜥鰻.md "wikilink")(*Saurenchelys cognita*)
  - [線尾蜥鰻](../Page/線尾蜥鰻.md "wikilink")(*Saurenchelys fierasfer*)
  - [裸蜥鰻](../Page/裸蜥鰻.md "wikilink")(*Saurenchelys finitimus*)
  - [側斑蜥鰻](../Page/側斑蜥鰻.md "wikilink")(*Saurenchelys lateromaculatus*)
  - [米氏蜥鰻](../Page/米氏蜥鰻.md "wikilink")(*Saurenchelys meteori*)
  - [柱蜥鰻](../Page/柱蜥鰻.md "wikilink")(*Saurenchelys stylurus*)
  - [臺灣蜥鰻](../Page/臺灣蜥鰻.md "wikilink")(*Saurenchelys taiwanensis*)

### 巫鰻屬(*Venefica*)

  - [多孔巫鰻](../Page/多孔巫鰻.md "wikilink")(*Venefica multiporosa*)
  - [眼斑巫鰻](../Page/眼斑巫鰻.md "wikilink")(*Venefica ocella*)
  - [黑吻巫鰻](../Page/黑吻巫鰻.md "wikilink")(*Venefica proboscidea*)
  - [長巫鰻](../Page/長巫鰻.md "wikilink")(*Venefica procera*)
  - [巫鰻](../Page/巫鰻.md "wikilink")(*Venefica tentaculata*)

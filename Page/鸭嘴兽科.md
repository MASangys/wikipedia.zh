**鴨嘴獸科**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：）是[單孔目下的一个科](../Page/單孔目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

  -   - †[單孔屬](../Page/單孔屬.md "wikilink") *Monotrematum*
          - †*[Monotrematum
            sudamericanum](../Page/Monotrematum.md "wikilink")*
      - †[頑齒鴨嘴獸属](../Page/頑齒鴨嘴獸属.md "wikilink") *Obdurodon*
          - †*[Obdurodon
            dicksoni](../Page/Obdurodon_dicksoni.md "wikilink")*
          - †*[Obdurodon
            insignis](../Page/Obdurodon_insignis.md "wikilink")*
          - †*[Obdurodon
            tharalkooschild](../Page/Obdurodon_tharalkooschild.md "wikilink")*
      - [鴨嘴獸屬](../Page/鴨嘴獸屬.md "wikilink") *Ornithorhynchus*
          - *[Ornithorhynchus
            anatinus](../Page/Ornithorhynchus_anatinus.md "wikilink")*

*[Steropodon](../Page/Steropodon.md "wikilink")*、*[Teinolophos](../Page/Teinolophos.md "wikilink")*原來也位於該科，但是後來劃分入新的科，即[硬齒鴨嘴獸科](../Page/硬齒鴨嘴獸科.md "wikilink")。\[1\]

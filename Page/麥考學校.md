**麥考學校**
（）是[费城](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/费城 "wikilink")的一所[公立](../Page/费城教育局.md "wikilink")[八年制學校](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/八年制學校 "wikilink")，服务Society
Hill、[费城华埠](../Page/费城华埠.md "wikilink")、\[1\]和。\[2\]麥考學校以得名。

## 历史

学校创立于1911年2月,\[3\] 麦考学校承担了一部分来自 Binney 学校的学生,第一校长（Louis Nusbaum）是Horace
Binney学校旧校长。\[4\]

McCall, in the 1920s and 1930s, was known for its "Continuation School",
a program for students aged 14 and up, until age 16, where they attend
school once per week while finding work. At the time compulsory school
attendance was until age 14.\[5\]
1950年代，麥考學校為學生提供[以英語為第二語言的教學課程](../Page/以英語為第二語言或外語.md "wikilink")，这是美国公立学校提供的首例。1953年，參加學校ESL課程的學生超過兩百人，而且來自二十多個國家\[6\]。

《黑人企业》 （Black
Enterprise）杂志称，大约在1964年时，麦考学校“以黑人学生为主，被人们所忽视，几乎不配称为一个学校”。\[7\]这也反映了学校所在社区当时的状态
--
同一杂志称其为“城市中最令人沮丧的贫民区之一”。\[8\]从那时起，富裕的居民开始把孩子送到其他学校。\[9\]到1976年，这个街区逐渐贵族化，以白人为主了。\[10\]

In the mid-1960s it received an auditorium and gymnasium,\[11\] and
prior to 1976 it received a renovation. By then the neighborhood became
mostly white and wealthy.\[12\] By 1976 the school became one of the
highest-ranked schools in Philadelphia; the student body of McCall
reflected the wealthier and white majority demographics.\[13\] According
to *Black Enterprise*, the demographic and academic transformation of
McCall was a demonstration of the phrase "The better the neighborhood,
the better the schools."\[14\]

By September 1998 the school, which had 650 students, established a
bilingual English-Chinese program to serve [Chinese
American](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_Americans "wikilink")
students, and that month it began hiring teachers fluent in both
languages to teach core subjects. At the time 25% of the students were
Chinese, and the number of Chinese students had doubled in the previous
two-year period.\[15\] Russell Scott Smith of
*[Edutopia](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edutopia "wikilink")* wrote
that in 2004 , compared with other schools in Philadelphia, "McCall
already had a fairly good reputation for academic rigor and safety" and
that by 2009 it had improved even more.\[16\] In 2012 Kristen A. Graham
of *[The Philadelphia
Inquirer](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Philadelphia_Inquirer "wikilink")*
stated that McCall was one of "the district's stronger neighborhood
schools".\[17\]

## 运营

家庭学校联谊会\[18\](McCall Home and School
Association)是麥考學校的[家長教師聯誼會](../Page/家長教師聯誼會.md "wikilink")。\[19\]

## 学生人口

2009年学生们包括：55%亚洲人、27%黑人、11%白人、4%其他民族人、1.7%西班牙裔/拉丁裔人。\[20\]
截止2012年，有超过一半的学生生活在[贫穷门槛以下](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/贫穷门槛 "wikilink")。\[21\]

2005年费城华埠的交不起私立學校学费的父母们将他们的孩子送到麥考學校。\[22\]

## 校园

In 1976 *Black Enterprise* stated that there is more emphasis on saving
historical buildings in Philadelphia compared to other major American
cities; it stated that "in any other large American city it would
probably have been torn down by now."\[23\]

The school received a "cybrary" in 2007a library facility with 22
computers and materials in [Mandarin
Chinese](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mandarin_Chinese "wikilink")
financed by fundraisers and bake sales.\[24\]

## 校服

2001年[费城教育局颁布所有学校的校服政策](../Page/费城教育局.md "wikilink") ,麥考學校颁布校服政策。\[25\]

## 交通

In 1993 the school district began a school bus route between Chinatown
and McCall.\[26\] The school is  away from the furthest point of
Chinatown.\[27\] The district was not required to install the route, but
parents advocated for the route because they believed that traffic at
Market Street and Washington Square endangered their children. When the
district proposed to eliminate the route in 1994, parents
complained.\[28\]

## 学区情况

All persons zoned to McCall are also zoned to [Benjamin Franklin High
School](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Benjamin_Franklin_High_School_\(Philadelphia\) "wikilink").\[29\]
Previously [Furness High
School](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Furness_High_School "wikilink")
was the zoned high school for McCall zone residents.\[30\]

**黃綠灰蝶屬**（**Gem**，學名：***Chloroselas***）是[灰蝶科](../Page/灰蝶科.md "wikilink")[噩灰蝶亞科中的一個屬](../Page/噩灰蝶亞科.md "wikilink")。物種分佈於非洲\[1\]。

## 物種

  - [銀黃綠灰蝶](../Page/銀黃綠灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Chloroselas argentea*
  - [雅黃綠灰蝶](../Page/雅黃綠灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Chloroselas azurea*
  - [黃綠灰蝶](../Page/黃綠灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Chloroselas esmeralda*
  - [小黃綠灰蝶](../Page/小黃綠灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Chloroselas minima*
  - [歐黃綠灰蝶](../Page/歐黃綠灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Chloroselas ogadenensis*
  - [奧黃綠灰蝶](../Page/奧黃綠灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Chloroselas overlaeti*
  - [偽黃綠灰蝶](../Page/偽黃綠灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Chloroselas pseudozeritis*
  - *Chloroselas tamaniba*
  - [塔黃綠灰蝶](../Page/塔黃綠灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Chloroselas taposana* = *C.
    tapnaosa* \[missp.?\]
  - [泰黃綠灰蝶](../Page/泰黃綠灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Chloroselas trembathi*
  - [婉黃綠灰蝶](../Page/婉黃綠灰蝶.md "wikilink") *Chloroselas vansomereni*

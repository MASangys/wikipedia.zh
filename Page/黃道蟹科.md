**黃道蟹科**（[學名](../Page/學名.md "wikilink")：Cancridae）是[黃道蟹總科的一](../Page/黃道蟹總科.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。它們由6個現存的[屬](../Page/屬.md "wikilink")\[1\]及11個只有[化石的屬組成](../Page/化石.md "wikilink")。\[2\]這些屬詳列如下：

  - Cancrinae
      - *Anatolikos*
      - *Anisospinos*
      - [黃道蟹屬](../Page/黃道蟹屬.md "wikilink")（*Cancer*）
      - *Ceronnectes*
      - *Cyclocancer*
      - *Glebocarcinus*
      - *Metacarcinus*
      - *Microdium*
      - *Notocarcinus*
      - *Platepistoma*
      - *Romaleon*
      - *Santeecarcinus*
      - *Sarahcarcinus*
  - Lobocarcininae
      - *Lobocarcinus*
      - *Miocyclus*
      - *Tasadia*

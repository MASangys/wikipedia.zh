**黑棘鮫科**（学名：**''Dalatiidae**''）又名**鎧鯊科**，是[板鰓亞綱](../Page/板鰓亞綱.md "wikilink")[角鯊目的其中一科](../Page/角鯊目.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**黑棘鮫科**下現存分7個屬9種，如下：

### [鎧鯊屬](../Page/鎧鯊屬.md "wikilink")(*Dalatias*) <small>[Rafinesque](../Page/Constantine_Samuel_Rafinesque.md "wikilink"), 1810</small>

  - [鎧鯊](../Page/鎧鯊.md "wikilink")(*Dalatias licha*)
    <small>[Bonnaterre](../Page/Pierre_Joseph_Bonnaterre.md "wikilink"),
    1788</small>：又稱黑鮫。

### 擬小鰭鯊屬(*Euprotomicroides*) <small>[Hulley](../Page/P._Alexander_Hulley.md "wikilink") & [M. J. Penrith](../Page/M._John_Penrith.md "wikilink"), 1966</small>

  - [亮尾擬小鰭鯊](../Page/亮尾擬小鰭鯊.md "wikilink")(*Euprotomicroides
    zantedeschia*) <small>Hulley and M. J. Penrith, 1966</small>

### 小鰭鯊屬(*Euprotomicrus*) <small>[T. N. Gill](../Page/Theodore_Gill.md "wikilink"), 1865</small>

  - [白邊小鰭鯊](../Page/白邊小鰭鯊.md "wikilink")(*Euprotomicrus bispinatus*)
    <small>[Quoy](../Page/Jean_René_Constant_Quoy.md "wikilink") &
    [Gaimard](../Page/Joseph_Paul_Gaimard.md "wikilink"), 1824</small>

### 似異鱗角鯊屬(*Heteroscymnoides*) <small>[Fowler](../Page/Henry_Weed_Fowler.md "wikilink"), 1934</small>

  - [白邊似異鱗角鯊](../Page/白邊似異鱗角鯊.md "wikilink")(*Heteroscymnoides marleyi*)
    <small>Fowler, 1934</small>

### [達摩鯊屬](../Page/達摩鯊屬.md "wikilink")(*Isistius*) <small>T. N. Gill, 1865</small>

  - [雪茄達摩鯊](../Page/雪茄達摩鯊.md "wikilink")(*Isistius brasiliensis*)
    <small>Quoy & Gaimard, 1824</small>：又稱**雪茄鮫**、**巴西達摩鯊**、**唇達摩鯊**
  - [大齒達摩鯊](../Page/大齒達摩鯊.md "wikilink")(*Isistius plutodus*)
    <small>[Garrick](../Page/Jack_Garrick.md "wikilink") & [S.
    Springer](../Page/Stewart_Springer.md "wikilink"), 1964</small>

### 軟鱗鎧鯊屬(*Mollisguama*) <small>[Dolganov](../Page/Vladimir_Nikolaevich_Dolganov.md "wikilink"), 1984</small>

  - [巴氏軟鱗鎧鯊](../Page/巴氏軟鱗鎧鯊.md "wikilink")(*Mollisguama parini*)
    <small>Dolganov, 1984</small>

### 擬角鯊屬(*Squaliolus*) <small>[H. M. Smith](../Page/Hugh_McCormick_Smith.md "wikilink") & [Radcliffe](../Page/Lewis_Radcliffe.md "wikilink"), 1912</small>

  - [阿里擬角鯊](../Page/阿里擬角鯊.md "wikilink")(*Squaliolus aliae*)
    <small>[Teng](../Page/Teng_Huo-Tu.md "wikilink"),
    1959</small>：又稱阿里擬角鮫。
  - [寬尾擬角鯊](../Page/寬尾擬角鯊.md "wikilink")(*Squaliolus laticaudus*)
    <small>H. M. Smith and Radcliffe, 1912</small>：又稱硬背侏儒鯊、小抹香鮫。

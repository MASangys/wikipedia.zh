**鼠麴草屬**（學名：）是[菊科](../Page/菊科.md "wikilink")[菊亞科](../Page/菊亞科.md "wikilink")[鼠麴草族之下一個](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/鼠麴草族 "wikilink")[被子植物](../Page/被子植物.md "wikilink")的[属](../Page/属.md "wikilink")\[1\]\[2\]，廣泛分佈於[温带](../Page/温带.md "wikilink")地區，儘管偶爾亦會在一些熱帶地區的高山或亞熱帶地區發現其踪影。

本屬物種為[北美紅蛺蝶](../Page/北美紅蛺蝶.md "wikilink")[幼蟲的重要食糧](../Page/毛蟲.md "wikilink")。

## Secondary metabolites

*Gnaphalium* species are known to contain [黄酮类化合物](../Page/黄酮类化合物.md "wikilink") and . Recently, two unique caffeoyl-D-glucaric acid derivatives, leontopodic acid and leontopodic acid B formerly only known from *[高山火絨草](../Page/高山火絨草.md "wikilink")* (L.) Cass. were detected in various species of *Gnaphalium* together with similar formerly unknown compounds.\[5\]

**鼬属**（学名
*Mustela*），是[哺乳綱](../Page/哺乳綱.md "wikilink")[食肉目](../Page/食肉目.md "wikilink")[鼬科的一屬](../Page/鼬科.md "wikilink")，共有17個種：

  - [纹腹鼬](../Page/纹腹鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela africana*
  - [香鼬](../Page/香鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela altaica*
  - [白鼬](../Page/白鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela erminea*
  - [艾鼬](../Page/艾鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela eversmannii*
  - [南美山鼬](../Page/南美山鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela felipei*
  - [長尾鼬](../Page/長尾鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela frenata*
  - [日本鼬](../Page/日本鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela itatsi*
  - [黄腹鼬](../Page/黄腹鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela kathiah*
  - [欧洲水鼬](../Page/欧洲水鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela lutreola*
  - [爪哇鼬](../Page/爪哇鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela lutreolina*
  - [黑足鼬](../Page/黑足鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela nigripes*
  - [伶鼬](../Page/伶鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela nivalis*
  - [裸足鼬](../Page/裸足鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela nudipes*
  - [歐洲雪貂](../Page/歐洲雪貂.md "wikilink") *Mustela putorius*
  - [黄鼬](../Page/黄鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela sibirica*
  - [鼬](../Page/鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela strigidorsa*
  - [埃及鼬](../Page/埃及鼬.md "wikilink") *Mustela subpalmata*
  - [台灣小黃鼠狼](../Page/台灣小黃鼠狼.md "wikilink") *Mustela. formosana*

[美洲水鼬](../Page/美洲水鼬.md "wikilink")（*Neovison
vison*）和已绝種的[海鼬](../Page/海鼬.md "wikilink")（*Neovison
macrodon*）在1999年已從鼬属歸類到美洲水鼬属（*Neovison*）\[1\]\[2\]\[3\]。

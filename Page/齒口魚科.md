**齒口魚科**（[学名](../Page/学名.md "wikilink")：），是[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[仙女魚目的其中一](../Page/仙女魚目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**齒口魚科**其下分3個屬，如下：

  - 穀口魚屬(*Coccorella*)
      - [大西洋穀口魚](../Page/大西洋穀口魚.md "wikilink")(*Coccorella atlantica*)
      - [阿氏穀口魚](../Page/阿氏穀口魚.md "wikilink")(*Coccorella atrata*)
  - 齒口魚屬(*Evermannella*)
      - [阿氏齒口魚](../Page/阿氏齒口魚.md "wikilink")(*Evermannella ahlstromi*)
      - [葡萄牙齒口魚](../Page/葡萄牙齒口魚.md "wikilink")(*Evermannella balbo*)
      - [印度齒口魚](../Page/印度齒口魚.md "wikilink")(*Evermannella indica*)
      - [大眼齒口魚](../Page/大眼齒口魚.md "wikilink")(*Evermannella megalops*)
      - [黑皮齒口魚](../Page/黑皮齒口魚.md "wikilink")(*Evermannella melanoderma*)
  - 擬強牙巨口魚屬(*Odontostomops*)
      - [細眼擬強牙齒口魚](../Page/細眼擬強牙齒口魚.md "wikilink")(*Odontostomops
        normalops*)

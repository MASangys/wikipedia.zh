**齒唇蘭屬**（[學名](../Page/學名.md "wikilink")：**）是蘭科下的一個屬，又稱**假金線蓮屬**。有時候[雙丸蘭屬亦會包含在本屬下](../Page/雙丸蘭屬.md "wikilink")。

## [物種](../Page/物種.md "wikilink")

  - *Odontochilus acalcaratus* (Aver.) Ormerod (2002).
    ([越南南部](../Page/越南.md "wikilink"))
  - *Odontochilus asraoa* (J.Joseph & Abbar.) Ormerod (2005)
    (以前稱為Evrardia asraoa) ([印度](../Page/印度.md "wikilink"),
    [不丹](../Page/不丹.md "wikilink")).
  - *Odontochilus duplex* (Holttum) Ormerod (2005) (以前稱為Anoectochilus
    duplex) ([泰國](../Page/泰國.md "wikilink"),
    [馬來西亞](../Page/馬來西亞.md "wikilink"))
  - *Odontochilus hatusimanus* Ohwi & T.Koyama (1957).
    ([日本](../Page/日本.md "wikilink"))
    (曾被以為[滅絕](../Page/滅絕.md "wikilink"),
    但再發現於[九州](../Page/九州.md "wikilink")，[鹿兒島](../Page/鹿兒島.md "wikilink")，[福岡](../Page/福岡.md "wikilink"))
  - [南嶺齒唇蘭](../Page/南嶺齒唇蘭.md "wikilink") *Odontochilus nanlingensis*
    (L.P.Siu & K.Y.Lang) Ormerod (2003).
    ([中國](../Page/中國.md "wikilink")) (原名*Anoectochilus
    nanlingensis* L. P. Siu & K. Y. Lang)
  - *Odontochilus poilanei* (Gagnep.) Ormerod (2002).
    ([日本](../Page/日本.md "wikilink") 至
    [東南亞](../Page/東南亞.md "wikilink"))
  - [腎葉齒唇蘭](../Page/腎葉齒唇蘭.md "wikilink") *Odontochilus reniformis*
    (Hook.f.) Ormerod (1998). (馬來西亞)
  - [腐生齒唇蘭](../Page/腐生齒唇蘭.md "wikilink") *Odontochilus saprophyticus*
    (Aver.) Ormerod (2003). (越南南部)
  - *Odontochilus umbrosus* (Aver.) Ormerod (2002). (越南)

**龍蝨科**（），亦作**龍虱科**，為的一種，隸屬於[鞘翅目之下的](../Page/鞘翅目.md "wikilink")[肉食亞目](../Page/肉食亞目.md "wikilink")。生活在田野、水溝、小溪等水體中，是一種[掠食性](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/掠食性動物 "wikilink")[水生昆蟲](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/水生昆蟲 "wikilink")。其學名源於希臘文**（*dytikos*），意思就是「肉食性的潛水甲蟲」。本科物種幾乎見於全世界所有淡水棲境，但亦有部分於落葉堆中棲息。成蟲體長一般在之間，儘管物種間的差異會比較大。當中見於歐洲的**及巴西的**有最大的體型，可分別長至到。與之相反，擁有最小體型的物種應當為棲息於[澳大利亞地下水中的](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/澳大利亞 "wikilink")*
atypicali*，只有約長\[1\]。
大多數物種的顏色都在深棕色、黑色或深[橄榄绿](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/橄榄绿 "wikilink")，某些[亚科的物種會在其邊沿帶有金黃色的條狀斑紋](../Page/亚科.md "wikilink")\[2\]。
They have short, but sharp s. Immediately upon biting, they deliver
digestive enzymes. The
[幼体e](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/幼体 "wikilink") are commonly
known as **water tigers**.\[3\] The family includes more than 4,000
described [物种](../Page/物种.md "wikilink") in numerous
[属](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/属 "wikilink").\[4\]

## 幼蟲及其發育

[Dytiscidae_larva.jpg](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Dytiscidae_larva.jpg "fig:Dytiscidae_larva.jpg")
When still in larval form, the beetles vary in size from about 1 to 5 cm
(0.5 to 2.0 in). The larval bodies are shaped like crescents, with the
tail long and covered with thin hairs. Six legs protrude from along the
[胸](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/胸 "wikilink"), which also sports the
same thin hairs. The head is flat and square, with a pair of long, large
pincers. When hunting, they cling to grasses or pieces of wood along the
bottom, and hold perfectly still until prey passes by, then they lunge,
trapping their prey between their front legs and biting down with their
pincers. Their usual prey includes [蝌蚪s](../Page/蝌蚪.md "wikilink") and
s, among other smaller water-dwelling creatures.

As the larvae mature, they crawl from the water on the sturdy legs, and
bury themselves in the mud for [蛹](../Page/蛹.md "wikilink"). After about
a week, or longer in some species, they emerge from the mud as adults.

## 食用

本科物種的成蟲，特别是*[Cybister](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cybister "wikilink")*屬的物種，均為[可食用昆蟲](../Page/食用昆蟲.md "wikilink")。在美國[内华达州](../Page/内华达州.md "wikilink")的一個山洞内，還在當地史前人類遺下的[粪化石内發現有](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/粪化石 "wikilink")**被食用的殘骸\[5\]。在[墨西哥](../Page/墨西哥.md "wikilink")，他們在吃*C.
explanatus*時會以[墨西哥夹饼夾着來吃](../Page/墨西哥夹饼.md "wikilink")。在日本多個地區（例如：[長野縣](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/長野縣 "wikilink")）有以[日本龍蝨](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/日本龍蝨 "wikilink")（**）為食的習慣；在[中华人民共和国](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/中华人民共和国 "wikilink")[广东省](../Page/广东省.md "wikilink")，除了日本龍蝨以外，亦會食用*[C.
bengalensis](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cybister_bengalensis "wikilink")*、*[C.
guerini](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cybister_guerini "wikilink")*、*[C.
limbatus](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cybister_limbatus "wikilink")*、*[C.
sugillatus](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cybister_sugillatus "wikilink")*、*[C.
tripunctatus](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cybister_tripunctatus "wikilink")*等物種。很可能也包括在內，但這個物種的肉很少。也有報告指在[台灣](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/台灣 "wikilink")、[泰国及](../Page/泰国.md "wikilink")[新幾內亞亦有食用本科物種的習慣](../Page/新幾內亞.md "wikilink")\[6\]。
[Dytiscidae.png](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Dytiscidae.png "fig:Dytiscidae.png")
Large but slow on land and not particularly fierce as adults, they are
also eaten with relish by many midsized [鸟s](../Page/鸟.md "wikilink"),
[哺乳动物s](../Page/哺乳动物.md "wikilink"), and other larger
[捕食s](../Page/捕食.md "wikilink"). The larvae are usually safer,
due to their [保護色](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/保護色 "wikilink") and
ability to escape by water jet; they can be quite hard to catch and may
become [顶级掠食者s](../Page/顶级掠食者.md "wikilink") in small
[池塘s](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/池塘 "wikilink").

## Cultural significance

The diving beetle plays a role in a [切羅基人](../Page/切羅基人.md "wikilink")
creation story. According to the narrative, upon finding nowhere to rest
in the "liquid chaos" the beetle brought up soft mud from the bottom.
This mud then spread out to form all of the land on Earth.\[7\]

## Ethnobiology

Adult Dytiscidae, as well as [豉甲科](../Page/豉甲科.md "wikilink"), are
collected by young girls in [东非](../Page/东非.md "wikilink"). It is
believed that inducing the beetles to bite the nipples will stimulate
breast growth.\[8\]

## 寄生蟲

[螨](../Page/螨.md "wikilink") of the genus
*[Dytiscacarus](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dytiscacarus "wikilink")*
were found to be are highly specialised [寄生](../Page/寄生.md "wikilink")
of [鞘翅目](../Page/鞘翅目.md "wikilink") in the family Dytiscidae, undergoing
their entire life cycle while inhabiting the space beneath the
[翅鞘](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/翅鞘 "wikilink") of their
hosts.\[9\]

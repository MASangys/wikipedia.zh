**龍鰧科**為[輻鰭魚綱](../Page/輻鰭魚綱.md "wikilink")[鱸形目的其中一個](../Page/鱸形目.md "wikilink")[科](../Page/科.md "wikilink")。

## 分類

**龍鰧科**其下分11個屬，如下：

### 棘龍鰧屬(*Acanthodraco*)

  -   - [棘龍鰧](../Page/棘龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Acanthodraco dewitti*)

### 裸頭龍鰧屬(*Akarotaxis*)

  -   - [裸頭龍鰧](../Page/裸頭龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Akarotaxis nudiceps*)

### 淵龍鰧屬(*Bathydraco*)

  -   - [南極淵龍鰧](../Page/南極淵龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Bathydraco antarcticus*)
      - [多耙淵龍鰧](../Page/多耙淵龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Bathydraco joannae*)
      - [大鱗淵龍鰧](../Page/大鱗淵龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Bathydraco macrolepis*)
      - [斑條淵龍鰧](../Page/斑條淵龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Bathydraco marri*)
      - [斷線淵龍鰧](../Page/斷線淵龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Bathydraco scotiae*)

### 天鵝龍鰧屬(*Cygnodraco*)

  -   - [天鵝龍鰧](../Page/天鵝龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Cygnodraco mawsoni*)

### 姥龍鰧屬(*Gerlachea*)

  -   - [澳洲姥龍鰧](../Page/澳洲姥龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Gerlachea australis*)

### 裸龍鰧屬(*Gymnodraco*)

  -   - [尖頭裸龍鰧](../Page/尖頭裸龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Gymnodraco acuticeps*)

### 副帶腭魚屬(*Parachaenichthys*)

  -   - [扁嘴副帶腭魚](../Page/扁嘴副帶腭魚.md "wikilink")(*Parachaenichthys
        charcoti*)
      - [副帶腭魚](../Page/副帶腭魚.md "wikilink")(*Parachaenichthys
        georgianus*)

### 鋸淵龍鰧屬(*Prionodraco*)

  -   - [鋸淵龍鰧](../Page/鋸淵龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Prionodraco evansii*)

### 滑龍鰧屬(*Psilodraco*)

  -   - [滑龍鰧](../Page/滑龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Psilodraco breviceps*)

### 拉氏淵龍鰧屬(*Racovitzia*)

  -   - [拉氏淵龍鰧](../Page/拉氏淵龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Racovitzia glacialis*)
      - [哈瑞森拉氏淵龍鰧](../Page/哈瑞森拉氏淵龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Racovitzia
        harrissoni*)

### 犁齒龍鰧屬(*Vomeridens*)

  -   - [犁齒龍鰧](../Page/犁齒龍鰧.md "wikilink")(*Vomeridens infuscipinnis*)

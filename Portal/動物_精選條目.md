## Instructions

The layout design for these subpages is at [Portal:動物/精選條目/排版](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal:動物/精選條目/排版 "wikilink").

1.  Add a new [Selected article](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/WP:FA "wikilink") to the next available subpage.
      - The list should only contain articles that have been given a quality rating of [Good Articles](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/WP:GA "wikilink").
2.  The "blurb" for all Selected articles should be approximately 8 lines, for appropriate formatting in the portal main page.
3.  Update "max=" to new total for its  on the main page.

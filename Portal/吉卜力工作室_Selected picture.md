## 用法

The layout design for these subpages is at [Portal:吉卜力工作室/Selected picture/Layout](https://zh.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal:吉卜力工作室/Selected_picture/Layout "wikilink").

1.  将一个新的精选作品添加到下一个可用子页面。
2.  Update "max=" to new total for its  on the main page. <small>([Edit main page](http://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Portal:Studio_Ghibli&action=edit))</small>
3.  When an image is chosen, place  onto the image's page.
